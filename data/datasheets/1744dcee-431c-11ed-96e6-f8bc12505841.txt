### Component Summary: HMC432ETR

**Description:**
The HMC432ETR is a low noise, divide-by-2 static divider utilizing InGaP GaAs HBT technology. It is designed for high-frequency applications, operating from DC to 8 GHz. The component is packaged in an ultra-small SOT26 plastic package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The device features single-ended inputs and outputs, which help reduce component count and overall system cost.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +3V ± 0.3V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 42 mA (typical), 56 mA (max)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Consumption: 126 mW (at Vcc = 3V, Icc = 42 mA)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT26 (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low SSB Phase Noise: -148 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz offset
  - Single-ended I/O configuration
  - Reverse Leakage: -30 dBm (RF output terminated)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC432ETR is primarily used in:
- Prescaler for DC to C Band PLL applications
- UNII, Point-to-Point, and VSAT radios
- 802.11a and HiperLAN WLAN systems
- Fiber optic communication systems
- Cellular and 3G infrastructure

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise frequency division with minimal noise, making it suitable for high-performance communication systems.
