  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 75 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**

  - Storage Temperature Range (T_stg): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD123
- **Special Features:**

  - Tin plated leads, solderable per J-STD-002 and JESD22-B102
  - Cathode line denotes the cathode end
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The 1N4148W is a small-signal fast switching diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It is characterized by its ability to handle moderate current levels while providing a high reverse voltage rating, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Extreme Fast Switching:** The diode is commonly used in applications requiring rapid switching, such as in digital circuits and signal processing.
- **Rectification:** It can be utilized in rectifier circuits for converting AC to DC.
- **Clamping and Protection:** The 1N4148W is often employed in clamping circuits to protect sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **General Purpose:** It serves as a general-purpose diode in various electronic devices, including power management systems and signal conditioning circuits.
