### Component Summary: ADG619BRTZ

**Description:**
The ADG619BRTZ is a monolithic CMOS single-pole double-throw (SPDT) switch designed for low on-resistance and low power dissipation. It is capable of conducting signals in both directions when activated. The device features a break-before-make switching action, which prevents shorting during channel switching.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single Supply: 2.7 V to 5.5 V
  - Dual Supply: ±2.7 V to ±5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current (S or D): 50 mA
  - Peak Current (S or D): 100 mA (pulsed at 1 ms, 10% duty cycle maximum)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical: <0.1 μW
  - IDD: 0.001 μA (typical), 1.0 μA (maximum)
  - ISS: 0.001 μA (typical), 1.0 μA (maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-lead SOT-23
  - 8-lead MSOP
- **Special Features:**
  - Low on-resistance: 4 Ω (typical), 6.5 Ω (maximum)
  - Low power dissipation
  - Fast switching times (tON: 80 ns, tOFF: 45 ns for ADG619)
  - Rail-to-rail operation
  - TTL-/CMOS-compatible inputs
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
The ADG619BRTZ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Automatic test equipment
- Power routing
- Communication systems
- Data acquisition systems
- Sample-and-hold systems
- Avionics
- Relay replacement
- Battery-powered systems

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal routing with minimal power loss and fast switching capabilities. Its low on-resistance and compatibility with both TTL and CMOS logic levels make it versatile for various electronic designs.
