### Component Summary: KP-1608SGD (Kingbright)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 20mA: 2.2V (typical) to 2.5V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 25mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 140mA
  - Reverse Current (IR) at 5V: 10µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 62.5mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +85°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 110°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 1.6 mm x 0.8 mm SMD Chip LED Lamp

- **Special Features:**
  - Super Bright Green color (Gallium Phosphide)
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (160°)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The KP-1608SGD is a Super Bright Green Light Emitting Diode (LED) manufactured by Kingbright. It is designed using Gallium Phosphide technology, which provides high luminous intensity and efficiency. The LED is housed in a compact surface-mount device (SMD) package, making it suitable for various applications where space is a constraint.

#### Typical Applications:
The KP-1608SGD is commonly used in:
- **Backlighting:** Ideal for illuminating displays in consumer electronics.
- **Status Indicators:** Used in devices to indicate power status or operational states.
- **Home and Smart Appliances:** Integrated into appliances for user interface feedback.
- **Wearable and Portable Devices:** Suitable for compact designs requiring visual indicators.
- **Healthcare Applications:** Utilized in medical devices for signaling and alerts.

This LED's characteristics make it a versatile choice for a wide range of electronic applications, particularly where visibility and low power consumption are critical.
