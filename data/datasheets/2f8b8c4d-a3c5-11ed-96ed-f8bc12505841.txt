### Component Summary: BLM21SN300SH1D

**Manufacturer:** Murata Electronics  
**Type:** Chip Ferrite Bead  
**Series:** BLM21_SH Series (Automotive Electronics)  
**AEC-Q200 Compliant:** Yes  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Impedance:** 30 ± 10 Ω at 100 MHz
- **Rated Current:** 8500 mA (typical)
- **DC Resistance:** 0.004 Ω (initial), 0.005 Ω (after testing)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +125°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:** Chip (dimensions: 2.0 mm x 1.25 mm x 0.85 mm)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

#### Description:
The BLM21SN300SH1D is a chip ferrite bead designed for use in automotive electronics. Ferrite beads are passive components that suppress high-frequency noise in electronic circuits. They are particularly effective in filtering out electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI), making them essential for maintaining signal integrity in sensitive electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in various automotive applications to reduce noise and improve signal quality.
- **Power Management:** Helps in filtering noise in power supply circuits, ensuring stable operation of electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Utilized in communication devices to enhance the quality of transmitted signals by minimizing noise.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and computers to improve performance by reducing unwanted noise.
