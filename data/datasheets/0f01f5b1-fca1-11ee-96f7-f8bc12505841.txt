### Component Summary: IPN65R1K5CE

**Description:**
The IPN65R1K5CE is a 650V CoolMOS™ CE power transistor, specifically a MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for high-voltage applications. It utilizes the superjunction (SJ) principle, which allows for high efficiency and low losses, making it suitable for cost-sensitive applications in consumer electronics and lighting.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 650V
  - Maximum Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 5.2A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulse): 9.2A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (Ptot): 5.0W at 25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - PG-SOT223

- **Special Features:**
  - Extremely low losses due to low figure of merit (FOM) Rdson*Qg and Eoss
  - High commutation ruggedness
  - Easy to use and drive
  - Pb-free plating and halogen-free mold compound
  - Qualified for standard grade applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 3 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

### Typical Applications:
The IPN65R1K5CE is primarily used in:
- **Adapters and Chargers:** Efficient power conversion and management.
- **Lighting Applications:** Suitable for LED drivers and other lighting solutions.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Ideal for power supply circuits where efficiency and cost-effectiveness are critical.

This component is designed to meet high efficiency standards while providing a cost-effective solution for various applications, making it a versatile choice for engineers in the power electronics field.
