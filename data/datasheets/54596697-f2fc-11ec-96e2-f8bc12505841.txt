### Component Summary: SMF36A

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SMF36A  
**Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)  
**Package Type:** SOD-123FL  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 40.00V (Min), 44.20V (Max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 36V (Uni-directional), 36V (Bi-directional)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Surge Current (I_PP): 1A
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): 36A with a 10/1000 μs waveform
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 200W with a 10/1000 μs waveform
  - Power Dissipation on Infinite Heat Sink (P_D): 0.4W at T_L = 75°C
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55°C to +150°C
  
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - RθJL: 26°C/W (Between junction and lead)
  - RθJA: 300°C/W (Between junction and ambient)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (Meets J-STD-020E)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low-profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Very fast response time
  - Low incremental surge resistance with excellent clamping capability
  - High temperature soldering guaranteed: 260°C for 10 seconds at terminals
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The SMF36A is a unidirectional surface mount transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is particularly effective in clamping voltage spikes, thereby safeguarding integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protection of sensitive components in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and home appliances.
- **Computers:** Safeguarding motherboards and peripheral devices from voltage spikes.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Protecting control circuits and sensors from transient voltages.
- **Telecommunication:** Ensuring signal integrity and protection in communication devices and networks.

The SMF36A is ideal for applications requiring robust transient voltage protection in a compact form factor, making it suitable for modern electronic designs where space and reliability are critical.
