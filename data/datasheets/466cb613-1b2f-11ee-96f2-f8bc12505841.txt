  - Very low switching losses
  - Low reverse current
  - High reverse voltage capability

#### Description:
The BYV26C is an ultra-fast avalanche diode designed for high-frequency applications. It features a glass passivated junction and is hermetically sealed, which enhances its reliability and performance in various environments. The diode is characterized by low switching losses and low reverse current, making it suitable for applications requiring efficient power management.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Switched Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)**: The BYV26C is commonly used in power supply circuits where rapid switching and high efficiency are critical.
- **High-Frequency Inverter Circuits**: Its fast recovery time and high reverse voltage capability make it ideal for inverter applications, particularly in renewable energy systems and motor drives.
- **General Rectification**: The diode can also be utilized in various rectification applications where high voltage and current handling are required.
