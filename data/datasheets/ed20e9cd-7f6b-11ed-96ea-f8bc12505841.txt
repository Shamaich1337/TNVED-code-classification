- **Capacitance**: 560 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR)**: 200 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS)**: 230 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC)**: -20% to +20% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR)**: 
  - 1.5 A at 120Hz, 105°C
  - 2.25 A at 100kHz, 105°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ)**: 15% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK)**: 1004 µA (5 min at 20°C)
- **Maximum ESR**: 360 mΩ at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +105°C
- **Package Type**: Snap-In type, dimensions 25mm (D) x 25mm (L)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The UJ561M200N250AP6 is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's UJ series, designed for long life and high reliability at elevated temperatures (up to 105°C). It features a snap-in mounting style, making it suitable for various electronic applications where space and stability are critical. The capacitor is characterized by its ultra-stable dissipation factor and low leakage current, which are essential for maintaining performance in demanding environments.

- **Power Supply Circuits**: For smoothing and filtering applications in power supplies, ensuring stable voltage levels.
- **Audio Equipment**: In audio amplifiers and other sound systems where high capacitance and low ESR are required for optimal performance.
- **Industrial Equipment**: In machinery and control systems that require reliable performance under varying temperature conditions.
- **Consumer Electronics**: In devices such as televisions and computers where space-saving components are necessary.
