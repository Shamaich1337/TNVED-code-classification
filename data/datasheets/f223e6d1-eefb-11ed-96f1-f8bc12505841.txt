### Component Summary: FZ2400R17HP4_B29 IGBT Module

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCES): 1700 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1700 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous DC Collector Current (IC nom): 2400 A
  - Repetitive Peak Collector Current (ICRM): 4800 A
  - Continuous DC Forward Current (IF): 2400 A
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 4800 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 15.5 kW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tvj op): -40 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - IHM-B Module with AlSiC base plate

- **Special Features:**
  - Low VCEsat (saturation voltage)
  - Enlarged diode for regenerative operation
  - High power density and thermal cycling capability
  - High creepage and clearance distances
  - CTI (Comparative Tracking Index) > 400

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The FZ2400R17HP4_B29 is an IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) module designed for high-power applications. It integrates Trench/Fieldstop IGBT4 technology along with an Emitter Controlled diode, making it suitable for efficient power conversion and control. The module is engineered to handle high voltage and current levels, making it ideal for demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Resonant Inverter Applications:** Used in systems that require efficient energy conversion with minimal losses.
- **High-Power Converters:** Suitable for applications in renewable energy systems, such as solar inverters and wind turbine converters.
- **Traction Drives:** Employed in electric rail systems and other transportation applications where high power and efficiency are critical.
- **Wind Turbines:** Utilized in the conversion of wind energy to electrical energy, ensuring reliable operation under varying conditions.

This IGBT module is particularly advantageous in applications requiring robust performance, high efficiency, and reliability under extreme conditions.
