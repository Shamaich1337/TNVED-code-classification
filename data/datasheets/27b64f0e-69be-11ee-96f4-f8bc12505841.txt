### Component Summary: SBTC-2-10+

**Description:**
The SBTC-2-10+ is a 2-way power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications. It operates within a frequency range of 5 to 1000 MHz and is characterized by low insertion loss and excellent amplitude and phase balance. This component is suitable for use in UHF/VHF receivers and transmitters, as well as cellular applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; typically not a primary concern for RF splitters.
- **Current Ratings:** Power input (as a splitter) is 0.5W max.
- **Power Consumption:** Internal dissipation is 0.125W max.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Case Style: AT790)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss (0.3 dB typ.)
  - Excellent amplitude unbalance (0.1 dB typ.)
  - Very good phase unbalance (1.0 deg. typ.)
  - Temperature stable LTCC base
  - Aqueous washable
  - Protected by US patent 6,963,255
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
- UHF/VHF receivers and transmitters
- Cellular communication systems
- RF signal distribution in various electronic devices

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal splitting with minimal loss, making it a valuable choice for RF engineers and designers in telecommunications and broadcasting sectors.
