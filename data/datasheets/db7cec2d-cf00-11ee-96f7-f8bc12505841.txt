  - Maximum Input/Output Voltage: -0.5 V to +4.6 V

  - Maximum Output Current: ±20 mA

  - Maximum Input Clamping Current: -50 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Static Supply Current (ICC): 0.9 μA (maximum)
  - Power Dissipation: 
    - SOT765-1 (VSSOP8): 250 mW
    - SOT833-1 (XSON8): 250 mW
    - SOT1089 (XSON8): 250 mW

    - SOT1233-2 (X2SON8): 300 mW

  - -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - XSON8 (no leads, 8 terminals, body size 1 × 1.95 × 0.5 mm)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Schmitt-trigger action at all inputs for noise immunity
  - Overvoltage tolerant inputs up to 3.6 V
  - Partial power-down mode operation with I_OFF circuitry
  - ESD protection: HBM class 3A (exceeds 5000 V), CDM class C3 (exceeds 1000 V)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **74AUP1G74** is a low-power, single positive-edge triggered D-type flip-flop. It features individual data (D), clock (CP), set (SD), and reset (RD) inputs, along with complementary Q and Q outputs. The device is designed to store data at the D-input that meets the setup and hold time requirements during the LOW-to-HIGH clock transition. The Schmitt-trigger action at all inputs allows for tolerance of slower input rise and fall times, making it suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The 74AUP1G74 is commonly used in:
- **Data Storage**: As a memory element in digital circuits.
- **State Machines**: For implementing finite state machines in digital logic designs.
- **Timing Circuits**: In applications requiring precise timing and control.
- **Signal Processing**: For managing and processing digital signals in various electronic devices.
- **Power Management**: In low-power applications where energy efficiency is critical.

This component is ideal for use in battery-operated devices, portable electronics, and other applications where low power consumption is essential.
