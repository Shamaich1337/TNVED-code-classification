### Component Summary: LM4752TS/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: 9V to 40V (operating), 40V (absolute maximum)
  - Input Voltage: ±0.7V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: Internally limited
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Quiescent Power Supply Current: 10.5 mA (max), 7 mA (min)
  - Power Dissipation: 62.5W (absolute maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 7-lead TO-220 (DDPAK)
- **Special Features:**
  - Internal gain resistors (AV = 34 dB)
  - Internal current limiting
  - Internal thermal protection
  - Single supply operation
  - Low external component requirement
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 3, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LM4752 is a stereo audio power amplifier designed to deliver high output power in a compact package. It is capable of providing 11W per channel into a 4Ω load and 7W per channel into an 8Ω load when powered by a single 24V supply at 10% Total Harmonic Distortion plus Noise (THD+N). The device integrates gain and bias resistors on-chip, which simplifies the design and reduces the number of external components required.

#### Typical Applications:
The LM4752 is primarily used in audio amplification applications, including:
- Compact stereo systems
- Stereo televisions
- Mini-component stereo systems
- Multimedia speakers

This amplifier is particularly suited for applications where space is limited and high power output is required, making it ideal for consumer electronics and portable audio devices. The ability to operate from a single supply and the low cost-per-watt further enhance its appeal in various audio applications.
