### Component Summary: LM2665M6/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.5V to 5.5V
  - Output Voltage (Voltage Doubler): Up to 2 * V+ (when V+ is within input range)
  - Voltage Divider Range: 1.8V to 11V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 40 mA
  - Continuous Output Current: 50 mA (absolute maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (No Load): 650 µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current: 1 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (6 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - 90% typical conversion efficiency at 40 mA load
  - 1 µA typical shutdown current
  - Integrated CMOS charge pump for voltage doubling and splitting
  - Low output impedance (12 Ω typical)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **LM2665** is a CMOS charge-pump voltage converter designed to operate as a voltage doubler or a voltage divider. It can double an input voltage in the range of 2.5V to 5.5V, providing an output current of up to 40 mA. The device utilizes two external capacitors and a diode (for startup) to achieve its voltage conversion. It operates at a frequency of 160 kHz, which helps minimize output resistance and voltage ripple. The LM2665 is particularly suited for battery-powered applications due to its low quiescent current and efficient operation.

#### Typical Applications:
The LM2665 is commonly used in various portable and battery-operated devices, including:
- **Cellular Phones**
- **Pagers**
- **Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs)**
- **Operational Amplifier Power Supplies**
- **Interface Power Supplies**
- **Handheld Instruments**
- **Fire Detection and Notification Systems**
- **Industrial Handheld Radios**
- **Blood Pressure Monitors**

This versatility makes the LM2665 an ideal choice for applications requiring efficient voltage conversion without the need for inductors, thus allowing for a compact design.
