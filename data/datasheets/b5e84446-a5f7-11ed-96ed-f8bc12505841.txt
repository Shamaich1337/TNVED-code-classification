### Component Summary: FTLD 7R54-2618

**Description:**
The FTLD 7R54-2618 is a CWDM (Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing) Coaxial Laser Diode Module utilizing a DFB (Distributed Feedback) laser diode. It operates within a wavelength range of 1270 to 1610 nm and is designed for high-speed data transmission, making it suitable for various communication applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Laser Forward Voltage (V_OP): 1.1 to 1.5 V
  - Laser Reverse Bias (V_R(LD)): +2 V
  - Photodiode Reverse Bias (V_R(PD)): +15 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Laser Forward Current (I_F(LD)): 120 mA (DC)
  - Threshold Current (I_th): 5 to 15 mA
  - Operating Current (I_OP): 30 to 50 mA
  - Monitor Dark Current (I_md): 1 to 10 nA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Optical Output Power (P_o): >2 mW (minimum 4 mW typical)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_OP): -20 to +70 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_ST): -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Connector: FC/APC
  - Type: Pigtailed

- **Special Features:**
  - InGaAsP/InP MQW DFB LD
  - Bandwidth: >800 MHz
  - Low Threshold Current
  - Excellent Reliability
  - High Linearity
  - Low RIN (Relative Intensity Noise)
  - RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The FTLD 7R54-2618 is primarily used in:
- WCDMA (Wideband Code Division Multiple Access) systems
- Analog transmission systems
- Trunk communications and Local Area Networks (LAN)

This module is ideal for applications requiring high-speed data transmission over fiber optic networks, leveraging its high bandwidth and low noise characteristics to ensure reliable communication.
