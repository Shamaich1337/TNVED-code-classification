### Component Summary: IR26-61C/L302/TR8

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 65 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Pd): 100 mW at or below 25°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Topr): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +100°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Small double-end SMD package, molded in water-clear plastic with a spherical top view lens.
- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage
  - Good spectral matching to silicon photodiodes and phototransistors
  - Pb-free and RoHS compliant
  - Packaged in 8mm tape on a 7-inch diameter reel
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not explicitly stated, but precautions for moisture handling are provided.

#### Description:
The **IR26-61C/L302/TR8** is an infrared emitting diode (LED) designed for use in various infrared applications. It features a miniature surface-mount device (SMD) package that is molded in clear plastic, allowing for efficient light emission. The device is specifically designed to be spectrally matched with silicon photodiodes and phototransistors, making it suitable for applications that require precise infrared light detection and transmission.

#### Typical Applications:
The IR26-61C/L302/TR8 is commonly used in:
- **Infrared Applied Systems:** This includes remote controls, optical communication systems, and various sensing applications where infrared light is utilized.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as TVs and other devices that require infrared signaling.
- **Industrial Applications:** Including automation and control systems that rely on infrared sensing technology.

This component is ideal for applications that require reliable infrared emission with low power consumption and good spectral compatibility with silicon-based detectors.
