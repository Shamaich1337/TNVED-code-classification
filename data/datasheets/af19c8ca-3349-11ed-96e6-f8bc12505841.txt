### Component Summary: BZX84B3V3-E3-08 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 3.3 V (nominal)
  - Voltage Range: 3.1 V to 3.5 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Test Current (IZT): 5 mA
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 5 µA (max at VZ)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot): 300 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Storage Temperature Range**: 
  - -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT-23
- **Special Features**: 
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (automotive grade)
  - ESD capability: > 8 kV (human body model), > 800 V (machine model)
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - MSL Level 1 (according to J-STD-020)

#### Description:
The BZX84B3V3-E3-08 is a small signal Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and protection applications. It is part of the BZX84 series of silicon planar Zener diodes, which are characterized by their low dynamic resistance and high reliability. The device is specifically designed to maintain a stable voltage across its terminals when reverse-biased, making it ideal for voltage clamping and reference applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation**: Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Signal Clamping**: Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Reference Voltage Generation**: Provides a stable reference voltage for various electronic circuits.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.

This component is widely used in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial applications where voltage regulation and protection are necessary.
