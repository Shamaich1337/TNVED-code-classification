### Component Summary: LS48-A3U-TI-N-DX

**Description:**
The LS48-A3U-TI-N-DX is a 1550 nm single-mode transceiver designed for high-speed data communication. It is a Small Form Pluggable (SFP) module that supports data rates of up to 155 Mbps for SONET OC-3/SDH ST M-1 and 125 Mbps for Fast Ethernet. This transceiver is equipped with diagnostic monitoring capabilities, making it suitable for various networking applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 3.1 V to 3.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 V to 4.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Supply Current: 300 mA (combined TX and RX)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Single power supply of 3.3V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Small Form Pluggable (SFP)

- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant
  - Compliant with SONET/SDH and Fast Ethernet standards
  - Diagnostic monitoring interface compliant with SFF8472
  - Hot pluggable
  - Class 1 laser product (complies with EN 60825-1)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The LS48-A3U-TI-N-DX transceiver is typically used in telecommunications and data networking applications, including:
- SONET/SDH networks for high-speed data transmission.
- Fast Ethernet connections for local area networks (LANs).
- Optical fiber communication systems where reliable data transfer is critical.
- Diagnostic monitoring in network equipment to ensure operational integrity.

This transceiver is ideal for environments requiring robust performance over extended temperature ranges, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor installations in various networking infrastructures.
