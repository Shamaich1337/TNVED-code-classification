### Component Summary: US1D-13-F (Diodes Incorporated)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 200 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 200 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (V_R): 200 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (V_R(RMS)): 140 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (I_O) at T_A = +75°C: 1.0 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 30 A (8.3 ms single half sine wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (V_FM) at I_F = 1.0 A: 1.7 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -65 to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -65 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMA (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Glass Passivated Die Construction
  - Ultra-Fast Recovery Time for High Efficiency
  - Surge Overload Rating to 30A Peak
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free (Green Device)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The US1D-13-F is a surface mount ultra-fast rectifier diode designed for high efficiency in power conversion applications. It features a glass passivated die construction that enhances reliability and performance. The diode is capable of handling high surge currents, making it suitable for demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The US1D-13-F is typically used in:
- Power management circuits
- Rectification in power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Motor control applications
- General-purpose rectification in electronic devices

This component is ideal for automated assembly processes due to its surface mount design and compliance with RoHS standards, making it suitable for modern electronic manufacturing practices.
