### Component Summary: FM16 Schottky Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** FM16  
**Type:** Surface Mount Schottky Rectifier  
**Package Type:** SOD-323FL  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1.0 A
  - Surge (Non-repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM): 25 A
  - Forward Surge Current (Non-repetitive): 50 A (1 ms, square wave)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VFM): 
    - 0.60 V (at IFM = 1.0 A, Tj = 25°C)
    - 0.65 V (at IFM = 1.0 A, Tj = 125°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - RθJ-A: 90 °C/W
  - RθJ-C: 46 °C/W
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Guardring for overvoltage protection
  - Low power losses and high efficiency
  - High forward surge capability
  - RoHS-compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The FM16 is a surface mount Schottky rectifier designed for low voltage and high-frequency applications. Schottky rectifiers are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for applications where efficiency is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- Low voltage high-frequency inverters
- Freewheeling applications
- DC/DC converters
- Polarity protection circuits

This component is particularly useful in power management systems, where it can help improve efficiency and reduce heat generation in electronic devices. Its robust surge current handling makes it suitable for applications that experience transient conditions.
