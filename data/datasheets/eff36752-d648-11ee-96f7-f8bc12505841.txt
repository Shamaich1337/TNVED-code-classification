#### Description:
The SCS Static Shielding Bag (3001212) is a transparent packaging solution designed to protect sensitive electronic devices from electrostatic discharge (ESD). It is constructed from a laminate of polyester, metal, and polyethylene, which provides effective shielding against static electricity. The exterior of the bag is static dissipative, allowing any electrostatic charges to be safely removed when grounded. The bags are available in two styles: Open Top and ZipTop, catering to different packaging needs.

- **Shipping**: Ensuring that electronic products remain safe from ESD during transit.
- **Field Service**: Providing a safe means to transport sensitive equipment to and from job sites.

This product is essential for maintaining the integrity and functionality of electronic devices by preventing damage caused by static electricity. It meets various industry standards for ESD protection, making it a reliable choice for commercial use.
