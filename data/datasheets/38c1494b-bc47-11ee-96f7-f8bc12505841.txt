**Manufacturer:** International Rectifier  
**Product Code:** IRFP4321PBF  
**Type:** N-Channel Power MOSFET

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): 150 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID @ TC = 25°C): 78 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID @ TC = 100°C): 55 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 330 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD @ TC = 25°C): 210 W
  - Linear Derating Factor: 1.68 W/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +175°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247AC

- **Special Features:**
  - Low RDS(on): 12 mΩ (typical), 15.5 mΩ (maximum)
  - Low Gate Charge: 71 nC (typical)
  - Improved diode recovery characteristics
  - Fully characterized avalanche SOA (Safe Operating Area)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRFP4321PBF is an N-Channel Power MOSFET designed for high-efficiency applications. It features low on-resistance and low gate charge, which contribute to reduced power losses and improved switching performance. The device is characterized for avalanche operation, making it robust for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motion Control Applications:** Used in motor drivers and control circuits.
- **High-Efficiency Synchronous Rectification:** Commonly found in switch-mode power supplies (SMPS) to enhance efficiency.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** Utilized in power management systems to ensure reliable power delivery.
- **Hard Switched and High-Frequency Circuits:** Suitable for applications requiring fast switching and high-frequency operation.

This MOSFET is ideal for engineers looking for a reliable and efficient component for power management and control applications in industrial and consumer electronics.
