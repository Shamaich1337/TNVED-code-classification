### Component Summary: APG1005SEC/E-T

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) @ 20mA: 2.0 - 2.4 V
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 25 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 120 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 60 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40 to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 to +85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 1.0 mm x 0.5 mm SMD LED, 0.2 mm thickness (0402)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (120°)
  - Compatible with automatic placement equipment
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 3

**Description:**
The APG1005SEC/E-T is a Hyper Red Light Emitting Diode (LED) made from AlGaInP on a GaAs substrate. It is a surface-mount device (SMD) designed for efficient light emission with low power consumption. The LED features a water-clear lens and is characterized by its compact size, making it suitable for various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Backlighting:** Ideal for illuminating displays in consumer electronics.
- **Status Indicators:** Used in devices to indicate operational status or alerts.
- **Home and Smart Appliances:** Commonly found in appliances for user interface lighting.
- **Wearable and Portable Devices:** Suitable for compact devices requiring efficient lighting solutions.
- **Healthcare Applications:** Utilized in medical devices for indicators and displays.

This LED is particularly noted for its robustness against electrostatic discharge, with a threshold of 3000 V, making it suitable for environments where static electricity may be a concern. The device is designed to be handled with care to prevent damage from electrostatic discharge.
