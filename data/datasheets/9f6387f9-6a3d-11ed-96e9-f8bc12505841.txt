### Component Summary: VLMU3100-GS08

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** VLMU3100-GS08  
**Component Type:** Ultraviolet (UV) SMD LED

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (Vf) at 20 mA: 2.8 V (min) to 3.8 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (If): 30 mA (maximum)
  - Surge Forward Current (Ifsm): 0.1 A (for pulse duration ≤ 10 μs)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot): 120 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -40 °C to +80 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 °C to +100 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): +100 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SMD PLCC-2 (dimensions: 3.2 mm x 2.8 mm x 1.9 mm)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency InGaN technology
  - Long lifetime due to silicone casting
  - Compatible with automatic placement equipment
  - Non-diffused lens for excellent coupling to light pipes and backlighting
  - ESD withstand voltage: up to 1 kV (according to JESD22-A114-B)
  - Preconditioning according to JEDEC level 2a
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E Level 2a

#### Description:
The VLMU3100-GS08 is a high-brightness ultraviolet (UV) surface-mount LED designed for various applications. It utilizes advanced InGaN technology to achieve exceptional brightness and efficiency. The LED is housed in a compact PLCC-2 package, which is suitable for automated assembly processes. The device emits UV light with a peak wavelength of 405 nm, making it ideal for applications requiring non-visible light.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Curing of Glue and Lacquer:** The UV light emitted by the LED is effective in curing adhesives and coatings, making it suitable for manufacturing and assembly processes.
- **Recognition of Safety Features on Currency:** The UV LED can be used in devices that detect security features in banknotes, enhancing anti-counterfeiting measures.

This component is particularly useful in industries where UV light is required for curing processes or for detecting specific features in materials, ensuring both efficiency and reliability in its applications.
