**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 4.5 - 13.2 VDC (Nominal: 9 VDC)
  - Output Voltage: 3.3 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 300 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 1 Watt
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +90°C (without derating)
- **Package Type:**
  - Compact SIP package (11.9 x 7.7 x 11.0 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Fully regulated outputs
  - I/O isolation voltage: 1,600 VDC
  - Short circuit protection
  - Designed to meet IEC/EN/UL 62368-1 (not certified)
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The TRN 1-0510 is a high-performance DC/DC converter from the TRN 1 Series by Traco Power. It is designed to provide a fully regulated output voltage of 3.3 VDC from a wide input voltage range of 4.5 to 13.2 VDC. This compact converter is housed in a small SIP package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. It features robust electrical isolation and protection mechanisms, ensuring reliable operation in various environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The TRN 1-0510 DC/DC converter is typically used in applications such as:
- Power management systems
- Telecommunications equipment
- Industrial automation
- Embedded systems
- Medical devices
- Any application requiring a compact, reliable power supply with high efficiency and thermal performance.

This converter is particularly advantageous in scenarios where a stable voltage is critical, and it can operate effectively in high-temperature environments without the need for derating, making it suitable for demanding applications.
