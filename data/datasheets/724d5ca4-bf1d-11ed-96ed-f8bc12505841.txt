### Component Summary: SN74LVC1G66DBVR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Voltage: Up to 5.5 V
  - I/O Voltage: 0 to VCC + 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
  - ON-state switch current: ±50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply current (ICC): 10 µA (typical at VCC = 5.5 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages including SOT-23 (DBV), SC70 (DCK), SON (DRY, DSF), and DSBGA (YZP).

- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed operation with a maximum propagation delay (tpd) of 0.8 ns at 3.3 V.
  - Low ON-state resistance, typically ≈ 5.5 Ω at VCC = 4.5 V.
  - ESD protection exceeding JESD 22 standards (2000 V HBM, 200 V MM, 1000 V CDM).
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 100 mA per JESD 78, Class II.
  - NanoFree™ package technology for reduced board space.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The SN74LVC1G66 is a single bilateral analog switch designed for low-voltage applications. It operates within a voltage range of 1.65 V to 5.5 V and is capable of handling both analog and digital signals. The device allows bidirectional transmission of signals with amplitudes up to 5.5 V (peak).
