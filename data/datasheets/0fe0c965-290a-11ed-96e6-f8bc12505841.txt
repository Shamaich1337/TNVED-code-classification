### Component Summary: NVMFD5C674NLT1G

**Description:**
The NVMFD5C674NLT1G is a dual N-channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for power applications. It features a compact footprint and is optimized for low conduction and switching losses, making it suitable for various power management tasks.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (V_DSS): 60 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (V_GS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D) at 25°C: 35.2 A (steady state)
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D) at 100°C: 24.9 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (I_DM) at 25°C: 119 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D) at 25°C: 37 W (steady state)
  - Power Dissipation (P_D) at 100°C: 18 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_stg): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - DFN8 (5x6 mm) with a 1.27 mm pitch

- **Special Features:**
  - Low R_DS(on) to minimize conduction losses (14.4 mΩ at V_GS = 10 V)
  - Low total gate charge (Q_G) to minimize driver losses
  - AEC-Q101 qualified and PPAP capable
  - Pb-free and RoHS compliant
  - Wettable flank option for enhanced optical inspection

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The NVMFD5C674NLT1G is typically used in:
- Power management systems
- DC-DC converters
- Motor control applications
- Battery management systems
- Automotive applications due to its AEC-Q101 qualification

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power switching and management, particularly in compact designs where space is a constraint. Its low on-resistance and high current handling capabilities make it suitable for high-efficiency power circuits.
