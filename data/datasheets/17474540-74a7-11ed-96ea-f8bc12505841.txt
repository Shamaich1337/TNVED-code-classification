It is capable of delivering up to 2 watts of output power, making it suitable for high-performance applications in various fields.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +11V to +15V (typical +15V)
  - Gate Bias Voltage (Vgg1): -3V to 0V
  - Gate Bias Voltage (Vgg2): Up to +11.5V (dependent on Vdd)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 500 mA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 9.9 W (at T = 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 32-lead Lead Frame Chip Scale Package (LFCSP), 5mm x 5mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High Gain: 15 dB
  - Output Power at 1 dB Compression (P1dB): +32 dBm

  - Output Third-Order Intercept Point (IP3): +42 dBm

  - RoHS compliant
