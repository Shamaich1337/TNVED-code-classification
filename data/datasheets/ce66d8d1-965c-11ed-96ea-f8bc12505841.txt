### Component Summary: Littelfuse 1210L005WR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Maximum voltage (Vmax) = 30 Vdc
- **Current Ratings**:
  - Hold current (Ihold) = 0.05 A
  - Trip current (Itrip) = 0.15 A
  - Maximum fault current (Imax) = 10 A
- **Power Consumption**: Power dissipated when tripped (Pd typ.) = 0.60 W
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: Surface Mount
- **Special Features**:
  - Fast response to fault currents
  - Compact design for space-saving
  - Low-profile and low resistance
  - Compatible with high-temperature solders
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 1210L005WR is a resettable Polymer Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) device designed for overcurrent protection in surface mount applications. It is particularly useful in scenarios where space is limited and resettable protection is required. The device automatically resets after the fault condition is removed, making it ideal for protecting sensitive electronic components.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Peripherals**: Protects against overcurrent conditions in devices like USB hubs and peripherals.
- **Disk Drives**: Provides protection for hard drives and SSDs against transient overcurrent events.
- **CD-ROMs**: Safeguards optical drives from damage due to excessive current.
- **PC Motherboards**: Offers plug-and-play protection for various components on the motherboard.
- **Mobile Phones**: Protects battery and port connections from overcurrent situations.
- **PDAs/Digital Cameras**: Ensures safe operation of portable electronic devices.
- **Game Consoles**: Protects ports and internal circuits from overcurrent damage.

This component is particularly suited for applications requiring reliable overcurrent protection while maintaining a compact footprint, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
