### Component Summary: CMD178C3

**Description:**
The CMD178C3 is a general-purpose double balanced mixer designed for up- and down-converting applications within the frequency range of 11 to 21 GHz. It features a leadless surface mount package and is optimized for high isolation at both RF and IF ports, making it suitable for various RF applications. The mixer can operate with a low local oscillator (LO) drive level of +9 dBm and can be configured as an image reject mixer or single sideband modulator with external components.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - LO Drive: +22 dBm (maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - IF Pin: Must not source or sink more than 16 mA
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 138 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage: -55°C to +150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 3x3 mm SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low conversion loss (typical 6 dB)
  - High isolation (LO to RF and LO to IF isolation of 45 dB)
  - Wide IF bandwidth (DC to 6 GHz)
  - Passive double balanced topology
  - Pb-Free and RoHS compliant
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The CMD178C3 is commonly used in:
- RF communication systems for signal mixing
- Upconversion and downconversion in radar systems
- Image reject mixers and single sideband modulators
- General RF signal processing applications in telecommunications and satellite communications

This component is particularly valuable in applications requiring high performance in terms of isolation and conversion loss, making it suitable for advanced RF designs.
