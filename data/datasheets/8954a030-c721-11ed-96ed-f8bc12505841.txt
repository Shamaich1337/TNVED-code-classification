### Component Summary: DH40-18A

**Manufacturer:** IXYS  
**Product Code:** DH40-18A  
**Type:** Fast Recovery Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Non-Repetitive Reverse Blocking Voltage (VRRM): 1800 V
  - Maximum Repetitive Reverse Blocking Voltage (VRSM): 1800 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (FAV): 40 A
  - Maximum Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 400 A (10 ms pulse)
  - RMS Current (IRMS): 70 A per terminal
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 280 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TVJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:** TO-247
- **Special Features:**
  - Planar passivated chips
  - Very low leakage current
  - Very short recovery time (trr: 300 ns)
  - Soft recovery behavior for low EMI/RFI
  - Improved thermal behavior
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The DH40-18A is a high-performance fast recovery diode designed for applications requiring low loss and soft recovery characteristics. It features a planar passivated chip structure that minimizes leakage current and enhances thermal performance. The diode is optimized for fast switching applications, making it suitable for high-frequency operations.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:**
  - Used as a freewheeling diode in switch mode power supplies (SMPS).
  - Acts as an antisaturation diode in power circuits.
- **Signal Processing:**
  - Functions as an antiparallel diode for high-frequency switching devices.
- **Energy Storage:**
  - Utilized in uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) to ensure reliable operation during power outages.
- **General Rectification:**
  - Employed in various rectifier applications due to its low forward voltage drop and efficient recovery characteristics.

This diode is particularly advantageous in applications where low electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI) are critical, thanks to its soft recovery behavior.
