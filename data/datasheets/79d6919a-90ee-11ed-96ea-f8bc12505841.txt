### Component Summary: BZT52C47

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** BZT52C47  
**Component Type:** Zener Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Zener Voltage (Vz):** 44V (typical), 47V (nominal), 50V (max)
- **Maximum Regulator Current (IZM):** 5 mA
- **Power Dissipation (PD):** 500 mW
- **Reverse Current (IR):** 100 µA at VZ
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Maximum Forward Voltage (VF at IF=10mA):** 0.9V
- **Thermal Resistance (Junction to Ambient Air):** 250 °C/W
- **Package Type:** SOD-123
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 2 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BZT52C47 is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. Zener diodes are semiconductor devices that allow current to flow in the reverse direction when the voltage exceeds a certain value, known as the Zener breakdown voltage. The BZT52C47 specifically provides a Zener voltage of 47V, making it suitable for applications requiring stable voltage references.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Overvoltage Protection:** Protects sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes.
- **Signal Clamping:** Used in signal processing to limit voltage levels to a specified range.
- **Reference Voltage Sources:** Provides a stable reference voltage for various electronic circuits.

This Zener diode is characterized by high reliability, low reverse current levels, and a very sharp reverse characteristic, making it a preferred choice in various electronic applications.
