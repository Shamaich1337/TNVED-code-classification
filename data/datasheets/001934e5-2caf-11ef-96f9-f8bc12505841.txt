The ER34615M is a primary lithium battery utilizing lithium-thionyl chloride (Li-SOCl2) chemistry. The battery is housed in a stainless steel container with glass-to-metal hermetic sealing, ensuring durability and reliability in various environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The ER34615M battery is commonly used in:
- Security Systems
- Smart Metering
- RFID and IoT Systems
- Wireless Transmitting Devices
- Smart Home Devices
- Military Devices
