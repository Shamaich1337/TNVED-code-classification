The MM3Z6V2B is a silicon planar Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and protection applications. It is housed in a compact SOD-323 package, making it suitable for surface-mounted designs. - **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Overvoltage Protection:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
