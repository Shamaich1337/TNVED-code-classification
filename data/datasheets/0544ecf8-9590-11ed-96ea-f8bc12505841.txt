### Component Summary: MMSD4148T3G

**Description:**
The MMSD4148T3G is a switching diode manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed for high-speed switching applications and is suitable for automotive and other applications that require unique site and control change requirements. The diode is AEC-Q101 qualified, making it suitable for automotive environments.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Continuous Reverse Voltage (VR): 100 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 2.0 A (for t < 1 sec)
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 0.5 A (Pulse Wave = 1 sec, Duty Cycle = 66%)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD): 425 mW at TA = 25 °C
  - Thermal Resistance Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 290 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-123 (Surface Mount Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - High breakdown voltage and fast switching time.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The MMSD4148T3G is typically used in:
- **Switching Applications:** Ideal for high-speed switching in various electronic circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in signal rectification and clamping applications.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Suitable for automotive circuits due to its AEC-Q101 qualification.
- **General Purpose Diode Applications:** Can be used in various electronic devices requiring reliable diode performance.

This diode is particularly valued for its reliability and performance in environments where space is limited, thanks to its compact SOD-123 package.
