### Component Summary: NTB0104GU12,115

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCC(A): 1.2 V to 3.6 V
  - VCC(B): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output current (IO): ±50 mA
  - Supply current (ICC): -100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total power dissipation (Ptot): 250 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - XQFN12 (extremely thin quad flat package; 12 terminals; body size: 1.70 x 2.0 x 0.50 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Auto direction sensing for bidirectional voltage level translation
  - High-impedance OFF-state when OE is LOW
  - ESD protection: HBM Class 3B (exceeds 15,000 V for B port)
  - Partial power-down mode operation using I_OFF circuitry
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **NTB0104** is a **4-bit dual supply translating transceiver** designed for bidirectional voltage level translation. It features two 4-bit input-output ports (An and Bn), an output enable input (OE), and two supply pins (VCC(A) and VCC(B)). The device allows for seamless communication between devices operating at different voltage levels, making it suitable for interfacing low voltage nodes (1.2 V, 1.5 V, 1.8 V, 2.5 V, 3.3 V, and 5.0 V).

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Level Translation:** The NTB0104 is primarily used in applications where devices operate at different supply voltages, enabling communication between them.
- **Interfacing Systems:** It is ideal for interfacing microcontrollers, sensors, and other digital devices that require level shifting.
- **Partial Power-Down Applications:** The device is suitable for applications that require power management features, allowing for reduced power consumption during inactive states.

This component is particularly useful in mixed-voltage environments, such as in mobile devices, IoT applications, and other electronic systems where different components operate at varying voltage levels.
