**Product Code:** IPP60R099C6

- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (V_DS): 600 V
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D): 60 A
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_D): 94 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:** 
  - PG-TO220 FullPAK
- **Special Features:**
  - CoolMOS technology for high efficiency
  - Low gate charge for reduced switching losses
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** 
  - MSL 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IPP60R099C6 is a high-voltage power MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for efficient power management applications. It features a low on-resistance and high-speed switching capabilities, making it suitable for various power conversion applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supplies, converters, and inverters for efficient energy conversion.
- **Switching Applications:** Ideal for applications requiring fast switching, such as in motor drives and lighting control.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices that require efficient power regulation and management.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Utilized in industrial power supplies and automation systems for reliable performance.

This component is particularly valued for its efficiency and reliability in high-voltage applications, making it a preferred choice for engineers designing power electronic systems.
