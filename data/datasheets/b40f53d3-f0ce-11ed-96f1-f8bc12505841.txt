### Component Summary: TPS62290DRVT

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.3 V to 6.0 V
  - Output Voltage: Adjustable (typically set to 1.8 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: Up to 1000 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 15 μA (in PFM mode)
  - Shutdown Current: 0.1 to 1 μA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 6-pin WSON (DRV) package, dimensions: 2 mm x 2 mm x 0.8 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High Efficiency: Up to 96%
  - Fixed Frequency Operation: 2.25 MHz
  - Power Save Mode for light load currents
  - 100% Duty Cycle for lowest dropout
  - Voltage positioning at light loads
  - Undervoltage lockout (UVLO) threshold: 1.85 V (falling), 1.95 V (rising)
  - Thermal shutdown protection

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TPS62290** is a highly efficient synchronous step-down DC/DC converter designed for battery-powered portable applications. It is optimized to deliver up to 1000 mA output current from a single Li-ion cell, making it suitable for devices that require low power consumption and compact size. The device operates at a fixed frequency of 2.25 MHz and can automatically switch to power save mode at light load conditions to maintain high efficiency.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Phones and Smartphones:** Provides power management for various components.
- **Wireless LAN Devices:** Supplies power to wireless communication modules.
- **Portable Media Players:** Powers audio and video playback components.
- **Low Power DSP Supply:** Supports digital signal processing applications.
- **Point-of-Load (POL) Applications:** Ideal for powering specific loads directly from the power source.

The TPS62290 is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, such as in handheld devices and portable electronics. Its ability to operate over a wide input voltage range and its low quiescent current make it an excellent choice for battery-operated systems.
