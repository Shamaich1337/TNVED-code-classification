### Component Summary: STFW3N170

**Description:**
The STFW3N170 is an N-channel Power MOSFET designed for high-voltage switching applications. It utilizes STMicroelectronics' MESH OVERLAY™ process, which enhances performance compared to standard MOSFETs. This component is particularly suited for applications requiring high voltage and efficient switching capabilities.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 1700 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±30 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at T_case = 25 °C: 2.6 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at T_case = 100 °C: 1.6 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 10.4 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT) at T_case = 25 °C: 63 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 150 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (Tj): Not specified, but typically up to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - TO-3PF
- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed switching capability
  - 100% avalanche tested
  - Minimized intrinsic capacitances and gate charge (Qg)
  - Enhanced creepage distance between leads due to TO-3PF package design
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The STFW3N170 is primarily used in:
- **Switching Applications:** Ideal for high-voltage switching in power management systems.
- **Power Conversion:** Suitable for use in power supplies and converters where high efficiency and reliability are critical.
- **Inductive Load Switching:** Can be employed in circuits that require switching of inductive loads, such as motors and transformers.

This MOSFET is designed to handle high voltages and currents, making it a robust choice for various industrial and consumer electronic applications where efficient power management is essential.
