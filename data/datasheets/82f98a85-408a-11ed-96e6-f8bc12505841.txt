### Component Summary: Littelfuse 2016L075/60MR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Maximum voltage (Vmax) of 60 V DC.
- **Current Ratings**:
  - Hold current (Ihold): 0.75 A
  - Trip current (Itrip): 1.50 A
  - Maximum fault current (Imax): 20 A
- **Power Consumption**: Power dissipated in the tripped state (Pd typ.): 1.40 W.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +85°C.
- **Package Type**: Surface mount, dimensions of 1.20 mm x 2.00 mm.
- **Special Features**: 
  - Fast response to fault currents.
  - Low-profile design.
  - RoHS compliant, lead-free, and halogen-free.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 2016L075/60MR is a surface mount Polymer Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) device designed for overcurrent protection in low voltage applications (≤60V). It is resettable, meaning it can return to its normal state after the fault condition is removed, making it suitable for applications where protection is needed without the need for replacement.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Protects circuits from overcurrent conditions in various electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing**: Used in data lines to prevent damage from transient overcurrents.
- **Telecommunications**: Provides protection for low voltage telecom equipment.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Safeguards automotive electronic control modules from overcurrent.
- **Networking**: Protects powered Ethernet ports (IEEE 802.3 af) and IEEE 1394 ports.

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and where a quick recovery from fault conditions is essential. It is designed to handle temporary overcurrent situations without permanent damage, making it a reliable choice for modern electronic designs.
