### Component Summary: IR2128SPBF

**Manufacturer:** International Rectifier  
**Product Code:** IR2128SPBF

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - High Side Floating Supply Voltage (VB): -0.3V to 625V
  - High Side Floating Offset Voltage (VS): 600V
  - Logic Supply Voltage (VCC): -0.3V to 25V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output High Short Circuit Pulsed Current: 200-250 mA
  - Output Low Short Circuit Pulsed Current: 420-500 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent VBS Supply Current: 200-400 µA
  - Quiescent VCC Supply Current: 60-120 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Lead PDIP and 8-Lead SOIC
- **Special Features:**
  - Floating channel designed for bootstrap operation
  - Undervoltage lockout
  - Compatible with 3.3V, 5V, and 15V input logic
  - FAULT lead indicates shutdown has occurred
  - Output is out of phase with input
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The IR2128SPBF is a high voltage, high-speed power MOSFET and IGBT driver. It utilizes proprietary high-voltage integrated circuit (HVIC) technology and latch immune CMOS technology, enabling robust monolithic construction. The device is designed to drive N-channel power MOSFETs or IGBTs in both high-side and low-side configurations, making it suitable for various applications requiring high voltage and fast switching capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Drives:** The IR2128 is particularly suited for motor drive applications, operating within a gate drive range of 12 to 20V.
- **Automotive Applications:** It can be used in automotive systems requiring gate drive voltages between 9 to 20V.
- **Power Management:** The device is ideal for applications that require efficient power management and control, such as inverters and converters.
- **Industrial Automation:** It is also applicable in industrial automation systems where high-speed switching and reliability are critical.

This component is designed for industrial markets and is qualified according to relevant standards, ensuring its reliability in demanding environments.
