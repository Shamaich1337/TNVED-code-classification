### Component Summary: LM2902MX/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V+): 3V to 26V (Single Supply)
  - Differential Input Voltage: ±32V (±26V for LM2902-N)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 700 μA (essentially independent of supply voltage)
  - Output Current: 40 mA (maximum output current to ground)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 
    - PDIP: 1130 mW
    - CDIP: 1260 mW
    - SOIC: 800 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC (14 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Internally frequency compensated for unity gain.
  - Large DC voltage gain of 100 dB.
  - Wide bandwidth (1 MHz at unity gain).
  - Low input bias current (45 nA) and low input offset voltage (2 mV).
  - Input common-mode voltage range includes ground.
  - Large output voltage swing (0V to V+ - 1.5V).
  - Compatible with all forms of logic.
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).

#### Description:
The LM2902MX/NOPB is a low-power, quad operational amplifier designed for use in a variety of applications. It features four independent, high-gain, internally frequency compensated operational amplifiers that can operate from a single power supply over a wide range of voltages. The device is particularly noted for its low power consumption and ability to function effectively in battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Transducer Amplifiers:** Used to amplify signals from sensors and transducers.
- **DC Gain Blocks:** Provides amplification for DC signals in various circuits.
- **Conventional Op-Amp Circuits:** Suitable for standard operational amplifier configurations, including inverting and non-inverting amplifiers.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Used in applications requiring signal filtering and conditioning.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Ideal for interfacing with analog sensors in data acquisition systems.

The LM2902MX/NOPB is versatile and can be utilized in both consumer electronics and industrial applications, making it a valuable component in electronic design.
