### Component Summary: HMC525ALC4

**Description:**
The HMC525ALC4 is a compact Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) monolithic microwave integrated circuit (MMIC) designed as an I/Q mixer. It operates within a frequency range of 4 GHz to 8.5 GHz and can function as either an image reject mixer or a single sideband (SSB) upconverter. The device utilizes two standard double balanced mixer cells and a 90° hybrid, fabricated using a metal semiconductor field effect transistor (MESFET) process. Its compact design eliminates the need for wire bonding, allowing for surface-mount manufacturing techniques.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - LO Input Power: 25 dBm (max)
  - RF Input Power: 20 dBm (max)
  - IF Input Power: 20 dBm (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - IF Source and Sink Current: 2 mA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Continuous Power Dissipation: 560 mW (at 85°C, derated at 6.22 mW/°C above 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage: -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 24-terminal, 4 mm × 4 mm Ceramic Leadless Chip Carrier (LCC)

- **Special Features:**
  - Passive operation (no DC bias required)
  - High isolation: LO to RF isolation of 47 dB (typical)
  - Low conversion loss: 8 dB (typical)
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC525ALC4 is suitable for various applications, including:
- Microwave and very small aperture terminal (VSAT) radios
- Test equipment for RF applications
- Point-to-point radio communications
- Military electronic warfare systems, including electronic countermeasures and command, control, communications, and intelligence (C3I) systems.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high-frequency signal processing, where compact size and efficient performance are critical.
