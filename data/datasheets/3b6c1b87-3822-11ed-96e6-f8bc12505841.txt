### Component Summary: HMC451LC3TR

**Description:**
The HMC451LC3 is a GaAs PHEMT (Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor) MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) medium power amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 5 to 20 GHz. It is housed in a leadless RoHS compliant SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package, making it suitable for modern manufacturing techniques. The amplifier is optimized for high efficiency and provides significant gain and output power, making it ideal for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd1 = Vdd2): +5V (max +5.5V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 114 mA at +5V
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1.1 W (derated at 12.4 mW/°C above 85 °C)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 16-Terminal Ceramic Leadless Chip Carrier (LCC), dimensions: 3 x 3 mm
  
- **Special Features:**
  - 50 Ohm matched input/output
  - No external components required for operation
  - DC blocking on RF I/O
  - RoHS compliant
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC451LC3 is well-suited for a variety of applications, including:
- Microwave Radio and VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal)
- Military and Space communications
- Test Equipment and Sensors
- Fiber Optics
- Local Oscillator (LO) Driver for HMC Mixers

This amplifier is particularly useful in systems requiring medium power amplification with high linearity and efficiency, making it a versatile choice for RF and microwave applications.
