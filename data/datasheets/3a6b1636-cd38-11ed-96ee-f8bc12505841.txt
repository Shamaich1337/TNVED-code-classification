  - Power Supply Voltage Range: 2.7 V to 10 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Current Drain: 125 µA at 25°C
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Quiescent Current: 125 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -25°C to 85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT-23 (3-pin)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Calibrated linear scale factor of 10 mV/°C
  - 600 mV DC offset for negative temperature readings
  - UL Recognized Component
  - Shutdown capability due to low power consumption
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LM61BIM3X/NOPB is a precision integrated-circuit temperature sensor designed to operate over a temperature range of -30°C to 100°C. It provides an output voltage that is linearly proportional to temperature, with a scale factor of 10 mV/°C and a DC offset of 600 mV. This offset allows the sensor to read negative temperatures without requiring a negative supply voltage. The device is characterized by its low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-operated applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The LM61BIM3X/NOPB is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Cellular Phones**: For temperature monitoring and management.
- **Computers**: To monitor internal temperatures for thermal management.
- **Power Supply Modules**: For temperature compensation and monitoring.
- **Battery Management Systems**: To ensure safe operating temperatures.
- **HVAC Systems**: For environmental control and monitoring.
- **Printers and FAX Machines**: To prevent overheating.
- **Disk Drives and Appliances**: For temperature regulation and safety.

This component is ideal for applications requiring accurate temperature sensing in a compact form factor, particularly where low power consumption is critical.
