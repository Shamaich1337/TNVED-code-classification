### Component Summary: ERA-2SM+ (Mini-Circuits)

**Description:**
The ERA-2SM+ is a wideband monolithic amplifier designed for high dynamic range applications. It operates over a frequency range of DC to 6 GHz and is fabricated using InGaP HBT technology. The amplifier is housed in a Micro-X package and is RoHS compliant. It features a Darlington configuration, ensuring unconditionally stable performance with low variation over temperature.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.20 V to 3.6 V (typical 3.4 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 40 mA (recommended), up to 75 mA (absolute maximum)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 330 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Micro-X package (Case Style: WW107)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - Low performance variation over temperature
  - Transient protected
  - Aqueous washable
  - Protected by US Patent 6,943,629
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020C

**Typical Applications:**
The ERA-2SM+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular/PCS/3G base stations
- CATV (Cable Television), cable modems, and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- Fixed wireless and WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network)
- Microwave radio and test equipment

This amplifier is ideal for use in systems requiring reliable amplification across a wide frequency range, making it a versatile choice for both commercial and industrial applications.
