### Component Summary: HX1332CNL

**Description:**
The HX1332CNL is an Ethernet module designed for medical safety applications. It is part of Pulse Electronics' series of Ethernet transformers that comply with IEEE standards for fast Ethernet and 1 Gigabit Ethernet. This component is specifically engineered to operate within an industrial temperature range and provides reinforced isolation for medical applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Working Voltage: 400 Vrms
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - DC Current: 350 mA at 25°C
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Power Supported: 15W (Power over Ethernet, PD/PSE)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:** 
  - Tube packaging (with option for tape and reel by adding 'T' suffix)
- **Special Features:**
  - Reinforced Medical Safety isolation: 1 MOPP/MOOP
  - Compliance with UL60601-1 and IEC62368-1 standards
  - Compatible with all major PHYs
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** 
  - Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The HX1332CNL is primarily used in medical devices that require Ethernet connectivity while ensuring safety and compliance with medical standards. Typical applications include:
- Medical imaging systems
- Patient monitoring equipment
- Telemedicine devices
- Any medical equipment requiring reliable data transmission over Ethernet with Power over Ethernet (PoE) capabilities.

This component is particularly suited for environments where both data integrity and patient safety are critical, making it an essential part of modern medical technology.
