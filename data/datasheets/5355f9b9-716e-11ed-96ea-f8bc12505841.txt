### Component Summary: MC74HC595ADTG

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage Range: 2.0 to 6.0 V
  - DC Supply Voltage (Referenced to GND): –0.5 to +7.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Input Current, per Pin: ±20 mA
  - DC Output Current, per Pin: ±35 mA
  - DC Supply Current (VCC and GND Pins): ±75 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation in Still Air: 
    - SOIC Package: 500 mW
    - TSSOP Package: 450 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: –65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: –55 to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP-16 (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Output Drive Capability: 15 LSTTL Loads
  - High Noise Immunity characteristic of CMOS devices
  - Improved propagation delays and 50% lower quiescent power compared to previous versions
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free, and RoHS Compliant
  - AEC-Q100 Qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1

**Description of the Component:**
The MC74HC595ADTG is an 8-bit serial-input/serial or parallel-output shift register with latched 3-state outputs. It consists of an 8-bit shift register and an 8-bit D-type latch, allowing for both serial and parallel data handling. The device features independent clock inputs for the shift register and latch, as well as an asynchronous reset for the shift register.

**Typical Applications:**
The MC74HC595ADTG is commonly used in applications that require data storage and transfer, such as:
- **Microcontroller interfacing:** It directly interfaces with SPI serial data ports on CMOS microprocessors and microcontrollers.
- **Data communication:** Used in digital communication systems for shifting data in and out.
- **LED control:** Often employed in LED display systems for controlling multiple outputs with minimal pin usage.
- **Signal processing:** Useful in applications where data needs to be shifted and latched for processing.

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient data handling and control in embedded systems, automotive electronics, and consumer electronics.
