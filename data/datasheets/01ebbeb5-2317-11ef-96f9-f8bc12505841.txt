### Component Summary: D1213A-01WS-7

**Description:**
The D1213A-01WS-7 is a low capacitance Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode array designed for ESD protection. It features a single channel of protection and is particularly suited for high-speed data ports such as USB 2.0, IEEE 1394, Serial ATA, DVI, HDMI, and PCI. This component is compliant with RoHS standards and is qualified for high reliability according to AEC-Q101 standards.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Working Voltage (VRWM): Up to 3.3 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.0 V at 1 mA

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 5 A (8/20 μs pulse)
  - Reverse Current (IR): 0.1 μA (typical) at VR = VRWM = 3.3 V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Package Power Dissipation (PD): 250 mW
  - Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 500 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD323 (Small Outline Diode package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low channel input capacitance of 0.85 pF (typical)
  - ESD protection ratings: ±15 kV (air discharge), ±8 kV (contact discharge) per IEC 61000-4-2
  - Halogen and Antimony Free, "Green" device
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 1 per J-STD-020

**Typical Applications:**
The D1213A-01WS-7 is primarily used in applications requiring ESD protection for high-speed data lines. Typical applications include:
- USB 2.0 interfaces
- HDMI and DVI connections
- Serial ATA (SATA) interfaces
- General-purpose ESD protection in consumer electronics and communication devices

This component is ideal for protecting sensitive electronic circuits from voltage spikes and electrostatic discharge, ensuring reliable operation in various electronic applications.
