### Component Summary: SMF-28e Optical Fiber

**Manufacturer:** SEAGNOL PHOTONICS CO., LTD  
**Product Code:** SMF-28e

#### Key Specifications:
- **Wavelength Range:** 1310 nm, 1550 nm, 1625 nm
- **Maximum Attenuation:**
  - 1310 nm: ≤ 0.32 dB/km
  - 1550 nm: ≤ 0.18 dB/km
  - 1625 nm: ≤ 0.20 dB/km
- **Point Discontinuity:** ≤ 0.05 dB at 1310 nm and 1550 nm
- **Macrobend Loss:** 
  - 1550 nm: ≤ 0.50 dB for 10 turns on a 10 mm mandrel
  - 1625 nm: ≤ 1.5 dB for 10 turns on a 10 mm mandrel
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -60°C to +85°C
- **Core Diameter:** 8.2 µm
- **Cladding Diameter:** 125.0 ± 0.7 µm
- **Coating Diameter:** 242 ± 5 µm
- **Numerical Aperture:** 0.14
- **Zero Dispersion Wavelength (λo):** 1317 nm
- **Zero Dispersion Slope (So):** ≤ 0.092 ps/(nm²•km)
- **Polarization Mode Dispersion (PMD):**
  - Link Design Value: ≤ 0.04 ps/√km
  - Maximum Individual Fiber PMD: ≤ 0.1 ps/√km
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The SMF-28e is a single-mode optical fiber designed for high-performance telecommunications and data transmission applications. It is compliant with ITU-T G.652.D standards and features enhanced low-loss and bend performance technologies. This fiber is engineered to provide superior specifications for attenuation, macrobend loss, and polarization mode dispersion, making it suitable for both new network deployments and upgrades to existing infrastructures.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Used in long-distance communication networks due to its low attenuation and high bandwidth capabilities.
- **Data Centers:** Ideal for high-speed data transmission in data centers where space and performance are critical.
- **Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH):** Commonly deployed in residential broadband networks to deliver high-speed internet access.
- **Cable Television (CATV):** Utilized in cable TV networks for signal transmission.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for environments requiring robust performance under varying temperature and humidity conditions.

The SMF-28e fiber's advanced specifications and versatility make it a preferred choice for a wide range of optical communication applications.
