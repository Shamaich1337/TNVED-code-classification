The following guidelines help theuser design acircuit with maximum rejection ofoutside EMI and\nminimum generation ofunwanted EMI.\nParasitic inductance can bereduced bykeeping thepower path components close together and keeping the\narea oftheloops small, onwhich high currents travel. Short, thick traces orcopper pours (shapes) arebest. In\nparticular, theswitch node (where L1,D1,and theSWpinconnect) must bejustlarge enough toconnect all\nthree components without excessive heating from thecurrent itcarries. The LM2734x andLM2734x-Q1 operate\nintwodistinct cycles (see Figure 27)whose high current paths areshown inFigure 58.\nFigure 58.Buck Converter Current Loops\nThedark grey, inner loop represents thehigh current path during theMOSFET on-time. Thelight grey, outer loop\nrepresents thehigh current path during theoff-time.\n10.1.2 Ground Plane andShape Routing\nThe diagram ofFigure 58isalso useful foranalyzing theflow ofcontinuous current versus theflow ofpulsating\ncurrents. The circuit paths with current flow during both theon-time andoff-time areconsidered tobecontinuous\ncurrent, while those that carry current during theon-time oroff-time only arepulsating currents. Preference in\nrouting must begiven tothepulsating current paths, asthese aretheportions ofthecircuit most likely toemit\nEMI. The ground plane ofaPCB isaconductor andreturn path, anditissusceptible tonoise injection justlike\nanyother circuit path. Thepath between theinput source andtheinput capacitor andthepath between thecatch\ndiode andtheload areexamples ofcontinuous current paths. Incontrast, thepath between thecatch diode and\ntheinput capacitor carries alarge pulsating current. This path must berouted with ashort, thick shape,\npreferably onthecomponent side ofthePCB. Multiple vias inparallel must beused right atthepadoftheinput\ncapacitor toconnect thecomponent side shapes totheground plane. Asecond pulsating current loop that is\noften ignored isthegate drive loop formed bytheSWandBOOST pins andboost capacitor CBOOST .Tominimize\nthisloop andtheEMI itgenerates, keep CBOOST close totheSWandBOOST pins.\n10.1.3 FBLoop\nThe FBpinisahigh-impedance input, and theloop created byR2,theFBpinand ground must bemade as\nsmall aspossible tomaximize noise rejection. ### Component Summary: LM27342QMY/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (PVIN, AVIN): 3V to 20V
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings: 24V for AVIN, PVIN; 28V for BOOST
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 2A
  - Switch Current Limit: 2.5A (minimum)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 2.4 mA (not switching), 70 nA (shutdown)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 10-Pin WSON and 10-Pin MSOP-PowerPAD
- **Special Features:**
  - High Switching Frequency: 2 MHz
  - Frequency Synchronization: 1 MHz to 2.35 MHz
  - Internal Voltage Reference Accuracy: 1%
  - Thermal Shutdown and Overvoltage Protection
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (WSON), Level 3 (MSOP-PowerPAD) per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LM27342QMY/NOPB is a high-frequency, peak current-mode PWM step-down DC-DC converter designed to deliver up to 2A of output current. It operates with a wide input voltage range of 3V to 20V and features a high switching frequency of 2 MHz, allowing for the use of small surface-mount components. The device includes an internal NMOS switch with a low on-resistance of 150 mΩ, which enhances power efficiency, achieving efficiencies of up to 90%.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:**
  - Local DC-DC conversion for processors and microcontrollers.
- **Consumer Electronics:**
  - Power supplies for set-top boxes, USB powered devices, and DSL modems.
- **Automotive Applications:**
  - Core power supplies in automotive electronics and HDDs.
- **Telecommunications:**
  - Radio power supplies and other communication devices.

The LM27342 is particularly suited for applications requiring compact designs with high efficiency and fast transient response, making it ideal for modern electronic devices where space and power efficiency are critical.
