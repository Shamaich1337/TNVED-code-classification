### Component Summary: FBMH4532HM132-T

**Manufacturer:** TAIYO YUDEN  
**Component Type:** Ferrite Bead Inductor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Impedance:** 1300 Ω ± 25 % at 100 MHz
- **Rated Current (max):** 3 A (operating temperature range: -40 to +125 °C)
- **DC Resistance (max):** 60 mΩ
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 °C to +125 °C (including self-generated heat)
- **Package Type:** 1812/4532 (EIA/JIS)
- **Dimensions:** 
  - Length: 4.5 ±0.4 mm
  - Width: 3.2 ±0.3 mm
  - Thickness: 3.2 ±0.3 mm
  - Terminal Pitch: 0.9 ±0.6 mm
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **RoHS Compliance:** Yes (10 substances)
- **REACH Compliance:** Yes (223 substances)
- **Halogen Free:** Yes

#### Description:
The FBMH4532HM132-T is a ferrite bead inductor designed to suppress high-frequency noise in electronic circuits. Ferrite beads are passive components that provide impedance to high-frequency signals while allowing lower frequency signals to pass through with minimal attenuation. This component is particularly effective in filtering out unwanted electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI).

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices such as mobile phones, PCs, and home appliances to improve signal integrity and reduce noise.
- **Office Equipment:** Helps in maintaining clean power supply and signal lines in printers, copiers, and other office devices.
- **Communication Equipment:** Employed in base stations and communication devices to ensure reliable signal transmission by minimizing noise.
- **AV Equipment:** Utilized in audio and video devices to enhance performance by filtering out noise that can affect sound and image quality.

This component is suitable for general electronic applications but should not be used in safety-critical systems without proper validation and evaluation.
