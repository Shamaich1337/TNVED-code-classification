### Component Summary: 4N35-300E (BROADCOM LIMITED)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CEO): 30 V
  - Emitter-Collector Voltage (V_ECO): 7 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 70 V
  - Isolation Voltage (V_iso): 3550 Vrms (for 1 minute)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (I_F): 60 mA
  - Collector Current (I_C): 100 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Input Power Dissipation (P_I): 100 mW
  - Collector Power Dissipation: 300 mW
  - Total Power Dissipation: 350 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_S): -55°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature (T_A): -55°C to +100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Dual-in-line package (DIP-6)

- **Special Features:**
  - High Current Transfer Ratio (CTR: min. 100% at I_F = 10 mA, V_CE = 10 V)
  - Fast response time (typ. 3 µs at V_CE = 10 V, I_C = 2 mA, R_L = 100 Ω)
  - UL and CSA approved
  - IEC/EN/DIN EN 60747-5-2 approved
  - Options for lead spacing and surface mounting available

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **4N35-300E** is a general-purpose optocoupler that consists of a light-emitting diode (LED) optically coupled to a phototransistor. This component is designed to provide electrical isolation between its input and output while allowing signal transmission. It is packaged in a 6-pin dual-in-line package (DIP) and is available in various configurations, including options for surface mounting.

#### Typical Applications:
- **I/O Interfaces for Computers:** Used to isolate and transmit signals between different parts of a computer system.
- **System Appliances and Measuring Instruments:** Provides isolation in measurement and control systems to protect sensitive components.
- **Signal Transmission Between Circuits of Different Potentials:** Ideal for applications where signals need to be transmitted across different voltage levels or ground potentials, ensuring safety and integrity of the signals.

This optocoupler is particularly useful in applications requiring high isolation voltage and fast response times, making it suitable for a wide range of electronic and industrial applications.
