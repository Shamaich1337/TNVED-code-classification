**Manufacturer:** ON Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** BC817-25LT1G  
**Type:** NPN Silicon Transistor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 45 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 50 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (FR-5 Board, TA = 25 °C): 225 mW (derates to 1.8 mW/°C)
  - Total Device Dissipation (Alumina Substrate, TA = 25 °C): 300 mW (derates to 2.4 mW/°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (TO-236)

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BC817-25LT1G is a general-purpose NPN silicon transistor designed for a variety of switching and amplification applications. It is characterized by its high voltage and current ratings, making it suitable for use in low to medium power applications. The device is packaged in a compact SOT-23 form factor, which is ideal for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Processing:** Used in audio and radio frequency applications for amplification.
- **Switching Applications:** Suitable for driving loads such as relays, motors, and LEDs.
- **Automotive Electronics:** AEC-Q101 qualification makes it suitable for automotive applications requiring high reliability.
- **General Purpose Amplification:** Can be used in various electronic circuits for signal amplification.

This transistor is versatile and can be utilized in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial applications, providing reliable performance in a compact package.
