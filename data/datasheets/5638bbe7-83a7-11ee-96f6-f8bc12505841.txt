### Component Summary: TPS62823DLCR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.4V to 5.5V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Adjustable from 0.6V to 4V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 3A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQ): 4µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current (ISD): 0.05µA to 0.5µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - QFN-8 (2mm x 1.5mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - DCS-Control™ topology for fast transient response
  - 1% feedback voltage accuracy
  - Internal soft-start circuitry
  - Power Good (PG) signal
  - Undervoltage lockout and thermal shutdown protection
  - HICCUP short-circuit protection
  - 100% duty-cycle mode operation
  - Low RDS(on) for internal switches: 26mΩ (high-side), 25mΩ (low-side)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TPS62823** is a synchronous step-down DC-DC converter designed for high efficiency and low quiescent current. It is capable of delivering up to 3A of output current from a wide input voltage range of 2.4V to 5.5V. The device utilizes DCS-Control™ topology, which allows for fast transient response and seamless transitions between power save mode and normal operation. The output voltage can be adjusted from 0.6V to 4V, making it suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Point of Load (POL) supply** in portable and battery-powered devices
- **Factory and building automation**
- **Mobile computing** and networking cards
- **Solid-state drives (SSDs)**
- **Data terminals** and point-of-sale systems
- **Servers**, projectors, and printers

The TPS62823 is particularly well-suited for applications requiring efficient power management in compact designs, where space and thermal performance are critical. Its low quiescent current makes it ideal for battery-operated devices, ensuring minimal power loss during idle states.
