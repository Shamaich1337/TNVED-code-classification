### Component Summary: P6SMB400A

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** P6SMB400A  
**Component Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)  
**Package Type:** DO-214AA (SMB)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 380V (Min) to 420V (Max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 400V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): 1.1A
  - Maximum Reverse Surge Current (I_FSM): 400A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 600W (10/1000 μs waveform)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low-profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Excellent clamping capability with low incremental surge resistance
  - Fast response time
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The P6SMB400A is a unidirectional surface mount transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is particularly effective in clamping voltage spikes, thereby safeguarding integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electronics Protection:** Used in consumer electronics, computers, industrial equipment, and telecommunications to protect against voltage transients.
- **Power Management:** Helps in managing and protecting power circuits from overvoltage conditions.
- **Signal Line Protection:** Ensures the integrity of signal lines in sensor units and other sensitive electronic devices.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited and reliability is critical, making it suitable for a wide range of electronic devices.
