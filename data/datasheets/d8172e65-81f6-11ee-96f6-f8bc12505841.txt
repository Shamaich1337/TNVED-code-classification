### Component Summary: BSC028N06NSATMA1 (Infineon Technologies)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 60 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 132 A (at 25°C)
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulse): 528 A (at 25°C)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (Ptot): 83 W (at 25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (Tj, Tstg): -55°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - PG-TDSON-8 (SuperSO8)
- **Special Features:**
  - N-channel MOSFET
  - 100% avalanche tested
  - Superior thermal resistance
  - Pb-free lead plating; RoHS compliant
  - Halogen-free according to IEC61249-2-21
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Qualified according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The BSC028N06NSATMA1 is a high-performance N-channel MOSFET designed for use in synchronous rectification and other high-efficiency switching applications. It features low on-state resistance (RDS(on)) of 2.8 mΩ, which minimizes power losses during operation. The device is optimized for high-performance switch-mode power supplies (SMPS) and is capable of handling high current loads, making it suitable for demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient voltage regulation and conversion.
- **Synchronous Rectification:** Ideal for applications requiring efficient rectification in DC-DC converters.
- **Motor Control:** Can be utilized in motor drive circuits where high current handling and fast switching are essential.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in power supplies for laptops, desktops, and other electronic devices requiring efficient power conversion.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where thermal performance and efficiency are critical, contributing to reduced energy consumption and improved system reliability.
