### Component Summary: B8CR1M39RH

**Manufacturer:** Beijing Microelectronics Technology Institute  
**Type:** Radiation-Hardened Static SRAM

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Core Supply Voltage (VDD1): 1.2 V (operating range: 1.08 V to 1.32 V)
  - I/O Supply Voltage (VDD2): 3.3 V (operating range: 2.97 V to 3.63 V)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 2 W
  - Supply Current (Read Operation at 50 MHz): Up to 110 mA (VDD1 = 1.32 V)
  - Standby Supply Current: 90 mA (at 0 Hz)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption due to CMOS technology.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Case Temperature: -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 84-lead Ceramic Quad Flatpack (CQFP84)

- **Special Features:**
  - 20 ns maximum read time
  - 10 ns minimum write time
  - Asynchronous operation
  - CMOS compatible inputs and outputs with a three-state bidirectional data bus
  - Enhanced ESD protection (better than 2000 V)
  - Radiation hardness: Total dose of 100 K Rad (Si), SEL immune > 75 MeV·cm²/mg, SEU error rate of 1E-10 errors/bit-day in geosynchronous orbit.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The B8CR1M39RH is a high-performance, radiation-hardened CMOS static SRAM organized as 1M words by 40 bits. It is designed for asynchronous operation, requiring no external clocks, making it suitable for high-speed applications in radiation-prone environments. The device combines fast access times with low power consumption, making it ideal for space and military applications where reliability and performance are critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Storage:** Used as a memory component in systems requiring fast data access and storage, particularly in space applications.
- **High-Speed Systems:** Suitable for applications in environments with high radiation levels, such as satellites and other aerospace systems.
- **Signal Processing:** Can be utilized in systems that require quick data retrieval and processing, such as radar and communication systems.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for use in embedded applications where low power consumption and high reliability are essential.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the B8CR1M39RH SRAM, highlighting its suitability for demanding environments.
