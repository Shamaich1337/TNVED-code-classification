- **Package Type:** TSSOP16
- **Special Features:**
  - 24-bit resolution with no missing codes
  - Integrated 50Hz and 60Hz rejection filters
  - Programmable gain from 1 to 128
  - Compatible with SPI interface
  - Four input channels
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The MS1242 is a high-precision, low-power delta-sigma analog-to-digital converter (ADC) designed for applications requiring accurate digital representation of analog signals. It features a 24-bit resolution with no missing codes and an effective resolution of 21 bits, making it suitable for high-fidelity signal processing.

**Typical Applications:**
The MS1242 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- Industrial process control
- Weight scales
- Liquid and gas chromatography
- Blood analysis
- Smart transmitters
- Portable instrumentation

This ADC is particularly beneficial in scenarios where high precision and low power consumption are critical, such as in medical devices and industrial automation systems. Its programmable gain and integrated filters enhance its versatility in handling different types of analog signals.
