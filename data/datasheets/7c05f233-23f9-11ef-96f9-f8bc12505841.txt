### Component Summary: TS5A23159RSER

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
  - Analog Voltage (VNO, VNC, VCOM): 0 to VCC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current through VCC: 100 mA
  - On-State Switch Current (INC): -200 mA to 200 mA
  - On-State Peak Switch Current: -400 mA to 400 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 10 nA (typical) at VCC = 5 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in VSSOP (10 pins) and UQFN (10 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low ON-State Resistance: 1 Ω
  - Break-Before-Make Switching
  - Isolation in Power-Down Mode (VCC = 0)
  - Control Inputs are 5.5-V Tolerant
  - Low Charge Injection
  - Excellent ON-State Resistance Matching
  - ESD Performance: 2000 V (Human Body Model), 1000 V (Charged Device Model)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The TS5A23159 is a bidirectional 2-channel single-pole double-throw (SPDT) analog switch designed for low-voltage applications. It operates from a supply voltage range of 1.65 V to 5.5 V and features low ON-state resistance, making it suitable for high-performance signal routing. The device includes a break-before-make feature to prevent signal distortion during switching.

**Typical Applications:**
The TS5A23159 is commonly used in various portable applications, including:
- Cell Phones
- PDAs (Personal Digital Assistants)
- Portable Instrumentation
- Audio and Video Signal Routing
- Low-Voltage Data-Acquisition Systems
- Communication Circuits
- Modems
- Hard Drives
- Computer Peripherals
- Wireless Terminals and Peripherals

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal switching with minimal distortion and low power consumption, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic devices.
