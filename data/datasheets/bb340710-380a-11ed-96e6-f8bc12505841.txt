#### Description:
The F17723472000 is a **Class X2 interference suppression film capacitor** designed for high stability applications. It is a radial capacitor made from metallized polyester film, suitable for use in AC applications where continuous operation across the line is required. The capacitor is characterized by its robust construction and high voltage ratings, making it ideal for suppressing electromagnetic interference in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Interference Suppression:** Used in applications where electromagnetic interference (EMI) needs to be minimized, particularly in AC power circuits.
- **Power Supply Circuits:** Commonly found in power supply units to filter noise and stabilize voltage.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Utilized in devices such as televisions, computers, and appliances to ensure compliance with electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standards.
- **Automotive Applications:** AEC-Q200 qualification makes it suitable for automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.

This capacitor is particularly well-suited for applications requiring high stability and reliability under continuous operation conditions, making it a preferred choice in both consumer and industrial electronics.
