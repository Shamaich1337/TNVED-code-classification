### Component Summary: TMP461AIRUNT-S

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (V+): 1.7 V to 3.6 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Operating Current: 35 µA (1 Sample Per Second)
  - Shutdown Current: 3 µA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Quiescent Current: 240 µA (local sensor active), 400 µA (remote sensor active)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 10-Pin WQFN (2.00 mm x 2.00 mm)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Built-in thermal transistor and diode
  - Overtemperature shutdown
  - Series resistance cancellation
  - Programmable digital filter
  - Diode fault detection
  - SMBus compatible with pin-programmable addresses
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - MSL Level 2 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TMP461 is a high-accuracy, low-power digital temperature sensor that features both local and remote temperature sensing capabilities. It utilizes a built-in local temperature sensor and can interface with external remote temperature sensors, typically discrete NPN or PNP transistors or diodes. The device provides temperature readings in a 12-bit digital format with a resolution of 0.0625°C. It communicates via a two-wire SMBus interface, allowing for easy integration into various systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Processor Temperature Monitoring**: Used in microprocessors and ASICs to monitor temperature and prevent overheating.
- **Telecommunication Equipment**: Ensures optimal operating temperatures in communication devices.
- **Servers and Personal Computers**: Monitors temperatures to maintain performance and reliability.
- **Precision Instruments**: Provides accurate temperature readings for scientific and industrial applications.
- **Test Equipment**: Used in calibration and testing setups where precise temperature measurements are critical.
- **Smart Batteries**: Monitors battery temperatures to enhance safety and performance.
- **Embedded Applications**: Integrated into various embedded systems for temperature monitoring.
- **LED Lighting Thermal Control**: Manages the thermal performance of LED lighting systems.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the TMP461 temperature sensor, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical applications in various fields.
