#### Description:
The CC0603KRX7R7BB104 is a surface-mount ceramic multilayer capacitor (MLCC) from YAGEO's X7R series. It is designed for general-purpose applications and offers high capacitance in a compact form factor. The X7R dielectric material provides stable capacitance over a wide temperature range, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in PCs, hard disk drives, game consoles, and mobile phones.
- **Audio/Video Equipment**: Commonly found in DVDs and video cameras.
- **Data Processing**: Employed in various data processing applications where stable capacitance is required.
- **Power Management**: Utilized in power supply circuits for decoupling and filtering applications.

This capacitor is ideal for applications requiring reliable performance in compact spaces, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
