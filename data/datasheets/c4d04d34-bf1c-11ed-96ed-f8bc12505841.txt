Documentation Feedback Copyright ©2008 –2016, Texas Instruments Incorporated9Detailed Description\n9.1 Overview\nThe TPS255x and TPS255x-1 arecurrent-limited, power-distribution switches using N-channel MOSFETs for\napplications where short circuits orheavy capacitive loads areencountered and provide upto1.5Aof\ncontinuous load current. These devices allow theuser toprogram thecurrent-limit threshold between 75mAand\n1.7A(typical) through anexternal resistor. Additional device shutdown features include overtemperature\nprotection and reverse-voltage protection. The device incorporates aninternal charge pump and gate drive\ncircuitry necessary todrive theN-channel MOSFET. The charge pump supplies power tothedriver circuit and\nprovides thenecessary voltage topullthegate oftheMOSFET above thesource. The charge pump operates\nfrom input voltages aslowas2.5Vandrequires little supply current. The driver controls thegate voltage ofthe\npower switch. The driver incorporates circuitry thatcontrols theriseand falltimes oftheoutput voltage tolimit\nlarge current andvoltage surges andprovides built-in soft-start functionality. #### Description:
The **TPS2553** is a precision adjustable current-limited power-distribution switch designed for applications requiring reliable current limiting and protection against short circuits and heavy capacitive loads. It utilizes N-channel MOSFET technology to provide up to 1.5A of continuous load current. The device features programmable current-limit thresholds, allowing users to set limits between 75mA and 1.7A using an external resistor. The TPS2553 also includes a latch-off feature that disables the power switch during overcurrent or reverse-voltage conditions, ensuring protection for downstream devices.

- **Digital TVs and Set-Top Boxes:** Provides reliable power management for consumer electronics.
