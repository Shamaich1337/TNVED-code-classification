### Component Summary: Qorvo 885135

**Component Type:**  
- **Type:** Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Band-pass Filter  
- **Primary Function:** The 885135 is designed to filter specific frequency bands, allowing for the coexistence of WiFi and LTE signals while providing high rejection of unwanted frequencies.

---

### Key Specifications:

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not specified in the datasheet.
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified in the datasheet.
- **Power Consumption:** Capable of handling up to 1 W of power.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - **Storage Temperature:** -55 to +125 °C
  - **Operating Temperature:** -40 to +95 °C
- **Package Type:** Ceramic Chip Scale Package (CSP), dimensions 1.70 x 1.30 x 0.46 mm.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not applicable (hermetic package).
- **Special Features:**
  - High rejection at 2390 MHz and 2483.5 MHz.
  - Hermetically sealed and RoHS compliant (Pb-free).
  - Industry-leading small size and power handling capabilities.

---

### Description:
The **885135** is a high-performance BAW band-pass filter that operates at a center frequency of 2439.5 MHz. It features extremely steep skirts, low insertion loss in the WiFi band, and high rejection in adjacent LTE bands. This filter is particularly suited for high-power WLAN access points and small cell base stations, enabling the simultaneous operation of WiFi and LTE signals in close proximity.

---

### Typical Applications:
- **High-Power WLAN Access Points:** Enhances performance by filtering out unwanted signals.
- **Small Cells:** Supports high-density environments where both LTE and WiFi signals are present.
- **Band-Edge Filtering:** Reduces interference from WiFi signal emissions at 2390 MHz and 2483.5 MHz.
- **ISM Band Applications:** Suitable for devices like smart meters and portable hotspots.
- **Mobile Routers:** Ensures reliable connectivity by managing signal interference.
