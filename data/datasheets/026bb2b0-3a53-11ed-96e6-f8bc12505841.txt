### Component Summary: IV1Q07015T4G

**Description:**
The IV1Q07015T4G is a 750V, 15mΩ Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFET designed for high-efficiency power applications. It features low on-resistance and high-speed switching capabilities, making it suitable for demanding environments such as electric vehicles (EVs) and industrial motor drives.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 750 V
  - Maximum DC Gate-Source Voltage (VGSmax): -5 to 22 V
  - Maximum Spike Voltage (VGSmax Spike): -10 to 25 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 156 A at TC=25°C, 112 A at TC=100°C
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 390 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 553 W at TC=25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 175 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to 175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO247-4

- **Special Features:**
  - High blocking voltage with low on-resistance
  - High-speed switching with low capacitance
  - High operating junction temperature capability
  - Robust intrinsic body diode
  - Kelvin gate input for easier driver circuit design

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The IV1Q07015T4G is commonly used in:
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Motor drivers for electric vehicles (EV/HEV)
- High voltage DC/DC converters
- Switch mode power supplies (SMPS)

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and high reliability under varying thermal conditions. Its characteristics make it particularly suitable for modern power electronics where efficiency and thermal performance are critical.
