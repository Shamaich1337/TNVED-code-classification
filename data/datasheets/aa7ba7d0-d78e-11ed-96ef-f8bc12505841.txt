### Component Summary: 1N5819HW-7-F

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Document Number:** DS30217 Rev. 16  
**Date:** June 2014

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 40 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 28 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (I_O): 1.0 A
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FRM): 1.5 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 25 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 450 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -65 to +125 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD123
- **Special Features:**
  - High surge capability
  - Low power loss and high efficiency
  - Guard ring die construction for transient protection
  - Totally lead-free and fully RoHS compliant
  - Halogen and antimony-free ("Green" device)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **1N5819HW** is a **surface mount Schottky barrier rectifier** designed for low voltage, high frequency applications. It is primarily used to rectify alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) in various electronic circuits. The Schottky design allows for lower forward voltage drop and faster switching speeds compared to standard diodes, making it suitable for high-efficiency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Low Voltage, High Frequency Inverters:** The 1N5819HW is ideal for use in inverters that require efficient rectification of high-frequency signals.
- **Free Wheeling Diodes:** It can be used in circuits to prevent back EMF from inductive loads, protecting other components from voltage spikes.
- **Polarity Protection:** The diode can be employed in power supply circuits to prevent damage from reverse polarity connections.
- **Power Management:** Its low forward voltage drop contributes to overall system efficiency in power management applications.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications where space is limited, and efficiency is critical, such as in portable electronics, power supplies, and automotive systems.
