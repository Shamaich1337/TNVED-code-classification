### Component Summary: VS-15ETL06-M3

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** VS-15ETL06-M3  
**Document Number:** 96175  
**Revision Date:** 23-Nov-17  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 600 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (I_F(AV)): 15 A
  - Non-repetitive Peak Surge Current (I_FSM): 250 A
  - Peak Repetitive Forward Current (I_FM): 30 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (V_F at I_F): 0.85 V (typical at 15 A, T_J = 150 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -65 °C to +175 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_Stg): -65 °C to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 2L TO-220AC
- **Special Features:**
  - Hyperfast recovery time
  - Benchmark ultralow forward voltage drop
  - Low leakage current
  - Designed and qualified according to JEDEC JESD 47
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The VS-15ETL06-M3 is an ultralow forward voltage drop (V_F) hyperfast rectifier diode designed for use in power factor correction (PFC) applications, particularly in discontinuous mode. It features a soft-switching design that minimizes conduction losses, optimizes stored charge, and reduces switching losses, making it suitable for high-efficiency power supply designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **AC/DC Switching Power Supplies (SMPS):** Suitable for power supplies ranging from 70 W to 400 W, including:
  - Laptop and printer AC adapters
  - Desktop PCs
  - TVs and monitors
  - Gaming units
  - DVD AC/DC power supplies
- **Freewheeling Diode Applications:** Can be used in various power switching applications to improve efficiency and performance.

This component is ideal for engineers looking to enhance the efficiency of their power supply designs while maintaining high performance in terms of recovery time and voltage drop.
