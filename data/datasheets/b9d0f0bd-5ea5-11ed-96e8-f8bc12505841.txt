### Component Summary: AZ23C5V6W-7-F

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Document Number:** DS30257 Rev. 9  
**Date:** May 2008  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Nominal Zener Voltage: 5.6V
  - Minimum Zener Voltage: 5.32V
  - Maximum Zener Voltage: 5.88V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Current (IR @ VR): 1.0 mA
  - Maximum Zener Impedance (ZZT @ IZT): 40 Ω
  - Zener Test Current (IZT): 5 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-323 (Ultra-Small Surface Mount Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Lead Free/RoHS Compliant
  - "Green" Device
  - ESD Sensitivity Rating: AEC-Q101, HBM - 8kV, MM - 400V
  - Ideal for transient suppression

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020D

#### Description:
The AZ23C5V6W-7-F is a dual surface mount Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and transient suppression applications. It provides a nominal Zener voltage of 5.6V, making it suitable for protecting sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes. The device is housed in a compact SOT-323 package, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power management circuits to maintain a stable output voltage.
- **Transient Suppression:** Protects circuits from voltage spikes and transients, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Can be used in signal processing applications to clamp voltage levels and prevent signal distortion.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in various consumer electronic devices where voltage regulation is critical.

This component is particularly useful in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics applications where robust performance and reliability are essential.
