### Component Summary for WP914CK/4IDT

**Description:**
The WP914CK/4IDT is a high-efficiency red light-emitting diode (LED) manufactured by Kingbright. It is designed using Gallium Arsenide Phosphide (GaAsP) on Gallium Phosphide (GaP) and features a quad-level design that saves board space. This LED is suitable for various applications, including status indicators, illuminators, signage, decorative lighting, and architectural lighting.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 10mA: 1.9V (typical), 2.3V (maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 30 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 160 mA (1/10 Duty Cycle, 0.1ms Pulse Width)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 75 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +85°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 2 x 3 mm Quad-Level Circuit Board Indicator
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Quad-level design for space-saving
  - Black case for enhanced contrast
  - RoHS compliant
  - UL rating: 94V-0 for housing material (Type 66 nylon)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - According to JEDEC J-STD-020E, the component is moisture sensitive and should be handled with care to avoid damage.

### Typical Applications:
The WP914CK/4IDT LED is commonly used in:
- **Status Indicators:** Providing visual feedback in electronic devices.
- **Illuminators:** Enhancing visibility in various applications.
- **Signage Applications:** Used in displays and signs for clear visibility.
- **Decorative and Entertainment Lighting:** Adding aesthetic appeal in various settings.
- **Commercial and Residential Architectural Lighting:** Used in lighting designs for buildings and homes.

This LED is ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, making it a versatile choice for both consumer and commercial electronics.
