GaN SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) Switch

It utilizes a 0.25 μm GaN (Gallium Nitride) technology, which allows for high performance in a compact die area of 1.48 mm². The switch features low insertion loss and high isolation, making it suitable for various RF applications.
