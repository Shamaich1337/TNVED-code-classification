### Component Summary: DMP2240UW

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** DMP2240UW  
**Document Number:** DS31372 Rev. 3 - 2  
**Date:** May 2010  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Type:** P-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET)
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): -20 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±12 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: -1.5 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 70°C: -1.0 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): -5 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD): 250 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:** SOT-323
- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance: 
    - 150 mΩ @ VGS = -4.5V
    - 200 mΩ @ VGS = -2.5V
    - 240 mΩ @ VGS = -1.8V
  - Very Low Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): ≤ 1V
  - Low Input Capacitance
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Low Input/Output Leakage
  - Lead Free By Design/RoHS Compliant
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for High Reliability
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The DMP2240UW is a P-Channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for efficient switching applications. It features low on-resistance and a low gate threshold voltage, making it suitable for various power management tasks. The device is housed in a compact SOT-323 package, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The DMP2240UW is commonly used in:
- Power management circuits
- Load switching applications
- Battery management systems
- DC-DC converters
- Signal switching in consumer electronics

This component is particularly advantageous in automotive and industrial applications due to its high reliability and compliance with AEC-Q101 standards. Its low on-resistance and fast switching capabilities make it suitable for high-efficiency designs.
