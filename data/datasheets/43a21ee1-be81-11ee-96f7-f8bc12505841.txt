### Component Summary: GL9962 by Genesys Logic Inc.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD33): 3.3V ±10% (3.0V to 3.6V)
  - Maximum Differential I/O Voltage: 1.5V
  - Control Pins Input Voltage: -0.5V to VDD33 + 0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Active Mode Current: 150 µA (typical)
  - Power Down Mode Current: 75 µA (typical)
  - Maximum Differential I/O Current: ±25 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Active Mode: 150 µA
  - Power Down Mode: 75 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - QFN 20 (20-pin Quad Flat No-lead)

- **Special Features:**
  - 2-channel bi-directional MUX/DeMUX switch
  - Supports data rates up to 20 Gbps
  - Compatible with PCIe Gen 4, USB 3.2, and other high-speed protocols
  - Low insertion loss and excellent dynamic characteristics
  - HBM ESD level: 4 kV
  - Low intra-pair skew (3 ps typical) and inter-channel skew (10 ps typical)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Component Description:
The GL9962 is a high-speed bi-directional MUX/DeMUX switch designed for high-performance data applications. It features two differential channels capable of switching multiple signal types at data rates of up to 20 Gbps. The device is optimized for use with various interface standards, including PCI Express Gen 4 and USB 3.2, making it suitable for modern computing environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The GL9962 is typically used in:
- Desktop and notebook PCs
- Servers and workstations
- Data center servers
- High-speed data communication systems

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal routing and switching, particularly in environments where high data throughput and low latency are critical. Its low power consumption and robust performance characteristics make it a valuable addition to high-speed electronic designs.
