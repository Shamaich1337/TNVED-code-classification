### Component Summary: DMN62D0U-13

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Document Number:** DS38023  
**Revision:** 2 - 2  
**Date:** August 2016  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (BVDSS): 60V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±20V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at VGS = 4.5V, TA = +25°C: 380mA
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at VGS = 2.5V, TA = +25°C: 340mA
  - Maximum Continuous Body Diode Forward Current (IS): 0.4A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 1.2A (10µs pulse, Duty cycle = 1%)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at TA = +25°C: 380mW (steady state), 590mW (short duration)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance (RDS(ON)): 2Ω max at VGS = 4.5V
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - ESD Protected up to 1kV
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability
  - Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The DMN62D0U-13 is an N-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for high efficiency in power management applications. It features low on-state resistance and superior switching performance, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Control:** Used in driving motors efficiently with minimal power loss.
- **Power Management Functions:** Ideal for applications requiring efficient power conversion and management.
- **Backlighting:** Suitable for LED backlighting applications where efficient power usage is critical.

This component is particularly beneficial in automotive and industrial applications due to its high reliability and compliance with stringent environmental standards.
