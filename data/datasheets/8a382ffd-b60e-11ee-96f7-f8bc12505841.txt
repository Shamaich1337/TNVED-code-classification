### Component Summary: MSMP13A-M3/89A

**Manufacturer:** Vishay  
**Product Code:** MSMP13A-M3/89A  
**Component Type:** Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 14.4 V to 15.9 V
  - Stand-off Voltage (VWM): 13 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 27.2 A (10/1000 μs waveform)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation (PPPM): 150 W (10/1000 μs)
  - Power Dissipation at T_A = 25 °C: 0.5 W
  - Power Dissipation at T_M = 120 °C: 1.0 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - MicroSMP (DO-219AD)
- **Special Features:**
  - Unidirectional polarity
  - ESD capability: 15 kV (air), 8 kV (contact)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (automotive grade for MSMP6.0A to MSMP8.0A)
  - Halogen-free and RoHS-compliant
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 per J-STD-020C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL Level 1

#### Description:
The MSMP13A is a low-profile, surface-mount Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It features a unidirectional design and is capable of clamping high voltage spikes, thereby safeguarding integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Transient Protection:** Used in sensitive electronics to protect against voltage transients induced by inductive load switching and lightning.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Suitable for automotive applications due to AEC-Q101 qualification.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protects ICs and signal lines in consumer electronic devices.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Provides protection for sensitive equipment against transient overvoltages.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against voltage spikes, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic systems.
