#### Description:
The NTHS0603N02N1002JE is a surface mount NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) thermistor. NTC thermistors are temperature-sensitive resistors that exhibit a decrease in resistance with an increase in temperature. This component is designed for temperature sensing, protection, and compensation in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Battery Chargers**: Used for monitoring and managing battery temperatures to prevent overheating.
- **Power Supplies**: Employed in power management systems to ensure safe operating temperatures.
- **Office Equipment**: Utilized in devices like printers and copiers for temperature regulation.
- **LCD Compensation**: Helps in adjusting the performance of LCD displays based on temperature changes.
- **In-Car Entertainment**: Monitors and compensates for temperature variations in automotive electronics.

This thermistor is suitable for a wide range of industrial, telecom, and consumer applications, making it a versatile component in electronic design.
