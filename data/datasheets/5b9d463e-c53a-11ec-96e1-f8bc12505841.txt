### Component Summary: MCP73833-GPI/UN

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Supply Voltage (VDD): 3.75V to 6V
  - Regulated Output Voltage (VREG): 4.20V (±0.75%)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Charge Current: 1A
  - Preconditioning Current Ratio: 10% to 100% of the programmed charge current

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (Charging): 2.0 mA (typical)
  - Standby Current: 100 µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current: 50 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C (specified)
  - -40°C to +125°C (operating)

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in DFN-10 (3 mm x 3 mm) and MSOP-10 packages

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated pass transistor and current sense
  - Reverse discharge protection
  - Constant current/constant voltage operation with thermal regulation
  - Automatic end-of-charge control and automatic recharge
  - Cell temperature monitoring
  - Two charge status outputs (STAT1, STAT2)
  - Under voltage lockout (UVLO)
  - Programmable charge current via external resistor

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The MCP73833 is a highly integrated linear charge management controller designed for charging lithium-ion and lithium-polymer batteries. It features a compact design suitable for space-constrained applications, making it ideal for portable devices. The device operates using a constant current/constant voltage (CC/CV) charging algorithm, which is essential for maintaining battery health and efficiency.

**Typical Applications:**
The MCP73833 is commonly used in various consumer electronics, including:
- Personal Data Assistants (PDAs)
- Cellular Telephones
- Digital Cameras
- MP3 Players
- Bluetooth Headsets
- USB Chargers

This component is particularly well-suited for applications that require efficient battery charging from USB power sources, adhering to USB power bus specifications. Its advanced features, such as thermal regulation and automatic recharge, enhance its reliability and performance in real-world applications.
