### Component Summary: BDP953H6327XTSA1

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Type:** Silicon NPN Transistor  
**Package Type:** SOT223

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 100 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 120 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 3 A
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM, tp ≤ 10 ms): 5 A
  - Base Current (IB): 200 mA
  - Peak Base Current (IBM, tp ≤ 10 ms): 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot, TS ≤ 100 °C): 5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Junction to Soldering Point (RthJS): ≤ 10 K/W

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The BDP953H6327XTSA1 is a silicon NPN transistor designed for use in audio frequency (AF) driver and output stages. It features high collector current capability, high current gain, and low collector-emitter saturation voltage, making it suitable for various amplification applications. The transistor is part of a complementary series that includes PNP types (BDP948, BDP950, BDP954).

#### Typical Applications:
- **Audio Amplification:** Used in AF driver and output stages for amplifying audio signals.
- **Power Management:** Suitable for applications requiring high current handling and efficient power dissipation.
- **Signal Processing:** Can be utilized in circuits that require signal amplification and switching.

This transistor is also RoHS compliant and qualified according to AEC Q101, indicating its suitability for automotive applications.
