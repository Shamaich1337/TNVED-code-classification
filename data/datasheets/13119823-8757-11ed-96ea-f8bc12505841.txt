- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 2.7V to 3.6V (typical 3.3V)
  - CTP USB Voltage (USB_VDD): 4.4V to 5.5V (typical 5V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (IDD): 101 mA (typical) to 150 mA (max) at Vcc = 3.3V
  - CTP Current: 83.6 mA (typical) to 126 mA (max)
  - LED Driving Current: Up to 450 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is approximately 3.3V * 101 mA = 333.3 mW (for the display).

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -30°C to +80°C
  - Storage Temperature: -30°C to +80°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 7.0-inch TFT LCD module

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - TFT display with a resolution of 800 x 480 pixels.
  - Normally black mode, transmissive type.
  - Capacitive touch panel (CTP) included.
  - LED backlight (normally white).
  - Glare surface finish.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The RFF700A9-AWH-DNB is a 7-inch TFT (Thin Film Transistor) LCD display module designed for various electronic applications. It features a resolution of 800 x 480 pixels, with each pixel composed of red, green, and blue sub-pixels arranged in a vertical stripe pattern. The display operates in a normally black mode and is suitable for transmissive applications. The module includes a capacitive touch panel interface, enhancing user interaction capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
The RFF700A9-AWH-DNB is commonly used in:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as handheld devices, tablets, and portable gaming consoles.
- **Industrial Equipment:** For user interfaces in machinery and control panels.
- **Automotive Displays:** In dashboards and infotainment systems.
- **Medical Devices:** For patient monitoring systems and diagnostic equipment.
- **Smart Home Devices:** In control panels for smart appliances and home automation systems.

This module is ideal for applications requiring a compact, high-resolution display with touch capabilities, making it versatile for various modern electronic devices.
