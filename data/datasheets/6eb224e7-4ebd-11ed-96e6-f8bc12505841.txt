#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCC (Charge Pump Power Supply): 1.65V to 5.5V
  - VL (Logic Power Supply): 1.65V to VCC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (Icc): 0.7 mA (typical at VCC = 3.3V)
  - Shutdown Current (Icc(off)): 0.4 to 10 µA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption with 0.5 µA in shutdown mode.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (24 pins), RGE package
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated charge pump for RS-232 level shifting.
  - Enhanced ESD protection (±15kV Air-Gap Discharge, ±8kV Contact Discharge).
  - Auto Powerdown Plus feature for low power operation.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The **TRS3122E** is a low-power dual RS-232 transceiver designed for mixed-voltage operation. It features a unique tripler charge pump architecture that allows it to operate at low supply voltages (as low as 1.8V) while maintaining compatibility with higher voltage systems (3.3V and 5V). The device includes integrated level-shifting functionality, eliminating the need for external components when interfacing with low-voltage microcontrollers.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Remote Radio Units (RRU)**
- **Base Band Units (BBU)**
- **Electronic Point of Sale (EPOS) systems**
- **Diagnostics and Data Transmission**
- **Battery-Powered Equipment**

The TRS3122E is particularly suited for applications requiring reliable RS-232 communication over short distances, such as between devices on separate PCBs. Its low power consumption and auto power-down features make it ideal for battery-operated devices, ensuring minimal energy usage during idle periods.
