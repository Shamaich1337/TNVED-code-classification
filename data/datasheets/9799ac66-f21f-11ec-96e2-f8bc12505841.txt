### Component Summary: BAS516,115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 100 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 250 mA
  - Reverse Current (IR) at VR = 80V: 0.5 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot) at T amb ≤ 25°C: 500 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD523 (SC-79), single ultra-small and flat lead

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - High-speed switching capability with a reverse recovery time (trr) of ≤ 4 ns.
  - Low capacitance and low leakage current.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BAS516 is a high-speed switching diode designed for general-purpose switching applications. It is encapsulated in a small surface-mounted device (SMD) package, making it suitable for compact electronic designs. The diode features low capacitance and low leakage current, which contribute to its efficiency in high-speed applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The BAS516 is commonly used in:
- **High-speed switching applications:** Ideal for circuits requiring rapid on/off switching.
- **General-purpose switching:** Suitable for various electronic circuits where diode functionality is needed.
- **Automotive applications:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics, including power management and signal processing circuits.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the BAS516 diode, highlighting its key specifications and typical applications in electronic designs.
