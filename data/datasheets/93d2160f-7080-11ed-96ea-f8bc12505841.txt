### Component Summary for YS470M035E080PTC

**Component Type:**  
Hybrid Conductive Polymer Capacitor

**Description:**  
The YS470M035E080PTC is a hybrid conductive polymer capacitor from CapXon's YS series. It is designed for long life, with a rated endurance of up to 1000 hours at high temperatures. This capacitor features low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high ripple current capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

### Key Specifications:

- **Capacitance (CR):** 47 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 35 VDC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 44 VDC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR):** 
  - 1300 mA at 100kHz and 105°C
  - 130 mA at 120Hz and 105°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ):** 16% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK):** 16.5 µA (measured for 2 minutes at 20°C)
- **Maximum ESR:** 60 mΩ at 100kHz and 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +105°C
- **Package Type:** Through-Hole (THT)
- **Size:** Diameter 6.3 mm x Length 8 mm
- **Pitch:** 2.5 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data
- **AEC-Q200 Qualified:** No

### Special Features:
- Long life up to 1000 hours at rated voltage and temperature.
- Low ESR and high ripple current handling capabilities.

### Typical Applications:
The YS470M035E080PTC capacitor is typically used in applications requiring stable capacitance and low ESR, such as:
- Power management circuits
- Signal processing
- Audio equipment
- DC-DC converters
- Consumer electronics

This capacitor is particularly beneficial in environments where high reliability and performance under varying temperature and load conditions are critical.
