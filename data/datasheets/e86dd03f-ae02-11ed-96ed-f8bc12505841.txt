### Component Summary: STPS2H100A

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 2 A
  - Surge Non-Repetitive Forward Current (IFSM): 75 A (10 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 0.65 V at 2 A
  - Repetitive Peak Avalanche Power (PARM): 173 W (10 µs, Tj = 125 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (Tj): 175 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SMA, SMA Flat, SMB, SMB Flat, and SMA Flat Notch packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Negligible switching losses
  - High junction temperature capability
  - Low leakage current
  - Good trade-off between leakage current and forward voltage drop
  - Avalanche capability specified
  - ECOPACK2 component
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text

#### Description:
The STPS2H100A is a Schottky rectifier designed for high-frequency applications, particularly in switched-mode power supplies (SMPS) such as adaptors and onboard DC/DC converters. It is characterized by its low forward voltage drop and high efficiency, making it suitable for applications where power loss needs to be minimized.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Switching Diodes:** Used in various switching applications due to its fast switching capabilities.
- **Battery Chargers:** Ideal for use in battery management systems where efficient rectification is required.
- **SMPS (Switched Mode Power Supplies):** Commonly used in power supply circuits to convert electrical energy efficiently.
- **DC/DC Converters:** Utilized in circuits that convert direct current from one voltage level to another.
- **Telecom Power:** Employed in telecommunications equipment for power management.
- **LED Lighting:** Suitable for LED drivers and lighting applications where efficiency is critical.

This component is particularly valued in applications that require high efficiency and reliability, especially in environments with varying temperature conditions.
