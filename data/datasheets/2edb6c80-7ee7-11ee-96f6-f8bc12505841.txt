### Component Summary: AS431ANTR-G1

**Description:**
The AS431 is a three-terminal adjustable shunt regulator designed for precision voltage regulation. It is characterized by its guaranteed thermal stability across a wide operational range, making it an ideal substitute for Zener diodes in various applications. The output voltage can be adjusted between 2.5V (VREF) and a maximum cathode voltage of 36V. The AS431 is available in multiple package types, including SOT23, SOT25, SOT89, and TO92.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reference Voltage (VREF): 2.5V
  - Maximum Cathode Voltage (VKA): 36V
  - Absolute Maximum Cathode Voltage: 40V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Cathode Current (IKA): 1mA to 100mA
  - Reference Input Current (IREF): 10mA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 
    - SOT23/SOT25: 370mW
    - TO92: 770mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature Range (TSTG): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT23, SOT25, SOT89, and TO92 (Ammo Packing)

- **Special Features:**
  - Programmable output voltage from 2.5V to 36V
  - Low temperature coefficient (20ppm/°C typical)
  - Low output noise and dynamic impedance (0.15Ω to 0.5Ω)
  - High stability under capacitive load
  - Lead-Free and RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The AS431 is commonly used in:
- Switching power supplies
- Voltage adapters
- Battery chargers
- Graphic cards
- Precision voltage references

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring adjustable voltage regulation and high precision, making it suitable for both consumer electronics and industrial applications.
