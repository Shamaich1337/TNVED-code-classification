### Component Summary: TSW-115-07-G-D

**Manufacturer:** Samtec  
**Product Code:** TSW-115-07-G-D  
**Component Type:** Standard Strip Header Connector

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - 550 VAC when mated with SSW
  - 500 VAC when mated with BCS
  - 515 VAC when mated with ESQ
  - 450 VAC when mated with BCS or SSM (RA/RE)
  - 400 VAC when mated with CES

  - 5.2 A when mated with ESW, SSW, SLW, SSM
  - 6.3 A when mated with SSQ
  - 4.6 A when mated with BCS
  - 4.3 A when mated with SNT

- **Power Consumption:** Not specified directly; current rating indicates capability.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55 °C to +125 °C with Gold plating
  - -55 °C to +105 °C with Tin plating

- **Package Type:** Through-hole header with .100" (2.54 mm) pin centers.

- **Special Features:**
  - Lead-Free Solderable: HTSW version is lead-free; TSW version is not (lead wave only).
  - Available in various lead styles and configurations (single, double, triple rows).
  - Elevated right-angle option available.
  - Locking lead and locking clip options available for certain styles.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The TSW-115-07-G-D is a standard strip header connector designed for through-hole mounting. It features a double row configuration with 15 pins per row, and a lead style that allows for reliable connections in various electronic applications. The connector is made from PBT insulator material and phosphor bronze terminals, with gold or tin plating options for enhanced conductivity and corrosion resistance.

The TSW series connectors are commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** Connecting power supplies to circuit boards.
- **Signal Processing:** Facilitating signal transmission between different components in electronic devices.
- **Data Storage:** Used in connections for data storage devices and interfaces.
- **General Electronics:** Suitable for a wide range of applications in consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and telecommunications.
