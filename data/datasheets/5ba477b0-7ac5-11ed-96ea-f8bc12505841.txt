  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOIC (8 pins) or PDIP (8 pins)

  - 1/8th Unit Load (up to 256 nodes on the bus)
  - Open-Circuit, Short-Circuit, and Idle-Bus Failsafe Receiver

  - Driver Output Voltage Slew-Rate Limited for optimum signal quality at 10 Mbps
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SN65HVD08** is a half-duplex RS-485 transceiver designed for balanced data transmission and interoperation with ANSI TIA/EIA-485-A and ISO-8482E compliant devices. It features a 3-state differential line driver and receiver, making it suitable for applications requiring robust communication over long distances. The device operates within a wide supply voltage range and is optimized for low power consumption, making it ideal for remote data transmission applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Transmission with Remote Stations**: The SN65HVD08 is commonly used in systems where data needs to be transmitted over long distances, such as in industrial automation and control systems.
- **Isolated Multipoint Data Buses**: It is suitable for applications that require multiple devices to communicate over a single bus, such as in point-of-sale networks and electric utility metering.
- **Industrial Process Control Networks**: The device is often employed in environments where reliable communication is critical, such as in factory automation and process control systems.

This transceiver's ability to handle multiple nodes on a bus and its robust ESD protection make it a reliable choice for various industrial and commercial applications.
