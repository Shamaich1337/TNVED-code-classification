### Component Summary: BUK7K29-100E

**General Description:**
The BUK7K29-100E is a dual N-channel MOSFET designed for high-performance automotive applications. It features TrenchMOS technology and is packaged in an LFPAK56D (Dual Power-SO8) format. This component is compliant with AEC Q101 standards, making it suitable for thermally demanding environments.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 29.5 A at 25 °C
  - Peak Drain Current (IDM): 126 A (pulsed, t_p ≤ 10 µs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 68 W at 25 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to 175 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to 175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - LFPAK56D (SOT1205), 8 leads
- **Special Features:**
  - Repetitive avalanche rated
  - True standard level gate with VGS(th) rating greater than 1 V at 175 °C
  - Qualified for automotive applications (AEC Q101 compliant)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The BUK7K29-100E is a dual N-channel MOSFET that serves as a switch in various electronic circuits. Its low on-state resistance (RDS(on)) of 24.5 mΩ at VGS = 10 V and Tj = 25 °C allows for efficient power management, minimizing energy loss during operation.

**Typical Applications:**
- Automotive systems operating at 12 V, 24 V, and 48 V
- Control of motors, lamps, and solenoids
- Transmission control systems
- High-performance power switching applications

This component is particularly well-suited for applications requiring robust performance under high thermal stress, making it ideal for automotive and industrial environments.
