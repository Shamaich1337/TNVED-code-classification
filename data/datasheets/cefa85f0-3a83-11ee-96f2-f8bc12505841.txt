The device meets the \nrequirements of TIA/EIA-232-F and provides the electrical interface between an asynchronous communication \ncontroller and the serial-port connector. The charge pump and four small external capacitors allow operation \nfrom a single 3-V to 5.5-V supply. The device operates at data signaling rates up to 250 kbit/s and a maximum of \n30-V/μs driver output slew rate. Outputs are protected against shorts to ground.\n8.2 Functional Block Diagram\nRXTXPOWER 3.3 V, 5 V\nDIN\nROUTDOUT\nRS232\nRIN\nRS2322\n22\n2\n8.3 Feature Description\n8.3.1 Power\nThe power block increases, inverts, and regulates voltage at V+ and V– pins using a charge pump that requires \nfour external capacitors.\n8.3.2 RS232 Driver\nTwo drivers interface standard logic level to RS232 levels. Both DIN inputs must be valid high or low.\n8.3.3 RS232 Receiver\nTwo receivers interface RS232 levels to standard logic levels. An open input will result in a high output on ROUT. \nEach RIN input includes an internal standard RS232 load.SN65C3232, SN75C3232\nSLLS540C – JULY 2002 – REVISED JUNE 2021 www.ti.com\n10 Submit Document Feedback Copyright © 2021 Texas Instruments Incorporated\nProduct Folder Links: SN65C3232  SN75C3232\n8.4 Device Functional Modes\nTable 8-1  and Table 8-2  list the functional modes of the drivers and receivers of MAX3232E.\nTable 8-1. ### Component Summary: SN65C3232PWR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0 V to 5.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.3 V to 6 V
  - Positive Charge Pump Output Voltage (V+): -0.3 V to 7 V
  - Negative Charge Pump Output Voltage (V-): -7 V to 0.3 V

- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current (ICC): 300 μA typical (no load)

- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Low supply current of 300 μA typical

- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40 °C to 85 °C

- **Package Type**: 
  - TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package), 16 pins

- **Special Features or Notes**: 
  - ESD protection exceeds ±15 kV using the human-body model (HBM)
  - Accepts 5-V logic input with a 3.3-V supply
  - Operates at data signaling rates up to 1 Mbit/s
  - Requires four external capacitors (4 × 0.1 μF)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **SN65C3232PWR** is a multichannel RS-232 compatible line driver and receiver designed for asynchronous communication. It integrates two line drivers and two line receivers along with a dual charge-pump circuit, allowing it to operate from a single supply voltage ranging from 3 V to 5.5 V. The device is particularly noted for its low power consumption and high ESD protection, making it suitable for various applications where robust communication is required.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Industrial PCs**: Used for communication between devices in industrial settings.
- **Wired Networking**: Facilitates data transmission over RS-232 connections in networking equipment.
- **Data Center and Enterprise Computing**: Provides reliable serial communication in server and data center environments.
- **Battery-Powered Systems**: Ideal for portable devices due to its low power consumption.
- **Notebooks, Laptops, and Palmtop PCs**: Used in personal computing devices for serial communication.
- **Hand-held Equipment**: Suitable for devices requiring RS-232 communication in a compact form factor.

This component is particularly useful in applications where reliable data transmission is critical, and it can interface with standard logic levels while providing RS-232 signal levels.
