### Component Summary: AS6C4008-55SIN

**Manufacturer:** Alliance Memory Inc.  
**Product Code:** AS6C4008-55SIN  
**Description:**  
The AS6C4008-55SIN is a low-power CMOS Static Random Access Memory (SRAM) chip organized as 512K x 8 bits (4,194,304 bits total). It is designed for applications requiring low power consumption and is particularly suitable for battery backup and non-volatile memory applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 2.7V to 5.5V
  - Data Retention Voltage: 1.5V (minimum)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (Icc): 30 mA (typical)
  - Standby Current (Isb): 4 µA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption with a typical operating current of 30 mA and standby current of 4 µA.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial Grade: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 32-pin 450 mil SOP (Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Fully static operation
  - Tri-state output
  - All outputs TTL compatible
  - ROHS compliant
  - Access time: 55 ns

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description of the Component:
The AS6C4008-55SIN is a low-power CMOS SRAM that provides fast access times and low standby power consumption. It operates with a single power supply and is designed for applications that require reliable data retention and low power usage. The device is fully static, meaning it does not require refresh cycles, making it ideal for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in systems where low power consumption is critical, such as portable devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for applications that require fast data access and manipulation.
- **Data Storage:** Ideal for temporary data storage in embedded systems and battery backup applications.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly used in devices like digital cameras, handheld devices, and other consumer electronics that require efficient memory solutions.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the AS6C4008-55SIN SRAM, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and use cases.
