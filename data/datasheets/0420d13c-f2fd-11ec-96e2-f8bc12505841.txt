**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** P-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): -60 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: -5.9 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: -3.7 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): -30 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 3.1 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOP-8 (Small Outline Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low RDS(ON): 
    - At VGS = -10V: < 47 mΩ
    - At VGS = -4.5V: < 60 mΩ
  - 100% UIS Tested (Unclamped Inductive Switching)
  - Split gate trench MOSFET technology
  - High density cell design for low RDS(ON)
  - Low Crss (Reverse Transfer Capacitance)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The YJS06GP06A is a P-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for efficient switching applications. It features a split gate trench technology that allows for a high-density cell design, resulting in low on-resistance (RDS(ON)) and improved performance in power management applications. This component is particularly suitable for applications requiring efficient load switching and battery protection.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Load Switching:** The YJS06GP06A can be used to control power to various loads in electronic circuits, making it ideal for power management systems.
- **Battery Protection:** It is commonly employed in battery management systems to prevent over-discharge and over-current conditions, thereby enhancing battery life and safety.
- **General Switching Applications:** This MOSFET can be utilized in various electronic devices where efficient switching is required, such as in power supplies and motor control circuits.
