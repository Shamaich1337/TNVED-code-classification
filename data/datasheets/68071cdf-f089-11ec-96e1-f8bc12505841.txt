**Component Type:** Half Bridge IGBT Module

**Manufacturer:** Zhuzhou CRRC Times Electric Co.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCES): 1200 V
  - Gate-Emitter Voltage (VGES): ±20 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Collector Current (IC): 600 A
  - Maximum Peak Collector Current (IC(PK)): 1200 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (Pmax): 3.0 kW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tvj): -40 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 °C to 125 °C
- **Package Type:** Module with Cu Baseplate
- **Special Features:**
  - Enhanced Al2O3 substrates
  - High thermal cycling capability
  - 10μs short circuit withstand capability
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The TG600HF12M1-G3A00 is a half-bridge insulated gate bipolar transistor (IGBT) module designed for high-power applications. It combines the benefits of both MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, allowing for efficient switching and high current handling capabilities. The module is constructed with a copper baseplate for improved thermal management and features enhanced substrates for better reliability.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Drives:** Used in various motor control applications, including industrial drives and servo systems.
- **Power Charging Equipment:** Suitable for electric vehicle charging stations and other power supply systems.
- **Solar Power:** Employed in photovoltaic systems for efficient energy conversion.
- **Electric Vehicles:** Integral in the power electronics of electric and hybrid vehicles for propulsion and energy management.

This IGBT module is particularly well-suited for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability under demanding conditions, making it a critical component in modern power electronics.
