### Component Summary: QPQ1282TR7

**Description:**
The QPQ1282 is a high-performance Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) duplexer designed specifically for LTE Band 1 uplink and downlink applications. It is optimized for small cell deployments, providing low insertion loss and high rejection, making it suitable for various wireless communication systems.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated in the datasheet.
- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated in the datasheet.
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Downlink: +30 dBm
  - Uplink: +23 dBm
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Storage Temperature: -40 to +125°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount Package (SMP)
- **Dimensions:** 2.00 mm x 2.50 mm x 0.91 mm
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Level 3 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020
- **Special Features:**
  - 60 MHz bandwidth for both uplink and downlink
  - High attenuation and low loss
  - No external matching required
  - RoHS compliant and Pb-free

**Typical Applications:**
The QPQ1282 is primarily used in:
- Base station infrastructure
- Small cells
- Repeaters
- Routers
- LTE dongles
- General-purpose wireless applications

This duplexer is particularly suited for enhancing the performance of LTE networks by allowing simultaneous transmission and reception of signals, thus improving overall communication efficiency in small cell environments.
