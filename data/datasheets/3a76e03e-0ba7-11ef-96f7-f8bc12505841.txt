### Component Summary: L79L05ABD13TR

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** L79L05ABD13TR  
**Component Type:** Negative Voltage Regulator

#### Key Specifications:
- **Output Voltage:** -5 V
- **Output Current:** Up to 100 mA
- **Input Voltage Range:** 
  - For VO = -5 V: -30 V
  - For VO = -12 to -15 V: -35 V
- **Power Dissipation:** Internally limited
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40 to 125 °C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40 to 150 °C
- **Package Type:** Available in TO-92, SO-8, and SOT-89 packages
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data
- **Special Features:**
  - Thermal overload protection
  - Short-circuit protection
  - No external components required
  - Output tolerance of ±4%

#### Description:
The L79L series of three-terminal negative voltage regulators are designed to provide a stable negative output voltage. They incorporate internal current limiting and thermal shutdown features, making them robust and reliable for various applications. The L79L05ABD13TR specifically outputs -5 V and can deliver up to 100 mA of current, making it suitable for local regulation in electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Local or On-Card Regulation:** Used to eliminate noise and distribution problems associated with single-point regulation.
- **Power Management:** Can be used in power supply circuits to provide stable negative voltage.
- **Zener Diode Replacement:** Acts as a more efficient alternative to Zener diode/resistor combinations, offering improved output impedance, lower quiescent current, and reduced noise.
- **High-Current Voltage Regulators:** Can be used in conjunction with power pass elements for applications requiring higher current outputs.

This component is ideal for applications in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and any circuit requiring a reliable negative voltage supply.
