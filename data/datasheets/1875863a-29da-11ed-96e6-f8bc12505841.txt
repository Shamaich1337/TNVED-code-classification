- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (Tj, Tstg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PG-SOT-323

- **Special Features:**
  - N-channel enhancement mode MOSFET
  - Logic level operation
  - dV/dt rated
  - Pb-free lead plating; RoHS compliant
  - Qualified according to AEC Q101
  - Halogen-free according to IEC61249-2-21

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The BSS138W is a small-signal N-channel MOSFET designed for low-voltage applications. It operates in enhancement mode, meaning it requires a positive gate-source voltage to conduct. This component is particularly noted for its logic-level compatibility, allowing it to be driven directly by low-voltage logic circuits. The device is also rated for high-speed switching applications due to its low gate charge and fast switching times.

#### Typical Applications:
The BSS138W is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Switching Applications:** Ideal for low-power switching in consumer electronics and portable devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Utilized in analog signal switching and amplification circuits.
- **Level Shifting:** Can be used to interface between different voltage levels in mixed-signal systems.
- **Power Management:** Suitable for load switching in power management circuits, including battery-powered devices.

This component's combination of low on-resistance, high-speed switching capabilities, and robust thermal performance makes it a versatile choice for engineers designing compact and efficient electronic systems.
