### Component Summary: K12T-6A-P DC/DC Converter

**Manufacturer:** MORNSUN  
**Product Code:** K12T-6A-P  
**Type:** Non-isolated DC/DC Converter

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage: 12V (Range: 8.3V to 14V)
  - Output Voltage: Adjustable from 0.75V to 5.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 6A
  - Input Current (full load): 2660 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Efficiency: Up to 94%
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Compact SMD package (Dimensions: 20.30 x 11.40 x 6.60 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Input under-voltage protection
  - Output short-circuit protection
  - Over-current protection (140-160% of Io)
  - High-speed transient response
  - EN62368 approved
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

#### Description:
The K12T-6A-P is a high-efficiency, non-isolated switching regulator designed to convert a DC input voltage into a regulated DC output voltage. It features an adjustable output voltage range, allowing it to cater to various application requirements. The converter is optimized for high current output with fast transient response, making it suitable for demanding electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The K12T-6A-P is widely used in:
- **Communications Equipment:** Providing power to networking devices and communication systems.
- **Computer Network Industry:** Powering components in servers, workstations, and LAN/WAN devices.
- **Power Distributed Architecture:** Serving as a power supply in distributed systems.
- **High-Speed Chips:** Supporting high-performance chips such as FPGA, DSP, and ASIC, which require stable and efficient power delivery.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited, and high efficiency is required, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
