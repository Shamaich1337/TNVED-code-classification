  - Output Voltage: 15 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**

- **Power Consumption:**

  - Efficiency: 83% (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +90°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SIP-8 (Single In-line Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Fully regulated outputs
  - Continuous short circuit protection
  - Remote On/Off control
  - I/O isolation voltage: 1600 VDC
  - Designed to meet IEC/EN/UL 62368-1 (not certified)
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided information.

**Description:**
The TEC 3-2413WI is a 3 Watt DC/DC converter from the TEC 3WI series by Traco Power. It is designed to convert a wide range of input voltages (9 - 36 VDC) into a stable output voltage of 15 VDC. This converter is housed in a compact SIP-8 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. It features high efficiency and low thermal loss, allowing it to operate effectively across a broad temperature range.

- **Signal Processing:** Powering sensors and signal processing circuits.
- **Telecommunications:** Used in communication equipment where stable voltage is critical.
- **Industrial Automation:** Suitable for powering control systems and automation devices.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for use in embedded applications requiring reliable power conversion.
