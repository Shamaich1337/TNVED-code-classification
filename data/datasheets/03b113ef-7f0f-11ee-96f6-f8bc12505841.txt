### Component Summary: MAX485ESA+T

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V_CC): 4.75V to 5.25V (typical 5V)
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: 12V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Current: 300 µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current: 0.1 µA (in low-power shutdown mode)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply current ranges from 120 µA to 500 µA depending on load conditions.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to +70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C
  - Military: -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Pin Plastic DIP, 8-Pin SO, and 8-Pin µMAX packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Low-power operation with shutdown mode.
  - Short-circuit current limiting and thermal shutdown protection.
  - Fail-safe receiver input guarantees a logic-high output if the input is open circuit.
  - Supports up to 128 transceivers on the bus (for MAX487 and MAX1487).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The MAX485ESA+T is a low-power, half-duplex transceiver designed for RS-485 and RS-422 communication. It integrates one driver and one receiver, allowing for bidirectional data transmission over twisted-pair cables. The device can operate at data rates up to 2.5 Mbps, making it suitable for high-speed communication applications. The MAX485 features a standard driver output and a fail-safe receiver input, ensuring reliable operation even in the event of an open circuit.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Data Communication:** Used in multipoint bus transmission lines for industrial control systems and local area networks.
- **Level Translators:** Acts as a level translator for interfacing different voltage levels in communication systems.
- **EMI-Sensitive Applications:** The slew-rate limited versions (like MAX483) are ideal for environments where electromagnetic interference (EMI) must be minimized.
- **Industrial Control:** Commonly used in industrial automation and control systems where robust communication over long distances is required.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the MAX485ESA+T, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical applications in electronic communication systems.
