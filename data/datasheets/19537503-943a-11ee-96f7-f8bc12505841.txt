#### Description:
The LM317LMX/NOPB is an adjustable linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage that can be set between 1.2V and 37V. It is capable of delivering up to 100 mA of output current and is known for its ease of use, requiring only two external resistors to set the desired output voltage. The device features built-in overload protection, including current limiting and thermal shutdown, ensuring safe operation under various conditions.

- **Automotive LED Lighting**: Provides stable voltage for LED drivers.
- **Battery Chargers**: Regulates voltage for charging batteries safely.
- **Post Regulation for Switching Supplies**: Smooths out voltage fluctuations from switching power supplies.
- **Constant-Current Regulators**: Maintains a steady current for applications like LED driving.
- **Microprocessor Supplies**: Supplies regulated voltage to microcontrollers and other digital circuits.
