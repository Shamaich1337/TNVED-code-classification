### Component Summary: TPS2419DR (Texas Instruments)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range (A, C): 0V to 16.5V
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings:
    - Voltage A, C: -0.3V to 18V
    - Voltage GATE, BYP: -0.3V to 30V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (I(A)): 0.66 mA (inactive), 4.25 mA (saturated high)
  - Gate Drive Current: 290 µA (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation Ratings:
    - TSSOP (θJA Lowk): 258 mW
    - SOIC (θJA Lowk): 176 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Pin TSSOP (PW) and SOIC (D) packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Controls external N-channel MOSFET for N+1 and ORing applications
  - Programmable turn-off threshold via RSET
  - Soft turn-on feature to reduce bus transients
  - Enable pin for forced MOSFET turn-off
  - Thermal shutdown at 135°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TPS2419** is a power management controller designed to control external N-channel MOSFETs for N+1 redundancy and ORing applications. It provides reverse current protection similar to that of an ORing diode but with the efficiency benefits of a MOSFET. The device features programmable turn-off thresholds, allowing for tailored operation in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **N+1 Power Supplies:** Used in systems requiring redundancy, ensuring continuous operation even if one power supply fails.
- **Telecom Systems:** Provides reliable power management in critical telecom infrastructure.
- **Server Blades:** Ensures high availability and reliability in server environments.
- **High Availability Power Modules:** Used in applications where power continuity is crucial.

The TPS2419 is ideal for applications that require efficient power management and redundancy, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic systems.
