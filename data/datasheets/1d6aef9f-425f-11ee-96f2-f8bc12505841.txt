### Component Summary: HMC849ALP4CETR

**Description:**
The HMC849ALP4CETR is a high isolation, non-reflective Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switch designed for operation in the frequency range of DC to 6 GHz. It utilizes GaAs pHEMT technology and is packaged in a compact 16-lead QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) format. The switch is characterized by its high isolation and low insertion loss, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Control Voltage (Vctl, EN): 0 to +5V
  - Bias Voltage (Vdd): 3V to 5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Idd: 1.2 mA at 3V, 1.3 mA at 5V
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 0.969 W (through path), 0.451 W (termination path)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 16-lead QFN, 4x4 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - High isolation up to 60 dB
  - Low insertion loss (1.0 dB at 2.0 - 4.0 GHz)
  - Non-reflective design
  - "All Off" state capability
  - High input third-order intercept point (IP3): +52 dBm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC849ALP4CETR is ideal for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular and 4G infrastructure
- WiMAX and WiBro systems
- Automotive telematics
- Mobile radio communications
- Test equipment for RF applications

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where high isolation and low insertion loss are critical, such as in RF signal routing and switching applications. Its ability to handle high input power and provide a non-reflective switching state makes it a versatile choice for modern communication systems.
