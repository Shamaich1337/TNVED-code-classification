### Component Summary: HMC506LP4ETR

**Description:**
The HMC506LP4ETR is a GaAs InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor (HBT) MMIC Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) with integrated resonators, negative resistance devices, varactor diodes, and buffer amplifiers. It operates within a frequency range of 7.8 to 8.7 GHz and is designed for applications requiring low phase noise and high stability.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +3.0V (max +3.5V)
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 0 to +11V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 77 mA (typical at Vcc = +3.0V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: +14 dBm (typical)
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 303 mW (at T = 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) 4 x 4 mm surface mount package

- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent phase noise performance: -103 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - No external resonator needed
  - Integrated buffer amplifier
  - Low noise MMIC design

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC506LP4ETR is suitable for various applications, including:
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) Radio
- Point-to-Point and Multipoint Radio Communications
- Test Equipment and Industrial Controls
- Military End-Use Applications

This component is particularly valuable in systems where low noise and high-frequency stability are critical, making it ideal for communication and radar systems.
