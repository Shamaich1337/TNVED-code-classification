### Component Summary: NCP170BMX300TCG

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Input Voltage Range:** 2.2 V to 5.5 V
  - **Output Voltage:** 3.0 V (fixed)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Output Current:** 150 mA
  - **Short Circuit Current Limit:** 225 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Quiescent Current:** 0.5 µA (typical)
  - **Standby Current:** 0.1 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Junction Temperature:** -40°C to +85°C
  - **Storage Temperature:** -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in XDFN4 (1.0 mm x 1.0 mm), SOT-563, and TSOP-5 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low quiescent current for extended battery life.
  - Low dropout voltage of 170 mV at 150 mA.
  - Over-current protection and thermal shutdown features.
  - Stable with ceramic capacitors.
  - Active discharge option available (not applicable for this variant).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The NCP170BMX300TCG is a high-performance, low-dropout (LDO) linear voltage regulator designed for battery-powered applications. It features an ultra-low quiescent current of 0.5 µA, making it ideal for devices that require minimal power consumption during idle states. The device provides a fixed output voltage of 3.0 V and can deliver up to 150 mA of output current, ensuring stable performance in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Battery-Powered Equipment:** The ultra-low quiescent current ensures long battery life, making it suitable for portable devices.
- **Portable Communication Equipment:** Ideal for smartphones, tablets, and other wireless communication devices.
- **Cameras and Image Sensors:** Provides stable voltage supply for image processing and sensor applications.
- **Wearable Devices:** Its small package size and low power consumption make it suitable for compact wearable technology.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the NCP170BMX300TCG, highlighting its suitability for low-power, battery-operated devices.
