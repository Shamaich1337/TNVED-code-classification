### Component Summary: VOM618A-3T (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Input Forward Voltage (Vf):** 1.1V to 1.6V (at If = 5 mA)
  - **Collector-Emitter Voltage (Vceo):** 80V
  - **Emitter-Collector Voltage (Veco):** 7V
  - **Isolation Test Voltage (Viso):** 3750 V RMS
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Input Forward Current (If):** 60 mA (max)
  - **Collector Current (Ic):** 50 mA (max), 100 mA (pulsed)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Input Power Dissipation (Pdiss):** 70 mW
  - **Output Power Dissipation (Pdiss):** 150 mW
  - **Total Power Dissipation (Ptot):** 170 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Ambient Temperature (Tamb):** -55 °C to +110 °C
  - **Storage Temperature (Tstg):** -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - **Type:** SOP-4 Mini-Flat Package
- **Special Features:**
  - High current transfer ratio (CTR) range: 100% to 200% (for VOM618A-3T)
  - Low coupling capacitance
  - Fast switching times
  - End-stackable design
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The VOM618A-3T is an optocoupler that features a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) infrared emitting diode optically coupled to a silicon planar phototransistor detector. It is designed to provide electrical isolation between two circuits while allowing signal transmission. The device is housed in a compact SOP-4 mini-flat package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs)**
- **Telecommunication Systems**
- **Lighting Control Systems**
- **Solar Inverters**
- **AC Drives**

This component is particularly useful in applications where electrical isolation is required, such as in control systems and signal processing, ensuring safe operation between high and low voltage circuits. Its high CTR and fast switching capabilities make it ideal for high-speed data transmission and control applications.
