- **Voltage Ratings:**

  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1200 V (for diode)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 75 A

  - Low switching losses
  - Low VCE(sat) with positive temperature coefficient
  - Fast & soft recovery anti-parallel FWD included
  - Low inductance case
  - High short circuit capability (10 µs)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The MG75P12E2A is an Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) module designed for high-efficiency power switching applications. It combines the advantages of both MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, providing high input impedance and low output saturation voltage. This module is particularly suitable for applications requiring high voltage and current handling capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Drivers:** Used in various motor control applications, including industrial drives and electric vehicles.
- **AC and DC Servo Drive Amplifiers:** Ideal for precision control in servo systems.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** Provides reliable power management and backup solutions.
- **Inverters:** Commonly used in renewable energy systems, such as solar inverters, to convert DC to AC power.

This module is designed to meet the demands of modern power electronics, offering robust performance in a compact package.
