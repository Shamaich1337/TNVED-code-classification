### Component Summary: PMA3-83LN+

**Description:**
The PMA3-83LN+ is a low noise, wideband monolithic amplifier based on PHEMT technology. It is designed to provide low noise figure and high linearity, making it suitable for sensitive, high-dynamic-range receiver applications. The amplifier operates on a single supply voltage of either 5V or 6V and is optimized for 50Ω systems. Its compact 3 x 3 x 0.89 mm package allows for efficient use in dense circuit board layouts.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VDD): 5V to 6V
  - Maximum DC Voltage: 7V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (IDD): 60 mA (at 5V), 77 mA (at 6V)
  - Current Variation vs. Temperature: -109 µA/°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation: 0.95 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to 105°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  - Junction Temperature: 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: DQ1225 (Plastic package with exposed paddle)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low noise figure: 1.3 dB at 2 GHz
  - High IP3: +35 dBm at 2 GHz
  - Wide bandwidth with flat gain: ±0.9 dB over 0.5 to 7 GHz, ±1.5 dB over 0.5 to 8 GHz
  - Low additive phase noise: -155 dBc/Hz at 10 KHz offset
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Typical Applications:**
The PMA3-83LN+ is widely used in various applications, including:
- Wireless communication systems (WiFi, WLAN, UMTS, LTE, WiMAX)
- Radar systems (S-band, C-band)
- Instrumentation and measurement devices
- Low phase noise synthesizers

This amplifier's combination of low noise, high linearity, and wide bandwidth makes it particularly suitable for use in the front end of low noise receiver systems, enhancing overall system performance in demanding applications.
