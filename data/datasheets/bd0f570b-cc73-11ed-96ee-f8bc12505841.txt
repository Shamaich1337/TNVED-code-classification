### Component Summary: ADR364BUJZ-REEL7

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): 4.096 V (A grade: 4.088 V to 4.104 V; B grade: 4.092 V to 4.100 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: 
    - Sourcing: +5 mA
    - Sinking: -1 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IIN): 150 µA (max 190 µA)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - A grade: -40°C to +125°C
  - B grade: -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 5-Lead TSOT (UJ-5)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Low dropout voltage: 300 mV
  - Ultralow output voltage noise: 11 µV p-p (0.1 Hz to 10 Hz)
  - No external capacitor required for stability
  - Qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The ADR364BUJZ-REEL7 is a precision voltage reference from Analog Devices, designed to provide a stable output voltage of 4.096 V with low power consumption and high accuracy. It features a low temperature coefficient and is available in a compact TSOT package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. The device is designed to operate over a wide temperature range and is qualified for automotive applications, ensuring reliability in demanding environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The ADR364BUJZ-REEL7 is commonly used in:
- Battery-powered instruments
- Portable medical devices
- Data acquisition systems
- Industrial process controls
- Automotive applications

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise voltage references with low noise and low power consumption, making it suitable for both consumer and industrial electronics.
