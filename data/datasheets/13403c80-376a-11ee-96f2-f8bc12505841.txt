  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55°C to +125°C
  - Soldering Temperature (T_sol): 260°C for 10 seconds

- **Package Type:**
  - 4-pin Mini-Flat package (SOP with a 2.0mm profile)

- **Special Features:**
  - Pb free and RoHS compliant
  - Current Transfer Ratio (CTR): Min. 50% at I_F=5mA, V_CE=5V
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Class 1
  - Agency Approvals: UL, c-UL, VDE, FIMKO, CQC

#### Description:
The KPC357 is a DC-input single-channel optocoupler that integrates a light-emitting diode (LED) and a phototransistor in a compact 4-pin Mini-Flat package. It provides electrical isolation between its input and output, making it suitable for various applications where signal isolation is necessary.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Hybrid Substrates:** Ideal for high-density mounting applications.
- **Programmable Controllers:** Used in control systems for automation.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Commonly found in power management circuits.
- **Telecommunication Equipment:** Utilized in devices requiring signal isolation and integrity.

This component is particularly useful in applications where electrical isolation is critical, such as in interfacing between different voltage levels or in noisy environments. Its compact size and compliance with environmental standards make it suitable for modern electronic designs.
