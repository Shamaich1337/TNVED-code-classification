### Component Summary: GT600HF120T9H

**Component Type:** IGBT Module

**Description:**
The GT600HF120T9H is a high-performance Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) module designed for high-efficiency power conversion applications. It features a trench and field stop IGBT structure, which allows for low switching losses and high current handling capabilities. This module is particularly suitable for applications requiring robust performance under demanding conditions, such as inverters and motor drives.

### Key Specifications:

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Blocking Voltage (VCES): 1200 V
  - Gate-Emitter Voltage (VGES): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC):
    - TC = 25°C: 1200 A
    - TC = 100°C: 600 A
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 1200 A (tp = 1ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD): 2770 W (TC = 25°C, TJmax = 150°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (TJmax): 150°C
  - Operating Junction Temperature Range (TJOP): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - The module is designed for easy mounting and connection, with specific screw torque requirements for power terminals (M5: 3.0 to 5.0 N·m; M6: 4.0 to 6.0 N·m).

- **Special Features:**
  - Short Circuit Rated > 10 μs
  - 100% RBSOA Tested (2×Ic)
  - Low Stray Inductance
  - Lead-Free, compliant with RoHS requirements

  - Not specified in the provided data.
