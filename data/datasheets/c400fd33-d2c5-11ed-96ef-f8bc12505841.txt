### Component Summary: ADP1612ARMZ-R7

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (ADP1612): 1.8 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Voltage (ADP1613): 2.5 V to 5.5 V
  - Adjustable Output Voltage: Up to 20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Switch Current Limit (ADP1612): 1.4 A
  - Maximum Switch Current Limit (ADP1613): 2.0 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (Nonswitching State): 900 µA (typical for ADP1612)
  - Shutdown Current: 0.01 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-lead Mini Small Outline Package (MSOP)

- **Special Features:**
  - Pin-selectable switching frequency (650 kHz or 1.3 MHz)
  - Adjustable soft start
  - Undervoltage lockout (UVLO)
  - Thermal shutdown (TSD)
  - High efficiency (up to 94%)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Description:**
The ADP1612ARMZ-R7 is a step-up (boost) DC-to-DC switching converter designed for applications requiring a higher output voltage from a lower input voltage. It features an integrated power switch and operates in current mode pulse-width modulation (PWM) to achieve high efficiency and fast transient response. The device is optimized for space-constrained applications, making it suitable for portable devices and TFT LCD bias supplies.

**Typical Applications:**
- **TFT LCD Bias Supplies:** Provides necessary voltage levels for driving LCD panels.
- **Portable Applications:** Ideal for battery-powered devices where efficient power conversion is critical.
- **Industrial/Instrumentation Equipment:** Used in various industrial applications requiring stable voltage supplies.

The ADP1612/ADP1613 series is supported by Analog Devices' ADIsimPower design tool, which aids in optimizing power designs for specific applications, ensuring efficient and reliable performance.
