### Component Summary: HMC1082LP4ETR

**Description:**
The HMC1082LP4E is a GaAs pHEMT MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) medium power amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 5.5 to 18 GHz. It features an integrated temperature-compensated on-chip power detector, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - DC Supply Voltage (Vdd): +5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 220 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1.81 W (at T = 85 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 24 Lead Lead Frame Chip Scale Package (LFCSP), 4 mm x 4 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - High Saturated Output Power: 26 dBm @ 26% PAE
  - High Output IP3: 35 dBm
  - High Gain: 22 dB
  - Integrated temperature-compensated power detector
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC1082LP4E is ideal for:
- Point-to-Point Radios
- Point-to-Multi-Point Radios
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) & SATCOM (Satellite Communications)
- Marine Radar
- Military Electronic Warfare (EW) & Electronic Countermeasures (ECM)

### Summary:
The HMC1082LP4E is a versatile medium power amplifier that excels in high-frequency applications, providing significant output power and gain while maintaining efficiency. Its compact size and integrated features make it suitable for a wide range of communication and radar systems, particularly in military and commercial sectors.
