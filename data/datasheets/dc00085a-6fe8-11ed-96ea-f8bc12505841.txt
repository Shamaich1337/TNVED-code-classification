The \ncharge pump operates in discontinuous mode; if the \noutput voltages are less than 5.5V, the charge pump \nis enabled, and if the output voltages exceed 5.5V, the charge pump is disabled. Each charge pump requires a \nflying capacitor (C1, C2) and a reservoir capacitor (C3, \nC4) to generate the V+ and V- supplies (Figure 1).\nRS-232 Transmitters\nThe transmitters are inverting level translators that convert TTL/CMOS-logic levels to ±5V EIA/TIA-232-compliant \nlevels.\nThe MAX3222E/MAX3232E/MAX3237E/MAX3241E/ \nMAX3246E transmitters guarantee a 250kbps data rate \nwith worst-case loads of 3kΩ in parallel with 1000pF, \nproviding compatibility with PC-to-PC communication \nsoftware (such as LapLink™). Transmitters can be paral -\nleled to drive multiple receivers or mice.\nThe MAX3222E/MAX3237E/MAX3241E/MAX3246E \ntransmitters are disabled and the outputs are forced into a high-impedance state when the device is in shutdown mode (SHDN = GND). The MAX3222E/MAX3232E/MAX3237E/MAX3241E/MAX3246E permit the outputs to \nbe driven up to ±12V in shutdown.\nThe MAX3222E/MAX3232E/MAX3241E/MAX3246E \ntransmitter inputs do not have pullup resistors. Connect \nunused inputs to GND or V\nCC. The MAX3237E’s transmit-\nter inputs have a 400kΩ active positive-feedback resistor, \nallowing unused inputs to be left unconnected.\nMAX3237E MegaBaud Operation\nFor higher-speed serial communications, the MAX3237E \nfeatures MegaBaud operation. In MegaBaud operating \nmode (MBAUD = V CC), the MAX3237E transmitters guar-\nantee a 1Mbps data rate with worst-case loads of 3kΩ in parallel with 250pF for +3.0V < V\nCC < +4.5V. For +5V \n±10% operation, the MAX3237E transmitters guarantee a 1Mbps data rate into worst-case loads of 3kΩ in parallel \nwith 1000pF.\nRS-232 Receivers\nThe receivers convert RS-232 signals to CMOS-logic \noutput levels. The MAX3222E/MAX3237E/MAX3241E/\nMAX3246E receivers have inverting three-state outputs. Drive EN high to place the receiver(s) into a high-imped -\nance state. The charge pump requires 0.1μF capacitors for \n3.3V operation. For other supply voltages, see Table 2 for required capacitor values. Do not use values smaller than \nthose listed in Table 2. Increasing the capacitor values \n(e.g., by a factor of 2) reduces ripple on the transmitter \noutputs and slightly reduces power consumption. C2, C3, \nand C4 can be increased without changing C1’s value. If in doubt, use capacitors \nwith a larger nominal value. ### Component Summary: MAX3222EEAP+T

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): +3.0V to +5.5V
  - Transmitter Output Voltage Swing: ±5V to ±5.4V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Active): 0.3 mA (MAX3222E), 0.5 mA (MAX3237E)
  - Shutdown Supply Current: 1 µA (MAX3222E), 10 nA (MAX3237E)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption with a shutdown mode reducing current to 1 µA or lower.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 20-Pin SSOP

- **Special Features:**
  - ±15kV ESD protection on all transmitter outputs and receiver inputs.
  - Low-dropout transmitter output stage.
  - Supports data rates up to 250 kbps (1 Mbps in MegaBaud mode for MAX3237E).
  - 1 µA shutdown mode for battery-powered applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Description:**
The MAX3222EEAP+T is a low-power, EIA/TIA-232 and V.28/V.24 communications interface device designed for use in battery-powered applications. It features a dual charge pump that allows it to operate from a low supply voltage while providing true RS-232 output levels. The device includes multiple transmitters and receivers, making it suitable for various serial communication applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- Battery-powered equipment (e.g., portable devices)
- Cell phones and smartphones
- Data cables for cell phones
- Notebook, subnotebook, and palmtop computers
- Printers
- xDSL modems

The MAX3222EEAP+T is particularly useful in applications where low power consumption and high ESD protection are critical, such as in mobile devices and communication peripherals. Its ability to maintain RS-232 levels at high data rates makes it a versatile choice for modern electronic communication systems.
