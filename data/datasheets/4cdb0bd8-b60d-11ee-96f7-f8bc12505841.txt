#### Description:
The ARJ-139 is an RJ45 connector designed for 1000 Base-T Ethernet applications. It features Power over Ethernet (PoE++) capabilities, allowing it to deliver power and data over the same cable. The component includes bi-color LEDs for visual status indication and is engineered to provide signal conditioning, electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression, and signal isolation, making it suitable for high-performance networking applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The ARJ-139 is typically used in:
- **Ethernet Switches**: Facilitating high-speed data transfer and power delivery.
- **Servers**: Providing network connectivity with power capabilities.
- **Access Point Routers**: Enabling wireless network access while supporting PoE for powering devices.
- **Network Interface Cards (NIC)**: Integrating into computers and devices for network connectivity.
- **LAN-on-Motherboard (LOM)**: Directly embedding into motherboards for compact designs.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust network performance and reliability, particularly in environments with extended temperature ranges.
