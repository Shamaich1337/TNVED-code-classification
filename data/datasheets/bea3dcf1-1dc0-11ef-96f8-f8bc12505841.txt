### Component Summary: APGA1602CGC/KA

**Description:**
The APGA1602CGC/KA is a right-angle surface-mount device (SMD) LED, specifically a green light-emitting diode (LED) made from AlGaInP on a GaAs substrate. It is designed for low power consumption and features a wide viewing angle, making it suitable for various applications such as backlighting and status indicators.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 20mA: 2.05V (typical) to 2.4V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 20mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 100mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 48mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 1.6 x 0.9 x 0.2 mm (Right Angle SMD)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (150°)
  - Tinned pads for improved solderability
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
- Backlighting for displays
- Status indicators in consumer electronics
- Home and smart appliances
- Wearable and portable devices
- Healthcare applications

**Additional Notes:**
- The device is sensitive to electrostatic discharge (ESD), with a threshold of 3000V (HBM). Proper handling precautions, such as using wrist bands or anti-static gloves, are recommended.
- The luminous intensity at 20mA is specified to be between 12 mcd (minimum) and 30 mcd (typical), with a dominant wavelength of 571 nm.
- The LED is designed for reflow soldering, and care should be taken to avoid stress during high-temperature exposure.

This concise summary provides an overview of the APGA1602CGC/KA LED's specifications and applications, ensuring that users can effectively integrate it into their designs.
