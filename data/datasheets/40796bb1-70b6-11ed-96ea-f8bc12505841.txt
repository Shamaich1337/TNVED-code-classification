#### Description:
The FL-3505S-FBS is part of the FL-3500 series of uncooled multi-quantum well (MQW) Fabry-Pérot (FP) laser diode modules designed for coupling with single-mode optical fibers. The module includes a built-in InGaAs photodiode for monitoring purposes.

#### Typical Applications:
The FL-3505S-FBS is primarily used in telecommunications and data communications (datacom) applications. It serves as a light source for trunk lines and fiber in the loop (FitL) systems, making it suitable for high-speed data transmission over optical fibers. Its ability to operate at high temperatures without active cooling makes it ideal for various environmental conditions in network infrastructure.
