### Component Summary: 1.5SMC36A/TR13

**Description:**
The 1.5SMC36A is a Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) designed for circuit protection applications. It is specifically used to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and transients, ensuring reliable operation in various electronic circuits.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-Off Voltage (VRWM): 36V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 30.80V (typical)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 36A (10/1000μs waveform)
  - Steady State Power Dissipation (PM(AV)): 6.5W at 50°C
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPPM): 1500W (10/1000μs waveform)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature: -55°C to +150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Case: JEDEC DO-214AB (Surface Mount)
  - Weight: 0.26g
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package with built-in strain relief
  - Glass passivated junction for enhanced reliability
  - Low inductance and excellent clamping capability
  - Fast response time with typical reverse leakage current (IR) less than 1μA above 12V
  - High-temperature soldering capability (260°C for 10 seconds)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL Level 1 (per J-STD-020E)
  - Safety certification: UL E244458
  - Compliance with IEC61000-4-2 ESD standards (30kV air, 30kV contact)

**Typical Applications:**
The 1.5SMC36A is commonly used in:
- AC/DC power supplies
- I/O interfaces
- Low-frequency signal transmission lines (e.g., RS232, RS485)
- General circuit protection in consumer electronics and industrial applications

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against voltage transients, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.
