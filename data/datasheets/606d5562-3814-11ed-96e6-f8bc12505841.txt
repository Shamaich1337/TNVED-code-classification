### Component Summary: ZPY100-TR (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Zener voltage range from 3.9V to 100V (specific for ZPY100: 94V to 106V)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Test current (I_ZT): 5 to 100 mA
  - Admissible Zener current: Up to 130 mA (for ZPY100)
- **Power Consumption**: Maximum power dissipation (P_tot): 1300 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction temperature (T_j): -55°C to +175°C
  - Storage temperature (T_stg): -55°C to +175°C
- **Package Type**: DO-41 (DO-204AL)
- **Special Features**: 
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (suitable for automotive applications)
  - Graded Zener voltages according to the international E12 standard
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL level 1 (according to J-STD-020)
- **Soldering Conditions**: Peak temperature max. 260°C

#### Description:
The ZPY100-TR is a silicon planar power Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and protection applications. It is primarily used to stabilize voltage levels in electronic circuits, providing a reference voltage and protecting sensitive components from voltage spikes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation**: Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Clipping Circuits**: Protects circuits from overvoltage conditions by clamping the voltage to a specified level.
- **Signal Processing**: Utilized in signal conditioning applications where voltage stabilization is critical.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics, ensuring reliability under harsh conditions.

This component is ideal for engineers looking for robust voltage regulation solutions in both consumer and automotive electronics.
