### Component Summary: NUF2042XV6T1G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 5.25 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR @ 1 mA): 5.6 to 6.8 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Leakage Current (IR @ 3.3 V): 100 nA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Steady State Power (PD): 225 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-563 (6-lead)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated USB line termination, EMI filtering, and ESD protection.
  - Compliance with IEC61000−4−2 (Level 4) for ESD protection: 8 kV (Contact), 15 kV (Air).
  - Pb-Free device.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOT-563 packages are classified under MSL 1 or MSL 2.

#### Description:
The NUF2042XV6T1G is a USB upstream terminator designed to provide line termination, EMI filtering, and ESD protection in a compact SOT-563 package. This integrated circuit solution is particularly beneficial for applications that require reliable performance in environments susceptible to electrical noise and electrostatic discharge.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Hubs:** Ensures stable communication and protection against ESD.
- **Computer Peripherals Using USB:** Enhances reliability and reduces the risk of damage from electrical surges.
- **Cellular Phones and Wireless Equipment:** Provides necessary filtering and protection in portable devices.
- **General Electronic Applications:** Suitable for any application requiring robust ESD protection and signal integrity.

This component is ideal for designers looking to minimize PCB space while enhancing system reliability through integrated solutions rather than discrete components.
