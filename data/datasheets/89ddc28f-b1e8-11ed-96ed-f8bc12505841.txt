### Component Summary: YJN180G10HJ

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** N-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 20A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: 12A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at case temperature (TC) 25°C: 180A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 600A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 5W
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 100°C: 2W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** TO-247AB
- **Special Features:**
  - Low RDS(ON) at VGS=10V: <4.3mΩ
  - 100% EAS Tested
  - 100% VDS Tested
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Halogen-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The YJN180G10HJ is an N-Channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for high-efficiency power switching applications. It utilizes split gate trench technology, which allows for excellent heat dissipation and low on-resistance, making it suitable for high current applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Switching Applications:** The YJN180G10HJ is ideal for use in power management circuits where efficient switching is critical.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** It can be used in UPS systems to manage power flow and ensure reliability during power outages.
- **DC-DC Converters:** This MOSFET is suitable for DC-DC conversion applications, providing efficient voltage regulation and power conversion.

This component is particularly valuable in applications requiring high current handling and low power loss, making it a preferred choice for modern electronic designs focused on efficiency and reliability.
