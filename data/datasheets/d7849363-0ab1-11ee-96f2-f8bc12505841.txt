### Component Summary: AG302-86G (Qorvo, Inc.)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - DC Voltage: +5 V (maximum)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Device Current: 35 mA (typical)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Supply Voltage: +5 V, resulting in a power consumption of approximately 175 mW (5 V * 35 mA).
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Storage Temperature: -55 to +125 °C
  - Junction Temperature: +177 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT-86 (lead-free/green/RoHS-compliant)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Internally matched to 50 Ω
  - Robust ESD protection (1000V, Class 1C)
  - High reliability with MTTF values exceeding 1000 years at +85 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 3 at +260 °C convection reflow (JEDEC J-STD-020)

#### Component Description:
The AG302-86G is an InGaP HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) gain block amplifier designed for high-frequency applications. It operates over a broad frequency range from DC to 6000 MHz and is optimized for wireless communication systems. The device features a Darlington-pair amplifier configuration, providing high gain and dynamic range while maintaining low noise figures.

#### Typical Applications:
The AG302-86G is suitable for various applications, including:
- **Mobile Infrastructure**: Enhancing signal strength and quality in cellular networks.
- **CATV / FTTX**: Used in cable television and fiber-to-the-x applications for signal amplification.
- **WLAN / ISM**: Suitable for wireless local area networks and industrial, scientific, and medical bands.
- **RFID**: Employed in radio-frequency identification systems for improved read ranges.
- **WiMAX / WiBro**: Utilized in broadband wireless access technologies.

This component is ideal for designers looking for a reliable and efficient amplifier solution in modern wireless communication systems.
