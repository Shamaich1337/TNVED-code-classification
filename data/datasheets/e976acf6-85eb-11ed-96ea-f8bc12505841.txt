### Component Summary: MC74LCX541DTR2G

**Description:**
The MC74LCX541 is a low-voltage CMOS octal buffer with a flow-through pinout, designed for high performance in digital applications. It operates with a supply voltage range of 2.3 to 3.6 V and features 5 V-tolerant inputs and outputs, making it compatible with both LVTTL and LVCMOS logic levels. This device is particularly useful for memory address driving and TTL-level bus-oriented transceiver applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.3 to 3.6 V
  - Maximum DC Supply Voltage: -0.5 to +7.0 V
  - Input Voltage (VI): -0.5 to +7.0 V
  - Output Voltage (VO): -0.5 to VCC + 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Drive Capability: ±24 mA
  - DC Supply Current (per supply pin): ±100 mA
  - Input Diode Current: -50 mA (for VI < GND), +50 mA (for VO > VCC)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 10 µA (typical at VCC = 3.6 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Free-Air Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in TSSOP-20 and SOIC-20 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - 5 V tolerant inputs and outputs.
  - Supports live insertion and withdrawal.
  - IOFF specification guarantees high impedance when VCC = 0 V.
  - Near zero static supply current in all three logic states.
  - Latchup performance exceeds 500 mA.
  - ESD performance: Human Body Model > 2000 V, Machine Model > 200 V.
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).

**Typical Applications:**
The MC74LCX541 is commonly used in:
- Memory address driving.
- TTL-level bus-oriented transceiver applications.
- General-purpose buffering in digital circuits.
- Applications requiring interfacing between 3.3 V and 5 V logic levels.

This component is ideal for use in systems where low power consumption and high-speed operation are critical, such as in portable devices, automotive applications, and consumer electronics.
