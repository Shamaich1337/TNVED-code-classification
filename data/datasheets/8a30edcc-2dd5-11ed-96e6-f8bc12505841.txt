#### Description:
The **CLVC1G125IDCKREP** is a single bus buffer gate with a 3-state output designed for low-voltage applications. It operates within a supply voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V and is suitable for interfacing between different voltage levels. The device features a high-impedance state that is controlled by an output-enable (OE) input, allowing for flexible signal routing in digital circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The CLVC1G125 is commonly used in:
- **Signal buffering**: To drive signals over longer distances without degradation.
- **Level shifting**: To interface between different logic levels in mixed-voltage systems.
- **Data communication**: In applications where multiple devices share a common bus.
- **Power management**: In systems requiring low power consumption during idle states.

This component is ideal for use in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and any digital circuit requiring reliable signal integrity and low power operation.
