#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Maximum reverse voltage (V_R) of 45V.
- **Current Ratings**: Maximum forward current (I_F) of 1A.
- **Power Consumption**: Power dissipation (P_D) of 200mW.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +150°C.
- **Package Type**: SOD123F.
- **Special Features**: Low forward voltage drop, fast switching speed.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: MSL 1 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The BAS16H.115 is a small-signal Schottky diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It features a low forward voltage drop and is optimized for use in various electronic circuits where fast response times are critical. The SOD123F package allows for surface mounting, making it suitable for compact designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in power supply circuits for rectification and voltage regulation.
- **Signal Processing**: Ideal for RF applications and signal clamping.
- **Data Storage**: Utilized in read/write circuits for memory devices.
- **General Purpose Switching**: Suitable for various switching applications in consumer electronics.

This component is particularly valued in applications requiring efficient power conversion and minimal signal distortion, making it a versatile choice for engineers in the electronics industry.
