### Component Summary: 74HC4051D,653

**General Description:**
The 74HC4051D,653 is an 8-channel analog multiplexer/demultiplexer (SP8T switch) designed for use in both analog and digital applications. It allows for the selection of one of eight inputs to be routed to a common output, or vice versa. The device features three digital select inputs and an enable input to control the switching operation.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): -0.5 V to +11.0 V
  - Input Voltage (VI): GND to VCC
  - Switch Voltage (VSW): VEE to VCC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input Clamping Current (IIK): ±20 mA
  - Switch Clamping Current (ISK): ±20 mA
  - Switch Current (ISW): ±25 mA
  - Supply Current (IEE): ±20 mA
  - Supply Current (ICC): 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 500 mW (for SO16, TSSOP16, and DHVQFN16 packages)
  - Power Dissipation per Switch: 100 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages: SO16, SSOP16, TSSOP16, and DHVQFN16.

- **Special Features:**
  - Low ON resistance: 
    - 80 Ω (typical) at VCC - VEE = 4.5 V
    - 70 Ω (typical) at VCC - VEE = 6.0 V
    - 60 Ω (typical) at VCC - VEE = 9.0 V
  - ESD protection: 
    - HBM JESD22-A114F exceeds 2000 V
    - MM JESD22-A115-A exceeds 200 V
    - CDM JESD22-C101E exceeds 1000 V
  - Logic level translation capability for interfacing 5 V logic with ±5 V analog signals.
  - Built-in 'break before make' feature.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
- Analog multiplexing and demultiplexing
- Digital multiplexing and demultiplexing
- Signal gating
- Data acquisition systems
- Audio signal routing
- Communication systems

This component is ideal for applications requiring the selection of multiple analog or digital signals, providing flexibility in circuit design and efficient signal management.
