### Component Summary: BZT52B5V6SQ

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** BZT52B5V6SQ  
**Type:** Small Signal Zener Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Zener Voltage (Vz):** 5.6V (typical)
- **Voltage Ratings:** 3.6V to 36V (various models in the series)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Maximum Regulator Current (IZM):** Calculated as PD/Vz, where PD is 300 mW.
  - **Maximum Forward Voltage (VF) at IF=10mA:** 0.9V
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - **Power Dissipation (PD):** 300 mW (derates to 2.4 mW/°C above 25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Storage Temperature:** -55°C to +150°C
  - **Junction Temperature (TJ):** -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** SOD323
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Level 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Rating of Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (indicated by suffix “Q”)

#### Description:
The BZT52B5V6SQ is a small signal Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It provides a stable reference voltage and is capable of clamping voltage spikes, making it essential in protecting sensitive electronic components from overvoltage conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant voltage level.
- **Voltage Clamping:** Protects circuits from voltage transients.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Used in analog circuits to stabilize voltage levels.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Suitable for automotive applications due to AEC-Q101 qualification.

This component is particularly useful in applications where precise voltage regulation is required, such as in power management systems, signal processing circuits, and various consumer electronics.
