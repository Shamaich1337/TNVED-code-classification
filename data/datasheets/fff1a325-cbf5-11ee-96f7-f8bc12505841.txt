  - Low insertion loss (2.0 dB typical).
  - Sharp rejection peaks close to the stop band.
  - Wrap-around terminations for excellent solderability.
  - Hermetically sealed for ruggedness in harsh environments.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **BFCN-8000+** is a **LTCC Band Pass Filter** designed for RF applications, specifically operating within the frequency range of **7900 to 8100 MHz**. Its compact size and advanced construction allow for high performance and reliability in various electronic systems. The filter is engineered to minimize insertion loss while providing effective signal rejection outside its pass band.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Harmonic Rejection**: Used in systems where unwanted harmonic frequencies need to be filtered out.
- **Transmitters and Receivers**: Ideal for RF communication systems, ensuring that only the desired frequency range is transmitted or received.
- **High-Density Circuit Boards**: Its small size allows for efficient use of space in compact electronic designs.
- **High Humidity and Temperature Environments**: The rugged LTCC construction makes it suitable for applications in challenging conditions.

This component is particularly useful in telecommunications, satellite communications, and other RF applications where precise frequency filtering is critical for system performance.
