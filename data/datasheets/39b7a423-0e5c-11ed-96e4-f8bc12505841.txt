### Component Summary: PZT3904 Transistor (NPN)

**Manufacturer:** CHANGJIANG ELECTRONICS TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD (CJ)

**Description:**
The PZT3904 is a low voltage, low current NPN transistor designed for general-purpose amplification and switching applications. It is complementary to the PZT3906 transistor, making it suitable for various electronic circuits requiring NPN transistor functionality.

### Key Specifications:

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Base Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)CBO): 60 V
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)CEO): 40 V
  - Emitter-Base Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)EBO): 6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Collector Current (IC): 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Collector Power Dissipation (PC): 1 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-223 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low voltage and low current operation
  - Suitable for general-purpose amplifier and switch applications
  - Complementary to PZT3906

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

### Typical Applications:
The PZT3904 is commonly used in:
- **Signal Amplification:** It can amplify weak signals in audio and radio frequency applications.
- **Switching Circuits:** It is suitable for use in electronic switching applications, such as in relay drivers and digital logic circuits.
- **General Purpose Electronics:** Its versatility allows it to be used in various electronic devices, including consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial control systems.

This transistor is ideal for designers looking for a reliable and efficient component for low-power applications, providing a balance of performance and ease of use in circuit design.
