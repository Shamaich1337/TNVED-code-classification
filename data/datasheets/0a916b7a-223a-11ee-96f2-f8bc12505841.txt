### Component Summary: ADG819BRTZ-REEL7

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 1.8 V to 5.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings: VDD to GND: -0.3 V to +7 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current: 200 mA
  - Peak Current (pulsed at 1 ms, 10% duty cycle): 400 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: <0.01 µW
  - IDD (at VDD = 5.5 V): 1.0 µA max

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C
  - Automotive: -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 6-lead SOT-23, 8-lead MSOP, and 6-ball WLCSP packages.

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Low on-resistance: 0.5 Ω typical, 0.8 Ω maximum at 125°C
  - Fast switching times: tON = 35 ns (typ), tOFF = 10 ns (typ)
  - Break-before-make switching action to prevent shorting
  - TTL-/CMOS-compatible inputs
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The ADG819 is a monolithic, CMOS, single-pole, double-throw (SPDT) switch designed for low power dissipation while providing high switching speed, low on-resistance, and low leakage currents. It is particularly suitable for battery-powered and portable instruments due to its low power consumption and wide operating voltage range.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power routing in various electronic devices
- Battery-powered systems where low power consumption is critical
- Communication systems, including cellular phones and modems
- Data acquisition systems
- PCMCIA cards and hard drives
- Relay replacement in applications requiring compact and efficient switching solutions

This component is ideal for applications that require efficient signal routing with minimal power loss and high reliability across a range of operating conditions.
