The WR-FPC SMT ZIF Horizontal Hinge is a type of zero insertion force (ZIF) connector designed for flexible printed circuits (FPC). It features a bottom contact configuration with a pitch of 0.5 mm, allowing for easy connection and disconnection of FPCs without the need for excessive force. This component is primarily used in applications where space is limited and a reliable connection is required.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Working Voltage: 50 V (AC)
  - Withstanding Voltage: 150 V (AC) for 1 minute

- **Current Ratings:**

  Not specified directly, but the current rating indicates the maximum current the connector can handle.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**  
  -40 °C to +105 °C

- **Package Type:**  
  Surface Mount Technology (SMT) with a tape and reel packaging for automated assembly.

  - Durability: 20 mating cycles
  - Insulator Material: PA6T with a flammability rating of UL94 V-0
  - Contact Material: Phosphor Bronze with tin over nickel plating
  - Insulation Resistance: 500 MΩ minimum
  - Contact Resistance: 50 mΩ maximum

  Not explicitly stated, but typically SMT components are classified based on their sensitivity to moisture.
