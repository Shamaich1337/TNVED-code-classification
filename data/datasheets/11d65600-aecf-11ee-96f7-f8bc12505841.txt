The A2412S-2WR3 is a DC-DC converter from the MORNSUN A_S-2WR3 series. It is designed to provide isolated dual output voltages from a stable input voltage source. This component is particularly useful in applications where a reliable and regulated power supply is required, with the added benefit of electrical isolation between input and output.

- Applications requiring input-to-output isolation voltage of up to 1500 VDC.
- Systems where the input voltage is relatively stable with minimal variation (±10%).
- Power management in telecommunications, industrial automation, and medical devices where dual output voltages are needed.
