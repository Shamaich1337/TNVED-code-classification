### Component Summary: BZB784-C6V8,115

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Nominal Zener Voltage (VZ): 6.8 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Non-repetitive Peak Reverse Current (IZSM): 40 A (tp = 100 μs)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 350 mW (2 diodes loaded), 180 mW (1 diode loaded)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -150 °C to +200 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT323 (SC-70) - a small surface-mounted package with 3 leads
- **Special Features or Notes**: 
  - Approx. 5% VZ tolerance
  - Suitable for general regulation functions and ESD/surge protection
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The BZB784-C6V8 is a low-power voltage regulator diode, part of the BZB784 series from Nexperia. It is designed to provide stable voltage regulation in electronic circuits. The component features a common anode configuration with two cathodes, allowing it to regulate voltage effectively while also providing protection against voltage spikes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage despite variations in input voltage or load conditions.
- **ESD and Surge Protection**: Protects sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and voltage surges, making it suitable for consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial equipment.

This component is ideal for applications requiring reliable voltage regulation and protection in compact electronic designs.
