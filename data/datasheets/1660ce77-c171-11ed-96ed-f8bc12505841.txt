### Component Summary: PMV65XP

**General Description:**
The PMV65XP is a P-channel enhancement mode Field-Effect Transistor (FET) utilizing Trench MOSFET technology. It is packaged in a compact SOT23 (TO-236AB) surface-mounted device (SMD) plastic package. This component is designed for low power applications, providing efficient switching capabilities.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): -20 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): -12 V to 12 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): -4.3 A (at VGS = -4.5 V, Tj = 25 °C)
  - Peak Drain Current (IDM): -16 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 10 µs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 480 mW (T_amb = 25 °C), 833 mW (T_sp = 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB), 3 leads

- **Special Features:**
  - Low threshold voltage
  - Low on-state resistance (RDS(on)): 58 mΩ (typical at VGS = -4.5 V, ID = -2.8 A, Tj = 25 °C)
  - Trench MOSFET technology for improved efficiency

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Description:**
The PMV65XP is a P-channel MOSFET designed for efficient switching in low power applications. Its low on-state resistance and low threshold voltage make it suitable for various electronic circuits where power efficiency is critical.

**Typical Applications:**
- Low power DC-to-DC converters
- Load switching applications
- Battery management systems
- Battery-powered portable equipment

This component is ideal for use in applications requiring reliable switching performance and low power consumption, making it a versatile choice for designers in the electronics industry.
