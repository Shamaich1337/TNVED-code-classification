### Component Summary: CC0805KKX5R6BB106 (YAGEO)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 4V to 50V
- **Capacitance Range**: 100 pF to 220 µF
- **Capacitance Tolerance**: ±10% and ±20%
- **Power Consumption**: Not applicable (passive component)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 0805 (2.0 mm x 1.25 mm)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features**: 
  - RoHS compliant
  - Halogen free
  - Nickel-barrier end termination
  - Supplied in tape on reel for automated assembly

#### Description:
The CC0805KKX5R6BB106 is a surface mount multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) from YAGEO's X5R series. It is designed for general-purpose applications and high capacitance needs. The capacitor features a ceramic dielectric material that allows for a compact design while providing a high capacitance per unit volume. The X5R dielectric class offers stable capacitance over a wide temperature range, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in power supply circuits to smooth voltage fluctuations and filter noise.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in devices such as PCs, hard disk drives, game consoles, DVD players, and mobile phones.
- **Data Processing**: Employed in circuits that require stable capacitance for signal integrity and performance.
- **Decoupling and Bypass**: Utilized to decouple AC signals from DC power supplies, ensuring stable operation of sensitive components.

This capacitor is ideal for applications where space is limited, and reliability is critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
