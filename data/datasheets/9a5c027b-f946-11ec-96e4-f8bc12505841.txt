### Component Summary: PGA-105+

**Description:**
The PGA-105+ is a monolithic amplifier designed for ultra-flat gain and low noise performance. It is fabricated using E-PHEMT technology, which allows for high dynamic range and low noise figure across a broad frequency range. The amplifier is particularly noted for its excellent gain flatness and does not require external matching components, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 4.8 to 5.2 V (typical 5 V)
  - Maximum DC Voltage on Pin 3: 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 63 to 77 mA (typical 77 mA at 5 V)
  - Current Variation vs. Temperature: 67 µA/°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.47 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89 (Case Style DF782)

- **Special Features:**
  - Gain Flatness: ±0.25 dB over 0.1 to 2.0 GHz
  - Low Noise Figure: 1.7 to 2.0 dB (typical 1.9 dB at 2 GHz)
  - High IP3: 39 dBm (typical at 0.9 GHz)
  - No external matching components required
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Typical Applications:**
The PGA-105+ is ideal for a variety of applications, including:
- Base station infrastructure
- Portable wireless devices
- CATV (Cable Television) and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) and Wireless LAN
- LTE (Long-Term Evolution) systems

This amplifier is particularly suited for use in driver amplifiers for complex waveform upconverter paths, linearized transmit systems, and secondary amplifiers in ultra-high dynamic range receivers, where gain flatness and low noise are critical for performance.
