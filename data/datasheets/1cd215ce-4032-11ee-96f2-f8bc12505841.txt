#### Key Specifications:

  - Input Voltage Range: -0.5 V to 6.5 V
  - Output Voltage Range: -0.5 V to V_CC + 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Input Clamp Current: -50 mA
  - Output Clamp Current: -50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 500 mW (at T_A = -40°C to 125°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C (standard)
  - -40°C to 125°C (extended)

- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (RGY) with 14 pins

- **Special Features:**
  - Inputs accept voltages up to 5.5 V, allowing for compatibility with both 3.3 V and 5 V devices.
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 250 mA per JESD 17.
  - ESD protection exceeds JESD 22 standards (2000 V Human-Body Model, 200 V Machine Model).
  - Typical propagation delay (t_pd) of 4.9 ns at 3.3 V.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The **SN74LVC10A** is a **triple 3-input positive-NAND gate** designed for low-voltage operation. It performs the Boolean function Y = A • B • C or Y = A + B + C in positive logic. This component is particularly useful in digital circuits where logic operations are required. The device is capable of operating in mixed-voltage environments, making it suitable for interfacing between 3.3 V and 5 V systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Logic Level Translation:** The ability to accept input voltages up to 5.5 V allows the SN74LVC10A to serve as a level translator between different voltage domains.
- **Digital Signal Processing:** Used in various digital circuits for implementing logic functions.
- **Control Systems:** Can be utilized in control applications where multiple inputs need to be processed to produce a single output.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices requiring logic gates for signal processing and control functions.

This component is ideal for applications in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and any digital logic design requiring reliable and fast NAND gate functionality.
