### Component Summary: HMC732LC4B

**Description:**
The HMC732LC4B is a wideband MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) with a buffer amplifier. It operates within a frequency range of 6 to 12 GHz and is designed to provide excellent output power and phase noise performance due to its monolithic construction. The device accepts an analog tuning voltage from 0 to +23V and operates from a single +5V supply.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 0 to +23V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 57 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - 285 mW (calculated from Vcc and Icc)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 24 Lead Ceramic SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package, dimensions 4x4 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - Low SSB Phase Noise: -95 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - No external resonator needed
  - Wide tuning bandwidth
  - Output power: +1 dBm
  - Pulling: 7 MHz pp
  - Pushing: -85 MHz/V @ Vtune= +20V, F = 12 GHz
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC732LC4B is ideal for various applications, including:
- **Industrial/Medical Equipment:** Used in devices requiring precise frequency control and low noise.
- **Test & Measurement Equipment:** Suitable for applications needing accurate signal generation and analysis.
- **Military Radar, Electronic Warfare (EW), and Electronic Countermeasures (ECM):** Provides reliable performance in demanding environments.

This component is particularly valued for its compact size, low power consumption, and wide tuning range, making it a versatile choice for high-frequency applications.
