### Component Summary: LFCG-2850+

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; typically handled through RF power input.
- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; inferred from RF power handling.
- **Power Consumption:** RF Power Input: 4.5 W max at 25°C (derates to 1 W at 125°C).
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 125°C.
- **Package Type:** 0805 ceramic (2.0 mm x 1.25 mm x 0.95 mm).
- **Special Features:**
  - Very good stopband rejection (45 dB typical).
  - Low insertion loss (0.9 dB typical).
  - Rugged LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) construction.
  - Wrap-around terminations for excellent solderability.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The LFCG-2850+ is a low pass filter designed for RF applications, specifically operating from DC to 2850 MHz. It is constructed using LTCC technology, which provides durability and stability in various environmental conditions. The filter is compact, making it suitable for high-density PCB layouts, and it minimizes performance variations due to parasitic effects.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Harmonic Rejection:** Used in systems requiring suppression of unwanted harmonic frequencies.
- **VHF/UHF Transmitters/Receivers:** Ideal for communication systems operating in the VHF and UHF bands.
- **Laboratory Use:** Suitable for experimental setups where precise frequency filtering is necessary.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited and high performance is required, such as in telecommunications, broadcasting, and RF signal processing.
