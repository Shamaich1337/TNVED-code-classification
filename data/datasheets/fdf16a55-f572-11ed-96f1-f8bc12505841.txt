**Description:**
The PSDH6060CCS1 is a fast recovery diode designed for high-performance applications. It features a short reverse recovery time, making it suitable for applications where efficiency and reduced switching losses are critical. The diode is housed in a TO-247AD-3LD package, which is a molded plastic case that allows for effective heat dissipation.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 600 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 600 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Diode Forward Current (IF(AV)): 30 A (per leg) at T_C = 115°C
  - Maximum Forward Current (Both Legs): 60 A
  - Repetitive Peak Surge Current (IFRM): 60 A (tp = 8.3 ms)
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 175 A (tp = 8.3 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (Ptotal): 156 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247AD-3LD molded plastic

- **Special Features:**
  - Fast recovery with suppressed switching loss
  - Soft recovery characteristics for improved EMI performance
  - High junction temperature rating of 150°C
  - Lead-free and compliant with EU RoHS 2.0
  - Green molding compound as per IEC 61249 standard

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The PSDH6060CCS1 diode is commonly used in various high-power applications, including:
- Power Factor Correction (PFC)
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Photovoltaic (PV) Inverters
- Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Stations
- Welding equipment

This diode is particularly advantageous in circuits where fast switching and low recovery times are essential, contributing to overall system efficiency and performance.
