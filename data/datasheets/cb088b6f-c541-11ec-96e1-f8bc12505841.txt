### Component Summary: LP2985AIM5-2.8/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.5V to 16V
  - Output Voltage: Fixed at 2.8V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 150 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: <1 µA (shutdown mode), 75 µA (at 1 mA load), 850 µA (at 150 mA load)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (5 pins)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low-Dropout Voltage: 300 mV at 150 mA load
  - Stable with Low-ESR Output Capacitors (as low as 5 mΩ)
  - Overtemperature and Overcurrent Protection
  - Low Output Noise: 30 µVRMS with a 10 nF bypass capacitor
  - ON/OFF control pin for enabling/disabling the regulator
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **LP2985-N** is a low-noise, ultra-low-dropout (LDO) linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage of 2.8V with a maximum output current of 150 mA. It features a very low dropout voltage of only 300 mV, making it suitable for battery-powered applications where efficiency is critical. The device is optimized for use with low equivalent series resistance (ESR) output capacitors, allowing for compact designs with minimal external components.

#### Typical Applications:
The LP2985-N is commonly used in various portable and battery-operated devices, including:
- **Cellular Phones**
- **Palmtop and Laptop Computers**
- **Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs)**
- **Camcorders and Personal Stereos**
- **Cameras**

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise voltage regulation with low noise and minimal power consumption, making it suitable for sensitive electronic devices.
