### Component Summary: SP3002-04JTG (Littelfuse)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 6.0 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Current (tp=8/20μs): 4.5 A
  - Reverse Leakage Current (ILEAK at 5V): 0.5 μA (max)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low leakage current, ensuring minimal power loss during operation.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to 125 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -55 to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SC70-6 (6 pins) or μDFN-6 (1.6x1.6x0.5 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low capacitance of 0.85 pF (typical) per I/O.
  - ESD protection of ±12 kV (contact discharge) and ±15 kV (air discharge) per IEC 61000-4-2.
  - EFT protection (IEC 61000-4-4) of 40A (5/50ns).
  - Lightning protection (IEC 61000-4-5) of 4.5A (8/20µs).
  - Halogen-free, lead-free, and RoHS compliant.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E is not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The SP3002-04JTG is a **TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) Diode Array** designed for low capacitance ESD protection. It features rail-to-rail diodes with an additional zener diode, utilizing proprietary silicon avalanche technology. This component is specifically engineered to protect sensitive electronic equipment from destructive electrostatic discharges (ESD), making it suitable for high-speed signal applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The SP3002-04JTG is commonly used in:
- **Computer Peripherals:** Protecting data lines in devices like keyboards and mice.
- **Mobile Phones:** Safeguarding sensitive components from ESD.
- **PDAs and Digital Cameras:** Ensuring reliability in portable electronics.
- **Network Hardware/Ports:** Protecting interfaces such as USB, HDMI, and DVI.
- **Test Equipment:** Providing robust protection in measurement devices.
- **Medical Equipment:** Ensuring the integrity of sensitive medical electronics.

This component is ideal for applications where high-speed data transmission is critical, and where ESD protection is necessary to maintain device performance and longevity.
