The TCM0605G-900-2P-T201 is a thin-film common mode filter designed for high-speed differential signal lines. The component is compact, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This common mode filter is primarily used for noise countermeasures in high-speed differential interfaces such as:
- USB 2.0
- LVDS (Low-Voltage Differential Signaling)
- MIPI (Mobile Industry Processor Interface)

Typical applications include mobile devices (smartphones, tablets), digital cameras, and portable music players, where maintaining signal integrity and reducing noise is critical for performance.
