### Component Summary: ERA-4SM+

**Description:**
The ERA-4SM+ is a surface mount monolithic amplifier designed for high-frequency applications. It utilizes InGaP HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) technology and is configured in a Darlington arrangement, providing a wide bandwidth and high dynamic range. The amplifier is RoHS compliant and is packaged in a Micro-X case style (WW107).

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 4.3 V to 4.9 V (typical operating voltage is 4.6 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 65 mA (maximum 85 mA)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 417 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Micro-X package (WW107), plastic, with a body diameter of 0.085 inches
  
- **Special Features:**
  - DC-4 GHz frequency range
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - Low performance variation over temperature
  - Transient protected
  - Aqueous washable
  - Protected by US Patent 6,943,629
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020

**Typical Applications:**
The ERA-4SM+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular/PCS/3G base stations
- CATV (Cable Television), cable modems, and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- Fixed wireless and WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network)
- Microwave radio and test equipment

This amplifier is particularly useful in scenarios where high gain and low noise are critical, making it ideal for signal amplification in communication systems and RF applications.
