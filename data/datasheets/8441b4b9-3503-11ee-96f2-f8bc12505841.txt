### Component Summary: ESDS302DBVR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VRWM): 3.6V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBRF): 4.5V (minimum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Surge Current: 12A (8/20μs)
  - Electrical Fast Transient (EFT) Peak Current: 80A (5/50 ns)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Surge Power: 85W (maximum)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 5-Pin SOT-23 (DBV)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - IEC 61000-4-2 Level 4 ESD Protection: ±30kV Contact Discharge, ±30kV Air Gap Discharge
  - Ultra Low Leakage Current: 3nA (typical)
  - Supports High-Speed Interfaces up to 1Gbps
  - Low Surge Clamping Voltage: 6V at 12A Ipp
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **ESDS302DBVR** is a bidirectional Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) ESD protection diode array designed for high-speed data lines. It is specifically engineered to protect sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and surge events. The device features ultra-low capacitance, making it suitable for high-speed applications such as Ethernet and USB interfaces.

#### Typical Applications:
- **End Equipment:**
  - Ethernet Switches
  - Access Points
  - Gateways
  - Printers
  - Digital Video Recorders (DVR) and Network Video Recorders (NVR)

- **Interfaces:**
  - Ethernet (10/100/1000 Mbps)
  - USB 2.0
  - General Purpose Input/Output (GPIO)

The ESDS302DBVR is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against ESD and surge events, ensuring reliable operation of high-speed data communication systems. Its compact SOT-23 package allows for easy integration into various electronic designs, making it a versatile choice for engineers looking to enhance the reliability of their products.
