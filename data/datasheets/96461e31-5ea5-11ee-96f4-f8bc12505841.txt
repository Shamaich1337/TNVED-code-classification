### Component Summary: LFCN-6400D+

**Description:**
The LFCN-6400D+ is a ceramic low pass filter designed for RF applications. It operates effectively from DC to 6400 MHz, providing excellent performance in terms of insertion loss and power handling. The filter is constructed using Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) technology, which ensures stability and reliability in various operating conditions. 

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Max DC Voltage at pins 1 & 3: 25 VDC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Current Input to Output: 0.5 A max at 25°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Power Input: 38 W max at 25°C (derated to 3 W at 100°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -55°C to 100°C
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to 100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: FV1206

- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent power handling (up to 38 W)
  - Small size with 7 sections
  - Temperature stable
  - Hermetically sealed
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 6,943,646
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.

**Typical Applications:**
The LFCN-6400D+ is commonly used in:
- Harmonic rejection circuits
- VHF/UHF transmitters and receivers
- Laboratory equipment for signal processing
- RF communication systems where filtering of high-frequency noise is required

This low pass filter is ideal for applications that require a compact solution with high power handling capabilities and stable performance across a wide frequency range.
