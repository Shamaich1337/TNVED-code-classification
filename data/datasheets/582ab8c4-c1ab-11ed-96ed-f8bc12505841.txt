  - Repetitive Peak Collector Current (ICRM): 600 A
  - Continuous DC Forward Current (IF): 300 A
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 600 A

  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptotal): 1500 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tvj): -40 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -40 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Module with a base plate material of Copper (Cu) and internal insulation of Aluminum Oxide (Al2O3).

- **Special Features:**
  - Low Vcesat (Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage): 1.79 V at 25 °C and 2.09 V at 150 °C.
  - High reliability and power density.
  - Low switching loss characteristics.
  - Isolation voltage: 4.0 kV (RMS, 50 Hz, 1 min).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The XNG300B24KC2S is a high-performance Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) module designed for high-voltage and high-current applications. It features low saturation voltage and low switching losses, making it suitable for efficient power conversion and control. The module is built for robust performance in demanding environments, with a focus on reliability and thermal management.

#### Typical Applications:
- **UPS Systems:** Used in uninterruptible power supplies to manage power flow and ensure reliability during outages.
- **Wind Turbines:** Employed in renewable energy systems for efficient power conversion from mechanical to electrical energy.
- **Motor Drives:** Utilized in various motor control applications, providing efficient switching and control of electric motors.
- **Inverters:** Commonly used in inverter circuits for converting DC to AC power, essential in solar power systems and other applications.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high efficiency, reliability, and performance in power management and conversion systems.
