### Component Summary: 74HC595D,118

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.0V to 6.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (IO): 
    - Q7S: ±25 mA
    - Qn pins: ±35 mA
  - Supply Current (ICC): 70 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot):
    - DIP16: 750 mW
    - SO16, SSOP16, TSSOP16, DHVQFN16: 500 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SO16 (Plastic Small Outline Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - 8-stage serial shift register with storage register and 3-state outputs
  - Asynchronous reset (active LOW)
  - ESD protection: HBM JESD22-A114F exceeds 2000 V, MM JESD22-A115-A exceeds 200 V
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **74HC595D** is an 8-bit serial-in, serial or parallel-out shift register with output latches and 3-state outputs. It is a high-speed Si-gate CMOS device that is pin-compatible with Low-power Schottky TTL (LSTTL). The device features separate clocks for the shift register and storage register, allowing for efficient data handling. Data is shifted on the positive-going transitions of the shift register clock input (SHCP), and the data in each register is transferred to the storage register on a positive-going transition of the storage register clock input (STCP).

#### Typical Applications:
The **74HC595D** is commonly used in applications that require:
- **Serial-to-parallel data conversion:** It allows for the conversion of serial data streams into parallel data outputs, making it useful in various digital communication systems.
- **Remote control holding registers:** It can be used to hold data for remote control applications, enabling the control of multiple outputs from a single serial input.
- **LED driving:** The device can control multiple LEDs by shifting data in and out, making it ideal for LED displays and lighting applications.
- **Data storage:** It can be utilized in data storage applications where data needs to be temporarily held before being processed or displayed.

This component is widely used in microcontroller interfacing, digital signal processing, and various consumer electronics applications due to its versatility and efficiency in handling data.
