### Component Summary: L6234PD013TR

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** L6234PD013TR  
**Component Type:** Three-phase motor driver

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VS): 7 to 52 V
  - Bootstrap Peak Voltage (Vboot): 62 V
  - Differential Output Voltage (VOD): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Output Current (Ipeak): 5 A
  - DC Output Current (Iout): 4 A at 25 °C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 2.3 W at 70 °C
  - Quiescent Supply Current (IS): 6.5 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 to 150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40 to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PowerSO20 (20-pin)

- **Special Features:**
  - RDSon: 0.3 Ω (typical at 25 °C)
  - Cross conduction protection
  - Thermal shutdown feature
  - TTL compatible driver
  - Operating frequency up to 150 kHz
  - Intrinsic fast free wheeling diodes
  - Input and enable function for each half bridge
  - 10 V external reference available

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The L6234 is a triple half-bridge motor driver designed specifically for driving brushless DC motors. It utilizes BCDmulti-power technology, which integrates isolated DMOS power transistors with CMOS and bipolar circuits on a single chip. This mixed technology allows for optimized performance in both logic and power stages. The device features independent control for each half-bridge, enabling precise motor control.

#### Typical Applications:
The L6234 is primarily used in applications involving:
- Brushless DC motor control in various industrial and consumer products.
- Robotics and automation systems where precise motor control is required.
- Power management systems that require efficient motor driving capabilities.
- Applications in automotive systems for driving motors in electric vehicles or actuators.

This component is ideal for scenarios where high efficiency, compact design, and reliable motor control are essential.
