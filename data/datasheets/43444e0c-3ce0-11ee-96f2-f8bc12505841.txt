### Component Summary: nanoSMDC050F/13.2

**Manufacturer:** Littelfuse  
**Product Code:** nanoSMDC050F/13.2  
**Type:** Resettable Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) Device

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Continuous Voltage (VMAX): 13.2 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Hold Current (IH): 0.50 A
  - Trip Current (IT): 1.10 A
  - Maximum Fault Current (IMAX): 100 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD MAX): 0.80 W (in tripped state)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 1206 Surface Mount
- **Special Features:**
  - Fast time-to-trip
  - Low resistance
  - RoHS compliant, lead-free, and halogen-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - Level 2a, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The nanoSMDC050F/13.2 is a surface mount resettable overcurrent protection device designed to protect electronic circuits from overcurrent conditions. It operates by switching from a low-resistance state to a high-resistance state when the current exceeds a specified trip level, effectively interrupting the circuit and preventing damage.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Electronics and Batteries:** Provides protection in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
- **Computers:** Used in power management circuits to prevent overcurrent conditions.
- **Portable Electronics:** Ideal for devices that require compact protection solutions.
- **Multimedia Devices:** Protects audio and video equipment from overcurrent.
- **Automotive Applications:** Used in automotive electronics for circuit protection.
- **Industrial Controls:** Provides safety in industrial equipment and machinery.
- **Game Machines:** Protects gaming consoles and handheld devices from electrical faults.

This component is particularly suited for applications where space is limited and resettable protection is desired, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
