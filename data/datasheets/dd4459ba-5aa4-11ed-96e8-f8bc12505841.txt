**Product Code:** BAV99-TP

- **Package Type:**

  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating

The BAV99-TP is a dual series switching diode designed for low power applications. It features a compact SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The diode is characterized by its low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, which are essential for efficient signal processing and rectification in electronic circuits.

- **Signal Processing:** Used in various signal routing and switching applications due to its fast response time.
- **Power Management:** Ideal for power supply circuits where efficient rectification is required.
