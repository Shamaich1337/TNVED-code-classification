### Component Summary: URA2415YMD-20WR3

**Description:**
The URA2415YMD-20WR3 is a 20W isolated DC-DC converter from the URA_YMD-20WR3 series produced by MORNSUN. It features an ultra-wide input voltage range and provides regulated dual output voltages of ±15V. This converter is designed for high efficiency and reliability in various applications, particularly in industrial and railway environments.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage: 24V nominal (9-36V range)
  - Output Voltage: ±15V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: ±667 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - No-load Power Consumption: 0.24W
  - Full Load Efficiency: 88% (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +105°C
- **Package Type:**
  - DIP (Dual In-line Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-wide 4:1 input voltage range
  - High efficiency up to 90%
  - I/O isolation test voltage: 1.5 kV DC
  - Input under-voltage protection, output short-circuit, over-current, and over-voltage protection
  - Input reverse polarity protection available in chassis or DIN-rail mounting versions
  - Meets EN50155 railway standard
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

The URA2415YMD-20WR3 is widely used in:
- Industrial control systems
- Electric power management
- Instrumentation
- Communication systems
- Railway applications

This component is particularly suitable for environments requiring robust performance under varying conditions, making it ideal for applications in transportation and industrial automation where reliability and efficiency are critical.
