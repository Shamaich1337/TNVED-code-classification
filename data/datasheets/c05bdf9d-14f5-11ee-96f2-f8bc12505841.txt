### Component Summary: SFH756V

**Component Type:**  
Plastic Fiber Optic Transmitter Diode

**Description:**  
The SFH756V is a low-cost optical transmitter designed for simple data transmission using polymer optical fibers. It features a 650 nm LED that enables data transmission speeds of up to 10 MBd. The component is housed in a transparent plastic package with a 2.2 mm aperture that allows for easy coupling of unconnectorized plastic optical fibers. The design includes an axial locking screw for secure fiber attachment, making it a cost-effective solution for various optical applications.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (I_F = 50 mA): 2.1 V (max 2.8 V)
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 3 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 50 mA
  - Surge Current (t ≤ 10 μs): 1 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_TOT): 120 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_OP): -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -40 °C to +100 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): 100 °C
  - Soldering Temperature (TS): 260 °C (for 2 mm from case bottom, t ≤ 5 s)

- **Package Type:**
  - Plastic Connector Housing

- **Special Features:**
  - Good linearity with forward current > 2 mA
  - Molded microlens for efficient coupling
  - Interference-free transmission from light-tight housing
  - Auto insertable and wave solderable
  - Supplied in tubes

  - Not specified in the provided data.
