### Component Summary: CDNBS08-SLVU2.8-4

**Manufacturer:** Bourns  
**Type:** Low Capacitance Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) Array

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 2.8 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 0.3 V (at 1 mA)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PP): 30 A (for 8/20 μs pulse)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (P_PP): 600 W (for 8/20 μs pulse)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55 °C to +150 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC-8 (Narrow Body)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant
  - Protects up to four I/O ports
  - Surge protection
  - ESD protection (up to ±30 kV for air discharge)
  - Low capacitance: 6 pF
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The CDNBS08-SLVU2.8-4 is a low capacitance transient voltage suppressor (TVS) array designed to provide surge and electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection for sensitive electronic components. It features a compact SOIC-8 package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. The device is compliant with RoHS standards, ensuring it meets environmental regulations.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Ethernet Devices:** Protection for 10/100 Base T Ethernet interfaces.
- **Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs):** Safeguarding internal circuits from voltage spikes.
- **LAN Devices:** Ensuring reliable operation in local area networks by protecting against surges.
- **Instrumentation:** Protecting sensitive measurement and control equipment from transient voltages.

This component is ideal for use in portable communications, computing, and video equipment, where robust protection against voltage transients is critical for maintaining device integrity and performance.
