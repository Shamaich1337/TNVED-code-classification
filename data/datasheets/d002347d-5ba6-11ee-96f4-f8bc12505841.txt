### Component Summary: SN74AHC1G04DBVR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2V to 5.5V
  - Input Voltage Range: -0.5V to 7V
  - Output Voltage Range: -0.5V to VCC + 0.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Drive Current: ±8 mA at 5V
  - Continuous Output Current: ±25 mA
  - Input Clamp Current: -20 mA (for VI < 0)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 10 µA at VCC = 5.5V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT-23 (DBV), SC-70 (DCK), and SOT-5X3 (DRL) packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Schmitt-Trigger action at all inputs for tolerance to slower input rise and fall times.
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 250 mA per JESD 17.
  - Low power consumption with a maximum ICC of 10 µA.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The **SN74AHC1G04** is a single inverter gate CMOS device that performs the Boolean function Y = A. It is designed to provide a low-drive output suitable for various digital logic applications. The device features Schmitt-Trigger inputs, which enhance its ability to handle slow input signals and improve noise immunity.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cameras:** Used in image processing and control circuits.
- **E-Meters:** Employed in energy measurement and monitoring systems.
- **Ethernet Switches:** Utilized in data communication and networking equipment.
- **Infotainment Systems:** Integrated into automotive and consumer electronics for signal processing.

This component is ideal for applications requiring low power consumption, high-speed operation, and the ability to interface with a variety of voltage levels, making it versatile for both consumer and industrial electronics.
