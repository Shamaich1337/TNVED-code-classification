This diode is particularly beneficial in applications where reduced switching losses and thermal management are critical.

  - Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFRM): 350 A (10 ms pulse)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**

  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to 175°C

- **Special Features:**
  - Zero reverse recovery current
  - Zero forward recovery voltage
  - High-temperature operation
  - High-frequency operation
