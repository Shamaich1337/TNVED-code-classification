### Component Summary for Littelfuse 0452.500NRL

**Component Type:** Surface Mount Fuse (NANO2® Slo-Blo® Fuse, 452 Series)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Rating:** 125V
- **Current Rating:** 0.5A (500mA)
- **Power Consumption:** Not applicable (fuses do not consume power; they protect circuits)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Tape and Reel packaging)
- **Special Features:**
  - Enhanced inrush withstand characteristics
  - Time delay feature to prevent nuisance opening
  - RoHS compliant and Halogen Free
  - UL Recognized to UL/CSA/NMX UL 248-1 and UL/CSA/NMX UL 248-14
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Level 1, J-STD-020

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 0452.500NRL is a NANO2® Slo-Blo® fuse designed to provide overcurrent protection in electronic circuits. It features a unique time delay characteristic that allows it to withstand temporary inrush currents without opening, making it suitable for applications where such conditions are common. This fuse is particularly effective in preventing nuisance tripping that can occur with fast-acting fuses.

#### Typical Applications:
The 0452.500NRL fuse is commonly used in various electronic devices and systems, including:
- Notebook PCs
- LCD/PDP TVs and monitors
- Portable DVD players
- Power supplies
- Networking equipment
- PC servers
- Cooling fan systems
- Telecom systems
- Wireless base stations
- Game consoles
- Office automation equipment
- Battery charging circuits
- Industrial equipment

This fuse is ideal for applications that require reliable protection against overcurrent conditions while accommodating inrush currents, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.
