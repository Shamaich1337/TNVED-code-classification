### Component Summary: MAX3483EESA+T

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0V to 3.6V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: +7V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 1.1 mA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current (ISHDN): 2 nA (typical)
  - Driver Short-Circuit Output Current: ±250 mA
  - Receiver Short-Circuit Output Current: ±60 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low-power shutdown mode with 2 nA current draw.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - MAX3483EESA: 0°C to +70°C
  - MAX3483EEPA: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Pin SO (Small Outline) or 8-Pin Plastic DIP (Dual In-line Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - ±15kV ESD protection (Human Body Model)
  - Slew-rate limited drivers for reduced EMI and reflections
  - Half-duplex communication capability
  - Thermal shutdown and current limiting for driver overload protection
  - Fail-safe receiver feature guarantees a logic-high output if both inputs are open circuit.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The MAX3483E is a low-power, half-duplex transceiver designed for RS-485 and RS-422 communications. It features a driver and a receiver, capable of transmitting data at rates up to 250 kbps. The device is powered by a single +3.3V supply and is designed to operate in environments where electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection is critical, offering robust protection against ESD events.

**Typical Applications:**
The MAX3483E is commonly used in:
- Telecommunications systems
- Industrial control networks
- Local area networks (LANs)
- Integrated Services Digital Networks (ISDN)
- Packet switching applications
- Any application requiring reliable data transmission over long distances with minimal interference.

This component is particularly suitable for environments sensitive to electromagnetic interference (EMI) due to its slew-rate limiting feature, which helps to minimize signal reflections and maintain data integrity.
