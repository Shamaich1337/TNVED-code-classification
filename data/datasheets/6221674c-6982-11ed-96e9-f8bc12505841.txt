### Component Summary: PBHV9560Z

**General Description:**
The PBHV9560Z is a PNP high-voltage low VCEsat (BISS) transistor designed for medium power applications. It is housed in a SOT223 (SC-73) surface-mounted plastic package. This transistor is particularly noted for its high voltage capability and low collector-emitter saturation voltage, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 600 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 600 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 0.5 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 
    - 0.65 W (Tamb ≤ 25 °C)
    - 1.4 W (with specific mounting conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT223 (SC-73) surface-mounted package with 4 leads.

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.
  - High collector current gain (hFE) at high IC.
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage (VCEsat).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Description:**
The PBHV9560Z is a PNP transistor primarily used for switching and amplification in high-voltage applications. Its low saturation voltage allows for efficient operation in various electronic circuits, particularly where power loss needs to be minimized.

**Typical Applications:**
- Electronic ballast for fluorescent lighting.
- LED drivers for LED chain modules.
- LCD backlighting systems.
- High-Intensity Discharge (HID) front lighting.
- Automotive motor management systems.
- Hook switches for wired telecommunications.
- Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS).

This transistor is well-suited for applications requiring high voltage and current handling capabilities, making it a versatile choice in both consumer electronics and automotive systems.
