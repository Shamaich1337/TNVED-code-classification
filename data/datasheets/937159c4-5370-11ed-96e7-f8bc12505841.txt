### Component Summary: VI-2WL-IV (Vicor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage: 10V to 36V (Nominal)
  - Output Voltage Options: 2V, 3.3V, 5V, 5.2V, 5.5V, 5.8V, 6.5V, 7.5V, 10V, 12V, 13.8V, 15V, 18.5V, 24V, 28V, 36V, 40V, 48V, 52V, 72V, 85V, 95V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: Up to 40A (depending on output voltage)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Output: 200W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C (I-Grade)
  - -25°C to +85°C (C-Grade)
  - -10°C to +85°C (E-Grade)

- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 4.6” x 2.4” x 0.5” (116.8 mm x 61.0 mm x 12.7 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Isolated output
  - Up to 90% efficiency
  - Remote sense and current limit
  - Over Voltage Protection (OVP) and thermal shutdown
  - Logic disable feature
  - Wide range output adjust
  - Compatible with power booster modules
  - Low noise FM control
  - CE Marked and RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **VI-2WL-IV** is a component-level DC-DC converter from Vicor's VI-200 family, designed for high-density power conversion applications. It utilizes zero-current-switching (ZCS) technology, allowing for efficient operation at high frequencies (up to 2MHz). This converter is particularly noted for its compact size and high efficiency, making it suitable for various demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Ideal for systems requiring efficient power conversion and management, such as telecommunications, industrial automation, and data centers.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in applications where stable and isolated power supplies are critical for signal integrity.
- **Embedded Systems:** Commonly found in embedded systems where space is limited, and high power density is required.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Suitable for powering devices that require reliable and efficient DC power sources.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the VI-2WL-IV DC-DC converter, highlighting its versatility and efficiency in various electronic systems.
