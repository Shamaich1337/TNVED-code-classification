### Component Summary: TMA 1515D DC/DC Converter

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 13.5 - 16.5 VDC (15 VDC nominal)
  - Output Voltage: +15 VDC, -15 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 34 mA for both +15 VDC and -15 VDC outputs
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 1 Watt
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Single-in-Line Package (SIP7)
- **Special Features:**
  - Non-regulated output
  - I/O isolation voltage: 1000 VDC
  - High efficiency (typical efficiency: 79%)
  - Short circuit protection with automatic recovery
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The TMA 1515D is a miniature, isolated 1 Watt DC/DC converter from the TMA series by Traco Power. It is designed to provide dual output voltages of +15 VDC and -15 VDC from a nominal input voltage of 15 VDC. The converter is housed in a compact SIP package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. It features high efficiency and is capable of operating over a wide temperature range, making it versatile for various electronic applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The TMA 1515D DC/DC converter is typically used in:
- **Power Management:** Providing stable power supply in various electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in circuits that require dual polarity power supplies for operational amplifiers and other analog components.
- **Telecommunications:** Suitable for powering communication equipment where space and efficiency are critical.
- **Industrial Automation:** Employed in control systems and sensors that require reliable power in harsh environments.

This component is ideal for applications that demand compact size, high efficiency, and reliable performance in a wide range of operating conditions.
