The AP63356Q is an automotive-compliant, synchronous buck converter designed for efficient DC-DC conversion. It integrates high-side and low-side power MOSFETs, allowing for a compact design with minimal external components. The device is optimized for low quiescent current and reduced electromagnetic interference (EMI), making it suitable for automotive applications.

  - Input Voltage (VIN): 3.8V to 32V
