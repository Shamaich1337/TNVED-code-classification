athousand times lower\nthan legacy standard devices. They arealso designed tooperate over awider range ofsupply voltages: from\nstandard ±15V opamp supplies down tothesingle 5-Vsupply used forIClogic. Their output iscompatible with\nRTL, DTL and TTL aswell asMOS circuits. Further, they candrive lamps orrelays, switching voltages upto50\nVatcurrents ashigh as50mA.\nBoth theinputs andtheoutputs oftheLM111, LM211 ortheLM311 canbeisolated from system ground, andthe\noutput candrive loads referred toground, thepositive supply orthenegative supply. Offset balancing andstrobe\ncapability areprovided and outputs can bewire ORed.   - Maximum Input Offset Current: 70nA

  - Supply Current from VCC-: -4.1mA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - LM211: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC (8-pin)

- **Special Features:**
  - Fast Response Time: 165ns
  - Strobe Capability
  - Offset balancing and wire-OR capability for outputs
  - Can operate from a single 5V supply
  - High-reliability automotive applications (Q-Temp version available)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The LM211DR is a high-speed voltage comparator designed for a wide range of applications. It features a single high-speed voltage comparator with low input bias and offset currents, making it suitable for precision applications. The device can operate from a variety of power supply voltages, including both dual and single supply configurations. The output is compatible with TTL and MOS circuits, allowing it to drive loads such as lamps or relays.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Desktop PCs:** Used for signal comparison and processing.
- **Body Control Modules:** Employed in automotive applications for monitoring and control.
- **White Goods:** Utilized in household appliances for various control functions.
- **Building Automation:** Implemented in systems for environmental control and monitoring.
- **Oscillators and Peak Detectors:** Used in signal processing applications to detect voltage levels and transitions.

The LM211DR is particularly advantageous in applications requiring fast response times and low power consumption, making it ideal for both consumer electronics and industrial applications.
