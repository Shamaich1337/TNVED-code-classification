### Component Summary: TXS02612RTWR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCCA: 1.1V to 3.6V
  - VCCB0 and VCCB1: 1.1V to 3.6V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous current through VCCA, VCCB0, VCCB1, or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply current (ICC) at 3.6V: 12 mA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - WQFN (RTW) with 24 pins
- **Special Features:**
  - Built-in level translator for voltage mismatch
  - 6-to-12 demultiplexer/multiplexer for SDIO port expansion
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 100 mA per JESD78, Class II
  - ESD performance: ±8 kV contact discharge (IEC61000-4-2)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TXS02612** is a **SDIO port expander** with integrated **voltage-level translation** capabilities. It is designed to facilitate the connection between a cellphone baseband processor and external SDIO peripherals, allowing for the expansion of a single SDIO port to interface with multiple devices. The device features a 6-channel SPDT switch that can connect to two SDIO peripherals, enabling seamless communication while accommodating different voltage levels.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Devices:** Used in smartphones and tablets to connect SDIO peripherals such as memory cards, Bluetooth, and WLAN modules.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Applicable in devices requiring SDIO communication, such as digital TVs and other multimedia devices.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for applications needing flexible data communication between a microcontroller and various peripherals while managing voltage level differences.

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where multiple SDIO devices need to be connected to a single port, ensuring compatibility across different voltage levels without additional external components.
