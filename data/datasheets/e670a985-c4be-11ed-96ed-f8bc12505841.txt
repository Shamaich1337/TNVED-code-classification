### Component Summary: CSD18534Q5A

**Manufacturer:** Texas Instruments  
**Product Code:** CSD18534Q5A  
**Description:**  
The CSD18534Q5A is a 60V N-channel NexFET™ power MOSFET designed for high-efficiency power conversion applications. It features ultra-low on-resistance and low gate charge, making it suitable for minimizing power losses in various electronic circuits.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): 60 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 50 A (package limited)
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C (silicon limited): 69 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM) at 25°C: 229 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 77 W
  - Thermal Resistance (RθJC): 2.0 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SON (5mm x 6mm) Plastic Package

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low on-resistance: RDS(on) at VGS=10V: 7.8 mΩ
  - Low gate charge: Qg (total) at VGS=10V: 17 nC
  - Logic level MOSFET
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Pb-Free terminal plating

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Typical Applications:
The CSD18534Q5A is commonly used in:
- **DC-DC Conversion:** Ideal for switching power supplies and converters.
- **Secondary Side Synchronous Rectification:** Enhances efficiency in power supply designs.
- **Isolated Converter Primary Side Switching:** Suitable for isolated power conversion applications.
- **Motor Control:** Effective in driving motors with high efficiency.

This component is designed to minimize losses in power conversion applications, making it a preferred choice for engineers looking to enhance the efficiency of their designs.
