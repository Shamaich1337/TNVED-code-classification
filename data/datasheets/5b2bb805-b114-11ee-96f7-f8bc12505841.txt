**Key Specifications:**

  - Supply Voltage: 6 V (absolute maximum)
  - Input Voltage Range: VSS − 0.3 V to VDD + 0.3 V
  - Operating Voltage: 2.7 V to 6 V (single-supply operation)

  - Supply Current per Amplifier: 0.85 mA (typical) at 2.7 V, 0.95 mA at 5 V
  - Short-Circuit Current: ±80 mA

  - Supply Current: 0.85 mA to 1.2 mA depending on temperature and voltage conditions.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - −40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 14-Lead TSSOP (RU-14) or 14-Lead SOIC_N (R-14)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low offset voltage: 400 µV (typical)
  - Low noise: 8 nV/√Hz
  - Unity-gain stable
  - Qualified for automotive applications
  - Very low input bias currents: 1 pA (maximum)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The AD8694ARZ is a quad rail-to-rail output operational amplifier designed for low-cost, low-noise applications. It features a single-supply operation, making it suitable for battery-powered devices. The amplifier is characterized by low offset voltage, low noise, and very low input bias currents, which enhance its performance in precision applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- Photodiode amplification
- Battery-powered instrumentation
- Medical instruments
- Multipole filters
- Sensors
- Portable audio devices

The AD8694ARZ is particularly useful in applications requiring high precision and low noise, such as audio amplification, sensor signal conditioning, and instrumentation. Its wide bandwidth and low distortion make it ideal for both AC and DC signal processing tasks.
