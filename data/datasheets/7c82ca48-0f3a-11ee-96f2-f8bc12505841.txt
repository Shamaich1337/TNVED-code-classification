The device includes active bus-hold circuitry that maintains valid logic levels on unused or undriven inputs, thus eliminating the need for external pull-up or pull-down resistors.

- **Bus systems**: The device can be used in bus systems where multiple devices need to communicate without interference.
- **Power management**: Its partial-power-down capability makes it useful in power-sensitive applications where minimizing power consumption is critical.
