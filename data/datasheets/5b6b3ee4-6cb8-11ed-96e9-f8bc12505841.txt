  - Working Voltage Range: 2.4 V to 75 V (E24 range)
  
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Forward Current (IF): 250 mA
  - Reverse Current (IR): Up to 500 µA (typical at Zener voltage)
  
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): ≤ 550 mW
  - Non-repetitive Peak Reverse Power Dissipation (PZSM): ≤ 40 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to +150 °C
  
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOD323F (SC-90), a very small and flat lead Surface-Mounted Device (SMD) plastic package
  
- **Special Features or Notes**: 
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Low differential resistance
  - Two tolerance series: ±2% and ±5%
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BZX84J-B3V3 is a general-purpose Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is part of the BZX84J series, which includes a variety of Zener diodes with different nominal working voltages. The primary function of this component is to provide voltage clamping and regulation, ensuring that the voltage across its terminals does not exceed a specified level, thus protecting sensitive electronic components from overvoltage conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: The BZX84J series is commonly used in power management circuits to maintain a stable voltage level.
- **Voltage Reference**: It can serve as a voltage reference in various electronic circuits.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.
- **Signal Processing**: It can be used in signal processing applications to stabilize voltage levels and protect against voltage spikes.
