### Component Summary: HI-8684PSI

**Manufacturer:** Holt Integrated Circuits Inc.  
**Product Code:** HI-8684PSI

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V ±10%
  - Maximum Voltage on Inputs (RINA/RINB): +29V to -29V
  - Other Inputs: -0.3V to Vcc + 0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Current per Input Pin: ±10mA
  - Operating Supply Current: Not explicitly stated, but typical values can be inferred from the power dissipation ratings.

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at 25°C:
    - Plastic 20-pin SOIC: 1.4W (derate 11.5mW/°C)
    - Plastic 20-pin PLCC: 2.0W (derate 17.2mW/°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial Screening: -40°C to +85°C
  - Military Screening: -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 20-Pin Plastic SOIC-WB (Wide Body)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated analog line receiver circuitry.
  - High-speed 8-bit parallel bus interface.
  - Error detection for word length and parity.
  - Input hysteresis of at least 2 volts.
  - Simplified lightning protection with the ability to add 10K ohm external series resistors (HI-8684-10 version).
  - Reset pin for power-on initialization.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The HI-8684PSI is an ARINC interface device that converts incoming ARINC429 and ARINC561 serial data into 8-bit parallel data. It integrates both digital logic and analog line receiver circuitry into a single device, making it suitable for applications requiring efficient data conversion from serial to parallel formats. The device operates using +5V analog/digital CMOS technology, ensuring compatibility with various digital systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Aerospace and Avionics:** The HI-8684PSI is primarily used in aerospace applications for interfacing ARINC429 and ARINC561 data buses with digital systems, facilitating communication between avionics equipment.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** It can be employed in systems that require the collection and processing of data from ARINC-compliant sources.
