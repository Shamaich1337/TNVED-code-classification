- **Package Type:** 3.2 x 2.5 mm ceramic SMD
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The ECS-3225MV-120-BN-TR is a miniature SMD HCMOS oscillator featuring MultiVolt™ capability, allowing operation across a wide voltage range of 1.62 V to 3.63 V. It is designed for applications requiring precise frequency generation with low jitter and a compact footprint of 3.2 x 2.5 mm. The oscillator is RoHS compliant and suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The ECS-3225MV-120-BN-TR is commonly used in:
- **LoRa WAN (Long Range Wide Area Network):** Ideal for low-power, long-range communication applications.
- **Low Power/Portable Devices:** Suitable for battery-operated devices where power efficiency is critical.
- **Industrial Applications:** Used in various industrial control systems requiring reliable timing signals.
- **IoT (Internet of Things):** Perfect for IoT devices that need stable frequency references for communication and processing.
