### Component Summary: BFN26E6327 (Infineon Technologies)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 300 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 300 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 200 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 500 mA
  - Base Current (IB): 100 mA
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 360 mW (at TS ≤ 74 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23

- **Special Features:**
  - High breakdown voltage
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage
  - Complementary type available (BFN27 - PNP)
  - Pb-free (RoHS compliant)
  - Qualified according to AEC Q101

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Component Description:
The BFN26E6327 is an NPN silicon high-voltage transistor designed for applications requiring high voltage and current handling capabilities. It features a high breakdown voltage of 300 V, making it suitable for demanding environments such as video output stages in television sets and switching power supplies. The transistor is characterized by low collector-emitter saturation voltage, which enhances its efficiency in switching applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Video Output Stages:** Used in television sets to drive video signals effectively.
- **Switching Power Supplies:** Employed in power management circuits to control power flow and improve efficiency.
- **General Amplification:** Suitable for various amplification tasks in electronic circuits due to its high voltage and current ratings.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications due to its qualification under AEC Q101, ensuring reliability in harsh conditions.
