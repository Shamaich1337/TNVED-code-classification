#### Description:
The ER26500-MG31 is a primary lithium battery utilizing lithium-thionyl chloride (Li-SOCl2) chemistry. It is designed as a C-size bobbin cell, providing a high and stable voltage output suitable for various applications. The battery is characterized by its long shelf life, high energy density (up to 590Wh/kg), and wide operating temperature range, making it ideal for demanding environments.

The ER26500-MG31 is commonly used in:
- **Security Systems**: Providing reliable power for alarms and surveillance equipment.
- **Smart Metering**: Powering devices that measure and report utility usage.
- **RFID / IoT Systems**: Supporting wireless communication and tracking applications.
- **Wireless Transmitting**: Used in devices that require battery-operated transmission capabilities.
- **Smart Home Devices**: Powering various home automation products.
- **Military Devices**: Suitable for use in rugged and extreme conditions.
