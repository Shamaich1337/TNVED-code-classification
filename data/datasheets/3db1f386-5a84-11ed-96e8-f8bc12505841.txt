### Component Summary: HCPL-181-00DE

**Description:**
The HCPL-181-00DE is a phototransistor optocoupler manufactured by Avago Technologies. It consists of a light-emitting diode (LED) optically coupled to a phototransistor, packaged in a compact 4-pin mini-flat surface mount device (SMD) with a profile of 2.0 mm. This design allows for significant space savings, making it suitable for applications where board space is limited. The device provides high input-output isolation and is compliant with RoHS standards.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (BV CEO): 80 V
  - Emitter-Collector Breakdown Voltage (BV ECO): 6 V
  - Isolation Voltage (V iso): 3750 Vrms (for 1 minute)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (I F): 50 mA
  - Collector Current (I C): 50 mA
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Input Power Dissipation (P I): 70 mW
  - Collector Power Dissipation: 150 mW
  - Total Power Dissipation: 170 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T S): -55°C to +155°C
  - Operating Temperature (T A): -55°C to +100°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Mini-flat SMD (4-pin SO-4)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - High Current Transfer Ratio (CTR): Minimum 50% at I F = 5 mA, V CE = 5 V
  - Fast response time: Typically 4 μs
  - UL and CSA approved, IEC/EN/DIN EN 60747-5-2 approved
  - Lead (Pb) Free and RoHS compliant
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

**Typical Applications:**
The HCPL-181-00DE is commonly used in:
- I/O interfaces for computers
- System appliances and measuring instruments
- Signal transmission between circuits with different potentials and impedances
- Feedback circuits in power supplies

This optocoupler is ideal for applications requiring electrical isolation between different parts of a system, ensuring safety and signal integrity in various electronic devices.
