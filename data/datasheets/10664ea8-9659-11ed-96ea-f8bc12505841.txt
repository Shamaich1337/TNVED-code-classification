### Component Summary: CVHD-950X-100.000

**Description:**
The CVHD-950X-100.000 is a Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) produced by Crystek Corporation. It operates within a frequency range of 40 MHz to 130 MHz, specifically tuned to 100 MHz for this model. The oscillator utilizes high Q crystal and 3rd overtone technology to achieve ultra-low phase noise and low jitter performance, making it suitable for high-precision applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** 3.3V ±0.3V
- **Current Ratings:** 15mA typical, 25mA maximum
- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but derived from current ratings at 3.3V (approximately 49.5 mW typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:** 9mm x 14mm Surface Mount Technology (SMT)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low phase noise: -140 dBc/Hz at 1 kHz offset, -155 dBc/Hz at 10 kHz, -164 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz, and -166 dBc/Hz at 1 MHz.
  - No sub-harmonics present.
  - Aging: <3 ppm in the first year, <1 ppm thereafter.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The CVHD-950X-100.000 VCXO is designed for applications requiring high precision and stability, including:
- High Definition Television (HDTV)
- Avionics systems
- Low phase signal sources
- Test and measurement equipment

This oscillator is particularly valuable in environments where low phase noise and jitter are critical for performance, such as in communication systems and high-frequency signal processing applications.
