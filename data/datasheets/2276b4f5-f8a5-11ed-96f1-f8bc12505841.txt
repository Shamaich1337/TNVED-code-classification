### Component Summary: FDN352AP

**Description:**
The FDN352AP is a Single P-Channel PowerTrench® MOSFET produced by ON Semiconductor (formerly Fairchild Semiconductor). It is designed for low voltage and battery-powered applications, providing low on-state resistance and low gate charge for superior switching performance. This MOSFET is particularly suitable for power management in notebook computers and other compact electronic devices.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (V_DSS): -30 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (V_GSS): ±25 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D): -1.3 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current: -10 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 0.5 W (0.46 W under certain conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SuperSOT™-3 package, which has a higher power handling capability than the standard SOT-23 package.

- **Special Features:**
  - R_DS(ON) = 180 mΩ at V_GS = -10 V
  - R_DS(ON) = 300 mΩ at V_GS = -4.5 V
  - High performance trench technology for extremely low R_DS(ON).
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided text, but typically referenced in JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power management in notebook computers.
- Low voltage and battery-powered applications where minimizing in-line power loss is critical.
- General switching applications in compact electronic devices.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the FDN352AP MOSFET, making it a valuable component for engineers and designers in the electronics field.
