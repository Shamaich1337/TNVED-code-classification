### Component Summary: 600L5R6CT200T

- **Voltage Rating (WVDC):** 200 V
- **Capacitance:** 5.6 pF
- **Capacitance Tolerance:** ±5%
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55ºC to +125ºC
- **Power Consumption:** Not applicable (capacitor)
- **Package Type:** 0402 (EIA Case Size)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically MSL for capacitors can be found in the manufacturer's guidelines.
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
  - High Q (Quality Factor) greater than 2,000 at 1 MHz (applies to capacitance ≥ 10 pF)
  - No aging effects
  - No piezoelectric effects
  - Insulation Resistance (IR): 10^5 Megohms min. @ 25ºC at rated WVDC

#### Description:
The **600L5R6CT200T** is a multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLC) designed for RF and microwave applications. It features ultra-low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high self-resonance, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. The capacitor is constructed using NPO (C0G) dielectric material, which provides stable capacitance over a wide temperature range and voltage.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Base Stations:** Used in RF circuits for filtering and coupling.
- **Broadband Wireless Services:** Essential for signal integrity in high-frequency communication systems.
- **Satellite Communications:** Provides reliable performance in demanding environments.
- **Subscriber-based Wireless Devices:** Used in devices requiring compact and efficient capacitors.
- **WiFi (802.11):** Supports high-frequency signal processing.
- **Public Safety Radio:** Ensures stable operation in critical communication systems.
- **Matching Networks:** Used to match impedance in RF circuits.
- **Tuning, Coupling, Bypass, and DC Blocking:** Versatile applications in various RF circuit designs.

This capacitor is particularly valuable in applications where low loss and high performance are critical, such as in RF filters and high-frequency oscillators.
