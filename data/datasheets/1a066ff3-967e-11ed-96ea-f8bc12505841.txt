### Component Summary: BLM21PG331BH1D

**Manufacturer:** Murata Electronics  
**Type:** Chip Ferrite Bead  
**Series:** BLM21_BH Series  
**AEC-Q200 Compliance:** Yes  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Impedance:** 330 Ω ± 25% at 100 MHz
- **Rated Current:** 1000 mA (1 A)
- **DC Resistance:** 
  - Typical at 85°C: 0.07 Ω
  - Typical at 125°C: 0.14 Ω
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** Chip (Dimensions: 2.0 mm x 1.25 mm x 0.85 mm)
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

#### Description:
The BLM21PG331BH1D is a chip ferrite bead designed for use in automotive electronics. Ferrite beads are passive components that suppress high-frequency noise in electronic circuits. They are particularly effective in filtering out electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI), making them essential for maintaining signal integrity in sensitive applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in various automotive applications to reduce noise and improve signal quality.
- **Power Management:** Helps in filtering noise in power supply circuits, ensuring stable operation of electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Utilized in communication devices to enhance the clarity of signals by minimizing unwanted noise.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and computers to improve performance by reducing EMI.

This component is particularly suited for applications requiring high reliability and performance, adhering to automotive standards as per AEC-Q200.
