### Component Summary: MMBT5401

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** MMBT5401  
**Type:** PNP General Purpose Amplifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CEO): -160 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): -180 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): -6.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): -600 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Collector Power Dissipation (P_C): 300 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): Up to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23

- **Special Features:**
  - Epoxy meets UL-94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Halogen-free option available (suffix "HF")
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The MMBT5401 is a PNP bipolar junction transistor (BJT) designed for general-purpose amplification and switching applications. It is characterized by its high voltage ratings and moderate current handling capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Amplification:** Used in audio and radio frequency applications to amplify weak signals.
- **Switching Applications:** Can be employed in digital circuits for switching tasks.
- **Power Management:** Utilized in power supply circuits to control power flow.
- **General Electronics:** Commonly found in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial control systems.

This component is ideal for engineers and designers looking for a reliable PNP transistor for a variety of electronic applications, particularly where space is a constraint due to its SOT-23 package.
