#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vbb): Up to 58 V
  - Output Voltage (VON): Up to 70 V
  - Input Voltage (VIN): ±42 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Nominal Load Current (IL): 2.5 A (one channel active), 4.0 A (two channels active)
  - Short-Circuit Current Limit (IL(LIM)): Up to 12 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot): 3.0 W (at Ta = 25°C, all channels active)
  - Standby Current: 13 µA (at Tj = -40°C to +25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** PG-DSO-14 (Plastic Dual Small Outline Package)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated protection functions (overvoltage, thermal overload, short-circuit)
  - Open load detection
  - ESD protection (up to 1 kV, Human Body Model)

#### Description:
The BTS723GW is a smart high-side power switch designed for automotive and industrial applications. It features integrated protection mechanisms to prevent damage under fault conditions, making it suitable for driving loads in various applications. The device can handle high voltages and currents, providing reliable performance in demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in automotive systems for controlling power to various loads such as motors, lights, and other electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Acts as a switch for signal routing in electronic circuits.
- **Load Switching:** Ideal for applications requiring the switching of inductive loads, such as relays and solenoids.
- **Diagnostics:** Provides feedback on load conditions, including open load and short-circuit detection, enhancing system reliability.

This component is particularly useful in applications where high reliability and protection against faults are critical, such as in automotive electronics and industrial control systems.
