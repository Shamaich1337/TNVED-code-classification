### Component Summary: TPS7A4001DGNR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Input Voltage: 100V
  - Output Voltage Range: 1.175V to 90V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 50mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 25µA
  - Quiescent Current in Shutdown: 4.1µA
  - Dropout Voltage: 290mV (at 50mA load)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - HVSSOP (8-pin) with dimensions of 3.00 mm x 5.00 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Very high maximum input voltage tolerance (up to 100V)
  - Stable with ceramic capacitors (minimum input capacitance of 1µF and output capacitance of 4.7µF)
  - CMOS logic-level compatible enable pin
  - Built-in current limit and thermal shutdown protection
  - Low noise and high power-supply rejection ratio (PSRR)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 2 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The TPS7A4001 is a very high voltage linear regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage from a high voltage input. It is capable of withstanding continuous DC or transient input voltages up to 100V, making it suitable for applications where high voltage transients are present. The device features a low quiescent current and is stable with a wide range of output capacitance, allowing for compact designs.

**Typical Applications:**
The TPS7A4001 is ideal for various applications, including:
- **Telecom Infrastructure:** Providing regulated power to sensitive components in high voltage environments.
- **Industrial Automation:** Powering microcontrollers and other devices that require stable voltage supplies amidst high voltage transients.
- **Power over Ethernet (PoE):** Ensuring reliable operation of powered devices in networking applications.
- **LED Lighting:** Supplying consistent voltage to LED drivers.
- **Bias Power Supplies:** Generating low-voltage supplies from high-voltage rails.

This component is particularly beneficial in designs that require robust voltage regulation under challenging electrical conditions, such as those found in industrial and telecom applications.
