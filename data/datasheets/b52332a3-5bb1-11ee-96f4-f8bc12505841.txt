#### Description:
The HDC36-24 is a dust cap designed for automotive connectors, specifically compatible with DEUTSCH HD30 series connectors. It is constructed from aluminum, providing durability and protection against environmental factors. The dust cap serves to protect the connector from dust, moisture, and other contaminants when not in use, ensuring reliable performance and longevity of the connector.

- **Heavy-Duty Equipment:** Ensuring connectors remain functional in rugged environments.
- **Industrial Applications:** Providing protection for connectors in machinery and equipment that may be exposed to the elements.
