### Component Summary: BAS116LP3-7

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Document Number:** DS35241 Rev. 7  
**Date:** October 2021  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 85 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 85 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (IFM): 215 mA
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 500 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 4.0 A (1.0 µs)
    - 1.0 A (1.0 ms)
    - 0.5 A (1.0 s)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 250 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Ultra-Small Leadless Surface Mount Package (X3-DFN0603-2), dimensions: 0.6 x 0.3 mm, height: 0.3 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Very Low Leakage Current
  - Low Capacitance (CT: 1.6 to 3.0 pF)
  - Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (per J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BAS116LP3-7 is an ultra-small surface mount low leakage diode designed for applications requiring minimal space and low power consumption. It features a leadless package that allows for efficient thermal management and is suitable for compact battery-powered portable electronics. The diode exhibits very low leakage current and low capacitance, making it ideal for high-frequency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to prevent reverse current flow.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for RF applications due to its low capacitance and fast switching characteristics.
- **Battery-Powered Devices:** Ideal for compact designs where space and power efficiency are critical.
- **General Purpose Rectification:** Can be used in various electronic circuits requiring diode functionality.
