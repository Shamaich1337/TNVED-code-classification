### Component Summary: BVSS138LT1G

**Description:**
The BVSS138LT1G is an N-Channel Power MOSFET designed for low voltage applications. It is housed in a miniature SOT-23 surface mount package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. This MOSFET is particularly noted for its low threshold voltage, which allows it to operate efficiently in low voltage environments.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDSS): 50 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25 °C: 200 mA
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 800 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25 °C: 225 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (TO-236)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)): 0.85 V to 1.5 V, ideal for low voltage applications.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The BVSS138LT1G is commonly used in:
- DC-DC converters
- Power management systems in portable and battery-powered devices such as:
  - Computers
  - Printers
  - PCMCIA cards
  - Cellular and cordless telephones

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited and low voltage operation is critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
