### Component Summary: BFR360L3 E6765

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** Low Noise Silicon Bipolar RF Transistor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 6 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCES): 15 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 15 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 2 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 35 mA
  - Base Current (IB): 4 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 210 mW (at TS ≤ 104°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TStg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSLP-3-1 (Thin Small Leadless Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low noise figure: 1.0 dB at 1.8 GHz
  - Pb-free (RoHS compliant) and halogen-free
  - Qualification report according to AEC-Q101 available
  - ESD sensitive device; handling precautions required

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The BFR360L3 is a low noise silicon bipolar RF transistor designed for applications requiring low voltage and low current operation. It is particularly suited for low noise amplifiers and oscillators operating up to 3.5 GHz, providing a power output greater than 10 dBm. The transistor features a low noise figure, making it ideal for RF applications where signal integrity is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs):** Used in RF front-end circuits to amplify weak signals without adding significant noise.
- **Oscillators:** Suitable for generating RF signals in various communication systems.
- **Signal Processing:** Employed in systems that require high-frequency signal manipulation, such as in wireless communication devices.
- **Telecommunications:** Utilized in devices that operate in the GHz frequency range, enhancing signal clarity and performance.

This component is particularly valuable in applications where space is limited due to its small package size and low power consumption characteristics.
