### Component Summary: TLV493DA1B6HTSA2

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** TLV493DA1B6HTSA2  
**Component Type:** 3D Magnetic Sensor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.8 V to 3.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 3.7 mA (peak during ADC measurements)
  - Low Power Mode: 10 µA
  - Power Down Mode: 7 nA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical operating current: 3.7 mA during ADC measurements
  - Power down mode: 7 nA
  - Ultra-low power mode: 10 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - PG-TSOP-6-6-5 (6-pin package)
- **Special Features:**
  - 3D magnetic sensing (Bx, By, Bz)
  - Integrated temperature sensor
  - Digital output via I2C interface (up to 1 MBit/sec)
  - 12-bit data resolution for each measurement direction
  - Configurable update frequencies and power modes
  - Interrupt signal available to wake up a microcontroller
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 1 (suitable for reflow soldering with profiles according to JEDEC J-STD-020D.1)

#### Description:
The TLV493DA1B6HTSA2 is a low-power 3D magnetic sensor designed for precise three-dimensional sensing of magnetic fields. It operates with a supply voltage range of 2.8 V to 3.5 V and features a small 6-pin TSOP package. The sensor can detect magnetic fields in the X, Y, and Z directions, making it suitable for applications that require accurate angular measurements or low power consumption.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Joysticks and Control Elements:** Used in gaming devices and control knobs for appliances.
- **Electric Meters:** Anti-tampering applications to ensure accurate readings.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Any device requiring precise angular measurements, such as smartphones and tablets.
- **Industrial Applications:** Robotics and automation systems where position sensing is critical.
- **Wearable Devices:** For motion tracking and orientation detection.

This sensor is particularly advantageous in applications where low power consumption is essential, such as battery-operated devices, due to its ultra-low power modes and efficient operation.
