### Component Summary: XZMDKVG56W

**Description:**
The XZMDKVG56W is a 3.0 mm x 1.0 mm Right Angle Surface Mount Device (SMD) Chip LED Lamp produced by SunLED. It is designed for use as an indication light in handheld products, providing visual feedback through red and green light emissions. The LED utilizes AlGaInP material for both colors, ensuring durability and a long operational life.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (Typical at I_F=20mA):
    - Red: 1.95 V
    - Green: 2.1 V
  - Forward Voltage (Maximum at I_F=20mA):
    - Red: 2.5 V
    - Green: 2.5 V
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 30 mA
  - Forward Current (Peak, 1/10 Duty Cycle, 0.1ms Pulse Width):
    - Red: 185 mA
    - Green: 150 mA
  - Reverse Current (Max at V_R=5V): 10 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 75 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_A): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Right Angle SMD Chip LED Lamp (3.0 mm x 1.0 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Long life and robust package
  - Halogen-free
  - RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 3

**Typical Applications:**
The XZMDKVG56W is primarily used in handheld electronic devices for indication purposes. Its compact size and right-angle design make it suitable for applications where space is limited. Typical applications include:

- Indicator lights in consumer electronics (e.g., mobile phones, remote controls)
- Status indicators in industrial equipment
- Backlighting for buttons and displays
- Decorative lighting in various electronic devices

This LED lamp is ideal for applications requiring reliable and efficient visual signaling, contributing to user interface design in modern electronic products.
