### Component Summary: OSG60R108HSZ

**Description:**
The OSG60R108HSZ is an Enhancement Mode N-Channel Power MOSFET produced by Oriental Semiconductor. It utilizes GreenMOS® technology, which incorporates charge balance technology to achieve low on-resistance and reduced gate charge. This MOSFET is designed for high voltage applications, providing excellent switching performance and robust avalanche capability. It is particularly suitable for resonant switching topologies, enhancing efficiency and reliability while minimizing size.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 600 V
  - Maximum Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (BVDSS): 650 V (at Tj=150°C)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=25°C: 30 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=100°C: 19 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID, pulse) at TC=25°C: 90 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at TC=25°C: 219 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO247-P

- **Special Features:**
  - Low RDS(ON): 108 mΩ (max at VGS=10V)
  - Extremely low switching loss
  - Ultra-fast and robust body diode
  - Integrated fast recovery diode (FRD) for minimized reverse recovery time

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The OSG60R108HSZ is commonly used in various power management applications, including:
- PC power supplies
- Telecom power systems
- Server power management
- Electric Vehicle (EV) chargers
- Motor drivers

This MOSFET is ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, particularly in environments where space is a constraint due to its compact form factor.
