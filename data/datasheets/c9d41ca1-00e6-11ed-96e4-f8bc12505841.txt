### Component Summary: TPS76901SDBVTEP

**Manufacturer:** Texas Instruments  
**Product Code:** TPS76901SDBVTEP  
**Package Type:** SOT-23 (DBV)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 5V to 10V
  - Output Voltage Range: 1.2V to 4.5V (adjustable)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: 0 to 100 mA
  - Quiescent Current: 335 μA (typical at 100 mA load), 1 μA in standby mode
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Dropout Voltage: Typically 71 mV at 100 mA load
  - Quiescent Current: 28 μA maximum at 100 mA load
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to 150°C
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Overcurrent limitation
  - Logic-enabled sleep mode
  - Low dropout voltage and ultralow power operation
  - Available in adjustable versions

#### Description:
The TPS76901 is an ultralow-power low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator designed for high-temperature applications. It features a PMOS pass element that allows for low dropout voltages and low quiescent current, making it suitable for micropower operations. The device is optimized for battery-operated equipment, providing significant improvements in system battery life due to its low power consumption.

#### Typical Applications:
- **High-Temperature Environments:** Ideal for applications such as down-hole drilling and other extreme temperature scenarios.
- **Portable Systems:** Commonly used in laptops, cellular phones, and other battery-operated devices where board space is limited.
- **Power Management:** Suitable for applications requiring efficient voltage regulation with minimal power loss.

This component is particularly beneficial in scenarios where maintaining performance over extended temperature ranges is critical, and where low power consumption is essential for prolonging battery life.
