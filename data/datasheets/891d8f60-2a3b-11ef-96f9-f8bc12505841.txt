### Component Summary: PESD5V0S1BA,115

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 5 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 5.5 - 9.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Current (IPPM): 12 A
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM): 5 - 100 nA at VRWM = 5 V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Power (PPPM): 130 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD323 (SC-76), Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)
  - Dimensions: 1.7 mm x 1.25 mm x 0.95 mm

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Bidirectional ESD protection for one signal line
  - ESD protection rating: > 30 kV (IEC 61000-4-2, level 4)
  - Low clamping voltage: 14 V
  - Low capacitance: 35 - 45 pF at 1 MHz
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The PESD5V0S1BA is a bidirectional ElectroStatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and transients caused by ESD events. It is housed in a compact SOD323 package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Handsets and Accessories:** Protects communication lines from ESD damage.
- **Portable Electronics:** Ensures reliability in devices like smartphones and tablets.
- **Computers and Peripherals:** Shields data lines in USB and other interfaces.
- **Communication Systems:** Safeguards signal integrity in networking equipment.
- **Audio and Video Equipment:** Protects against surges that could damage sensitive circuitry.

This component is ideal for use in environments where ESD protection is critical, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.
