### Component Summary: HMC784MS8GE

**Description:**
The HMC784MS8GE is a high-power Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switch designed for transmit-receive applications. It is a GaAs MMIC (Gallium Arsenide Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) capable of handling high input signal power levels with very low distortion. The switch operates over a frequency range from DC to 4 GHz and is packaged in an 8-lead MSOPG (Mini Small Outline Package).

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +3V to +8V
  - Control Voltage (Vctl): -0.2V to Vdd + 0.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Idd (at Vdd = +3V): 0.5 μA
  - Typical Idd (at Vdd = +5V): 2 μA
  - Typical Idd (at Vdd = +8V): 20 μA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 1.217 W (derated at 25 mW/°C above 85 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - MSOP8G (8-lead Mini Small Outline Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss: 0.4 dB (DC - 1.0 GHz)
  - High third-order intercept point: +62 dBm
  - Positive control voltage operation compatible with CMOS and TTL logic
  - Reflective shorts when "OFF" (RF1 and RF2)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC784MS8GE is suitable for various applications, including:
- Cellular and 4G infrastructure
- WiMAX, WiBro, and fixed wireless communications
- Automotive telematics
- Mobile radio systems
- Test equipment for RF applications

This component is designed to provide reliable switching performance in high-frequency applications, making it ideal for modern communication systems that require efficient signal routing with minimal loss and distortion.
