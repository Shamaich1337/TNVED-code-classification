### Component Summary: QCN-34

**Description:**
The QCN-34 is a 2-way power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications, operating within the frequency range of 2500 to 3400 MHz. It is characterized by its ultra-small ceramic package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The component is primarily used to split or combine RF signals with minimal loss and high isolation.

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; typically handled within RF power specifications.
- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; power input is limited to 15W max.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** Ultra-small ceramic package (dimensions: 0.12" x 0.06" x 0.035").
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss (typical 0.4 dB).
  - High isolation (typical 30 dB).
  - Wrap-around terminal for excellent solderability.
  - Patent pending.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

- **Balanced Amplifiers:** Used in RF amplification circuits where signal integrity is crucial.
- **Modulators:** Employed in signal modulation applications for communication systems.
- **MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service):** Utilized in wireless communication systems for distributing signals.
- **Defense Communications:** Suitable for military applications requiring reliable RF signal management.

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal distribution with minimal loss and high reliability, particularly in telecommunications and defense sectors.
