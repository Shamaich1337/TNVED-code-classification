  - Continuous short-circuit protection

  - High efficiency (typical efficiency: 78%)

  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The TMR 4811 is a 2 Watt isolated DC/DC converter module designed for efficient voltage conversion in a compact SIP-8 package. It features a wide input voltage range of 36 to 75 VDC, making it suitable for various applications requiring stable output voltage. The converter provides a regulated output of 5 VDC with a maximum output current of 400 mA, ensuring reliable performance in demanding environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The TMR 4811 is ideal for use in:
- Communication equipment
- Instrumentation
- Industrial electronics
- Space-critical applications where size and efficiency are paramount

This converter is particularly useful in scenarios where electrical isolation is required, and it can be employed in systems that need to manage power efficiently while maintaining a small footprint. The remote On/Off control feature adds flexibility for integration into various electronic systems.
