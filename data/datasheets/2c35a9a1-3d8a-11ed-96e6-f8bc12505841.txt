### Component Summary: DMP3056LSD-13

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Document Number:** DS31420  
**Revision:** 7 - 2  
**Date:** January 2014  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (V_DSS): -30 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (V_GSS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D) at TA = +25°C: -6.9 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at TA = +70°C: -5.8 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (I_DM): -24 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_D): 2.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Dual P-Channel Enhancement Mode MOSFET
  - Low On-Resistance (R_DS(on)): 45 mΩ @ V_GS = -10V, 65 mΩ @ V_GS = -4.5V
  - Low Gate Threshold Voltage (V_GS(th)): -1.7 V (typical)
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Low Input Capacitance
  - Low Input/Output Leakage
  - Fully RoHS Compliant and Halogen-Free
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The DMP3056LSD-13 is a dual P-channel enhancement mode field-effect transistor (MOSFET) designed for high efficiency in power management applications. It features low on-state resistance and superior switching performance, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management Functions:** Used in circuits that require efficient power conversion and management.
- **Backlighting:** Ideal for controlling LED backlighting in displays.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Utilized in power supply circuits to convert DC voltage levels efficiently.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where low power loss and high reliability are critical, such as in automotive and consumer electronics.
