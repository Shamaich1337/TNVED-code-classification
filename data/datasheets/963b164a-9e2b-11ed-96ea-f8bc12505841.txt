### Component Summary: 1.5SMC47CAQ

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.

**Component Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor Diode (TVS)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 44.65V (min) to 49.35V (max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 44V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): 64.8A (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (I_R): 1μA at V_RWM

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 1500W (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 6.5W on an infinite heat sink at T_L=75°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +175°C
  - Storage Temperature Range (T_STG): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AB (SMC)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low-profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Excellent clamping capability and very fast response time
  - ESD protection compliant with IEC 61000-4-2 (30kV Air, 30kV Contact)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (suffix “Q” indicates automotive grade)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1 per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260°C

#### Description:
The 1.5SMC47CAQ is a unidirectional transient voltage suppressor diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is particularly effective in clamping voltage spikes and preventing damage to integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protection of sensitive components in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and computers.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Safeguarding automotive control systems and sensors from voltage transients.
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting signal lines and data lines in communication equipment.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Used in various industrial applications to protect against electrical surges.

This component is essential for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices by providing robust protection against transient voltage events.
