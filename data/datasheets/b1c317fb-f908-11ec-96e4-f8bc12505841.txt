- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated in the provided text
- **Power Consumption:** Designed to conserve power while maintaining high linear output power
- **Operating Temperature Range:** Not specified in the provided text
- **Package Type:** 24 Pin 5x3 mm QFN (Quad Flat No-lead)
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated 5 GHz power amplifier (PA)
  - Single pole two throw switch (SP2T)
  - Bypassable low noise amplifier (LNA)
  - Integrated RF power detector coupler and DC power detector
  - Integrated die-level filtering for 2nd and 3rd harmonics
  - 2.4 GHz rejection for DBDC operation
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 2 (IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)

#### Description:
The QPF4518 is an integrated front end module designed specifically for Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) systems. It combines a power amplifier, a switch, and a low noise amplifier into a single compact device, which minimizes layout area in applications. The module is optimized for a 5V supply voltage, focusing on power efficiency while delivering high output power and throughput. It features integrated filtering to reduce harmonic interference and improve performance.

- **Access Points:** Enhancing wireless connectivity in homes and businesses.
- **Wireless Routers:** Providing robust internet access and signal strength.
- **Residential Gateways:** Serving as a central hub for home networking.
- **Customer Premise Equipment:** Supporting various telecommunications services.
- **Internet of Things (IoT):** Enabling connectivity for smart devices and applications.

This module is particularly suited for applications requiring efficient power management and high-performance wireless communication, making it ideal for modern networking solutions.
