### Component Summary: ES-VMHRPX28C

**Description:**
The ES-VMHRPX28C is an AlGaInP (Aluminum Gallium Indium Phosphide) LED chip produced by Epistar Corporation. It is designed for high luminous intensity applications, making it suitable for various lighting and display technologies. The chip features a thin film structure and vertical electrode design, which enhances its performance and efficiency.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (Vf1 at If = 10µA): 1.35 V
  - Forward Voltage (Vf2 at If = 250mA): 2.2 V (typical) to 2.6 V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (Vr): ≤ 10 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward DC Current (If): ≤ 350 mA
  - Reverse Current (Ir at Vr = 25V): ≤ 5.0 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption is not explicitly stated but can be derived from the forward voltage and current ratings.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): ≤ 125 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Chip-on-tape/storage: 5 °C to 35 °C
  - Chip-on-tape/transportation: -20 °C to +65 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Chip size: 28 mil x 28 mil (710 ± 25 μm x 710 ± 25 μm)
  - Thickness: 8.8 mil (225 ± 25 µm)

- **Special Features:**
  - High luminous intensity (up to 11,000 mcd)
  - Long-term supply assurance
  - High driving current capability
  - Thin film structure
  - Vertical electrode design

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The ES-VMHRPX28C LED chip is primarily used in automotive applications, including:
- Automotive lighting (e.g., headlights, taillights)
- Indicator lights
- Display backlighting
- General illumination in automotive interiors

This LED chip is designed to meet the stringent requirements of automotive applications, ensuring reliability and performance under various environmental conditions.
