### Component Summary: BAS16HT1G

**Description:**
The BAS16HT1G is a switching diode manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed for high-speed switching applications and is particularly suitable for automotive and other applications that require stringent quality and reliability standards. The device is AEC-Q101 qualified, making it suitable for automotive environments.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Continuous Reverse Voltage (VR): 100 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 1.8 A
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 1.0 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD) at TA = 25 °C: 200 mW
  - Thermal Resistance Junction to Ambient (RθJA): 635 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-323 (CASE 477)

- **Special Features:**
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The BAS16HT1G is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Switching Applications:** Ideal for high-speed switching in electronic circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in signal routing and processing circuits.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Suitable for automotive applications due to its AEC-Q101 qualification.
- **General Purpose Diode Applications:** Can be used in rectification and clamping applications.

This diode is particularly valued for its reliability and performance in demanding environments, making it a preferred choice for engineers designing automotive and high-performance electronic systems.
