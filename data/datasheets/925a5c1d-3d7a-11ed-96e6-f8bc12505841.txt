### Component Summary: XC95144-15PQ100I

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply voltage for internal logic (VCCINT): 4.75V to 5.25V (Commercial), 4.5V to 5.5V (Industrial)
  - Supply voltage for output drivers (VCCIO): 4.75V to 5.25V (5V operation), 3.0V to 3.6V (3.3V operation)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating supply current (low power mode, active): 160 mA (typical)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Programmable power reduction mode available for each macrocell to minimize power dissipation.
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to 70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to 85°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 100-pin Plastic Quad Flat Pack (PQFP)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - 7.5 ns pin-to-pin logic delays
  - 144 macrocells with 3,200 usable gates
  - Enhanced pin-locking architecture
  - Extensive IEEE Std 1149.1 boundary-scan (JTAG) support
  - High-drive 24 mA outputs
  - Supports both 3.3V and 5V I/O capability
  - 10,000 program/erase cycles endurance
  - Programmable ground pin capability
  - Extended pattern security features for design protection
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The XC95144-15PQ100I is a high-performance Complex Programmable Logic Device (CPLD) from Xilinx. It is designed for advanced in-system programming and testing capabilities, making it suitable for general-purpose logic integration. The device features eight 36V18 function blocks, providing a total of 3,200 usable gates with a propagation delay of 15 ns.

**Typical Applications:**
The XC95144 is typically used in applications that require:
- **Logic Integration:** It can replace discrete logic components, allowing for more compact designs.
- **Signal Processing:** The device can be utilized in digital signal processing applications where speed and flexibility are crucial.
- **Data Management:** It is suitable for data routing and management tasks in various electronic systems.
- **Prototyping:** The in-system programmability allows for rapid prototyping and testing of digital circuits.
- **Control Systems:** It can be used in control applications where programmable logic is needed to manage various inputs and outputs.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where design flexibility and rapid iteration are essential, such as in telecommunications, automotive electronics, and consumer electronics.
