### Component Summary: LM38-A3C-TI-N-APZ

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0V to 3.65V (Recommended: 3.14V to 3.47V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (ICC): Up to 350 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Supply Voltage: 3.3V (±5%)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Small Form Pluggable (SFP)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Built-in PHY and MCU supporting SGMII Interface
  - 125 Mbps IEEE 802.3u 100BASE-FX compliant
  - AC-coupled differential inputs and outputs
  - Diagnostic monitoring interface compliant with SFF8472
  - Fully metallic enclosure for low EMI
  - Hot-swappable design

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The LM38-A3C-TI-N-APZ is a **100BASE-FX SGMII Optical Transceiver** designed for Fast Ethernet applications. It is a hot-swappable device that connects to a Gigabit Ethernet SFP port, providing full-duplex 100 Mbps connectivity over multimode or single-mode fiber. The transceiver integrates a PHY chip, enabling efficient data transmission and reception while supporting a variety of diagnostic functions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Ethernet Networking:** Used in 125 Mbps 100Base-FX Ethernet and FDDI applications.
- **Switching Interfaces:** Facilitates connections between switches (Switch to Switch interface).
- **Backplane Applications:** Suitable for switched backplane applications.
- **Router Links:** Connects Switch/Router to Switch/Router links.
- **Optical Transmission Systems:** Can be utilized in various optical transmission systems requiring reliable data transfer over fiber optics.

This transceiver is particularly beneficial in industrial Ethernet environments due to its robust design, which allows it to operate reliably in electrically harsh and climatically demanding conditions.
