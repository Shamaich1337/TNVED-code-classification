### Component Summary: GSD2004S-HE3-08

**Description:**
The GSD2004S-HE3-08 is a dual in-series small signal high voltage switching diode manufactured by Vishay. It is designed for applications that require high voltage capability and fast switching performance. This component is AEC-Q101 qualified, making it suitable for automotive applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Continuous Reverse Voltage (V_R): 240 V
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 300 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 225 mA
  - Peak Repetitive Forward Current (I_FRM): 625 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FSM): 
    - 4.0 A (tp = 1 μs)
    - 1.0 A (tp = 1 s)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_tot): 350 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - T_op: -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Storage Temperature Range:**
  - T_stg: -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23
- **Special Features:**
  - Fast switching dual in-series diode
  - AEC-Q101 qualified
  - RoHS-compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOT-23 packages are MSL 1 or 2.

**Typical Applications:**
The GSD2004S-HE3-08 is commonly used in:
- High voltage switching applications
- Automotive electronics where high reliability and performance are required
- Signal processing circuits
- Power management systems

This diode is particularly suited for environments where space is limited and high voltage handling is necessary, making it ideal for compact electronic designs in automotive and industrial applications.
