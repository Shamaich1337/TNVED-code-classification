### Component Summary: KPT-1608SURCK

**Manufacturer:** Kingbright  
**Product Code:** KPT-1608SURCK  
**Type:** SMD LED Lamp  
**Color:** Hyper Red  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF): 1.95V (typical), 2.5V (maximum) at 20mA
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current: 30mA
  - Peak Forward Current: 185mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 75mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 1.6mm x 0.8mm SMD
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 3
- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (120°)
  - Ideal for backlight and indicator applications
  - RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The KPT-1608SURCK is a Hyper Red SMD LED lamp designed for various lighting applications. It utilizes Al-Ga-In-P technology on a GaAs substrate to emit light. The compact size of 1.6mm x 0.8mm and a thickness of 0.75mm make it suitable for applications where space is limited.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Backlighting:** Commonly used in displays and indicators where illumination is required.
- **Indicators:** Ideal for status indicators in consumer electronics, automotive dashboards, and appliances.
- **Signal Processing:** Can be used in communication devices for visual signaling.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Suitable for various electronic devices requiring compact and efficient lighting solutions.

This LED is particularly advantageous in applications that require a bright, efficient light source with a wide viewing angle, making it versatile for both consumer and industrial uses.
