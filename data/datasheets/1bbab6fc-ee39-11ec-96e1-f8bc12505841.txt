**Description:**
The PE014005 is a miniature PCB relay designed for general-purpose applications. It features a single pole with a 1 form C (CO) contact configuration, allowing it to switch both normally open (NO) and normally closed (NC) circuits. The relay is characterized by its low height of 10.0 mm and is suitable for use in various electronic devices.

**Typical Applications:**
The PE014005 relay is commonly used in:
- **Industrial Electronics:** For controlling machinery and automation systems.
- **White Goods:** In household appliances such as washing machines and refrigerators.
- **Measurement and Control:** In devices that require precise control of electrical signals and power management.

This relay is ideal for applications where space is limited, and reliable switching is essential, making it a versatile choice for engineers and designers in various fields.
