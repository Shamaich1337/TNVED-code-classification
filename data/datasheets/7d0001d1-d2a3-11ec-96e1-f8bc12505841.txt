### Component Summary: SN74LVC244APWR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 1.65 V to 3.6 V
  - Input Voltage: Accepts voltages up to 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 1 µA (typical) at VCC = 3.6 V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 500 mW (maximum)
  - Power Dissipation Capacitance: 44 pF (typical at 3.3 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: -40°C to +85°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package), 20 pins

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Supports mixed-mode signal operation (5-V input/output with 3.3-V VCC)
  - I_off supports live insertion and partial-power-down mode
  - ESD protection exceeds JESD 22 standards (2000-V HBM, 1000-V CDM)
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 250 mA per JESD 17
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SN74LVC244A** is an octal buffer/driver with three-state outputs designed for asynchronous communication between data buses. It features two sets of four buffers, allowing for efficient data transfer while providing high drive capability. The device is particularly useful for applications requiring voltage level translation, as it can accept input voltages up to 5.5 V while operating at a lower supply voltage (VCC) of 1.65 V to 3.6 V.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Servers:** Enhancing data communication between components.
- **LED Displays:** Driving LED segments with high current capability.
- **Network Switches:** Buffering signals in networking equipment.
- **Telecom Infrastructure:** Ensuring reliable data transmission in communication systems.
- **Motor Drivers:** Controlling motor operations with precise signal management.
- **I/O Expanders:** Expanding input/output capabilities in microcontroller applications.

This component is ideal for applications where signal integrity and voltage compatibility are critical, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
