### Component Summary: MACA-242H+

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - DC Power: 5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - IF Current: 40 mA
  - LO Power: 10 mW
- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Power: 100 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C (Operating)
  - -55°C to 100°C (Storage)
- **Package Type:**
  - Ceramic, Case Style: DZ1034
- **Special Features:**
  - Low conversion loss (typical 6.1 dB)
  - Wide bandwidth (750 to 2400 MHz)
  - LTCC double balanced mixer with LO amplifier
  - Aqueous washable
  - Low profile and low cost
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 7,027,795
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The MACA-242H+ is an active mixer designed for RF applications. It is a low-cost, low-profile component that utilizes a double balanced mixer architecture with a local oscillator (LO) amplifier. This component is specifically engineered to provide low conversion loss and wide bandwidth, making it suitable for various communication applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **PCN (Personal Communication Networks)**
- **Defense and Weather Radar Systems**
- **Cellular Communication**
- **UHF Television Broadcasting**

The MACA-242H+ is ideal for use in systems requiring efficient signal processing, such as in RF front-end modules, where it can effectively mix signals from different frequency bands while maintaining high performance and reliability.
