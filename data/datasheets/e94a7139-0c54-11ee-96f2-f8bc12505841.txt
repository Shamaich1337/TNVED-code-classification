### Component Summary: HMC220BMS8GE

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Local Oscillator (LO) Input Power: 25 dBm (max)
  - RF Input Power: 25 dBm (max)
  - IF Input Power: 25 dBm (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - IF Source and Sink Current: 3 mA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Continuous Power Dissipation: 495 mW (at 85°C, derate 5.5 mW/°C above 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage: -65°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Lead Mini Small Outline Package with Exposed Pad (MINI_SO_EP)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low conversion loss: 9 dB
  - No DC bias and no external matching required
  - Wideband IF range: DC to 4 GHz
  - RoHS compliant, eliminating the need for wire bonding

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (Peak Reflow Temperature: 260°C)

### Description:
The **HMC220B** is an ultraminiature, double-balanced mixer designed for operation in the frequency range of **5 GHz to 12 GHz**. It is constructed using gallium arsenide (GaAs) Schottky diodes and planar transformer baluns, making it suitable for both upconversion and downconversion applications. The device is packaged in an 8-lead MINI_SO_EP format, which facilitates high-volume surface-mount manufacturing.

### Typical Applications:
The HMC220B is widely used in various applications, including:
- **Very Small Aperture Terminals (VSAT)** and mobile satellite communication terminals
- **Microwave and military radio** systems
- **Wireless backhaul equipment**
- **Automotive applications**, including Dedicated Short Range Communications (DSRC) and Intelligent Vehicle Highway Systems (IVHS)
- **Military radar**, electronic warfare (EW), and electronic countermeasure (ECM) subsystems

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal processing in high-frequency environments, providing excellent isolation between the local oscillator, radio frequency, and intermediate frequency signals.
