**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): 1.25 V (B Grade: 1.2485 V to 1.2515 V; A Grade: 1.2470 V to 1.2530 V)
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.7 V to 18 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: +5 mA / -2 mA
  - Quiescent Current (IQ): 80 μA to 95 μA (depending on input voltage)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low typical operating current of 85 μA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 6-Lead TSOT (UJ-6)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low dropout voltage: 1.45 V (VOUT - VIN)
  - High initial accuracy: ±0.12% (B Grade), ±0.24% (A Grade)
  - Low temperature coefficient: 9 ppm/°C (B Grade), 25 ppm/°C (A Grade)
  - Patented temperature-drift curvature correction technique
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The ADR127 is a precision, micropower, low dropout (LDO) voltage reference from Analog Devices. It is designed to provide a stable output voltage with high accuracy and low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-powered applications. The ADR127 features a bandgap reference architecture that minimizes output voltage variations with temperature changes, ensuring reliable performance across a wide temperature range.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Battery-Powered Instrumentation:** The low quiescent current and low dropout voltage make it ideal for devices that rely on battery power.
- **Portable Medical Equipment:** High precision and stability are crucial in medical devices, where accurate voltage references are needed.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** The ADR127 can be used in systems that require precise voltage references for accurate data collection.
- **Automotive Applications:** Its wide operating temperature range and robustness make it suitable for automotive electronics.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the ADR127AUJZ-REEL7, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical applications in various electronic systems.
