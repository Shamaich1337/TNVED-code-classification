### Component Summary: STPS1H100U

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 1 A
  - Forward RMS Current (IF(RMS)): 10 A
  - Surge Non-Repetitive Forward Current (IFSM): 50 A (10 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Forward Voltage Drop (VF(max)): 0.62 V at 1 A
  - Average Forward Power Dissipation: P = 0.54 x IF(AV) + 0.08 x IF²(RMS)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (Tj(max)): 175 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SMB, SMA, SMA Flat, and SMA Flat Notch packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Negligible switching losses
  - High junction temperature capability
  - Low leakage current
  - Avalanche capability specified
  - ECOPACK2 halogen-free component
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The STPS1H100U is a Schottky rectifier designed for high-frequency applications, particularly in miniature switched-mode power supplies such as adapters and onboard DC/DC converters. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for efficient power conversion.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power management in switched-mode power supplies
- DC/DC converters for efficient voltage regulation
- Lighting applications, including LED drivers
- Telecom power applications, where reliability and efficiency are critical

This component is particularly suited for applications requiring low power loss and high efficiency, making it a popular choice in modern electronic designs.
