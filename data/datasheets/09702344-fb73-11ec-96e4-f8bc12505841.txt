  - Miniature waterproof plastic connector

- **Special Features**: 
  - Waterproof construction (IP67 and IP68)
  - Push/pull locking mechanism
  - Lightweight design (approximately 6g for plug and receptacle)
  - Mis-insertion prevention with polarized mating
  - RoHS compliant materials

#### Description:
The HR30-6R-6P(71) is a miniature waterproof plastic connector designed for reliable electrical connections in environments where moisture and dust are concerns. It features a compact design with a maximum outer diameter of Ø12.6mm and a mated length of 32.3mm. The connector is equipped with a push/pull locking mechanism that ensures secure mating and easy operation.

#### Typical Applications:
The HR30 series connectors are typically used in applications requiring robust and waterproof connections, such as:
- **Automotive**: For connecting various electronic components in vehicles where exposure to moisture is common.
- **Industrial Equipment**: In machinery and equipment that operate in harsh environments.
- **Consumer Electronics**: For devices that require reliable connections in outdoor or wet conditions.
- **Telecommunications**: In systems where moisture ingress could affect performance.

This connector is particularly suitable for applications that demand high reliability and durability, making it an excellent choice for both commercial and industrial uses.
