#### Description:
The CTS 218-4LPSTJ is a half-pitch, surface mount DIP switch designed for compact applications. It features a Single Pole Single Throw (SPST) configuration with 4 positions. The switch is characterized by its low profile actuators, which help to minimize the risk of accidental actuation. The design includes a positive detent mechanism that ensures the contactor does not deflect during actuation, enhancing reliability. The switch is also optimized for soldering processes, making it suitable for environments where board washing is necessary.
