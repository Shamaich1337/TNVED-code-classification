**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 9 - 36 VDC (24 VDC nominal)
  - Output Voltage: 5 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 4,000 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power: 20 W
  - Efficiency: Up to 89%
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +75°C (up to +85°C with heat sink)
- **Package Type:**
  - Ultra-compact metal casing: 1.0" x 1.0" x 0.4"
- **Special Features:**
  - Remote On/Off control
  - Adjustable output voltage
  - I/O isolation voltage: 1600 VDC
  - Input filter meets EN 55032 class A without external components
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The THN 20-2411WI is a high-performance DC/DC converter from the THN 20WI series by Traco Power. It is designed to provide a regulated output voltage of 5 VDC with a maximum output current of 4,000 mA, making it suitable for various applications requiring reliable power conversion. The converter features an ultra-compact size, making it ideal for space-constrained environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The THN 20-2411WI is commonly used in:
- Mobile equipment
- Instrumentation
- Distributed power architectures in communication systems
- Industrial electronics
- Battery-operated systems due to its low no-load input current and remote On/Off control feature.

This converter is particularly beneficial in applications where high efficiency and compact design are critical, as well as in environments with varying temperature conditions.
