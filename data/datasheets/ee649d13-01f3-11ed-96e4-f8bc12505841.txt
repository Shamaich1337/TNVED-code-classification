### UPRtek MK350N Premium Spectrometer Summary

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Not explicitly stated in the provided text.
- **Current Ratings**: Not explicitly stated in the provided text.
- **Power Consumption**: 2500 mAh (3.7V rechargeable Li-ion battery).

- **Storage Temperature Range**: -10 to 40 °C, relative humidity 70% or less without condensation.
- **Package Type**: Handheld device with a 3.5" resistive touchscreen.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.
- **Special Features**: 
  - WiFi connectivity for remote control via mobile app.
  - Multiple measurement modes including basic parameters, spectrum, CRI, CIE color coordinates, TM-30-15, flicker measurement, and more.
  - Data storage capability of approximately 68,000 files on an 8GB SD card.
  - USB connectivity for data transfer and charging.

#### Description:
The **MK350N Premium** is a portable spectrometer designed for measuring light parameters. It utilizes a CMOS sensor to capture light spectra in the range of 380 to 780 nm with a resolution of 1 nm. The device is capable of measuring various light characteristics, including illuminance (lux), correlated color temperature (CCT), color rendering index (CRI), and flicker metrics. It features a user-friendly interface with a touchscreen display and supports data export in Excel and JPG formats.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Lighting Quality Assessment**: Used in the evaluation of lighting conditions in various environments, ensuring compliance with standards for color quality and flicker.
- **Color Science**: Employed in research and development for color measurement and analysis in industries such as photography, film, and design.
- **Agriculture**: Utilized in horticulture to assess light conditions for plant growth.
- **Manufacturing**: Applied in quality control processes to ensure that lighting products meet specified performance criteria.
- **Environmental Monitoring**: Used to measure and analyze light pollution and its effects on ecosystems.

This spectrometer is particularly valuable for professionals in lighting design, environmental science, and color technology, providing precise measurements and data analysis capabilities.
