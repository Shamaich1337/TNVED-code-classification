- **Frequency Synthesizers:** Used in RF applications to generate stable frequencies.
- **Radio Communication:** Ideal for use in radio transmitters and receivers.
- **Phase-Locked Loops (PLLs):** Commonly integrated into PLL circuits for frequency modulation and demodulation.
- **Signal Processing:** Employed in various signal processing applications where precise frequency control is required.

This component is particularly useful in systems that require high-frequency stability and low noise, such as in telecommunications and radar systems.
