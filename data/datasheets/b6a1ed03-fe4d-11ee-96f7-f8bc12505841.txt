### Component Summary: BS170FTA

**Description:**
The BS170FTA is an N-Channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for low-voltage applications. It features a low threshold voltage and low input capacitance, making it suitable for high-speed switching applications. This component is particularly useful in interfacing with logic-level signals and driving various loads.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 60 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25 °C: 250 mA
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100 °C: 150 mA
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 800 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 25 °C: 0.30 W
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 100 °C: 0.13 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low threshold voltage (typical 2 V)
  - Fast switching speed (25 ns)
  - Low input and output leakage
  - 1200V ESD protection
  - Halogen-free and compliant with RoHS Directive 2002/95/EC

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The BS170FTA is commonly used in:
- Direct logic-level interfacing (TTL/CMOS)
- Driving relays, solenoids, lamps, and other loads
- Battery-operated systems
- Solid-state relays
- High-speed circuits requiring low error voltage and low offset voltage

This component is ideal for applications where efficient switching and low power consumption are critical, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
