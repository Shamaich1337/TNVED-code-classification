  - Junction temperature (Tj): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Ambient temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type**: SOD323 (SC-76) plastic surface-mounted package
- **Special Features**: 
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Non-repetitive peak reverse power dissipation of ≤ 40 W
  - Available in two tolerance series: ±2% (BZX384-B) and approximately ±5% (BZX384-C)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for SMD components.

#### Description:
The BZX384-C8V2 is a low-power voltage regulator diode designed for surface mounting. It is part of the BZX384 series, which includes a range of diodes with various breakdown voltages. The primary function of this component is to provide voltage regulation, ensuring that the output voltage remains stable despite variations in load current or input voltage.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: The BZX384 series is commonly used in applications requiring stable voltage regulation, such as power supplies and voltage reference circuits.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics, where reliability and performance under varying conditions are critical.
- **Consumer Electronics**: It can be utilized in various consumer electronic devices that require voltage regulation to protect sensitive components.
