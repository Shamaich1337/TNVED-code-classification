### Component Summary: OP3405D SFP Transceiver

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.135V to 3.465V (typical 3.3V)
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: 4V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 300 mA
  - Inrush Current (Isurge): Icc + 30 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power: < 1W (typically)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: 0°C to 70°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) module with Duplex LC connector
- **Special Features:**
  - Hot-pluggable
  - 850nm VCSEL laser transmitter
  - Monitoring Interface compliant with SFF-8472
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The OP3405D is a high-performance SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceiver module designed for 1.25 Gb/s data links. It features a duplex LC optical interface and utilizes an 850nm VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) for transmission. The module is hot-pluggable, allowing for easy installation and replacement without powering down the system. It is compliant with the SFF-8472 standard for digital diagnostics, enabling monitoring of key parameters such as temperature, laser power, and supply voltage.

**Typical Applications:**
The OP3405D transceiver is primarily used in:
- Metro and access networks
- 1.25 Gb/s 1000Base-SX Ethernet applications
- Fiber Channel connections
- Other optical link applications where high-speed data transmission over multimode fiber (MMF) is required, with a maximum link length of 550 meters on 50/125μm MMF.

This transceiver is suitable for various networking and communication systems, providing reliable and efficient data transfer in environments where space and power efficiency are critical.
