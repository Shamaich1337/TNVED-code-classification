### Component Summary: HFCN-1910+

**Description:**
The HFCN-1910+ is a high-pass filter designed for RF applications, specifically operating within the frequency range of 2000 to 5200 MHz. It is a ceramic filter that utilizes Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) technology, which provides excellent temperature stability and power handling capabilities. The component is hermetically sealed, ensuring reliability in various environmental conditions.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, the RF power input is rated at a maximum of 37W at 25°C.
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified; current handling is typically inferred from power ratings.
- **Power Consumption:** Maximum RF power input of 37W at 25°C, derating linearly to 3W at 100°C ambient.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +100°C.
- **Package Type:** FV1206 (small size).
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss (typical 1.3 dB in the passband).
  - Excellent power handling (up to 7W).
  - Temperature stable with a hermetically sealed construction.
  - RoHS compliant.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The HFCN-1910+ is commonly used in:
- Sub-harmonic rejection applications.
- Transmitters and receivers in communication systems.
- Laboratory use for RF testing and signal processing.

This high-pass filter is ideal for applications requiring reliable performance in high-frequency environments, making it suitable for various RF and microwave applications.
