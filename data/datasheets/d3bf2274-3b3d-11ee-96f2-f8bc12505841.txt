### Component Summary: ZXMP7A17GTA

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** ZXMP7A17G  
**Document Number:** DS33594 Rev. 5 - 2  
**Date:** March 2015  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): -70V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at VGS = -10V: -2.6A
  - Continuous Drain Current at TA = +70°C: -2.9A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM) at VGS = -10V: -9.6A
  - Continuous Source Current (Body Diode) (IS): -4.8A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 2.0W (derating factor: 16.0 mW/°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT223

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance: 160mΩ @ VGS = -10V, 250mΩ @ VGS = -4.5V
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - 100% Unclamped Inductive Switch (UIS) Test in Production
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free; "Green" Device
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The ZXMP7A17G is a P-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for high efficiency in power management applications. It features low on-state resistance and superior switching performance, making it suitable for various power management tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Control:** Used in applications requiring efficient control of motor operations.
- **Transformer Driving Switch:** Acts as a switch in transformer circuits for efficient energy transfer.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Utilized in circuits that convert direct current from one voltage level to another.
- **Power Management Functions:** Employed in systems that manage power distribution and efficiency.
- **Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS):** Integral in systems that provide backup power during outages.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where efficiency and reliability are critical, such as automotive and industrial power management systems.
