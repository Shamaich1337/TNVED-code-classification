### Component Summary: PTA09N90A-220F

**Description:**
The PTA09N90A is a 900V N-Channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) produced by Perfect Intelligent Power Semiconductor Co., Ltd. It utilizes proprietary new planar technology, which enhances its performance characteristics. The primary function of this component is to act as a switch or amplifier in electronic circuits, allowing for efficient control of electrical power.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDSS): 900 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 9.0 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at Tc=100°C: Not specified
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): Not specified

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 75 W
  - Derating Factor above 25°C: 0.60 W/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-220F

- **Special Features:**
  - R_DS(ON), typ. = 0.98 Ω @ V_GS = 10V
  - Low gate charge to minimize switching loss
  - Fast recovery body diode

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The PTA09N90A is commonly used in various applications, including:
- Adaptor chargers
- Switched-Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)
- Power supplies for LCD panels

This MOSFET is particularly suitable for high-voltage applications due to its high breakdown voltage and low on-resistance, making it ideal for efficient power management and signal processing in electronic devices.
