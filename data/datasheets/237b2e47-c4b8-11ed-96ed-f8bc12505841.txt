### Component Summary: HMC1096LP3E

**Description:**
The HMC1096LP3E is a GaAs pHEMT MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) active frequency multiplier designed to double input frequencies in the range of 1.9 to 2.8 GHz, producing output frequencies from 3.8 to 5.6 GHz. It is packaged in a leadless RoHS-compliant Low Stress Injection Molded Plastic SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): +5 V (max +6 V)
  - Gate Voltage (VG1, VG2): +2 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 100 mA (typical)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1.2 W (at T = 85 °C, derating at 13.3 mW/°C above 85 °C)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 16 Lead 3 x 3 mm SMT Package
  
- **Special Features:**
  - High Output Power: 12 dBm (typical)
  - Low Input Power Drive: -2 to +5 dBm
  - Fo, 3 Fo Isolation: +22 dBc
  - Phase Noise (@ 10 KHz Offset): -142 dBc/Hz
  - DC blocked I/Os
  - ESD Sensitivity (HBM): Class 0, passed 150 V
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC1096LP3E is suitable for various applications, including:
- Point-to-Point and VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) radios
- Test instrumentation
- Military and space applications

This component is particularly useful in LO (Local Oscillator) multiplier chains, providing a compact solution that reduces parts count compared to traditional methods. Its high output power and efficiency make it ideal for RF signal processing in communication systems.
