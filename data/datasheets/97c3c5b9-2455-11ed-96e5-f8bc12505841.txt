### Component Summary: INA210BIRSWR (Texas Instruments)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V+): 2.7 V to 26 V
  - Common-Mode Voltage Range: -0.3 V to 26 V (Version A), -0.1 V to 26 V (Versions B and C)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Current: 100 µA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Supply Voltage: 26 V
  - Operating Voltage Range: 2.7 V to 26 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Specified Range: -40 °C to +125 °C
  - Extended Range: -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - UQFN (10 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Zero-drift architecture for low offset voltage (±35 µV maximum for INA210)
  - High common-mode rejection ratio (CMRR) of 105 dB to 140 dB
  - Multiple gain options: 50, 75, 100, 200, 500, and 1000 V/V
  - Low offset drift (0.5 µV/°C maximum)
  - Low gain drift (10 ppm/°C maximum)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The INA210BIRSWR is a current-shunt monitor (also known as a current-sense amplifier) designed for high-precision current measurement in various applications. It features a voltage output and can operate in both low-side and high-side configurations. The device is capable of measuring voltage drops across shunt resistors with a common-mode voltage range that exceeds the supply voltage, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to monitor current flow and ensure efficient operation.
- **Battery Chargers:** Helps in monitoring charging currents to optimize battery performance and longevity.
- **Telecom Equipment:** Utilized in telecom systems for current monitoring to prevent overcurrent conditions.
- **Notebook Computers and Cell Phones:** Employed in portable devices for accurate current sensing to enhance battery life and performance.

The INA210 series is particularly beneficial in applications requiring high accuracy and low power consumption, making it ideal for battery-operated devices and systems where space is limited.
