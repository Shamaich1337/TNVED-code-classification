**Component Type:**  
Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor

**Description:**  
The RP332M400SA05E53HWRZ is a long-life aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's RP series, designed for high reliability and performance in various electronic applications. It features a screw-type terminal for secure mounting and is suitable for use in environments with elevated temperatures.

- **Capacitance (CR):** 3300 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 400 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 440 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR):** 
  - 11 A at 120Hz and 85°C
  - 15.39 A at 100kHz and 85°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ):** 15% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK):** 2873 µA (measured for 5 minutes at 20°C)
- **Equivalent Series Resistance (ESR):** 23 mΩ at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:** Screw type, dimensions 63.5 mm (D) x 105 mm (L)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

### Special Features:
- Designed for long life at high temperatures (85°C).
- High ripple current capability.
- Suitable for applications requiring high capacitance and voltage ratings.

### Typical Applications:
The RP332M400SA05E53HWRZ aluminum electrolytic capacitor is typically used in:
- Power supply circuits for smoothing and filtering applications.
- Energy storage in power management systems.
- Decoupling and bypassing in electronic circuits.
- Applications in industrial equipment, automotive electronics, and consumer electronics where reliability and longevity are critical.

This capacitor is particularly well-suited for environments where high ripple currents and elevated temperatures are present, making it ideal for use in power electronics and other demanding applications.
