### Component Summary: LD1117ASTR

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** LD1117ASTR  
**Description:** The LD1117A is a low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with low dropout voltage and low quiescent current. It can deliver up to 1 A of output current and is available in both fixed and adjustable output voltage configurations.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Fixed output voltages: 1.2 V, 1.8 V, 3.3 V
  - Adjustable output voltage: from 1.25 V (VREF)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum output current: 1 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent current: 5 mA (typical at 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction temperature: 0 °C to 125 °C
  - Storage temperature: -40 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT-223, DPAK, and TO-220 packages

- **Special Features:**
  - Low dropout voltage: 1.15 V typical at 1 A output
  - High supply voltage rejection: 80 dB typical at 25 °C
  - Internal current and thermal limit protection
  - Requires only a 10 µF capacitor for stability
  - Output voltage tolerance: ± 2% at 25 °C and ± 4% over the full temperature range

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The LD1117A is a low-dropout linear voltage regulator that provides a regulated output voltage with minimal voltage drop from the input to the output. It is designed for applications requiring a stable voltage supply with low power loss, making it suitable for battery-powered devices and other applications where efficiency is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to provide stable voltage levels for various electronic components.
- **Signal Processing:** Provides clean and stable power to sensitive analog circuits, ensuring accurate signal processing.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other portable electronics where space and efficiency are important.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for microcontrollers and other embedded systems that require a reliable power source.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the LD1117ASTR voltage regulator, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
