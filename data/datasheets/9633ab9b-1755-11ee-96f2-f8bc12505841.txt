### Component Summary: PTN78020WAH (Texas Instruments)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 
    - PTN78020W: 7V to 36V
    - PTN78020H: 15V to 36V
  - Output Voltage Range:
    - PTN78020W: 2.5V to 12.6V
    - PTN78020H: 11.85V to 22V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 6A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Output Power: 90W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount Module (EUL) or Through-Hole Module (EUK)

- **Special Features:**
  - High Efficiency (up to 96%)
  - On/Off Inhibit Control
  - Undervoltage Lockout
  - Output Current Limit
  - Overtemperature Shutdown

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 3 (260°C, 168 hours) per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The PTN78020 is a high-efficiency, step-down integrated switching regulator (ISR) designed for industrial applications. It is part of a series of power modules that provide adjustable output voltage and are suitable for a wide range of input voltages. The PTN78020 is smaller and lighter than its predecessors, offering improved electrical performance characteristics. It features a caseless, double-sided package that enhances thermal performance and is compliant with RoHS and lead-free standards.

#### Typical Applications:
The PTN78020 is commonly used in:
- General-purpose power management
- Industrial controls
- HVAC systems
- Test and measurement equipment
- Medical instrumentation
- AC/DC adapters
- Automotive, marine, and avionics applications

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient voltage regulation with a wide input range and adjustable output, making it versatile for various electronic designs.
