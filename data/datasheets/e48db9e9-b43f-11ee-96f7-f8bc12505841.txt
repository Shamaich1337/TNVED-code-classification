### Component Summary: HI-8445PST

**Manufacturer:** Holt Integrated Circuits Inc.  
**Product Code:** HI-8445PST

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 3.0 V to 5.5 V
  - Logic Input Voltage Range: -0.3 V to +5.5 V
  - ARINC Input Voltage: -120 V to +120 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 5.5 mA (typical for HI-8445)
  - Driver Peak Output Current: +1.0 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at 25°C: 350 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial Screening: -40°C to +85°C
  - Hi-Temp Screening: -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 20-pin Plastic TSSOP (20HS)

- **Special Features:**
  - Quad ARINC 429 line receiver IC
  - Operates from either a 5V or 3.3V supply
  - Test inputs (TESTA and TESTB) for bypassing analog inputs (not available on HI-8445)
  - Lightning protection compliant with DO-160G (for -10 configurations)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The HI-8445 is a quad ARINC 429 line receiver integrated circuit (IC) designed for use in avionics and aerospace applications. It translates incoming ARINC 429 data bus signals into TTL/CMOS outputs. The device is built using analog/digital CMOS technology and is available in a compact 20-pin TSSOP package. The HI-8445 is similar to the HI-8444 but does not include the TESTA and TESTB pins.

#### Typical Applications:
The HI-8445 is primarily used in:
- **Avionics Systems:** For receiving ARINC 429 data, which is a standard for data communication in aircraft.
- **Data Communication:** In systems requiring reliable data transfer and signal processing.
- **Signal Processing:** Where conversion of ARINC signals to digital outputs is necessary for further processing or interfacing with other digital systems.

This component is particularly suited for applications that require robust performance in harsh environments, such as those found in aerospace and military systems.
