### Component Summary: IS2801-4 by ISOCOM

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (V_F): 1.2V to 1.4V at 20mA
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (BV_CEO): 80V
  - Emitter-Collector Breakdown Voltage (BV_ECO): 7V
  - Isolation Voltage: 3000V RMS

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 50mA (max)
  - Output Current (I_C): 50mA (max)
  - Reverse Current (I_R): 10µA at 4V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Input Power Dissipation: 70mW
  - Output Power Dissipation: 100mW
  - Total Power Dissipation: 170mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -55°C to 110°C
  - Storage: -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount, Package Code: THP4

- **Special Features:**
  - High AC Isolation voltage of 3000V RMS
  - Pb Free and RoHS Compliant
  - UL Approval E91231

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The IS2801-4 is a four-channel optically coupled isolator, which consists of an infrared emitting diode optically coupled to an NPN silicon phototransistor for each channel. This component is part of ISOCOM's Compact Range of Optocouplers, designed to provide electrical isolation between its input and output while allowing signal transmission.

#### Typical Applications:
The IS2801-4 is suitable for various applications, including:
- **Hybrid Substrates with High Density Mounting:** Ideal for compact electronic designs where space is limited.
- **Industrial System Controllers:** Used in automation and control systems to isolate different parts of the circuit.
- **Measuring Instruments:** Provides isolation in measurement devices to protect sensitive components.
- **System Appliances:** Commonly found in consumer electronics and appliances requiring signal isolation.

This component is particularly useful in environments where high voltage isolation is necessary, ensuring safety and reliability in electronic systems.
