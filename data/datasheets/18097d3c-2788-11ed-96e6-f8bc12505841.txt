### Component Summary: US1M Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** US1M  
**Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1000 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 700 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 1000 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1.0 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 30 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave)
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 60 A (non-repetitive, 1ms square wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 1.0 A: 1.7 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - MSL Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - High forward surge capability
  - Super fast reverse recovery time
  - RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The US1M is a surface mount high-efficiency rectifier designed for high-frequency rectification applications. It features a low profile, making it suitable for automated assembly processes. The glass passivated chip junction enhances reliability and performance, particularly in demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The US1M rectifier is commonly used in:
- Power supplies
- Inverters
- Converters
- Freewheeling diodes for consumer electronics
- Telecommunication equipment

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient rectification and high surge current handling, making it a versatile choice in various electronic circuits.
