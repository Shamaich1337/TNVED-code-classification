  - TTL/CMOS-compatible inputs
  - Low leakage currents (100 pA typical)

It is designed for low-voltage applications and features a low ON resistance, which minimizes signal distortion. The device is particularly useful in applications where low power consumption is critical, such as in portable and battery-operated devices.

- **Battery-Powered Systems:** Ideal for applications where power efficiency is essential, such as handheld devices and portable instruments.
