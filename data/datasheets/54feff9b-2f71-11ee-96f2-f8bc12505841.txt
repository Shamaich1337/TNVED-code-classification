### Component Summary: YJD212080NCFG1

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** YJD212080NCFG1  
**Type:** Silicon Carbide Power MOSFET (N-Channel Enhancement)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS,max): 1200 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS,max): -8/+22 V (absolute maximum), -4/+18 V (recommended operational)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 39 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: 28 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulsed): 80 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PTOT) at 25°C: 223 W
  - Power Dissipation at 110°C: 97 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (Tj, Tstg): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO247-4L

- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed switching with essentially no switching losses
  - Reduction of heat sink requirements
  - Fast intrinsic diode with low recovery current
  - High-frequency operation
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The YJD212080NCFG1 is a high-performance N-Channel Silicon Carbide (SiC) Power MOSFET designed for high-voltage applications. It features high-speed switching capabilities and low on-resistance, making it suitable for efficient power management. The device operates effectively at elevated temperatures, with a maximum junction temperature of 175°C, which allows for reduced cooling requirements in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The YJD212080NCFG1 is commonly used in:
- Power Factor Correction (PFC) circuits
- Solar inverters
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Motor drives
- Photovoltaic inverters
- Electric vehicles and chargers

This component is ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability in power conversion and management systems. Its robust design and high voltage ratings make it suitable for demanding environments in renewable energy and electric mobility sectors.
