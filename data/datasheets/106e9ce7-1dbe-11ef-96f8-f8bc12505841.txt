### Component Summary: APDA3020LSECK/J3-PF

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF): 1.8V (typical) to 2.1V (maximum) at 2mA
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 30 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 150 mA
  - Reverse Current (IR): 10 µA (at VR = 5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 84 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +85°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 115°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 3.0 mm x 2.0 mm Right Angle SMD LED, 2.8 mm thickness

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Tinned pads for improved solderability
  - Electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection: 3000 V (HBM)

#### Description:
The APDA3020LSECK/J3-PF is a Hyper Red surface-mount light-emitting diode (LED) based on an aluminum gallium indium phosphide (AlGaInP) chip. It is designed for low power consumption and is suitable for various lighting applications. The LED emits light in the Hyper Red spectrum, with a peak wavelength of 640 nm and a dominant wavelength of 625 nm.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Backlighting:** Ideal for use in displays and indicators where backlighting is required.
- **Status Indicators:** Commonly used in consumer electronics to indicate power status or operational states.
- **Home and Smart Appliances:** Utilized in various household devices for visual feedback.
- **Wearable and Portable Devices:** Suitable for compact applications where space is limited.
- **Healthcare Applications:** Can be used in medical devices for signaling and indicators.

This LED is particularly useful in applications where space is constrained, and low power consumption is critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
