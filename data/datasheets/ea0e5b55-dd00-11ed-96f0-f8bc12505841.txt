**Product Code:** LMV321LICT  
**Description:** The LMV321L is a low-power, general-purpose operational amplifier designed for battery-powered and portable applications. It features rail-to-rail output capability and operates efficiently at low supply voltages.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.7 V to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (per channel): 250 µA max at 5 V
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum power consumption: 250 µA at 5 V
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SC70-5 (SOT323-5)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low offset voltage: 7 mV max at 25 °C
  - Gain bandwidth product: 1.3 MHz
  - Rail-to-rail output capability
  - Input common-mode range includes ground
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The LMV321L is a single operational amplifier that is optimized for low-voltage operation, making it suitable for applications where power efficiency is critical. It provides a low offset voltage and a wide operating temperature range, which enhances its performance in various environments. The rail-to-rail output allows the amplifier to utilize the full range of the supply voltage, making it versatile for different circuit designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Battery-Powered Applications:** Ideal for devices that require low power consumption to extend battery life.
- **Portable Devices:** Suitable for compact electronic devices where space is limited.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Used in circuits that require amplification of weak signals.
- **Active Filtering:** Can be employed in filter circuits to process signals.
- **Medical Instrumentation:** Useful in medical devices that require precise signal processing under varying environmental conditions.

This operational amplifier is a cost-effective solution for designers looking to optimize performance while minimizing power consumption in their applications.
