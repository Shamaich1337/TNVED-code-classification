### Description:
The CC2-1000 is a **Simplex Plastic Optical Fiber Cable** designed for transmitting optical signals. It utilizes a core made of polymethylmethacrylate, which provides good light transmission properties, and is surrounded by a fluorinated polymer cladding. This cable is characterized by its step index profile and a numerical aperture of 0.5, making it suitable for various optical applications.

### Typical Applications:
- **Data Communication:** Used in short-distance data transmission applications where flexibility and lightweight characteristics are essential.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices requiring optical signal transmission, such as audio and video equipment.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for environments where resistance to bending and mechanical stress is necessary.
- **Lighting Applications:** Can be used in decorative lighting and illumination systems due to its flexibility and optical properties.
