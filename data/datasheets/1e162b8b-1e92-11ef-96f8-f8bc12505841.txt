### Component Summary: P4SMA12CA (Bourns Inc.)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 11.4V to 12.6V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 10V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1μA at VRWM
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (Ipp): 24.6A (8/20 µs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPK): 400 Watts (1 ms pulse duration)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount SMA (DO-214AC)
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Fast response time: <1.0 picosecond (unidirectional), <5.0 picoseconds (bidirectional)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - ESD Classification: 3B (HBM)

#### Description:
The P4SMA12CA is a Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge (ESD), electrical fast transients (EFT), and surge events. It is available in a compact surface mount package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Surge Protection:** Used in power management circuits to protect against voltage spikes.
- **ESD Protection:** Commonly employed in communication devices, computing equipment, and consumer electronics to safeguard sensitive components from ESD events.
- **Signal Integrity:** Helps maintain signal integrity in high-speed data lines by clamping voltage spikes that could disrupt signal transmission.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Suitable for automotive applications where transient voltage protection is critical.

This component is ideal for use in various electronic devices, including portable communications, computing, and video equipment, where robust protection against transient voltages is essential.
