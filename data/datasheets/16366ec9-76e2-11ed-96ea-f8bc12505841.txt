### Component Summary: P82B96DGKR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2V to 15V
  - Maximum Voltage on Pins: -0.3V to 18V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Continuous Output Current: 250 mA
  - Static Current Sink Capability: >30 mA
  - Dynamic Output Sink Capability: 100 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 0.9 mA (typical at VCC = 5V)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - VSSOP (DGK), 8-Pin
- **Special Features**: 
  - Supports bidirectional data transfer between I2C buses with different voltage levels.
  - Allows for longer cable lengths (up to 20 meters) and higher bus capacitance (up to 4000 pF).
  - Provides galvanic isolation capabilities.
  - High current sink capability for driving low-impedance or high-capacitive buses.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **P82B96** is an I2C-compatible dual bidirectional bus buffer designed to facilitate communication between I2C buses operating at different voltage levels. It effectively isolates bus capacitance, allowing for longer cable lengths and more devices on the I2C bus without degrading performance. The device features separate transmit (Tx) and receive (Rx) paths, enabling it to handle bidirectional data transfer while maintaining signal integrity.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Long-Distance I2C Communication**: The P82B96 is ideal for applications requiring communication over extended distances, such as connecting devices in different locations within a system.
- **Galvanic Isolation**: It can be used in systems where electrical isolation is necessary, such as interfacing with optocouplers.
- **Voltage Level Translation**: The device can translate between different logic levels, making it suitable for interfacing components operating at various voltage levels.
- **Industrial Communications**: It is commonly used in industrial settings where robust communication over long distances is required, such as in sensor networks and control systems.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the P82B96DGKR, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical use cases in electronic systems.
