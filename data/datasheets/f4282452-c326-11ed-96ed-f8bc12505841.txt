### Component Summary: MURS260T3G

**Description:**
The MURS260T3G is a surface mount ultrafast power rectifier designed for high voltage and high frequency rectification applications. It is particularly suitable for use as freewheeling and protection diodes in compact electronic systems where size and weight are critical.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 600 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 600 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage: 600 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 2.0 A at T_L = 125 °C
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 35 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF): 
    - 1.45 V at 2.0 A, T_J = 25 °C
    - 1.20 V at 2.0 A, T_J = 150 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMB (Surface Mount) Case 403A

- **Special Features:**
  - High Temperature Glass Passivated Junction
  - Low Forward Voltage Drop
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified and PPAP Capable
  - Pb-Free and RoHS Compliant
  - ESD Ratings: Human Body Model = 3B (> 8 kV)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The MURS260T3G is commonly used in:
- Power management circuits
- High-frequency rectification applications
- Freewheeling diodes in switching power supplies
- Protection diodes in automotive and other electronic systems requiring compact components

This component is ideal for applications where efficiency and reliability are paramount, particularly in automotive and industrial electronics.
