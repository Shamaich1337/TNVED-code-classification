### Component Summary: IRF7380TRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** Power MOSFET (HEXFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (V_DS): 80 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (V_GS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D) at 25°C: 3.6 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D) at 100°C: 2.9 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (I_DM): 29 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (P_D) at 25°C: 3.6 W
  - Linear Derating Factor: 0.02 W/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low Gate-to-Drain Charge to Reduce Switching Losses
  - Fully Characterized Capacitance Including Effective C_OSS
  - Fully Characterized Avalanche Voltage and Current
  - Lead-Free

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRF7380TRPBF is a high-performance N-channel power MOSFET designed for high-frequency DC-DC converters and other power management applications. It features low on-resistance (R_DS(on) max of 73 mΩ at V_GS = 10V), which contributes to reduced conduction losses. The device is characterized for low gate charge, making it suitable for fast switching applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in DC-DC converters for efficient power conversion.
- **Switching Power Supplies:** Ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and low heat generation.
- **Motor Control:** Can be utilized in motor driver circuits due to its fast switching capabilities.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for applications where signal integrity and low distortion are critical.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in designs where efficiency and thermal performance are paramount, making it a preferred choice for modern electronic systems.
