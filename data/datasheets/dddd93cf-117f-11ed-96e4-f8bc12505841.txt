### Component Summary: IHLP2525CZER220M11

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Rated operating voltage across inductor = 50 V
- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Heat Rating Current (DC Typ.)**: 2.9 A
  - **Saturation Current (DC Typ.)**: 2.5 A
- **Power Consumption**: Not explicitly stated; however, the low DCR indicates efficient power handling.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: IHLP-2525CZ (dimensions: 0.125 x 0.255 x 0.270 inches)
- **Special Features**:
  - Shielded construction
  - Frequency range up to 1.0 MHz
  - Lowest DCR/μH in this package size
  - Soft saturation characteristics
  - Handles high transient current spikes without hard saturation
  - Ultra-low buzz noise due to composite construction
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IHLP2525CZER220M11 is a commercial inductor from Vishay's IHLP series, specifically designed for low DCR applications. It features a shielded construction that minimizes electromagnetic interference and is optimized for high-frequency applications. The inductor has an inductance value of 22 μH with a tolerance of ±20% at 100 kHz.

#### Typical Applications:
This inductor is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Power management in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers
- High current Point of Load (POL) converters
- Low profile, high current power supplies
- Battery-powered devices
- DC/DC converters in distributed power systems
- DC/DC converters for field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs)

The IHLP2525CZER220M11 is ideal for applications requiring efficient power conversion and management, particularly in compact electronic devices where space and thermal performance are critical.
