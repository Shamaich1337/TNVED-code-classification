### Component Summary: SKY13286-359LF

**Manufacturer:** Skyworks Solutions, Inc.  
**Product Code:** SKY13286-359LF  
**Type:** High Isolation SPDT Absorptive Switch

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.7 V to 5.5 V
  - Control Voltage (VCTL): 0 to 3 V or 0 to 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (VDD = 5 V): 100 µA
  - Control Current: 5 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum RF Input Power: 1 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_OP): -40 °C to +95 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead), 16-pin, 4 x 4 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High isolation of 64 dB at 1 GHz and 2 GHz
  - Integrated silicon CMOS driver
  - Absorptive switch design
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): MSL1, 260 °C per JEDEC J-STD-020

#### Description:
The SKY13286-359LF is a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) pHEMT FET high-isolation absorptive switch. It is designed for applications where high isolation is critical, such as in base station environments. The switch operates with a single positive voltage control, allowing for efficient switching between RF paths.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Base Stations:** Used in GSM, PCS, and WCDMA base stations where synthesizer isolation is essential.
- **ISM Devices:** Suitable for 2.4 and 5.8 GHz ISM (Industrial, Scientific, and Medical) applications.
- **Wireless Local Loops:** Ideal for wireless communication systems that require reliable signal routing.

This component is particularly valuable in RF applications where minimizing signal loss and maximizing isolation between different paths is crucial for performance.
