### Component Summary: GALI-39+ by Mini-Circuits

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.1 V to 3.9 V (typical 3.5 V)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Recommended Operating Current: 35 mA
  - Maximum Operating Current: 55 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: 123.5 mW (3.5 V * 35 mA)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89 (DF782)
- **Special Features:**
  - Wideband amplifier with frequency range from DC to 7 GHz
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - High output power (10.5 dBm typ.)
  - Low additive phase noise (-172 dBc/Hz @ 10 KHz offset)
  - ESD protection and rugged design for supply switch-on transients
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020C

#### Description:
The GALI-39+ is a monolithic InGaP HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) amplifier. It is designed for high dynamic range applications and offers repeatable performance across different production lots. The amplifier is housed in a compact SOT-89 package, which features an exposed metal bottom for effective heat dissipation. The device is particularly noted for its robustness against electrostatic discharge (ESD) and supply switch-on transients, making it suitable for demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The GALI-39+ is widely used in various communication applications, including:
- **Cellular and PCS (Personal Communications Service) systems**
- **Communication receivers and transmitters**
- **Low phase noise applications**, such as in RF signal processing
- **General RF amplification** in both commercial and industrial settings

This amplifier is ideal for applications requiring high-frequency operation and low noise, making it a valuable component in modern communication systems.
