### Component Summary: ILD217T

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** ILD217T  
**Document Number:** 84268  
**Revision Date:** 19-Jul-2023  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Reverse Voltage (V_R): 6 V
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (B_VCEO): 70 V
  - Emitter-Collector Breakdown Voltage (B_VECO): 7 V
  - Isolation Test Voltage (V_ISO): 4000 V RMS

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current per Channel (I_F): 30 mA
  - Peak Pulsed Current (1 μs, 300 pps): 1 A
  - Collector-Emitter Leakage Current (I_CEO): 5 to 50 nA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_diss) per Channel: 125 mW
  - Total Package Dissipation (2 LEDs and 2 detectors): 350 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature (T_amb): -55 to +100 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Dual channel optocoupler
  - High isolation voltage (4000 V RMS)
  - Compatible with various soldering methods (dual wave, vapor phase, IR reflow)
  - High collector-emitter breakdown voltage for enhanced safety margin

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

#### Description:
The ILD217T is a dual-channel optocoupler that consists of a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) infrared LED and a silicon NPN phototransistor. Its primary function is to transmit signal information, including DC levels, while providing a high degree of electrical isolation between the input and output. This makes it suitable for applications where electrical isolation is critical, such as in power management and signal processing.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to isolate different sections of the circuit, ensuring safety and preventing noise interference.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for transmitting signals in communication systems where isolation is necessary to protect sensitive components.
- **Data Communication:** Commonly used in data transmission systems to maintain signal integrity while providing electrical isolation.
- **Industrial Automation:** Utilized in control systems to isolate control signals from high-voltage or high-current circuits.

The ILD217T is particularly well-suited for high-density applications due to its compact SOIC-8 package, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
