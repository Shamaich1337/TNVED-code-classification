### Component Summary: AG203-63G

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Product Type:** InGaP HBT Gain Block  
**Package Type:** SOT-363  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - DC Supply Voltage: +4.75 V to +5.25 V (typical +5 V)
  - Absolute Maximum Device Voltage: +4.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Device Current: 20 mA (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output P1dB: +8 dBm at 900 MHz
  - Output IP3: +20 dBm at 900 MHz
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Temperature (TCASE): -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): Up to 177 °C for 106 hours MTTF
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Level 3 (IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)
- **Special Features:**
  - Internal matching to 50 Ω
  - Robust ESD protection (1000 V, Class 1C)
  - Lead-free, Green, and RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The AG203-63G is a general-purpose buffer amplifier utilizing InGaP/GaAs HBT technology. It is designed to provide high dynamic range and reliable performance in a compact SOT-363 surface-mount package. The amplifier typically delivers a gain of +20 dB at 900 MHz, making it suitable for various RF applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Infrastructure:** Enhancing signal strength and quality in cellular networks.
- **CATV / FTTX:** Used in cable television and fiber-to-the-x applications for signal amplification.
- **WLAN / ISM:** Suitable for wireless local area networks and industrial, scientific, and medical applications.
- **RFID:** Amplifying signals in radio-frequency identification systems.
- **WiMAX / WiBro:** Supporting broadband wireless access technologies.

The AG203-63G is ideal for applications requiring reliable amplification across a wide frequency range (DC to 6000 MHz), making it versatile for both current and next-generation wireless technologies such as GPRS, GSM, CDMA, and WCDMA.
