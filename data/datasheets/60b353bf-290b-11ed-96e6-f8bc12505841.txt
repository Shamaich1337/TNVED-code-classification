### Component Summary: BC848CPDW1T1G

**Manufacturer:** ON Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** BC848CPDW1T1G  
**Type:** NPN Transistor (Dual General Purpose)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 30 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 30 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 100 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD): 380 mW (at 25 °C)
  - Derating: 3.0 mW/°C above 25 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-363 (SC-88)

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BC848CPDW1T1G is a dual NPN transistor designed for general-purpose amplification applications. It is part of the BC848 series, which includes complementary PNP transistors, making it suitable for various electronic circuits requiring both NPN and PNP configurations. The device is housed in a compact SOT-363 package, ideal for low-power surface mount applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Amplification:** Used in audio and radio frequency applications to amplify weak signals.
- **Switching Applications:** Suitable for low-power switching tasks in various electronic devices.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for automotive applications where reliability and performance are critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as televisions, radios, and other consumer electronics requiring amplification or switching functions.

This transistor is versatile and can be utilized in a wide range of applications, making it a popular choice among engineers and designers in the electronics industry.
