### Component Summary: LMV321ILT

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** LMV321ILT  
**Description:** The LMV321ILT is a low-cost, low-power, rail-to-rail operational amplifier (op-amp) designed for general-purpose applications. It is part of the LMV321/358/324 family, which includes single, dual, and quad configurations. The LMV321 operates with supply voltages as low as 2.7 V and features both input and output rail-to-rail capabilities, making it suitable for battery-powered and portable devices.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.7 V to 6 V
  - Input Voltage Range: VDD - 0.3 V to VCC + 0.3 V
  - Common Mode Input Voltage Range: VDD - 0.2 V to VCC + 0.2 V (extended at 25 °C)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (per amplifier): 145 µA (typical)
  - Output Current: 48 mA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current: 145 µA (typical) at Vout = VCC/2

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23-5 (5-pin micropackage)

- **Special Features:**
  - Rail-to-rail input and output
  - Gain Bandwidth Product (GBP): 1 MHz
  - ESD Tolerance: 2 kV (Human Body Model)
  - Latch-up immunity
  - Thermal resistance junction-to-ambient: 250 °C/W (SOT23-5)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Typical Applications:
The LMV321ILT is well-suited for various applications, particularly in battery-powered and portable electronic devices. Typical applications include:
- Two-cell battery-powered systems
- Battery-powered electronic equipment
- Cordless phones
- Personal medical devices (e.g., glucose meters)
- Laptops and PDAs

### Conclusion:
The LMV321ILT operational amplifier is an efficient choice for low-power applications requiring rail-to-rail performance. Its low supply current and wide operating temperature range make it ideal for portable and battery-operated devices, while its compact SOT23-5 package facilitates easy integration into various electronic designs.
