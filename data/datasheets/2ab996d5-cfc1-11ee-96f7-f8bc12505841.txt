**Manufacturer:** Skyworks Solutions, Inc.  
**Product Type:** Mixer and Detector Schottky Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): Rated V_B (typically 2V)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 50 mA (steady state)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 75 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature (T_A): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SC-79, SOD-882, and SOT-23 packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Low-cost, surface-mountable design
  - 100% DC tested for tight parameter distribution
  - Can be used at frequencies up to 24 GHz
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): MSL1, rated for 260 °C for 5 seconds (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SMS7630-005LF is a low-cost, surface-mountable silicon Schottky diode designed for RF and microwave applications. It functions primarily as a mixer and detector, utilizing low-barrier diodes and zero-bias detection capabilities. The device is engineered to minimize performance variability through tight parameter distribution, making it suitable for sensitive RF and microwave circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Sensitive RF and Microwave Detector Circuits:** Used in applications requiring high sensitivity and low noise.
- **Sampling and Mixer Circuits:** Ideal for signal processing in communication systems.
- **High-Volume Wireless Applications:** Suitable for integration in consumer electronics such as Wi-Fi and mobile devices.
- **Low-Noise Receivers:** Effective in high-sensitivity ID tags and radio designs.

This component is particularly valuable in modern wireless communication systems, where performance and reliability are critical.
