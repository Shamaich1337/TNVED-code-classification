### Component Summary: VS-30CPH03-N3

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** VS-30CPH03-N3  
**Document Revision Date:** 15-Oct-2018  
**Document Number:** 94012  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 300 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (I_F(AV)): 2 x 15 A (total 30 A)
  - Non-repetitive Peak Surge Current (I_FSM): 140 A (per leg, at 25 °C for 10 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (V_F at I_F): 0.85 V (at 15 A, T_J = 125 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -65 °C to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247AC 3L
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultrafast recovery time
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - Low leakage current
  - Designed and qualified according to JEDEC JESD 47
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The VS-30CPH03-N3 is an **ultrafast recovery rectifier** designed for high-performance applications. It features a planar structure with platinum-doped lifetime control, which ensures optimal performance, ruggedness, and reliability. This component is particularly noted for its low forward voltage drop and ultrafast recovery time, making it suitable for demanding electrical environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The VS-30CPH03-N3 is primarily used in:
- **Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)**
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)**
- **DC/DC Converters**
- **Freewheeling Diodes in Low Voltage Inverters**
- **Chopper Motor Drives**

These applications benefit from the component's ability to minimize switching losses and reduce over-dissipation in switching elements and snubbers, enhancing overall system efficiency and reliability.
