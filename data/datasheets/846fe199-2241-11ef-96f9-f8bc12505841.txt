- **Mass**: 240 g
- **Special Features**:

  - High-quality plan apochromat design
  - Flat image surface over the entire field of view

#### Description:
The Mitutoyo M Plan APO 10X is a high-quality optical microscope objective designed for bright field observation. It features a plan apochromat design, which ensures that images are sharp and free from chromatic aberrations across the entire field of view. This objective is particularly suited for applications requiring high-resolution imaging and long working distances, making it ideal for various microscopy techniques.

- **Bright Field Microscopy**: Ideal for observing stained specimens and biological samples.
- **Material Science**: Useful for examining the microstructure of materials.
- **Quality Control**: Employed in laboratories for inspecting samples in manufacturing processes.
- **Research**: Widely used in biological and medical research for detailed imaging of specimens.
