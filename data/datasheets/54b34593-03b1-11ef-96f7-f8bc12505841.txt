#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Maximum working voltage is determined by the formula \( V = \sqrt{P \times R} \), where P is the power rating and R is the resistance value.
- **Current Ratings**: Power rating of 0.5 W at 70 °C.
- **Power Consumption**: 0.5 W (P70 °C).
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -65 °C to +170 °C.
- **Package Type**: 0805 (2.03 mm x 1.27 mm).
- **Special Features**:
  - Very high power-to-footprint size ratio.
  - Sulfur resistance, making it suitable for high sulfur environments.
  - Extremely low inductance (0.5 nH to 5 nH).
  - Low thermal EMF (< 3 μV/°C).
  - AEC-Q200 qualified for automotive applications.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

#### Description:
The WSLP0805R0200FEA is a Power Metal Strip® resistor designed for high power applications in a compact surface-mount package. It features a very low resistance value, down to 0.005 Ω, and is constructed using a welded design that enhances its reliability and performance in various applications. The resistor is particularly noted for its low thermal coefficient of resistance (TCR), which ensures stable performance across a wide temperature range.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Current Sensing**: Ideal for applications requiring precise current measurement due to its low resistance values and high power rating.
- **Voltage Division**: Used in circuits where voltage needs to be divided accurately.
- **Pulse Applications**: Suitable for applications that involve pulsed signals, thanks to its low inductance characteristics.
- **Automotive Electronics**: AEC-Q200 qualification makes it suitable for automotive applications, including power management and signal processing in vehicles.

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where space is limited but high power handling and low resistance are critical, such as in power management systems, automotive electronics, and industrial applications.
