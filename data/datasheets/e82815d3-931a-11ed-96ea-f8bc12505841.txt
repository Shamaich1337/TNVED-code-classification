### Component Summary: HMC363S8GETR

**Description:**
The HMC363S8GETR is a low noise, divide-by-8 static divider utilizing InGaP GaAs HBT technology. It is designed for high-frequency applications, operating from DC to 12 GHz. This component is packaged in an 8-lead surface mount plastic package, making it suitable for compact electronic designs.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V ± 0.25V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 70 mA (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 680 mW (derated at 13.7 mW/°C above 85°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-lead Standard Small Outline Package (SOIC_N_EP) with exposed pad
- **Special Features:**
  - Low additive SSB phase noise: -153 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz offset
  - Wide bandwidth with output power of -6 dBm
  - Reverse leakage: 65 dB
  - Supports differential operation
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC363S8GETR is primarily used in prescaler applications for DC to X-band Phase-Locked Loop (PLL) systems. Typical applications include:
- Satellite communications systems
- Fiber optic communication
- Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multi-Point radios
- Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) systems

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise frequency division and low noise performance, making it suitable for high-frequency signal processing tasks in modern communication systems.
