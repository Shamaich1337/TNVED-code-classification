**Description:**
The RPS-2-30+ is a 2-way power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications. It operates over a wide frequency range from 10 MHz to 3000 MHz and is suitable for surface mount technology (SMT) applications. This component is primarily used to split or combine RF signals while maintaining signal integrity.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Frequency Range:** 10 to 3000 MHz
- **Isolation:** 
  - Typ: 22 dB
  - Min: 15 dB

  - Typ: 3.5 dB (includes 3 dB splitter loss)
  - Max: 4.68 dB at 3000 MHz

  - Typ: 0.6 degrees
  - Max: 1.5 degrees

  - Typ: 0.9 dB
  - Max: 2.5 dB
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C

- **Internal Dissipation:** 0.125 W max
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Case Style: TT1413)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

**Special Features:**
- Wideband performance covering a large frequency range.
- Good isolation characteristics, making it suitable for applications requiring minimal signal interference.
- Compact size suitable for space-constrained designs.
- RoHS compliant, indicating it meets environmental regulations regarding hazardous substances.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Instrumentation:** Used in measurement and testing equipment where signal integrity is crucial.
- **CATV (Cable Television):** Employed in cable distribution systems to split signals for multiple outputs.
- **Cellular and PCS (Personal Communications Service):** Utilized in base stations and other RF communication systems.
- **GSM (Global System for Mobile Communications):** Integrated into mobile communication systems for effective signal management.
