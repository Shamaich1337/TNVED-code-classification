#### Description:
The 0402B471K500NT is a multi-layer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) manufactured by Fenghua Advanced Technology. It is classified as a Class 2 capacitor with a dielectric material that exhibits a semi-stable temperature characteristic. This capacitor is designed for applications requiring compact size and reliable performance in electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Decoupling**: Used to filter out noise in power supply lines.
- **Bypassing**: Helps to stabilize voltage levels in circuits.
- **Coupling**: Facilitates signal transmission between different stages of a circuit.
- **Timing Circuits**: Utilized in oscillators and timing applications due to its stable capacitance.
- **RF Applications**: Suitable for high-frequency circuits, including RF and microwave applications, due to its low equivalent series resistance (ESR).
