**Description:**
The HMC784AMS8GE is a high-power, single-pole double-throw (SPDT) switch designed for transmit-receive applications. It operates in the frequency range of DC to 4 GHz and is built using Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) MMIC technology. This component is particularly noted for its low distortion and high input power handling capabilities, making it suitable for various RF applications.

  - Control Voltage (Vctl): 0V to Vdd + 0.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Idd at Vdd = +3V: 0.5 µA

  - Typical Idd at Vdd = +8V: 20 µA

  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C

  - MSOP8G (8-lead MSOP package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss: 0.3 dB (typical)
  - High third-order intercept point: +60 dBm
  - Positive control voltage operation compatible with CMOS logic
  - Reflective shorts when "OFF" (RF1 and RF2)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

- Cellular and 4G infrastructure

- Automotive telematics
- Mobile radio communications
- Test equipment for RF applications
