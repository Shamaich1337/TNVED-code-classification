### Component Summary: 1.5SMC150CA-E3/57T

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Unidirectional: 128 V (VWM)
  - Bidirectional: 128 V (VWM)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 1.0 A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 200 A (unidirectional only)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 6.5 W
  - Peak Power Dissipation (PPPM): 1500 W (10/1000 μs waveform)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (DO-214AB)
- **Special Features:**
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - Low profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Excellent clamping capability and very fast response time
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 per J-STD-020
  - AEC-Q101 qualified versions available for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1

#### Description:
The **1.5SMC150CA-E3/57T** is a **Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)** diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is available in both unidirectional and bidirectional configurations, making it versatile for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This component is typically used in:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protecting ICs and MOSFETs from voltage spikes.
- **Computers:** Safeguarding signal lines and sensitive components.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Providing transient protection in control systems.
- **Automotive Applications:** Ensuring reliability in automotive electronics against voltage transients.
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting communication lines from surges.

The 1.5SMC150CA-E3/57T is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against transient voltages, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic devices.
