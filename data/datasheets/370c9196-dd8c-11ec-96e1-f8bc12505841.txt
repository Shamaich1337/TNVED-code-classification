A few metals form dens e, stable, tightly adhering,  \nelectrically insulating oxid es. Of these, the most valuable for the  \nelectronics industry are aluminum and tantalum.Capacitors are basic to all kinds of electrical equipment,  \nfrom radios and television sets to missile controls and  \nautomobile ignitions. Their function is to store an electrical  charge for later use.Capacitors consist of two co nducting surfaces, usually  \nmetal plates, whose function is to conduct electricity. They  \nare separated by an insulating material or dielectric. The  \ndielectric used in all tantalum electrolytic capacitors is  \ntantalum pentoxide.Tantalum pentoxide compound  possesses high-dielectric  \nstrength and a high-dielectric constant. As capacitors are  \nbeing manufactured, a film of tantalum pentox ide is applied  \nto their electrodes by means of  an electrolytic process. The  \nfilm is applied in various thic knesses and at various voltages  \nand although transparent to begin with, it takes on different  \ncolors as light refracts through it. This coloring occurs on the  \ntantalum electrodes of all types of tantalum capacitors.Rating for rating, tantalum capacitors tend to have as much  \nas three times better capacitance / volume efficiency than  \naluminum electrolytic capacitors. ### Component Summary for 593D107X9010D2TE3 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Rated Voltage: 10 V DC
  - Surge Voltage: 13 V
  - Category Voltage: 7 V at +125 °C
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Ripple Current (IRMS): 6.0 A at 100 kHz
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 0.55 W at +25 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55 °C to +125 °C (with voltage derating above +85 °C)
- **Package Type**: 
  - Molded Chip Capacitor (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
  - 100% Surge Current Tested
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Compliant with EIA-535-BAAC mechanical and performance requirements

#### Description:
The **593D107X9010D2TE3** is a solid tantalum surface mount chip capacitor from Vishay's 593D series. It is designed for applications requiring high reliability and stability, particularly in environments with varying temperatures. The capacitor features a molded case that provides excellent protection and is compatible with high-volume automatic pick-and-place equipment.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in power supply circuits, DC/DC converters, and energy storage applications.
- **Telecommunications**: Suitable for telecom infrastructure where stable capacitance is critical.
- **Industrial Electronics**: Commonly found in industrial control systems and automation equipment.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Utilized in various consumer devices for filtering and decoupling applications.

This capacitor is particularly valued for its low ESR characteristics, making it ideal for high-frequency applications where efficiency and performance are paramount.
