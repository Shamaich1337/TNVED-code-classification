  - Non-repetitive peak reverse power dissipation of ≤ 40 W.
  - Low differential resistance.
  - Available in two tolerance series: ±2% (BZV55-B) and ±5% (BZV55-C).
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically SMD components are classified under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The BZV55-C36,115 is a low-power voltage regulator diode designed for surface-mount applications. It is part of the BZV55 series, which includes a range of diodes with nominal working voltages from 2.4V to 75V. The BZV55-C36 variant specifically provides a regulated output voltage of 36V, making it suitable for various voltage regulation tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: The BZV55 series diodes are primarily used for voltage regulation in electronic circuits, ensuring stable voltage levels for sensitive components.
- **Power Management**: They can be utilized in power supply circuits to maintain consistent voltage levels, protecting downstream components from voltage fluctuations.
- **Signal Processing**: The diodes can also be employed in signal processing applications where voltage regulation is critical for maintaining signal integrity.

This component is ideal for applications requiring reliable voltage regulation in compact electronic designs, particularly in consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and automotive systems.
