### Component Summary: Si52112-B6-GTR

**Description:**
The Si52112-B6-GTR is a high-performance PCIe clock generator designed to provide two PCIe clocks from a 25 MHz crystal or clock input. It is compliant with PCIe Gen 1, Gen 2, and Gen 3 specifications, making it suitable for various applications requiring precise clock signals. The component features a compact footprint and low power consumption, making it ideal for space-constrained designs.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VDD): 3.3 V ± 5% (3.13 V to 3.46 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Current (IDD): Up to 17 mA
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption with HCSL differential output buffers
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 85 °C (Extended Temperature Range)
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-pin TSSOP (Tape and Reel)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Supports down spread modulation of -0.5% for EMI reduction
  - No termination resistors required
  - Triangular spread spectrum profile for maximum EMI reduction
  - Supports Serial-ATA (SATA) at 100 MHz
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
The Si52112-B6-GTR is commonly used in:
- Network Attached Storage (NAS)
- Multi-function printers
- PCIe add-on cards
- Network interface cards (NICs)
- Docking stations
- Wireless access points
- Routers
- Digital still cameras
- Digital video cameras

This component is particularly beneficial in applications where precise timing and low jitter are critical, such as in high-speed data communication and storage systems. Its compact size and low power requirements make it suitable for modern electronic devices that demand efficient space utilization and energy consumption.
