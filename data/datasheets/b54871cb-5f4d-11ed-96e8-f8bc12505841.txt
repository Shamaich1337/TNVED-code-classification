  - Dynamic dv/dt Rating
  - Lead-Free
  - Available in Tape & Reel

  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IRF7341TRPBF is a fifth-generation HEXFET® Power MOSFET designed for high-efficiency applications. It features a dual N-channel configuration, which allows for effective switching and power management in various electronic circuits. The device is characterized by its low on-resistance and fast switching capabilities, making it suitable for applications requiring high performance and reliability.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits, DC-DC converters, and battery management systems.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for switching applications in audio and RF circuits.
- **Motor Control:** Suitable for driving motors in various industrial and consumer applications.
- **Lighting Control:** Can be used in LED drivers and lighting control systems.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices requiring efficient power switching and management.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, as its SO-8 package allows for compact designs while maintaining excellent thermal performance.
