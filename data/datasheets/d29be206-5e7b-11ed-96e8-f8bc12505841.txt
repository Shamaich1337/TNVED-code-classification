### Component Summary: RFM1040G-AWH-LNN

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Voltage (VDD): 3.0V to 3.6V (typical 3.3V)
  - Backlight Voltage (VLED): 12V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (IVDD): 385 mA (typical), 424 mA (max)
  - Backlight Control: PWM control with a maximum frequency of 10 kHz

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption is primarily determined by the backlight and display operation, with a typical operating current of 424 mA at 3.3V.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -30°C to +80°C
  - Storage Temperature: -30°C to +80°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Module dimensions: 230 mm x 180.2 mm x 12.4 mm
  - Active area: 210.432 mm x 157.824 mm

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - TFT display with a resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels.
  - Anti-glare surface treatment.
  - LVDS interface for data transmission.
  - LED backlight with a lifetime of approximately 100,000 hours.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The RFM1040G-AWH-LNN is a 10.4-inch TFT (Thin Film Transistor) liquid crystal display (LCD) module. It features a color active matrix display with a resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels. The display utilizes a transmissive type with normally black operation, making it suitable for various lighting conditions. The module includes driver ICs, polarizers, and a flexible printed circuit (FPC) for connectivity.

#### Typical Applications:
The RFM1040G-AWH-LNN is commonly used in applications that require visual output, such as:
- Industrial control panels
- Medical devices
- Consumer electronics
- Point-of-sale systems
- Automotive displays
- Any application requiring a compact, high-resolution display for user interfaces or data visualization.

This module is particularly well-suited for environments where durability and a wide operating temperature range are essential, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications.
