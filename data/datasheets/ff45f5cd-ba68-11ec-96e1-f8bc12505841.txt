### Component Summary: SMA5929A

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SMA5929A  
**Series:** SMA59XXA Series  
**Type:** Surface Mount Zener Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Nominal Zener Voltage (VZ): 14.25V (typical), 15.0V (max), 15.75V (min)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Zener Current (IZM): 100.0 mA
  - Test Current (IZT): 25.0 mA
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.5 μA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - DC Power Dissipation (PD) at TL = 75 °C: 1.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AC (SMA)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - High forward surge capability
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL Level 1 (J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SMA5929A is a surface mount Zener diode designed to provide voltage regulation and protection in electronic circuits. It operates by allowing current to flow in the reverse direction when the voltage exceeds its Zener breakdown voltage, thus maintaining a stable output voltage. This characteristic makes it essential for voltage clamping and transient voltage suppression applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant voltage level.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protects sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes.
- **Signal Clamping:** Ensures signal levels remain within specified limits in communication circuits.
- **Power Management:** Helps in managing voltage levels in various electronic devices, ensuring reliability and performance.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the SMA5929A Zener diode, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical applications in electronic circuits.
