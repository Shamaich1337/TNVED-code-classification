### Component Summary: ULQ2003D1013TR

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** ULQ2003D1013TR  
**Document Reference:** February 2011 Doc ID 1537 Rev 6

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Output Voltage (VO): 50 V
  - Input Voltage (for ULQ2003A/D1): 30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 500 mA
  - Peak Collector Current: 600 mA
  - Continuous Base Current (IB): 25 mA

- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but power dissipation can be inferred from current ratings and saturation voltage.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40 °C to 105 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): 150 °C

- **Package Type:** SO16 (Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated suppression diodes for inductive load driving.
  - Outputs can be paralleled for higher current.
  - TTL/CMOS/PMOS/DTL compatible inputs.
  - Inputs pinned opposite outputs to simplify layout.
  - Automotive Grade product qualified according to AEC-Q100.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The ULQ2003D1013TR is a high-voltage, high-current Darlington array that contains seven open-collector Darlington pairs with common emitters. Each driver is capable of handling continuous currents of up to 500 mA and can withstand peak currents of 600 mA. The device is designed to interface with various logic families, making it versatile for different applications. The integrated suppression diodes allow for safe operation when driving inductive loads.

#### Typical Applications:
The ULQ2003D1013TR is commonly used in applications that require driving high-power loads, such as:
- Solenoids
- Relays
- DC motors
- LED displays
- Filament lamps
- Thermal print-heads
- High-power buffers

This component is particularly suitable for automotive applications due to its automotive-grade qualification, making it reliable for use in harsh environments.
