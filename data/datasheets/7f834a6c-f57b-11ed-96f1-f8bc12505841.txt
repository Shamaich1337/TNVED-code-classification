**Component Type:** Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor  
**Series:** FH Series  
**Manufacturer:** CapXon  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Capacitance (CR):** 3300 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 35 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 40 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz, 20°C

  - 4230 mA at 100kHz, 105°C

- **Max. Impedance (Z):** 0.014 Ω at 100kHz, 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 105°C
- **Package Type:** Through-Hole (THT)
- **Size:** 18 mm (Diameter) x 35.5 mm (Length)
- **Pitch:** 7.5 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **AEC-Q200 Qualified:** No

#### Description:
The FH332M035K355A is a high-performance aluminum electrolytic capacitor designed for applications requiring low impedance and high ripple current handling. It is a Through-Hole (THT) type capacitor, suitable for various electronic circuits where space is not a critical constraint.

#### Typical Applications:
This capacitor is typically used in:
- **Power Management:** Filtering and smoothing in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Coupling and decoupling applications in audio and RF circuits.
- **Energy Storage:** Providing temporary energy storage in power circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices that require stable voltage and current supply, such as audio amplifiers and power amplifiers.

The FH series capacitors are particularly noted for their reliability in high-temperature environments, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications where thermal stability is crucial.
