### Component Summary: 1N4148WS-E3-08 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 75 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Current (I_F(AV)): 150 mA (half wave rectification with resistive load)
  - Surge Forward Current (I_FSM): 350 mA (for t < 1 s and T_j = 25 °C)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_tot): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature (T_op): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-323

- **Special Features:**
  - Silicon epitaxial planar diode
  - Fast switching capabilities
  - AEC-Q101 qualified versions available
  - RoHS-compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **1N4148WS** is a small signal fast switching diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It is a silicon epitaxial planar diode that is commonly used in various electronic circuits due to its reliability and performance characteristics. The diode is particularly noted for its fast switching speed, making it suitable for applications where rapid response times are critical.

#### Typical Applications:
The 1N4148WS diode is typically used in:
- **Signal Processing:** As a rectifier in signal processing circuits.
- **Clamping and Protection Circuits:** To protect sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Switching Applications:** In digital circuits where fast switching is required.
- **General Purpose Rectification:** In low-power applications where small size and efficiency are important.

This diode is widely utilized in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and various other electronic devices where compact size and fast switching capabilities are essential.
