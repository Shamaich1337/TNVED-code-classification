- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current: 16 µA (Typical), 40 µA (Maximum)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power Supply Current: 16 µA (Typical), 40 µA (Maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 5-pin SOT-23
- **Special Features**: 
  - Factory preset trip point of 110°C
  - Hysteresis options of 2°C or 10°C
  - Open-drain active-low digital output
  - Internal voltage reference and DAC for trip-point setting
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LM26CIM5-YHA/NOPB is a precision, single digital-output thermostat (temperature switch) designed for low-power applications. It integrates an internal temperature sensor, a reference voltage, a digital-to-analog converter (DAC), and a comparator. The device can be factory programmed to have a trip point (TOS) anywhere in the range of -55°C to +120°C, with the specific variant LM26CIM5-YHA preset to 110°C. The output can be configured as either open-drain or push-pull, and it supports different output functionalities for over-temperature (OS) and under-temperature (US) shutdown events.

#### Typical Applications:
The LM26CIM5-YHA/NOPB is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Microprocessor Thermal Management**: To monitor and control the temperature of processors and prevent overheating.
- **Appliances**: Used in household appliances for temperature regulation and safety.
- **Fan Control**: To manage fan speeds based on temperature readings, ensuring efficient cooling.
- **Industrial Process Control**: For monitoring and controlling temperatures in industrial settings.
- **HVAC Systems**: To maintain desired temperature levels in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.
- **Remote Temperature Sensing**: For applications requiring temperature monitoring from a distance.

This component is ideal for applications where precise temperature control and monitoring are critical, providing reliable performance in a compact package.
