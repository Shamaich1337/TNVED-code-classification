### Component Summary: QCN-19+

**Description:**
The QCN-19+ is an ultra-small ceramic power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications. It operates within a frequency range of 1100 to 1925 MHz and is characterized by low insertion loss and high isolation. This component is particularly useful in balanced amplifiers, modulators, GPS systems, and PCS/DCS applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not specified (typically RF components do not have voltage ratings in the same way as DC components).
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified (current handling is typically related to power input).
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Input (as a splitter): 15W (derates to 7W at 100°C ambient).
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** Ultra-small ceramic package (Case Style: FV1206-1) with dimensions 0.12" x 0.06" x 0.035".
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss: 0.4 dB typical.
  - High isolation: 26 dB typical.
  - Wrap-around terminal for excellent solderability.
  - Patent pending.
  - RoHS compliant.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The QCN-19+ is commonly used in:
- **Balanced Amplifiers:** To split or combine signals while maintaining balance.
- **Modulators:** For RF signal processing.
- **GPS Systems:** To manage signal distribution.
- **PCS/DCS:** In mobile communication systems for efficient signal handling.

This component is ideal for applications requiring compact size and high performance in RF signal distribution.
