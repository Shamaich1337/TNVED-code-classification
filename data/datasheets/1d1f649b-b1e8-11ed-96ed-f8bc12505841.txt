### Component Summary: MBR40200PT

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** MBR40200PT  
**Type:** Schottky Barrier Rectifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IFAV): 40 A
  - Surge (Non-repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM): 300 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 20 A: 0.9 V
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +175 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247AB
- **Special Features:**
  - High frequency operation
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - High purity, high temperature epoxy encapsulation for enhanced mechanical strength and moisture resistance
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability
  - Solder dip 275 °C max. for 7 seconds, per JESD 22-B106
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The MBR40200PT is a Schottky barrier rectifier designed for high-frequency applications. It features a low forward voltage drop, making it efficient for power conversion tasks. The component is encapsulated in a robust epoxy material that provides excellent mechanical strength and moisture resistance, ensuring reliability in various operating conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The MBR40200PT is commonly used in:
- Switching power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Freewheeling diodes
- Reverse battery protection circuits

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient rectification and fast switching capabilities, making it suitable for modern electronic devices and power management systems.
