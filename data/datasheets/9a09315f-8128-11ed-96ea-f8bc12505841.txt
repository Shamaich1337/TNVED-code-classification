### Component Summary: SMAJ22CA (Littelfuse)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 22V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 24.4V to 26.9V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (Ipp, 10/1000 µs): 46.2A
  - Maximum Forward Surge Current (IFSM, 8.3 ms): 40A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Minimum Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (TP = 1 ms): 400W
  - Steady State Power Dissipation (PM(AV) @ TL = 75 °C): 1.0W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount, SMA (DO-214AC)
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant
  - UL Recognized
  - ESD protection compliant with IEC 61000-4-2 (Level 4)
  - EFT protection compliant with IEC 61000-4-4
  - Surge protection compliant with IEC 61000-4-5
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The SMAJ22CA is a Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge (ESD), electrical fast transients (EFT), and surge events. It is a bidirectional device, meaning it can clamp voltage spikes in both directions, making it suitable for AC applications as well as DC.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to protect against voltage transients.
- **Signal Processing:** Protects sensitive signal lines in communication devices from ESD and other transient events.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and computers to safeguard internal circuits.
- **Automotive Applications:** Provides protection for automotive electronics against voltage spikes from inductive loads and other sources.

This component is essential in ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices by preventing damage from unexpected voltage surges.
