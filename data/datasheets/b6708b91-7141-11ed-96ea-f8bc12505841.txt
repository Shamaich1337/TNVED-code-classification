### Component Summary: GXHTC3

**Manufacturer:** BEIJING SHANGTONGDA TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD.  
**Product Code:** GXHTC3  
**Type:** Digital Humidity and Temperature Sensor IC

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Operating Voltage: 1.62 V to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Idle State: 45 µA (max 70 µA)
  - Sleep Mode: 0.3 µA (max 0.6 µA)
  - Measurement Mode: 430 µA (average in normal mode)
  - Low Power Mode: 270 µA (average)
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Average current consumption in normal mode: 4.9 µA (continuous operation with one measurement per second)
  - Average current consumption in low power mode: 0.5 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type:** 
  - DFN (Dual Flat No Leads), dimensions: 2 × 2 × 0.75 mm³
- **Special Features:**
  - Fully calibrated and reflow solderable
  - I2C Fast Mode Plus communication interface
  - High reliability and long-term stability
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: MSL1 (according to IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)

#### Description:
The GXHTC3 is a compact digital humidity and temperature sensor designed for high-volume consumer electronics, particularly in battery-operated devices. It integrates a capacitive humidity sensor, a bandgap temperature sensor, and includes analog and digital signal processing, an A/D converter, and calibration data memory. The sensor is optimized for low power consumption, making it suitable for mobile and wireless applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables for environmental monitoring.
- **Home Automation:** Integrated into smart home devices for climate control and monitoring.
- **HVAC Systems:** Employed in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for efficient temperature and humidity regulation.
- **Weather Stations:** Utilized in portable weather stations for accurate environmental data collection.
- **Industrial Applications:** Applied in various industrial settings for monitoring and controlling environmental conditions.

The GXHTC3 sensor is designed to meet the demands of modern applications requiring compact size, low power consumption, and high accuracy, making it a versatile choice for a wide range of humidity and temperature sensing needs.
