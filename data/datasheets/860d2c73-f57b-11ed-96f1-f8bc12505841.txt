#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 50 V (max)
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 50 V (max)
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 5 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (I_O): 100 mA (max)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 250 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25°C)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65°C to +150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Built-in bias resistors (R1 = 4.7 kΩ, R2 = 47 kΩ)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Reduces component count and simplifies circuit design
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT packages are moisture sensitive.

#### Description:
The PDTC143ZT is an NPN resistor-equipped transistor (RET) designed for surface-mounted applications. It features built-in bias resistors that simplify circuit design and reduce the overall component count, making it a cost-effective solution for various electronic applications. The transistor is suitable for digital applications, particularly in automotive and industrial segments.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Control of Integrated Circuit (IC) Inputs:** The PDTC143ZT can be used to interface with digital logic levels, providing a controlled input to ICs.
- **Switching Loads:** It can be employed in switching applications where it controls the power to loads, such as LEDs or small motors.
- **Cost-Saving Alternative:** It serves as a replacement for traditional transistors like the BC847/857 series in digital applications, providing a more compact solution.

This component is particularly advantageous in automotive electronics due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, ensuring reliability under harsh conditions.
