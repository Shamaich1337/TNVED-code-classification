### Component Summary: HMC441LC3B

**Description:**
The HMC441LC3B is a GaAs pHEMT (Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor) MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) medium power amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 6 to 18 GHz. It is housed in a leadless, RoHS-compliant SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing. The amplifier is optimized for linear gain applications and does not require external components, simplifying integration into various systems.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +5V (max +6V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 90 mA (typical at +5V)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.74 W (at T = 85°C)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 12 Lead Ceramic Leadless Chip Carrier (LCC), dimensions: 3x3 mm
  
- **Special Features:**
  - 14 dB of gain
  - Saturated Output Power: +21.5 dBm
  - 27% Power Added Efficiency (PAE)
  - 50 Ohm matched input/output
  - DC blocking on I/Os for ease of integration
  - ESD Sensitivity: Class 0 (passed 100V)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC441LC3B is ideal for various applications, including:
- Point-to-Point Radios
- Point-to-Multi-Point Radios & VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal)
- Local Oscillator (LO) Driver for HMC Mixers
- Military Electronic Warfare (EW) and Electronic Countermeasures (ECM)

This amplifier is particularly suited for applications requiring medium power amplification in microwave frequency ranges, making it a versatile component in communication systems and radar technologies.
