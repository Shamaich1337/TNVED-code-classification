### Component Summary: P4SMA10A

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** P4SMA10A  
**Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor Diode (TVS Diode)  
**Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 9.50V (min), 10.50V (max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 10V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): Refer to the next table for specific values.
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (I_R): 1μA at V_RWM
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 400W (10/1000μs waveform)
  - Power Dissipation on infinite heat sink at T_L=75°C (P_D): 1.0W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (meets J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low-profile package suitable for automated placement.
  - Excellent clamping capability and very fast response time.
  - High-temperature soldering guaranteed (260°C for 10 seconds at terminals).
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free.

#### Description:
The P4SMA10A is a unidirectional transient voltage suppressor diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is particularly effective in clamping voltage spikes, thereby safeguarding integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electronics Protection:** Used in consumer electronics, computers, industrial equipment, and telecommunication devices to protect against voltage transients.
- **Signal Line Protection:** Ideal for safeguarding signal lines in sensor units and other sensitive electronic circuits.
- **Power Management:** Helps in managing voltage levels and protecting against surges in power supply circuits.

This component is essential for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices by preventing damage from unexpected voltage spikes.
