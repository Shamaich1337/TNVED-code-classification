#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF): 3.2 to 3.5 V at 20 mA
  - Reverse Voltage (VREV): 5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 30 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IF Peak): 100 mA (10% duty cycle at 1 kHz)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PDiss): 105 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): 95 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Chip LED, size 1206
- **Special Features:**
  - Emitting Color: Blue
  - Luminous Intensity (IV): 90 to 145 mcd at 20 mA
  - Peak Wavelength (λPeak): 465 nm
  - Dominant Wavelength (λDom): 470 nm
  - Viewing Angle: 140°
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The WL-SMCW SMT Mono-color Chip LED (150120BS75000) is a surface-mount device designed for general electronic applications. It utilizes InGaN chip technology to emit blue light, making it suitable for various lighting and indication purposes. The LED is characterized by its compact size and efficient power consumption, making it ideal for integration into modern electronic devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Lighting:** Used in backlighting for displays, indicators, and decorative lighting.
- **Signal Indication:** Commonly employed in electronic devices to signal operational status or alerts.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Integrated into products such as televisions, smartphones, and appliances for visual feedback.
- **Automotive Lighting:** Can be used in dashboard indicators and interior lighting applications.

This LED is not intended for use in safety-critical applications such as military, aerospace, or medical devices, and users are advised to conduct thorough reliability evaluations for any high-stakes applications.
