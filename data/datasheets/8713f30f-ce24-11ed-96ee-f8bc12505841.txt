### Component Summary: TPS61160DRVT

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.7 V to 18 V
  - Open LED Protection: 26 V (TPS61160)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 0.7 A (typical)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Quiescent Current: 1.8 mA
  - Shutdown Current: 1 µA
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature: -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to 125 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - WSON (6-pin) with thermal pad
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Built-in soft start
  - Up to 90% efficiency
  - Flexible digital and PWM brightness control
  - Integrated open LED protection
  - 200 mV reference voltage with ±2% accuracy
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS61160** is a high-efficiency, high-output voltage boost converter designed specifically for driving white LEDs in series. It features a 40-V rated integrated switch FET and operates at a fixed switching frequency of 600 kHz, which helps to reduce output ripple and improve conversion efficiency. The device can be controlled via a one-wire digital interface or through PWM signals for brightness dimming, making it versatile for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Phones**
- **Portable Media Players**
- **Ultra Mobile Devices**
- **GPS Receivers**
- **White LED Backlighting for Media Form Factor Displays**

The TPS61160 is particularly suitable for applications requiring efficient LED driving with precise brightness control, making it ideal for modern portable electronic devices where space and power efficiency are critical.
