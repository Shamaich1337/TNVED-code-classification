This type ofcontroller isused tohelp improve linetransient response,\nthereby simplifying thecompensation network used forboth continuous and discontinuous current conduction\noperation. The voltage andcurrent loops areinternally compensated using aType-III compensation scheme that\nprovides enough phase boost forstable operation, allowing theuseofsmall ceramic capacitors with very low\nESR. There isa0.5V offset onthebottom ofthePWM ramp toallow thedevice tooperate between 0%to100%\nduty cycle.\nThe internal PWM gate drive candirectly control theinternal PMOS andNMOS power MOSFETs. Aninput P-MOSFET prevents battery discharge when INis\nlower than BAT. ### Component Summary: BQ24125RHLR (Texas Instruments)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 4.35V to 16V
  - Absolute Maximum Voltage on IN and OUT Pins: 20V
  - Battery Voltage Regulation: 4.2V (single-cell) or 8.4V (two-cell)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Charge Current: Up to 2A
  - Output Current (average): 2.2A
  - Cycle-by-Cycle Current Limit: 3.6A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - VCC Supply Current (PWM switching): 10mA
  - VCC Supply Current (not switching): 5mA
  - Battery Discharge Sleep Current: 5.5μA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (20-pin) with dimensions 3.5mm x 4.5mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated Power FETs
  - Enhanced EMI Performance
  - High-Accuracy Voltage and Current Regulation
  - Battery Temperature Monitoring
  - Automatic Sleep Mode for Low Power Consumption
  - Reverse Leakage Protection
  - Thermal Shutdown and Protection
  - Built-In Battery Detection
  - Programmable Charge Timer

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 2, Peak Temperature 260°C

#### Description:
The BQ24125RHLR is a highly integrated Li-Ion and Li-Polymer charge-management IC designed for portable applications. It features a synchronous fixed-frequency PWM controller operating at 1.1MHz, which allows for efficient battery charging with minimal external components. The device is capable of charging single-cell or multi-cell battery packs and includes features such as charge preconditioning, status outputs for charge indication, and fault detection.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable Media Players**
- **Handheld Products**
- **Industrial and Medical Equipment**
- **Portable Equipment**
- **Portable DVD Players**
- **Power Management Systems**

The BQ24125 is particularly suitable for applications requiring efficient battery management and charging, making it ideal for consumer electronics and portable devices where space and power efficiency are critical.
