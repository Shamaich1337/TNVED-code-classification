### Component Summary: EDP to HDMI Adapter Board (Model: S.EDP-HDMI-4K2K.V1.2)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - EDP-VCC: 3.3V (default support for 5-12V)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Current: 1000mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Ripple Voltage: 50mV P-P
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -20°C to 60°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Signal conversion board
- **Special Features:**
  - Supports EDP/DP signal conversion to HDMI with a maximum resolution of 4K at 60Hz.
  - Compliant with VESA EDP/DP1.2 and HDMI2.0 standards.
  - Supports multiple lanes (1, 2, or 4) for input.
  - Features such as TMDS scrambling for EMI/RFI reduction and SCDC support.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The S.EDP-HDMI-4K2K.V1.2 is a signal conversion board designed to convert EDP (Embedded DisplayPort) or DP (DisplayPort) signals into HDMI signals. It supports high-definition output with a maximum resolution of 4K at 60Hz, making it suitable for high-quality video applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This adapter board is commonly used in various fields, including:
- **Set-top Boxes:** For connecting devices to HDMI displays.
- **Advertising Displays:** Enabling high-resolution content playback on digital signage.
- **Conference Systems:** Facilitating presentations and video conferencing by connecting laptops or other devices to HDMI projectors or screens.
- **Embedded Systems:** Compatible with various ARM and X86 motherboards, including popular chipsets like RK3288, RK3399, and MTK.

The board is particularly useful in scenarios where EDP output is required to interface with HDMI displays, ensuring compatibility with modern video standards.
