### Component Summary: AD8210YRZ-REEL7

**Manufacturer:** Analog Devices  
**Product Code:** AD8210YRZ-REEL7

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Supply Voltage (V+): 2.7V to 36V
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Maximum Output Sink Current: 7 mA
  - Maximum Output Source Current: 11 mA
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Supply Current: 5 mA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:** 
  - SOIC-8 (Small Outline Integrated Circuit)
- **Special Features or Notes:** 
  - High-side current sensing
  - Adjustable gain (up to +20)
  - Bidirectional and unidirectional operation modes
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** 
  - MSL 1 (not moisture sensitive)

#### Description:
The AD8210 is a high-precision, high-side current sense amplifier designed for measuring current through a shunt resistor. It features a differential input and provides an output voltage proportional to the current flowing through the shunt. The amplifier can be configured for both unidirectional and bidirectional current sensing, making it versatile for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in battery management systems to monitor current flow and optimize battery usage.
- **Motor Control:** Employed in H-bridge motor control circuits to measure motor current and provide feedback for control algorithms.
- **Industrial Automation:** Utilized in industrial equipment for current monitoring and protection.
- **Automotive Applications:** Suitable for automotive current sensing applications, including electric power steering and battery management systems.

The AD8210 is particularly valuable in applications where accurate current measurement is critical, and its ability to operate in harsh environments (up to 125°C) makes it suitable for automotive and industrial applications.
