### Component Summary: SS36A Schottky Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SS36A (part of the SS32A to SS320A series)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 3.0A
  - Surge (Non-Repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM): 70A
- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated; characterized by low power losses and high efficiency.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +125°C (up to +150°C for some variants)
- **Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Guardring for overvoltage protection
  - High forward surge capability
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 per J-STD-020, with a maximum peak of 260°C
  - RoHS-compliant and halogen-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The SS36A is a surface mount Schottky rectifier designed for efficient rectification in low voltage, high-frequency applications. Schottky rectifiers are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them suitable for various power management tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Low Voltage High Frequency Inverters:** Used in power conversion applications where efficiency is critical.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Employed in inductive load circuits to prevent voltage spikes.
- **DC/DC Converters:** Utilized in power supply circuits to convert DC voltage levels efficiently.
- **Polarity Protection:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections, ensuring safe operation.

This component is particularly valuable in modern electronic devices where space is limited, and efficiency is paramount. Its robust design and high surge capability make it suitable for a wide range of applications in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.
