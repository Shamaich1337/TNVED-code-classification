### Component Summary: SP3010-04UTG

**Description:**
The SP3010-04UTG is a TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) Diode Array from Littelfuse's SP3010 series, designed for low capacitance ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) protection. It integrates four channels of ultra-low capacitance rail-to-rail diodes along with an additional zener diode, making it suitable for protecting sensitive electronic equipment from destructive ESD events.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 6.0 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Current (tp=8/20μs): 3.0 A
  - Reverse Leakage Current (ILEAK): 0.1 µA (typical) at 5V
- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated; primarily functions as a protective device.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -40 to 125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTOR): -55 to 150 °C
- **Package Type:** 
  - µDFN-10 (2.5 x 1.0 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD protection compliant with IEC 61000-4-2 (±8kV contact, ±15kV air)
  - Low capacitance of 0.45 pF (typical) per I/O
  - Low leakage current of 0.1 µA (typical) at 5V
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The SP3010-04UTG is commonly used in various electronic devices that require protection from ESD, including:
- LCD/PDP TVs
- DVD Players
- Desktop Computers
- MP3/PMP Players
- Set-Top Boxes
- Mobile Phones
- Notebooks
- Digital Cameras

This component is particularly effective for high-speed signal lines such as HDMI, USB 3.0, USB 2.0, and IEEE 1394, where low capacitance is critical to maintaining signal integrity while providing robust ESD protection. 

### Conclusion
The SP3010-04UTG is a compact and efficient solution for protecting sensitive electronic components from ESD, making it ideal for a wide range of consumer electronics and communication devices. Its low capacitance and high ESD withstand voltage make it particularly suitable for high-speed applications.
