### Component Summary: TLP4176G(TP.F)

**Manufacturer:** Toshiba Electronic Devices & Storage Corporation  
**Product Code:** TLP4176G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Off-State Voltage (VOFF): 350 V (min)
  - Isolation Voltage: 1500 Vrms (min)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - LED Forward Current (IF): 50 mA (max)
  - Trigger LED Current (IFC): 3 mA (max)
  - On-State Current (ION): 120 mA (max)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Diode Power Dissipation (PD): 50 mW (max)
  - Output Power Dissipation (PO): 324 mW (max)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Topr): -40 to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 125 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 4-pin SOP (2.54SOP4): 2.1 mm high, 2.54 mm pitch
  
- **Special Features:**
  - 1-form-B configuration
  - UL-recognized under UL 1577, File No. E67349
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: Not specified in the provided data

#### Component Description:
The TLP4176G is a photorelay that consists of an infrared emitting diode optically coupled to a photo-MOSFET. It is designed for surface mount assembly, making it suitable for applications where space is a constraint. The device is particularly useful in modem applications, such as modem and fax cards, and is ideal for telecommunication and measurement instrumentation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunication:** Used in modem and fax cards, as well as modems in PCs.
- **Measurement Instrumentation:** Suitable for various measurement devices that require isolation and control.
- **Power Management:** Can be utilized in circuits requiring switching and isolation of high voltages.

This component is designed to provide reliable performance in applications that require high voltage isolation and low on-state resistance, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
