### Component Summary: TLV431BSN1T1G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Cathode to Anode Voltage (VKA): 18 V
  - Programmable Output Voltage Range: 1.24 V to 16 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Cathode Current (IK): -20 mA to 25 mA
  - Reference Input Current (Iref): 0.05 mA to 10 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Current Range: 0.1 mA to 20 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature: -40 °C to 125 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): Up to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23-3 (Case 318)

- **Special Features:**
  - Precision low voltage shunt regulator
  - Reference accuracy: ±1.0% (A series), ±0.5% (B series), ±0.2% (C series)
  - Low dynamic output impedance of 0.20 Ω from 100 µA to 20 mA
  - AEC-Q100 qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free and Halide-Free

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TLV431BSN1T1G is a precision low voltage adjustable shunt regulator designed to provide a stable reference voltage across a wide range of applications. It operates effectively as a voltage reference and can replace traditional Zener diodes in various circuits requiring precise voltage regulation. The device features a programmable output voltage range from 1.24 V to 16 V, making it versatile for different applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Switching Power Supplies:** Used as an error amplifier in feedback loops for isolated low output voltage switching power supplies (3.0 V to 3.3 V).
- **Voltage Regulation:** Ideal for adjustable voltage or current linear and switching power supplies.
- **Voltage Monitoring:** Can be utilized in circuits that require precise voltage monitoring.
- **Current Source/Sink Circuits:** Suitable for applications needing accurate current sourcing or sinking.
- **Analog and Digital Circuits:** Provides precision references in both analog and digital applications.
- **Zener Diode Replacement:** Acts as a low voltage Zener diode replacement in various electronic circuits.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications due to its robustness and precision, ensuring reliable performance in demanding environments.
