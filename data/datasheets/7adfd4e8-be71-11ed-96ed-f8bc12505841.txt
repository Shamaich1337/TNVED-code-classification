### Component Summary: B330B-13-F (Diodes Incorporated)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 30 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO) at 100°C: 3.0 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 3.0 A: 0.70 V (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMB (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Guard Ring Die Construction for Transient Protection
  - Low Power Loss, High Efficiency
  - Surge Overload Rating to 125A Peak
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability
  - Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free, "Green" Device
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 per J-STD-020

#### Component Description:
The B330B-13-F is a surface mount Schottky barrier rectifier designed for high efficiency and low power loss applications. It features a guard ring die construction that provides transient protection, making it suitable for various electronic applications. The device is characterized by its ability to handle significant surge currents and is compliant with automotive standards, ensuring reliability in demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The B330B-13-F is typically used in:
- **Power Management:** Efficient rectification in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in circuits requiring fast switching and low forward voltage drop.
- **Inverters:** Ideal for low voltage, high frequency inverters.
- **Free Wheeling Diodes:** Protects against voltage spikes in inductive loads.
- **Polarity Protection:** Prevents damage from reverse polarity connections in power circuits.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications where reliability and efficiency are critical.
