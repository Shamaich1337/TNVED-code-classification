  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The TMM-101-03-S-D is a dual-row (double row) surface mount connector designed for high-density applications. It features a robust construction with a black liquid crystal polymer insulator and phosphor bronze terminals, which can be plated with either tin or gold. This connector is suitable for various electronic applications where reliable electrical connections are required.

The TMM series connectors are typically used in:
- **Data Communication**: Ideal for connecting circuit boards in data transmission applications.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in devices requiring compact and reliable interconnections.
- **Industrial Equipment**: Used in machinery and control systems where durability and performance are critical.
- **Telecommunications**: Suitable for networking equipment and telecommunications devices.

This connector is particularly advantageous in applications that require a combination of high current capacity and a compact footprint, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
