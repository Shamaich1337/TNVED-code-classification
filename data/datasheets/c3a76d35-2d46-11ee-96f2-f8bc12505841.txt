Documentation Feedback Copyright ©2009 –2016, Texas Instruments Incorporated8Detailed Description\n8.1 Overview\nThe TPS6223x synchronous step-down DC–DCconverter family includes aunique hysteretic PWM controller\nscheme which enables switch frequencies over 3MHz, excellent transient and ACload regulation aswell as\noperation with cost-competitive external components.\nThe controller topology supports forced PWM mode aswell aspower-save mode operation. ### Component Summary: TPS622318DRYR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.05 V to 6 V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): 1.25 V (fixed)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Peak Current: Up to 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 22 µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current: < 1 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 6-pin USON (DRY) package, dimensions: 1.45 mm x 1.00 mm x 0.6 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency (up to 94%)
  - Supports forced PWM mode and power-save mode for low output ripple voltage
  - 100% duty cycle for low dropout operation
  - Excellent power supply rejection ratio (PSRR) up to 90 dB
  - Small external filter components (1 µH inductor and 4.7 µF output capacitor)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TPS622318 is a high-frequency, synchronous step-down DC-DC converter designed for battery-powered portable applications. It is optimized to deliver a fixed output voltage of 1.25 V while supporting a wide input voltage range from 2.05 V to 6 V. The device is capable of providing up to 500 mA of output current, making it suitable for various low-power applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **LDO Replacement:** The TPS622318 can replace linear regulators in applications where efficiency is critical.
- **Portable Audio and Media Devices:** Ideal for powering audio systems and media players due to its low quiescent current and high efficiency.
- **Low Power Wireless Applications:** Suitable for powering wireless communication devices that require efficient power management.
- **Digital Cameras:** Can be used to supply power to DSP cores in digital cameras, enhancing battery life and performance.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, as it requires minimal external components and has a compact package size. Its ability to operate efficiently across a range of load conditions makes it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
