### Component Summary: HMC441LP3ETR

**Description:**
The HMC441LP3ETR is a GaAs pHEMT MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) medium power amplifier designed for broadband applications. It operates within the frequency range of 6.5 to 13.5 GHz, making it suitable for various RF applications. The amplifier is packaged in a leadless plastic QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) surface mount package, which allows for efficient thermal management and compact design.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +5V (max +6V)
  - Gate Bias Voltage (Vgg1, Vgg2): -8V to 0V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 90 mA (typical at +5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.76 W (at T = 85 °C, derating 8.5 mW/°C above 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 16 Lead 3x3 mm SMT Package (QFN)

- **Special Features:**
  - 14 dB of gain
  - +20 dBm saturated output power at 20% PAE (Power Added Efficiency)
  - 50 Ohm matched input/output
  - Optional gate bias for gain and power adjustment
  - ESD sensitivity class 0 (passed 100V)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC441LP3ETR is commonly used in:
- Point-to-Point Radios
- Point-to-Multi-Point Radios
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) systems
- LO (Local Oscillator) Driver for HMC Mixers
- Military Electronic Warfare (EW) and Electronic Countermeasures (ECM)

This component is ideal for applications requiring medium power amplification in the microwave frequency range, providing reliable performance with minimal external components needed for integration.
