The converter is fully encapsulated in a metal housing, which enhances its resistance to electromagnetic interference and mechanical shocks.

- Railway systems for power management
- Industrial automation where reliable power conversion is critical
- Applications requiring robust performance in harsh environmental conditions, such as temperature extremes and mechanical vibrations

This component is ideal for use in scenarios where high efficiency, reliability, and safety are paramount, such as in transportation and industrial sectors.
