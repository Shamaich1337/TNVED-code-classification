### Component Summary: ES1D-E3/61T from Vishay

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 140 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current (IF(AV)): 1.0 A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 30 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF at IF): 0.92 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMA (DO-214AC)

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultrafast recovery times for high efficiency
  - Low forward voltage and low power losses
  - High forward surge capability
  - Glass passivated pellet chip junction
  - AEC-Q101 qualified options available for automotive applications
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The ES1D-E3/61T is a surface-mount ultrafast plastic rectifier diode. Its primary function is to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) in various electronic circuits. The ultrafast recovery time makes it suitable for high-frequency applications, ensuring minimal power loss during operation.

#### Typical Applications:
The ES1D-E3/61T is commonly used in:
- High-frequency rectification
- Freewheeling applications in switching mode converters
- Inverters for consumer electronics
- Automotive applications
- Telecommunication devices

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and signal processing, particularly in environments where space is limited due to its low-profile package design.
