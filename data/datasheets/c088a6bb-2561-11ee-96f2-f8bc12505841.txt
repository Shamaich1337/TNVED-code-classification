### Component Summary: WMB060N10HGS

**Description:**
The WMB060N10HGS is a 100V N-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET manufactured by Wayon. It utilizes advanced power trench MOSFET technology designed to minimize on-state resistance while maintaining superior switching performance. This component is particularly suited for high-efficiency fast switching applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 95A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: 60A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 380A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 113.6W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - PDFN 5060-8L
- **Special Features:**
  - RDS(on) < 6mΩ @ VGS = 10V
  - Low Gate Charge
  - 100% EAS Guaranteed
  - Green Device Available
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The WMB060N10HGS is commonly used in:
- Power Management Switches
- DC/DC Converters
- LED Backlighting

This MOSFET is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and fast switching capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic devices and systems where high performance and reliability are essential.
