  - Output Voltage: 15 V (typical)
  - Input Voltage Range: 17.5 V to 30 V
  - Absolute Maximum Input Voltage: 30 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Output Current: 100 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power Dissipation: Internally limited (depends on thermal conditions)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature: Up to 150°C
  - Operating Temperature Range: -40°C to +125°C (for NCV variants)
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOIC-8 (D suffix) or TO-92 (P suffix)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Internal short circuit current limiting
  - Internal thermal overload protection
  - No external components required for many applications
  - AEC-Q100 qualified for automotive applications (NCV prefix)
  - Pb-Free device
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **MC78L15ABDR2G** is a fixed voltage regulator that provides a stable output voltage of 15 V with a maximum output current of 100 mA. It is designed to be easy to use and cost-effective, making it suitable for a wide range of applications requiring regulated power supplies. The device features built-in protection mechanisms, including thermal shutdown and current limiting, which enhance its reliability and robustness.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in power supply circuits to provide a stable voltage output for various electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing**: Can be utilized in circuits that require a consistent voltage level for signal integrity.
- **Automotive Applications**: The NCV variant is specifically designed for automotive applications, ensuring compliance with industry standards.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in consumer electronics where regulated voltage is necessary for operation.
