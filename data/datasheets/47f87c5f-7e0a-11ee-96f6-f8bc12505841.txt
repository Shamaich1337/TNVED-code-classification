- **Rated Voltage**: 16 WVDC
- **Dielectric Withstanding Voltage (DWV)**: 250% of rated WVDC for 5 seconds
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +125°C

- **Special Features**: 
  - Low insertion loss performance
  - Compatible with high-speed automated pick and place SMT manufacturing
  - Rugged ceramic construction
  - RoHS compliant terminations

#### Description:
The ATC530L104KT16T is a broadband multilayer capacitor designed for high-frequency applications. It features a low loss X7R dielectric, making it suitable for various RF and microwave applications. The capacitor is specifically engineered to provide low insertion loss across a wide frequency range from 16 kHz to 18 GHz, making it ideal for applications that require minimal signal degradation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Broadband DC Blocking**: Prevents DC voltage from passing through while allowing AC signals to pass, essential in RF circuits.
- **Coupling**: Used to connect two circuits while isolating their DC components, commonly found in audio and RF applications.
- **Bypassing**: Provides a low-impedance path to ground for high-frequency noise, ensuring stable operation in electronic circuits.
- **Feedback Applications**: Used in amplifiers and oscillators to stabilize and control gain.

The ATC530L series capacitors are particularly well-suited for optical communication systems and equipment utilizing high-speed digital logic, where performance and reliability are critical.
