### Component Summary: MBRAF360T3G

**Description:**
The MBRAF360T3G is a surface mount Schottky power rectifier designed for low voltage, high frequency rectification applications. It utilizes the Schottky Barrier principle in a large area metal-to-silicon power diode, making it ideal for compact designs where space and weight are critical. This component is particularly suited for use as free wheeling and polarity protection diodes.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 60 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 
    - 3.0 A at T_L = 100 °C
    - 4.0 A at T_L = 80 °C
  - Peak Repetitive Forward Current (IFRM): 6 A at T_C = 125 °C
  - Nonrepetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 125 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF): 0.63 V at IF = 3.0 A, T_J = 25 °C
  - Maximum Instantaneous Reverse Current (IR): 
    - 0.03 mA at rated DC voltage, T_J = 25 °C
    - 3.0 mA at rated DC voltage, T_J = 100 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature Range (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (T_J): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMA−FL (Epoxy, Molded, meets UL 94, V−0)
  - Weight: Approximately 95 mg

- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package for space-constrained applications
  - Highly stable oxide passivated junction
  - Guard-ring for stress protection
  - AEC−Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free and Halide-Free device
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL 1

**Typical Applications:**
The MBRAF360T3G is commonly used in:
- Power management systems
- Signal rectification in power supplies
- Free wheeling diodes in motor control circuits
- Polarity protection in automotive and consumer electronics
- High-frequency applications where low forward voltage drop is essential

This component is particularly valuable in applications requiring efficient power conversion and compact design, making it suitable for modern electronic devices and automotive systems.
