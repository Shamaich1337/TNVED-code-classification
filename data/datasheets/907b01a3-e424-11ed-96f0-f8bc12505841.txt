- **Voltage Ratings**:

  - Maximum Switching Voltage: 400 VAC / 300 VDC
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Carrying Current: 7 A
  - Rated Current: 5 A (resistive load)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Nominal Power: 530 mW
  - Operate Power: 260 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +75°C (no frost)
  - -40°C to +70°C for -RG type
- **Package Type**: 
  - Low profile relay (height: 16.5 mm)
- **Special Features**: 
  - DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw) configuration
  - Reinforced insulation with dielectric strength of 5 kV
  - Surge strength of 10 kV
  - UL, CSA, VDE, SEMKO, SEV, CQC, FIMKO, IMQ, DEMKO, NEMKO recognized
  - RoHS compliant since date code: 0434R
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Not applicable to electromechanical relays (Moisture Sensitivity Level standard is not applicable)

#### Description:
The FTR-F1CA024V is a low-profile power relay designed for switching applications. It features a double pole double throw (DPDT) configuration, allowing it to control multiple circuits simultaneously. The relay is constructed with reinforced insulation, providing higher isolation between the coil and contacts, making it suitable for applications requiring robust electrical performance.

The FTR-F1CA024V relay is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Power Management**: Controlling power to devices in industrial and consumer electronics.
- **Signal Processing**: Switching signals in communication equipment.
- **Automotive Systems**: Used in automotive electronics for controlling various functions.
- **Home Appliances**: Implemented in appliances for controlling motors and other electrical components.
- **Industrial Automation**: Utilized in control systems for machinery and equipment.

This relay is ideal for applications where space is limited, and reliable switching performance is critical. Its compliance with various safety standards and RoHS directives makes it suitable for modern electronic designs.
