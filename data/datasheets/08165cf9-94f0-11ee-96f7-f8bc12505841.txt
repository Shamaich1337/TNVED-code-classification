### Component Summary: SRR0604-100ML (Bourns)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance**: 10 µH (±20% tolerance)
- **DC Resistance (RDC)**: 0.12 Ω (Max)
- **Rated Current (I rms)**: 1.30 A (Max)
- **Saturation Current (I sat)**: 1.80 A (Typ)
- **Test Voltage**: 1 Volt
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40 °C to +125 °C (Temperature rise included)
- **Storage Temperature**: -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level**: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type**: Shielded Power Inductor
- **Special Features**: Low unit height of 4.8 mm, high current capability, RoHS compliant.

#### Description:
The SRR0604-100ML is a shielded power inductor designed for use in various electronic applications. It is part of the SRR0604 series from Bourns, which features low-profile inductors suitable for high current applications. The inductor is constructed with a ferrite core and enameled copper wire, ensuring efficient performance in power management scenarios.

#### Typical Applications:
- **DC/DC Converters**: Used in the input/output stages to smooth out voltage fluctuations and provide stable power.
- **Power Supplies**: Ideal for portable communication equipment, camcorders, and LCD TVs, where compact size and high efficiency are critical.
- **General Power Management**: Suitable for applications requiring reliable inductance in a small footprint, contributing to overall system efficiency and performance.

This component is particularly valuable in modern electronic devices where space is limited, and high performance is essential.
