  - 0.5mm pitch
  - Stacking height: 8mm to 30mm
  - Floating movement capability: ±0.6mm in both X and Y directions
  - High-speed transmission capability (up to 8+ Gbps)
  - Hybrid power design with 4 lines of power contacts
  - Automatic mounting capability with absorption tape included
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The FX23-120S-0.5SH is a high-speed board-to-board connector designed by Hirose Electric. It features a compact 0.5mm pitch and is capable of supporting both signal and power connections. The connector is designed for vertical stacking and allows for a floating movement, which helps in alignment during assembly. This feature is particularly beneficial in applications where precise positioning is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
The FX23-120S-0.5SH connector is typically used in:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops where space is limited and high-speed data transfer is required.
- **Automotive Applications:** For connecting various electronic modules within vehicles, where reliability and performance are crucial.
- **Industrial Equipment:** In machinery that requires robust and reliable connections for power and signal transmission.
- **Telecommunications:** For high-speed data transmission in networking equipment.

This connector is ideal for applications that demand high-speed data transfer along with the ability to handle power, making it versatile for various electronic designs.
