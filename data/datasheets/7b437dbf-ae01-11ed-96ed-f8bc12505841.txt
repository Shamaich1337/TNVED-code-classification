### Component Summary: PBHV8115T

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 150 V (max)
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 400 V (max)
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6 V (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 1 A (max)
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 2 A (single pulse, tp ≤ 1 ms)
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): 400 mA (single pulse, tp ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 300 mW (at T amb ≤ 25°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB), 3 leads, Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)

- **Special Features:**
  - High voltage capability
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage (VCEsat)
  - High collector current gain (hFE) at high IC
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT23 packages are classified as MSL 1 or MSL 2.

#### Description:
The PBHV8115T is an NPN high-voltage low VCEsat (Breakthrough In Small Signal, BISS) transistor designed for various applications requiring high voltage and low saturation voltage. It is packaged in a compact SOT23 surface-mount package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **LED Driver:** Used in LED chain modules for efficient lighting solutions.
- **LCD Backlighting:** Provides the necessary drive for backlighting in LCD displays.
- **Automotive Lighting:** Suitable for High Intensity Discharge (HID) front lighting applications.
- **Motor Management:** Utilized in automotive motor management systems for controlling motors.
- **Telecom:** Acts as a hook switch in wired telecom applications.
- **Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS):** Employed in power management circuits for efficient energy conversion.

This transistor is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications due to its high voltage ratings and robust performance characteristics.
