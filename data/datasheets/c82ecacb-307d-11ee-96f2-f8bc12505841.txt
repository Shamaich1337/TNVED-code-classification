### Component Summary: TPR3330-S3TR

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Fixed Output Voltage of 3.0 V
- **Current Ratings:** Output current capability of ±5 mA
- **Power Consumption:** Quiescent Current of 180 μA (215 μA over the full temperature range)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:** SOT23G-3
- **Special Features:**
  - High initial accuracy of ±0.15%
  - Low temperature coefficient of 30 ppm/°C
  - Stable with capacitive loads ranging from 0.1 μF to 10 μF
  - Output noise of 60 μVpp (0.1 Hz to 10 Hz)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL3 as per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The TPR3330-S3TR is a precision voltage reference designed to provide a stable output voltage of 3.0 V. It is characterized by its high accuracy and low temperature drift, making it suitable for applications requiring precise voltage regulation. The device operates over an extended temperature range, ensuring reliability in various environmental conditions.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to provide a stable reference voltage.
- **Instrumentation:** Ideal for precision measurement systems where accurate voltage references are critical.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for use in industrial control systems and automation where voltage stability is essential.

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where voltage reference stability is crucial, such as in analog-to-digital converters (ADCs), digital-to-analog converters (DACs), and other precision electronic applications.
