### Component Summary: PA0648NL

**Description:**
The PA0648NL is a low power flyback transformer designed for surface mount technology (SMT) applications. It is primarily used in power management circuits, particularly in isolated power supplies where voltage conversion and electrical isolation are required. The transformer is optimized for low power applications, handling up to 2 watts.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Hi-Pot Primary to Secondary: 1500 Vdc
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Primary Inductance: 247 µH ±20%
  - Minimum Primary Inductance at 0.5 Apk: 170 µH
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Maximum Power: Up to 2W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -40°C to 130°C
- **Package Type:** 
  - SMT (Surface Mount Technology)
  - Dimensions: 9.0mm x 8.6mm Max, Height: 7.6mm Max
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant (indicated by "NL" suffix)
  - Optional Tape & Reel packaging available
  - Leakage Inductance: 5.5 µH MAX with specific terminals shorted
  - K1 Factor: 6838
  - Peak flux density should be kept below 3400 Gauss at 25°C and 2900 Gauss at 100°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** 
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for SMT components.

**Typical Applications:**
The PA0648NL transformer is commonly used in:
- Isolated power supplies for consumer electronics
- Power management systems in various electronic devices
- Flyback converter circuits where electrical isolation and voltage transformation are necessary
- Applications requiring compact and efficient power conversion solutions.

This transformer is suitable for use in a variety of electronic devices, including but not limited to, chargers, adapters, and other low power electronic systems where space and efficiency are critical.
