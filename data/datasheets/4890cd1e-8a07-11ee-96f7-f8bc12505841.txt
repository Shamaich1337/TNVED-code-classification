### Component Summary: EL3H7(C)(TA)-VG

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 80 V
  - Emitter-Collector Voltage (VECO): 7 V
  - Isolation Voltage (Viso): 3750 Vrms

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input Forward Current (IF): 50 mA
  - Collector Current (IC): 50 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFP): 1 A (1 µs pulse)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 200 mW
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 70 mW (derating factor: 2.0 mW/°C above Ta = 90 °C)
  - Output Power Dissipation (PC): 150 mW (derating factor: 3.1 mW/°C above Ta = 70 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOPR): -55 °C to +110 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Soldering Temperature (TSOL): 260 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 4-pin SSOP (Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Halogen-free (Br < 900 ppm, Cl < 900 ppm)
  - Compliance with EU REACH
  - Pb-free and RoHS compliant
  - UL and cUL approved (No. E214129)
  - VDE approved (No. 132249)
  - SEMKO, NEMKO, DEMKO, and FIMKO approved

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **EL3H7-G series** is a **4-pin phototransistor photocoupler** that consists of an infrared emitting diode optically coupled to a phototransistor detector. It is designed to provide electrical isolation between input and output while allowing signal transmission. The device is encapsulated in a compact green compound, making it suitable for surface mount applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The EL3H7(C)(TA)-VG is commonly used in:
- **DC-DC Converters:** For isolating different voltage levels in power supply circuits.
- **Programmable Controllers:** In industrial automation for signal isolation and control.
- **Telecommunication Equipment:** For signal transmission between circuits with different potentials and impedances.
- **Signal Transmission:** In various electronic applications where isolation is required to prevent interference or damage to sensitive components.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high isolation voltage and a compact form factor, making it versatile for various electronic designs.
