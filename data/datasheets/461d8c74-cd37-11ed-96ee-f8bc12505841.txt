### Component Summary: VS-30BQ040-M3/9AT

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** VS-30BQ040-M3

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Reverse Voltage (VR): 40 V
  - Maximum Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 40 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 3.0 A (at 50% duty cycle, T_L = 115 °C)
  - Maximum Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 1600 A (tp = 5 μs)
    - 90 A (10 ms pulse)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 3.0 A, T_J = 125 °C: 0.46 V
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM) at 125 °C: 30 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (DO-214AB)

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low forward voltage drop
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability
  - High frequency operation
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The VS-30BQ040-M3 is a high-performance Schottky rectifier designed for applications that require low forward voltage drop and compact size. Schottky rectifiers are semiconductor devices that allow current to flow in one direction while blocking it in the opposite direction, making them ideal for rectification in power supply circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in switching power supplies to improve efficiency by reducing power loss.
- **Signal Processing:** Employed in circuits where fast switching is required.
- **Battery Charging:** Utilized in battery management systems to prevent reverse current flow.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Commonly used in inductive load applications to protect against voltage spikes.
- **Reverse Battery Protection:** Protects circuits from damage due to incorrect battery connections.

This component is particularly suited for applications in disk drives, converters, and other electronic devices where space is limited and efficiency is critical.
