The ACD10HS065C is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Schottky diode designed for high-efficiency power applications. It features zero forward/reverse recovery current, making it suitable for high-frequency operations. This diode is particularly beneficial in applications where thermal management and reliability are critical.

  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 650 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 650 V

- **Special Features:**
  - Zero Forward/Reverse Recovery Current
  - High Blocking Voltage
  - High Frequency Operation
  - Positive Temperature Coefficient on Forward Voltage (VF)
  - High surge current capability
  - Temperature Independent Switching Behavior

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
