### Component Summary: HMC490LP5ETR

**Description:**
The HMC490LP5ETR is a GaAs pHEMT MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) Low Noise Amplifier designed for high dynamic range applications. It operates within the frequency range of 12 to 16 GHz and is optimized for low noise and high linearity, making it suitable for both receive and transmit applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain Bias Voltage (Vdd): +5.5V (max)
  - Gate Bias Voltage (Vgg): -4V to 0V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 200 mA (typical at Vdd = 5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 1.6 W (at T = 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 32 Lead 5x5 mm QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) surface mount package

- **Special Features:**
  - Noise Figure: 2.5 dB (typical)
  - Gain: 23 dB (typical)
  - Output IP3: +34 dBm
  - 50 Ohm matched input/output
  - Excellent stability with a P1dB output power of +25 dBm

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (for both HMC490LP5 and HMC490LP5E variants)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC490LP5ETR is ideal for various applications, including:
- Point-to-Point Radios
- Point-to-Multi-Point Radios
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) systems
- Military Electronic Warfare (EW), Electronic Countermeasures (ECM), and Communications

This amplifier is particularly suited for systems requiring low noise and high linearity, making it a valuable component in RF communication systems.
