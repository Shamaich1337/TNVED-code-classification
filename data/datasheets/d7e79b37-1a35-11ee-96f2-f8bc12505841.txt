### Component Summary: FDZ Connector (12FDZ-BT(S)(LF)(SN))

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Rating:** 250V AC, DC
- **Current Rating:**
  - Tin-plated copper foil lead: 1.0A AC, DC
  - Silver paste carbon-printed lead: 50mA AC, DC
- **Power Consumption:** Not specified directly; dependent on application and load.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +85°C (including temperature rise due to electrical current)
- **Package Type:** Connector with a 2.54mm pitch
- **Special Features:**
  - Zero Insertion Force (ZIF) mechanism for easy lead insertion
  - Double-sided contact for enhanced reliability
  - Locking mechanism to secure leads after insertion
  - Temporary board retention feature
  - Boss for polarity identification
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The FDZ connector is a Zero Insertion Force (ZIF) connector designed primarily for connecting Flexible Printed Circuits (FPC) and membrane switch leads. Its innovative design allows for easy insertion of leads without the need for force, ensuring that once the leads are inserted, they are securely held in place by a sliding mechanism. The double-sided contact design enhances reliability, making it suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The FDZ connector is typically used in applications where space-saving and ease of connection are critical. Common applications include:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Connecting FPCs in devices like smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Used in control panels and machinery where membrane switches are prevalent.
- **Medical Devices:** Connecting flexible circuits in portable medical equipment.
- **Automotive:** Used in dashboard displays and control systems where space is limited.

This connector is compliant with RoHS standards, ensuring it meets environmental regulations for hazardous substances.
