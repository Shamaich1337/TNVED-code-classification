### Component Summary: ATmega48-20AU

**Manufacturer:** Microchip Technology (formerly Atmel)  
**Product Code:** ATmega48-20AU  
**Type:** 8-bit AVR Microcontroller

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Active Mode: 0.3 mA at 1 MHz, 1.8V, 25°C
  - Power-down Mode: 0.1 µA
  - Power-save Mode: 0.8 µA (including 32 kHz RTC)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption with various sleep modes
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 28-pin PDIP, 32-lead TQFP, and 28-pad QFN/MLF
- **Special Features:**
  - 4KB of Flash memory (In-System Programmable)
  - 256 Bytes of EEPROM
  - 512 Bytes of SRAM
  - 23 Programmable I/O lines
  - 10-bit ADC with up to 8 channels
  - Multiple timer/counter configurations
  - Capacitive touch support via QTouch library
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The ATmega48-20AU is a low-power, high-performance 8-bit microcontroller based on the AVR enhanced RISC architecture. It is designed to execute powerful instructions in a single clock cycle, achieving throughput close to 1 MIPS per MHz. This microcontroller features a rich set of peripherals, including timers, an analog-to-digital converter (ADC), and various communication interfaces (SPI, USART, I2C), making it suitable for a wide range of applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Embedded Control Systems:** Ideal for applications requiring efficient processing and low power consumption.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices such as remote controls, home automation systems, and small appliances.
- **Sensor Interfaces:** The integrated ADC allows for easy interfacing with various sensors for data acquisition.
- **Capacitive Touch Interfaces:** Supports touch-sensitive applications through the QTouch library, enabling the development of touch buttons, sliders, and wheels.
- **Automated Systems:** Suitable for applications in robotics and automation where precise control and monitoring are required.

The ATmega48-20AU is a versatile microcontroller that balances performance and power efficiency, making it a popular choice for both hobbyist and professional embedded system designs.
