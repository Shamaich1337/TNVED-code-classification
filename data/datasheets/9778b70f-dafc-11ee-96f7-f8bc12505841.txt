### Component Summary: SMD260F

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings (VMAX):** 6 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Hold Current (IH):** 2.60 A
  - **Trip Current (IT):** 5.20 A
  - **Maximum Fault Current (IMAX):** 40 A
- **Power Consumption (PD MAX):** 1.70 W (Power dissipated when in the tripped state)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (2920 size)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Level 2a, J-STD-020
- **Special Features:**
  - Resettable overcurrent protection
  - RoHS compliant, lead-free, and halogen-free
  - Fast time-to-trip (typically 8 seconds)

**Description:**
The SMD260F is a PolySwitch® Resettable Polymer Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) device manufactured by Littelfuse. It is designed to provide overcurrent protection in electronic circuits. When the current exceeds the specified trip level, the device transitions from a low-resistance state to a high-resistance state, effectively interrupting the current flow and protecting downstream components.

**Typical Applications:**
The SMD260F is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- **Mobile Electronics and Batteries:** Protecting battery packs and charging circuits from overcurrent conditions.
- **Computers and Portable Electronics:** Safeguarding sensitive components in laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
- **Multimedia Devices:** Ensuring the reliability of audio and video equipment by preventing overcurrent damage.
- **Automotive Applications:** Providing protection in automotive electronics against fault conditions.
- **Industrial Controls:** Used in control systems to prevent damage from unexpected current surges.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, as it offers a compact footprint while delivering effective overcurrent protection.
