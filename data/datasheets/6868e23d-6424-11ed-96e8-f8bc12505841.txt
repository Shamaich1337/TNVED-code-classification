### Component Summary: HI-1573PSI

**Manufacturer:** Holt Integrated Circuits Inc.  
**Product Code:** HI-1573PSI  
**Description:** The HI-1573 is a low power CMOS dual transceiver designed to meet the MIL-STD-1553 specification. It is used for communication in military and aerospace applications, converting digital input data into bi-phase Manchester encoded signals suitable for driving a bus isolation transformer.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.15V to 3.45V (typical 3.3V)
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings: VDD -0.3V to +5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Total Supply Current (Not Transmitting): 4 to 10 mA
  - Transmit Current (One Channel): 225 to 250 mA (50% duty cycle)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Not Transmitting): 0.06 W
  - Power Dissipation (Transmitting): 0.3 to 0.5 W (100% duty cycle)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial Screening: -40°C to +85°C
  - Military Screening: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 20-pin Plastic ESOIC (Thermally Enhanced SOIC)
  - 20-pin Ceramic DIP
  - 44-pin Plastic Chip-Scale Package
- **Special Features:**
  - Compliant with MIL-STD-1553A & B, ARINC 708A
  - Smallest footprint available in its class
  - Internal connection of transmitter outputs to receiver inputs for reduced pin count
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** JEDEC J-STD-020E (specific level not provided in the text)

#### Description:
The HI-1573 is a dual transceiver that facilitates communication over the MIL-STD-1553 data bus. It features a transmitter that encodes digital data into a bi-phase format and a receiver that decodes the incoming signals back into digital data. The device is designed for low power consumption and operates on a single 3.3V supply, making it suitable for various military and aerospace applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Military Communication Systems:** Used in avionics and other military applications that require reliable data transmission.
- **Aerospace Systems:** Facilitates communication between different components in aircraft systems.
- **Data Bus Interfaces:** Acts as a transceiver for data buses adhering to the MIL-STD-1553 standard, ensuring compatibility and reliability in data transmission.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the HI-1573PSI transceiver, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and applications in military and aerospace environments.
