### Component Summary: BSS123 N-Channel MOSFET

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 200mA
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 70°C: 160mA
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 800mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 350mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23
- **Special Features:**
  - Trench Power MV MOSFET technology
  - Low RDS(ON) values: 
    - RDS(ON) at VGS=10V: <5.0Ω
    - RDS(ON) at VGS=4.5V: <5.5Ω
  - Fast switching speed
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Halogen-free and epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating

**Description:**
The BSS123 is an N-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (FET) designed for low voltage applications. It operates as a voltage-controlled small signal switch, utilizing advanced trench power technology to achieve low on-resistance and fast switching capabilities. This makes it suitable for various electronic applications where efficient switching is required.

**Typical Applications:**
- Small servo motor control
- Power MOSFET gate drivers
- General switching applications in electronic circuits

This component is ideal for use in low-power electronic devices, where it can effectively manage power and signal switching tasks, contributing to the overall efficiency and performance of the system.
