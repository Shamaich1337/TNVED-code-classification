The TPI 100-112A-J is a 100 Watt AC/DC power supply from the TPI 100A Series by TRACO POWER. It is designed for industrial applications, featuring a compact open frame design that allows for high power density in a small footprint. The power supply provides a regulated output voltage of 12 VDC and is equipped with reinforced isolation for safety and reliability.
