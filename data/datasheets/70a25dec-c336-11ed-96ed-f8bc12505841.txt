The TCM2-33WX+ is a surface mount RF transformer designed for balanced transmission line applications. The transformer features excellent return loss and is aqueous washable, which enhances its usability in different environments.

- **PCS (Personal Communications Service):** Used in mobile communication systems.
- **Wideband Push-Pull Amplifiers:** Ideal for amplifying RF signals in push-pull configurations.
- **Cellular Applications:** Suitable for use in cellular communication devices and infrastructure.

This component is particularly useful in RF signal processing, where maintaining signal integrity and minimizing losses are critical. Its balanced design helps in reducing common-mode noise, making it a valuable component in high-frequency applications.
