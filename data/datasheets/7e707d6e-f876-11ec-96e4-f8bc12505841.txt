### Component Summary: ROS-1900+ by Mini-Circuits

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Absolute Max Supply Voltage (Vcc): +11V
  - Absolute Max Tuning Voltage (Vtune): +22V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Current: 25 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Power: 10V DC

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -55°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to 100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount, Case Style: CK605

- **Special Features:**
  - Wide frequency range: 1450 to 1900 MHz
  - Low phase noise: -106 dBc/Hz at 10 kHz offset (typical)
  - Linear tuning sensitivity: 22-34 MHz/V (typical)
  - Wide modulation bandwidth: 100 MHz (typical)
  - Aqueous washable
  - Protected by US patent 6,549,084
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The ROS-1900+ is a **Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO)** designed for applications requiring precise frequency generation. It operates within a frequency range of 1450 to 1900 MHz and features linear tuning capabilities, allowing for fine adjustments in frequency output based on the tuning voltage applied. The device is characterized by low phase noise, making it suitable for high-performance applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The ROS-1900+ is commonly used in:
- **Instrumentation:** For precise measurement and control in various electronic systems.
- **Phase-Locked Loops (PLLs):** As part of frequency synthesis and modulation systems.
- **Communication Systems:** In RF applications where stable and adjustable frequency sources are required.
- **PCN (Process Control Networks):** For reliable communication in industrial automation and control systems.

This component is ideal for engineers and designers looking for a reliable VCO that meets stringent performance criteria in demanding environments.
