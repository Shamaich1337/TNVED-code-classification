**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** N-Channel Power MOSFET

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **VDS (Drain-to-Source Voltage):** 30V
  - **VGS (Gate-to-Source Voltage):** ±20V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - **ID @ TC = 25°C (Continuous Drain Current):** 120A (VGS = 10V)
  - **ID @ TC = 100°C (Continuous Drain Current):** 60A (VGS = 10V)
  - **IDM (Pulsed Drain Current):** 1050A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - **PD @ TC = 25°C (Maximum Power Dissipation):** 190W
  - **PD @ TC = 100°C (Maximum Power Dissipation):** 105W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **TJ (Operating Junction Temperature):** -55°C to +175°C
  - **TSTG (Storage Temperature):** -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:** TO-220AB

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low RDS(on) at 4.5V VGS (1.95mΩ @ VGS = 10V)
  - Ultra-low gate impedance
  - Fully characterized avalanche voltage and current
  - Lead-free
  - Optimized for UPS/Inverter applications
  - Suitable for high-frequency isolated DC-DC converters with synchronous rectification for telecom and industrial use

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRLB3813PBF is an N-channel power MOSFET designed for high-efficiency switching applications. It features a low on-resistance, which minimizes power loss during operation, making it ideal for applications requiring high current handling and low voltage drop. The device is characterized by its robust avalanche ratings, allowing it to handle transient conditions effectively.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supplies and converters to efficiently manage power distribution.
- **UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply):** Ideal for providing backup power and maintaining voltage stability.
- **Inverters:** Commonly used in solar inverters and other applications where DC to AC conversion is required.
- **High-Frequency DC-DC Converters:** Suitable for telecom and industrial applications where efficiency and performance are critical.
- **Power Tools:** Utilized in various power tools for efficient motor control and operation.

This component is particularly valued in applications where high efficiency, reliability, and thermal performance are essential.
