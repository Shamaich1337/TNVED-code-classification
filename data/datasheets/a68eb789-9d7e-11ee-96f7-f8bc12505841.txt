- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption can be calculated as \( P = V_F \times I_F \). For typical values:

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage: -40 °C to 100 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - White SMT package with colored diffused silicone resin
- **Special Features:**
  - Side emission LED with a viewing angle of 120°
  - Optical efficiency: 45 lm/W (typical for white)
  - ESD withstand voltage: 2 kV (Class 2)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 4 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LW Y1SG-BFCF-EKFM-1 is a Micro SIDELED, a surface-mount technology (SMT) LED designed for side emission. It features a low profile, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. The LED utilizes InGaN technology on a sapphire substrate, providing high luminous intensity and efficiency.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Backlighting:** Ideal for illuminating displays and panels in compact devices.
- **Coupling into Light Guides:** Used in applications where light needs to be directed through optical fibers or light guides.
- **Signal and Symbol Luminaries:** Commonly employed in indicator lights, signage, and decorative lighting where visibility and color accuracy are essential.

This LED is particularly advantageous in applications requiring compact design and efficient light output, making it suitable for consumer electronics, automotive lighting, and various signaling applications.
