The highly integrated \ndevice includes four buck DC -DC converters, eight high performance ldos, two low Rds switches, \nI2C interface, programmable power sequencing and a n RTC. \n \nThe RK808 improves performance, reduces component count and size, and therefore provides \nlower cost solution compared to conventional p ortable designs.  The ultra fast 2MHz current \nmode DC/DC architecture optimizes the transie nt performance and is compatible with tiny low \ncost ceramic inductors and capacitors.   All DC/DC channels include integrated MOSFETS. \nInternal soft -start and compensation circuits minimize external components count.  Most \noutputs can be programmed through  the I2C interface  \n \n1.2 Feature  \n\uf06c Input voltage range: 2.7V to 5.5V  \n\uf06c 2MHz Switching Frequency for bucks  \n\uf06c Current mode architecture for best transient performance  \n\uf06c Internal compensation and soft start  \n\uf06c I2C Programmable output levels and power sequencing  \n\uf06c High efficiency architecture  \n\uf06c Integrated Vout Discharge Circuit for BUCK and LDO  \n\uf06c Power:  \n• CH1: Synchronous Buck regulator, 5A max  \n• CH2: Synchronous Buck regulator, 5A max  \n• CH3: Synchronous Buck regulator, 3A max  \n• CH4: Synchronous Buck regulator, 2.5A max  \n• CH6,CH7,CH9, CH11: Linear regulators, 150mA max  \n• CH8: Low noise and high PSRR linear regulator,100mA max  \n• CH10,CH12,CH13: Linear regulators, 300mA max  \n• CH14: Low Rds switch, 0.2ohm@Vgs=3v  \n• CH15: Low Rds switch, 0.2ohm@Vgs=3v  \n\uf06c Auxiliary:  Flexible Power Sequence control  \n\uf06c Package:  7mmx7mm QFN68 ### RK808-B Power Management IC Summary

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.7V to 5.5V
  - Buck Regulators Output Voltage Range: 
    - CH1: 0.7125V to 1.5V
    - CH2: 0.7125V to 1.5V
    - CH3: 1.0V to 1.8V
    - CH4: 1.8V to 3.3V
  - LDO Output Voltage Range: 
    - Various outputs ranging from 0.8V to 3.4V depending on the channel.

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Buck Regulators:
    - CH1: 5A max
    - CH2: 5A max
    - CH3: 3A max
    - CH4: 2.5A max
  - LDOs: 
    - Various outputs ranging from 100mA to 300mA depending on the channel.

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 2.5W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - QFN68 (7mm x 7mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated RTC (Real-Time Clock)
  - I2C programmable output levels and power sequencing
  - Internal soft-start and compensation circuits
  - Integrated MOSFETs for all DC/DC channels
  - High efficiency architecture with a 2MHz switching frequency

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **RK808-B** is a highly integrated power management IC designed for portable systems. It features multiple buck DC-DC converters and linear regulators, providing a complete power supply solution. The device is optimized for performance, reducing the component count and size, which leads to a lower cost solution compared to traditional designs. The architecture supports fast transient response and is compatible with low-cost ceramic inductors and capacitors.

#### Typical Applications:
The RK808-B is typically used in:
- **Portable Devices:** Smartphones, tablets, and other handheld devices requiring efficient power management.
- **Battery Management Systems:** It can manage power sequencing and voltage regulation for battery-operated devices.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Suitable for devices that require multiple voltage rails and efficient power distribution.
- **Real-Time Clock Applications:** The integrated RTC allows for timekeeping functions in various electronic devices.

This IC is particularly beneficial in applications where space and efficiency are critical, making it ideal for modern electronic designs.
