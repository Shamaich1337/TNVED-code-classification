### Component Summary: BAS16H,115

**General Description:**
The BAS16H is a high-speed switching diode encapsulated in a small and flat lead SOD123F surface-mounted plastic package. It is designed for applications requiring fast switching speeds and low capacitance.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): Up to 100 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): Up to 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): Up to 215 mA
  - Non-repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FSM): 
    - 4 A for pulse duration of 1 µs
    - 1 A for pulse duration of 1 ms
    - 0.5 A for pulse duration of 1 s
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FRM): Up to 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 
    - 380 mW at T_amb ≤ 25 °C
    - 830 mW under specific mounting conditions

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD123F (2 leads, dimensions: 2.6 mm x 1.6 mm x 1.1 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - High switching speed (t_rr ≤ 4 ns)
  - Low capacitance (C_d ≤ 1.5 pF at VR = 0 V, f = 1 MHz)
  - Low leakage current (I_R ≤ 0.5 µA at VR = 80 V, T_amb = 25 °C)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1 (no dry pack required)

**Description:**
The BAS16H is a high-speed switching diode primarily used for fast switching applications. Its low capacitance and leakage current make it suitable for high-frequency circuits, where rapid response times are critical.

**Typical Applications:**
- High-speed switching applications
- General-purpose switching in electronic circuits
- Signal processing in communication systems
- Rectification in power supply circuits

This diode is ideal for use in applications where quick switching and low power loss are essential, such as in RF applications, digital circuits, and various consumer electronics.
