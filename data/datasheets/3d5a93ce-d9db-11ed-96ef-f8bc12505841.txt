### Component Summary: SY89329VMG-TR

**Manufacturer:** Microchip Technology (formerly Micrel, Inc.)  
**Product Code:** SY89329VMG-TR  
**Package Type:** 8-pin MLF™ (MicroLeadFrame)  
**Moisture Sensitivity Level:** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0V to 3.6V (3.3V nominal) or 4.5V to 5.5V (5V nominal)
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: –0.5V to +4.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current (IOUT): 50mA (continuous), 100mA (surge)
  - Maximum Input Current (IN): ±50mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 20mA (typical, no load)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): –40°C to +85°C
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low jitter design: <1ps RMS random jitter, <10ps PP deterministic jitter
  - Differential LVPECL output
  - Defaults HIGH with open inputs
  - No negative supply required
- **Propagation Delay (tpd):** <100ps IN-to-OUT
- **Maximum Operating Frequency (fMAX):** 800MHz

### Component Description:
The SY89329V is a high-speed TTL/CMOS-to-differential PECL (Positive Emitter-Coupled Logic) translator. It is designed to convert single-ended LVTTL/LVCMOS signals into differential LVPECL signals, making it suitable for high-speed data transmission applications. The device operates from a single positive supply of either 3.3V or 5V, eliminating the need for a negative supply, which simplifies circuit design.

### Typical Applications:
The SY89329V is commonly used in:
- **High-Speed Logic Applications:** It is ideal for interfacing between different logic families, particularly in high-speed digital circuits.
- **Data Communications Systems:** The device is suitable for applications requiring reliable signal transmission over long distances.
- **Wireless Communications Systems:** It can be used in RF and microwave applications where low jitter and high-speed performance are critical.
- **Telecom Systems:** The SY89329V is applicable in telecom equipment where differential signaling is required for robust data integrity.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, as its ultra-small MLF™ package allows for compact designs without compromising performance.
