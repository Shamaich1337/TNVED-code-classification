### Component Summary: XC9536XL-7VQ44I

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply voltage for internal logic (VCCINT): 3.0V to 3.6V
  - Supply voltage for output drivers (VCCIO): 2.5V (2.3V to 2.7V) or 3.3V (3.0V to 3.6V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating supply current (ICC) in low power mode: 10 mA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power dissipation varies based on system frequency and design application; low power mode available to reduce consumption.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to 70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 44-pin Quad Flat Pack (VQFP)

- **Special Features:**
  - 5 ns pin-to-pin logic delays
  - In-system programmable
  - 36 macrocells with 800 usable gates
  - 5V tolerant I/O pins
  - Enhanced data security features
  - ESD protection exceeding 2,000V
  - Endurance exceeding 10,000 program/erase cycles
  - 20-year data retention

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **XC9536XL-7VQ44I** is a high-performance Complex Programmable Logic Device (CPLD) designed for low-voltage applications in advanced communication and computing systems. It features a 3.3V operation with a propagation delay of 7.5 ns, making it suitable for high-speed logic applications. The device is built using advanced 0.35-micron CMOS Fast FLASH™ technology, allowing for efficient performance and low power consumption.

#### Typical Applications:
The XC9536XL is typically used in:
- **Communications Systems:** For implementing complex logic functions and protocols.
- **Computing Systems:** As a glue logic device to connect various components and manage data flow.
- **Embedded Systems:** In applications requiring programmable logic for specific tasks.
- **Data Processing:** For signal processing and data manipulation tasks.
- **Control Systems:** In automation and control applications where programmable logic is beneficial.

This CPLD is particularly advantageous in scenarios where flexibility, low power consumption, and high-speed operation are critical.
