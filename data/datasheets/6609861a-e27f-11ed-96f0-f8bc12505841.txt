### Component Summary: B360A-13-F (Diodes Incorporated)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 60 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 3.0 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 80 A (8.3 ms single half sine wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Total Power Dissipation (PD): 850 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMA (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Guard Ring Die Construction for Transient Protection
  - Low Power Loss, High Efficiency
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free (Green Device)
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The B360A-13-F is a surface mount Schottky barrier rectifier designed for low-voltage, high-frequency applications. It features a maximum forward voltage drop of 0.70 V at 3 A and a low leakage current of 0.5 mA at rated VR. The device is constructed with a guard ring die for enhanced transient protection, making it suitable for various power management applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient rectification.
- **Inverters:** Ideal for low-voltage, high-frequency inverters.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Utilized in DC-DC conversion applications for efficient power transfer.
- **Polarity Protection:** Employed in circuits to prevent reverse polarity damage.
- **Freewheeling Applications:** Commonly used in inductive load circuits to manage back EMF.

This component is particularly well-suited for automated assembly processes due to its surface mount design and compliance with environmental regulations, making it a reliable choice for modern electronic applications.
