### Component Summary: BC857BS

**Manufacturer:** Nexperia  
**Product Code:** BC857BS  
**Type:** PNP General Purpose Double Transistor  
**Package Type:** SOT363 (TSSOP6)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): -45 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): -50 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): -5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): -100 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): -200 mA
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): -200 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 200 mW (per transistor), 300 mW (per device)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to 150 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low collector capacitance
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage
  - Closely matched current gain
  - No mutual interference between the transistors

#### Description:
The BC857BS is a PNP general-purpose double transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is housed in a compact SOT363 (TSSOP6) surface-mounted package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The device features low collector capacitance and low saturation voltage, which enhances its performance in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- General-purpose switching
- Signal amplification
- Low-power applications where space is limited
- Consumer electronics
- Audio equipment

This component is ideal for use in applications that require efficient switching and amplification with minimal board space, making it a versatile choice for designers in various electronic fields.
