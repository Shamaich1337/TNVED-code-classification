### Component Summary: FS820R08A6P2B

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCES): 750 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 750 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Implemented Collector Current (IC): 820 A
  - Continuous DC Collector Current (IC nom): 450 A (at TF = 80°C, T vj max = 175°C)
  - Repetitive Peak Collector Current (ICRM): 1640 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 714 W (at TF = 75°C, T vj max = 175°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Operating Temperature (Tvj op): -40°C to 150°C (up to 175°C for short-time operation)

- **Package Type:**
  - HybridPACK™ Drive Module

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated NTC temperature sensor
  - Direct cooled PinFin base plate for superior thermal characteristics
  - PressFIT contact technology for easy assembly
  - RoHS compliant and UL 94 V0 module frame
  - High creepage and clearance distances for increased voltage handling

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **FS820R08A6P2B** is a **HybridPACK™ Drive Module** designed for high-power applications, particularly in automotive environments such as hybrid and electric vehicles. It features the EDT2 IGBT technology, which is optimized for electric drive train applications, providing high current density, short circuit ruggedness, and improved efficiency at light loads. The module is compact and integrates mechanical guiding elements for easy assembly, making it suitable for modern automotive power electronics.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Hybrid and Electric Vehicles (HEVs and EVs):** The module is primarily used in the power electronics of electric drive trains, enabling efficient motor control and energy management.
- **Motor Drives:** Suitable for various motor drive applications, including industrial and commercial vehicles.
- **Commercial Agriculture Vehicles:** The robust design and high power handling make it ideal for agricultural machinery requiring reliable power management.

This module is engineered to operate under harsh environmental conditions, ensuring reliability and efficiency in demanding automotive applications.
