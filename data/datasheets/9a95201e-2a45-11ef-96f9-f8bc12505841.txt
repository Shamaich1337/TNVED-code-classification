  - Output Current: Up to 80 mA for both positive and negative outputs

  - Operating Quiescent Current: 1.7 mA
  - Shutdown Current: 0.1 to 2 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature: -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 16-Terminal Thin QFN (RTE) Package, dimensions 3 mm x 3 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Single-Inductor, Multiple-Output (SIMO) Regulator
  - Short-Circuit Protection
  - Thermal Shutdown
  - Excellent Line Regulation
  - Advanced Power-Save Mode for Light-Load Efficiency
  - Out-of-Audio Mode to minimize audible noise

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS65136** is a highly integrated power management solution designed specifically for Active-Matrix OLED (AMOLED) displays. It utilizes a single-inductor, multiple-output (SIMO) topology to generate both positive and negative supply voltages, making it ideal for applications that require dual voltage rails. The device operates in a buck-boost configuration, allowing it to efficiently manage varying input voltages while maintaining stable output voltages.

#### Typical Applications:
- **AMOLED Display Power Supply:** The TPS65136 is primarily used in mobile devices, such as smartphones, where it powers the AMOLED screens that require both positive and negative voltage supplies.
- **Portable Electronics:** Its compact size and efficiency make it suitable for various portable electronic devices that demand high-quality power management.
- **Consumer Electronics:** The device can also be found in other consumer electronics that utilize OLED technology, ensuring optimal performance and display quality.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, providing a reliable solution for powering advanced display technologies.
