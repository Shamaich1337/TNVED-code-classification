### Component Summary: HFCN-2700A+

**Description:**
The HFCN-2700A+ is a high-pass filter designed for RF applications, specifically operating within the frequency range of 2900 to 8700 MHz. It is a ceramic filter that utilizes LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) technology, providing excellent performance in terms of power handling and temperature stability. The component is hermetically sealed, ensuring reliability in various environmental conditions.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not specified directly; however, the RF power input is rated at 37 W max at 25°C.
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified; the focus is on RF power handling.
- **Power Consumption:** 37 W max at 25°C; derates linearly to 3.5 W at 100°C ambient.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** FV1206-1 (small size).
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant.
  - Excellent power handling capability (up to 7 W).
  - Temperature stable performance.
  - Protected by US Patent 7,760,485.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The HFCN-2700A+ is typically used in:
- Sub-harmonic rejection applications.
- Transmitters and receivers in RF communication systems.
- Any application requiring high-frequency signal processing where unwanted lower frequency signals need to be filtered out.

This component is ideal for use in telecommunications, satellite communications, and other RF applications where high-frequency performance and reliability are critical.
