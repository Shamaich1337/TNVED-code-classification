It is designed using high-performance silicon-gate CMOS technology, making it suitable for a variety of digital applications. The device features TTL-compatible inputs, allowing it to interface with TTL logic levels.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Supply Voltage (VCC):**

  - **Input Voltage (VI):** 0.5 to VCC - 0.5 V
  - **Output Voltage (VO):** 0.5 to VCC - 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Output Sink/Source Current (IO):** ±24 mA
  - **DC Supply Current per Output Pin (ICC):** 50 mA
  - **DC Input Diode Current (IIK):** ±20 mA
  - **DC Output Diode Current (IOK):** ±50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Power Dissipation (PD):** 
    - SOIC: 520 mW
    - TSSOP: 382 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Storage Temperature (TSTG):** -65 to +150 °C
  - **Junction Temperature (TJ):** 150 °C
  - **Ambient Temperature Range (TA):** -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOIC-14 and TSSOP-14 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Pb-free device.
  - TTL compatible inputs.
  - High output drive capability (24 mA).
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E).

- **Logic Level Shifting:** It can be used to convert signals between different voltage levels.
- **Signal Inversion:** As an inverter, it is used in applications where signal inversion is required.
- **Data Processing:** It can be part of larger digital systems for data manipulation and processing.
- **Timing Circuits:** It can be used in timing applications where signal delay or inversion is necessary.

This component is ideal for use in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial control systems where reliable digital logic operations are required.
