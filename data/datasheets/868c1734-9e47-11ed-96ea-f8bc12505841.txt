### Component Summary: CNY75GB (Vishay Semiconductors)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Rated isolation voltage (RMS): 600 V RMS (848 V peak)
  - Rated recurring peak voltage (VIORM): 600 V RMS
  - Rated impulse voltage (VIOTM): 6 kV peak
  - AC isolation test voltage: 5000 V RMS

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward current (IF): 60 mA
  - Collector current (IC): 50 mA
  - Collector peak current (ICM): 100 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total power dissipation (Ptot): 200 mW
  - Power dissipation (P diss): 70 mW (input/output)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient temperature range (Tamb): -55 to +110 °C
  - Storage temperature range (Tstg): -55 to +125 °C
  - Junction temperature (Tj): 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DIP-6 (Dual Inline Package), 400 mil

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Low temperature coefficient of Current Transfer Ratio (CTR)
  - Rated for reinforced isolation according to safety class II
  - Compliant with RoHS directive 2002/95/EC and WEEE 2002/96/EC
  - Pollution degree 2 (DIN/VDE 0110/IEC 60664)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **CNY75GB** is an optocoupler featuring a phototransistor output with a base connection. It consists of a gallium arsenide infrared-emitting diode optically coupled to a phototransistor, encapsulated in a 6-pin plastic dual inline package. This component is designed to provide electrical isolation between its input and output, making it suitable for various applications where signal integrity and safety are paramount.

#### Typical Applications:
The CNY75GB is commonly used in:
- **Switch-mode power supplies:** To provide feedback and control signals while maintaining isolation.
- **Line receivers:** For transmitting signals over long distances without direct electrical connection.
- **Computer peripheral interfaces:** To isolate and protect sensitive components from high voltages.
- **Microprocessor system interfaces:** For safe communication between microcontrollers and other devices.
- **Circuits requiring safe protective separation against electrical shock:** Particularly in applications where mains voltage is involved, ensuring compliance with safety standards.

This optocoupler is particularly valued for its ability to maintain signal integrity while providing high voltage isolation, making it a critical component in many electronic systems.
