### Component Summary: 2N7001TDPWR

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCCA (Input Supply Voltage): 1.65 V to 3.6 V
  - VCCB (Output Supply Voltage): 1.65 V to 3.6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous output current: ±50 mA
  - Continuous current through VCCA: ±10 mA
  - Continuous current through VCCB: ±50 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum quiescent current (ICCA + ICCB): 14 µA (at 125°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction temperature: -40°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - X2SON (5 pins, 0.80 mm x 0.80 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Supports up to 100 Mbps data rates
  - VCC isolation feature: Output becomes high-impedance if either VCC input is below 100 mV
  - Ioff supports partial-power-down mode operation
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 100 mA per JESD 78, Class II
  - ESD protection: 2000 V (Human Body Model), 1000 V (Charged Device Model)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The **2N7001T** is a single-bit dual-supply buffered voltage signal converter designed for up and down voltage translation of unidirectional signals. It operates with two separate configurable power-supply rails (VCCA and VCCB), allowing it to translate signals between different voltage levels ranging from 1.65 V to 3.6 V. The device is particularly useful in applications where interfacing between devices operating at different voltage levels is required.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Microcontroller (MCU) / FPGA / Processor GPIO Translation:** Facilitates communication between devices with different voltage levels.
- **Communications Modules:** Used for translating signals between processors and communication modules.
- **Push-Pull I/O Buffering:** Provides buffering for input/output signals to improve signal integrity and drive capability.
- **Level Translation Applications:** Ideal for interfacing between devices or systems that operate at different interface voltages, such as in mixed-voltage environments.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications requiring low power consumption, high-speed data transmission, and robust ESD protection, making it suitable for modern electronic designs.
