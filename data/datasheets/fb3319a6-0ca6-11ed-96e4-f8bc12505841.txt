### Component Summary: MMBD1503A

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VR): 200 V (at IR = 5.0 µA)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 200 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 1.0 A (Pulse Width = 1.0 s)
    - 2.0 A (Pulse Width = 1.0 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 350 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): −55 to +150 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): −55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (TO-236)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Low reverse leakage current (IR) at high reverse voltages.
  - Suitable for high-speed switching applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1 (no dry pack required).

**Description:**
The MMBD1503A is a small-signal diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It is part of a family of diodes that includes variants with different specifications. The MMBD1503A is characterized by its ability to handle high reverse voltages and moderate forward currents, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Signal Processing:** Used in signal rectification and clamping applications.
- **Power Management:** Ideal for power supply circuits where voltage regulation and rectification are required.
- **Switching Circuits:** Commonly used in high-speed switching applications due to its fast recovery time.
- **General Purpose:** Can be utilized in various electronic circuits requiring small-signal diodes.

This diode is particularly useful in applications where space is limited, and efficiency is critical, such as in portable electronics and compact power supplies.
