#### Description:
The TSM 0515D is an isolated DC/DC converter designed to provide a dual output of ±15 VDC with a maximum output current of ±30 mA. It is part of the TSM series from TRACO POWER, which features a compact SMD package suitable for automated surface mount device (SMD) production. The converter is constructed using lead frame technology, ensuring high reliability and performance in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The TSM 0515D is ideal for:
- **Point of Load Power Conversion:** Efficiently converting voltage levels close to the load.
- **Ground Loop Elimination:** Reducing noise and interference in sensitive electronic circuits.
- **Noise Reduction:** Providing stable power supply in environments with electrical noise.
- **Digital Interface Applications:** Powering digital circuits that require stable voltage levels.

This component is particularly useful in telecommunications, industrial automation, and medical devices where reliable power conversion is critical. Its high efficiency and wide operating temperature range make it suitable for demanding applications.
