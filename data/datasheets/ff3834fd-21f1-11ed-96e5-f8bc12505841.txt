### Component Summary: BAV199

**Description:**
The BAV199 is a dual surface mount low leakage diode manufactured by Diodes Incorporated. It is designed for applications requiring low leakage current and high reliability. The diode is housed in a SOT23 package, making it suitable for automated insertion in circuit boards.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 85 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 85 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (V_R(RMS)): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (I_FM): 160 mA (single diode), 140 mA (double diode)
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FRM): 500 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 
    - 4.0 A (1.0 μs)
    - 1.0 A (1.0 ms)
    - 0.5 A (1.0 s)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 250 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -65 to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -65 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low leakage current
  - Lead, halogen, and antimony free; RoHS compliant
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for high reliability
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 1 per J-STD-020

### Typical Applications:
The BAV199 is typically used in various electronic applications, including:

- **Power Management:** As a rectifier in power supply circuits to ensure efficient energy conversion.
- **Signal Processing:** In signal clamping and protection circuits to prevent voltage spikes from damaging sensitive components.
- **Data Communication:** Used in data lines to protect against reverse polarity and to ensure signal integrity.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its high reliability and compliance with automotive standards, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics.

This diode is particularly advantageous in applications where low leakage is critical, such as battery-powered devices and sensitive electronic circuits.
