### TLV431AQPK - Texas Instruments

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reference Voltage (VREF): 1.24 V
  - Adjustable Output Voltage (VO): 1.24 V to 6 V
  - Maximum Cathode Voltage (VKA): 7 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Cathode Current (IK): -20 mA to 20 mA
  - Minimum Cathode Current for Regulation (IK(min)): 55 µA (typical 80 µA)
  - Reference Current (Iref): 0.15 µA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low operational cathode current: 80 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - TLV431AQPK: -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89 (3 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low-voltage operation
  - Adjustable output voltage
  - Low output impedance (0.25 Ω typical)
  - Integrated reference voltage with thermal stability
  - Compliant with MIL-PRF-38535 for certain parameters

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The TLV431AQPK is a low-voltage adjustable precision shunt regulator. It is a three-terminal device that provides a stable reference voltage of 1.24 V, which can be adjusted to output voltages ranging from 1.24 V to 6 V using external resistors. The device is designed for applications requiring precise voltage regulation and is characterized by low operational current and low output impedance, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Adjustable Voltage and Current Referencing:** The TLV431 can be used to create adjustable voltage references in circuits.
- **Secondary Side Regulation in Flyback Switching Mode Power Supplies (SMPS):** It is commonly used in isolated feedback circuits for power supplies operating at 3 V to 3.3 V.
- **Zener Diode Replacement:** The TLV431 can serve as a more efficient alternative to traditional Zener diodes in voltage regulation applications.
- **Voltage Monitoring:** It can be utilized in circuits that require monitoring of voltage levels.
- **Comparator with Integrated Reference:** The device can function as a comparator in various applications, providing a reference voltage for comparison.

This component is ideal for use in industrial, automotive, telecommunications, and computing applications where precise voltage regulation is critical.
