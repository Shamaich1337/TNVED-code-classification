The device is designed to provide high-speed logic functions while maintaining low power consumption, making it suitable for a variety of digital applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V_CC): 
    - Recommended Operating Conditions: 
      - AC: 2.0V to 6.0V
      - ACT: 4.5V to 5.5V
    - Absolute Maximum Ratings: -0.5V to +7.0V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Output Source/Sink Current (I_O): ±50mA
  - DC Input Diode Current (I_IK):

    - +20mA (when V_I = V_CC + 0.5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Quiescent Supply Current (I_CC): 20µA (at V_CC = 5.5V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -65°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): 140°C
  - Ambient Operating Temperature (T_A): -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 14-Lead Small Outline Integrated Circuit (SOIC), JEDEC MS-012, 0.150" Narrow

- **Special Features:**
  - TTL-compatible inputs
  - Outputs can source/sink up to 24mA
  - Reduced I_CC by 50% compared to previous versions

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

The 74ACT00SC is commonly used in digital logic circuits for applications such as:
- **Data Processing:** Implementing logic functions in microcontrollers and digital signal processors.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Used in circuits that require signal inversion and logic level manipulation.
- **Control Systems:** Employed in control logic for various electronic devices and systems.
- **Power Management:** Utilized in power management circuits to control power distribution and switching.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high-speed operation and low power consumption, making it suitable for modern electronic designs.
