### Component Summary: SMF5.0A (Littelfuse)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VR): 5.0 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.4 V (min), 7.0 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR) at VR: 10 µA
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (Ipp) at 10/1000 µs: 400 A
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPPM) at 10/1000 µs: 200 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65 °C to 175 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-123FL (Surface Mount)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile (maximum height of 1.1 mm)
  - Fast response time (typically < 1.0 ns)
  - Built-in strain relief
  - Glass passivated junction
  - ESD protection compliant with IEC-61000-4-2 (30 kV air, 30 kV contact)
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The SMF5.0A is a Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by lightning strikes and other transient voltage events. It is a bi-directional device, meaning it can clamp voltage spikes in both directions, making it suitable for protecting circuits that may experience reverse polarity.

#### Typical Applications:
The SMF5.0A is commonly used in:
- Protection of I/O interfaces in electronic devices
- VCC bus protection in portable electronics
- Safeguarding sensitive components in cellular phones and business machines
- Power supply protection in consumer electronics
- ESD protection for data lines in accordance with IEC standards

This component is particularly valuable in applications where space is limited due to its small footprint and low profile, making it ideal for modern electronic designs that require robust transient protection without compromising on size.
