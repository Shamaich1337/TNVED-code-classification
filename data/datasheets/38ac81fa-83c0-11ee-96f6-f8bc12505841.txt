### Component Summary: THN 30-2422WI DC/DC Converter

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 9 - 36 VDC (24 VDC nominal)
  - Output Voltage: +12 VDC and -12 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 1,250 mA for both +12 VDC and -12 VDC outputs
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power: 30 Watts
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +80°C (up to +85°C with heat sink)
- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 1.0" x 1.0" x 0.4" (25.4 mm x 25.4 mm x 10.16 mm)
  - Shielded metal case with isolated baseplate
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency up to 89% across the full load range
  - Over temperature protection
  - Remote On/Off control
  - Output voltage adjustable
  - I/O isolation voltage: 1600 VDC
  - Low no-load input current (10 mA typical at 24 V)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The THN 30-2422WI is a high-performance DC/DC converter module designed to provide a regulated output voltage of +12 VDC and -12 VDC from a wide input voltage range of 9 to 36 VDC. It is part of the THN 30WI series, known for its compact size and high power density, achieving 30 Watts of output power in a small footprint. The converter features a shielded metal case, ensuring durability and reliability in various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The THN 30-2422WI is suitable for a variety of applications, particularly where space is limited on the PCB. Typical uses include:
- Mobile equipment
- Instrumentation
- Distributed power architectures in communication systems
- Industrial electronics
- Battery-operated systems due to its low no-load input current and remote control capabilities.

This converter is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and reliable performance in challenging environments, making it a versatile choice for engineers and designers.
