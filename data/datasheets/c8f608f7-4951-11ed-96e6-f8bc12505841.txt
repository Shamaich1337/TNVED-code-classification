  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±12 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 650 mA
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDP): 1300 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 1 W (1.5 W for short durations)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Channel Temperature (Tch): -55 to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23F

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified
  - 3.0-V gate drive voltage
  - Built-in internal Zener diodes and resistors

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The SSM3K357R is a Silicon N-Channel MOSFET designed for various switching applications. It features a low on-resistance and is capable of handling significant current and voltage levels, making it suitable for efficient power management in electronic circuits. The device is AEC-Q101 qualified, indicating its reliability for automotive applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Relay Drivers:** The SSM3K357R can be used to drive relays in automotive and industrial applications, providing efficient control of high-power devices.
- **Power Management:** It is suitable for power management circuits, where it can switch power to various components efficiently.
- **Signal Switching:** The MOSFET can be utilized in signal processing applications, allowing for the control of signal paths in electronic devices.
- **Automotive Applications:** Given its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is particularly well-suited for automotive electronics, including control systems and power distribution.

This component is ideal for designers looking for a reliable and efficient MOSFET for various applications, particularly in automotive and power management systems.
