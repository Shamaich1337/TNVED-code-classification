  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SN74LVCH8T245** is an 8-bit dual-supply non-inverting bus transceiver designed for asynchronous communication between two data buses. It features configurable voltage level shifting, allowing it to interface between different voltage levels (1.8V, 2.5V, 3.3V, and 5V). The device has two separate power supply rails (VCCA and VCCB), enabling it to operate with different voltage levels on each side.

- **Personal Electronics:** It can be found in smartphones, tablets, and other consumer electronics that require communication between components with varying voltage levels.
- **Industrial Applications:** Used in industrial automation systems where different sensors and controllers operate at different voltage levels.
- **Telecommunications:** Facilitates communication between devices in telecom systems that may operate at different voltage levels.
- **Enterprise Systems:** Useful in data centers and enterprise-level hardware where multiple voltage levels are present.
