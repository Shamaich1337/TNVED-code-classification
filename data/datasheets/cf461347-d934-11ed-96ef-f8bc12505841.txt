### Component Summary: SFH6156-3T (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 6 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CEO): 70 V
  - Emitter-Collector Voltage (V_ECO): 7 V
  - Isolation Test Voltage (V_ISO): 5300 V RMS

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (I_F): 60 mA
  - Surge Forward Current (I_FSM): 2.5 A
  - Collector Current (I_C): 50 mA (100 mA for pulse ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - LED Power Dissipation (P_diss): 100 mW
  - Output Power Dissipation (P_diss): 150 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -55 to +100 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMD-4, 100 mil pitch

- **Special Features:**
  - High reliability with excellent current transfer ratio (CTR) linearity.
  - Fast switching times and low coupling capacitance.
  - Material categorization compliant with Vishay's standards.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SFH6156-3T** is an optocoupler featuring a phototransistor output designed for high reliability applications. It consists of a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) infrared diode emitter optically coupled to a silicon planar phototransistor detector. This component is housed in a surface-mount device (SMD) package and is specifically designed for signal transmission between electrically isolated circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The SFH6156-3T is commonly used in:
- **Switchmode Power Supplies:** For isolating control signals from high voltage sections.
- **Telecommunications:** To ensure signal integrity and isolation in communication devices.
- **Battery-Powered Equipment:** Where low power consumption and high reliability are critical.

This optocoupler is suitable for applications requiring safe electrical insulation and is compliant with various safety standards, making it a versatile choice for designers in the electronics industry.
