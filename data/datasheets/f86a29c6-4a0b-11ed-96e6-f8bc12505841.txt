  - High LO-RF isolation
  - Protected by multiple US patents

#### Description:
The ZX05-10L-S+ is a **frequency mixer** designed for RF applications. Its primary function is to convert an input radio frequency (RF) signal into an intermediate frequency (IF) signal, which is essential in various communication systems. The mixer operates over a frequency range of 10 MHz to 1000 MHz and is optimized for low conversion loss and high isolation between the local oscillator (LO) and RF ports.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Communication**: Used in mobile devices and base stations for signal processing.
- **PCS (Personal Communications Service)**: Facilitates communication in personal devices.
- **Instrumentation**: Employed in measurement and testing equipment for signal analysis.
- **Satellite Communication**: Utilized in satellite transceivers for signal conversion and processing.

This component is particularly suitable for applications requiring reliable performance in harsh environments, given its wide operating temperature range and robust construction.
