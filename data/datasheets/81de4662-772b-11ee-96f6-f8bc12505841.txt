### Component Summary: IPP60R199CPXKSA1

**Description:**
The IPP60R199CP is a CoolMOS® Power Transistor manufactured by Infineon Technologies. It is a high-performance MOSFET designed for hard switching applications, particularly in server and telecom power management systems. This component is characterized by its low on-resistance and low gate charge, making it suitable for efficient power conversion.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 650 V
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): 600 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=25 °C: 16 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=100 °C: 10 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulse): 51 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot) at TC=25 °C: 139 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PG-TO220 (TO220-3)

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low gate charge (Qg,typ: 32 nC)
  - High peak current capability
  - Extreme dv/dt rated
  - Qualified according to JEDEC standards
  - Pb-free lead plating; RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

### Typical Applications:
The IPP60R199CP is primarily used in power management applications, including:
- Power supplies for servers and telecom equipment
- DC-DC converters
- Motor control circuits
- High-efficiency power conversion systems

This component's design allows it to handle high voltages and currents efficiently, making it ideal for applications where performance and reliability are critical. Its low on-resistance and gate charge characteristics contribute to reduced power losses and improved thermal performance in various electronic circuits.
