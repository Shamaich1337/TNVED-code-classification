### Component Summary: MLPF-WB-01E3

**Description:**
The MLPF-WB-01E3 is a low pass filter designed specifically for RF applications, particularly optimized for use with STMicroelectronics' STM32WB series microcontrollers. It integrates an impedance matching network and a harmonics filter, enhancing RF performance by minimizing insertion loss and maximizing signal integrity. The device is built using STMicroelectronics' IPD technology on a non-conductive glass substrate, which contributes to its high RF performance.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Power (RF IN): 10 dBm
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Not specified in the datasheet.
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Not explicitly stated; primarily passive component.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +105 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Bumpless CSP (Chip Scale Package) on glass substrate
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated impedance matching for STM32WB series
  - 50 Ω nominal impedance on the antenna side
  - Deep rejection harmonics filter
  - Low insertion loss (typical 0.90 - 1.1 dB)
  - Small footprint with low thickness (≤ 450 μm)
  - ECOPACK2 compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The MLPF-WB-01E3 is suitable for various wireless communication protocols, including:
- Bluetooth 5
- OpenThread
- Zigbee®
- IEEE 802.15.4

These applications typically involve low-power wireless communication, making the MLPF-WB-01E3 ideal for IoT devices, smart home applications, and other wireless sensor networks where efficient RF performance is critical.
