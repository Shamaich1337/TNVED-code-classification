### Component Summary: SFH6156-2T (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 6 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CEO): 70 V
  - Emitter-Collector Voltage (V_ECO): 7 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (I_F): 60 mA
  - Collector Current (I_C): 50 mA (100 mA for pulse ≤ 1 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - LED Power Dissipation: 100 mW
  - Output Power Dissipation: 150 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -55 to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature: -55 to +100 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SMD-4, 100 mil pitch
- **Special Features:**
  - High isolation voltage of 5300 V RMS
  - Fast switching times
  - Low coupling capacitance
  - Excellent current transfer ratio (CTR) linearity depending on forward current
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 1 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **SFH6156-2T** is an optocoupler featuring a phototransistor output. It consists of a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) infrared diode emitter that is optically coupled to a silicon planar phototransistor detector. This component is designed for high reliability and is housed in a plastic surface-mount device (SMD) package. The optocoupler is primarily used for signal transmission between two electrically isolated circuits, providing safety and noise immunity.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Switchmode Power Supplies:** Used for feedback and control in power conversion applications.
- **Telecommunications:** Provides isolation in communication lines to protect sensitive components.
- **Battery-Powered Equipment:** Helps in reducing power consumption while maintaining signal integrity.
- **Industrial Automation:** Used in control systems to isolate different parts of the circuit for safety and reliability.

This component is suitable for applications requiring high voltage isolation and reliable signal transmission, making it ideal for various electronic systems where safety and performance are critical.
