### Component Summary: HCPL-2601-000E

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Working Insulation Voltage (VIORM): 1414 V peak
  - Momentary Withstand Voltage: 5000 Vrms for 1 minute
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input Forward Current (IF): 20 mA (max)
  - Output Collector Current (IO): 50 mA (max)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Power Dissipation: 40 mW (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Pin DIP (300 mil)
- **Special Features:**
  - High speed: 10 MBd typical
  - 15 kV/µs minimum Common Mode Rejection (CMR) at VCM = 1 kV
  - Strobable output (single channel products only)
  - UL recognized with safety approvals (3750 Vrms for 1 minute)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The HCPL-2601-000E is an optically coupled isolator (opto-coupler) that integrates a Gallium Arsenide Phosphide (GaAsP) light-emitting diode and a high-gain photodetector. It is designed to provide electrical isolation between input and output while allowing for high-speed data transmission. The device features an open collector Schottky-clamped transistor output, which is TTL compatible, making it suitable for interfacing with digital logic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The HCPL-2601-000E is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Isolated Line Receivers:** To ensure signal integrity in noisy environments.
