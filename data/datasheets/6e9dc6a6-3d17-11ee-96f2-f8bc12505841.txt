### Component Summary: MAX4360EAX+

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Voltage: ±4.5V to ±5.5V
  - Total Supply Voltage (V+ to V-): +12V
  - Positive Supply Voltage (V+): -0.3V to +12V
  - Negative Supply Voltage (V-): -12V to +0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (All Buffers On): 37 mA
  - Supply Current (All Buffers Off): 1.6 to 5 mA
  - Output Leakage Current: ±100 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Continuous Power Dissipation (T_A = +70°C): 941 mW (for 36-Pin SSOP)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 36-Pin SSOP (A36-2)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Three-state output capability
  - Internal programmable active loads
  - Power-saving disable feature
  - 250 V/µs slew rate
  - 65 MHz -3dB bandwidth
  - 80 dB all-channel off-isolation at 5 MHz
  - 70 dB single-channel crosstalk

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1

**Description:**
The MAX4360EAX+ is a low-cost video crosspoint switch designed for high-speed signal routing in video applications. It features an 8x4 switch matrix that allows any of its eight video inputs to be connected to any of its four buffered outputs. Each output is buffered by a high-speed amplifier capable of driving loads of 400Ω and 20pF at 2.6V P-P. The device is optimized for applications requiring minimal crosstalk and high isolation between channels.

**Typical Applications:**
The MAX4360EAX+ is commonly used in:
- Video-on-demand systems
- Video test equipment
- Video conferencing systems
- Security systems
- High-speed signal routing applications

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where multiple video sources need to be switched to a limited number of outputs, allowing for flexible video signal management while maintaining signal integrity.
