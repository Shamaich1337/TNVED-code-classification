**Description:**
The TEL 10-2412WI is an isolated 10 Watt DC/DC converter from the TEL 10 series by Traco Power. It is designed in a compact DIP-16 metal package, making it suitable for applications where space is critical. The converter provides a stable output voltage of 12 VDC and is capable of delivering up to 833 mA of output current. It features high efficiency, which minimizes thermal losses, and is equipped with built-in filtering to comply with conducted emissions standards.

- Power management in embedded systems
- Signal processing in communication devices
- Power supply for sensors and control systems
- General-purpose DC/DC conversion in space-constrained environments
