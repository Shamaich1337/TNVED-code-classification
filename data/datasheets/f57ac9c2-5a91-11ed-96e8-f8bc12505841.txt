### Component Summary: TDR 3-4822WISM

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 18 - 75 VDC (Nominal: 48 VDC)
  - Output Voltage: +12 VDC and -12 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 125 mA for both +12 VDC and -12 VDC outputs
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 3 Watts
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C (no derating)
- **Package Type:**
  - SMD (Surface-Mount Device)
- **Special Features:**
  - Fully regulated outputs
  - Low ripple and noise (30 mVp-p typical)
  - Continuous short-circuit protection
  - Remote On/Off control
  - I/O isolation: 1600 VDC
  - High efficiency (82% typical)
  - Fully RoHS compliant
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2a (J-STD-033C)

**Description:**
The TDR 3-4822WISM is a compact 3 Watt DC/DC converter from the TDR 3WISM series by Traco Power. It features an ultra-wide input voltage range of 18 to 75 VDC and provides tightly regulated dual outputs of +12 VDC and -12 VDC. This converter is designed for high efficiency and reliability, making it suitable for demanding applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The TDR 3-4822WISM is ideal for space-critical high-end applications in:
- Communication equipment
- Instrumentation
- Industrial electronics

Its robust design, including features like continuous short-circuit protection and a wide operating temperature range, ensures reliable performance in various environments. The remote On/Off control capability adds flexibility for integration into different systems.
