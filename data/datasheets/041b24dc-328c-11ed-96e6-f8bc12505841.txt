### Component Summary: TQP3M9037

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Product Code:** TQP3M9037  
**Type:** Ultra Low-Noise, High Linearity Low Noise Amplifier (LNA)  
**Package Type:** 8 Pin 2x2 mm DFN (Dual Flat No-lead)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): +3.0 V to +5.25 V (typical operation at +5 V)
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: +7 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Operating Current (IDD): 70 mA at +5 V
  - Power Down Current: 3-4 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Consumption: 350 mW (at 70 mA and +5 V)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Case Temperature: -40°C to +105°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low Noise Figure: 0.4 dB at 1.95 GHz
  - High Gain: 20 dB at 1.95 GHz
  - High Linearity: +35 dBm Output IP3
  - Integrated Active Bias and Shutdown Control
  - Unconditionally Stable
  - Integrated On-chip Matching (50 Ohm In/Out)

#### Description:
The TQP3M9037 is a high-performance low noise amplifier designed for applications requiring high linearity and low noise figure. It operates over a wide frequency range of 0.7 to 6.0 GHz, making it suitable for various wireless communication applications. The device is housed in a compact 2x2 mm surface-mount package and is compliant with RoHS standards.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Wireless Infrastructure:** Used in base stations and repeaters to amplify weak signals.
- **Mobile Infrastructure:** Enhances signal quality in mobile communication systems.
- **LTE/WCDMA/CDMA/GSM:** Supports various cellular communication standards.
- **General Purpose Wireless:** Applicable in a range of wireless communication devices.
- **TDD or FDD Systems:** Suitable for Time Division Duplexing and Frequency Division Duplexing systems.

This amplifier is particularly beneficial in scenarios where maintaining signal integrity and minimizing noise is critical, such as in high-frequency communication systems. The integrated features simplify design requirements, reducing the need for external components.
