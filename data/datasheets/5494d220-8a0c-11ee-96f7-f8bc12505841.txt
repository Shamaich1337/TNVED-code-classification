### Component Summary for TE Connectivity 2029150-5

**Component Type:**  
Rectangular Power Connector

**Description:**  
The TE Connectivity 2029150-5 is a 5-position rectangular power connector designed for wire-to-board applications. It features a header and plug configuration with a centerline (pitch) of 4.2 mm (0.165 in). This connector is suitable for mounting on printed circuit boards (PCBs) and is constructed from nylon material with a UL 94V-2 flammability rating.

  - Operating Voltage: 600 VAC

- **Current Ratings:**

  - Mating retention with latch

  - Keyed mating alignment for proper connection
  - Contact plating material: Tin

  - Not specified in the provided data.

### Typical Applications:
The 2029150-5 connector is primarily used in power applications where reliable connections are required between wires and printed circuit boards. Typical applications include:

- Power management systems
- Industrial equipment
- Consumer electronics
- Automotive applications
- Any application requiring robust wire-to-board connections

This connector is ideal for environments where durability and resistance to environmental factors are critical, given its wide operating temperature range and UL flammability rating.
