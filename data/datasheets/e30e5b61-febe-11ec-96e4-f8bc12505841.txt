### Component Summary: ATF-58143-TR1G

**Description:**
The ATF-58143 is a low noise enhancement mode pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor (HEMT) designed for high dynamic range applications. It is housed in a compact 4-lead SC-70 (SOT-343) surface mount plastic package. This device is particularly noted for its high gain, high linearity, and low noise characteristics, making it suitable for use as a low noise amplifier (LNA) in various wireless communication systems.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 5V (Absolute Maximum)
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): -5V to 1V (Absolute Maximum)
  - Gate-Drain Voltage (VGD): -5V to 1V (Absolute Maximum)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Drain Current (IDS): 100 mA (Absolute Maximum)
  - Gate-Source Current (IGS): 2 mA (Absolute Maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Pdiss): 500 mW (Absolute Maximum)
