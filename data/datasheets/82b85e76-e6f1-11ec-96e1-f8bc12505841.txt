### Component Summary: PI6C557-03LEX

**Manufacturer:** Pericom Semiconductor Corporation (now part of Diodes Incorporated)  
**Product Code:** PI6C557-03LEX  
**Document Number:** DS43154 Rev 1-2  
**Date:** October 2020  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: 3.0V to 3.6V (3.3V ±10%)
  - Maximum Supply Voltage to Ground: 5.5V
  - Input/Output Voltage Range: -0.5V to VDD + 0.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Current: 95 mA (typical)
  - Output Enable Current (OE = LOW): 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Supply Current: 95 mA (with RL = 50Ω, CL = 2pF)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature: 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 16-pin TSSOP (L)

- **Special Features:**
  - LVDS compatible outputs
  - HCSL outputs with 0.7V current mode differential pair
  - Jitter: 60 ps cycle-to-cycle (typical)
  - Spread spectrum clock generator with selectable spread options (±0.25%, -0.5%, -0.75%, and no spread)
  - Fully RoHS compliant and lead-free
  - Suitable for automotive applications with specific change control

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **PI6C557-03** is a spread spectrum clock generator designed to meet the requirements of PCI Express and Ethernet applications. It is utilized in PC and embedded systems to significantly reduce electromagnetic interference (EMI). The device accepts a 25MHz crystal input and generates two pairs of differential outputs (HCSL) at various clock frequencies (25MHz, 100MHz, 125MHz, and 200MHz) using patented Phase-Locked Loop (PLL) techniques.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** The device helps in managing power consumption by reducing EMI, which is crucial in high-speed digital applications.
- **Signal Processing:** It is used in systems requiring precise clock signals, such as PCI Express and Ethernet interfaces.
- **Data Communication:** The PI6C557-03 is suitable for applications that require reliable clock generation for data transmission, ensuring signal integrity and compliance with communication standards.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where EMI reduction is critical, such as in automotive and industrial applications, and is compliant with stringent environmental regulations.
