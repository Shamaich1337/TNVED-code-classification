  - Output voltage trimming
  - Short circuit protection

It is part of the TEN 60 series from Traco Power, which is known for its compact design and high efficiency, making it suitable for various applications in industrial and communication electronics.

**Typical Applications:**
- Battery-operated equipment
- Distributed power architectures
- Communication devices
- Industrial electronics
- Any application requiring reliable voltage conversion with minimal space requirements

This converter is particularly useful in scenarios where efficiency and thermal management are critical, given its high efficiency rating and wide operating temperature range. The remote On/Off feature also allows for flexible control in power management applications.
