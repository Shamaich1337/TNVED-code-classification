### Component Summary: ERA-50SM+

**Description:**
The ERA-50SM+ is a surface mount monolithic amplifier designed for wideband applications. It utilizes InGaP HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) technology and is configured in a Darlington arrangement. This amplifier is characterized by its high dynamic range and stable performance across various temperatures. It is housed in a Micro-X package and is RoHS compliant.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 4.2 V to 4.6 V (typical 4.4 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 60 mA (maximum 85 mA)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 451 mW
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Micro-X package (Case Style: WW107)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - DC-2 GHz frequency range
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - Unconditionally stable
  - Low performance variation over temperature
  - Transient protected
  - Aqueous washable
  - Protected by US Patent 6,943,629
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020C

**Typical Applications:**
The ERA-50SM+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular/PCS/3G base stations
- CATV (Cable Television), cable modems, and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- Fixed wireless and WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network)
- Microwave radio and test equipment

This amplifier is ideal for use in systems requiring reliable amplification over a wide frequency range, making it a versatile choice for modern communication technologies.
