### Component Summary: STPS5L60S

**Description:**
The STPS5L60S is a power Schottky rectifier designed for use in switch mode power supplies and high-frequency DC to DC converters. It is available in various package types including DO-201AD, SMB, SMC, and SMB Flat Notch. This component is particularly suited for applications requiring low voltage and high frequency, making it ideal for small battery chargers and telecom battery chargers where space is a constraint.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 5 A
  - Forward RMS Current (IF(RMS)): 15 A
  - Surge Non-Repetitive Forward Current (IFSM): 100 A (SMB, DO-201AD, SMC) for 10 ms
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 0.42 V (typical at 5 A)
  - Average Forward Power Dissipation: Calculated using P = 0.39 x IF(AV) + 0.028 x IF²(RMS)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Operating Junction Temperature (Tj(max)): +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in DO-201AD, SMB, SMC, and SMB Flat Notch packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Negligible switching losses
  - Low thermal resistance
  - Avalanche rated
  - ECOPACK2 compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
- Lighting systems
- Battery chargers
- Set-top boxes
- DC/DC converters
- Notebook adapters
- Switching diodes

This component is particularly useful in applications where efficiency and thermal management are critical, such as in power management systems and compact electronic devices. Its low forward voltage drop and negligible switching losses contribute to improved energy efficiency in various electronic circuits.
