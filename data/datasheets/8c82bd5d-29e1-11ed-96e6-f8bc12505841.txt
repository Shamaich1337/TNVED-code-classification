### Component Summary: TPS22860DBVR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Bias Voltage Range (VBIAS): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Voltage Range (VIN): 0 V to VBIAS
  - Output Voltage Range (VOUT): 0 V to VBIAS

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Continuous Switch Current: 200 mA
  - Maximum Pulsed Switch Current: 400 mA (pulse < 300 µs, 2% duty cycle)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQ,VBIAS): 10 µA to 100 µA (IOUT = 0, VIN = VON = VBIAS = 3.3 V)
  - Shutdown Current: 10 nA to 100 nA (VON = 0 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Free-Air Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Maximum Junction Temperature: 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 6-pin SOT-23 and SC70 packages
  - SOT-23 dimensions: 2.80 x 2.90 mm
  - SC70 dimensions: 2.10 x 2.00 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low leakage current: VIN leakage current = 2 nA, VBIAS leakage current at 5.5 V = 10 nA
  - ON-resistance (RON) varies with input voltage:
    - RON = 0.73 Ω at VIN = 5 V (VBIAS = 5 V)
    - RON = 0.68 Ω at VIN = 3.3 V (VBIAS = 5 V)
    - RON = 0.63 Ω at VIN = 1.8 V (VBIAS = 5 V)
  - ESD performance: Tested per JESD 22, with ratings of 2 kV HBM and 1 kV CDM

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
