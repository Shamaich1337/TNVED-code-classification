### Component Summary: QPQ1270TR7

**Component Type:** Band 7 BAW Duplexer  
**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Product Code:** QPQ1270TR7  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not specified (typically RF components do not have voltage ratings in the same way as other components).
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified (current ratings are generally not applicable for duplexers).
- **Power Consumption:** High Power Rating: 30 dBm on Downlink (DL) or Uplink (UL) at +95 ºC.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 ºC to +105 ºC (Guaranteed specifications); Storage Temperature: -40 ºC to +125 ºC.
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount Package (SMP), dimensions: 2.00 mm x 2.50 mm x 1.015 mm.
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss and high out-of-band attenuation.
  - No external matching required.
  - RoHS compliant, Pb-free.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 3 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020.

#### Description:
The QPQ1270 is a high-performance Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) duplexer specifically designed for Band 7 uplink and downlink applications. It is engineered to provide low insertion loss and high rejection, making it suitable for small cell applications. The compact size and RoHS compliance make it ideal for modern wireless communication systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Base Station Infrastructure:** Used in cellular base stations to manage uplink and downlink signals efficiently.
- **Small Cells:** Enhances signal quality and coverage in dense urban environments.
- **Repeaters:** Used in signal amplification systems to extend coverage areas.
- **LTE Dongles:** Integrated into devices that provide LTE connectivity.
- **General Purpose Wireless:** Applicable in various wireless communication systems requiring efficient signal processing.

This duplexer is part of Qorvo's extensive portfolio of RF BAW and SAW filters, emphasizing its role in modern telecommunications and wireless technology.
