### QPC3025 Technical Summary

**Component Overview:**
The QPC3025 is a high-power Silicon on Insulator (SOI) single-pole, double-throw (SPDT) switch designed for high-performance communication systems, particularly in 4G/5G wireless infrastructure applications. It features a symmetric switch structure that provides low insertion loss and excellent linearity, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): +2.7 V to +5.5 V (typical +5 V)
  - Control Voltage (VCTRL): -0.3 V to +6 V
  - Negative Supply Voltage (VSS): -5.5 V to -3.15 V (when internal NVG is disabled)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD) at VDD = +5 V: 90 µA to 200 µA
  - Control Current (ICRTL): 2 µA
  - Negative Supply Current (ISS): -100 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Continuous Wave (CW) Power Handling: 20 W
  - Pulsed Power Handling: 30 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Temperature (TCASE): -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -50 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 32 Pin 5 mm x 5 mm leadless surface-mount package (RoHS compliant)

- **Special Features:**
  - 30 MHz to 4200 MHz operational frequency range
  - 0.35 dB insertion loss at 2 GHz
  - 39 dB high isolation at 2 GHz
  - No blocking capacitors required on RF ports
  - +1.8 V logic compatible
  - Reflective switch ports

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020

**Description:**
The QPC3025 is a high-performance RF switch that allows for the routing of signals between different paths in communication systems. Its design minimizes signal loss and maximizes isolation, making it ideal for applications where signal integrity is critical.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Wireless Infrastructure:** Used in repeaters and boosters for enhancing signal strength and quality in cellular networks.
- **Test and Measurement Equipment:** Employed in RF testing setups where precise signal routing is necessary.
- **Communication Systems:** Suitable for various high-performance communication applications, including 4G and 5G networks.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where high power handling and low insertion loss are essential, ensuring reliable performance in demanding RF applications.
