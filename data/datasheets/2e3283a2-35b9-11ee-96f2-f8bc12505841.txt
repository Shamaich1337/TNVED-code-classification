### Component Summary: BUK6D125-60E

**General Description:**
The BUK6D125-60E is a 60V N-channel enhancement mode Trench MOSFET designed for medium power applications. It is housed in a DFN2020MD-6 (SOT1220) surface-mounted device (SMD) package, which is plastic and leadless, providing a compact footprint suitable for various electronic applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 60 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): -20 V to +20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at VGS = 10 V, Tsp = 25 °C: 7.4 A
  - Peak Drain Current (IDM): 30 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 10 µs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot) at Tsp = 25 °C: 15 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +175 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to +175 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DFN2020MD-6 (SOT1220), dimensions: 2 mm x 2 mm x 0.65 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Extended temperature range (Tj = 175 °C)
  - Side wettable flanks for optical solder inspection
  - ESD protection > 2 kV (HBM, class H2)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Description:**
The BUK6D125-60E is an N-channel MOSFET that utilizes Trench technology, which allows for lower on-state resistance and improved efficiency. It is designed to switch and amplify electronic signals in various applications, making it suitable for high-speed switching and power management tasks.

**Typical Applications:**
- **LED Lighting:** Used for driving LED circuits efficiently.
- **High-Speed Line Driver:** Suitable for applications requiring fast signal transmission.
- **Low-Side Load Switch:** Acts as a switch to control power to loads.
- **Switching Circuits:** Ideal for various switching applications in power management systems.

This MOSFET is particularly beneficial in automotive and industrial applications due to its robustness and high-temperature operation capabilities.
