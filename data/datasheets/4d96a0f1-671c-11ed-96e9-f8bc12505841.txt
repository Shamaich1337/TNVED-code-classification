### Component Summary: VS-10CTQ150-M3

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Type:** Schottky Rectifier  
**Description:** The VS-10CTQ150-M3 is a high-performance Schottky rectifier designed for low reverse leakage at high temperatures. It features a center tap configuration and is optimized for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Reverse Voltage (VR): 150 V
  - Maximum Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 150 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 10 A (2 x 5 A per leg)
  - Maximum Surge Current (IFSM): 620 A (tp = 5 μs sine)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 5 Apk, TJ = 125 °C: 0.73 V
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM max): 7 mA at 125 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Package: 3L TO-220AB

- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - High frequency operation
  - High purity, high-temperature epoxy encapsulation for enhanced mechanical strength and moisture resistance
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability
  - Designed and qualified according to JEDEC®-JESD 47

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Typical Applications:
The VS-10CTQ150-M3 is commonly used in:
- Switching power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Freewheeling diodes
- Reverse battery protection circuits

This component is ideal for applications that require efficient rectification with minimal power loss, particularly in high-temperature environments. Its robust design and high surge current capability make it suitable for demanding electronic applications.
