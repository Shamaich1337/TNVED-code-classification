The NL27WZ07DFT2G is a high-performance dual buffer with open-drain outputs designed for operation within a voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V. It is part of the ON Semiconductor (onsemi) product line and is suitable for various applications requiring signal buffering and level shifting.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 1.65V to 5.5V
  - Maximum DC Supply Voltage: 
    - SC−88 (NLV): -0.5V to +7.0V
    - SC−74, UDFN6: -0.5V to +6.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Sink Current: 24 mA at 3.0V
  - Output Source/Sink Current: ±50 mA
  - Input Diode Current: -50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 
    - 1.0 mA (max) at VCC = 5.5V
    - 10 mA (max) at VCC = 3.0V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SC−88, SC−74, and UDFN6 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Inputs/Outputs are overvoltage tolerant up to 5.5V.
  - Supports partial power-down protection (IOFF).
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.
  - AEC−Q100 qualified for automotive applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The NL27WZ07DFT2G is commonly used in applications such as:
- **Signal buffering:** It can be used to buffer signals in digital circuits, ensuring signal integrity and reducing loading effects on previous stages.
- **Level shifting:** It is suitable for interfacing between different voltage levels in mixed-voltage systems.
- **Data communication:** The device can be utilized in communication protocols where signal integrity is critical.
- **Automotive applications:** Due to its AEC-Q100 qualification, it is suitable for automotive electronics where reliability and performance are essential.

This component is ideal for designers looking for a reliable buffer solution in low-voltage applications, particularly in automotive and consumer electronics.
