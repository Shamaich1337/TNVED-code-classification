### Component Summary: KPFA-3010RGBC-11

**Description:**
The KPFA-3010RGBC-11 is a right-angle surface mount device (SMD) LED lamp produced by Kingbright. It features three different emitting colors: Hyper Red (AlGaInP), Green (InGaN), and Blue (InGaN). This LED is designed for low power consumption and offers a wide viewing angle, making it suitable for various applications such as backlighting and status indicators in consumer electronics.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 20mA:
    - Hyper Red: 2.0V (typ), 2.5V (max)
    - Green: 3.3V (typ), 4.1V (max)
    - Blue: 3.3V (typ), 4.0V (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF):
    - Hyper Red: 30mA
    - Green: 25mA
    - Blue: 30mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM):
    - Hyper Red: 195mA
    - Green: 150mA
    - Blue: 150mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD):
    - Hyper Red: 75mW
    - Green: 102.5mW
    - Blue: 120mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 3.0 x 1.0 mm right-angle SMD LED

- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (150°)
  - Tinned pads for improved solderability
  - RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The KPFA-3010RGBC-11 is commonly used in:
- Backlighting for displays
- Status indicators in home appliances and smart devices
- Wearable and portable electronic devices
- Healthcare applications

**Notes:**
- The luminous intensity for each color is specified at 20mA:
  - Hyper Red: 80-140 mcd
  - Green: 300-500 mcd
  - Blue: 40-70 mcd
- The device is sensitive to electrostatic discharge; proper handling precautions should be observed.
- The specifications are subject to change, and users should refer to the latest datasheet for the most accurate information.
