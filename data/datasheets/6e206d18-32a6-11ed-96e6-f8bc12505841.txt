**Description:**
The XZMDKVG54W-4 is a 2.0 x 1.25mm Surface Mount Device (SMD) Chip LED Lamp produced by SunLED. It is designed for use as an indication light in handheld products, providing visual feedback through red and green light emissions. The LED is characterized by its long life and robust packaging, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (Typical at I_F=20mA):
    - Red: 1.95V
    - Green: 2.1V
  - Forward Voltage (Maximum at I_F=20mA):
    - Red & Green: 2.5V
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 30mA
  - Forward Current (Peak, 1/10 Duty Cycle, 0.1ms Pulse Width):
    - Red: 185mA
    - Green: 150mA
  - Reverse Current (Max at V_R=5V): 10µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 75mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_A): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMD Chip LED Lamp (2.0 x 1.25mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 3
  - Halogen-free
  - RoHS compliant
  - Long life and robust package

**Typical Applications:**
The XZMDKVG54W-4 is primarily used in handheld electronic devices for indication purposes. Its compact size and efficient light output make it ideal for applications such as:

- Indicator lights in consumer electronics (e.g., mobile phones, remote controls)
- Status indicators in appliances and machinery
- Decorative lighting in various electronic products
- General signaling applications where visual feedback is required

This LED lamp is suitable for reflow soldering, and it is recommended to follow specific soldering profiles to ensure optimal performance. The device is designed for use in environments where moisture sensitivity is a concern, and precautions should be taken to handle it properly to avoid electrostatic discharge (ESD) damage.
