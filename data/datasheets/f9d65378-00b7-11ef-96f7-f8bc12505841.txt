### Component Summary: SD107WS-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components (MCC)  
**Product Code:** SD107WS-TP  
**Type:** Schottky Barrier Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Maximum Ratings:**
  - **Maximum Recurrent Peak Reverse Voltage (VR):** 30V
  - **Maximum RMS Voltage:** 21V
  - **Maximum DC Blocking Voltage:** 30V
  - **Maximum DC Reverse Current (IR) at Rated DC Blocking Voltage:** 1000nA
  - **Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM):** 0.75A
  - **Average Forward Current (IF(AV)):** 0.1A
  - **Power Dissipation (Pd):** 250mW

- **Electrical Characteristics @ 25°C:**
  - **Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF):** 
    - 550mV (Max) at IF = 50mA
  - **Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR):** 30V at IR = 100μA
  - **Maximum Junction Capacitance (CJ):** 7.0pF (measured at 1.0MHz, VR = 10.0V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Operating Junction Temperature:** -55°C to +150°C
  - **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C

- **Thermal Resistance:** 500°C/W (Junction to Ambient)

- **Package Type:** SOD-323 (Microminiature Plastic Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low turn-on voltage
  - Fast switching capability
  - Protected by a PN junction guard ring against excessive voltage (e.g., ESD)
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SD107WS-TP is a Schottky barrier diode designed for applications requiring low forward voltage drop and fast switching speeds. Schottky diodes are known for their efficiency in rectification and are commonly used in power management applications due to their low conduction losses.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for rectification and voltage clamping.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for RF applications due to low capacitance.
- **Data Storage:** Utilized in circuits for data protection and signal integrity.
- **General Purpose Switching:** Suitable for various electronic circuits where fast switching and low voltage drop are critical.

This diode is particularly advantageous in battery-powered devices and other applications where efficiency and thermal performance are paramount.
