**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 18 - 75 VDC (Nominal: 48 VDC)
  - Output Voltage: 5 VDC (400 mA), 12 VDC (167 mA), 15 VDC (134 mA)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 400 mA for 5 VDC, 167 mA for 12 VDC, 134 mA for 15 VDC

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 2 Watts

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C (no derating)

- **Package Type:**
  - THD (Through-Hole Device)

- **Special Features:**
  - Fully regulated outputs
  - Low ripple and noise (30 mVp-p typical)
  - I/O isolation of 1600 VDC
  - Continuous short-circuit protection
  - Remote On/Off control
  - Fully RoHS compliant
  - 3-year product warranty

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Description:**
The TDR 2-4811WI is a compact 2 Watt DC/DC converter designed for high efficiency and reliability in various applications. It features an ultra-wide 4:1 input voltage range, allowing it to operate effectively in diverse environments. The converter provides tightly regulated output voltages, ensuring stable performance even under varying load conditions. Its robust design includes features such as continuous short-circuit protection and a remote On/Off control, making it suitable for demanding applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The TDR 2-4811WI is ideal for space-critical high-end applications in:
- Communication equipment
- Instrumentation
- Industrial electronics

Its ability to function in extreme temperatures without derating and its excellent immunity to environmental influences make it a reliable choice for power management in various electronic systems.
