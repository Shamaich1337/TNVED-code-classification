### Component Summary: SHB018280-V-N-B300

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 9-36 VDC
  - Output Voltage: 28 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: 10.71 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power: 300 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -60 to 125 °C
  - Operating Case Temperature: -45 to 105 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Half Brick (DOSA pinout)
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency (typical 87%)
  - Built-in 2250 VDC isolation
  - Over Voltage Protection (OVP), Over Current Protection (OCP), Under Voltage Lockout (UVLO), and Over Temperature Protection (OTP)
  - Remote ON/OFF control
  - No life-span constraints
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The SHB018280-V-N-B300 is a high-performance DC-DC converter from the Supreme series, designed as a half-brick module. It utilizes isolated, board-mountable, fixed switching frequency technology with synchronous rectification to achieve high power conversion efficiency. The module is enclosed in a six-sided metal case, providing robust protection against harsh environmental conditions, making it suitable for various industrial and transportation applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Railway and Military:** Used in power management systems where reliability and efficiency are critical.
- **Automation and Semiconductor Networks:** Ideal for powering control systems and semiconductor fabrication equipment.
- **Telecommunications:** Provides stable power for network equipment.
- **Medical Devices:** Ensures reliable power supply for sensitive medical equipment.
- **Automobile and Aviation:** Used in onboard power systems and chargers, ensuring efficient power conversion in vehicles and aircraft.

This component is particularly well-suited for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability in demanding environments, making it a versatile choice for various industrial and commercial power management needs.
