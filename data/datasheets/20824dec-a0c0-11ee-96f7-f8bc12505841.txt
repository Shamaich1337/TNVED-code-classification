### Component Summary: ESD36VD3B

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** ESD36VD3B  
**Type:** Bi-directional Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Stand-off Voltage (VRWM):** ±36V Max
- **Peak Pulse Power (Ppk):** 380 W (t_p = 8/20μs)
- **Peak Pulse Current (IPP):** 5 A (t_p = 8/20μs)
- **Reverse Leakage Current (IR):** 0.5 μA at VRWM = 36V
- **Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR):** 38V (min), 47V (max) at IBR = 1mA
- **Clamping Voltage (VCL):** 
  - 52V (typ) at IPP = 1A
  - 70V (typ) at IPP = 5A
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 150°C
- **Junction Temperature (TJ):** 125°C Max
- **Storage Temperature (TSTG):** -55°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:** SOD-323
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 per J-STD-020E
- **Special Features:** 
  - Low leakage current
  - Ultra low clamping voltage
  - RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The ESD36VD3B is a bi-directional transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge (ESD) and other transient events. It is particularly effective in safeguarding devices against high-voltage surges, ensuring reliable operation in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Handsets and Accessories:** Protects mobile devices from ESD damage.
- **Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs):** Ensures the longevity and reliability of handheld devices.
- **Notebooks and Handhelds:** Provides transient protection for portable computing devices.
- **Portable Instrumentation:** Safeguards measurement and diagnostic equipment.
- **Peripherals:** Protects external devices connected to computers and other systems.
- **Pagers:** Ensures reliable operation of communication devices.
- **Desktop and Servers:** Protects critical components in computing environments from voltage spikes.

This component is essential in applications where electronic devices are exposed to potential ESD events, ensuring their durability and functionality in everyday use.
