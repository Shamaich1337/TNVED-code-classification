### Component Summary: SMAJ11A-13-F

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** SMAJ11A-13-F  
**Document Number:** DS19005 Rev. 19 - 2

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 11V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 12.2V (min), 13.5V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1.0 µA at VRWM
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 22.0 A
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPK): 400 W
  - Steady State Power Dissipation (PM(AV)): 1.0 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMA (Surface Mount)
  - Weight: Approximately 0.064 grams
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Glass Passivated Die Construction
  - Unidirectional and Bidirectional Versions Available
  - Excellent Clamping Capability
  - Fast Response Time
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free (Green Device)
  
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The SMAJ11A-13-F is a surface mount transient voltage suppressor (TVS) designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and transients. It is capable of handling high peak pulse power dissipation, making it suitable for applications where overvoltage protection is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Protecting power supply circuits from voltage transients.
- **Signal Processing:** Safeguarding signal lines in communication devices.
- **Automotive Applications:** Suitable for automotive electronics requiring robust transient protection.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other portable electronics to prevent damage from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and other voltage spikes.

This component is particularly useful in environments where electrical noise and transients are common, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic systems.
