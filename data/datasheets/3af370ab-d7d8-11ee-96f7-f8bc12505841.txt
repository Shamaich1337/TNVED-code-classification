### Component Summary: TPD1E6B06DPLR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VRWM): ±5V
  - Breakdown Voltage: ±6V (Minimum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 3.8A (8/20 µs)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 50W (8/20 µs)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - X2SON (0201) package (0.6 mm x 0.3 mm)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - IEC61000-4-2 Level 4 ESD Protection (±15-kV Contact and Air-Gap Discharge)
  - Low ESD Clamping Voltage
  - Low Leakage Current: 10nA (Typical)
  - I/O Capacitance: 6pF (Typical)
  - Dynamic Resistance (RDYN): 0.55Ω (Typical)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (260°C, Unlim)

#### Description:
The **TPD1E6B06** is a single-channel Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed for electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection. It is housed in a compact 0201 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. The device is capable of dissipating ESD strikes above the maximum levels specified in the IEC 61000-4-2 international standard, providing robust protection for sensitive electronic components.

#### Typical Applications:
The TPD1E6B06 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Mobile Phones:** Protecting audio lines and other sensitive interfaces from ESD.
- **Tablets and Wearables:** Ensuring reliable operation in compact devices.
- **Remote Controllers and Electronic Point of Sale (EPOS) Systems:** Safeguarding user interfaces and communication lines.
- **General-Purpose Input/Output (GPIO) Interfaces:** Providing protection for bidirectional signals.
- **Audio Interfaces:** Protecting microphone, earphone, and speakerphone connections.

This device is particularly effective in applications where high data rates (up to 800 Mbps) are required, and it is essential to maintain signal integrity while providing ESD protection.
