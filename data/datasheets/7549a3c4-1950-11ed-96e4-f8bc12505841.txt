  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The **XCR3256XL-12TQ144I** is a low-power, 3.3V, 256 macrocell Complex Programmable Logic Device (CPLD) from Xilinx's CoolRunner™ XPLA3 family. It is designed for applications that require programmable logic solutions with a focus on power efficiency. The device features a total of 16 function blocks, allowing for high-density logic implementation with fast pin-to-pin propagation delays of 12 ns and a maximum system frequency of 88 MHz.

- **Embedded Systems**: Commonly found in embedded applications where flexibility and low power are critical.
