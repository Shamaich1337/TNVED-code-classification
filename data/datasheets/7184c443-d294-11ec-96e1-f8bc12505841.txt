### Component Summary: UJ271M400O250AP6 (CapXon Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor)

- **Capacitance**: 270 µF
- **Voltage Rating**: 400 Vdc
- **Surge Voltage**: 440 Vdc
- **Capacitance Tolerance**: ±20% (@120Hz, +20°C)
- **Ripple Current (max.)**: 1.3 A (@120Hz, +105°C)
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ)**: 15% (@120Hz, +20°C)
- **Leakage Current (max.)**: 986 µA (@20°C)
- **Equivalent Series Resistance (ESR)**: 740 mΩ (@120Hz, +20°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40 to +105 °C
- **Endurance**: 2000 hours at 105 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically aluminum electrolytic capacitors are classified under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The UJ271M400O250AP6 is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor designed for various electronic applications. It features a capacitance of 270 µF and a voltage rating of 400 Vdc, making it suitable for high-voltage applications. The capacitor is characterized by its ability to handle ripple currents up to 1.3 A and operates effectively within a wide temperature range of -40 to +105 °C. 

#### Typical Applications:
This capacitor is commonly used in:
- **Power Supply Circuits**: For smoothing and filtering applications in power supplies, ensuring stable voltage levels.
- **Energy Storage**: In applications requiring temporary energy storage, such as in power backup systems.
- **Signal Coupling and Decoupling**: In audio and RF circuits to block DC while allowing AC signals to pass.
- **Motor Drives**: In industrial applications where capacitors are used to improve the performance of electric motors.

The UJ series capacitors are known for their reliability and long endurance, making them suitable for demanding environments in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial machinery.
