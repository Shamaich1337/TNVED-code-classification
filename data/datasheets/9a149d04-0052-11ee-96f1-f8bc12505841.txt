- **Package Type:** Through-Hole (THT)

- **Pitch:** 2.5 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **AEC-Q200 Qualified:** No

The GF101M025E110A is a low impedance aluminum electrolytic capacitor designed for through-hole mounting. It is characterized by its ability to handle high ripple currents, making it suitable for applications that require stable performance under varying load conditions. The capacitor operates effectively within a wide temperature range, ensuring reliability in diverse environments.

- **Power Management:** Filtering and smoothing in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Coupling and decoupling applications in audio and RF circuits.

- **Industrial Equipment:** Applications that demand robust performance under high temperatures and varying electrical loads.
