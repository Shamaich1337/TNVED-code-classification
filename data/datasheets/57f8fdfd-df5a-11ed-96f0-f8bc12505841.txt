#### Description:
The SMEV-1550-B-06-L-10-SU is a MEMS-based Variable Optical Attenuator (VOA) specifically designed for use in fiber optic communication systems. This allows for precise control of optical power levels, which is crucial in various applications where signal strength needs to be managed.

- **Fiber Communication:** Used in Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) systems to manage signal levels.
- **EDFA Power Protection:** Protects Erbium-Doped Fiber Amplifiers (EDFA) from excessive optical power.
