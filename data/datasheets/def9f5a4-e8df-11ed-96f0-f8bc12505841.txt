### Component Summary: LM95245CIMM/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Supply Voltage (VDD) range from 3.0V to 3.6V.
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current at 1Hz conversion rate: 350 µA (typical).
  - Quiescent Current in Standby Mode: 300 µA.
- **Power Consumption**: Maximum power consumption is approximately 1.2 mW at 3.6V.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +125°C.
- **Package Type**: Available in VSSOP (DGK) and SOIC (D) packages.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E).
- **Special Features**: 
  - 11-bit digital temperature sensor with TruTherm® BJT Beta Compensation Technology.
  - Supports SMBus 2.0 interface.
  - Programmable T_CRIT and OS thresholds.
  - Diode fault detection and programmable shared hysteresis register.
  - Digital filter for remote channel temperature readings.

#### Description:
The **LM95245** is a precision remote diode digital temperature sensor designed for monitoring the temperature of external devices, such as microprocessors, using a 2-wire System Management Bus (SMBus) interface. It features TruTherm technology, which allows for accurate temperature measurements by compensating for variations in the beta of the bipolar junction transistor (BJT) used in the remote diode.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Processor/Computer System Thermal Management**: The LM95245 is particularly suited for laptops, desktops, workstations, and servers, where it can monitor the temperature of the processor's thermal diode.
- **Electronic Test Equipment**: Used in various testing setups to ensure components operate within safe temperature limits.
- **Office Electronics**: Can be integrated into office equipment to monitor and manage thermal conditions.

The LM95245 is optimized for use with Intel processors fabricated on 45nm, 65nm, and 90nm processes, making it an ideal choice for modern computing applications where precise thermal management is critical.
