#### Description:
The component 011L1135 is a **solder fitting** designed for use with Danfoss thermostatic expansion valves (TE2 and T2 series). It serves as a connection point for refrigerant lines in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. The fitting is specifically designed for 3/8” (10 mm) copper tubing and is used to facilitate the installation of the expansion valve in a cooling system.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Refrigeration Systems**: Used in commercial and industrial refrigeration systems to control the flow of refrigerant into the evaporator based on the temperature of the refrigerant vapor.
- **Air Conditioning Systems**: Employed in HVAC systems to regulate refrigerant flow, ensuring efficient cooling and temperature control.
- **Heat Pumps**: Utilized in heat pump systems for both heating and cooling applications, allowing for precise control of refrigerant flow based on thermal load.

### Additional Information:
The thermostatic expansion valves (TE2 and T2) are designed to automatically regulate the flow of refrigerant based on the superheat of the vapor exiting the evaporator. They are particularly suitable for "dry" evaporators where the superheat is proportional to the thermal load. The valves can handle a nominal capacity range from 0.5 to 15.5 kW for refrigerant R22, and they are equipped with features that protect the compressor motor from excessively high boiling pressures. 

The 011L1135 fitting is essential for ensuring a secure and leak-free connection in these systems, contributing to the overall efficiency and reliability of refrigeration and air conditioning applications.
