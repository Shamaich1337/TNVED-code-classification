### Component Summary: XAL4020-102MEC

**Component Type:** Shielded Power Inductor

**Description:**
The XAL4020-102MEC is a shielded power inductor designed for applications requiring high current handling and low DC resistance (DCR). It is part of the XAL40 series from Coilcraft, which is known for its compact size and efficient performance in power management applications.

### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance:** 1.0 µH (±20%)
- **DCR (Max):** 13.25 mΩ
- **Self-Resonant Frequency (SRF):** 14.60 MHz
- **Saturation Current (Isat):** 8.7 A
- **RMS Current (Irms):**
  - 20°C rise: 6.7 A
  - 40°C rise: 9.6 A
- **Operating Voltage:** 0 – 60 V
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +125°C (with 40°C rise)
- **Maximum Part Temperature:** +165°C (ambient + temperature rise)
- **Storage Temperature:** -55°C to +165°C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (unlimited floor life at <30°C / 85% relative humidity)
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Tape and reel packaging available)

### Special Features:
- **AEC-Q200 Grade 1:** Suitable for automotive applications.
- **Halogen Free:** Compliant with environmental regulations.
- **Soft Saturation:** Ideal for Voltage Regulator Modules (VRM) and Voltage Regulator Down (VRD) applications.

### Typical Applications:
The XAL4020-102MEC is commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** Efficiently managing power in various electronic devices.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Providing inductance in switching power supplies.
- **Voltage Regulation:** Supporting VRM and VRD applications in computing and automotive electronics.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices requiring compact and efficient power inductors.

This inductor is particularly suited for applications where space is limited, and high performance is required, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
