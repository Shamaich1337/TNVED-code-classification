### Component Summary: PSU04N65B

**Description:**
The PSU04N65B is a 650V N-channel planar MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for high-voltage applications. Its primary function is to act as a switch or amplifier in electronic circuits, allowing for efficient control of electrical power.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDSS): 650V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±30V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 4.0A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 16A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 86W
  - Derating Factor above 25°C: 0.68W/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in TO-251 and TO-252 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - RDS(ON) (typical): 1.9Ω at VGS=10V
  - Low gate charge to minimize switching loss.
  - Fast recovery body diode.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

**Typical Applications:**
The PSU04N65B is commonly used in:
- Adapters
- Chargers
- Switched-Mode Power Supplies (SMPS) for standby power applications

This MOSFET is particularly suitable for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability in power management, making it ideal for use in consumer electronics, industrial power supplies, and renewable energy systems.
