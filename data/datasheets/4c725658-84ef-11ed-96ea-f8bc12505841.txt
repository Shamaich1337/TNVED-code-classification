### Component Summary: BC846A.215 (Nexperia)

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 65 V (max)
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 80 V (max)
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 6 V (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): 100 mA (max)
  - Peak Collector Current (I_CM): 200 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)
  - Peak Base Current (I_BM): 200 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 250 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB) - Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - General-purpose NPN transistor with two different gain selections.
  - Available in a small SMD plastic package.
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT23 packages are classified under MSL 1 or MSL 2.

#### Description:
The BC846A is an NPN general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is part of the BC846x series, which is characterized by its high voltage rating of up to 65 V and a maximum collector current of 100 mA. The BC846A variant specifically offers a DC current gain (hFE) range of 180 to 220, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General-Purpose Switching:** The BC846A can be used in various switching applications where moderate current and voltage handling is required.
- **Amplification:** It is suitable for signal amplification in audio and RF applications.
- **Signal Processing:** The transistor can be utilized in circuits that require signal modulation or processing.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as amplifiers, oscillators, and other electronic circuits where small signal amplification is needed.
