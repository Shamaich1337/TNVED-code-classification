### Component Summary: LMZ12003TZE-ADJ/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 4.5V to 20V
  - Output Voltage Range: 0.8V to 6V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 3A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Total Power Output: 18W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature Range: -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - TO-PMOD (NDW) package, 7 pins
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated shielded inductor
  - Fast transient response
  - Low output voltage ripple
  - Protection features including thermal shutdown, input undervoltage lockout, output overvoltage protection, and short circuit protection
  - Adjustable output voltage with external resistors
  - Soft-start capability
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 3, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **LMZ12003** is a **step-down DC-DC power module** designed for point-of-load applications. It is capable of delivering up to 3A of output current with high efficiency and low output voltage ripple. The module integrates a shielded inductor, which simplifies the design and reduces the footprint on the PCB. The device features a constant ON-time control scheme, allowing for fast transient response and excellent line and load regulation.

#### Typical Applications:
The LMZ12003 is typically used in:
- **Point-of-load conversions** from 5V and 12V input rails
- **Power management** in space-constrained applications
- **Time-critical projects** where quick response times are essential
- Applications requiring **protection against inrush currents** and faults
- **Negative output voltage applications** (as referenced in AN-2027)

This module is particularly suitable for powering FPGAs, ASICs, and other digital circuits that require stable and efficient power supply solutions. Its robust design and integrated features make it ideal for a variety of electronic applications where space and efficiency are critical.
