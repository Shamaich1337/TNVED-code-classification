### Component Summary: XC95288XL-7FG256I

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply voltage for internal logic (VCCINT): 3.0V to 3.6V
  - Supply voltage for output drivers (VCCIO): 2.5V (2.3V to 2.7V) or 3.3V (3.0V to 3.6V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating supply current (ICC) in low power mode: 85 mA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power dissipation varies based on system frequency and design application; low power mode available to reduce consumption.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to +70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 256-ball Plastic Fineline Ball Grid Array (FG256)

- **Special Features:**
  - 6 ns pin-to-pin logic delays
  - In-system programmable
  - 288 macrocells with 6,400 usable gates
  - 5V tolerant I/O pins
  - Enhanced data security features
  - ESD protection exceeding 2,000V
  - Endurance exceeding 10,000 program/erase cycles
  - 20-year data retention

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **XC95288XL-7FG256I** is a high-performance Complex Programmable Logic Device (CPLD) designed for low-voltage applications in advanced communication and computing systems. It features a high degree of programmability and flexibility, allowing for the implementation of complex logic functions with fast propagation delays of 7.5 ns. The device is optimized for 3.3V systems but can tolerate 5V signals, making it versatile for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Communications:** Used in high-speed data processing and routing applications.
- **Computing Systems:** Ideal for implementing custom logic functions in computing hardware.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for applications requiring fast logic operations and data manipulation.
- **Control Systems:** Can be utilized in embedded systems for control and automation tasks.
- **Data Security:** Enhanced features make it suitable for applications requiring secure data handling.

This component is particularly beneficial in environments where space is limited due to its small footprint and high integration capabilities, making it a popular choice for modern electronic designs.
