### Component Summary: MMSZ5V6T1G

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 5.6 V (Nominal)
  - Reverse Voltage Range: 2.4 V to 56 V (various models in the series)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Reverse Current (IZT): 40 mA (at VZ)
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1 µA (at VR)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation: 500 mW (on FR-5 board at TL = 75 °C)
  - Derated above 75 °C: 6.7 mW/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature Range: -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-123 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - ESD Rating: Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified and PPAP Capable
  - Pb-Free Packages Available
  - Designed for optimal automated board assembly

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1

### Description of the Component:

The MMSZ5V6T1G is a Zener diode, specifically designed for voltage regulation applications. Zener diodes are semiconductor devices that allow current to flow in the reverse direction when the voltage exceeds a certain value, known as the Zener voltage. This characteristic makes them ideal for providing stable reference voltages and protecting circuits from voltage spikes.

### Typical Applications:

- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage despite variations in load current or input voltage.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protects sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and surges.
- **Signal Clamping:** Used in signal processing to limit the voltage levels to a specified range.
- **Automotive Applications:** The AEC-Q101 qualification indicates suitability for automotive environments, where reliability and performance are critical.

This component is particularly useful in high-density applications due to its small package size and is suitable for automated assembly processes, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
