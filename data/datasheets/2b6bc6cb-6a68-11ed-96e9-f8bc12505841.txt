### Component Summary: IRF6775MTRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** IRF6775MTRPBF  
**Component Type:** Digital Audio MOSFET

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): 150 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at T_C = 25°C: 250 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at T_A = 25°C: 150 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at T_A = 70°C: 100 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 300 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at T_C = 25°C: 150 W
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at T_A = 25°C: 50 W
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at T_A = 70°C: 30 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature Range (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** DirectFET™
- **Special Features:**
  - Low on-resistance (RDS(on)) of 47 mΩ at VGS = 10V
  - Optimized for Class-D audio amplifier applications
  - Low gate charge (Qg) of 25 nC
  - Low reverse recovery charge (Qrr) for reduced EMI
  - Dual-sided cooling capability
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRF6775MTRPBF is a Digital Audio MOSFET designed specifically for Class-D audio amplifier applications. It employs advanced processing techniques to achieve low on-resistance per silicon area, which enhances efficiency, total harmonic distortion (THD), and electromagnetic interference (EMI) performance. The DirectFET™ packaging technology utilized in this component allows for lower parasitic inductance and resistance compared to traditional packaging, improving overall performance in high-frequency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Class-D Audio Amplifiers:** The primary application for the IRF6775MTRPBF is in Class-D audio amplifiers, where it can deliver up to 250W per channel into a 4Ω load in a half-bridge configuration.
- **Power Management Systems:** Its low on-resistance and optimized switching characteristics make it suitable for various power management applications.
- **Consumer Electronics:** The device is designed for use in consumer audio products, enhancing sound quality and efficiency.

This MOSFET is a robust and reliable choice for designers looking to improve the performance of audio amplification systems while maintaining efficiency and minimizing EMI.
