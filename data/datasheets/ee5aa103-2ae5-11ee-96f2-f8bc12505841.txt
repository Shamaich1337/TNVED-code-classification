### Component Summary: NC7WZ08K8X

**Description:**
The NC7WZ08 is a dual 2-input AND gate from ON Semiconductor’s Ultra High Speed Series of TinyLogic. It is designed using advanced CMOS technology, enabling ultra-high speed operation with high output drive while maintaining low static power dissipation across a wide voltage range. The device is particularly noted for its high-speed performance and versatility in various applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Supply Voltage (VCC):** 1.65 V to 5.5 V (operating)
  - **Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage:** -0.5 V to 6.5 V
  - **Input Voltage Tolerance:** Up to 6.5 V independent of VCC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Output Drive Current:** ±24 mA at 3 V VCC
  - **DC Output Current:** ±50 mA
  - **DC Input Diode Current:** -50 mA (when VIN < 0 V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Power Dissipation:** 
    - US8 Package: 500 mW
    - MicroPak Package: 539 mW
  - **Quiescent Supply Current:** 1 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Storage Temperature:** -65 °C to +150 °C
  - **Operating Temperature:** -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-lead US8 and MicroPak packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra high-speed operation with a typical propagation delay (t_PD) of 2.5 ns at 5 V VCC.
  - High impedance inputs and outputs when VCC is 0 V.
  - Overvoltage tolerant inputs for 5 V to 3 V translation.
  - Patented noise and EMI reduction circuitry.
  - Pb-free, Halogen-free/BFR-free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The NC7WZ08 is commonly used in various digital logic applications, including:
- **Power Management:** Efficiently controlling power distribution in circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in data routing and manipulation within digital systems.
- **Level Shifting:** Facilitating communication between devices operating at different voltage levels.
- **General Logic Functions:** Implementing basic logic operations in digital circuits.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high-speed logic operations with minimal power consumption, making it suitable for modern electronic devices and systems.
