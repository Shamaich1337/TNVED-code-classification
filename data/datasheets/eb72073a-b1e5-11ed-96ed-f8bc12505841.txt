#### Description:
The ZMM5237B is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. This characteristic makes it ideal for protecting circuits from voltage spikes and for providing reference voltages.

#### Typical Applications:

- **Overvoltage Protection:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.

- **Reference Voltage Sources:** Provides a stable reference voltage for various electronic applications.
