### Component Summary: CY7C1071DV33-12BAXI

**Manufacturer:** Cypress Semiconductor Corporation  
**Product Code:** CY7C1071DV33-12BAXI  
**Type:** Static RAM (SRAM)  
**Density:** 32 Mbit (2 M × 16 bits)  
**Package Type:** 48-ball FBGA (Pb-free)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.3 V ± 0.3 V
  - Data Retention Voltage: 2.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Operating Current (ICC): 250 mA at 83.3 MHz
  - CMOS Standby Current (ISB2): 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low active power consumption with automatic power down when deselected.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed access time (tAA = 12 ns)
  - TTL compatible inputs and outputs
  - Automatic power down feature
  - High impedance state during deselection and output disable conditions

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The **CY7C1071DV33** is a high-performance CMOS Static RAM organized as 2,097,152 words by 16 bits. It is designed for applications requiring fast access times and low power consumption. The device features a high-speed access time of 12 ns, making it suitable for high-speed data processing applications. The SRAM operates with a supply voltage of 3.3 V and has a low standby power consumption, making it efficient for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
The CY7C1071DV33 is typically used in applications such as:
- **Data Storage:** Temporary data storage in computing systems.
- **Cache Memory:** Used in processors for fast data access.
- **Networking Equipment:** For buffering and data handling in routers and switches.
- **Embedded Systems:** In microcontrollers and other embedded applications where fast memory access is critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** In devices requiring quick data retrieval and low power consumption.

This component is ideal for applications that demand high-speed memory with low power requirements, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
