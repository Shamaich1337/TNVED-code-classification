### Component Summary: TSR 0.5-2450

**Product Code:** TSR 0.5-2450  
**Manufacturer:** TRACO POWER  
**Type:** Non-Isolated DC/DC Converter (POL)  
**Series:** TSR 0.5 Series  
**Output Current:** 0.5 A  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Input Voltage Range:** 6.5 - 32 VDC (24 VDC nominal)
  - **Output Voltage:** 5 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Maximum Output Current:** 0.5 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Input Current (No Load):** 5 mA typ.
  - **Input Power (Max):** 1.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - **Operating Temperature:** –40°C to +90°C
  - **Case Temperature:** +100°C max.
  - **Storage Temperature:** –55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:** SIP3 (Plastic Case)
- **Special Features:**
  - Very high efficiency up to 94% (at Vin min)
  - Excellent line/load regulation
  - Low standby current
  - Over-temperature protection
  - Short circuit protection
  - Natural convection cooling
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The TSR 0.5-2450 is a step-down non-isolated switching regulator designed for efficient voltage conversion in compact applications. It is part of the TSR 0.5 series, which is characterized by its high efficiency, reaching up to 94% at minimum input voltage. This converter is particularly suitable for replacing linear regulators like the LM78 series, especially in designs where energy efficiency is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
The TSR 0.5-2450 is ideal for various applications, including:
- **Power Management:** Efficiently converting higher DC voltages to lower levels for powering sensitive electronic components.
- **Embedded Systems:** Providing stable voltage supplies for microcontrollers and other digital devices.
- **Telecommunications:** Used in devices requiring reliable power supplies with minimal heat generation.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Suitable for powering sensors and control circuits in harsh environments due to its wide operating temperature range and robust design.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications where space is limited, and high efficiency is required to minimize heat dissipation and improve overall system reliability.
