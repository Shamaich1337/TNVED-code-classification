### Component Summary: LG M67K-G1J2-24-Z

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF): 1.80 V (typical), 2.20 V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 12 V (maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 20 mA (maximum)
  - Surge Current (IFS): 100 mA (maximum, for t ≤ 10 µs)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption can be calculated as \( P = V_F \times I_F \). For typical conditions: \( P = 1.80 V \times 2 mA = 0.0036 W \) (3.6 mW).
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40 °C to 100 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 °C to 100 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 125 °C (maximum)
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Mini TOPLED® (Surface Mount Technology - SMT)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Chip Technology: InGaAlP
  - Typical Radiation: 120° (Lambertian emitter)
  - Corrosion Robustness Class: 3B
  - ESD withstand voltage: 2 kV (Class 2)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 2 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The LG M67K-G1J2-24-Z is a green light-emitting diode (LED) packaged in a Mini TOPLED® format. It utilizes InGaAlP chip technology to emit light at a dominant wavelength of approximately 570 nm. This LED is designed for surface mount applications and features a clear resin encapsulation that enhances its optical performance.

**Typical Applications:**
The LG M67K-G1J2-24-Z is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Cluster and Button Backlighting:** Ideal for automotive dashboards and electronic devices where visibility and aesthetics are important.
- **Interior Illumination:** Suitable for ambient lighting in vehicles and home appliances.
- **White Goods:** Used in household appliances for functional and decorative lighting.

This LED is particularly valued for its high luminous intensity and wide viewing angle, making it effective for applications requiring bright and evenly distributed light.
