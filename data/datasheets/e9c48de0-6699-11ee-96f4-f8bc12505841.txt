The\nTPD2E001 israted todissipate ESD strikes atthemaximum level specified intheIEC 61000-4-2 Level 4\ninternational standard.\n7.2 Functional Block Diagram\n7.3 Feature Description\nTPD2E001 isauni-directional ESD protection device with lowcapacitance. This central ESD\nclamp isalso connected toVCCtoprovide protection fortheVCCline. Each IOlineisrated todissipate ESD\nstrikes above themaximum level specified intheIEC61000-4-2 level 4international standard. During ESD events, voltages ashigh as±15kVcan\nbedirected toground andVCCviatheinternal diode network. ### Component Summary: TPD2E001DRLR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 0.9V to 5.5V
  - IO Pin Voltage: 0V to VCC + 0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 1 nA (max)
  - IO Leakage Current: 1 nA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Supply Current: 1 nA (max)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages: SOT-5X3 (DRL), SON (DRS), SOP (DZD), and others.

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - IEC61000-4-2 ESD Protection (Level 4)
    - ±8-kV Contact Discharge
    - ±15-kV Air-Gap Discharge
  - Low Capacitance: 1.5 pF (typical)
  - Low Leakage Current: 1 nA (maximum)
  - Space-saving package options available.
  - Non-magnetic package option for medical imaging applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (260°C, unlimited time) for DRL and DRY packages.
  - Level 2 (260°C, 1 year) for DRS package.

**Description:**
The TPD2E001 is a two-channel Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) based Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode array. It is designed to protect high-speed data interfaces from ESD strikes, rated to dissipate ESD events at the maximum levels specified in the IEC 61000-4-2 Level 4 standard. The device features low capacitance, making it suitable for high-speed signal applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- USB 2.0 interfaces
- Ethernet connections
- FireWire™ interfaces
- LVDS (Low-Voltage Differential Signaling)
- SVGA video connections
- Medical devices such as glucose meters and imaging equipment

The TPD2E001 is ideal for applications requiring robust ESD protection while maintaining signal integrity, particularly in environments where high-speed data transmission is critical.
