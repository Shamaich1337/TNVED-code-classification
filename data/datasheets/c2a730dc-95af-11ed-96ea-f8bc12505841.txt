#### Description:
The IHLP4040DZER220M11 is a low DCR shielded inductor from Vishay's IHLP series. It is designed for high current applications and is characterized by its compact size and efficient performance. The inductor features a composite construction that minimizes noise and enhances thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Ideal for high current Point of Load (POL) converters.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers.
- **Power Supplies**: Suitable for low profile, high current power supplies and battery-powered devices.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Commonly employed in distributed power systems and for field programmable gate arrays (FPGAs).

This inductor is particularly useful in applications where space is limited, and high efficiency is required, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
