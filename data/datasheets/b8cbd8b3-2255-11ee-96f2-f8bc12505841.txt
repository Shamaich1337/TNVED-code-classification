### Component Summary: VEMD2020X01

**Description:**
The VEMD2020X01 is a high-speed, high-sensitivity silicon PIN photodiode manufactured by Vishay. It is designed for surface mount applications and features a dome lens along with a daylight blocking filter. This photodiode is optimized for infrared (IR) emitters operating within the wavelength range of 830 nm to 950 nm, making it suitable for various optical applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 60 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 32 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Reverse Dark Current (I_ro): 11 nA (at V_R = 10 V)
  - Short Circuit Current (I_k): 11 μA (at E_e = 1 mW/cm², λ = 950 nm)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_V): 215 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -40 °C to +100 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -40 °C to +100 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): 100 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount (SMD)
  - Package Form: Gullwing
  - Dimensions: 2.3 mm x 2.3 mm x 2.8 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified
  - High radiant sensitivity
  - Fast response times (rise and fall times of 100 ns)
  - Angle of half sensitivity: ± 15°
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 2a, according to J-STD-020

**Typical Applications:**
The VEMD2020X01 is commonly used in:
- High-speed photo detection
- Infrared remote control systems
- Infrared data transmission
- Photo interrupters
- Shaft encoders

This photodiode is particularly useful in applications where rapid response to light changes is critical, such as in communication systems and optical sensing devices. Its compatibility with IR emitters makes it ideal for remote control and data transmission applications.
