**Manufacturer:** Analog Devices  
**Product Code:** ADCMP350YKSZ-REEL7  
**Component Type:** Comparator with Reference Circuit

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.25 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 0 V to 22 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 10 µA (typical), 15 µA (max)
  - Input Bias Current: 10 nA (at VIN = 0.6 V), 170 µA (at VIN = 22 V)
- **Power Consumption:** 10 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:** 4-lead SC70
- **Special Features:**
  - Open-drain active low output
  - High voltage tolerance (up to 22 V)
  - 15 mV hysteresis
  - 5 µs propagation delay
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The ADCMP350 is a high-performance comparator designed for general-purpose applications. It features an on-chip reference voltage of 0.6 V and is capable of operating with high input and output voltage tolerances, making it suitable for a variety of applications. The device is optimized for low power consumption, making it ideal for battery-powered devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Detectors:** Used to monitor voltage levels and provide logic outputs when thresholds are crossed.
- **Microprocessor Systems:** Acts as a comparator in digital circuits for signal processing.
- **Computers:** Utilized in various computing applications for voltage monitoring and signal comparison.
- **Battery Monitors:** Monitors battery voltage levels to ensure proper operation and prevent over-discharge.
- **Intelligent Instruments:** Employed in smart devices for accurate voltage detection and processing.
- **Portable Equipment:** Ideal for use in portable devices due to its low power consumption and compact SC70 packaging.
