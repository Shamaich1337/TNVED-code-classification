### Component Summary: HMC326MS8GETR

**Description:**
The HMC326MS8GETR is a high-efficiency GaAs InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor (HBT) MMIC driver amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 3.0 to 4.5 GHz. It is packaged in a compact 8-lead surface mount package with an exposed base, which enhances RF and thermal performance. This amplifier is optimized for high gain and power efficiency, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vs): +5V
  - Control Voltage (Vpd): +5V
  - Maximum Collector Bias Voltage: +5.5V
  - Maximum Control Voltage Range: +5.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc) at Vpd = 5V: 110 to 160 mA (typical: 130 mA)
  - Control Current (Ipd): 7 mA
  - Supply Current (Icc) at Vpd = 0V: 1 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Pdiss) at T = 85 °C: 0.916 W (derate 14 mW/°C above 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Lead Mini Small Outline Package with Exposed Pad (MSOP8G)

- **Special Features:**
  - High Gain: 21 dB
  - Saturated Output Power (Psat): +26 dBm
  - Output Third Order Intercept (IP3): +36 dBm
  - Power Added Efficiency (PAE): > 40%
  - Power down capability to conserve current consumption when not in use.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC326MS8GETR is ideal for:
- Microwave radios
- Broadband radio systems
- Wireless local loop driver amplifiers
- Any application requiring high-frequency amplification with efficient power management.

This component is particularly suited for RF applications where high gain and low power consumption are critical, making it a valuable choice for modern communication systems.
