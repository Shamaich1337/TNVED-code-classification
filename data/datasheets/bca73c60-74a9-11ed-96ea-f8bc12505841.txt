### Component Summary: AD822ARZ-REEL7

**Description:**
The AD822ARZ-REEL7 is a dual precision, low power FET-input operational amplifier (op-amp) designed for single-supply and dual-supply applications. It features true single-supply operation with an input voltage range that extends below ground, making it suitable for a variety of signal processing tasks. The op-amp is optimized for low power consumption while maintaining high performance in both AC and DC characteristics.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single-supply: 5 V to 30 V
  - Dual-supply: ±2.5 V to ±15 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Minimum output current: 15 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum quiescent current: 800 μA per amplifier
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Military grade: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Lead SOIC_N (R Suffix)
- **Special Features:**
  - Rail-to-rail output swing
  - Input voltage range extends below ground
  - Excellent AC performance with a unity-gain bandwidth of 1.8 MHz and a slew rate of 3 V/μs
  - Low noise: 13 nV/√Hz at 10 kHz
  - Enhanced product features for defense and aerospace applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
- Photodiode preamplifiers
- Active filters
- Data acquisition systems (12-bit to 14-bit)
- Low power references and voltage regulators

### Conclusion:
The AD822ARZ-REEL7 is a versatile operational amplifier suitable for applications requiring low power consumption and high precision. Its ability to operate from a single supply and handle input signals below ground makes it ideal for various signal processing applications in both commercial and military environments.
