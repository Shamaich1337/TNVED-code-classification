### Component Summary: V430CH8T (Littelfuse CH Varistor Series)

#### Key Specifications:

  - Maximum AC Voltage (VM(AC)RMS): 275V
  - Maximum DC Voltage (VM(DC)): 369V

  - Surface Mount Chip, dimensions: 5 x 8 mm

  - Lead-Free
  - No derating up to 125°C ambient temperature
  - Recognized under UL file #E75961
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for surface mount devices.

#### Description:
The V430CH8T is a surface mount metal-oxide varistor (MOV) designed for transient voltage surge suppression. It is part of the Littelfuse CH Varistor Series, which features a compact, leadless chip form factor that allows for reduced size and weight in electronic designs. The varistor functions by clamping voltage spikes, protecting sensitive electronic components from transient overvoltage conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The V430CH8T varistor is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- **Power Management:** Protecting power supply circuits from voltage surges.
- **Signal Processing:** Safeguarding signal lines in communication devices.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Providing protection in devices such as televisions, computers, and home appliances.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Ensuring reliability in machinery and control systems by preventing damage from electrical transients.

This component is particularly useful in environments where electrical surges are common, helping to enhance the reliability and longevity of electronic systems.
