This avoids excessive inrush current and creates asmooth output voltage rise\nslope. Italso prevents excessive voltage drops ofprimary cells and rechargeable batteries with high internal\nimpedance.\n7TLV62568A ,TLV62569A\nwww.ti.com SLVSE95B –APRIL 2018 –REVISED MARCH 2020\nProduct Folder Links: TLV62568A TLV62569ASubmit Documentation Feedback Copyright ©2018 –2020, Texas Instruments IncorporatedFeature Description (continued)\nThe device isable tostart intoapre-biased output capacitor. ### Component Summary: TLV62569APDRLT

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.5 V to 5.5 V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Adjustable from 0.6 V to VIN

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current (IOUT): 2 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Shutdown Current: < 2 µA
  - Efficiency: Up to 95%

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40 °C to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT563 (6-pin)

- **Special Features:**
  - Forced PWM operation to reduce output voltage ripple
  - Internal soft-start to limit inrush current
  - Overcurrent protection and thermal shutdown
  - Power good output for system monitoring
  - Pin-to-pin compatible with TLV62568 variants

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TLV62569APDRLT is a synchronous step-down (buck) DC-DC converter designed for high efficiency and compact size. It integrates internal switches capable of delivering up to 2 A of output current while maintaining a high efficiency of up to 95%. The device operates in pulse width modulation (PWM) mode at a typical switching frequency of 1.5 MHz, which allows for a smaller inductor size and reduced overall solution footprint. 

#### Typical Applications:
The TLV62569APDRLT is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- General-purpose point-of-load (POL) power supplies
- Set-top boxes (STB) and digital video recorders (DVR)
- IP network cameras
- Wireless routers
- Solid-state drives (SSD) for enterprise applications

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management in compact designs, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic devices.
