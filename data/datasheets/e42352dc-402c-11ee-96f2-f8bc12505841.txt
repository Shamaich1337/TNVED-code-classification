### Component Summary: PESD5V0S2UQ

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 15 A (for 8/20 μs pulse)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 150 W (for 8/20 μs pulse)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT663 (plastic surface-mounted package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Unidirectional ESD protection for up to two lines
  - ESD protection up to 30 kV (IEC 61000-4-2, level 4)
  - Low clamping voltage: VCL = 20 V at IPP = 15 A
  - Low reverse leakage current: IRM < 1 nA
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The PESD5V0S2UQ is a unidirectional double ElectroStatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode designed to safeguard electronic circuits from voltage spikes caused by ESD and other transient events. It is housed in a compact SOT663 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Computers and Peripherals:** Protecting data lines from ESD damage.
- **High-Speed Data Lines:** Ensuring signal integrity in high-speed communication.
- **Audio and Video Equipment:** Preventing signal degradation due to transients.
- **Parallel Ports and Communication Systems:** Providing reliable protection for data transmission lines.

This component is particularly useful in environments where sensitive electronic devices are exposed to potential ESD threats, ensuring longevity and reliability in various applications.
