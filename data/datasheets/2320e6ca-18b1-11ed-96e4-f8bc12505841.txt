**Primary Function:** Designed to support 1000Base-T Ethernet transceivers with a 1:1 turns ratio.

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated, but typically operates within standard Ethernet voltage levels.
- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, it is designed for 1000Base-T applications.
- **Power Consumption:** Not specified in the provided text.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 0°C to 70°C.
- **Package Type:** Mini-Magnetics Module.
- **Insertion Loss (dB MAX):** 1 dB at 1-100 MHz.
- **Return Loss (dB MIN @ 100 W):** -18 dB at 1 MHz, -16 dB at 30 MHz, -12 dB at 40-50 MHz, -10 dB at 60-80 MHz, -10 dB at 100 MHz.

- **Weight:** 1.2 grams.
- **Packaging Options:** Tape & Reel (400 units), Tube (30 units).

#### Description:
The H5079NL is a Mini-Magnetics module designed for networking applications, specifically for 1000Base-T Ethernet transceivers. It features a 1:1 turns ratio, which is essential for maintaining signal integrity in high-speed data transmission. The module is compliant with the IEEE 802.3ab standard, ensuring compatibility with Ethernet networking protocols.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Networking:** Used in Ethernet applications to facilitate high-speed data transmission over twisted pair cables.
- **Telecommunications:** Suitable for use in various telecommunication devices that require reliable data transfer.
- **Data Centers:** Ideal for use in data center environments where space-saving components are necessary.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Can be integrated into consumer networking devices such as routers and switches.
