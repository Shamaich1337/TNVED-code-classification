### Component Summary: XHF2-912+

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, the component is designed for RF applications.
- **Current Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; RF power input ratings provided.
- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Power Input, Passband (F3-F5): 21.26W at 25°C
  - RF Power Input, Stopband (DC-F3): 30.25W at 25°C
  - Note: Power ratings derate linearly to 0.63W (Passband) and 0.12W (Stopband) at 105°C.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +105°C
- **Package Type:** 2x2 mm QFN (CASE STYLE: MC1630-1)
- **Special Features:**
  - Reflectionless design eliminates in-band spurs and stop band reflections.
  - Cascadable for sharper and higher attenuation.
  - Excellent power handling capabilities.
  - Stable performance over temperature with ±0.3 dB variation.
  - RoHS compliant.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The XHF2-912+ is a reflectionless high pass filter designed by Mini-Circuits. It utilizes a patented filter topology that absorbs and terminates stop band signals internally, preventing reflections back to the source. This innovative design allows for unique applications that traditional reflective filters cannot accommodate, making it suitable for integration with sensitive devices.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Wi-Fi and WiMax:** Used in wireless communication systems to filter out unwanted frequencies while allowing desired signals to pass.
- **Microwave Radio:** Essential for maintaining signal integrity in high-frequency applications.
- **Military & Space:** Suitable for environments where high reliability and performance are critical, particularly in RF applications.

The XHF2-912+ is particularly advantageous in systems requiring high dynamic range and stability, as it minimizes intermodulation and interference caused by reflected signals. Its compact size and high power handling capabilities make it an ideal choice for modern RF circuit designs.
