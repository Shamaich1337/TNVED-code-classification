### Component Summary: CDCV304PWR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.3V to 3.6V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5V to 4.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±50 mA
  - High-Level Output Current (VDD=2.5V): -12 mA; (VDD=3.3V): -24 mA
  - Low-Level Output Current (VDD=2.5V): 12 mA; (VDD=3.3V): 24 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Dynamic Current (IDD) at 67 MHz: 28 mA (VDD=2.7V), 37 mA (VDD=3.6V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Pin TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - General-purpose and PCI-X compliant clock buffer
  - Low output skew: <100 ps
  - Output enable control that drives outputs low when OE is low
  - Operates from a single 3.3V or 2.5V supply

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **CDCV304** is a high-performance, low-skew, general-purpose clock buffer designed for PCI-X applications. It takes a single clock input (CLKIN) and distributes it to four buffered output clocks (1Y[0:3]). The device is optimized for low output skew and operates at frequencies up to 200 MHz, making it suitable for high-speed digital applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The CDCV304 is typically used in:
- **Clock Distribution:** It is ideal for distributing clock signals in systems requiring precise timing, such as PCI-X based systems.
- **Signal Integrity Improvement:** The low skew characteristics help maintain signal integrity across multiple outputs.
- **Data Communication:** It can be used in various data communication applications where clock synchronization is critical.
- **Automotive and Industrial Applications:** Given its operating temperature range, it is suitable for automotive and industrial environments.

This component is particularly valuable in designs where multiple clock signals are needed from a single source, ensuring that all outputs maintain synchronization with minimal delay.
