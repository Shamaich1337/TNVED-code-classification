### Component Summary: RT9187GQV

**Manufacturer:** Richtek Technology Corporation  
**Product Code:** RT9187GQV  
**Type:** Low Dropout (LDO) Regulator

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.5V to 5.5V
  - Absolute Maximum Input Voltage: 6V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: Up to 1A
  - Current Limit: 2A (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 380 µA (typical)
  - Standby Current: <0.1 µA in shutdown mode
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
  - Ambient Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - VDFN-8L 3x3 (QV)
  - SOP-8 (Exposed Pad)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low dropout voltage: 240 mV at 1A
  - High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR)
  - Fast turn-on time: <40 µs
  - Thermal shutdown protection
  - Current limiting protection
  - Noise reduction pin for output noise reduction
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The RT9187 is a high-performance, ultra-low dropout (LDO) regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with minimal voltage drop. It is capable of delivering up to 1A of output current while maintaining high power supply rejection and low noise, making it suitable for sensitive RF and wireless applications. The device features a noise reduction pin that can be used to further minimize output noise, which is critical in RF applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **CDMA/GSM Cellular Handsets:** Provides stable power supply for RF components.
- **Battery-Powered Equipment:** Extends battery life due to low quiescent and standby currents.
- **Laptop, Palmtops, Notebook Computers:** Supplies power to various components while maintaining efficiency.
- **Hand-Held Instruments:** Ideal for portable devices requiring compact power solutions.
- **Mini PCI & PCI-Express Cards:** Supports power management in compact form factors.
- **PCMCIA & New Cards:** Ensures reliable operation in portable information appliances.

The RT9187 is particularly well-suited for applications where space is limited and performance is critical, such as in mobile devices and portable electronics. Its combination of low dropout voltage, high PSRR, and low power consumption makes it an excellent choice for modern electronic designs.
