- **Voltage Ratings:**

  - **V_GS (Gate-to-Source Voltage):** ±20V (max)

  - **I_D (Continuous Drain Current):**

  - **T_J (Junction Temperature):** -55°C to +150°C
  - **T_STG (Storage Temperature):** -55°C to +150°C

  - Ultra-low gate impedance
  - Fully characterized avalanche voltage and current
  - Suitable for synchronous rectification in isolated DC-DC converters

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRF7351TRPBF is an N-channel power MOSFET designed for high-efficiency switching applications. It features low on-resistance (R_DS(on) max of 17.8 mΩ at V_GS = 10V), which minimizes conduction losses, making it suitable for power management applications.
