### Component Summary: ESDA17-5SC6

**Description:**
The ESDA17-5SC6 is a monolithic Transil™ array designed for ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) protection. It features five unidirectional Transil functions, making it suitable for protecting multiple lines against transient overvoltage events. This component is particularly advantageous for applications requiring high-density board layouts due to its compact SOT23-6L package.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Minimum Breakdown Voltage (VBR min): 17 V
  - Maximum Breakdown Voltage (VBR max): 19 V
  - Stand-off Voltage (VRM): 14 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP, 8/20 µs): 150 W
  - Leakage Current at Stand-off Voltage (IR): < 100 nA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - The device has a low leakage current, ensuring minimal power consumption during operation.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40 to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23-6L (6-lead surface mount package)

- **Special Features:**
  - High ESD protection level compliant with IEC 61000-4-2 level 4 (15 kV air discharge, 8 kV contact discharge).
  - MIL STD 883E, Method 3015-7: class 3 (25 kV human body model).
  - Suitable for high-density boards.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Complies with JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The ESDA17-5SC6 is ideal for use in environments where transient overvoltage protection is critical, particularly in ESD-sensitive equipment. Typical applications include:
- Computers and peripherals (e.g., printers)
- Communication systems
- Cellular phones and accessories
- Consumer electronics (e.g., set-top boxes, DVD players, TVs)

This component is essential for safeguarding sensitive electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge, ensuring reliability and longevity in various electronic applications.
