### Component Summary: CNY174SM (ON Semiconductor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (V_CEO): 70 V (minimum)
  - Maximum Working Insulation Voltage (V_IORM): 850 V (peak)
  - Input-to-Output Isolation Voltage: 4170 V AC RMS for 1 minute

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (I_F): 60 mA (maximum)
  - Continuous Collector Current (I_C): 50 mA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Power Dissipation: 270 mW at 25°C
  - LED Power Dissipation: 120 mW at 25°C
  - Detector Power Dissipation: 150 mW at 25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -40 to +125 °C
  - Ambient Operating Temperature (T_A): -40 to +100 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -40 to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 6-Pin Dual In-Line Package (DIP)

- **Special Features:**
  - High Current Transfer Ratio (CTR): 160% to 320% at I_F = 10 mA
  - Very low coupled capacitance for minimum noise susceptibility
  - Safety and regulatory approvals including UL1577 and DIN EN/IEC 60747-5-5

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **CNY174SM** is an optocoupler that consists of a gallium arsenide infrared emitting diode coupled with an NPN phototransistor. It is designed to provide electrical isolation between its input and output while allowing signal transmission. The device is housed in a 6-pin DIP package, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supply Regulators:** Used to isolate feedback signals in power supply circuits.
- **Digital Logic Inputs:** Provides isolation for digital signals in microprocessor and logic circuits.
- **Microprocessor Inputs:** Interfaces between microcontrollers and other components while maintaining electrical isolation.
- **Appliance Sensor Systems:** Used in sensor applications to ensure safe operation and signal integrity.
- **Industrial Controls:** Commonly found in industrial automation systems for signal isolation and control.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the CNY174SM optocoupler, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical applications in electronic systems.
