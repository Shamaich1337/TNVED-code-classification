### Component Summary: PDTC143ZU,115 (NXP Semiconductors)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): -50 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): -50 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): -10 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (DC, IO): -100 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): -100 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot):
    - SOT323 Package: 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to 150 °C
  - Ambient Operating Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT323 (SC-70)

- **Special Features:**
  - Built-in bias resistors (R1 = 4.7 kΩ, R2 = 47 kΩ)
  - Simplified circuit design
  - Reduction of component count
  - Reduced pick and place costs

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The PDTC143ZU,115 is a PNP resistor-equipped transistor designed for general-purpose switching and amplification applications. It features built-in bias resistors that simplify circuit design and reduce the number of external components required, making it a cost-effective solution for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- General-purpose switching and amplification
- Inverter circuits
- Interface circuits
- Circuit drivers

This component is particularly useful in applications where space and component count are critical, such as in compact electronic devices and consumer electronics. The built-in resistors allow for easier integration into circuits, making it suitable for both hobbyist and professional designs.
