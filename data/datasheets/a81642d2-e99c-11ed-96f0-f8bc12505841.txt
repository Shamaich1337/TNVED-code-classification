**Key Characteristics:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V_S): 1.8V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Current (I_Q): 2.9 µA (max), 5.5 µA (typ)
  - Output Short-Circuit Current (I_SC): ±5 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption suitable for battery-powered applications.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40°C to +125°C
  - Storage: -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SC70-5 (DCK)
- **Special Features:**
  - Rail-to-Rail output
  - Low noise: 2.8 µV PP (0.1Hz - 10Hz)
  - Low offset voltage: 1.5 mV (max)
  - High CMRR: 100 dB (typ)
  - Low bias current: 5 pA (typ)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The OPA379AIDCKR is a micropower, low-voltage operational amplifier designed for battery-powered applications. It features rail-to-rail input and output, allowing it to operate effectively across a wide range of supply voltages. The device is optimized for low power consumption while maintaining high performance, making it suitable for various precision applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Battery-Powered Instruments:** Ideal for devices that require low power consumption to extend battery life.
- **Portable Devices:** Used in handheld devices where space and power efficiency are critical.
- **Medical Instruments:** Suitable for medical devices that require precise signal amplification with minimal power usage.
- **Handheld Test Equipment:** Commonly found in portable testing and measurement tools where accuracy and low power are essential.

This operational amplifier is particularly useful in applications where low noise and low offset voltage are critical, such as in sensor signal conditioning, data acquisition systems, and precision analog circuits.
