#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings (Vmax)**: 6.0 Volts
- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Maximum Current (Imax)**: 100 Amps
  - **Hold Current (Ihold)**: 0.35 Amps
  - **Trip Current (Itrip)**: 0.75 Amps
- **Resistance**:

  - **Maximum Resistance (R1max)**: 1.20 Ohms
  - **Typical Resistance**: 0.10 Ohms

- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type**: 1206 Surface Mount
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.
- **Special Features**:
  - Compliant with AEC-Q200 Rev-C for automotive applications.
  - Compact design for space-saving on PCBs.
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free.
  - Agency recognition as a PTC resettable fuse.

#### Description:
The **MF-NSMF035-2** is a **Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) Resettable Fuse** designed to protect electronic circuits from overcurrent conditions. When the current exceeds the specified trip level, the fuse increases its resistance significantly, effectively interrupting the circuit until the fault condition is removed and the device cools down, allowing it to reset.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Port Protection**: Safeguarding USB 2.0, 3.0, and OTG ports from overcurrent.
- **HDMI Source Protection**: Protecting HDMI 1.4 sources from fault conditions.
- **PC Motherboards**: Providing plug-and-play protection for various components.
- **Mobile Devices**: Battery and port protection in smartphones and PDAs.
- **Game Consoles**: Protecting ports from overcurrent situations.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Used in electronic control modules to ensure reliability and safety.

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and fast response to fault conditions is critical, making it ideal for modern electronic devices.
