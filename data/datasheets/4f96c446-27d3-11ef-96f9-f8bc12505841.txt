### Component Summary: FSA223UMX

**Manufacturer:** Fairchild Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** FSA223UMX  
**Revision Date:** November 2012

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VAudio Supply Voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V
  - VCC Supply Voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings: -0.5V to 6.0V for both VAudio and VCC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Switch I/O Current: 50mA (USB and Audio)
  - Peak Switch Current (Pulsed at 1ms): 100mA (USB and Audio)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current (Audio): 4µA to 10µA
  - Quiescent Supply Current (VCC): 12µA to 20µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 10-Lead Quad Ultrathin MLP (1.4 x 1.8 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On Resistance: 4Ω (USB), 3Ω (Audio)
  - Power-off Protection on Common D+/R, D-/L Ports
  - Automatically Detects VCC for Switch Path Selection
  - Handles negative signals, eliminating the need for large coupling capacitors.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The FSA223 is a Double-Pole, Double Throw (DPDT) multiplexer designed to facilitate the sharing of audio and USB2.0 High-Speed (480Mbps) signals through a common connector. It is optimized for low distortion and allows both audio and USB data to swing below ground, making it suitable for portable devices that require a combined audio and USB interface.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** 
  - Cell Phones
  - PDAs (Personal Digital Assistants)
  - Digital Cameras
  - Notebooks
  - LCD Monitors
  - TVs
  - Set-Top Boxes

The FSA223 is particularly useful in applications where space is limited, and a single connector can be used for both audio and USB data transfer, enhancing the functionality of portable devices while minimizing the number of connectors required.
