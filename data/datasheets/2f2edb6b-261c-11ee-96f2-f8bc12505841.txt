#### Description:
The TMUX1134 is a precision complementary metal-oxide semiconductor (CMOS) switch featuring four independently controlled single-pole double-throw (SPDT) switches. It is designed for low-voltage applications and supports both analog and digital signals. The device operates with a wide supply voltage range and is compatible with 1.8V logic levels, making it suitable for interfacing with various digital systems.

The TMUX1134 is ideal for a variety of applications, including:
- **Field Transmitters:** Used in industrial and medical equipment for signal routing.
- **Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC):** Facilitates control and automation in manufacturing processes.
- **Factory Automation and Control:** Enables efficient management of multiple signal paths.
- **Medical Devices:** Such as ultrasound scanners and patient monitoring systems, where precision switching is critical.
- **Data Acquisition Systems (DAQ):** For collecting and processing data from various sensors.
- **Instrumentation:** Including lab and analytical equipment that require precise signal management.
- **Smart Meters:** For water and gas measurement applications.
- **Optical Networking:** In systems requiring high-speed data transmission.
- **Portable Point of Sale (POS) Systems:** Where compact and efficient signal routing is necessary.
