### Component Summary: LMZ14203HTZE/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 6V to 42V
  - Output Voltage: Adjustable down to 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 3A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Efficiency: Up to 97% (varies with load and input voltage)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-PMOD (7 pins), dimensions: 10.16 mm x 9.85 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated shielded inductor
  - Low output voltage ripple
  - Input undervoltage lockout (UVLO)
  - Output short-circuit protection
  - Thermal shutdown
  - Soft-start capability
  - Negative output voltage applications
  - Pin-to-pin compatible with other members of the LMZ family

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 3, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **LMZ14203H** is a high-efficiency step-down DC-DC power module designed for applications requiring a compact power supply solution. It integrates a shielded inductor and provides a simple PCB layout, making it suitable for time-critical projects and space-constrained applications. The module is capable of delivering up to 3A of output current with excellent line and load regulation, and it features a low output voltage ripple.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Intermediate Bus Conversions:** Ideal for converting higher voltage rails (e.g., 24V or 36V) to lower voltage levels (e.g., 12V or 5V).
- **Power Management:** Used in various power management applications where efficient voltage regulation is required.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for powering signal processing circuits that require stable voltage levels.
- **Data Storage:** Can be utilized in data storage devices that require reliable power supplies.
- **Telecommunications:** Commonly used in telecom equipment for voltage regulation.

The LMZ14203H is particularly beneficial in designs where thermal performance and efficiency are critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic applications.
