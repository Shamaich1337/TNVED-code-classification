### Component Summary: MJD117T4 (STMicroelectronics)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 100 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 100 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 2 A
  - Collector Peak Current (ICM): 4 A
  - Base Current (IB): 0.05 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Dissipation (PTOT) at Tcase = 25 °C: 20 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65 to 150 °C
  - Maximum Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DPAK (TO-252)

- **Special Features:**
  - Monolithic Darlington configuration with integrated antiparallel collector-emitter diode.
  - Good hFE linearity and high fT frequency.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The MJD117T4 is a complementary power Darlington transistor manufactured by STMicroelectronics. It features a monolithic Darlington configuration, which allows for high current gain and improved performance in switching applications. The device is designed to handle significant power levels, making it suitable for various industrial applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The MJD117T4 is commonly used in:
- **Linear and Switching Industrial Equipment:** It is ideal for applications requiring high current and voltage handling capabilities.
- **Power Management:** The transistor can be utilized in power supply circuits and motor control systems.
- **Signal Processing:** Its high gain characteristics make it suitable for amplifying weak signals in various electronic circuits.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where robust performance and reliability are critical, such as in industrial automation and control systems.
