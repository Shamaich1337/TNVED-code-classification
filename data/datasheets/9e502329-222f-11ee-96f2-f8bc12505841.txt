### Component Summary: BSC026N04LS

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** BSC026N04LS  
**Type:** N-channel Power MOSFET  
**Package Type:** PG-TDSON-8  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 40 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 119 A (at 25°C)
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulse): 476 A (at 25°C)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (Ptot): 63 W (at 25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to 150°C
- **On-Resistance:**
  - Maximum RDS(on) at VGS=10V: 2.6 mΩ
- **Gate Threshold Voltage (VGS(th)):** 1.2 V to 2 V
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Qualified according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The BSC026N04LS is a high-performance N-channel Power MOSFET optimized for synchronous rectification in switch-mode power supplies (SMPS). It features very low on-resistance, which minimizes conduction losses, and is 100% avalanche tested, ensuring reliability under high-stress conditions. The device is designed to operate efficiently in a wide range of applications, making it suitable for various power management tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient energy conversion and management.
- **Synchronous Rectification:** Ideal for applications requiring high efficiency in converting AC to DC.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Commonly utilized in buck converters and other DC-DC conversion applications.
- **Motor Control:** Can be employed in motor drive circuits for efficient control of electric motors.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Suitable for use in power supplies for consumer electronics, enhancing overall system efficiency.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where thermal performance and efficiency are critical, making it a preferred choice for modern electronic designs.
