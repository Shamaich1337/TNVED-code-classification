#### Description:
The **USM-G16M** is an **Ultrasonic Gas Meter Module** designed to measure the flow velocity of gas using ultrasonic transducers based on the Time of Flight (TOF) principle. It calculates the consumed gas volume and displays it on the meter's screen. The module is compact and suitable for various applications, particularly in environments where explosive gases may be present.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Natural Gas Metering:** Used for measuring the flow of natural gas in residential and commercial applications.
- **Gas Detection Equipment:** Can be integrated into systems for monitoring gas levels and ensuring safety in hazardous environments.
- **Smart Metering Solutions:** Ideal for smart city applications where accurate gas consumption data is essential for resource management.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for use in industries that require precise gas flow measurements for process control and monitoring.

This module is particularly beneficial in applications requiring high accuracy and low pressure loss, making it a reliable choice for gas measurement in various settings.
