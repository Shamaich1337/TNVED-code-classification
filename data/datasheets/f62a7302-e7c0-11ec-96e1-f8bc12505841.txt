### Component Summary: ADV7513BSWZ

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - 1.8 V Supply Voltage (DVDD, AVDD, PVDD, BGVDD)
  - 3.3 V Supply Voltage (DVDD_3V)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power-Down Current: 300 µA
  - Total Power Consumption: 
    - At 1.8 V: 256 mW
    - At 3.3 V: 1 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -25°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 64-Lead Low Profile Quad Flat Package (LQFP_EP) with exposed pad
- **Special Features:**
  - Supports HDMI v1.4 features including 3D video
  - Integrated CEC buffer/controller
  - Compatible with DVI v1.0 and HDCP v1.4
  - Programmable 2-way color space converter
  - On-chip microcontroller for HDCP operations and EDID reading
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The ADV7513 is a high-performance HDMI transmitter designed for applications requiring high-definition video and audio transmission. It operates at a maximum clock frequency of 165 MHz, supporting all video formats up to 1080p and UXGA. The device is compliant with HDMI v1.4 and DVI v1.0 standards, making it suitable for a wide range of multimedia applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Gaming Consoles:** Provides high-quality video and audio output for gaming systems.
- **PCs:** Used in computer systems for connecting to displays and audio systems.
- **DVD Players and Recorders:** Facilitates the transmission of high-definition video and audio from DVD devices.
- **Digital Set-Top Boxes:** Enables high-definition content delivery to televisions.
- **A/V Receivers:** Integrates with audio/video receivers for enhanced multimedia experiences.

The ADV7513 is particularly noted for its ability to handle high bit rate audio and support for various audio formats, including uncompressed LPCM and compressed audio formats like Dolby Digital and DTS. Its advanced features and compatibility make it a versatile choice for modern multimedia applications.
