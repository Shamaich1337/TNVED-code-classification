### Component Summary: ESDA6V1SC6

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.1 V (min)
  - Stand-off Voltage (VRM): 6.1 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 18 A (for ESDA6V1SC6)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 400 W (8/20 µs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to +150 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (T_op): -55 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23-6L (SC-59)
- **Special Features:**
  - 4 unidirectional ESD protection
  - High ESD protection level: up to 30 kV (air and contact discharge)
  - Complies with IEC 61000-4-2 level 4
  - MIL STD 883E - Method 3015-7: class 3B
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL: Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOT packages are MSL 1 or 2.

**Description:**
The ESDA6V1SC6 is a monolithic voltage suppressor designed specifically for ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) protection. It features a quad Transil™ array that provides robust protection for components connected to data and transmission lines. The device clamps voltage spikes just above the logic level supply for positive transients and below ground for negative transients, effectively safeguarding sensitive electronic components from transient overvoltage events.

**Typical Applications:**
The ESDA6V1SC6 is commonly used in applications where transient overvoltage protection is critical, particularly in ESD-sensitive equipment. Typical applications include:
- Computers
- Printers
- Communication systems
- Cellular phone handsets and accessories
- Other telephone sets
- Set-top boxes

This component is ideal for high-density boards due to its compact SOT23-6L package and high integration capabilities, making it suitable for modern electronic designs that require reliable ESD protection.
