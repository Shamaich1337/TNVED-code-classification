- **Package Type:**
  - SMD (Surface-Mount Device)

  - Continuous short-circuit protection

  - High efficiency (79% typical)
  - Fully RoHS compliant
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2a (according to J-STD-033C)

**Description:**
The TDR 2-1211WISM is a compact 2 Watt DC/DC converter designed for surface-mount applications. The converter provides a tightly regulated output voltage of 5 VDC, ensuring stable performance even under varying load conditions. It is designed to operate efficiently across its full load range and includes features such as remote On/Off control and continuous short-circuit protection.

- Embedded systems requiring reliable power conversion
