A 1 inc h long, 5 mm wide trace \nof 1 ounce  copper has a res istance of approximately 100  mΩ at \nroom temperature; at a load current of 10 mA, this can introduce a full millivolt of error. In an ideal board layout, the reference should be mounted as close to the load as possible to minimize the length of the output t races, and, therefore, the \nerror introduced by voltage drop.  However, in applications \nwhere this is not possible or convenient, force and sense connections (sometimes referred to as Kelvin sensing connections) are provided as a means of minimizing the IR \ndrop and improving accuracy.  \nKelvin connections work by providing a set of high impedance \nvoltage -sensing lines to the output and ground nodes. Because \nvery little current flows through these connections, the IR drop across their traces is negligible, and t he output and ground voltages can be sensed accurately. ### Component Summary: ADR3412ARJZ

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Output Voltage: 1.200 V
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.3 V to 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: +10 mA (source), -3 mA (sink)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 100 μA (maximum)
  - Shutdown Current: 5 μA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 6-lead SOT-23 (RJ-6)

- **Special Features:**
  - Initial accuracy: ±0.1% (maximum)
  - Low dropout voltage: 250 mV at 2 mA
  - Low output noise: <10 μV p-p (0.1 Hz to 10 Hz)
  - DigiTrim® technology for high accuracy and low temperature coefficient (8 ppm/°C)
  - Enable/Shutdown feature for low power consumption

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

**Description:**
The ADR3412ARJZ is a low-cost, low-power, high-precision CMOS voltage reference. It is designed to provide a stable output voltage with high accuracy and low noise, making it suitable for precision applications. The device utilizes proprietary DigiTrim® technology to achieve its specifications, ensuring that the output voltage and temperature coefficient are finely tuned during manufacturing.

**Typical Applications:**
The ADR3412ARJZ is commonly used in:
- Precision data acquisition systems
- Industrial instrumentation
- Medical devices
- Battery-powered devices

These applications benefit from the component's low power consumption, high accuracy, and stability over a wide temperature range, making it ideal for environments where precision voltage references are critical.
