### Component Summary: MAX9920ASA+T

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5V to 5.5V
  - Input Common-Mode Voltage Range: -20V to +75V
  - Differential Input Voltage (MAX9920): ±5V (Continuous)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current into Any Pin: ±20mA
  - Supply Current (ICC): 1.0 to 1.6 mA (depending on conditions)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Shutdown Supply Current: 0.5 to 10 µA
  - Continuous Power Dissipation: 1951.2 mW (at 70°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Pin SO-EP (Exposed Pad)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Adjustable gain set by external resistive-divider network.
  - AEC-Q100 qualified for automotive applications.
  - Supports both unidirectional and bidirectional current sensing.
  - Rail-to-rail output.
  - Input offset voltage: 400µV (max).
  - Gain accuracy: 0.6% (max).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The MAX9920 is a high-accuracy, single-supply current-sense amplifier designed for monitoring current in inductive loads such as motors and solenoids. It features a wide input common-mode voltage range from -20V to +75V, making it suitable for applications where common-mode voltages can fluctuate due to inductive kickback or transient events. The device can operate in both unidirectional and bidirectional modes, depending on the reference voltage applied.

**Typical Applications:**
- H-Bridge motor current sensing
- Solenoid current sensing
- Current monitoring of inductive loads
- High- and low-side precision current sensing
- Super-capacitor charge/discharge monitoring
- Precision high-voltage current monitoring
- Automotive applications requiring robust current sensing solutions

This component is particularly beneficial in automotive and industrial applications where accurate current measurement is critical for system performance and reliability.
