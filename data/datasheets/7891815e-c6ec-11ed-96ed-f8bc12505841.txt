### Component Summary: TPA1517DWPR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V_CC): 9.5 V to 18 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: 22 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (I_CC): 45 mA (typical at 12 V), 50 mA (typical at 14.5 V)
  - Non-repetitive Peak Output Current (I_O(SM)): 4 A
  - Repetitive Peak Output Current (I_O(RM)): 2.5 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Continuous Power Output: 6 W per channel into a 4 Ω load at 10% THD+N
  - Power Dissipation: 2.94 W (DWP package) at 25 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (T_A): -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -40 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 20-Pin PowerPAD™ SOIC (DWP) or 20-Pin DIP (NE)

- **Special Features:**
  - Thermal Protection
  - Mute and Standby Operation
  - Fixed Gain: 20 dB
  - High Power Outputs (6 W/channel)
  - Surface Mount Availability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The TPA1517 is a stereo audio power amplifier designed to drive 4-Ω speakers, capable of delivering up to 6 W of continuous average power per channel. It features a fixed gain of 20 dB and includes thermal protection to prevent overheating. The amplifier supports mute and standby modes, making it suitable for power-sensitive applications. The device is available in a thermally-enhanced PowerPAD™ package, which aids in heat dissipation and reduces board space.

**Typical Applications:**
The TPA1517 is primarily used in audio amplification applications, including:
- Consumer audio devices (e.g., televisions, soundbars)
- Portable audio systems
- Automotive audio systems
- Home theater systems
- Any application requiring efficient audio amplification with low distortion and power management features.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited and thermal performance is critical, making it a popular choice for modern audio equipment.
