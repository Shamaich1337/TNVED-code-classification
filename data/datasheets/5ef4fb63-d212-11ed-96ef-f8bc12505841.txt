Safety Recommendations\n(For Information Only)\nFor the efficient and safe operation of your GSM application based on \nthe AirPrime SL808X Embedded Module, please read the following information carefully.\n12.1 RF safety\n12.1.1 General\nY our GSM terminal is based on the GSM standard for cellular technology. The GSM standard is spread all over the world. It covers Europe, Asia and some parts of America and Africa. This is the most used telecommunication standard.\nY our GSM terminal is actually a low power radio transmitter and \nreceiver. It sends out as well as receives radio frequency energy. When you use your GSM application, the cellular system which handles your calls controls both the radio frequency and the power level of your cellular modem.\n12.1.2 Exposure to RF energy\nThere has been some public concern about possible health effects of using GSM terminals. Although research on health effects from RF energy has focused on the current RF technology for many years, scientists have begun research regarding newer radio technologies, such as GSM. After existing research had been reviewed, and after compliance to all applicable safety standards had been tested, it has been concluded that the product was fitted for use.\nIf you are concerned about exposure to RF energy, there are things \nyou can do to minimize exposure. Obviously, limiting the duration of your calls will reduce your exposur e to RF energy. In addition, you \ncan reduce RF exposure by operating your cellular terminal efficiently by following the guidelines below.\n12.1.3 Efficient terminal operation\nFor your GSM terminal to operate at the lowest power level, consistent with satisfactory call quality:\nIf your terminal has an extendable antenna, extend it fully. Some \nmodels allow you to place a call with the antenna retracted. However your GSM terminal operates more efficiently with the antenna when it is fully extended.\nProduct Technical Specification & Customer Design Guidelines\n118 Proprietary and Confidential - Specifications subject to change 2400058Do not hold the antenna when the terminal is "IN USE". If a damaged antenna comes into contact with the skin, a minor burn may result. Replace a damaged antenna immediately. Y ou may repair antenna to yourself by following the instructions provided to you. If so, use only a manufacturer-approved antenna. Otherwise, have your antenna repaired by a qualified technician.\nBuy or replace the antenna only from the approved suppliers list. Using \nunauthorized antennas, modifications or attachments could damage the terminal and may contravene local RF emission regulations or invalidate type approval.\n12.2 General safety\n12.2.1 Driving\nCheck the laws and the regulations regarding the use of cellular devices in the area where you have to drive as you always have to comply with them. ### Component Summary: Sierra Wireless AirPrime SL8083

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 3.3V to 4.3V
  - VREF_1V8: 1.65V to 1.95V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Burst Transmission Current: Up to 3A (for short durations)
  - Standby Current: 3.5mA (HSDPA/WCDMA), 4mA (GPRS/EDGE)
  - Average Call Mode Current: Up to 550mA (WCDMA)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Average standby power consumption: 3.5mA (HSDPA/WCDMA), 4mA (GPRS/EDGE)
  - Average call mode power consumption: 550mA (WCDMA)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Class A (3GPP compliant): -30°C to +70°C
  - Class B (non-3GPP compliant): -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 74-pin LGA (Land Grid Array) soldered-down module
  - Dimensions: 25mm x 30mm x 2.35mm
- **Special Features**: 
  - Supports HSDPA, WCDMA, EDGE, and GPRS networks
  - Integrated GPS functionality
  - Embedded SIM (eSIM) support
  - Voice functionality with echo cancellation and noise reduction
  - Multiple interfaces including USB 2.0, UART, SPI, and audio interfaces
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **AirPrime SL8083** is a compact embedded wireless module designed for high-speed data communication over cellular networks. It supports various network technologies including HSDPA, WCDMA, EDGE, and GPRS, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. The module is equipped with an integrated GPS receiver, enabling location-based services. It features a small form factor and is designed for easy integration into devices requiring cellular connectivity.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telematics**: Used in vehicles for tracking and communication.
- **M2M Communication**: Facilitates machine-to-machine communication in industrial applications.
- **IoT Devices**: Powers Internet of Things applications requiring cellular connectivity.
- **Remote Monitoring**: Enables remote monitoring and control of devices in various sectors such as healthcare, agriculture, and smart cities.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Integrated into devices like tablets and portable hotspots for mobile internet access.

This module is ideal for developers looking to implement robust wireless communication capabilities in their products, with a focus on ease of integration and versatility across different network standards.
