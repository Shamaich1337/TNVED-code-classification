### Component Summary: DMN2004TK

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** DMN2004TK  
**Document Number:** DS30936 Rev. 5 - 2  
**Date:** November 2010  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): 20 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±8 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 540 mA
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 85°C: 390 mA
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 1.5 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD): 150 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-523

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance
  - Low Gate Threshold Voltage
  - Low Input Capacitance
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Low Input/Output Leakage
  - ESD Protected up to 2kV
  - Lead Free By Design/RoHS Compliant
  - "Green" Device
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The DMN2004TK is an N-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for efficient switching applications. It features low on-resistance and low gate threshold voltage, making it suitable for various power management tasks. The device is housed in a compact SOT-523 package, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** The DMN2004TK is commonly used in power supply circuits, DC-DC converters, and battery management systems due to its low on-resistance and efficient switching capabilities.
- **Signal Processing:** It can be utilized in signal switching applications where fast switching speeds are required.
- **Automotive Applications:** Given its qualification to AEC-Q101 standards, it is suitable for automotive electronics, including motor control and lighting systems.
- **Consumer Electronics:** The MOSFET can be found in various consumer electronic devices for power regulation and control.
