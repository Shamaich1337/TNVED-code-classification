  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The YJD112020NCTG1 is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Schottky diode designed for high-performance applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. The use of silicon carbide technology allows this diode to operate at higher temperatures and voltages compared to traditional silicon diodes.

#### Typical Applications:
The YJD112020NCTG1 is typically used in:
- Power Factor Correction (PFC)
- Solar Inverters
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Motor Drives
- Photovoltaic Inverters
- Electric Vehicles and Chargers

These applications benefit from the diode's high efficiency, reduced heat generation, and ability to handle high voltage and current levels, making it suitable for demanding power management tasks.
