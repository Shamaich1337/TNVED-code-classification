### Component Summary: SN74ACT244DWR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5V to 5.5V
  - Input Voltage: 0V to VCC + 0.5V
  - Output Voltage: 0V to VCC + 0.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Input Clamp Current: ±20 mA
  - Continuous Current through VCC or GND: ±200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Supply Current (ICC): 40 µA at VCC = 5.5V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC (20 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - 3-state outputs
  - TTL compatible inputs
  - Active low output enable (OE)
  - High drive capability (24 mA per channel)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SN74ACT244 is an octal buffer and driver designed to improve the performance and density of 3-state memory address drivers, clock drivers, and bus-oriented receivers and transmitters. It consists of two 4-bit buffers and drivers with separate output-enable (OE) inputs. When the OE input is low, the device passes non-inverted data from the A inputs to the Y outputs. When OE is high, the outputs enter a high-impedance state.

#### Typical Applications:
- **LED Displays:** The SN74ACT244 can be used to drive LED displays by providing the necessary current and voltage levels.
- **Servers and Telecommunications:** It is suitable for use in server applications and telecommunications equipment where signal buffering and driving are required.
- **Switching Networks:** The device can be utilized in switching networks to manage data flow and signal integrity.

This component is ideal for applications requiring reliable signal buffering and driving capabilities, particularly in digital circuits where high-speed operation and low power consumption are critical.
