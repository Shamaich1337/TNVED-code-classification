#### Description:
The Bourns 2036-07-SM-RPLF is a precision Gas Discharge Tube (GDT) surge protector designed for surface mount applications. The device operates by creating a short-to-ground circuit during voltage transients, effectively protecting sensitive electronic components from damage caused by surges.

- Cable broadband
- High-speed Ethernet
