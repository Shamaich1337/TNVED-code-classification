**Manufacturer:** APAC OPTO ELECTRONICS INC  
**Product Code:** LS48-A3U-TI-N-YD  
**Type:** Bi-directional SFP Transceiver Module  
**Connector Type:** LC Simplex  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 3.1 to 3.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 to 4.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (IOP): Up to 400 mA
  - Supply Current (ITX + IRX): Up to 300 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is approximately 1.32 W (3.3 V at 400 mA).

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Industry standard small form-factor pluggable (SFP)

- **Special Features:**
  - Hot pluggable
  - Diagnostic monitoring capabilities
  - Class 1 laser product compliant with EN 60825-1
  - TTL signal detect indicator

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The LS48-A3U-TI-N-YD is a bi-directional SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceiver module designed for high-speed data communication. It operates at wavelengths of 1550 nm for transmission and 1310 nm for reception, utilizing single-mode fiber optics. This module supports data rates of up to 155 Mbps, making it suitable for SONET OC-3/SDH STM-1 and Fast Ethernet applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Used in SONET/SDH networks for high-speed data transmission.
- **Data Communication:** Ideal for Fast Ethernet applications, including 100Base-BX10-D.
- **Networking Equipment:** Commonly integrated into routers, switches, and other networking devices for reliable data transfer.
- **Diagnostic Monitoring:** The built-in diagnostic features allow for real-time monitoring of the module's performance, making it suitable for critical applications where uptime is essential.

This transceiver is particularly useful in environments requiring robust performance over extended temperature ranges, making it suitable for industrial and outdoor applications.
