### Component Summary: EMA520

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** EMA520  
**Type:** Surface Mount General Purpose Rectifier  
**Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 2000 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 1400 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 2000 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 2.0 A (at 60Hz sine wave, resistance load)
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 30 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave, 1 cycle, Tj=25°C)
    - 60 A (non-repetitive, 1ms square wave, 1 cycle, Tj=25°C)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 1.3 V (at IFM=2A)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C

- **Thermal Characteristics:**
  - Typical Thermal Resistance (RθJ-A): 70 °C/W
  - RθJ-L: 25 °C/W
  - RθJ-C: 15 °C/W

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The EMA520 is a surface mount general-purpose rectifier designed for various electronic applications. It features a low-profile package that is ideal for automated placement, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing. The device is constructed with a glass passivated chip junction, which enhances its reliability and performance under high surge conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The EMA520 is commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** As a rectifier in power supply circuits to convert AC to DC.
- **Signal Processing:** In circuits where rectification of signals is required.
- **General Electronics:** Suitable for various consumer electronics and industrial applications where reliable rectification is needed.

This component is compliant with RoHS standards, ensuring it meets environmental regulations for hazardous substances. Its robust design and high surge capability make it a reliable choice for engineers looking for efficient rectification solutions in their designs.
