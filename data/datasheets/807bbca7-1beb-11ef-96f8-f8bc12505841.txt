### Component Summary: RFY1230B-AWH-LNN

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0V to 3.6V (typical 3.3V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (ICC): Up to 500 mA at VCC = 3.3V
  - Backlight LED Current (IF): 280 mA (typical)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum power consumption is determined by the supply current and voltage, approximately 1.65W at 3.3V and 500 mA.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -30°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +90°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 12.3-inch TFT LCD module with dimensions of 308.1 mm (H) x 130.0 mm (V) x 7.6 mm (D).

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - 1920 x 720 pixel resolution (RGB).
  - Anti-glare surface treatment.
  - 85-degree viewing angle in all directions.
  - LED backlight with a lifetime of approximately 30,000 hours at typical operating conditions.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The RFY1230B-AWH-LNN is a 12.3-inch color active matrix thin film transistor (TFT) liquid crystal display (LCD). It utilizes amorphous silicon TFT technology as a switching device, which allows for high-quality image rendering and responsiveness. The display features a resolution of 1920 horizontal by 720 vertical pixels, providing clear and vibrant visuals suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This TFT LCD module is commonly used in:
- **Industrial Equipment:** For control panels and monitoring systems where clear visual feedback is essential.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as tablets, portable devices, and other handheld gadgets requiring a compact display.
- **Automotive Displays:** For dashboards and infotainment systems where high visibility and durability are critical.
- **Medical Devices:** In diagnostic equipment and patient monitoring systems where reliable display performance is necessary.

The RFY1230B-AWH-LNN is designed for applications that demand a combination of high resolution, wide viewing angles, and robust performance across a range of environmental conditions.
