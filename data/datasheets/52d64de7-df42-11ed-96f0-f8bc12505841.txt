### Component Summary for WA06X391JTL

**Component Type:** Chip Resistor Array

**Description:**
The WA06X391JTL is a general-purpose chip resistor array manufactured by Walsin Technology Corporation. It is designed with a high-grade ceramic body and features internal metal electrodes connected by a resistive paste. The resistance values are trimmed to tolerance using laser cutting, and the component is RoHS compliant and lead-free. This specific variant is part of the WA06 series, which includes convex type resistors.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Operating Voltage: 50V (DC or RMS)
  - Maximum Overload Voltage: 100V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Rating at 70°C: 1/10W
  - Rated Current: 1A
  - Peak Current: 3A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Dissipation: 1/10W at 70°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to +155°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Size: 0603 (1608) with 4 resistors in the array

- **Special Features:**
  - Tolerance: ±1% (for this specific variant)
  - Temperature Coefficient of Resistance (TCR): ≤ ±200 ppm/°C for 10Ω ≤ R ≤ 1MΩ
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The WA06X391JTL is suitable for various applications, including:
- Consumer electrical equipment
- Electronic data processing (EDP) and computer applications
- Telecommunications

This component is ideal for use in circuits requiring compact and reliable resistive elements, contributing to reduced size and weight of final equipment, lower assembly costs, and enhanced reliability of electronic devices.
