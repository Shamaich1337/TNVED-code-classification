### Component Summary: TPS7A1601DRBR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 3V to 60V
  - Output Voltage Range: 1.169V to 18.5V (adjustable version)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: 100mA
  - Quiescent Current: 5µA (typical), 1µA (shutdown)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Dropout Voltage: 60mV at 20mA, 265mV at 100mA
  - Ground Current: 5µA at 100mA output

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in MSOP-8 and SON-8 PowerPAD™ packages

- **Special Features:**
  - Enable pin (EN) for on/off control
  - Power-Good output with programmable delay
  - Current limit and thermal shutdown protections
  - Stable with ceramic output capacitors (≥2.2µF)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL2 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The TPS7A1601 is an adjustable low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator designed for applications requiring high input voltage and low quiescent current. It features an enable pin for power management and a power-good output that indicates when the output voltage is stable. The device is optimized for battery-powered applications where minimizing power consumption is critical.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power supplies for notebook PCs, digital TVs, and private LAN systems
- High cell-count battery packs for power tools and other battery-powered microprocessor systems
- Automotive applications, including car audio, navigation, and infotainment systems
- Smoke and CO2 detectors, as well as battery-powered alarm and security systems

The TPS7A1601 is particularly suitable for applications where high voltage transients are present, such as automotive environments, and where low quiescent current is essential for extending battery life.
