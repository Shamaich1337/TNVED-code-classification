- **Special Features:**
  - Schmitt-trigger inputs for improved noise immunity and tolerance to slow input signals.
  - Partial power-down mode with Ioff support to prevent backflow current.
  - ESD protection exceeding JESD 22 standards (2000V HBM, 200V MM, 1000V CDM).
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 100 mA per JESD 78, Class II.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).

#### Description:
The **SN74LVC1G14** is a single Schmitt-trigger inverter designed for operation within a voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V. It performs the Boolean function Y = A, where the output Y is the inverted version of the input A. The device features Schmitt-trigger inputs, which provide hysteresis, making it suitable for applications where input signals may be slow or noisy. This characteristic enhances the reliability of the device in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The SN74LVC1G14 is commonly used in:
- **Debounce Circuits:** To eliminate noise from mechanical switches (e.g., push buttons).
- **Signal Conditioning:** For processing slow or noisy signals in digital circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as audio docks, Blu-ray players, and personal digital assistants (PDAs).
- **Embedded Systems:** In applications requiring reliable logic level shifting and signal inversion.
- **Power Management:** In telecom and server applications for AC/DC supply control.

This component is ideal for use in a wide range of electronic devices due to its low power consumption, high-speed operation, and robust performance in various environmental conditions.
