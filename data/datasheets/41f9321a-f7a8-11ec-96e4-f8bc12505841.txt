### Component Summary: NSD914XV2T1G

**Description:**
The NSD914XV2T1G is a high-speed switching diode manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed for applications requiring fast switching capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic circuits where rapid response times are essential.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 100 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 200 mA (DC)
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFM(surge)): 500 mA (DC)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD): 200 mW
  - Thermal Resistance Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 635 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-523 (Extremely small package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Lead Finish: 100% Matte Sn (Tin)
  - Qualified Maximum Reflow Temperature: 260 °C
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOD-523 packages are MSL 1.

**Typical Applications:**
The NSD914XV2T1G is commonly used in:
- High-speed switching applications
- Signal processing circuits
- Rectification in power supplies
- Protection circuits for sensitive components
- General-purpose switching applications in consumer electronics

This diode's fast recovery time (trr = 4.0 ns) and low forward voltage drop (VF = 1.0 V at IF = 10 mA) make it ideal for applications where efficiency and speed are critical. Its compact size allows for integration into space-constrained designs, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic devices.
