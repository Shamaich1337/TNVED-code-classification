### Component Summary: BCP56-16,115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 80 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 100 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): 1 A
  - Peak Collector Current (I_CM): 2 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)
  - Base Current (I_B): 0.3 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 
    - BCP56: 0.65 W (T_amb ≤ 25°C)
    - BCP56: 1.00 W (with larger copper pad)
    - BCP56: 1.35 W (with even larger copper pad)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -55°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT223 (SC-73), SOT89 (SC-62), and SOT1061 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - High current capability with three current gain selections.
  - High power dissipation capability with exposed heatsink for excellent thermal and electrical conductivity.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The BCP56-16,115 is an NPN medium power transistor designed for surface-mounted applications. It is part of a series that offers high current handling and power dissipation capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic applications. The transistor features a compact leadless package, which enhances thermal performance and allows for efficient heat dissipation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in voltage regulation and power supply circuits.
- **Low-Side Switching:** Acts as a switch to control loads in various electronic devices.
- **MOSFET Drivers:** Drives MOSFETs in power conversion applications.
- **Battery-Driven Devices:** Suitable for applications requiring efficient power management in battery-operated devices.
- **Amplifiers:** Can be used in audio and signal amplification circuits.

This transistor is particularly useful in automotive applications due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, ensuring reliability under harsh conditions.
