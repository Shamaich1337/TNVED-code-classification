- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to +105°C

- **Package Type:**

- **Special Features:**
  - Low input voltage noise: 1.2 nV/√Hz
  - Low harmonic distortion: HD2/HD3 at 10 MHz: -104/-101 dBc
  - Adjustable output common-mode voltage
  - Fast overdrive recovery: 1.5 ns
  - Externally adjustable gain
  - Supports defense and aerospace applications (AQEC standard)

- **Differential Buffers and Line Drivers:** It can be used to buffer signals and drive lines with minimal distortion.
