  - Supports IEEE 802.11ac/a/b/g/n standards

#### Description:
The AP6256 is a compact WiFi and Bluetooth combo module designed for efficient wireless communication. It integrates a 1T1R (one transmit, one receive) WiFi solution supporting multiple standards (802.11ac/a/b/g/n) and Bluetooth 5.0 technology. The module is optimized for low power consumption and is suitable for a variety of portable devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Tablets, OTT (Over-The-Top) boxes, and portable devices.
- **IoT Devices:** Smart home applications, wearables, and other connected devices requiring wireless communication.
- **Industrial Applications:** Remote monitoring and control systems that benefit from reliable wireless connectivity.

This module provides a comprehensive solution for integrating WiFi and Bluetooth functionalities into various electronic devices, making it ideal for developers looking to enhance connectivity in their products.
