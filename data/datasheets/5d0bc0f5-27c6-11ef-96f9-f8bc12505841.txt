### Component Summary: ESDSLC8V0D3BA

**Description:**
The ESDSLC8V0D3BA is a 1-Line Low Capacitance Bi-directional Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge (ESD) and lightning strikes. It features ultra-low capacitance and leakage, making it suitable for high-speed data lines and power lines.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Working Voltage (V_RWM): 8V
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 8.5V (min) to 10V (max) at 1mA
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PP): 18A (8/20μs pulse)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (P_pk): 350W (8/20μs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** Not specified in the provided text, but typically available in surface mount packages.
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low capacitance: 1pF (typical)
  - Ultra-low leakage current: 0.2μA at 8V
  - Low clamping voltage: 9V at 1A and 19.5V at 18A
  - Complies with IEC 61000-4-2 (ESD) and IEC 61000-4-5 (Lightning) standards
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The ESDSLC8V0D3BA is commonly used in applications requiring protection against ESD and voltage transients, such as:
- Data line protection in communication devices
- Power line protection in consumer electronics
- Automotive electronics
- Industrial control systems
- Any sensitive electronic equipment that requires robust protection against transient voltage spikes.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where ESD events are common, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices.
