#### Component Description:
The **Bourns 2038-15-SM-RPLF** is a **miniature symmetrical 3-electrode gas discharge tube (GDT)** designed for surge protection in electronic circuits. It functions by creating a virtual short-to-ground circuit during electrical surges, effectively diverting excess voltage away from sensitive components. When the surge exceeds the defined breakdown voltage, the gas within the tube ionizes, allowing current to flow and protecting the circuit.

#### Typical Applications:
This component is ideal for **board-level protection** in high bandwidth applications, including:
- **Broadband circuits** (e.g., xDSL, cable broadband)
- **High-speed Ethernet** connections
- General **surge protection** in telecommunications and data communication systems

The 2038 series is particularly suited for environments where high energy-handling capability and low capacitance (less than 1 pF) are critical, ensuring minimal impact on signal integrity while providing robust protection against transient disturbances.
