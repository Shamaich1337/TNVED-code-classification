### TLV6710DDCR - Texas Instruments

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.8V to 36V
  - Output Voltage (VOUTA, VOUTB): Up to 25V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Pin Current: Up to 10mA
  - Quiescent Current: 7µA (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 8µA to 11µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Thin SOT-23-6 (DDC)
- **Special Features:**
  - High Threshold Accuracy: ±0.25% (typical), ±0.75% (max over temperature)
  - Internal Hysteresis: 5.5mV (typical)
  - Open-Drain Outputs
  - Adjustable Threshold: Down to 400mV
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The TLV6710 is a micropower, high-voltage window comparator designed to operate within a supply voltage range of 1.8V to 36V. It features two high-accuracy comparators with an internal reference voltage of 400mV and open-drain outputs capable of sinking current up to 10mA. The device is optimized for low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-powered applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The TLV6710 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Notebook PCs and Tablets:** For voltage monitoring and power management.
- **Smartphones:** To ensure safe operating voltage levels.
- **Digital Cameras:** For battery management and voltage regulation.
- **Video Game Controllers:** To monitor battery levels and ensure proper operation.
- **Relays and Circuit Breakers:** For overvoltage and undervoltage detection.
- **Portable Medical Devices:** To maintain safe operating conditions.
- **Door and Window Sensors:** For security applications.
- **Portable and Battery-Powered Products:** To optimize power usage and extend battery life.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring precise voltage monitoring and control, ensuring reliable operation in a variety of electronic devices.
