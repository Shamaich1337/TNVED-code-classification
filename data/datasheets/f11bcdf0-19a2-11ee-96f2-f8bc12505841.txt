### Component Summary: HMC587LC4B

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V (max +5.5V)
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 0 to +18V (max +22V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 40 to 75 mA (typical 55 mA)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 625 mW (at T = 85°C, derate 12.5 mW/°C above 85°C)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - RoHS compliant SMT package, 4 x 4 mm, Alumina body with gold over nickel lead finish
  
- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Wide tuning bandwidth from 5.0 to 10.0 GHz
  - Low SSB Phase Noise: -95 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - No external resonator needed
  - Pulling: 4 MHz pp into a 2.0:1 VSWR
  - Pushing: 15 MHz/V at Vtune = +5V
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

### Description:
The HMC587LC4B is a wideband Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) designed using GaAs InGaP technology. It integrates a resonator, negative resistance device, and varactor diode into a single monolithic construction. This design allows for excellent output power and phase noise performance across a wide temperature range. The VCO operates from a single +5V supply and is capable of accepting an analog tuning voltage from 0 to +18 volts.

### Typical Applications:
The HMC587LC4B is primarily used in applications requiring low noise and wideband frequency generation, including:
- **Industrial and Medical Equipment:** For precise frequency control in various devices.
- **Test and Measurement Equipment:** Used in signal generators and analyzers for accurate testing.
- **Military Radar, Electronic Warfare (EW), and Electronic Countermeasures (ECM):** Essential for radar systems and communication devices that require stable and reliable frequency sources.

This component is particularly valued for its compact size, low power consumption, and ability to maintain performance across varying environmental conditions, making it suitable for a wide range of high-frequency applications.
