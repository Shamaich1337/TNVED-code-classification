### Component Summary: HMC511LP5E

**Description:**
The HMC511LP5E is a GaAs InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor (HBT) Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit (MMIC) Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) with a half-frequency output. It operates in the frequency range of 9.05 to 10.15 GHz and provides a typical output power of +13 dBm from a +5V supply. The device is designed for applications requiring low phase noise and high stability.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V (absolute maximum +5.5V)
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 2V to 13V (absolute maximum +15V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 200 mA (typical 265 mA, maximum 300 mA)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output (RFOUT): +13 dBm (typical)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 32 Lead QFN (5x5 mm) surface mount package
  
- **Special Features:**
  - No external resonator needed
  - Excellent phase noise performance: -115 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - Dual output: Fo = 9.05 - 10.15 GHz and Fo/2 = 4.525 - 5.075 GHz
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL3 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC511LP5E is suitable for various applications, including:
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) Radio
- Point-to-Point and Multi-Point Radio Communications
- Test Equipment
- Industrial Controls
- Military End-Use

This component is particularly useful in systems requiring low noise and high-frequency stability, making it ideal for communication and control applications in both commercial and military sectors.
