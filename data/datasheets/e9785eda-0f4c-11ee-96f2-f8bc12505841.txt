### KEMET R60EW61005000K Capacitor Summary

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 1000 VDC
- **Capacitance Range**: 100 µF
- **Capacitance Tolerance**: ±10%
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +105°C
- **Climatic Category**: 55/105/56
- **Package Type**: Radial leads with lead spacing of 10.0 – 37.5 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Self-healing properties
  - RoHS compliant and lead (Pb)-free terminations
  - Automotive grade (AEC-Q200 qualified)

#### Description:
The KEMET R60EW61005000K is a general-purpose metallized polyester film capacitor designed for through-hole mounting. It features a radial lead configuration and is constructed using metallized polyester film technology, which provides excellent electrical performance and reliability. The capacitor is encapsulated in a thermosetting resin that meets UL 94V-0 flammability standards, ensuring safety and durability in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The R60EW61005000K capacitor is suitable for a wide range of applications, particularly in low voltage environments. Typical uses include:
- **Blocking**: Preventing DC voltage from passing while allowing AC signals to pass.
- **Coupling**: Connecting two circuits while isolating their DC components.
- **Decoupling**: Stabilizing voltage and reducing noise in power supply circuits.
- **Bypassing**: Providing a low-resistance path to ground for high-frequency signals, thus reducing interference.
- **Interference Suppression**: Filtering out unwanted noise in automotive and electronic applications.

This capacitor is particularly well-suited for automotive electronics, where reliability and performance under varying environmental conditions are critical.
