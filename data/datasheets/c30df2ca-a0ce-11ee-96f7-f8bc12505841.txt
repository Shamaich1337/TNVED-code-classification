### Component Summary: SMAJ5949B-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components Corp.  
**Component Type:** Zener Diode  
**Product Code:** SMAJ5949B-TP

#### Key Specifications:
- **Zener Voltage (VZ):** 100 V (±5% tolerance)
- **Maximum Forward Voltage (VF):** 1.5 V at IF = 200 mA (T ≤ 75°C)
- **Power Dissipation (PD):** 1.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -65°C to +150°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** SMA (DO-214AC)
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Junction to Lead: 50°C/W
  - Junction to Ambient: 83°C/W
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Halogen Free and RoHS Compliant
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 Flammability Rating
  - Lead Free Finish

#### Description:
The SMAJ5949B is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and protection applications. It provides a stable reference voltage and is capable of clamping voltage spikes, making it essential in protecting sensitive electronic components from overvoltage conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protects circuits from voltage spikes and transients.
- **Signal Clamping:** Ensures that signal levels do not exceed specified limits, protecting downstream components.
- **Power Management:** Commonly used in power management systems to stabilize voltage levels.

This Zener diode is suitable for various electronic applications, including consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment, where reliable voltage regulation and protection are critical.
