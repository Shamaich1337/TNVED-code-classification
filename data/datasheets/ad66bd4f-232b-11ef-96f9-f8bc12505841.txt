### Component Summary: SMCJ36A by Littelfuse

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VR): 36.0 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 40.0 V (min) to 44.2 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (Ipp): 25.9 A
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): < 1 µA at VR

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPPM): 1500 W (single die), 2000 W (stacked die)
  - Power Dissipation on Infinite Heat Sink: 6.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65°C to 175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount, JEDEC DO-214AB (SMC J-Bend)

- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent clamping capability and low incremental surge resistance.
  - Fast response time: typically less than 1.0 ps from 0V to VBR min.
  - Built-in strain relief and low profile package.
  - Glass passivated chip junction.
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free.
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The SMCJ36A is a Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic equipment from voltage transients caused by lightning and other transient voltage events. It is particularly effective in safeguarding I/O interfaces, VCC buses, and other vulnerable circuits in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting data lines and interfaces from voltage spikes.
- **Computers:** Safeguarding internal circuits and components from transient voltages.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Providing protection for control systems and sensitive electronics.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Ensuring the reliability of devices by preventing damage from electrical surges.

The SMCJ36A is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against electrostatic discharge (ESD) and electrical fast transients (EFT), making it a versatile choice for a wide range of electronic designs.
