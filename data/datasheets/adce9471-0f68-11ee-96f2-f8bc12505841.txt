### Component Summary: SPU0410HR5H-1

**Description:**
The SPU0410HR5H-1 is a precision silicon microphone utilizing Knowles’ SiSonic™ MEMS technology. It is designed to provide high-performance audio capture in a compact form factor, making it suitable for various portable electronic devices. The microphone integrates an acoustic sensor, a low-noise input buffer, and an output amplifier, ensuring excellent audio performance and RF immunity.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.5 to 3.6 V
  - Absolute Maximum Rating: -0.5 to +5.0 V (VDD to Ground)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 120 µA (typical), 160 µA (maximum)
  - Input Current to Any Pin: ±5 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: 120 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 to +100 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 3.76 mm (L) x 2.95 mm (W) x 1.10 mm (H)
  - Omnidirectional microphone with an acoustic port diameter of 0.50 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Matched sensitivity
  - Low current consumption
  - Max RF protection
  - Ultra-stable performance
  - Standard SMD reflow compatible
  - Omnidirectional directivity

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Class 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The SPU0410HR5H-1 is ideal for a wide range of applications, including:
- Mobile devices (cell phones, smartphones)
- Laptop computers
- Digital still cameras
- Portable music recorders
- Various other portable electronic devices where high-quality audio capture and RF immunity are essential.

This microphone is particularly suited for applications requiring excellent wideband audio performance, making it a popular choice in consumer electronics and communication devices.
