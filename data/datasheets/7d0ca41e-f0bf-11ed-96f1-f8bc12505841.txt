### Component Summary: UD471M400P450AP6Z

**Component Type:**  
Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor

**Description:**  
The UD471M400P450AP6Z is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's UD series, designed for ultra-long life applications at high temperatures (up to 85°C). It features a snap-in type design, making it suitable for various electronic circuits requiring stable capacitance and low leakage current.

### Key Specifications:

- **Capacitance (CR):** 470 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 400 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 440 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR):** 
  - 2.87 A at 120Hz, 85°C
  - 4.1 A at 100kHz, 85°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ):** 15% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK):** 1301 µA (measured after 5 min at 20°C)
- **Maximum ESR:** 290 mΩ at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Size (D x L):** 35 mm x 45 mm
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data
- **AEC-Q200 Qualified:** No
- **Vibration Proof Version:** No

### Special Features:
- Ultra stable dissipation factor
- Low leakage current
- Designed for high reliability and long life (10,000 hours at 85°C under rated voltage and ripple current)

### Typical Applications:
The UD471M400P450AP6Z capacitor is typically used in applications that require reliable power management and filtering, such as:
- Power supply circuits
- Audio equipment
- Industrial electronics
- Consumer electronics
- Automotive applications (non-AEC-Q200 qualified)

This capacitor is particularly suited for environments where high temperature and long operational life are critical, making it ideal for use in power electronics and other demanding applications.
