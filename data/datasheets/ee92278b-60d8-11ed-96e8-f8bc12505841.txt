This converter is compact and available in both SIP and SMD packages, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

- **Instrumentation**: Provides reliable power for measurement and control systems.
- **Industrial Electronics**: Suitable for powering sensors and control circuits in industrial automation.
- **Embedded Systems**: Can be utilized in embedded applications where efficient power conversion is necessary.
