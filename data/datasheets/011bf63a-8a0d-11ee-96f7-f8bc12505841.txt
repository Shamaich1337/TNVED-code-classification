### Component Summary: LDS3985M33R

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage: 2.5 V to 6 V
  - Output Voltage: 3.3 V (fixed)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Guaranteed Output Current: Up to 300 mA
  - Peak Output Current: 550 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 85 µA (typical at no load), 200 µA (typical at 300 mA load), max 1.5 µA in OFF mode

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 125 °C (standard version)
  - -40 °C to 85 °C (automotive grade version)

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT23-5L and DFN6 (3 x 3 mm) packages

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Very low dropout voltage (150 mV typical at 300 mA load)
  - Stable with low ESR ceramic capacitors
  - Logic-controlled electronic shutdown
  - Internal current and thermal limit protection
  - Low output voltage noise: 30 µVRMS over 10 Hz to 100 kHz
  - Supply Voltage Rejection (SVR): 55 dB at 1 kHz, 50 dB at 10 kHz

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The LDS3985M33R is a very low drop and low noise BiCMOS voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage of 3.3 V with an input voltage range of 2.5 V to 6 V. It is capable of delivering up to 300 mA of output current while maintaining low quiescent current, making it suitable for low-power applications. The device features a fast turn-on time and can be controlled via a logic shutdown feature, allowing for reduced power consumption in standby modes.

**Typical Applications:**
The LDS3985M33R is ideal for use in battery-powered systems and low-power applications, such as:
- Mobile phones
- Portable information appliances
- Wireless communication devices
- Any application requiring efficient voltage regulation with minimal power loss and noise.

This component is particularly beneficial in scenarios where space is limited and efficiency is critical, such as in consumer electronics and automotive applications.
