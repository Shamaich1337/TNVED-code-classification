### Component Summary: BAS16-HE3-08 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Non-repetitive peak reverse voltage (V_RM): 100 V
  - Repetitive peak reverse voltage (V_RRM): 75 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average forward current (I_F(AV)): 150 mA
  - Maximum forward current (I_F): 300 mA
  - Peak forward surge current (I_FSM): 1 A (tp = 1 s), 2 A (tp = 1 μs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power dissipation (P_tot): 350 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and storage temperature (T_j, T_stg): -55 to +150 °C
  - Operating temperature (T_op): -55 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-fast switching speed
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (automotive grade)
  - RoHS-compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The BAS16-HE3-08 is a small signal fast switching diode manufactured by Vishay. It is designed for high-speed switching applications and is particularly suitable for surface mount technology (SMT) due to its compact SOT-23 package. The diode features a silicon epitaxial planar construction, which contributes to its high conductance and fast switching capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
The BAS16-HE3-08 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for high-frequency applications where fast switching is essential.
- **Rectification:** Suitable for half-wave rectification in power supply circuits.
- **Clamping and Protection:** Used in clamping circuits to protect sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.

This diode is a versatile component that can be utilized in a wide range of electronic circuits, particularly where space is limited and fast response times are required.
