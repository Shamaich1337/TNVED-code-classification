### Component Summary: KTY82/110

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Not explicitly stated in the provided data, but the sensor operates with a continuous current.
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Continuous sensor current in free air at 25°C: **10 mA**
  - Continuous sensor current in free air at 150°C: **2 mA**
- **Power Consumption**: Not directly specified; however, the recommended operating current is **1 mA** for optimal performance.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: **-55°C to +150°C**
- **Package Type**: **SOT23** (plastic surface-mounted package with 3 leads)
- **Special Features**:
  - High accuracy and reliability
  - Long-term stability
  - Positive temperature coefficient (PTC) with fail-safe behavior
  - Virtually linear characteristics
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The KTY82/110 is a silicon temperature sensor characterized by a positive temperature coefficient of resistance. It is designed for use in measurement and control systems, providing accurate temperature readings. The sensor is encapsulated in a compact SOT23 package, making it suitable for surface mounting in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The KTY82/110 temperature sensor is typically used in:
- **Measurement and Control Systems**: For precise temperature monitoring and control in various industrial and consumer applications.
- **HVAC Systems**: To monitor and control heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.
- **Automotive Applications**: For engine temperature monitoring and other automotive temperature sensing needs.
- **Home Appliances**: In devices such as refrigerators and ovens for temperature regulation.
- **Medical Equipment**: For monitoring patient temperature in medical devices.

This sensor's reliability and accuracy make it a preferred choice in applications requiring precise temperature measurements.
