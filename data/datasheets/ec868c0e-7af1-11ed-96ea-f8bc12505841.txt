### Component Summary for Littelfuse 0448.400MR

**Component Type:** Surface Mount Fuse (NANO2® Fuse, Very Fast-Acting, 448 Series)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Rating:** 125V
- **Current Rating:** 0.400A
- **Power Consumption:** Not applicable (fuses do not consume power; they protect circuits)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (small, square design)
- **Special Features:**
  - Very fast-acting
  - RoHS compliant, Halogen Free, and 100% lead-free
  - Compatible with lead-free solder alloys and higher temperature profiles
  - UL Recognized to UL/CSA/NMX UL 248-1 and UL/CSA/NMX UL 248-14
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Level 1, J-STD-020

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 0448.400MR is a very fast-acting surface mount fuse designed to provide overcurrent protection in electronic circuits. It is part of the NANO2® series, which is characterized by its small size and high reliability. The fuse is constructed from ceramic materials with gold-plated terminations, ensuring excellent conductivity and durability.

#### Typical Applications:
The 0448.400MR fuse is suitable for a wide range of applications, including:
- Notebook PCs
- LCD/PDP TVs and monitors
- LCD backlight inverters
- Portable DVD players
- Power supplies
- Networking equipment
- PC servers
- Cooling fan systems
- Telecom systems
- Wireless base stations
- Game consoles
- Office automation equipment
- Battery charging circuit protection
- Industrial equipment

This fuse is particularly useful in protecting sensitive electronic components from overcurrent conditions, thereby enhancing the reliability and safety of electronic devices.
