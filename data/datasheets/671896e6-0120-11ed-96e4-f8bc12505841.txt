**Description:**
The SYM-18H+ is a high-performance frequency mixer designed for VHF/UHF applications. It operates within a frequency range of 5 to 1800 MHz and is characterized by its low conversion loss and high isolation between the local oscillator (LO), radio frequency (RF), and intermediate frequency (IF) ports. The component is RoHS compliant, indicating it meets environmental standards for hazardous substances.

**Typical Applications:**
The SYM-18H+ is primarily used in:
- VHF/UHF television systems
- Communication systems requiring signal mixing
- RF signal processing applications
- Any application where low conversion loss and high isolation are critical for performance.

This component is suitable for use in various RF and microwave applications, including but not limited to, receivers, transmitters, and signal processing units in telecommunications and broadcasting.
