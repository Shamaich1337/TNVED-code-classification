#### Description:

This component is particularly effective in applications where high voltage protection is critical.

- **Industrial Equipment/Electronics:** Used in machinery and control systems to safeguard sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Telecommunications Electronics:** Protects communication lines and equipment from lightning strikes and other transient voltages.
