- **Telecommunication Devices:** Including FAX machines, modems, and ISDN systems.
- **General Signal Processing:** Where noise reduction is essential for maintaining signal integrity.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive applications due to its high operating temperature range and compliance with automotive standards, making it suitable for use in harsh environments typically found in vehicles.
