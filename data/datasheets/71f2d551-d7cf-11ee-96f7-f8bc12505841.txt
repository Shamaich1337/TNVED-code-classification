### Component Summary: BAR64-02V H6327

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** RF PIN Diode  
**Description:** The BAR64-02V is a low signal distortion, surface mount RF PIN diode optimized for applications requiring low distortion and the ability to handle large RF voltages. It operates effectively at frequencies up to 6 GHz, making it suitable for high-speed data networks and wireless communication.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): -150 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 150 V (at IR = 5 µA)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 100 mA (maximum)
  - Reverse Current (IR): 20 nA (at VR = 20 V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 250 mW (at TS ≤ 125 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SC79 (1.6 mm x 0.8 mm x 0.55 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low signal distortion with a charge carrier lifetime (trr) of 1.55 µs (typical)
  - Ultra-low capacitance (C) of 0.13 pF (typical) at VR = 0 and frequencies ≥ 1 GHz
  - Low forward resistance (RF) of 2.1 Ω (typical) at IF = 10 mA and f = 100 MHz
  - Low inductance (Ls) of 0.6 nH (typical)
  - Pb-free, RoHS compliant, and halogen-free

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The BAR64-02V is a surface mount RF PIN diode designed for applications that require low distortion and the ability to handle high RF voltages. Its construction features a nominal 50 μm I-region width, which contributes to its low forward resistance and distortion characteristics. The diode is particularly effective in high-frequency applications, making it a valuable component in RF switching and signal processing.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Wireless Communication:** Used in devices that require efficient signal switching and low distortion.
- **High-Speed Data Networks:** Ideal for high-speed interface switches and attenuators where signal integrity is critical.
- **RF Switching:** Suitable for applications in RF signal routing and management.

This diode is particularly beneficial in scenarios where low bias current is essential, ensuring minimal signal distortion and high performance in demanding RF environments.
