### Component Summary: VS-40CPQ100-N3

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Reverse Voltage (VR): 100 V
  - Maximum Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 40 A (rectangular waveform, 50% duty cycle at T_C = 145 °C)
  - Maximum Peak One Cycle Non-Repetitive Surge Current (IFSM): 2950 A (tp = 5 μs sine)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 20 A, T_J = 125 °C: 0.61 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to 175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247AC

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Low forward voltage drop and high frequency operation.
  - High purity, high-temperature epoxy encapsulation for enhanced mechanical strength and moisture resistance.
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability.
  - Halogen-free according to IEC 61249-2-21 definition (-N3 only).
  - Designed and qualified according to JEDEC®-JESD47.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description of the Component:**
The VS-40CPQ100-N3 is a high-performance Schottky rectifier optimized for low reverse leakage at high temperatures. It features a common cathode configuration and is designed to operate reliably at junction temperatures up to 175 °C. The proprietary barrier technology used in its construction allows for efficient performance in various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
This component is typically used in:
- Switching power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Freewheeling diodes
- Reverse battery protection circuits

The VS-40CPQ100-N3 is ideal for applications requiring efficient rectification with minimal power loss, particularly in high-temperature environments. Its robust design and high surge current capability make it suitable for demanding electronic applications.
