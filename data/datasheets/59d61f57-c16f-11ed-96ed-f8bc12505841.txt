#### Description:
The RLM-63-2W+ is a broadband RF limiter designed to protect sensitive RF components from excessive input power levels. It operates over a wide frequency range of 30 to 6000 MHz and is capable of handling input power levels from +12 dBm to +32 dBm while maintaining low insertion loss and fast recovery times. This component is particularly useful in applications where signal integrity must be preserved despite varying input power levels.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Military and High-Reliability Applications**: The RLM-63-2W+ is suitable for environments where reliability is critical.
- **Stabilizing Generator Outputs**: It helps maintain consistent output levels from RF generators.
- **Reducing Amplitude Variations**: The limiter can smooth out fluctuations in signal amplitude, ensuring stable performance.
- **Protection of Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs)**: It safeguards sensitive amplifiers and other RF devices from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and input power damage.

This component is ideal for use in RF systems where signal protection and integrity are paramount, making it a valuable addition to various RF applications.
