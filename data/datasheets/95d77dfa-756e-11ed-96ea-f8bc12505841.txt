### Component Summary: CRF0805-FZ-R020ELF

**Manufacturer:** Bourns  
**Product Type:** High Power Current Sense Chip Resistor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Resistance Value:** 0.020 Ω
- **Power Rating @ 70 °C:** 0.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55 °C to +170 °C (derated to zero load at +170 °C)
- **Maximum Working Voltage:** (P x R) 1/2
- **Resistance Tolerance:** ±1%, ±5%
- **Temperature Coefficient:** ±50 PPM/°C
- **Inductance:** Less than 5 nH
- **Low Thermal EMF:** <3 µV/°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified, but compliant with AEC-Q200 and RoHS directives.
- **Package Type:** 0805 (2.0 mm x 1.25 mm)

#### Description:
The CRF0805-FZ-R020ELF is a high power current sense chip resistor designed for precise current measurement in various electronic applications. It features a low resistance value, high power density, and low thermal EMF, making it suitable for applications where accurate current sensing is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supplies:** Used in power management circuits to monitor and control current flow.
- **Stepper Motor Drives:** Essential for feedback control in motor applications to ensure efficient operation.
- **Input Amplifiers:** Utilized in signal processing to maintain signal integrity while measuring current.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications due to its compliance with AEC-Q200 standards, ensuring reliability in harsh environments. Its RoHS compliance also makes it suitable for environmentally conscious designs.
