### Component Summary: CPDT-12V

**Description:**
The CPDT-12V is a Surface Mount Device (SMD) ESD protection diode manufactured by Comchip Technology. It is designed to protect sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) events, ensuring reliability and longevity in electronic circuits.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBD): 12V (typical)
  - Clamping Voltage: 16V (at 1A pulse current)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 25W
  - Leakage Current (IL): 0.117 µA (at 12V)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power rating is not explicitly stated, but the device is designed to handle peak pulse power of 25W.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to 150°C
  - Operating Temperature (TJ): Up to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 standard package, molded plastic.

- **Special Features:**
  - Common anode configuration for ESD protection.
  - IEC61000-4-2 Level 4 ESD protection (8kV contact, 16kV air).
  - High component density suitable for compact designs.
  - RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - According to JEDEC J-STD-020E, the moisture sensitivity level is not explicitly stated in the provided text, but SOT-23 packages typically have a level of 1 or 2.

**Typical Applications:**
The CPDT-12V is commonly used in:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protecting sensitive components in smartphones, tablets, and laptops from ESD.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Safeguarding control circuits and sensors from voltage spikes.
- **Telecommunications:** Ensuring signal integrity in communication devices by preventing ESD damage.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Protecting automotive control systems and infotainment units from transient voltage events.

This component is essential for applications where ESD protection is critical to maintaining the functionality and reliability of electronic devices.
