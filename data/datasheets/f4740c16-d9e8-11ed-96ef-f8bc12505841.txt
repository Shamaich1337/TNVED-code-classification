### Component Summary: ECS-2520MVLC-250-BN-TR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Frequency Range**: 1.000 MHz to 75.000 MHz
- **Frequency Stability**: ±50 ppm (over -40°C to +85°C)
- **Supply Voltage**: 1.6 V to 3.6 V
- **Output Load**: 15 pF (CMOS)
- **Output Voltage Levels**: 
  - VOL: 10% Vdd max
  - VOH: 90% Vdd min
- **Rise & Fall Time**: 7 ns (10% Vdd to 90% Vdd)
- **Start-Up Time**: 5 ms (to 90% Vdd)
- **Phase Jitter**: 150 fS (12 kHz to 20 MHz, F=50 MHz)
- **Duty Cycle**: 45/55% at ½ Vdd
- **Standby Current**: 10 µA
- **Frequency Aging**: ±3 ppm (1st Year at +25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range**: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: 2.5 x 2.0 mm SMD (ceramic)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Component Description:
The ECS-2520MVLC-250-BN-TR is a miniature surface-mount device (SMD) low current CMOS crystal oscillator. It features MultiVolt™ capability, allowing it to operate across a wide voltage range of 1.6V to 3.6V. The oscillator is designed for low power consumption and is suitable for applications requiring precise frequency generation.

#### Typical Applications:
The ECS-2520MVLC-250-BN-TR is ideal for various applications, including:
- **LoRa WAN**: Used in low-power wide-area networks for IoT devices.
- **Low Power/Portable Devices**: Suitable for battery-operated devices where power efficiency is critical.
- **Industrial Applications**: Can be utilized in industrial control systems requiring reliable timing.
- **IoT Applications**: Perfect for Internet of Things devices that need stable frequency references for communication and processing.

This oscillator's compact size, low power consumption, and wide operating temperature range make it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
