### Component Summary: BC847.215

**General Description:**
The BC847 is an NPN general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is housed in a small SOT23 (TO-236AB) surface-mounted plastic package, making it suitable for compact electronic designs.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 45 V (max)
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 50 V (max)
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 6 V (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): 100 mA (max)
  - Peak Collector Current (I_CM): 200 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)
  - Peak Base Current (I_BM): 100 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 250 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB) - 3 leads

- **Special Features:**
  - Available in three different gain selections (BC847, BC847A, BC847B).
  - Suitable for surface mount technology (SMT).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The BC847 is a versatile NPN transistor primarily used for general-purpose switching and amplification tasks in electronic circuits. Its compact SOT23 package allows for easy integration into various applications, particularly in space-constrained environments.

**Typical Applications:**
- General-purpose switching applications, such as driving loads or controlling signals.
- Amplification in audio and signal processing circuits.
- Used in consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and industrial applications where compact size and reliability are essential.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the BC847 transistor, highlighting its key specifications and typical applications, making it suitable for engineers and designers looking to implement this component in their projects.
