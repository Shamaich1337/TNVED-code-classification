### Component Summary: SRP4020TA-2R2M

**Manufacturer:** Bourns Inc.  
**Product Series:** SRP4020TA Series - Shielded Power Inductors

#### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance:** 2.2 µH (±20% tolerance)
- **DC Resistance (DCR):** 45 mΩ (typical), 61 mΩ (maximum)
- **Rated Current (Irms):** 4.0 A (inductance drops 20% at Isat)
- **Saturation Current (Isat):** 6.0 A
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55 °C to +150 °C (temperature rise included)
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type:** Lead Frame
- **Special Features:** 
  - Shielded construction
  - Carbonyl powder core
  - High saturation current
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - AEC-Q200 compliant (suitable for automotive applications)

#### Description:
The SRP4020TA-2R2M is a shielded power inductor designed for use in various electronic applications. It features a carbonyl powder core that provides high saturation current capabilities, making it suitable for power management applications. The shielded construction minimizes electromagnetic interference, which is critical in sensitive electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in DC-DC converters, voltage regulators, and power supply circuits to smooth out current and reduce ripple.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Due to its AEC-Q200 compliance, it is suitable for automotive applications, including infotainment systems and powertrain control modules.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices requiring efficient power conversion and energy storage, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Utilized in various industrial applications where reliable power management is essential.

This inductor is ideal for applications that require compact size, high efficiency, and reliability under varying temperature conditions.
