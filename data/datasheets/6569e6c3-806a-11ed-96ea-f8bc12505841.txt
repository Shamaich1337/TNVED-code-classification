### Component Summary: HMC985ALP4KETR

**Description:**
The HMC985ALP4KETR is a GaAs MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) voltage-variable attenuator designed to operate in the frequency range of 10 to 40 GHz. Its primary function is to control RF signal levels using analog DC control signals, providing a wide dynamic range of attenuation.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Control Voltage (Vctl1, Vctl2): -5V to 0V
  - Absolute Maximum Control Voltage: +0.3V to -6.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input RF Power: 30 dBm
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Not explicitly stated; operates with control voltages.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:**
  - -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 24-lead 4x4 mm SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package
- **Special Features:**
  - Wide bandwidth (10 - 40 GHz)
  - Excellent linearity with +32 dBm Input IP3 (Third Order Intercept Point)
  - Wide attenuation range of 35 dB
  - No external matching required
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC985ALP4KETR is ideal for various applications, including:
- Point-to-point radio communications
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) radio systems
- Test instrumentation
- Microwave sensors
- Military applications, including electronic countermeasures (ECM) and radar systems

The HMC985ALP4KETR is a versatile voltage-variable attenuator suitable for high-frequency applications, providing precise control over RF signal levels with a significant dynamic range.
