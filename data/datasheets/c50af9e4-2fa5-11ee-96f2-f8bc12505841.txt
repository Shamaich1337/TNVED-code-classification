### Component Summary: RB751S-40

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** Small Signal Schottky Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RM): 40V
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 30V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (I_FM): 30mA
  - Non-repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 0.2A (for 8.3ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 150mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (T_j): 125°C
  - Storage Temperature Range (T_stg): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD523
- **Special Features:**
  - Tin plated leads, solderable per J-STD-002 and JESD22-B102
  - Cathode line denotes the cathode end
  - Marking: 4B
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The RB751S-40 is a small signal Schottky diode designed for applications requiring fast switching and low forward voltage drop. Schottky diodes are known for their efficiency in rectification and are commonly used in power management circuits due to their rapid response times.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to rectify and regulate voltage.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for clamping and protection circuits due to its fast switching capabilities.
- **Low Voltage Applications:** Suitable for low voltage and low current applications where efficiency is critical.
- **RF Applications:** Can be utilized in radio frequency applications due to its low capacitance.

This diode is particularly useful in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and other electronic devices where space is limited and efficiency is paramount.
