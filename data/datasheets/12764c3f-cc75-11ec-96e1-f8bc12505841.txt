### Component Summary: C6D10065A

**Description:**
The C6D10065A is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Schottky diode, specifically a Z-Rec® rectifier from Wolfspeed. It is designed for high-efficiency power applications, leveraging the advantages of SiC technology to provide superior performance in terms of voltage drop, recovery characteristics, and thermal management.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 650 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VD): 650 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 
    - 10 A at TC = 155°C
    - 19 A at TC = 125°C
    - 37 A at TC = 25°C
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFRM): 
    - 45 A at TC = 25°C (10 ms pulse)
    - 27 A at TC = 110°C (10 ms pulse)
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 86 A at TC = 25°C (10 ms pulse)
    - 75 A at TC = 110°C (10 ms pulse)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot): 
    - 109 W at TC = 25°C
    - 47 W at TC = 110°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +175°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-220-2

- **Special Features:**
  - Low Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 1.27 V (typical at 10 A, 25°C)
  - Zero Reverse Recovery Current
  - Zero Forward Recovery Voltage
  - Low Leakage Current (Ir): 2 μA (max at 650 V, 25°C)
  - Temperature-Independent Switching Behavior
  - Positive Temperature Coefficient on VF

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

The C6D10065A is suitable for a variety of high-performance power applications, including:
- Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)
- Server and Telecom Power Supplies
- Industrial Power Supplies
- Solar Power Systems
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)

The C6D10065A is a high-efficiency SiC Schottky diode that excels in applications requiring robust performance under high voltage and current conditions. Its low forward voltage drop and zero recovery characteristics make it ideal for modern power management systems, contributing to increased efficiency and reduced thermal management requirements.
