### Component Summary: OP3903D

**Description:**
The OP3903D is a 10G SFP+ (Small Form-factor Pluggable Plus) optical transceiver module designed for high-speed serial optical communication applications. It converts a 10Gb/s serial electrical data stream into a 10Gb/s optical output signal and vice versa. The module utilizes an 850nm VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) transmitter and a PIN (Positive-Intrinsic-Negative) photodetector, making it suitable for Ethernet applications with a maximum link length of 300 meters over multimode fiber (MMF).

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: +3.3V (3.135V to 3.465V)
  - Maximum Supply Voltage: 4V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 250 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - < 1 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: 0°C to 70°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SFP+ Module (Hot Pluggable)
- **Special Features:**
  - Duplex LC connector
  - Digital diagnostic monitoring interface compliant with SFF-8472
  - Supports bit rates from 8.5 to 11.3 Gb/s
  - Maximum link length of 300m on 2000 MHz-km MMF
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
- 10GBASE-SR/SW Ethernet
- SONET OC-192 / SDH STM-64
- 10G Fibre Channel

### Summary:
The OP3903D is a compact and efficient optical transceiver module that facilitates high-speed data transmission in various networking applications. Its hot-pluggable design allows for easy upgrades and maintenance, while its compliance with industry standards ensures compatibility with existing systems. The module is particularly suited for data centers and enterprise networks requiring reliable and high-performance optical communication solutions.
