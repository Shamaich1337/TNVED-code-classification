### Component Summary: HSEC8-130-01-S-DV-A

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Rating**: 240 VAC
- **Current Rating**: 2.8 A (for 2 pins)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: Edge Card Socket
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.
- **Special Features**:
  - Supports 56 Gbps PAM4 performance.
  - Compatible with PCI Express® 3.0, 4.0, and 5.0.
  - Edge Rate® contacts optimized for signal integrity and cycle life.
  - Available in vertical, right-angle, edge mount, and pass-through orientations.
  - Power/signal combo with press-fit tails, rugged weld tabs, locks, and latches.
  - Mating cable assemblies available.

#### Description:
The HSEC8-130-01-S-DV-A is a high-speed edge card socket designed for reliable connections in high-performance applications. It features a pitch of 0.80 mm and can accommodate up to 130 positions. The socket is constructed from black LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) and utilizes BeCu (Beryllium Copper) for its contacts, ensuring durability and excellent electrical performance.

#### Typical Applications:
The HSEC8-130-01-S-DV-A is primarily used in high-speed data communication applications, including:
- **Data Centers**: For connecting high-speed data cards and modules.
- **Telecommunications**: In networking equipment that requires high bandwidth and low latency.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Such as advanced computing devices and gaming consoles that utilize PCI Express interfaces.
- **Industrial Automation**: For connecting various control and data acquisition systems that demand robust and reliable connections.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high mating cycles and excellent signal integrity, making it suitable for modern electronic systems that operate at high speeds.
