### Component Summary: CDRH3D28/LDNP-470NC

**Description:**
The CDRH3D28/LDNP-470NC is a surface-mount (SMD) power inductor manufactured by Sumida. It features a ferrite drum core construction and is magnetically shielded, making it suitable for applications requiring efficient energy storage and management. The inductor is designed to handle DC-DC conversion tasks in compact electronic devices.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Inductance:** 47 μH (±30%)
- **D.C. Resistance (DCR):** 305 mΩ (typical at 20°C), 245 mΩ (maximum at 20°C)
- **Saturation Current:** 0.23 A (when inductance drops to 65% of nominal)
- **Temperature Rise Current:** 0.19 A (with a temperature rise of 40°C at 20°C ambient)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +105°C (including self-temperature rise of the coil)
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40°C to +105°C
- **Solder Reflow Temperature:** 260°C (peak)
- **Package Type:** 4.0 mm x 4.0 mm x 3.0 mm (L x W x H)
- **Weight:** 130 mg (approx.)
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **RoHS Compliance:** Yes

**Typical Applications:**
The CDRH3D28/LDNP-470NC is primarily used in portable electronic devices such as:
- Mobile phones
- Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs)
- MP3 players
- Digital Still Cameras (DSC) and Digital Video Cameras (DVC)
- Portable DVD players

These applications benefit from the inductor's ability to efficiently manage power in DC-DC converter circuits, ensuring stable voltage levels and improved energy efficiency in compact designs.
