#### Description:
The CR0805-FX-3901ELF is a thick film chip resistor from Bourns' CR Series. It is designed for surface mount applications and provides precise resistance values with tight tolerances. The resistor is constructed using a thick film technology that ensures reliability and stability across a wide range of operating conditions.
