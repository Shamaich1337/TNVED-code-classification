#### Description:
The KGM05DR50J226MH is a surface mount ceramic capacitor utilizing an X5R dielectric. It is designed for general-purpose applications, providing stable capacitance over a wide temperature range. The capacitor is characterized by its compact size (0402) and high capacitance value of 22μF, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Decoupling**: Used to filter out noise and stabilize voltage in power supply circuits.
- **Filtering**: Effective in smoothing out voltage fluctuations in power management applications.
- **Signal Coupling**: Can be used in audio and RF applications to couple signals while blocking DC components.
- **Timing Circuits**: Useful in timing applications where stable capacitance is required over temperature variations.

This component is ideal for use in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and any circuit requiring reliable performance in a compact form factor.
