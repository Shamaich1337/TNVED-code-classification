### Component Summary: SI53344-B-GM

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.71V to 3.63V (1.8V, 2.5V, 3.3V)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Core Supply Current (IDD): 280 mA
  - Output Supply Current (per clock output): 21 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Total power consumption can be calculated based on the supply current and voltage.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 32-QFN (5 x 5 mm)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Ultra-low additive jitter: 50 fs rms
  - Built-in LDOs for high PSRR performance
  - Up to 10 LVDS outputs
  - Any-format inputs (LVPECL, LVDS, LVCMOS, etc.)
  - 2:1 input multiplexer
  - RoHS compliant, Pb-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - MSL Level 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SI53344-B-GM** is a low-jitter LVDS fanout clock buffer designed for clock and data distribution applications. It features a universal input that can accept various clock formats, including LVPECL, LVDS, and LVCMOS, making it versatile for different system requirements. The device is capable of providing up to 10 LVDS outputs from a single input, with a wide frequency range from DC to 1250 MHz. 

#### Typical Applications:
The SI53344-B-GM is typically used in:
- **Clock/Data Distribution**: Ideal for distributing clock signals across multiple devices in a system.
- **Redundant Clocking Applications**: Provides reliability in systems requiring backup clock sources.
