- **Package Type:** Molded case, surface-mount

#### Description:
The T55A226M010C0200 is a high-performance polymer surface-mount tantalum capacitor. It features a molded case design, which provides enhanced reliability and stability in various electronic applications. The capacitor utilizes a conductive polymer cathode, resulting in ultra-low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high ripple current capability, making it suitable for high-frequency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Decoupling and Smoothing:** Used in power supply circuits to stabilize voltage and reduce noise.
- **Filtering:** Effective in filtering applications to remove unwanted frequencies from signals.
- **Bulk Energy Storage:** Commonly found in wireless communication devices, computer motherboards, smartphones, and tablets, where it stores energy for quick discharge.
- **Infrastructure Equipment:** Utilized in various infrastructure applications, including networking and storage devices.

This component is ideal for applications requiring compact size, high capacitance, and reliability under varying temperature conditions.
