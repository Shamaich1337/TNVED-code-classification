### Component Summary: TS3USB221ARSER

**Manufacturer:** Texas Instruments  
**Product Code:** TS3USB221A  
**Package Type:** UQFN (2mm x 1.5mm)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.3V to 3.6V
  - I/O Voltage (VI/O): 0V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - ON-state switch current: ±120 mA
  - Continuous current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (active): 30 μA (max)
  - Low Power Mode: 1 μA (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD protection: ±7000V (HBM), ±12000V (I/O to GND)
  - High bandwidth: 900 MHz
  - Low ON-state resistance: RON = 6Ω (max), ΔRON = 0.2Ω (typical)
  - Low bit-to-bit skew and high channel-to-channel noise isolation
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TS3USB221A is a high-speed USB 2.0 (480 Mbps) 1:2 multiplexer and demultiplexer switch designed for switching high-speed USB signals in portable and consumer applications, such as mobile phones, digital cameras, and notebooks. It features a wide bandwidth of 900 MHz, allowing for minimal signal distortion and attenuation. The device is bidirectional and integrates ESD protection on all pins, making it suitable for environments where electrostatic discharge may be a concern.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Signal Routing:** The TS3USB221A is ideal for routing USB signals from a host device to multiple outputs, effectively expanding the number of USB ports available on devices with limited I/O.
- **Mobile Devices:** Used in smartphones and tablets to manage USB connections efficiently.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Applicable in digital cameras and other devices requiring USB connectivity.
- **Notebooks:** Helps in USB I/O expansion for laptops with limited USB ports.

This component is particularly useful in applications where low power consumption is critical, as it can operate in a low-power mode, making it suitable for battery-operated devices.
