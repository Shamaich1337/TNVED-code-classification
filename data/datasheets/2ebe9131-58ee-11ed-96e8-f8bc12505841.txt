### Component Summary: WLBD1608K1U221TB

**Manufacturer:** Walsin Technology Corporation  
**Product Type:** Chip Bead (Inductor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Impedance:** 220 Ω ±25% at 100 MHz
- **DC Resistance:** 0.30 Ω (max)
- **Rated Current:** 550 mA (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +125°C
- **Storage Temperature:** -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:** 1608 (EIA 0603), dimensions 1.6 mm x 0.8 mm
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Level 1 (according to IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D)

#### Description:
The WLBD1608K1U221TB is a chip bead inductor designed for noise reduction in electronic circuits. It features a closed magnetic circuit, which helps in minimizing electromagnetic interference (EMI) and improving signal integrity. This component is particularly effective in filtering applications where high-frequency noise needs to be suppressed.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Noise Reduction:** Used in general electronic circuits to reduce noise on signal and DC lines.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in PCs, networking devices, and other consumer electronics where signal integrity is crucial.
- **Power Management:** Helps in managing power supply lines by filtering out unwanted high-frequency noise.

This component is ideal for applications requiring compact size and effective noise suppression, making it suitable for modern electronic devices that demand high performance in a small footprint.
