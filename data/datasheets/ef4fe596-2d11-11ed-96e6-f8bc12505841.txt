### Component Summary: LM2904VDR2G

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single Supply: 3.0 V to 32 V
  - Split Supplies: ±16 V
  - Input Common Mode Voltage Range: -0.3 V to 32 V
  - Input Differential Voltage Range: ±32 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Source Current: 20 mA (typical)
  - Output Sink Current: 10 mA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: Approximately 0.7 mA to 1.2 mA (depending on supply voltage)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC-8 (D, VD suffix) or Micro8 (DMR2 suffix)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Short Circuit Protected Outputs
  - True Differential Input Stage
  - Internally Compensated
  - ESD Clamps on Inputs for increased ruggedness
  - NCV prefix for automotive applications (AEC-Q100 qualified)
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1

**Description:**
The LM2904VDR2G is a dual operational amplifier designed for low power consumption and versatility in various applications. It features a common mode input voltage range that extends to ground, allowing for single-supply operation without the need for external biasing components. The device is internally compensated, which simplifies its use in various circuit configurations.

**Typical Applications:**
The LM2904VDR2G is commonly used in:
- Signal conditioning and amplification
- Active filters
- Comparators
- Voltage followers
- Data acquisition systems
- Automotive applications (due to its NCV qualification)
- General-purpose analog signal processing

This operational amplifier is particularly suitable for applications requiring low power consumption and the ability to operate over a wide voltage range, making it ideal for battery-powered devices and automotive electronics.
