### Component Summary: HMC3653LP3BETR

**Description:**
The HMC3653LP3BETR is a High Electron Mobility Transistor (HBT) Gain Block MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 7 to 15 GHz. It is packaged in a compact 3x3 mm plastic QFN SMT (Surface Mount Technology) package. This amplifier is optimized for low noise and high gain, making it suitable for various RF applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Vcc (Power Supply Voltage): +5V
  - Vpd (Power Control Voltage): +5V (can be set to 0V for low power mode)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Icc (Vpd = 5V): 40 to 55 mA
  - Icc (Vpd = 0V): 4 to 6 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum power dissipation: 512 mW (at T = 85 °C, derated by 7.87 mW/°C above 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 12-Lead Lead Frame Chip Scale Package (LFCSP), 3 mm x 3 mm body size

- **Special Features:**
  - High output IP3: +28 dBm
  - Low noise figure: 4.0 dB
  - Gain: 15 dB typical
  - Input and output return loss: 14 dB and 8 dB respectively
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC3653LP3BETR is ideal for:
- Point-to-point radios
- Point-to-multipoint radios
- Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) systems
- Local Oscillator (LO) driver for mixers
- Military electronic warfare (EW) and electronic countermeasures (ECM)

This versatile amplifier can be used as a cascadable RF gain stage in 50-ohm applications, making it suitable for various RF and microwave communication systems. Its low noise figure and high gain characteristics make it particularly useful in applications requiring signal amplification with minimal degradation.
