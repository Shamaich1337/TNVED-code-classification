### Description of the Component:

The 02K243-40ME3 is a high-frequency right-angle jack connector designed for PCB mounting. It is part of the RPC-2 series and is mechanically compatible with RPC-3.50 and SMA connectors. The connector is constructed with high-quality materials, including copper beryllium for the center contact and brass for the outer contact, both plated with gold over nickel for enhanced conductivity and corrosion resistance. The dielectric materials used are PEEK and PTFE, which provide excellent thermal and electrical insulation.
