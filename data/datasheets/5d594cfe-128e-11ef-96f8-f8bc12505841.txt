### Component Summary: BAS85 Schottky Barrier Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 30 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 200 mA
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 300 mA (for tp ≤ 1s)
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFSM): 5 A (for tp = 10 ms)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at IF = 100 mA: 800 mV
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to +125 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD80C (hermetically sealed glass surface-mounted package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated guard ring for stress protection
  - Low forward voltage and high breakdown voltage
  - Suitable for automatic placement and can withstand immersion soldering
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SMD packages like SOD80C are rated MSL 1 or 2.

#### Description:
The BAS85 is a planar Schottky barrier diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It features an integrated guard ring that provides stress protection, making it suitable for various electronic applications. The diode is encapsulated in a small, hermetically sealed glass SMD package, which allows for efficient automatic placement in circuit boards and ensures reliability during soldering processes.

#### Typical Applications:
The BAS85 is commonly used in:
- **Ultra High-Speed Switching:** Ideal for applications requiring rapid switching capabilities.
- **Voltage Clamping:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Protection Circuits:** Used in circuits to prevent damage from reverse polarity or over-voltage conditions.
- **Blocking Diodes:** Prevents reverse current flow in power supply circuits.

This diode is particularly useful in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and automotive applications where reliability and performance are critical.
