### Component Summary: BC857C,215 (Nexperia)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): Max -45 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): Max -50 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): Max -5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): Max -100 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (I_CM): Max -200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): Max 250 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): Max 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB) - Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Low current and voltage ratings suitable for general-purpose applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BC857C is a PNP general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is housed in a compact SOT23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The transistor is characterized by its ability to handle low currents (up to 100 mA) and moderate voltages (up to 45 V), making it versatile for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General-Purpose Switching:** The BC857C can be used in various switching applications, such as controlling loads in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.
- **Amplification:** It is suitable for signal amplification in audio and radio frequency applications.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Given its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is ideal for use in automotive applications where reliability and performance are critical.

This transistor is a reliable choice for engineers looking to implement efficient switching and amplification solutions in their designs.
