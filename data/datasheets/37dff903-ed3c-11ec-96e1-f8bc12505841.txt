### Component Summary: RT8096CHGJ5

**Manufacturer:** Richtek Technology Corporation  
**Product Code:** RT8096CHGJ5  
**Description:** The RT8096C is a high-efficiency synchronous step-down DC-DC converter designed to provide a regulated output voltage from a varying input voltage. It is capable of delivering up to 1A of output current, making it suitable for various low-voltage applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.5V to 6V
  - Output Voltage Range: Adjustable from 0.6V to 3.4V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 1A
  - Valley Current Limit: 1.1A to 2A (typical 1.5A)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Efficiency: Up to 95%
  - Quiescent Current: 22 µA (active), 1 µA (shutdown)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 150°C
  - Ambient Temperature: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in TSOT-23-5 and TSOT-23-6 packages

- **Special Features:**
  - CMCOT™ Control Loop Design for optimized transient response
  - Internal soft-start (1.2 ms)
  - Cycle-by-cycle over-current protection
  - Input under-voltage lockout
  - Output under-voltage protection (Hiccup mode)
  - Thermal shutdown protection
  - Power saving at light load

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The RT8096C is a synchronous step-down converter that efficiently converts a higher input voltage to a lower output voltage. It utilizes internal synchronous low on-resistance power switches, which enhance efficiency and eliminate the need for an external Schottky diode. The device operates using a constant on-time, current mode architecture, which allows for fast transient response and robust loop stability.

#### Typical Applications:
- Set-Top Boxes (STB)
- Cable Modems and xDSL Platforms
- LCD TV Power Supplies
- General Purpose Point of Load (POL) applications

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring efficient power management and regulation, especially in consumer electronics and communication devices where space and thermal management are critical.
