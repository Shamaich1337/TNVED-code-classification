### Component Summary: CSD25402Q3A

**Description:**
The CSD25402Q3A is a -20V P-Channel NexFET™ Power MOSFET designed for efficient power management applications. It features ultra-low on-state resistance and low gate charge, making it suitable for high-performance switching applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): -20 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): +12 V / -12 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 25°C: -76 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (Package Limit): -35 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): -148 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at TC = 25°C: 2.8 W
  - Thermal Resistance (RθJA): 55 °C/W (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SON (Small Outline No-lead) 3.3mm x 3.3mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low gate charge (Qg): 7.5 nC (typical at VGS = -4.5V)
  - Low RDS(on): 7.7 mΩ (typical at VGS = -4.5V)
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 (260°C, IR and convection)

**Typical Applications:**
- **DC-DC Converters:** The CSD25402Q3A is ideal for use in DC-DC converter circuits where efficient power conversion is critical.
- **Battery Management:** It can be utilized in battery management systems to control power flow and enhance battery life.
- **Load Switches:** The MOSFET can act as a load switch, enabling or disabling power to various components in a circuit.
- **Battery Protection:** It is suitable for applications requiring protection circuits for batteries, ensuring safe operation under various conditions.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where minimizing power loss and improving thermal performance are essential. Its compact package and high current handling capabilities make it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
