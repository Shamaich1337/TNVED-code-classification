### Component Summary: LM7301IM5X/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: 1.8V to 32V
  - Common Mode Voltage Range: -0.25V to 5.2V
  - Rail-to-Rail Output Swing: 0.07V to 4.93V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 0.6mA (typical)
  - Output Short-Circuit Current: ±20mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Supply Current: 0.6mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (5-pin)

- **Special Features:**
  - Rail-to-Rail Input and Output
  - Gain-Bandwidth Product: 4MHz
  - High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 104dB
  - High Common Mode Rejection Ratio (CMRR): 93dB
  - Excellent Gain: 97dB
  - Capable of driving large capacitive loads

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The LM7301 is a low-power, rail-to-rail input-output operational amplifier designed for a wide range of applications. It features a compact SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The amplifier provides a gain-bandwidth product of 4MHz and operates with a low supply current of only 0.6mA, making it ideal for battery-powered devices. Its rail-to-rail input and output capabilities allow for maximum dynamic range, which is particularly beneficial in low-voltage applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable Instrumentation:** The LM7301 is well-suited for use in handheld devices where low power consumption and compact size are critical.
- **Signal Conditioning Amplifiers/ADC Buffers:** It can be used to amplify signals before they are processed by analog-to-digital converters.
- **Active Filters:** The amplifier can be employed in filter circuits to process signals with minimal distortion.
