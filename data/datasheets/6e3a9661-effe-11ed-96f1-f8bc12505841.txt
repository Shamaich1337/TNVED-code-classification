### Component Summary: RFPA3800TR13

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Product Code:** RFPA3800TR13  
**Type:** GaAs HBT Power Amplifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC and VBIAS) >300MHz: 7.5 V
  - Supply Voltage (VCC and VBIAS) <300MHz: 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Supply Current (IC): 2300 mA
  - Operating Current (Quiescent): 500 mA to 700 mA (typical at VCC=7.0V)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Down Current: 20 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** SOIC-8 (Thermally Enhanced Slug Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - 5W Output Power (P1dB)
  - High Linearity: OIP3 > 48 dBm
  - Low Noise Figure: NF = 3.2 dB at 945 MHz
  - High Efficiency: Up to 50% at P3dB
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** MSL 2

#### Description:
The RFPA3800 is a single-stage GaAs HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) power amplifier designed for high power and high efficiency applications. It is optimized for both linear and saturated operation, making it versatile for various RF applications. The device features excellent thermal performance due to its thermally-enhanced plastic surface-mount slug package, which aids in heat dissipation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Wireless Infrastructure:** Used as a driver for base station amplifiers.
- **Power Amplifier Stages:** Suitable for commercial wireless infrastructure applications.
- **Femtocell and Repeater Applications:** Acts as the final stage power amplifier.
- **High Efficiency, High Power Applications:** Ideal for Class AB operation in LTE and GSM transceiver applications.

The RFPA3800 is particularly well-suited for applications requiring high linearity and low noise, making it an excellent choice for modern communication systems.
