**Manufacturer:** Chipown  
**Product Code:** AP2965

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 6 V to 38 V
  - Output Voltage Range: 3.2 V to 13.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 4.2 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (non-switching): 500 µA to 1 mA
  - Shutdown Supply Current: 1 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to 165 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -55 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOP8-EP (Thermally Enhanced)
- **Special Features:**
  - High Efficiency (up to 95%)
  - Fixed Switching Frequency: 125 kHz
  - Internal Soft Start Circuitry
  - Cable Compensation for Output Voltage Drop
  - Input Under Voltage Lockout (UVLO)
  - Input and Output Over Voltage Protection (OVP)
  - Adjustable Constant Current Limit
  - Output Auto Discharge
  - Cycle by Cycle Peak Current Limit
  - Hiccup Mode Output Under Voltage Protection (UVP)
  - Thermal Shutdown
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The AP2965 is a high-performance synchronous DC-DC buck converter designed for efficient power management. It operates in both Constant Voltage (CV) and Constant Current (CC) modes, making it suitable for applications requiring precise voltage and current regulation. The device can output up to 4.2 A and is particularly effective in scenarios where high efficiency and compact design are critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Car Chargers:** Ideal for automotive applications due to its wide input voltage range and cable compensation features.
