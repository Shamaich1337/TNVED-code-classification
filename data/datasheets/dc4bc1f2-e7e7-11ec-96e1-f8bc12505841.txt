### Component Summary: MADRCC0005

**Manufacturer:** M/A-COM Technology Solutions Inc. (MACOM)  
**Product Code:** MADRCC0005  
**Description:** The MADRCC0005 is a single-channel driver designed to convert TTL control inputs into complementary gate voltages for GaAs FET microwave switches and attenuators. It utilizes high-speed analog CMOS technology, ensuring low power dissipation while operating at moderate to high speeds, making it suitable for various microwave switching applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Positive DC Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5 V to 5.5 V
  - Negative DC Supply Voltage (VEE): -5.5 V to -4.5 V
  - Supply Range (VCC - VEE): 9.0 V to 11.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Output Current (HIGH): Up to -1.0 mA
  - DC Output Current (LOW): Up to 1.0 mA
  - Quiescent Supply Current (ICC): Up to 400 µA

- **Power Consumption:** Low power dissipation, specific values not provided but indicated as low.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:** SOIC-8 (Plastic SOIC Package for SMT Applications)

- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed CMOS technology
  - Complementary outputs
  - Positive voltage control
  - Tape and reel packaging available
  - Lead-free and RoHS compliant
  - 100% matte tin plating over copper
  - Halogen-free “green” mold compound
  - 260°C reflow compatible

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Typical Applications:
The MADRCC0005 is primarily used in microwave switching applications, particularly in systems that require the control of GaAs FET switches and attenuators. Typical applications include:
- Telecommunications
- RF and microwave signal processing
- Automated test equipment
- Signal routing in communication systems

This component is ideal for scenarios where efficient control of microwave signals is necessary, leveraging its low power consumption and high-speed operation.
