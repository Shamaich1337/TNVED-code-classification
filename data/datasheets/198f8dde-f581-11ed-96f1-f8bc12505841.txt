### Component Summary: NPTB00004A

**Manufacturer:** M/A-COM Technology Solutions Inc.  
**Product Code:** NPTB00004A  
**Type:** GaN Power Transistor  
**Primary Function:** RF amplification in various communication applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (V_DS): 100 V (absolute maximum)
  - Gate-Source Voltage (V_GS): -10 to 3 V (absolute maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain Current (I_D,MAX): 1.4 A (pulsed)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Saturated Output Power (P_SAT): 37.1 dBm (5 W) at 2.5 GHz
  - Drain Efficiency: 57% at 4 W output power (2.5 GHz)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): +200°C (maximum)
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC 8-Lead Plastic Package
  
- **Special Features:**
  - GaN on Si HEMT D-Mode Transistor
  - Suitable for linear and saturated applications
  - Tunable from DC to 6 GHz
  - 100% RF tested
  - RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: JEDEC J-STD-020E Level 3

#### Description:
The NPTB00004A is a Gallium Nitride (GaN) High Electron Mobility Transistor (HEMT) designed for RF power amplification. It operates efficiently across a frequency range from DC to 6 GHz, making it suitable for various applications that require high power and efficiency. The device is optimized for continuous wave (CW), pulsed, and linear operation, providing a maximum output power of 5 W.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Defense Communications:** Used in military communication systems for reliable and robust signal transmission.
- **Land Mobile Radio:** Employed in two-way radio systems for effective communication in various environments.
- **Avionics:** Utilized in aircraft communication systems for secure and efficient data transmission.
- **Wireless Infrastructure:** Supports cellular base stations and other wireless communication systems.
- **ISM (Industrial, Scientific, and Medical):** Applicable in devices operating in the ISM bands.
- **VHF/UHF/L/S-Band Radar:** Used in radar systems for detection and ranging applications.

This component is ideal for engineers and designers looking for high-performance RF amplification solutions in demanding environments.
