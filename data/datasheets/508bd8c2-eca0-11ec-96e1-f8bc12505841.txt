#### Description:
The BSX1-900IOV1HA is a current sensor designed for the electronic measurement of various types of currents, including DC, AC, pulsed, and mixed currents. It utilizes the Hall effect for sensing and provides galvanic isolation between the primary circuit (where the current flows) and the secondary circuit (where the measurement is taken). This component is particularly suited for low voltage applications and operates with a unipolar +5VDC power supply.

#### Typical Applications:
The BSX1-900IOV1HA is commonly used in industrial applications, including:
- Standard battery monitoring
- Current sensing in hybrid and electric vehicle (EV) battery packs
- Fuel cell current control
- DC/DC converters and AC/DC inverters
- Motor inverter drives for hybrid and electric vehicles
- Electric power steering (EPS) and x-by-wire applications
- Electric compressors for air conditioning systems

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise current measurement and monitoring, contributing to the efficiency and safety of various electronic systems.
