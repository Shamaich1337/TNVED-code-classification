### Component Summary: LFCN-2250D+

**Description:**
The LFCN-2250D+ is a ceramic low pass filter designed for RF applications. It operates effectively from DC to 2200 MHz, providing excellent performance in filtering out high-frequency noise while allowing lower frequencies to pass through. This component is particularly useful in applications requiring harmonic rejection and signal integrity.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Max DC Voltage at pins 1 & 3: 25 VDC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Current Input to Output: 0.5 A max at 25°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Power Input: 10 W max at 25°C (derates to 3.5 W at 100°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -55°C to 100°C
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to 100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: FV1206

- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS Compliant
  - Excellent power handling capability
  - Temperature stable
  - LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) construction
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 6,943,646

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The LFCN-2250D+ is commonly used in:
- VHF/UHF transmitters and receivers
- Harmonic rejection circuits
- Laboratory equipment for signal processing
- RF communication systems where filtering of unwanted high-frequency signals is critical

This low pass filter is ideal for applications that require a compact size and high reliability in filtering out noise while maintaining signal integrity across a wide frequency range.
