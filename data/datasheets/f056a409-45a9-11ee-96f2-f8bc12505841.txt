  - Supply Input Voltage: Minimum 1.7 V, Maximum 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current: ±5 mA
  - Short-Circuit Current: 180 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current: 3.9 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SC70-3 (DCK)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low Dropout Voltage: 110 mV (typical at ±5 mA load)
  - High Initial Accuracy: ±0.15% (max)
  - Low Temperature Drift: 30 ppm/°C (max)
  - Noise: 35 µVPP (0.1 Hz to 10 Hz)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The REF3312 is a low-power, precision band-gap voltage reference designed for applications requiring a stable reference voltage. It provides a fixed output voltage of 1.25 V with very low dropout voltage, making it suitable for battery-powered devices. The device is available in compact SC70-3 packaging, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable Equipment:** Ideal for devices that require a stable voltage reference while minimizing power consumption.
- **Tablets and Smartphones:** Used in power management circuits to ensure accurate voltage levels.
- **Sensor Modules:** Provides a stable reference voltage for sensor readings.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Ensures precision in measurements by providing a reliable reference voltage.
- **Medical Equipment:** Used in devices where accuracy and low power consumption are critical.
- **Test Equipment:** Provides a stable reference for calibration and testing purposes.

The REF3312 is particularly beneficial in applications where low power consumption and high accuracy are essential, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
