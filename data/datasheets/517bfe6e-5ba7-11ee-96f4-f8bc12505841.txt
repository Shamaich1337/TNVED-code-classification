### Component Summary: LM285DR-1-2

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Reference Voltage (VZ): 1.235V (typical at 25°C)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Operating Current Range: 10 µA to 20 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Very low power consumption, suitable for micropower applications.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOIC (8 pins), D82500
- **Special Features**: 
  - On-chip trimming for tight voltage tolerance.
  - Low noise and long-term stability.
  - Tolerant of capacitive loading.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The LM285DR-1-2 is a micropower, two-terminal band-gap voltage reference. It is designed to provide a stable reference voltage with low noise and excellent temperature stability. The device features on-chip trimming to ensure tight voltage tolerance, making it suitable for precision applications. Its design allows for easy integration into various circuits, particularly where capacitive loading is present.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable Meter References**: Used in devices that require accurate voltage references for measurement.
- **Portable Test Instruments**: Ideal for handheld devices that need reliable voltage references.
- **Battery-Operated Systems**: Its low power consumption makes it suitable for battery-powered applications, extending battery life.
- **Current-Loop Instrumentation**: Can be used in systems that require precise current measurements.
- **General-Purpose Analog Circuitry**: Useful in various analog applications where a stable reference voltage is needed.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where power efficiency is critical, such as in battery-operated devices, while also providing the necessary precision for accurate measurements and control.
