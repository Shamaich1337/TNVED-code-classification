### Component Summary: OPQC01 - 40Gb/s 100m QSFP+ Transceiver

**Manufacturer:** Shenzhen Opway Communication Co., Ltd.

**Description:**
The OPQC01 is a 40Gbps Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable (QSFP+) optical transceiver module designed for high-speed data communication. It features four independent full-duplex channels, each capable of transmitting data at speeds up to 11.2Gbps, resulting in an aggregate bandwidth of over 40Gbps. The module utilizes an MTP/MPO optical connector and operates at a wavelength of 850nm using Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Lasers (VCSELs) for multimode fiber applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: +3.3V (with a range of +3.13V to +3.47V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: Up to 1.0A (typical 0.75A)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1.5W (typical 1.0W)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: 0°C to 70°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - QSFP+ form factor with MTP/MPO connector
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Hot pluggable
  - Digital diagnostic capabilities
  - CML compatible electrical I/O
  - RoHS compliant
  - Capable of over 100m transmission on OM3 multimode fiber and 150m on OM4 multimode fiber
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically QSFP+ modules are rated according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
- Rack-to-rack connections in data centers
- High-speed data transmission in metro networks
- Interconnections for switches and routers
- Infiniband applications (4x SDR, DDR, QDR)

### Conclusion:
The OPQC01 transceiver module is an advanced optical communication component suitable for high-bandwidth applications in data centers and networking environments. Its design allows for efficient data transfer over multimode fiber, making it ideal for modern data communication infrastructures.
