### Component Summary: TBU-CA050-050-WH

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Impulse Voltage: 500 V
  - Continuous AC RMS Voltage: 250 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Trigger Current: 50 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (triggered state): 0.25 to 1.00 mA (depending on voltage)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage: -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DFN (Dual Flat No-lead) package
- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed performance
  - Low capacitance
  - Overcurrent and overvoltage protection
  - Blocks surges up to rated limits
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TBU-CA050-050-WH is a high-speed protector from Bourns' TBU® series, designed to provide robust protection against electrical surges, including those caused by short circuits, AC power cross, induction, and lightning. Utilizing MOSFET semiconductor technology, this device monitors current and activates to protect sensitive electronics from high voltage and current during surge events. It operates in approximately 1 microsecond, transitioning from a normal operating state to a protected state when the line current exceeds the trigger current.

#### Typical Applications:
The TBU-CA050-050-WH is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Voice and VDSL cards
- Protection modules and dongles
- Process control equipment
- Test and measurement equipment
- General electronics

This component is particularly useful in environments where electrical surges are a concern, providing a reliable barrier to protect sensitive components in various electronic systems.
