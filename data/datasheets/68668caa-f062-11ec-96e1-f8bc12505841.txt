### Component Summary: 1SMB5921BT3G

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Zener Voltage Range: 6.8V (Nominal) to 7.14V (Max)
- **Current Ratings:** Maximum DC Zener Current (IZM): 5.2 mA
- **Power Consumption:** Maximum Steady State Power Dissipation: 4.4W at 25°C, derating above 25°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** SMB (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Rating of Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model
  - AEC−Q101 Qualified and PPAP Capable for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free Device
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The 1SMB5921BT3G is a Zener voltage regulator diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is part of the 1SMB59xxBT3G series, which offers a wide range of Zener voltages from 3.3V to 200V. This component is specifically designed to provide stable voltage regulation and protect circuits from overvoltage conditions.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power management circuits to maintain a constant voltage level.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protects sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly used in devices requiring stable voltage supply for operational integrity.

This component is ideal for applications where precise voltage regulation is necessary, and its robust design ensures reliability in various operating conditions.
