### Component Summary: LM337LMX/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Output Voltage Range: -1.2V to -37V
  - Maximum Input-Output Voltage Differential: 40V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Output Current: 100 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Internally limited power dissipation
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -25°C to 125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Available in TO-92 and SOIC (8-pin) packages
- **Special Features**: 
  - Adjustable output voltage
  - Line regulation: Typically 0.01%/V
  - Load regulation: Typically 0.1%
  - Short circuit protection
  - Thermal overload protection
  - Ripple rejection: 80 dB
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **LM337L** is a 3-terminal adjustable negative voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage ranging from -1.2V to -37V. It is capable of delivering up to 100 mA of output current and is particularly noted for its ease of use, requiring only two external resistors to set the desired output voltage. The device features built-in overload protection, including current limiting and thermal shutdown, ensuring reliable operation under various conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The LM337L is widely used in applications requiring adjustable negative voltage supplies. Typical applications include:
- **Industrial Power Supplies**: Providing regulated negative voltages for various industrial equipment.
- **Factory Automation Systems**: Used in control systems that require precise voltage regulation.
- **Building Automation Systems**: For powering sensors and control circuits.
- **PLC Systems**: Supplying power to programmable logic controllers.
- **Instrumentation**: Used in measurement and testing equipment that requires stable negative voltages.
- **IGBT Drive Negative Gate Supplies**: Providing necessary gate drive voltages for IGBT devices.
- **Networking Equipment**: Powering various components that require negative voltage.
- **Set-Top Boxes**: Used in consumer electronics for voltage regulation.

This component is ideal for applications where adjustable voltage levels are necessary, and it can also be configured for use as a precision current regulator.
