  - DIP-16 metal casing
- **Special Features:**
  - 6-side shielded metal case with insulated base plate

  - Current limitation and short circuit protection
  - 3-year product warranty

  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The TEL10-4813 is a compact, isolated DC/DC converter from the TEL 10 series by Traco Power, designed to deliver 10 Watts of output power in a DIP-16 metal package. It features a wide input voltage range of 36 to 75 VDC, making it suitable for various applications requiring reliable voltage conversion. The converter is engineered for high efficiency, achieving up to 88% efficiency, which minimizes thermal losses and allows for operation at elevated temperatures.

- **IT Equipment:** Powering servers, routers, and other networking devices.
- **Industrial Electronics:** Supplying power in automation and control systems.
- **Telecommunications:** Ensuring reliable power for communication devices and systems.
