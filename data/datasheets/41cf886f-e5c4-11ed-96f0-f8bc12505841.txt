### Component Summary: LS38-A3U-TI-N-D3

**Description:**
The LS38-A3U-TI-N-D3 is a single-mode bi-directional SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceiver designed for optical communication applications. It operates at a wavelength of 1310 nm for transmission and 1550 nm for reception, making it suitable for various networking standards including SONET OC-3 and Fast Ethernet. This component features diagnostic monitoring capabilities, allowing for real-time performance assessment.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 3.1 to 3.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 to 4.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: Up to 400 mA
  - Supply Current (TX + RX): Up to 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is approximately 1.32 W (calculated from supply voltage and current).

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) with LC Simplex Connector

- **Special Features:**
  - Hot pluggable
  - Class 1 laser product compliant with EN 60825-1
  - TTL signal detect indicator
  - Diagnostic monitoring capabilities

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The LS38-A3U-TI-N-D3 is typically used in:
- Telecommunications for SONET/SDH networks
- High-speed data communication in Fast Ethernet applications
- Optical networking systems requiring bi-directional data transmission
- Data centers and enterprise networks for interconnecting switches and routers

This component is ideal for applications that require reliable and efficient data transmission over long distances, leveraging its 60 km range capability. Its diagnostic features also make it suitable for monitoring and maintaining network performance.
