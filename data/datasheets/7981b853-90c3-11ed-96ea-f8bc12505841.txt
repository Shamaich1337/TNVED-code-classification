### Component Summary: ADG601BRTZ-REEL7

**Description:**
The ADG601BRTZ-REEL7 is a monolithic CMOS single-pole single-throw (SPST) switch designed for low on-resistance applications. It is primarily used to switch analog signals in various electronic systems. The ADG601 is a normally open (NO) switch, making it suitable for applications where the switch should be closed only when activated.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Dual Supply: ±2.7 V to ±5.5 V
  - Single Supply: +2.7 V to +5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current: 100 mA
  - Peak Current (Pulsed at 1 ms, 10% Duty Cycle Max): 200 mA
