### Component Summary: SMV1430-079LF

**Manufacturer:** Skyworks Solutions, Inc.  
**Product Code:** SMV1430-079LF  
**Component Type:** Varactor Diode (Silicon Abrupt Junction Tuning Varactor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 30 V (maximum)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 20 mA (maximum)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 250 mW (maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_OP): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SC-79 (Single)
- **Special Features:**
  - High Q factor for low phase noise
  - Low series resistance
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): MSL1, rated for 260 °C per JEDEC J-STD-020
  - Halogen-free and RoHS-compliant (Skyworks Green™)

#### Description:
The SMV1430-079LF is part of the SMV1405 to SMV1430 series of silicon abrupt junction varactor diodes. These diodes are specifically designed for use in Voltage Controlled Oscillators (VCOs) that require tight capacitance tolerances. The low series resistance of these varactors makes them suitable for high-Q resonators in wireless systems, particularly at frequencies exceeding 10 GHz. The series is characterized for capacitance over temperature, ensuring reliable performance across varying conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Wireless Communication:** Used in VCOs for RF applications, enabling frequency modulation and signal generation.
- **High-Q Resonators:** Ideal for applications requiring high quality factors to minimize phase noise.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices that utilize RF technology, such as mobile phones and wireless transmitters.
- **Telecommunications:** Employed in systems that require precise frequency control and stability.

This varactor diode is essential for applications where precise tuning and low noise are critical, making it a valuable component in modern electronic designs.
