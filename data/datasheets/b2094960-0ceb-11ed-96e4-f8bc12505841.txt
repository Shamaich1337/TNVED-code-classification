### Component Summary: BAT54C Schottky Barrier Diode

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** BAT54C  
**Type:** Schottky Barrier Diode  
**Package Type:** SOT-23

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Continuous Reverse Voltage (V_R): 30 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (I_F): 200 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 0.6 A (for t < 1s)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_D): 200 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature Range (T_stg): -55°C to +125°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Junction to Ambient (R_θJA): 500 °C/W
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** 
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low Forward Voltage
  - Surface Mount Device
  - Epoxy meets UL-94 V-0 flammability rating, halogen-free

#### Description:
The BAT54C is a Schottky barrier diode designed for high-speed switching applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching speeds, making them ideal for applications where efficiency and speed are critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to rectify and regulate voltage.
- **Signal Processing:** Employed in RF applications for signal detection and mixing.
- **Data Storage:** Utilized in memory circuits and data storage devices to prevent backflow of current.
- **General Purpose Switching:** Suitable for various electronic circuits requiring fast switching capabilities.

This diode is particularly useful in applications where low voltage drop and fast response times are essential, such as in power converters, battery chargers, and other electronic devices that require efficient power management.
