### Component Summary: VSMB2020X01

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 100 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (I_FM): 200 mA (t_p/T = 0.5, t_p = 100 μs)
  - Surge Forward Current (I_FSM): 1 A (t_p = 100 μs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_V): 160 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -40 °C to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -40 °C to +100 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount Device (SMD)
  - Package Form: Gullwing
  - Dimensions: 2.3 mm x 2.3 mm x 2.8 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - High radiant power and intensity
  - Low forward voltage
  - Suitable for high pulse current operation
  - AEC-Q101 qualified
  - Compliant with RoHS Directive 2002/95/EC
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL 2a, according to J-STD-020

#### Description:
The VSMB2020X01 is a high-speed infrared emitting diode (IRED) that operates at a peak wavelength of 940 nm. It is constructed using Gallium Aluminum Arsenide (GaAlAs) double heterostructure technology, which allows for high radiant power and intensity. The device is molded in a clear, un-tinted plastic package suitable for surface mounting, making it ideal for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The VSMB2020X01 is commonly used in:
- **IrDA Compatible Data Transmission:** Facilitating wireless communication between devices.
- **Miniature Light Barriers:** Used in safety and detection systems.
- **Photointerrupters:** For detecting the presence or absence of an object.
- **Optical Switches:** Enabling control in various electronic circuits.
- **Control and Drive Circuits:** Used in automation and control systems.
- **Shaft Encoders:** For position sensing in motors and machinery.

This component is particularly valuable in applications requiring reliable and efficient infrared light emission, making it suitable for both consumer electronics and industrial applications.
