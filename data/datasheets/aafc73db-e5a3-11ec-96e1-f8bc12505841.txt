### Component Summary: HI-1579PST

**Manufacturer:** Holt Integrated Circuits Inc.  
**Product Code:** HI-1579PST

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.15V to 3.45V (typical 3.3V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Total Supply Current (Not Transmitting): 4 mA to 10 mA
  - Total Supply Current (Transmitting one bus at 50% duty cycle): 225 mA to 300 mA
  - Total Supply Current (Transmitting one bus at 100% duty cycle): Up to 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Not Transmitting): 0.06 W
  - Power Dissipation (Transmitting one bus at 100% duty cycle): Up to 0.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C
  - Extended: -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 44-pin Plastic Chip-Scale Package (QFN)

- **Special Features:**
  - Compliant with MIL-STD-1553A/B, MIL-STD-1760, and ARINC 708A
  - Low power CMOS design
  - Integrated transmitter inhibit control signals
  - Small footprint (7mm x 7mm)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1 (not moisture sensitive)

#### Description:
The HI-1579PST is a low-power CMOS dual transceiver designed for applications that require compliance with the MIL-STD-1553 specification. It features a transmitter section that converts complementary CMOS/TTL Manchester II bi-phase data into differential voltages suitable for driving a bus isolation transformer. The receiver section converts bi-phase differential data from the MIL-STD-1553 bus back into complementary CMOS/TTL data for input into a Manchester decoder. The device is designed to minimize package size by internally connecting transmitter outputs to receiver inputs, allowing for a simplified connection to coupling transformers.

#### Typical Applications:
The HI-1579PST is primarily used in military and aerospace applications where reliable data communication is critical. Typical applications include:
- **Data Bus Communication:** Used in systems that require robust communication protocols such as MIL-STD-1553 for avionics and military vehicles.
- **Signal Processing:** Facilitates the transmission and reception of data in harsh environments.
- **Control Systems:** Integrated into control systems for aircraft and military equipment to ensure reliable data transfer.

This component is ideal for applications that demand high reliability and compliance with military standards, making it suitable for use in defense and aerospace industries.
