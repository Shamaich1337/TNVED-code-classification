### Component Summary: SBB4089Z

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Product Code:** SBB4089Z  
**Description:** The SBB4089Z is a high-performance InGaP HBT MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) amplifier designed for use in various RF applications. It utilizes a Darlington configuration with an active bias network, providing stable current over temperature and process variations. This amplifier is optimized for high linearity gain block applications and is internally matched to 50 Ω.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Device Voltage (V_CC): +5V (max +5.5V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Device Current (I_CC): 70 mA (typical), 100 mA (max)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.55 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -55 to +150 °C
  - Case Temperature (T_CASE): -40 to +105 °C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): +150 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 3-pin SOT-89 package
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Operational Frequency Range: 50 MHz to 6000 MHz
  - OIP3: +35.2 dBm at 1950 MHz
  - P1dB: +19.3 dBm at 1950 MHz
  - Robust ESD protection: 1000V, Class 1C
  - Patented thermal design and bias circuit
  - Low thermal resistance
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 3 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020

#### Typical Applications:
The SBB4089Z is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- PA (Power Amplifier) Driver Amplifier
- Cellular communication (GSM, UMTS, PCS)
- Wideband instrumentation
- Wireless data transmission
- Satellite terminals

### Conclusion:
The SBB4089Z is a versatile and efficient RF amplifier designed for high-performance applications in telecommunications and data transmission. Its compact size, low power consumption, and robust design make it ideal for modern wireless systems.
