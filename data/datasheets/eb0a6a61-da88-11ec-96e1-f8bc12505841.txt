### Component Summary: IRS20957STRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** IRS20957STRPBF  
**Type:** High Voltage, High Speed MOSFET Driver  
**Primary Function:** Designed for Class D audio amplifier applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - High Side Floating Supply Voltage (VB): -0.3V to 215V
  - High Side Floating Supply Voltage Offset (VS): VB - 15V to VB + 0.3V
  - Low Side Fixed Supply Voltage (VCC): 10V to 15V
  - PWM Input Voltage (VIN): VSS - 0.3V to VDD + 0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output High Short Circuit Current (Io+): 1.0 A
  - Output Low Short Circuit Current (Io-): 1.2 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQCC): Up to 3 mA
  - Quiescent Current (IQBS): Up to 1 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to 125°C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOIC-16N (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Programmable bidirectional over-current protection with self-reset function.
  - Programmable preset dead-time for improved Total Harmonic Distortion (THD) performance.
  - High noise immunity and compatibility with 3.3V/5V logic inputs.
  - Operates up to 800 kHz.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL2 (260°C per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)

#### Description:
The IRS20957S is a high voltage, high-speed MOSFET driver specifically designed for Class D audio amplifier applications. It features a floating PWM input that simplifies half-bridge implementations. The device includes built-in protection mechanisms for over-current conditions, allowing for safe operation without the need for external shunt resistors. The programmable dead-time generation enhances audio performance by minimizing distortion and noise.

#### Typical Applications:
- Home theater systems
- Mini component stereo systems
- Powered speaker systems
- General-purpose audio power amplifiers

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management in audio amplification, providing robust performance and protection features that enhance audio quality and reliability.
