### Component Summary: MMSZ5246BT1G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Zener Voltage (VZ) nominal: 15.20V to 16.80V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Reverse Current (IZT): 7.8 mA
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.1 mA at VR
- **Power Consumption**: Maximum Power Dissipation (PD): 500 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type**: SOD-123 (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features**: 
  - ESD Rating: Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model
  - Pb-Free Device
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The MMSZ5246BT1G is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is part of the MMSZ52xxxT1G series of Zener voltage regulators, which are available in a compact SOD-123 surface mount package. This component is specifically engineered to provide stable voltage reference and regulation in electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation**: Used to maintain a constant voltage level in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Clamping**: Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Reference Voltage**: Provides a stable reference voltage for various electronic applications.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical.

This Zener diode is ideal for applications requiring medium current handling and is particularly useful in high-density circuit designs due to its small package size.
