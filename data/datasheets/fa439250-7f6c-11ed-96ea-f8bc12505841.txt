### Component Summary: AD820ARZ-REEL

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single-supply: 5 V to 36 V
  - Dual-supply: ±2.5 V to ±18 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Minimum output current: 15 mA
  - Quiescent current: 800 μA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum power consumption is determined by quiescent current and supply voltage.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial temperature range: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Lead SOIC_N (R-8)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Rail-to-rail output
  - True single-supply operation
  - Input voltage range extends below ground
  - Excellent load drive capability (up to 350 pF capacitive load)
  - Low noise: 13 nV/√Hz @ 10 kHz
  - Input offset voltage: 800 μV max
  - Input bias current: 25 pA max
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Description of the Component:

The **AD820** is a precision, low-power, FET-input operational amplifier (op-amp) designed for single-supply applications. It features true single-supply capability, allowing it to operate with input signals that extend below ground. The output can swing rail-to-rail, providing a maximum dynamic range. The AD820 is characterized by low noise, low input bias current, and excellent DC and AC performance, making it suitable for a variety of applications.

### Typical Applications:

The AD820 is commonly used in:
- **Battery-powered precision instrumentation:** Its low power consumption and precision make it ideal for portable devices.
- **Photodiode preamps:** The low noise and high input impedance are beneficial for amplifying signals from photodiodes.
- **Active filters:** Its high input impedance allows for the design of low-frequency filters with minimal signal distortion.
- **Data acquisition systems:** The op-amp's precision and low noise characteristics are suitable for 12-bit to 14-bit data acquisition.
- **Medical instrumentation:** Its accuracy and reliability make it suitable for various medical devices.
- **Low power references and regulators:** The AD820 can be used in reference circuits due to its low dropout performance.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the AD820ARZ-REEL, highlighting its specifications, functionality, and applications in electronic systems.
