  - -40°C to +105°C

  - Through-Hole Technology (THT) type

- **Special Features:**
  - High ripple current capability
  - Long life endurance: 10,000 hours at 105°C
  - High reliability
  - Wide voltage range (10V to 450V DC)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for capacitors.

#### Description:
The KH220M450J200A is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's KH series, designed for high voltage applications. It features a rated voltage of 450V DC and is capable of operating in a wide temperature range from -40°C to +105°C. This capacitor is particularly noted for its long life expectancy of up to 10,000 hours at high temperatures, making it suitable for demanding applications.

- **Industrial Equipment:** In machinery and control systems where reliability is critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** In devices requiring stable power delivery and energy storage.
- **Automotive Applications:** For use in electronic control units and other automotive electronics.
- **Renewable Energy Systems:** Such as inverters for solar power systems.
