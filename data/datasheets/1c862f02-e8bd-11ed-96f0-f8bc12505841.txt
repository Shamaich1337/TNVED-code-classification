### Component Summary: APA2106CGCK

**Description:**
The APA2106CGCK is a green surface mount LED (Light Emitting Diode) manufactured by Kingbright. It features a right-angle design, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. The LED is constructed using AlGaInP on a GaAs substrate, providing efficient light emission with low power consumption.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF): 2.1V (typical) to 2.5V (maximum) at 20mA
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 30mA
  - Peak Forward Current: 150mA (1/10 Duty Cycle, 0.1ms Pulse Width)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 75mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 2.1mm x 1.0mm x 0.6mm (Right Angle SMD LED)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (140°)
  - Tinned pads for improved solderability
  - RoHS compliant

**Typical Applications:**
The APA2106CGCK is ideal for various applications, including:
- **Backlighting:** Used in displays and panels to provide illumination.
- **Indicators:** Suitable for status indicators in consumer electronics, automotive dashboards, and appliances.
- **Signal Processing:** Can be utilized in communication devices for visual signaling.

This LED is particularly advantageous in designs requiring compact components with efficient light output and low energy consumption.
