### Component Summary: MBRA130LT3G

**Description:**
The MBRA130LT3G is a surface mount Schottky power rectifier designed for low voltage, high frequency applications. It utilizes the Schottky Barrier principle in a metal-to-silicon configuration, featuring epitaxial construction with oxide passivation and metal overlay contact. This component is particularly suited for use in switching power supplies, free wheeling diodes, and polarity protection diodes.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 30 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage: 30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IO): 1.0 A (at TC = 105 °C)
  - Peak Repetitive Forward Current (IFRM): 2.0 A (at TC = 105 °C)
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 25 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 1.0 A: 0.41 V (TJ = 25 °C)
  - Maximum Instantaneous Reverse Current (IR) at 30 V: 1.0 mA (TJ = 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMA (Surface Mount Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Compact package with J-bend leads for automated handling.
  - Highly stable oxide passivated junction.
  - Guardring for over-voltage protection.
  - Low forward voltage drop.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The MBRA130LT3G is commonly used in:
- Low voltage, high frequency switching power supplies.
- Free wheeling diodes in power conversion circuits.
- Polarity protection diodes to prevent reverse voltage damage.
- Automotive applications requiring robust performance and reliability.

This component is ideal for applications where efficiency and thermal performance are critical, making it suitable for modern electronic devices and systems.
