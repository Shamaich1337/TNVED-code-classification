### Component Summary: TLV1117LV33DCYR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2V to 5.5V (Absolute Maximum VIN: 6V)
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Fixed at 3.3V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (IOUT): Up to 1A
  - Minimum Ensured Current Limit: 1.1A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQ): 100 µA (maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-223 (4 pins)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low Dropout Voltage: 455 mV at 1A for VOUT = 3.3V
  - High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 65 dB at 1 kHz
  - Stable with 0 mA output current (no minimum load requirement)
  - Thermal shutdown and overcurrent protection
  - Internally compensated for stability with low ESR ceramic capacitors
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TLV1117LV33DCYR is a low-dropout (LDO) linear voltage regulator designed to provide a fixed output voltage of 3.3V with a maximum output current of 1A. It is part of the TLV1117LV series, which is optimized for low quiescent current consumption, making it suitable for battery-powered applications. The device features a precision bandgap reference and an error amplifier that ensures a typical output accuracy of 1.5%. 

#### Typical Applications:
The TLV1117LV33DCYR is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Set-top boxes**
- **Televisions and monitors**
- **PC peripherals, notebooks, and motherboards**
- **Modems and other communication devices**
- **Post-regulation for switching power supplies**

This LDO is particularly advantageous in applications requiring low standby current and the ability to handle both small and large load currents efficiently. Its low dropout voltage and high PSRR make it ideal for sensitive power supply rails in electronic devices.
