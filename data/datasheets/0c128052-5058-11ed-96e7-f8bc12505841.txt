**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** Smart Power High-Side Switch (N-channel MOSFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (Vbb): 4.75 V to 41 V
  - Maximum Supply Voltage: 43 V
  - Load Dump Protection: Up to 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Nominal Load Current (IL): 21 A
  - Current Limitation (IL(lim)): 65 A
  - Short-Circuit Current: Self-limited

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Standby Current: 5 µA (typical at Tj = -40...+25°C)
  - Operating Current (Pin 1): 2 to 4 mA (VIN = 5 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PG-TO263-5 (SMD package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated protection functions including short circuit, current limitation, overload, thermal shutdown, and overvoltage protection.
  - Diagnostic feedback with open drain output.
  - Green Product (RoHS compliant) and AEC Qualified.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BTS441RG is a high-side switch designed for automotive applications, utilizing Smart SIPMOS technology. It features a low on-state resistance of 20 mΩ, making it efficient for controlling various loads. The device is designed to be compatible with microcontrollers and CMOS inputs, providing a robust solution for switching applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in automotive systems for controlling power to various components.
- **Load Switching:** Suitable for resistive, inductive, and capacitive loads, including lamps and motors.
- **Relay Replacement:** Acts as a solid-state alternative to electromechanical relays and fuses, enhancing reliability and reducing size.
- **High Inrush Current Loads:** Ideal for applications where loads may experience high inrush currents.

This component is particularly well-suited for automotive environments due to its robust protection features and compliance with automotive standards.
