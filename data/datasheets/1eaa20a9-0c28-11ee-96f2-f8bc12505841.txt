- **Capacitance Tolerance**: ±5%, ±10%, ±20%
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: 0402 (1.0 mm x 0.5 mm)

  - Halogen-free
  - Nickel-barrier end termination
  - Supplied in tape on reel for automated assembly

#### Description:
The CC0402KRX7R9BB103 is a surface-mount ceramic multilayer capacitor (MLCC) from YAGEO, designed for general-purpose and high-capacitance applications. It utilizes X7R dielectric material, which provides stable capacitance over a wide temperature range and varying voltage conditions. The capacitor is characterized by its small footprint, making it suitable for compact electronic designs.

#### Typical Applications:
This capacitor is commonly used in various electronic devices, including:
- **Consumer Electronics**: PCs, hard disks, game consoles, DVDs, and video cameras.
- **Mobile Devices**: Mobile phones and tablets.
- **Data Processing**: Applications requiring stable capacitance for filtering, decoupling, and energy storage.
- **Power Management**: Used in power supply circuits to smooth voltage fluctuations and improve overall circuit stability.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited, and reliability is critical, making it a popular choice in modern electronic designs.
