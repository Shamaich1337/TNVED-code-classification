### Component Summary: MMBTH81 (ON Semiconductor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 20 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 20 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 3.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 50 mA (Continuous)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD): 350 mW (derated to 225 mW above 25°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in TO-92 and SOT-23 packages

- **Special Features:**
  - Designed for general RF amplifier and mixer applications up to 250 MHz.
  - High current gain-bandwidth product (fT) of 600 MHz at 5 mA collector current.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The MMBTH81 is a PNP RF transistor designed primarily for use in radio frequency (RF) applications, including amplifiers and mixers. It operates effectively at frequencies up to 250 MHz and is suitable for low-power applications with collector currents ranging from 1.0 mA to 30 mA. The device is sourced from Process 75, ensuring reliability and performance in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The MMBTH81 is commonly used in:
- **RF Amplifiers:** Enhancing weak signals in communication devices.
- **Mixers:** Combining signals in RF applications for modulation and demodulation.
- **General Purpose Switching:** Suitable for low-power switching applications in consumer electronics.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in circuits that require amplification of analog signals.

This transistor is ideal for applications in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and other RF-related fields where compact size and efficient performance are critical.
