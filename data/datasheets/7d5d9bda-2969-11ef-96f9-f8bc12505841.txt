### Component Summary: CDBU0230L-HF

**Component Type:** Schottky Barrier Diode

**Manufacturer:** Comchip Technology

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 30 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Surge Current (IFSM): 8.3 A (single half sine-wave, 8.3 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 150 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -40 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -40 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 0603/SOD-523F (Surface Mount Device)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - Extremely thin and leadless package
  - Majority carrier conduction
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The CDBU0230L-HF is a Schottky barrier diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It features a low forward voltage drop, which enhances efficiency in power management circuits. The diode's construction allows for rapid switching, making it suitable for applications where fast response times are critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to rectify and regulate voltage.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for RF applications due to its fast switching capabilities.
- **Data Storage:** Utilized in circuits for data protection and signal integrity.
- **General Purpose Rectification:** Commonly found in various electronic devices for rectifying AC to DC.

This diode is particularly advantageous in compact electronic designs due to its small footprint and low profile, making it suitable for modern surface-mount technology (SMT) applications.
