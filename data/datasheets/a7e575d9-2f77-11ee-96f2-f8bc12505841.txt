**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** MUR1620FCT  
**Type:** Ultra-Fast Recovery Diode (FRED)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 16 A (at 60Hz sine wave)
  - Surge (Non-repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM): 100 A (1 cycle, Tj=25°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +175°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +175°C
- **Package Type:** ITO-220AB
- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage drop (0.90 V at 8 A, Tj=25°C)
  - Fast reverse recovery time (Trr: 25 ns typical)
  - High frequency operation
  - High purity, high-temperature epoxy encapsulation for enhanced mechanical strength and moisture resistance
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The MUR1620FCT is an ultra-fast recovery diode designed for high-efficiency applications. It features a low forward voltage drop and fast reverse recovery time, making it suitable for high-frequency operations. The diode is encapsulated in a robust ITO-220AB package, which provides excellent thermal performance and mechanical strength.

#### Typical Applications:
The MUR1620FCT is commonly used in:
- Switching power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Freewheeling diodes
- Reverse battery protection circuits

These applications benefit from the diode's fast switching capabilities and high current handling, making it ideal for power management and signal processing tasks in various electronic devices.
