### Component Summary: ASM712H

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.

**Component Type:** ESD Protection Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 12V (Pin 1 to Pin 3 and Pin 2 to Pin 3), 7V (Pin 3 to Pin 1 and Pin 3 to Pin 2)
  - Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 13.3V (Pin 1 to Pin 3 and Pin 2 to Pin 3), 7.5V (Pin 3 to Pin 1 and Pin 3 to Pin 2)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 30A (Pin 1 or 2 to Pin 3), 38A (Pin 3 to Pin 1 or 2)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 600-1000W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:** Not specified in the provided text, but typically in a standard diode package.

- **Special Features:**
  - Low clamping voltage
  - Low leakage current (Reverse Leakage Current: 0.5 µA at VRWM)
  - Compliant with RoHS
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 3 per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The ASM712H is an ESD protection diode designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) events. It features low clamping voltage and low leakage current, making it suitable for protecting circuits from high-voltage transients.

#### Typical Applications:
- **ESD Protection:** The primary function of the ASM712H is to protect sensitive electronic devices from ESD, which can occur during handling or operation.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices to ensure reliability and longevity.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Protects control circuits and communication lines in industrial applications.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in automotive applications to protect against ESD events that can occur due to the vehicle's environment.

This component is essential in applications where electronic devices are exposed to potential ESD threats, ensuring their functionality and reliability in various environments.
