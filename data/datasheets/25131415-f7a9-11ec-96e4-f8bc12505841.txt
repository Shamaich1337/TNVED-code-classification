### Component Summary: FDMS7658AS

**Description:**
The FDMS7658AS is an N-Channel PowerTrench® SyncFET™ designed for power conversion applications. It features a low on-resistance and high efficiency, making it suitable for synchronous rectification. The device integrates a Schottky body diode, which enhances its performance in various applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain to Source Voltage (VDS): 30 V
  - Gate to Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 25 °C: 176 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at TC = 100 °C: 112 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at TA = 25 °C: 29 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current: 670 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at TC = 25 °C: 89 W
  - Power Dissipation at TA = 25 °C: 2.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Power 56 package

- **Special Features:**
  - Maximum rDS(on): 1.9 mΩ at VGS = 10 V, ID = 28 A
  - SyncFET™ Schottky Body Diode
  - MSL1 (Moisture Sensitivity Level 1) per JEDEC J-STD-020E
  - 100% UIL Tested
  - RoHS Compliant

### Typical Applications:
The FDMS7658AS is commonly used in:
- Synchronous rectifiers for DC/DC converters
- Low side switches in notebook Vcore and GPU applications
- Networking point of load low side switches
- Secondary side rectification in telecom applications

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and minimal losses during power conversion, making it a valuable choice for modern electronic designs.
