#### Description:
The ACPL-C87AT is a high precision DC voltage isolation sensor designed for applications requiring accurate voltage sensing in high noise environments. It utilizes advanced optical coupling technology, including a sigma-delta analog-to-digital converter and differential circuit topology, to achieve low offset, high gain accuracy, and stability. The device is particularly suited for automotive applications, providing galvanic isolation and reinforced insulation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Battery Management Systems (BMS):** Voltage sensing for battery packs.
- **DC/DC Converter Voltage Sensing:** Monitoring voltage levels in power conversion systems.
- **Motor Inverter DC Bus Voltage Sensing:** Ensuring accurate voltage readings in motor control applications.
- **AC/DC Charger Voltage Sensing:** Monitoring output voltage in charging systems.
- **General Purpose Voltage Sensing and Monitoring:** Used in various electronic applications requiring precise voltage measurements.

This component is ideal for applications where high precision and reliability are critical, especially in automotive and industrial environments. The shutdown feature allows for power-saving modes, making it versatile for various designs.
