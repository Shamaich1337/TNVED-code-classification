### Component Summary: SZ1SMA5.0AT3G

**Manufacturer:** Littelfuse  
**Product Code:** SZ1SMA5.0AT3G  
**Type:** Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) Diode  
**Series:** SZ1SMA Series

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 5.0 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.4 V (min), 6.7 V (nom), 7.0 V (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 400 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 40 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation: 400 W @ 1 ms
  - DC Power Dissipation: 0.5 W @ 25°C, derating above 25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount, JEDEC DO-214AC

- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Rating of Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model
  - Fast response time typically < 1 ns
  - Pb-free and RoHS-compliant
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Recognized to UL 497B as an Isolated Loop Circuit Protector

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020

#### Description:
The SZ1SMA5.0AT3G is a unidirectional transient voltage suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from high voltage transients. It features a low profile surface mount package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. The device is capable of clamping high energy transients effectively, ensuring that voltage-sensitive components remain within safe operating limits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Communication Systems:** Protecting signal lines from voltage spikes.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Safeguarding sensitive circuits in vehicles against transients.
- **Medical Equipment:** Ensuring reliability and safety in medical devices.
- **Power Supplies:** Protecting power management circuits from overvoltage conditions.
- **Industrial Controls:** Shielding control systems from electrical noise and surges.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Providing protection in various consumer devices against voltage transients.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust protection against voltage spikes, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic systems.
