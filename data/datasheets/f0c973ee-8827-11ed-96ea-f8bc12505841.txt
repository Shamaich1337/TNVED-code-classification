### Component Summary: SBRT25U80SLP-13

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** SBRT25U80SLP-13  
**Document Number:** DS36715 Rev. 5  
**Date:** April 2015  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 80 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 80 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VRM): 80 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 25 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 200 A (8.3ms single half sine wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF): Max 0.61 V at 25 A and +25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - POWERDI5060-8 (compact thermally efficient package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low forward voltage drop for better efficiency and cooler operation
  - Reduced high-temperature reverse leakage for increased reliability
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free "Green" Device
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The SBRT25U80SLP is a 25A Trench Super Barrier Rectifier (SBR) designed for high efficiency and reliability in various applications. It features a low forward voltage drop and excellent reverse leakage stability at elevated temperatures, making it suitable for demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
- **DC-DC Converters:** Used for converting direct current from one voltage level to another, essential in power management systems.
- **AC-DC Adaptors:** Functions as a rectifier to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), commonly found in power supplies for electronic devices.
- **Freewheel Diodes:** Protects circuits by allowing current to flow in a reverse direction when the main current is interrupted, preventing voltage spikes.
- **Blocking Diodes:** Prevents reverse current flow, ensuring that current only flows in one direction, which is critical in battery management systems.

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power conversion and management, particularly in compact and thermally constrained environments.
