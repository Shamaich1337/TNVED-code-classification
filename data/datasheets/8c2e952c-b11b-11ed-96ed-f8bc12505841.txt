### Component Summary for Littelfuse 0451.100MRL

**Component Type:** Surface Mount Fuse (NANO2® Very Fast-Acting Fuse, 451 Series)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Maximum Voltage Rating: 125V
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Ampere Rating: 0.1A (100mA)
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Not applicable for fuses; however, the nominal cold resistance is 3.1 Ohms.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -55°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:** 
  - Surface Mount, dimensions: 6.86mm x 3.15mm x 2.95mm
- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Very fast-acting
  - RoHS compliant and Halogen Free
  - UL Listed and Recognized to UL/CSA/NMX standards
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 1, J-STD-020
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** 
  - Level 1

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 0451.100MRL is a very fast-acting surface mount fuse designed for over-current protection in secondary side circuits. It features a compact, square shape suitable for PCB applications using surface mount technology. The fuse is constructed with a ceramic body and is available with gold-plated or silver-plated caps, ensuring compatibility with RoHS standards.

The 0451.100MRL fuse is commonly used in various electronic devices and systems, including:
- Notebook PCs
- LCD/PDP TVs and monitors
- LCD backlight inverters
- Portable DVD players
- Power supplies
- Networking equipment
- PC servers
- Cooling fan systems
- Storage systems
- Telecom systems
- Wireless base stations
- Game consoles
- Office automation equipment
- Battery charging circuits
- Industrial equipment

This fuse is particularly suited for applications requiring reliable over-current protection in compact electronic designs.
