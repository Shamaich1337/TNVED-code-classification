### Component Summary: TE0703 Carrier Board

**Product Code:** TE0703  
**Manufacturer:** Trenz Electronic GmbH  
**Revision:** v.45  
**Export Date:** 2024-03-21  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 5V (4.75V to 5.25V recommended)
  - VCCIO Voltage Selection: 1.8V or 3.3V (jumper selectable)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Input Current: 4A
  - Recommended Minimum Current Capability for Startup: 3A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: 5V / 0.55A (with specific modules and conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -40°C to +100°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 100 mm x 64.5 mm
  - PCB Thickness: 1.65 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - On-board USB JTAG and UART interface (FTDI FT2232H)
  - SDIO port expander with voltage-level translation
  - User-configurable DIP switches for various functionalities
  - Integrated DC-DC step-down converter for 3.3V power supply
  - Multiple I/O connectors including Micro SD, USB, Ethernet, and VG96 connectors

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Component Description:
The TE0703 is a carrier board designed for 4 x 5 cm System on Modules (SoMs). It serves as a baseboard that exposes the MIO (Multiplexed I/O) and PS/PL (Processing System/Programmable Logic) pins of the SoM to accessible connectors. The board includes various on-board components that facilitate testing and evaluation of the SoMs, making it suitable for development and prototyping applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Embedded Systems Development:** The TE0703 is ideal for developers working with Xilinx SoMs, providing a platform for rapid prototyping and testing.
- **IoT Solutions:** With integrated Ethernet and USB interfaces, it can be used in Internet of Things (IoT) applications.
- **Data Acquisition and Processing:** The board can be utilized in systems requiring data collection and processing capabilities, leveraging the SoM's processing power.
- **Educational Purposes:** It serves as a practical tool for learning about embedded systems and hardware interfacing.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the TE0703 Carrier Board, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and intended use cases.
