  - Power Rating: 0.1W (1/10 W) or 0.2W (1/5 W) depending on configuration.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to +155°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Size: 0603 (1.6mm x 0.8mm)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - AEC-Q200 qualified for automotive applications.
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: MSL 1 (no dry pack required).
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free.
  - 100% automatic optical inspection prior to taping.

#### Description:
The AC0603FR-070RL is a thick film chip resistor designed for surface mount applications. It is part of YAGEO's AC series, which is specifically tailored for automotive and industrial applications. The resistor is constructed on a ceramic substrate with a resistive glaze, providing reliable performance in various environments.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in various automotive circuits for signal processing and power management.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for general-purpose applications where reliability and precision are critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Can be utilized in devices requiring compact and efficient resistive components.

This component is ideal for applications that demand high reliability and performance under varying environmental conditions, making it suitable for both automotive and industrial sectors.
