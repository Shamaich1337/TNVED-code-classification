The ACD20PS120A is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Schottky diode designed for high-performance applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for high-frequency and high-efficiency applications.

  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1200 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 1200 V

    - 20 A at TC = 150°C
    - 58 A at TC = 25°C

  - Non-repetitive Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 
    - 140 A at TC = 25°C (10 ms pulse)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD): 
    - 250 W at TC = 25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to 175 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 to 175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247-2

- **Special Features:**
  - Zero Forward/Reverse Recovery Current
  - High Blocking Voltage
  - High Frequency Operation
  - Positive Temperature Coefficient on Forward Voltage (VF)
  - Temperature Independent Switching Behavior
  - High surge current capability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

The ACD20PS120A is suitable for various applications, including:
- Motor Drives

- AC/DC Converters
- DC/DC Converters
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
