### Component Summary: FDS86242 N-Channel PowerTrench® MOSFET

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain to Source Voltage (VDS): 150 V
  - Gate to Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 4.1 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current: 20 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at TC = 25 °C: 5.0 W
  - Power Dissipation at TA = 25 °C: 2.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Max rDS(on) = 67 mΩ at VGS = 10 V, ID = 4.1 A
  - Max rDS(on) = 98 mΩ at VGS = 6 V, ID = 3.3 A
  - High performance trench technology for low rDS(on)
  - 100% UIL Tested
  - RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The FDS86242 is an N-Channel PowerTrench® MOSFET designed for high efficiency and performance in power management applications. It utilizes ON Semiconductor's advanced PowerTrench® process, which optimizes the device for low on-resistance (rDS(on)), fast switching performance, and ruggedness. This MOSFET is suitable for various applications requiring efficient power handling and switching capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
- **DC/DC Converters:** Used in power supply circuits to convert voltage levels efficiently.
- **Off-Line UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply):** Provides backup power and voltage regulation.
- **Distributed Power Architectures and Voltage Regulator Modules (VRMs):** Supports power distribution in complex systems.
- **Primary Switch for 24V and 48V Systems:** Acts as a main switching element in power circuits.
- **High Voltage Synchronous Rectifiers:** Used in rectification processes to improve efficiency in power conversion.

This component is ideal for applications where low on-resistance and high current handling are critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
