### Component Summary: NE3210S01-T1B

**Manufacturer:** Renesas (formerly NEC)

**Description:**
The NE3210S01 is a pseudomorphic Hetero-Junction Field Effect Transistor (HFET) designed for low noise applications. It utilizes a junction between Si-doped AlGaAs and undoped InGaAs to achieve very high electron mobility. The device features mushroom-shaped TiAl gates, which reduce gate resistance and enhance power handling capabilities. This component is particularly suitable for low noise amplifiers (LNAs) in both industrial and commercial systems.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (V_DS): 2 V (typical operating condition)
  - Gate-Source Voltage (V_GS): -3.0 V (maximum)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Drain Current (I_D): 10 mA (typical operating condition)
  - Gate Current (I_GS): 100 µA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation: 100 mW (maximum)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Channel Temperature (T_CH): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO1 (Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Super low noise figure (NF) and high associated gain (G_A).
  - Excellent performance for low noise applications.
  - High reliability due to stringent quality assurance and testing procedures.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The NE3210S01 is primarily used in:
- Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs) for RF applications.
- Communication systems, including cellular and satellite communications.
- Signal processing applications where low noise and high gain are critical.
- Industrial and commercial systems requiring high-performance RF components.

This component is ideal for applications that demand high sensitivity and low noise, making it a preferred choice in modern RF and microwave circuits.
