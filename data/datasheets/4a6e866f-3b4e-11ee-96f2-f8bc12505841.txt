### Component Summary: B560C-13-F (Diodes Incorporated)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 60 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 60 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 42 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 5.0 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A (8.3 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF max): 0.70 V at 5.0 A

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (Surface Mount Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Guard Ring Die Construction for Transient Protection
  - Low Power Loss, High Efficiency
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free; "Green" Device
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The B560C-13-F is a 5.0 A surface mount Schottky barrier rectifier designed for general commercial applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for applications requiring efficient power management.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Polarity Protection Diode:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections.
- **Re-Circulating Diode:** Used in inductive loads to prevent back EMF from damaging components.
- **Switching Diode:** Suitable for high-frequency applications, such as in power supplies and converters.
- **Low-Voltage, High-Frequency Inverters:** Efficiently manages power in inverter circuits.
- **Free Wheeling Applications:** Provides a path for current when the main circuit is interrupted, protecting sensitive components.

This component is particularly useful in automotive and industrial applications where reliability and efficiency are critical. Its compliance with AEC-Q101 standards ensures it meets the rigorous demands of automotive environments.
