asimple solution tomonitor thepower\nsupplies inmicroprocessor anddigital systems andprovide areset during power-up, power-down, andbrown-out\nconditions. The reset signal iscontrolled bythefactory-programmed reset threshold ontheVCCsupply voltage\npin.When thevoltage declines below thereset threshold, thereset signal isasserted andremains asserted for\n240msafter VCCrises above thethreshold. The LM809 hasanactive-low RESET output, while theLM810 has\nanactive-high RESET output. The available threshold options are2.63 V,2.93 V,3.08 V,4.38 V,and4.63 Vto\nprovide precision monitoring ofsupply voltages.\n7.2 Functional Block Diagram\n7.3 Feature Description\n7.3.1 Benefits ofPrecision Reset Thresholds\nAmicroprocessor supply supervisor must provide areset output within apredictable range ofthesupply voltage.\nAcommon threshold range isbetween 5%and10% below thenominal supply voltage. ### Component Summary: LM809M3X-2.93/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Input Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.2V to 5.5V (operating range), absolute maximum of 6V.
  - Reset Threshold Voltage: 2.93V (specific to LM809M3X-2.93).
  
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current (ICC): 15 µA (typical), up to 100 µA at higher temperatures.
  - Output Current (RESET): 20 mA maximum.

- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Low supply current of 15 µA typical, making it suitable for battery-powered applications.

- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to 105°C.

- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT-23 (3-pin).

- **Special Features**: 
  - Active-low RESET output.
  - 240 ms typical reset timeout after VCC rises above the threshold.
  - Ensured RESET output valid for VCC ≥ 1V.
  - Precision monitoring of supply voltages with multiple threshold options.
  - Low supply current ideal for portable applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The LM809 is a microprocessor supervisory circuit designed to monitor power supplies in microprocessor and digital systems. It asserts a reset signal during power-up, power-down, and brown-out conditions, ensuring that the microprocessor operates reliably. The LM809M3X-2.93 variant specifically features a reset threshold of 2.93V, making it suitable for systems that operate at this voltage level.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Microprocessor Systems**: Provides reset functionality to ensure proper operation during voltage fluctuations.
- **Portable/Battery-Powered Equipment**: Low power consumption makes it ideal for battery-operated devices.
- **Factory and Building Automation**: Used in control systems to maintain operational integrity.
- **Automotive Applications**: Ensures reliable operation of electronic systems in vehicles.
- **Programmable Logic Control**: Monitors supply voltages in industrial control systems.
- **Intelligent Instruments**: Used in devices that require precise voltage monitoring and reset capabilities.

The LM809 is a versatile component that enhances the reliability of electronic systems by ensuring that microprocessors are reset appropriately during critical power conditions.
