### Component Summary: LS48-A3L-TI-N-D5

**Description:**
The LS48-A3L-TI-N-D5 is a single-mode bi-directional SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceiver designed for optical communication applications. It operates at wavelengths of 1550 nm for transmission and 1310 nm for reception, making it suitable for SONET OC-3/SDH STM-1 and Fast Ethernet applications. This component features diagnostic monitoring capabilities and is compliant with RoHS standards.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 3.1 to 3.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 to 4.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (ITX + IRX): Up to 220 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is not explicitly stated, but the supply current indicates a maximum of 220 mA at 3.3 V, leading to a maximum power consumption of approximately 0.726 W.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) with LC Simplex Connector

- **Special Features:**
  - Hot pluggable
  - Class 1 laser product compliant with EN 60825-1
  - TTL signal detect indicator
  - Diagnostic monitoring capabilities

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The LS48-A3L-TI-N-D5 is typically used in:
- **Telecommunications:** For SONET/SDH applications, enabling high-speed data transmission over fiber optic networks.
- **Data Networking:** In Fast Ethernet applications, facilitating data transfer rates of 125 Mbps.
- **Optical Networking:** Suitable for 100Base-BX10-D and ITU-T G.985 standards, allowing for efficient long-distance communication.

This transceiver is ideal for use in environments requiring robust performance and reliability, such as data centers, enterprise networks, and telecommunications infrastructure.
