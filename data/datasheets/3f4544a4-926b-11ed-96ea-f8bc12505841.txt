#### Description:
The **LM9036MM-3.3/NOPB** is an ultra-low quiescent current voltage regulator designed for applications requiring low power consumption. It features a low dropout voltage and is capable of providing a fixed output voltage of 3.3V with a maximum output current of 50mA. The device is particularly suited for battery-operated systems due to its low standby current and robust protection features.

- **Automotive Systems:** Due to its ability to operate over a wide temperature range and withstand high input voltages.
- **Battery-Powered Devices:** Its ultra-low quiescent current makes it ideal for applications where power efficiency is critical.
