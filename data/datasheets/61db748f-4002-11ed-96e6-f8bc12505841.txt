### Component Summary: TLP2748(D4-TP,E)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5 V to 30 V
  - Isolation Voltage: 5000 Vrms (minimum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input Forward Current (IF): 15 mA (maximum)
  - Supply Current (ICCL/ICCH): 3 mA (maximum)
  - Output Current (IO): 50 mA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Input Power Dissipation: 40 mW (maximum)
  - Output Power Dissipation: 100 mW (maximum)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 110°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO6L (Small Outline 6 Lead)

- **Special Features:**
  - Inverter logic type (totem pole output)
  - Internal Faraday shield for common-mode transient immunity of ±30 kV/µs
  - Low threshold input current (IFHL): 1.6 mA (maximum)
  - Propagation delay time: 120 ns (maximum)
  - Pulse width distortion: 40 ns (maximum)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TLP2748 is a high-speed photocoupler that integrates a GaAℓAs infrared LED and a high-gain photodetector. It is designed for signal isolation in various applications, providing reinforced insulation with a guaranteed isolation voltage of 5 kVrms. The device is housed in a compact SO6L package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Intelligent Power Modules:** Used for isolating control signals in power electronics.
- **Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs):** Provides isolation between control and power circuits, enhancing safety and reliability.
- **High-Speed Digital Interfacing:** Ideal for instrumentation and control devices where fast signal transmission and isolation are required.
- **Data Communication:** Facilitates safe data transfer in noisy environments by isolating different sections of a circuit.

The TLP2748 is particularly beneficial in applications requiring low power consumption and high-speed operation, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
