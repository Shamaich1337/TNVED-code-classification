  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Description of the Component:

The **MAX3747A/MAX3747B** are low-power multirate limiting amplifiers designed for high-speed data communication applications. They function primarily as data quantizers for optical receivers in synchronous optical networks (SONET), Fibre Channel, and Gigabit Ethernet systems. The amplifiers provide constant-level, current-mode logic (CML) output voltages with controlled edge speeds, making them suitable for high-speed data transmission.

### Typical Applications:

- **Optical Transceiver Modules:**
  - Gigabit Ethernet SFP/SFF Optical Transceiver Modules
  - 1G/2G Fibre Channel SFP/SFF Optical Transceiver Modules
  - Multirate OC-3 to OC-48 FEC SFP/SFF Optical Transceiver Modules
  - 10G LX4 Transceiver Modules

These amplifiers are ideal for applications requiring reliable signal integrity over varying distances and conditions, particularly in high-speed networking environments. Their programmable loss-of-signal detection and output disable features enhance their utility in complex communication systems.
