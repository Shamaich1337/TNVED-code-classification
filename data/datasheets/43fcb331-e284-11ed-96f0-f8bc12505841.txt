They have two sup-ply pins: V\nCCand GND. V CCand GND are used to drive\nthe internal CMOS switches and set the limits of the ana-log voltage on any switch. Reverse ESD-protectiondiodes are internally connected between each analog-signal pin and both V\nCCand GND. ### Component Summary: MAX4619EUE+

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Operating Voltage: +2V to +5.5V
  - Absolute Maximum Ratings: -0.3V to +6V for VCC, A, B, C, or Enable; -0.3V to (VCC + 0.3V) for analog terminals.
  
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Continuous Current: ±75mA into any terminal.
  - Peak Current (pulsed at 1ms, 10% duty cycle): ±200mA.

- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power-Supply Current (ICC): 1 µA (typical) at VCC = 5.5V.

- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +85°C.

- **Package Type**: 
  - 16-pin TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package).

- **Special Features**: 
  - Fast switching times: 15ns (t_ON), 10ns (t_OFF).
  - Low on-resistance: 10Ω max at +5V supply, 20Ω max at +3V supply.
  - Low off-leakage current: 1nA at +25°C.
  - Low distortion: <0.017% (600Ω).
  - High off-isolation: <-93dB.
  - Low crosstalk: <-96dB.
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).

#### Description:
The MAX4619EUE+ is a high-speed, low-voltage CMOS analog multiplexer/switch. It is designed to operate as a single-pole/double-throw (SPDT) switch, allowing for the routing of analog signals in various applications. The device can handle rail-to-rail analog signals and is compatible with TTL/CMOS logic levels, making it versatile for integration into digital systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Battery-Operated Equipment**: The low power consumption and wide operating voltage range make it suitable for portable devices.
- **Audio/Video Signal Routing**: Ideal for switching audio and video signals in consumer electronics.
- **Low-Voltage Data-Acquisition Systems**: Useful in applications requiring precise signal routing and low distortion.
- **Communications Circuits**: Can be employed in various communication devices for signal switching and routing.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where low power consumption, fast switching, and high signal integrity are critical.
