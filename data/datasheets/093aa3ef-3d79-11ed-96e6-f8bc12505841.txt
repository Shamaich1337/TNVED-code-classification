### Component Summary: C1Q Series Chip Fuse

**Manufacturer:** Bel Fuse Inc.  
**Product Code:** C1Q  
**Type:** Surface Mount Very Fast-Acting Chip Fuse  
**Package Type:** 1206 Size SMD (Surface Mount Device)  
**Moisture Sensitivity Level:** MSL = 1 (according to IPC J-STD-020E)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - 125V AC for current ratings from 250mA to 5A
  - 63V DC for current ratings from 250mA to 5A
  - 63V AC & DC for 7A
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Available from 250mA to 7A
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Power dissipation at 100% In varies from 0.06W (250mA) to 1.12W (7A)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -55°C to +125°C
- **Special Features:**
  - Fast acting
  - RoHS 2 compliant
  - Halogen-free and lead-free
  - Compatible with IR Pb-free and wave soldering processes
  - Tape and reel packaging for automatic SMD placement

#### Description:
The C1Q series is a very fast-acting chip fuse designed for surface mount applications. It provides overcurrent protection in a compact 1206 package, making it suitable for various electronic devices. The fuse is designed to interrupt the circuit quickly in the event of an overcurrent condition, thereby protecting sensitive components from damage.

#### Typical Applications:
The C1Q series chip fuses are commonly used in:
- Notebook computers
- LCD monitors
- PC computers
- Office electronic equipment
- Industrial equipment
- Medical devices
- Power over Ethernet (POE, POE+)
- LCD/LED monitors and TVs
- Power supply circuits
- DC-DC converters

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited and reliable overcurrent protection is essential. Its fast-acting nature ensures that it responds quickly to fault conditions, safeguarding the overall system integrity.
