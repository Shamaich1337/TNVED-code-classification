### Component Summary: TC1-1-13MX+

**Description:**
The TC1-1-13MX+ is a surface mount RF transformer designed for balanced to unbalanced transformation. It operates over a wide frequency range of 4.5 to 3000 MHz, making it suitable for various RF applications. This component is particularly useful in push-pull amplifiers and other RF signal processing applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - RF Power: 0.25 W
  - DC Current: 30 mA
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Current: 30 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Power: 0.25 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:**
  - -55°C to 100°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: AT1521 (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features:**
  - Balanced to unbalanced transformation
  - Good return loss
  - Excellent amplitude unbalance (0.5 dB typ.) and phase unbalance (2° typ.)
  - Aqueous washable
  - RoHS compliant
  - Available in tape and reel packaging
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

**Typical Applications:**
- RF signal processing
- Push-pull amplifiers
- PCS (Personal Communication Service)
- DCS (Digital Cellular System)
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service)
- General RF applications requiring balanced transmission lines

This transformer is ideal for applications that require efficient signal transformation with minimal loss, making it a valuable component in modern RF circuit designs.
