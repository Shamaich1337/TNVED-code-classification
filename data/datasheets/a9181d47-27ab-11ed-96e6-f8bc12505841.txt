  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The **16SVPF180M** is a **Conductive Polymer Aluminum Solid Capacitor** from Panasonic's SVPF series. It is designed for high-performance applications requiring stable capacitance and low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance). This capacitor is particularly noted for its high voltage and capacitance ratings, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The 16SVPF180M capacitor is commonly used in:
- **Power Management**: Providing stable voltage and current in power supply circuits.
- **Audio/Visual Equipment**: Filtering and smoothing power supply lines in audio and video devices.
- **Computers and Office Equipment**: Used in power supply circuits and signal processing applications.
- **Optical Equipment**: Stabilizing power supplies in sensitive optical devices.
- **Measuring Equipment**: Ensuring accurate readings by stabilizing voltage levels.

This capacitor is ideal for applications where reliability and performance are critical, especially in environments with varying temperatures and humidity levels.
