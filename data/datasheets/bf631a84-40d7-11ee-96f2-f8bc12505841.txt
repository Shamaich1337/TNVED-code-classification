### Component Summary: GORE® PolyVent Ex+ (Product Code: PMF200444)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Not applicable (passive venting component).
- **Current Ratings**: Not applicable (passive venting component).
- **Power Consumption**: Not applicable (passive venting component).
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40 °C to +125 °C (or +150 °C for PolyVent XS).
- **Package Type**: Screw-in vent.
- **Special Features**:
  - IECEx and ATEX certified for use in potentially explosive environments.
  - Oleophobic membrane to block contaminant ingress.
  - High airflow performance (1600 ml/min at dp = 70 mbar).
  - Rugged construction for chemical, UV, and temperature resistance.
  - 100% quality control with full traceability.
  - Flammability rating: UL 94 V-0.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E)**: Not specified (passive venting component).

#### Description:
The GORE® PolyVent Ex+ is a protective vent designed for use in sealed enclosures, particularly in environments that may be hazardous due to the presence of explosive gases or dust. It functions by equalizing pressure and reducing condensation within the enclosure while preventing the ingress of solid and liquid contaminants. The vent is constructed from durable materials, including stainless steel, and features a specialized oleophobic membrane that enhances its protective capabilities.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Outdoor Electronics**: Used in devices exposed to harsh environmental conditions, such as telecommunications equipment, sensors, and control systems.
- **Industrial Equipment**: Ideal for enclosures housing sensitive electronics in potentially explosive atmospheres, ensuring safety and reliability.
- **Automotive and Aerospace**: Protects critical components from moisture and contaminants while allowing for necessary pressure equalization.
- **Renewable Energy Systems**: Suitable for solar panels and other renewable energy applications where durability and environmental resistance are crucial.

The GORE® PolyVent Ex+ is engineered to enhance the longevity and reliability of electronic devices in challenging conditions, making it a vital component in various industrial and outdoor applications.
