### Component Summary: LI-PI-IPEX-1233-ADPT

**Manufacturer:** Leopard Imaging Inc.  
**Product Code:** LI-PI-IPEX-1233-ADPT

#### Key Specifications:
- **Interface Conversion:** Converts a 15-pin Jetson Nano or Raspberry Pi camera interface to a 30-pin I-PEX interface.
- **15-pin Connector:** 
  - Part Number: 1-1734248-5
  - Number of Positions: 15
  - Pitch: 1 mm
  - FFC, FCB Thickness: 0.30 mm
- **30-pin Connector:** 
  - Part Number: 20525-030E-02C
  - Number of Positions: 30
  - Pitch: 0.4 mm
- **Board Size:** 40 mm x 30 mm
- **Cable Length:** 100 mm

#### Description:
The LI-PI-IPEX-1233-ADPT is an adapter board designed to facilitate the connection between camera modules and development boards such as the Jetson Nano or Raspberry Pi. It serves as a bridge, allowing users to utilize camera modules that require a 30-pin I-PEX interface with devices that have a 15-pin camera interface.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Camera Integration:** Used in projects that involve integrating camera modules with Jetson Nano or Raspberry Pi platforms, commonly found in robotics, computer vision, and IoT applications.
- **Prototyping:** Ideal for developers and engineers who are prototyping camera-based applications and need a reliable interface solution.
- **Embedded Systems:** Useful in embedded systems where camera functionality is required, such as surveillance systems, drones, and smart devices.

This adapter is particularly beneficial for developers looking to expand the capabilities of their Raspberry Pi or Jetson Nano projects by incorporating high-quality camera modules that utilize the I-PEX interface.
