### Component Summary: BC817-40

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** BC817-40  
**Type:** NPN General Purpose Amplifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CEO): 45 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 5 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 50 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Collector Power Dissipation (P_C): 300 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -55 to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23

- **Special Features:**
  - Capable of 0.3 Watts of power dissipation at 25 °C.
  - Epoxy meets UL-94 V-0 flammability rating.
  - Halogen-free option available upon request (add suffix "HF").
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E).

#### Description:
The BC817-40 is an NPN general-purpose amplifier transistor designed for a variety of electronic applications. It is capable of handling moderate power levels and is suitable for signal amplification and switching applications. The device features a high current gain (h_FE) ranging from 250 to 600 at a collector current of 100 mA, making it effective for low-power amplification tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Amplification:** Used in audio and radio frequency applications to amplify weak signals.
- **Switching Applications:** Can be utilized in digital circuits for switching purposes, such as turning on/off loads.
- **General Purpose Use:** Suitable for various electronic circuits where moderate power handling and amplification are required, including consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial control systems.
