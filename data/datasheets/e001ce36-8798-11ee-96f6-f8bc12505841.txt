### Component Summary: DT756/DT756V

**Description:**
The DT756/DT756V is a low-cost plastic fiber optic transmitter diode designed for simple optical data transmission using polymer optical fiber. It features a 650nm LED that supports data rates of up to 10 Mbps. The device is housed in a transparent plastic package with a 2.2mm aperture for easy coupling of unconnectorized plastic optical fiber, secured with an axial locking screw.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (Vr): Max 3 V
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at If=50mA: 2.1 to 2.8 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (If): Max 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Pd): Max 120 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Case Temperature (Top): -45°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -45°C to +100°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Max 100°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Plastic Connector Housing

- **Special Features:**
  - Good linearity with forward current > 2 mA
  - Molded microlens for efficient coupling
  - Interference-free transmission with light-tight housing
  - Auto insertable and wave solderable
  - RoHS 2.0 compliance

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The DT756/DT756V is suitable for various applications, including:
- Household electronics
- Power electronics
- Optical networks
- Light barriers

This component is particularly useful in systems requiring efficient and cost-effective optical data transmission, making it ideal for consumer electronics and industrial applications where optical communication is needed.
