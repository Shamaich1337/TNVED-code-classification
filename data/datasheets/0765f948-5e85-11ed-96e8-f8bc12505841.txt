#### Description:
The ACM2012-361-2P-T002 is a common mode filter designed for high-speed differential signal lines. It is a downsized wound type chip that effectively suppresses common mode noise while maintaining the integrity of differential signals. This component is particularly useful in applications where electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) is critical, as it provides high impedance to common mode noise (up to 1500 Ω at 100 MHz) while having minimal impact on differential mode signals.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Lines:** Used in PCs and peripheral devices to mitigate common mode noise.
- **IEEE1394 Lines:** Employed in high-speed data transfer applications.
- **Ethernet Lines:** Utilized in networking equipment to ensure signal integrity.
- **LCD Panel LVDS and Panel Link Lines:** Helps in reducing noise in display interfaces, ensuring clear signal transmission.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust noise suppression without compromising the performance of high-speed data signals. It is suitable for general electronic equipment, including telecommunications, AV equipment, and industrial applications.
