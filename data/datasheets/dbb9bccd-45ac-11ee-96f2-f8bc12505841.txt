For improved thermal dissipation, the recommended layout uses PCB copper areas which extend beyond the exposed solder pad. The exposed solder pads should be defined by a solder mask which matches the pad layout of the TBU\n® device in size and spacing. It is \nrecommended that they should be the same dimension as the TBU\n® pads but if smaller solder pads are used, they should be \ncentered on the TBU® package terminal pads and not more \nthan 0.10-0.12 mm (0.004-0.005 in.) smaller in overall width or length. Solder pad areas should not be larger than the TBU\n® \npad sizes to ensure adequate clearance is maintained. The recommended stencil thickness is 0.10-0.12 mm (0.004-0.005 in.) with a stencil opening size 0.025 mm (0.0010 in.) less than the solder pad size. Extended copper areas beyond the solder pad significantly improve the junction to ambient thermal resistance, resulting in operation at lower junction temperatures with a corresponding benefit of reliability. All pads should soldered to the PCB, including pads marked as NC or NU but no electrical connection should be made to these pads. ### Component Summary: TBU-CA025-050-WH

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Impulse Voltage: 250 V
  - Continuous AC RMS Voltage: 100 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Trigger Current: 50 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Series Resistance (R_device): 13.3 Ω (at V_imp = 250 V, I_trigger = 50 mA)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage: -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DFN (Dual Flat No-lead) package
- **Special Features:**
  - Low capacitance, single bidirectional high-speed protection
  - Overcurrent and overvoltage protection
  - Fast response time (approximately 1 μs to transition to protected state)
  - ESD protection per IEC 61000-4-2: ±2 kV
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TBU-CA025-050-WH is a high-speed protector from Bourns' TBU® series, designed to provide superior circuit protection against overcurrent and overvoltage conditions. Utilizing MOSFET semiconductor technology, this component effectively monitors current and triggers to create a barrier that protects sensitive electronics from high voltage and current surges, such as those caused by short circuits, AC power cross, induction, and lightning strikes. 

#### Typical Applications:
The TBU-CA025-050-WH is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Voice and VDSL cards
- Protection modules and dongles
- Process control equipment
- Test and measurement equipment
- General electronics

This component is particularly useful in environments where electronic devices are exposed to potential surge events, ensuring the longevity and reliability of sensitive electronic circuits.
