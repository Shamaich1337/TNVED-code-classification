### Component Summary: MMBD4148SE

**Description:**
The MMBD4148SE is a small signal diode manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed for general-purpose applications, primarily used for rectification and signal processing in electronic circuits. This diode is part of the MMBD4148 series, which includes variants like MMBD4148CC and MMBD4148CA.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 200 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 1.0 A (1.0 s pulse width), 2.0 A (1.0 µs pulse width)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 350 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (TO-236)
- **Special Features:**
  - Pb-Free device
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The MMBD4148SE is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Rectification:** Converting AC to DC in power supplies.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in clamping, clipping, and switching applications.
- **Protection Circuits:** Protecting sensitive components from voltage spikes and reverse polarity.
- **General Purpose Switching:** Suitable for low-frequency switching applications in electronic circuits.

This diode's robust specifications make it suitable for a wide range of electronic applications, ensuring reliability and performance in both consumer and industrial electronics.
