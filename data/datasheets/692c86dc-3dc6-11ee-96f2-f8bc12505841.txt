### Component Summary: Bourns 2027-15-SM-RPLF

**Component Type:** Precision Gas Discharge Tube Surge Protector

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - DC Sparkover: 150 V (±15%)
  - Impulse Sparkover: 500 V (1000 V/μs)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Impulse Discharge Current: 
    - 25,000 A (8/20 μs) - 1 operation minimum
    - 10,000 A (8/20 μs) - > 10 operations
    - 2,000 A (10/350 μs) - 2 operations
    - 500 A (10/1000 μs) - > 400 operations
    - 100 A (10/1000 μs or 10/700 μs) - > 1000 operations
  - Alternating Discharge Current: 
    - 65 Arms, 11 cycles - 1 operation minimum
    - 10 Arms, 1 s - > 10 operations

- **Power Consumption:** Not specified directly; operates as a surge protector.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (SM), dimensions 8 mm diameter x 6 mm long

- **Special Features:**
  - High surge current rating
  - Low capacitance (< 1 pF at 1 MHz)
  - Stable breakdown voltage throughout life
  - UL Recognized (File E153537)
  - RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The Bourns 2027-15-SM-RPLF is a precision gas discharge tube (GDT) surge protector designed for surface mount applications. It provides reliable over-voltage protection by allowing excess voltage to discharge safely, thus protecting sensitive electronic components from damage due to voltage spikes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Protects communication equipment from voltage surges.
- **Industrial Electronics:** Safeguards machinery and control systems from transient voltages.
- **Commercial Electronics:** Used in various consumer electronics to enhance durability and reliability against electrical surges.

This component is particularly suitable for compact applications due to its small size and compatibility with automated assembly processes, making it ideal for modern PCB designs in various electronic devices.
