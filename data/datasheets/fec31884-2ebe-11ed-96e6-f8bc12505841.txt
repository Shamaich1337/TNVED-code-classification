  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 60 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot):
    - SOT23 Package: 330 mW (at T_S ≤ 71°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT23 and SOT363 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - High DC current gain (hFE) ranging from 30 to 300 depending on the collector current.
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage (VCEsat) of 0.2 V at 10 mA and 0.3 V at 50 mA.
  - Pb-free (RoHS compliant) and qualified according to AEC Q101 standards.
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).
