### Component Summary: LM335AH/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Operating Output Voltage: 2.92V to 3.04V at 25°C with 1mA current.
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Operating Current Range: 400μA to 5mA.
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power consumption is not explicitly stated, but the device operates within the specified current range.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Continuous: -40°C to 100°C.
  - Intermittent: Up to 125°C.
- **Package Type**: 
  - Available in TO-92 and TO-46 packages.
- **Special Features**: 
  - Directly calibrated to the Kelvin temperature scale.
  - 1°C initial accuracy available.
  - Less than 1Ω dynamic impedance.
  - Easily calibrated with an adjustment pin.
  - Low-cost temperature sensing.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The LM335AH/NOPB is a precision temperature sensor integrated circuit (IC) that operates as a 2-terminal zener diode. Its breakdown voltage is directly proportional to absolute temperature, providing a linear output of 10mV/°K. This device is designed for high accuracy and low impedance, making it suitable for various temperature sensing applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supplies**: Used for temperature monitoring and control in power supply circuits.
- **Battery Management**: Helps in monitoring battery temperatures to prevent overheating and ensure safe operation.
- **HVAC Systems**: Employed in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for accurate temperature readings.
- **Appliances**: Utilized in household appliances for temperature regulation and monitoring.
- **General Temperature Sensing**: Suitable for any application requiring precise temperature measurement over a wide range.

The LM335AH/NOPB is particularly advantageous in applications where accurate temperature readings are critical, and its ease of calibration allows for flexibility in various designs.
