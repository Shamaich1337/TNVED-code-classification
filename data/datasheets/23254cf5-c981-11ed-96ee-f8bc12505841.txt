### Component Summary: MC74AC139DR2G

**Description:**
The MC74AC139DR2G is a high-speed dual 1-of-4 decoder/demultiplexer from ON Semiconductor. It features two independent decoders, each accepting two binary-weighted inputs and providing four mutually exclusive active-Low outputs. The device can also function as a data input for a 4-output demultiplexer and can generate all four minterms of two variables, making it useful in various logic applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - **Supply Voltage (VCC):** 
    - 74AC: 2.0 to 6.0 V
    - 74ACT: 4.5 to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - **Output Sink/Source Current (IO):** ±24 mA
  - **DC Supply Current per Output Pin (ICC):** 50 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - **Power Dissipation (PD):** 500 mW (at 65 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
