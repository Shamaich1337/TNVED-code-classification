#### Description:
The **DS90LV012A** is a single CMOS differential line receiver designed for high-speed data communication applications. It utilizes Low Voltage Differential Signaling (LVDS) technology, which allows for high data rates while minimizing power consumption and electromagnetic interference. The device accepts low voltage differential input signals and translates them to standard CMOS output levels, making it suitable for interfacing with various digital systems.

#### Typical Applications:
The DS90LV012A is commonly used in:
- **High-speed data communication**: Ideal for point-to-point connections in systems requiring fast data transfer rates.
- **Telecommunications**: Used in applications where low power and high-speed signaling are critical.
- **Industrial automation**: Suitable for connecting sensors and actuators in control systems.
- **Consumer electronics**: Employed in devices that require reliable data transmission over short distances.

This component is particularly advantageous in environments where power efficiency and signal integrity are paramount, such as in embedded systems and high-performance computing applications.
