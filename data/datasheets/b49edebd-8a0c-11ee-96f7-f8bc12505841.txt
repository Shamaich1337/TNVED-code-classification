### Component Summary: 74AC04MTCX

**Description:**
The 74AC04MTCX is a hex inverter from ON Semiconductor, which contains six independent inverters. It is designed to provide high-speed signal inversion with low power consumption. The device is available in a thin shrink small outline package (TSSOP) and is Pb-free, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): -0.5 to +7.0 V
  - Recommended Operating Voltage: 
    - AC: 2.0 to 6.0 V
    - ACT: 4.5 to 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Output Source/Sink Current (IO): ±50 mA
  - DC Input Diode Current: ±20 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Quiescent Supply Current (ICC): 
    - AC: 20 µA (typical at 5.5 V)
    - ACT: 40 µA (typical at 5.5 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature (TA): -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP-14 (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Outputs can source/sink 24 mA.
  - TTL-compatible inputs for the ACT version.
  - Reduced ICC by 50% for the 74AC variant.
  - Pb-free device compliant with JEDEC standards.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The 74AC04MTCX is commonly used in:
- **Digital Logic Circuits:** As a basic building block for creating complex logic functions.
- **Signal Processing:** Inverting signals in various digital applications.
- **Data Communication:** Enhancing signal integrity in communication systems.
- **Power Management:** Used in circuits requiring signal inversion for control logic.

This component is ideal for applications requiring reliable and efficient signal inversion, making it a versatile choice in both consumer and industrial electronics.
