### Component Summary: PZT3904T1G

**Description:**
The PZT3904T1G is a general-purpose NPN silicon transistor manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed for various applications requiring switching and amplification. The device is AEC-Q101 qualified, making it suitable for automotive and other applications that demand high reliability and performance.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 40 Vdc
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 60 Vdc
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6.0 Vdc

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 200 mAdc

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Device Dissipation (PD) at TA = 25 °C: 1.5 W
  - Thermal Resistance Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 83.3 °C/W
  - Thermal Resistance Junction-to-Lead (RθJL): 35 °C/W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-223 (TO-261), Case 318E

- **Special Features:**
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The PZT3904T1G is commonly used in:
- Signal amplification in audio and radio frequency applications
- Switching applications in power management circuits
- General-purpose amplification in various electronic devices
- Automotive electronics where reliability and performance are critical

This transistor is ideal for applications that require a compact, efficient, and reliable switching or amplification solution, particularly in automotive and consumer electronics.
