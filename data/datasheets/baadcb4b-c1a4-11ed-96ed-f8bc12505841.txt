#### Description:
The **TPS2062** is a dual-channel, current-limited power distribution switch designed for applications where heavy capacitive loads and short circuits are likely to be encountered. It features integrated N-channel MOSFETs with a low on-resistance of 70 mΩ, allowing for efficient power distribution in systems requiring multiple power switches in a single package. Each switch is controlled by a logic enable input, and the device includes an internal charge pump to manage the rise and fall times of the power switches, minimizing current surges during switching.

#### Typical Applications:
The TPS2062 is commonly used in:
- **Power Management**: It is ideal for managing power distribution in USB hubs, where it can supply power to multiple downstream devices while ensuring current limits are adhered to.
- **Signal Processing**: The device can be used in applications requiring controlled power delivery to sensitive components.
- **Hot-Plug Applications**: The controlled rise time feature makes it suitable for hot-plugging scenarios, where devices can be added or removed without disrupting the power supply.
- **Consumer Electronics**: It is often found in devices that require robust power management solutions, such as laptops, tablets, and other portable electronics.
