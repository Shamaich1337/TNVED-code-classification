### Component Summary: ZL40216LDF1

**Manufacturer:** Microchip Technology (formerly Microsemi Corporation)  
**Product Code:** ZL40216LDF1  
**Package Type:** 32 Pin QFN (Tape and Reel)  
**Package Size:** 5 x 5 mm  
**Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage: 2.5 V ± 5% or 3.3 V ± 5%
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 93 mA (when all outputs are active)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power supply options: 2.5 V or 3.3 V
  - On-chip Low Drop Out (LDO) regulator for noise rejection

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Performance:**
  - Operating Frequency: Up to 750 MHz
  - Ultra-low additive jitter: 104 fs RMS

- **Special Features:**
  - Accepts differential or single-ended inputs (LVPECL, LVDS, CML, HCSL, LVCMOS)
  - Six precision LVDS outputs
  - Designed for low-jitter clock distribution and signal restoration

### Description:
The ZL40216 is a precision LVDS fanout buffer designed to distribute clock signals with minimal jitter. It features six identical output clock drivers capable of operating at frequencies up to 750 MHz. The device can accept various input signal types, including differential and single-ended signals, making it versatile for different applications. The internal LDO regulator helps mitigate power supply noise, enhancing the overall performance of the clock distribution.

### Typical Applications:
- **Clock Distribution:** General-purpose clock distribution for various digital systems.
- **Low Jitter Clock Trees:** Used in applications requiring low jitter for high-performance clock trees.
- **Logic Translation:** Facilitates logic level translation between different signal standards.
- **Signal Restoration:** Restores clock and data signals in wired communications.
- **Wired Communications:** Supports protocols such as OTN, SONET/SDH, GE, 10 GE, FC, and 10G FC.
- **PCI Express:** Clock distribution for PCI Express generations 1, 2, and 3.
- **Wireless Communications:** Utilized in high-performance microprocessor clock distribution.

This component is ideal for applications in telecommunications, data centers, and high-speed computing environments where reliable clock distribution is critical.
