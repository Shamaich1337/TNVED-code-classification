### Component Summary: PI6C557-03BLEX

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 3.0V to 3.6V (typical 3.3V)
  - Maximum Supply Voltage: 5.5V
  - Input/Output Voltage Range: -0.5V to VDD + 0.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Current: 95 mA (typical)
  - Output Enable Current: 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Supply Current: 95 mA (with load)
  - Input Leakage Current: -20 µA to 20 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient: -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage: -65°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature: 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 16-pin TSSOP (L16) and 16-pin QSOP (Q16)

- **Special Features:**
  - PCIe 3.0 compliant
  - HCSL and LVDS compatible outputs
  - Phase jitter: 0.45 ps RMS (high frequency typ.)
  - Cycle-to-cycle jitter: 35 ps (typ)
  - Spread spectrum options: -0.5%, -0.75%, and no spread
  - Lead-free and RoHS compliant
  - Industrial temperature range

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Component Description:**
The **PI6C557-03B** is a **spread spectrum clock generator** designed for PCI Express 3.0 and Ethernet applications. It utilizes a Phase-Locked Loop (PLL) to generate two pairs of differential outputs (HCSL) from a 25 MHz crystal input, producing clock frequencies of 25 MHz, 100 MHz, 125 MHz, and 200 MHz. The device is engineered to minimize electromagnetic interference (EMI) in electronic systems.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Data Communication:** Used in PCIe and Ethernet interfaces to provide stable clock signals.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for applications requiring precise timing and reduced EMI.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Suitable for devices that require high-speed data transfer with minimal jitter.
- **Automotive Applications:** Can be used in automotive electronics that require stringent change control and compliance with AEC-Q100 standards.

This component is particularly beneficial in environments where signal integrity and timing precision are critical, making it a valuable choice for modern electronic designs.
