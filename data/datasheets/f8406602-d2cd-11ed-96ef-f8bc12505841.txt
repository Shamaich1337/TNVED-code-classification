### Component Summary: TPS61235PRWLR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.3 V to (VOUT - 0.6 V)
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Fixed at 5.1 V (TPS61235P)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current (IOUT): Up to 3.5 A for 3.3 V to 5 V conversion

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQ): 10 µA under light load conditions
  - Shutdown Current: < 1 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40 °C to 125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (9 pins), dimensions: 2.5 mm x 2.5 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Up to 97% efficiency
  - Programmable constant output current
  - True disconnection during shutdown
  - Built-in protections: Over Voltage, Over Temperature, and Current Limit
  - Load status indication via INACT pin
  - Soft-start feature to prevent inrush current

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS61235PRWLR** is a high-efficiency synchronous boost converter designed for battery-powered applications, particularly those utilizing single-cell Li-Ion or Li-Polymer batteries. It features a fixed output voltage of 5.1 V and is capable of delivering up to 3.5 A of output current. The device integrates low-resistance power switches, enabling high efficiency (up to 97%) and low quiescent current, making it suitable for portable devices that require efficient power management.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Banks:** The TPS61235 is ideal for power bank applications where efficient energy conversion from a battery to a higher voltage is required.
- **USB Charging Ports:** It can be used in USB charging applications, including USB Type-C, where a stable output voltage is necessary.
- **Battery-Powered USB Hubs:** The device is suitable for powering USB hubs that operate from battery sources.
- **Tablet PCs and Other Portable Devices:** It is commonly used in tablets and other portable electronics that require efficient power conversion from a lower voltage battery source.

This component is particularly advantageous in designs where space is limited, as it reduces the need for external components and simplifies the overall system design. The built-in protections and low power consumption features enhance the reliability and efficiency of the applications it serves.
