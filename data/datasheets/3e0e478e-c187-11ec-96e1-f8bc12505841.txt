### Component Summary: ADM3485ARZ-REEL7

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0 V to 3.6 V
  - Digital I/O Voltage: -0.3 V to +6 V
  - Driver Output/Receiver Input Voltage: -7.5 V to +12.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (I_CC): 1.1 mA (typical), 2.2 mA (max)
  - Shutdown Current (I_SHDN): 2 nA (typical)
  - Driver Short-Circuit Output Current: ±250 mA
  - Receiver Short-Circuit Output Current: ±60 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical supply current in active mode: 1.1 mA
  - Shutdown mode current: 2 nA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Lead Narrow Body Small Outline (SOIC_N)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Half-duplex communication
  - 10 Mbps data rate capability
  - Low power shutdown mode
  - Thermal shutdown protection
  - Fail-safe feature for floating inputs
  - Interoperable with 5 V logic
  - Slew rate limited drivers for reduced EMI

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1 (not moisture sensitive)

**Description:**
The ADM3485ARZ-REEL7 is a low-power, half-duplex differential line transceiver designed for RS-485 and RS-422 communications. It operates from a single 3.3 V power supply and is capable of transmitting data at rates up to 10 Mbps. The device features a low power shutdown mode, which significantly reduces power consumption when not in use, making it suitable for power-sensitive applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The ADM3485ARZ-REEL7 is commonly used in:
- Industrial process control systems
- Telecommunications
- HVAC systems
- Multipoint data communications
- Applications requiring robust data transmission over long distances with minimal electromagnetic interference (EMI).

This transceiver is ideal for environments where low power consumption and reliable data communication are critical, particularly in industrial and telecom applications.
