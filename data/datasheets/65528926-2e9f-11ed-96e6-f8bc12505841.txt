### Component Summary: NZH16C,115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 15.69 V to 16.51 V (at IZ = 20 mA)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Forward Current (IF): 250 mA
  - Zener Test Current (IZ): 20 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 500 mW (at T amb ≤ 25°C), up to 1 W under specific conditions
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOD123F (small and flat lead Surface-Mounted Device)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low differential resistance
  - Suitable for surface-mounted design
  - Wide working voltage range
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The NZH16C,115 is a general-purpose Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is housed in a compact SOD123F package, making it suitable for surface-mounted designs. The primary function of this component is to provide voltage clamping and regulation, ensuring that the voltage across the load remains stable despite variations in input voltage or load conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: The NZH16C is commonly used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Voltage Clamping**: It can protect sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Signal Conditioning**: Used in circuits where precise voltage levels are required for signal processing.
- **Reference Voltage Generation**: It can serve as a reference voltage in various electronic applications.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and any circuit requiring reliable voltage regulation and protection.
