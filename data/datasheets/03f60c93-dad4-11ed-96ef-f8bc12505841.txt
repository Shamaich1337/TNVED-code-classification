### Component Summary: SMBJ13A (SMAJ Series)

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SMBJ13A

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 14.4V (Min) to 15.9V (Max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 13.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (I_R): 1 μA at V_RWM
  - Maximum Reverse Surge Current (I_PP): 21.5 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 400 W (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Power Dissipation on infinite heat sink (P_D): 1.0 W at T_L = 75 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AC (SMA)
- **Special Features:**
  - Uni-directional design
  - Low-profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Excellent clamping capability and very fast response time
  - High temperature soldering guaranteed: 260 °C for 10 seconds at terminals
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The SMBJ13A is a surface mount transient voltage suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is uni-directional, meaning it allows current to flow in one direction while clamping voltage spikes to a safe level.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electronics Protection:** Used in consumer electronics, computers, industrial equipment, and telecommunication devices to safeguard integrated circuits (ICs), MOSFETs, and signal lines from voltage spikes.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Ideal for applications where voltage transients can occur, ensuring the longevity and reliability of sensitive components.
- **Automated Assembly:** The low-profile design makes it suitable for automated placement in high-volume manufacturing environments.

This component is essential for maintaining the integrity and functionality of electronic systems in various applications, particularly where voltage transients are a concern.
