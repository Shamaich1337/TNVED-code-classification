#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Maximum working voltage is determined by the formula \( V = \sqrt{P \times R} \), where P is the power rating and R is the resistance value.
- **Current Ratings**: Power rating at 70°C is 0.25 W.
- **Power Consumption**: Rated power is 0.25 W at 70°C.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -65°C to +170°C.
- **Package Type**: 1206 (3.20 mm x 1.60 mm).
- **Special Features**:
  - Extremely low resistance values (down to 0.0005 Ω).
  - Sulfur resistance, making it suitable for high sulfur environments.
  - Very low inductance (0.5 nH to 5 nH).
  - Low thermal EMF (< 3 μV/°C).
  - AEC-Q200 qualified for automotive applications.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

#### Description:
The WSL1206R1000FEA is a Power Metal Strip® resistor designed for low-value resistance applications. It features a welded construction that enhances its performance in current sensing, voltage division, and pulse applications. The resistor is characterized by its low thermal EMF and high reliability in harsh environments, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Current Sensing**: Used in power management systems to monitor current flow.
- **Voltage Division**: Employed in circuits where voltage needs to be divided for measurement or control.
- **Pulse Applications**: Suitable for applications that require handling of high-frequency signals with minimal inductance.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Due to its AEC-Q200 qualification, it is ideal for use in automotive systems where reliability and performance are critical.
