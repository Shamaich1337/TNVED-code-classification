  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 (unlimited floor life at ≤ 30°C/85% RH).

#### Component Description:
The KEMET T498D106K050ATE1K0 is a high-temperature, automotive-grade tantalum surface mount capacitor. It is designed to operate reliably in environments with temperatures up to 150°C, making it suitable for demanding applications. The capacitor features a manganese dioxide (MnO2) dielectric and is constructed to withstand high levels of electrical stress while maintaining performance.

#### Typical Applications:

- **Decoupling and Filtering**: Essential for stabilizing voltage and reducing noise in power supply circuits.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Commonly found in automotive and industrial applications where efficient power conversion is critical.
- **Portable Electronics**: Suitable for devices that require compact and reliable energy storage.
- **Telecommunications**: Used in control units and communication devices that operate under high-temperature conditions.

This capacitor is particularly valuable in automotive applications due to its high reliability and compliance with automotive standards, ensuring performance in harsh environments.
