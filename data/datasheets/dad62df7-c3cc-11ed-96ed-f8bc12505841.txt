  - Dissipation Factor (tanδ): 14% at 120 Hz and 20°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Hybrid conductive polymer technology
  - Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance) of 20 mΩ at 100 kHz and 20°C
  - Capacitance tolerance of -20% to +20% at 120 Hz and 20°C
  - Leakage Current (ILEAK): 82.5 µA for 2 minutes at 20°C
  - Not AEC-Q200 qualified
  - Not vibration proof

  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The YC331M025G105PTR is a hybrid conductive polymer capacitor designed for surface mount applications. It features a capacitance of 330 µF and is rated for a maximum voltage of 25 VDC. This capacitor is characterized by its low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high ripple current capabilities, making it suitable for applications requiring efficient energy storage and delivery.

#### Typical Applications:
This component is typically used in:
- Power management systems
- Signal processing circuits
- DC-DC converters
- Audio equipment
- Consumer electronics where space-saving and high performance are critical

The YC series capacitors are particularly beneficial in applications that demand long life and reliability under high temperature and ripple current conditions.
