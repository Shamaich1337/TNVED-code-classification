### Component Summary: IGA200-APD

**Manufacturer:** OTRON Electronic Technology Co., Ltd  
**Product Code:** IGA200-APD  
**Type:** InGaAs Avalanche Photodiode (APD)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 40V (Min) to 60V (Max)
  - Operating Voltage: 0.9 * V(BR)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Dark Current: 60 nA (Min) to 96 nA (Max) at M=10, T=298K
  - Absolute Reverse Current: 3 mA
  - Absolute Forward Current: 5 mA
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Not explicitly stated; however, the low dark current indicates low power consumption in operation.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -75°C to +75°C
  - Storage: -80°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Hermetically sealed in a modified TO-46 package.

- **Special Features:**
  - Back illumination planar APD
  - High operation frequency (Bandwidth: 550 MHz)
  - Low voltage operation
  - Large active area diameter (Φ200 µm)
  - Low dark current
  - Spectral range from 950 nm to 1700 nm, optimized for 1550 nm.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The IGA200-APD is a large-area InGaAs avalanche photodiode designed for applications requiring high sensitivity and low noise in the near-infrared spectrum. It is particularly optimized for use in eye-safe laser range-finding systems, making it suitable for various optical applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Range Finding:** Utilized in distance measurement systems, particularly in environments where safety is a concern due to laser usage.
- **Optical Communication Systems:** Employed in fiber optic communication for signal detection.
- **Optical Time-Domain Reflectometry (OTDR):** Used for testing and monitoring fiber optic cables.
- **Low Light Level Detection:** Effective in applications requiring detection of weak optical signals.

This component is ideal for systems that require precise distance measurements and reliable performance in low-light conditions, making it a versatile choice in both industrial and research applications.
