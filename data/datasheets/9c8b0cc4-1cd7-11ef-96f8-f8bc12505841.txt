### Component Summary: BSS138PW,115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 60 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 320 mA
  - Peak Drain Current (IDM): 1.2 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 10 μs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot) at 25°C: 260 mW (maximum)
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT323 (SC-70) plastic surface-mounted package; 3 leads

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Logic-level compatible
  - Very fast switching capabilities
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Utilizes Trench MOSFET technology

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT packages are MSL 1 or 2.

#### Description:
The BSS138PW is an N-channel enhancement mode Field-Effect Transistor (FET) designed for efficient switching applications. It employs Trench MOSFET technology, which allows for lower on-state resistance and faster switching speeds compared to traditional MOSFETs. This component is particularly suitable for applications requiring compact size and high performance.

#### Typical Applications:
