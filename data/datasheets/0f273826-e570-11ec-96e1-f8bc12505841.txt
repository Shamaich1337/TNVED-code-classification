#### Description:
The USM-G4M is an **Ultrasonic Gas Meter Module** designed to measure the flow velocity of gas using ultrasonic transducers based on the Time of Flight (TOF) principle. It calculates the consumed gas volume and displays it on the meter's screen. The module is equipped with built-in temperature sensors and smart algorithms to ensure accurate measurements across various gas types.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Natural Gas Metering:** Used in residential and commercial gas metering applications to accurately measure gas consumption.
- **Gas Detection Equipment:** Can be integrated into systems for monitoring gas levels and ensuring safety in environments where explosive gases may be present.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for use in various industrial processes where gas flow measurement is critical for operational efficiency and safety.

This module is particularly valuable in applications requiring precise gas flow measurement, low power consumption, and compliance with safety standards in hazardous environments.
