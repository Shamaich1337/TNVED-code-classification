#### Description:
The C1210C102FBGACTU is a high voltage multilayer ceramic capacitor designed for surface mount applications. It utilizes a C0G dielectric, which is known for its stability and reliability across a wide range of temperatures and voltages. This capacitor is particularly suited for applications requiring high precision and stability, such as resonant circuits and timing applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **High Frequency Power Converters:** Used in power management systems to filter and stabilize voltage.
- **Wide Bandgap (WBG) Systems:** Suitable for silicon carbide (SiC) and gallium nitride (GaN) applications, which require high voltage and high frequency performance.
- **Snubber Circuits:** Effective in protecting circuits from voltage spikes.
- **Resonant Circuits:** Commonly used in LLC resonant converters and wireless charging systems.
- **Timing and Filtering Applications:** Provides stable capacitance for timing circuits and filtering in various electronic devices.

This capacitor is ideal for applications where high voltage, low loss, and stable performance are critical, making it a preferred choice among design engineers in various industries.
