#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.0 V to 5.5 V
  - Positive Output Supply Voltage (V+): 0.3 V to 7 V
  - Negative Output Supply Voltage (V-): 0.3 V to -7 V
  - Input Voltage Range (Receivers): -25 V to +25 V
  - Driver Output Voltage Range: -13.2 V to +13.2 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (I_CC): 300 µA (typical)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Supply Current: 300 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package), 16 pins

- **Special Features:**
  - ±15 kV ESD protection (Human Body Model)
  - Qualified for automotive applications
  - Meets TIA/EIA-232-F and ITU v.28 standards
  - Accepts 5-V logic input with 3.3-V supply
  - Operates up to 250 kbit/s data signaling rates

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TRS3232EQPWRQ1** is a multichannel RS-232 line driver/receiver designed for automotive applications. It features two line drivers and two line receivers, along with a dual charge-pump circuit that allows operation from a single supply voltage ranging from 3 V to 5.5 V. The device is equipped with robust ESD protection, making it suitable for environments where electrical noise and surges are common.

#### Typical Applications:
The TRS3232EQPWRQ1 is primarily used in:
- **Automotive Communication Systems:** Facilitating serial communication between microcontrollers and peripherals.
- **Data Communication:** Connecting devices that require RS-232 communication, such as modems and printers.
- **Industrial Automation:** Used in systems that require reliable data transmission over longer distances.
- **Embedded Systems:** Ideal for applications where low power consumption and high ESD protection are critical.

This component is particularly valuable in applications that demand robust performance in harsh environments, ensuring reliable data transmission while adhering to automotive standards.
