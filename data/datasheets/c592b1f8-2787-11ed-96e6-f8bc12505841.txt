### Component Summary: LL4148

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** LL4148  
**Type:** Small Signal Fast Switching Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 75 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)): 100 V (at IR = 100 µA)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (IF): 300 mA
  - Average Forward Current (IFAV): 150 mA
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 2 A (for tp = 1 µs)
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 500 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot): 500 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (Tj): 175 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - MELF SOD-80 (LL-35) and MELF (LL-34)

- **Special Features:**
  - Fast switching capability
  - Low reverse recovery time (trr): 4 ns to 8 ns depending on conditions
  - Compliant with RoHS standards

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The LL4148 is a small signal fast switching diode designed for general-purpose applications. It is characterized by its ability to handle moderate voltage and current levels while providing fast switching capabilities, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Processing:** Used in signal rectification and clamping applications.
- **Power Management:** Ideal for voltage regulation and protection circuits.
- **Switching Applications:** Commonly employed in high-speed switching applications due to its low reverse recovery time.
- **General Electronics:** Utilized in various consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial equipment.

This diode is particularly useful in applications where fast response times are critical, such as in digital circuits and RF applications.
