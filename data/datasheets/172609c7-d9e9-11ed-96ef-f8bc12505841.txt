- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Ihold (Hold Current)**: 7.00 Amperes
  - **Itrip (Trip Current)**: 14.00 Amperes

- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL)**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low ohmic PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) resettable fuse
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Designed for automated assembly with a small footprint suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Description:
The **MF-USML700-2** is a low ohmic PTC resettable fuse designed for surface mount applications. Its primary function is to provide overcurrent protection by resetting itself after a fault condition is cleared. When the current exceeds the hold current (Ihold), the device will trip and limit the current flow, preventing damage to the circuit. Once the fault is removed, the fuse resets automatically, allowing normal operation to resume.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Thermal Protection**: Commonly used in lithium-ion and polymer battery packs to prevent overheating and potential fire hazards.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Ideal for use in game consoles, mobile phones, digital cameras, and PC motherboards.
- **USB Port Protection**: Protects USB 2.0, 3.0, and OTG ports from overcurrent conditions.

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and where automatic reset capabilities are desired, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic devices.
