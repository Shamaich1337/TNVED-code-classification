### Component Summary: 1.5KE36CA (Littelfuse)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Stand-off Voltage (VR): 30.80 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 34.20 V
  - Maximum Clamping Voltage (VC) at Ipp: 49.9 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Current (Ipp): 30.5 A
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR) at VR: 1 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPPM): 1500 W (single die), 2000 W (stacked die)
  - Steady State Power Dissipation (PD): 6.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to 175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - DO-201 molded plastic body

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Fast response time: typically less than 1.0 ps
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - ESD protection: 30 kV (Air), 30 kV (Contact)
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **1.5KE36CA** is a **Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode** designed to protect sensitive electronic equipment from voltage transients caused by lightning and other transient voltage events. It is particularly effective in safeguarding I/O interfaces, VCC buses, and other vulnerable circuits in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting communication lines from voltage spikes.
- **Computers:** Safeguarding data lines and power supply circuits.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Protecting control circuits and sensitive components from transient voltages.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Ensuring the reliability of devices by preventing damage from electrical surges.

This component is essential in applications where voltage transients can cause significant damage, ensuring the longevity and reliability of electronic systems.
