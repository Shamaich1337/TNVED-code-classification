- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (V_CC): 2 V to 5.5 V
  - Input Voltage Range: -0.5 V to 7 V
  - Output Voltage Range: -0.5 V to V_CC + 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±25 mA
  - Input Clamp Current: -20 mA
  - Output Clamp Current: ±20 mA
  - Continuous Current through V_CC or GND: ±75 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation Capacitance (C_pd): 114 pF (at 5 V, f = 10 MHz)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package), 16 pins

- **Special Features:**
  - Qualified for Automotive Applications
  - 8-bit Serial-In, Parallel-Out Shift Register
  - Direct Clear (SRCLR) input
  - Cascading capability via serial output
  - High-impedance state outputs when OE (Output Enable) is high

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SN74AHC595QPWRQ1** is an 8-bit shift register with 3-state output registers designed for automotive applications. It features a serial-in, parallel-out architecture, allowing data to be shifted in serially and then output in parallel. The device includes a direct overriding clear input (SRCLR) to reset the shift register, and separate clocks for the shift and storage registers, enabling precise control over data flow. The outputs can be placed in a high-impedance state, making it suitable for bus-oriented applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Storage and Transfer:** Used in applications where data needs to be shifted and stored, such as in digital signal processing.
- **Automotive Systems:** Ideal for automotive electronics due to its qualification for automotive applications, including dashboard displays and control systems.
- **Microcontroller Interfaces:** Can be used to expand the number of output pins available from a microcontroller by shifting data into the register and outputting it in parallel.
- **LED Control:** Commonly used in LED display systems where multiple outputs are needed to control individual LEDs or segments.

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where space is limited, and efficient data handling is required, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
