### Component Summary: ERA-9SM+

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (Vd): 3.6 V to 4.4 V (typical 4.0 V)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Recommended Operating Current (Id): 50 mA
  - Maximum Operating Current: 74 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power at 1 dB compression: 
    - 14.3 dBm at 0.1 GHz
    - 14.1 dBm at 1 GHz
    - 14.1 dBm at 2 GHz
    - 12.5 dBm at 4 GHz
    - 10.9 dBm at 5 GHz
    - 7.9 dBm at 8 GHz
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage: -65°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Micro-X package, 0.085” diameter
- **Special Features:**
  - Wideband operation from DC to 8 GHz
  - Good gain flatness of ±0.9 dB
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - Low additive phase noise, typically -173 dBc/Hz @ 10 KHz offset
  - Aqueous washable
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Description:**
The ERA-9SM+ is a wideband, low-gain monolithic amplifier fabricated using HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) technology. It is designed for applications requiring a medium dynamic range and offers repeatable performance across different production lots. The amplifier is housed in a compact micro-X package, which provides excellent thermal performance.

**Typical Applications:**
The ERA-9SM+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular and PCS communication systems
- Communication receivers and transmitters
- Satellite communication systems
- Military applications
- Instrumentation requiring low phase noise and broadband amplification

This component is particularly advantageous in scenarios where a low gain increase is needed in an amplifier chain, allowing for reduced component count by eliminating the need for separate amplifiers and attenuators. Its broadband capabilities make it ideal for diverse RF applications.
