### Component Summary: PICIR-1550-3-9-10-FA

**Description:**
The PICIR-1550-3-9-10-FA is a 3-port polarization insensitive optical circulator designed for use in fiber optic applications. It operates at a center wavelength of 1550 nm and is constructed using SMF-28e fiber. The primary function of this component is to change the signal light transmission path, allowing signals to be routed from one port to another while providing high isolation to block back reflection light.
