### Component Summary: SN65LVDS93DGGR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.3V (typical), with a range of 3.0V to 3.6V.
  - Input Voltage Range: -0.5V to 5.5V.
  - Output Voltage Range: -0.5V to VCC + 0.5V.

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Current (I_CC): 95 mA (typical).
  - Short-Circuit Output Current (I_OS): ±12 mA.

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: 250 mW.
  - Power Consumption when Disabled: < 1 mW.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C.

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP (Thin Shrink Small Outline Package), 56 pins.

- **Special Features:**
  - LVDS (Low-Voltage Differential Signaling) transmitter.
  - Supports 28 data channels plus clock.
  - Selectable rising or falling clock edge triggered inputs.
  - Integrated phase-locked loop (PLL) for clock synchronization.
  - 6 kV HBM ESD protection on bus pins.
  - No external components required for PLL operation.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The **SN65LVDS93** is a low-voltage differential signaling (LVDS) serializer/deserializer (SERDES) transmitter designed for high-speed data transmission. It converts 28 bits of single-ended LVTTL data into a serial format for transmission over differential pairs, significantly reducing electromagnetic interference (EMI) and allowing for longer transmission distances. The device integrates multiple shift registers and a clock synthesizer, enabling efficient data handling and synchronization.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Communication:** Ideal for point-to-point communication systems where low EMI and high data rates are critical.
- **Backplane Interfaces:** Used in high-speed backplane applications to extend data buses over longer distances.
- **Bus Extension:** Suitable for extending 16-bit or wider data buses in industrial and telecommunications applications.
- **Virtual Backplane Transceivers:** Can be implemented in systems requiring virtual backplane architectures, allowing for flexible data routing and management.

The SN65LVDS93 is particularly useful in environments where space is limited and power consumption needs to be minimized while maintaining high data integrity and speed.
