### Component Summary: WMB60P03TA

**Description:**
The WMB60P03TA is a 30V P-Channel Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET designed using advanced power trench technology. This technology minimizes on-state resistance while maintaining superior switching performance, making it suitable for high-efficiency applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): -30V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=25°C: -60A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC=100°C: -38A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): -240A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at TC=25°C: 48W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - PDFN 5060-8L

- **Special Features:**
  - RDS(on) < 8.5mΩ @ VGS = -10V
  - RDS(on) < 11mΩ @ VGS = -4.5V
  - Low Gate Charge
  - 100% EAS Guaranteed
  - Green Device Available

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The WMB60P03TA is primarily used in:
- Power management switches
- Battery protection applications

This MOSFET is ideal for applications requiring efficient power control and management, particularly in battery-operated devices and systems where low on-resistance and high current handling are critical. Its advanced technology allows for effective thermal management and reliability in various electronic circuits.
