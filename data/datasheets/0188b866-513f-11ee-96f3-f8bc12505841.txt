- **Voltage Ratings:**

  - Surge Voltage (VS): 1.15 × VR = 40.25V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Ripple Current at +125°C: 3040 mA rms
  - Maximum Ripple Current at +135°C: 1860 mA rms

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Leakage Current (ILEAK) at 20°C with VR applied: ≤ 0.03 × CR × VR or 4µA, whichever is greater (after 2 minutes).

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +135°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Through-Hole Technology (THT) type

- **Special Features:**
  - Endurance of 135°C for 2000 to 3000 hours.
  - Short-term load capability at 150°C for 100 hours guaranteed.
  - AEC-Q200 version available for automotive applications.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

The TU332M035Z400A is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor designed for high-temperature and high-ripple applications, particularly suitable for automotive environments. It features a robust construction that allows it to operate at elevated temperatures up to 135°C, making it ideal for applications where heat dissipation is a concern.

- **Industrial Equipment:** Its ability to withstand high temperatures makes it suitable for industrial applications where ambient conditions can be extreme.
- **Consumer Electronics:** It can also be utilized in consumer electronics that require reliable performance under varying temperature conditions.
