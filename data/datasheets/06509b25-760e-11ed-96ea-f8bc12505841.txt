### Component Summary: HMC391LP4ETR

**Description:**
The HMC391LP4ETR is a GaAs InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor (HBT) MMIC Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) with an integrated buffer amplifier. It operates within the frequency range of 3.9 to 4.45 GHz and is designed for applications requiring low phase noise and high stability.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +3V (max +3.5V)
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 0 to +10V (max +11V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 30 mA (typical), up to 40 mA (max)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: +5.0 dBm (typical)
  - Supply Power: 90 mW (at +3V and 30 mA)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 24 Lead QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) package, dimensions 4 x 4 mm
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent phase noise performance: -106 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - No external resonator required
  - Integrated buffer amplifier
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC391LP4ETR is suitable for various applications, including:
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) and microwave radio systems
- Radio altimetry
- Test equipment and industrial controls
- Military communications and radar systems

This component is particularly valued for its low noise characteristics and robust performance in demanding environments, making it ideal for high-frequency signal generation and modulation tasks.
