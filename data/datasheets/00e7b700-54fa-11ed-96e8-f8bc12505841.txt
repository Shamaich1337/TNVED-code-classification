The GF1000 Ferrite Sheet is an electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding material designed to absorb electromagnetic waves. #### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Devices:** Used in NFC (Near Field Communication) function mobile phones and contactless IC cards to improve communication efficiency.
