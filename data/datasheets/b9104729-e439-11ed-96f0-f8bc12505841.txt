**Type:** RF Power LDMOS Transistor  
**Product Code:** AFT27S010NT1

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDSS): -0.5 to +65 Vdc
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): -6.0 to +10 Vdc
  - Operating Voltage (VDD): 32 Vdc (max)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Drain Current (IDQ): 90 mA (typical for performance tests)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Average Output Power (Pout): 1.26 W (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Case Operating Temperature: -40 to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature: -40 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PLD-1.5W (Plastic)

- **Special Features:**
  - Designed for digital predistortion error correction systems.
  - Greater negative gate-source voltage range for improved Class C operation.
  - Universal broadband driven device with internal RF feedback.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 3 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E), with a peak temperature of 260 °C.

#### Description:
The AFT27S010NT1 is an N-channel enhancement-mode lateral MOSFET designed specifically for RF power amplification applications. It operates efficiently across a wide frequency range of 728 MHz to 3600 MHz, making it suitable for cellular base station applications. The device is optimized for high linearity and efficiency, particularly in W-CDMA (Wideband Code Division Multiple Access) systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Base Stations:** The AFT27S010NT1 is primarily used in cellular infrastructure, providing the necessary power amplification for transmitting signals over long distances.
- **RF Power Amplifiers:** It is suitable for use in RF power amplifiers that require high efficiency and linearity, particularly in modern communication systems.
- **Digital Predistortion Systems:** The transistor is designed to work with digital predistortion techniques, which help to improve signal quality and reduce distortion in RF transmissions.

This component is ideal for engineers and designers working on RF amplification solutions in telecommunications, ensuring robust performance in demanding environments.
