### Component Summary for MK02/0-1A66-500W

**Component Type:** Reed Proximity Switch

**Description:**
The MK02/0-1A66-500W is a reed proximity switch designed to operate in the presence of magnetically conductive materials. Unlike traditional reed switches that require an actuating magnet, this sensor can be activated by a simple piece of iron. It is part of the MK2 series from MEDER Electronic, which specializes in ferromagnetic metal detection sensors.

### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Switching Voltage: Up to 200 V DC or Peak AC
  - Breakdown Voltage: 310 V DC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Switching Current: 0.5 A DC or Peak AC
  - Carry Current: 1.75 A DC or Peak AC

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Rating: 10 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -20 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature: -35 °C to 85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Round cable with dimensions of 2 x 0.35 mm² (AWG22), with a length of 500 mm.

- **Special Features:**
  - Available in Form A and B configurations.
  - High power switches are available.
  - Various cable terminations and lengths can be customized.
  - The sensor should not be mounted on ferromagnetic materials or with ferromagnetic screws for optimal performance.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

### Typical Applications:
- **Industrial Applications:**
  - End travel sensing limit switches in pneumatic cylinders.
  - Position control in various machinery.
  - Control functions in plant and utility vehicles.
  - Security applications, including door and window control.
  - Opening recognition contacts for fire protection doors.

### Conclusion:
The MK02/0-1A66-500W reed proximity switch is a versatile and robust sensor suitable for various industrial applications where the detection of magnetically conductive materials is required. Its ability to operate without a traditional magnet makes it particularly useful in environments where space or mounting options are limited.
