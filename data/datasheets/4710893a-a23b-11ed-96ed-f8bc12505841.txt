### Component Summary: IRF6648TRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** IRF6648TRPBF  
**Package Type:** DirectFET™ Medium Can, Tape and Reel (Quantity: 4800)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): 60V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 25°C: 86A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 70°C: 69A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 260A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at TA = 25°C: 2.8W
  - Power Dissipation at TA = 70°C: 1.8W
  - Power Dissipation at TC = 25°C: 89W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -40°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** DirectFET™ (Low Profile, <0.7mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low on-state resistance (RDS(on)): 5.5mΩ at VGS = 10V
  - Dual-sided cooling capability for improved thermal performance
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free (Qualified up to 260°C reflow)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020D)

#### Description:
The **IRF6648TRPBF** is a high-performance N-channel Power MOSFET that utilizes Infineon's HEXFET® technology combined with the advanced DirectFET™ packaging. This design allows for a very low on-state resistance and enables efficient thermal management through dual-sided cooling. The component is optimized for synchronous rectification applications and is compatible with existing PCB layouts and assembly techniques.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Ideal for synchronous rectification circuits with output voltages ranging from 5V to 12V.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Suitable for use as a primary side switch in 24V input forward converters.
- **High-Efficiency Systems:** The low conduction losses and high thermal performance make it suitable for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, such as power supplies and converters in consumer electronics and industrial applications.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the IRF6648TRPBF, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical use cases in electronic systems.
