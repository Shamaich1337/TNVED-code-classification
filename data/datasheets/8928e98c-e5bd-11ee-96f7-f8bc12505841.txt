### Component Summary: HMC773ALC3B

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Not explicitly stated; operates within specified input power limits.
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - IF Source and Sink Current: 2 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Continuous Power Dissipation (at T = 85°C): 400 mW (derated by 4.44 mW/°C above 85°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range**: -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type**: E-12-41 (ceramic LCC package)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: MSL3 (maximum peak reflow temperature: 260°C)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Double balanced mixer
  - Excellent LO to RF and LO to IF suppression
  - Compatible with high volume, surface-mount manufacturing techniques

#### Description:
The HMC773ALC3B is a general-purpose double balanced mixer designed for use as both an upconverter and downconverter. It operates effectively across a frequency range of 6 GHz to 26 GHz, converting radio frequencies (RF) to intermediate frequencies (IF) and vice versa. The device is optimized for local oscillator (LO) drives of 13 dBm or higher, ensuring efficient performance with low conversion loss and noise figure.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Communication Systems**: Used in RF front-end modules for wireless communication, including satellite and terrestrial systems.
- **Signal Processing**: Ideal for applications requiring frequency translation, such as in radar and electronic warfare systems.
- **Test Equipment**: Utilized in laboratory setups for signal generation and analysis.
- **Broadcasting**: Employed in upconversion and downconversion processes in broadcasting equipment.

The HMC773ALC3B is particularly suited for applications where high performance and reliability are critical, making it a versatile choice for engineers in the RF and microwave domains.
