#### Description:
The KEMET C0603C104J4REC7867 is a surface mount multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) utilizing X7R dielectric material. It is designed for applications requiring reliable performance in automotive and commercial electronics, particularly where electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection is critical. The capacitor is non-polarized, which simplifies circuit design and installation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection**: Protects sensitive components from ESD events during assembly or operation.
- **Integrated Circuit (IC) Protection**: Used in circuits to safeguard ICs from voltage spikes.
- **Radio Frequency (RF) Filtering**: Suitable for filtering applications in RF circuits.
- **Automotive Applications**: Commonly found in controllers, navigation systems, airbags, and keyless entry systems, where reliability and compliance with automotive standards are essential.

This capacitor is particularly valuable in environments where temperature stability and ESD resilience are paramount, making it suitable for a wide range of electronic applications.
