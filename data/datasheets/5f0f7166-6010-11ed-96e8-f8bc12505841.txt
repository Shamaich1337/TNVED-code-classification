### Component Summary: RX682M600XB20E67RTZL

**Component Type:**  
Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor

**Description:**  
The RX682M600XB20E67RTZL is a high-capacity aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's RX series, designed for ultra-long life at elevated temperatures. It features a screw-type terminal for secure mounting and is suitable for applications requiring high reliability and performance.

### Key Specifications:

- **Capacitance:** 6800 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 600 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 660 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR):** 
  - 26.9 A at 120Hz and 85°C
  - 37.66 A at 100kHz and 85°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ):** 25% at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK):** 5000 µA (5 min at 20°C)
- **Typical ESR:** 16 mΩ at 120Hz and 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:** Screw type, dimensions 89 mm (D) x 220 mm (L)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

### Special Features:
- Designed for ultra-long life at high temperatures (up to 85°C).
- High ripple current capability, making it suitable for demanding applications.
- Endurance and useful life tests indicate reliability under specified conditions.

### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits, smoothing and filtering applications.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Suitable for use in machinery and equipment that require stable power supply.
- **Renewable Energy Systems:** Can be utilized in solar inverters and wind turbine controllers.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Employed in devices that require high capacitance for energy storage and filtering.

This capacitor is ideal for applications where high reliability and long operational life are critical, particularly in environments with high temperatures and varying load conditions.
