- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IHLP5050FDERR30M01 is a high-performance shielded inductor from Vishay's IHLP series, designed for applications requiring high saturation current and low DC resistance. It is a surface-mount device (SMD) that provides efficient energy storage and filtering in power management circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Notebook / Desktop / Server Applications**: Used in power supply circuits to manage voltage levels and reduce noise.
- **High Current Point of Load (POL) Converters**: Ideal for converting voltage levels in high-efficiency power supplies.
- **Low Profile, High Current Power Supplies**: Suitable for compact designs where space is limited.
- **Battery Powered Devices**: Helps in managing power efficiently to extend battery life.
- **DC/DC Converters in Distributed Power Systems**: Used in systems where power needs to be distributed across multiple components.
- **DC/DC Converter for Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA)**: Provides stable power supply for FPGAs which require precise voltage levels.

This inductor is particularly beneficial in applications where high efficiency and low noise are critical, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
