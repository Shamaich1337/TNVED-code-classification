### Component Summary: HS1K (from HS1A to HS1M series)

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** HS1K

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 800 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 560 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 800 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1.0 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 30 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave)
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 60 A (non-repetitive, 1ms square wave)

- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but related to forward voltage drop and current ratings.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement.
  - Glass passivated chip junction.
  - High forward surge capability.
  - Super fast reverse recovery time.
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1, per J-STD-020, with a maximum peak of 260 °C.

#### Description:
The HS1K is a surface mount high-efficiency rectifier diode. Its primary function is to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) in various electronic applications. The diode is designed to handle high voltage and current levels, making it suitable for demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supplies:** Used in the rectification process to convert AC input to DC output.
- **Inverters:** Essential in converting DC back to AC, particularly in renewable energy systems.
- **Converters:** Utilized in various types of DC-DC converters for voltage regulation.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Commonly found in inductive load applications to protect against voltage spikes.
- **Consumer Electronics and Telecommunications:** Employed in devices requiring efficient power management and signal processing.
