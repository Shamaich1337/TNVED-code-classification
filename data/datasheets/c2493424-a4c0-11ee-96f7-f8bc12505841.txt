The THN 20-2411WIR is a ruggedized 20 Watt DC/DC converter designed for high reliability in harsh environments, particularly suitable for railway and transportation systems. The converter operates efficiently with a maximum efficiency of 90%, allowing it to function across a wide input voltage range without derating.

- **Railway Applications:** Due to its compliance with EN 50155 and EN 61373 standards, it is ideal for use in rolling stock and other transportation systems.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Its rugged design makes it suitable for various industrial applications where reliability is critical.
- **Telecommunications:** The converter can be used in telecom equipment that requires stable power supply under varying conditions.
- **Embedded Systems:** It is suitable for powering embedded systems that operate in harsh environments.
