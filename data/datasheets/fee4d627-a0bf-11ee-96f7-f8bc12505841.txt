### Component Summary: ESD24VD3B

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** ESD24VD3B  
**Type:** Bi-directional Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Stand-off Voltage (VRWM):** ±24V Max
- **Peak Pulse Power (Ppk):** 300W (t_p = 8/20μs)
- **Peak Pulse Current (IPP):** 7A (t_p = 8/20μs)
- **ESD Protection Level:** ±30kV (contact and air discharge, IEC61000-4-2)
- **Reverse Leakage Current (IR):** 0.5μA at VRWM = 24V
- **Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR):** 25V (min), 32V (max) at IBR = 1mA
- **Clamping Voltage (VCL):** 
  - 33V at IPP = 16A, t_p = 100ns
  - 28V to 32V at IPP = 1A, t_p = 8/20μs
  - 38V to 44V at IPP = 7A, t_p = 8/20μs
- **Operating Temperature Range (TOP):** -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range (TSTG):** -55°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:** SOD-323
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 per J-STD-020E
- **Lead Finish:** Matte Tin
- **Case Material:** “Green” Molding Compound
- **RoHS Compliant:** Yes

#### Description:
The ESD24VD3B is a bi-directional transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge (ESD) and other transient events. It features a low clamping voltage and low leakage current, making it suitable for applications requiring reliable protection without significant power loss.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Handsets and Accessories:** Protects against ESD damage in mobile devices.
- **Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs):** Ensures the longevity and reliability of handheld devices.
- **Notebooks and Handhelds:** Provides transient protection for portable computing devices.
- **Portable Instrumentation:** Safeguards sensitive measurement equipment from voltage spikes.
- **Peripherals:** Protects USB and other peripheral devices from ESD.
- **Pagers:** Ensures reliable operation of communication devices.
- **Desktop and Servers:** Protects critical components in computing environments from transient voltages.

This component is essential in various electronic applications where ESD protection is critical to maintaining device integrity and performance.
