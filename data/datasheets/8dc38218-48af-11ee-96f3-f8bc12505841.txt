### Component Summary: SMBJ33A by Littelfuse

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Stand-off voltage (V_R): 33 V
  - Breakdown voltage (V_BR): 36.7 V (typical at 25 °C)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum reverse leakage current (I_RM): 0.2 µA at 25 °C
  - Maximum reverse leakage current (I_RM): 1 µA at 85 °C
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Peak pulse power dissipation: 600 W (10/1000 µs)
  - High power capability at T_j max: 515 W (10/1000 µs)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction temperature (T_j): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SMB (DO-214AA)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Unidirectional and bidirectional types available
  - Low leakage current
  - High power capability
  - Complies with IEC 61000-4-2 level 4 (15 kV air discharge, 8 kV contact discharge)
  - Resin meets UL 94, V0
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

#### Description:
The **SMBJ33A** is a **Transil™** device designed for transient voltage suppression. It is primarily used to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharges (ESD) and electrical overstress. The SMBJ series is known for its low leakage current and high power handling capabilities, making it suitable for high-end applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electrostatic Discharge Protection**: Protects sensitive equipment from ESD according to IEC 61000-4-2 standards.
- **Surge Protection**: Used in applications where voltage surges may occur, such as in power management systems and signal processing circuits.
- **High-End Equipment**: Ideal for use in switch-mode power supplies (SMPS) and other high-reliability applications where stability over time is critical.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in devices that require protection from voltage spikes, ensuring longevity and reliability.
