**Product Code:** BZX84C7V5  
**Component Type:** Zener Diode

#### Key Specifications:

- **Storage Temperature Range (TSTG):** -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** SOT-23
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOT-23 packages are MSL 1 or 2.
- **Special Features:** High reliability, very sharp reverse characteristic, low reverse current level.

#### Description:
The BZX84C7V5 is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It provides a stable reference voltage of 7.5V, making it suitable for various electronic circuits that require voltage clamping or regulation. The diode exhibits low reverse leakage current and high reliability, which are critical for maintaining performance in sensitive electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Clamping Circuits:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Reference Voltage Sources:** Provides a stable reference voltage for analog circuits.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Used in signal processing applications to stabilize voltage levels.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and any circuit where voltage regulation is necessary to ensure the proper functioning of electronic components.
