MicroSiP ™power module isbased onDCS-Control ™(Direct\nControl with Seamless transition intoPower Save Mode). DCS-Control ™\nsupports both operation modes using asingle building block andtherefore hasaseamless transition from PWM\ntoPSM without effects ontheoutput voltage. ### Component Summary: TPS82140SILR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 3.0 V to 17 V
  - Output Voltage Range: 0.9 V to 6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: 2 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Quiescent Current: 20 µA (typical)
  - Shutdown Current: 1.5 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - MicroSiP™ Module, 8-Pin SIL (3.0 mm x 2.8 mm x 1.5 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - DCS-Control™ topology for efficient operation
  - Power Save Mode for light load efficiency
  - Programmable soft startup with tracking
  - Thermal shutdown protection
  - Power Good output
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS82140** is a highly integrated step-down converter MicroSiP™ power module designed for applications requiring a compact solution with high efficiency. It integrates an asynchronous step-down converter and an inductor, significantly reducing the number of external components and PCB area required for implementation. The device operates using DCS-Control™ topology, which allows for seamless transitions between PWM and Power Save modes, optimizing efficiency across varying load conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The TPS82140 is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- **Industrial Applications:** Powering sensors and control systems in industrial environments.
- **Telecom and Networking Applications:** Providing stable power for communication devices and networking equipment.
- **Solid State Drives (SSDs):** Serving as a power supply for storage devices that require efficient voltage regulation.
- **Inverting Power Supply:** Can be configured for applications needing negative voltage outputs.

This module is particularly advantageous in battery-powered applications due to its low quiescent current and ability to maintain high efficiency across a wide range of load conditions. The compact size and integrated design make it ideal for automated assembly processes and space-constrained designs.
