### Component Summary: HF41F/24-HST Relay

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Nominal Coil Voltage: 24 VDC
  - Max Switching Voltage: 400 VAC / 125 VDC
  - Max Switching Power: 1500 VA / 180 W

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Max Switching Current: 6 A
  - Contact Rating (Resistive Load): 6 A at 277 VAC / 30 VDC

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Coil Power: 170 mW (for 5 to 24 VDC), 210 mW (for 48 VDC, 60 VDC)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Subminiature relay, dimensions: 28.0 x 5.0 x 15.0 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High breakdown voltage of 4 kV between coil and contacts
  - Surge voltage up to 6 kV between coil and contacts
  - High sensitivity with low coil power consumption
  - Available in 1 Form A and 1 Form C configurations
  - RoHS compliant (environmentally friendly)
  - Sealed IP67, flux proof construction

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The HF41F/24-HST is a subminiature power relay designed for switching applications. It features a compact design with a slim width of 5 mm, making it suitable for space-constrained environments. The relay operates with a nominal coil voltage of 24 VDC and is capable of switching high voltages and currents, making it versatile for various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The HF41F relay is commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** For controlling power circuits in various electronic devices.
- **Signal Processing:** In automation systems where signal switching is required.
- **Home Appliances:** For controlling motors, lights, and other electrical components.
- **Industrial Equipment:** In control panels and machinery for reliable switching operations.
- **Automotive Applications:** For controlling various electrical systems within vehicles.

This relay is ideal for applications requiring reliable performance in harsh environments, thanks to its sealed construction and high insulation resistance.
