### Component Summary: VS-30BQ060HM3/9AT

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** VS-30BQ060HM3

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Reverse Voltage (VR): 60 V
  - Maximum Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Current (IF(AV)): 3.0 A (at 50% duty cycle, T_L = 123 °C)
  - Maximum Peak One Cycle Non-Repetitive Surge Current (IFSM): 1200 A (tp = 5 μs sine)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 3.0 A, T_J = 125 °C: 0.52 V
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM) at 125 °C: 20 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case: SMC (DO-214AB)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - Guard ring for enhanced ruggedness and long-term reliability
  - High frequency operation
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (automotive grade)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The VS-30BQ060HM3 is a high-performance Schottky rectifier designed for applications that require low forward voltage drop and compact size. It is a surface mount device (SMD) that provides efficient rectification in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in switching power supplies and converters to improve efficiency.
- **Data Storage:** Commonly found in disk drives for rectification purposes.
- **Battery Charging:** Utilized in battery charging circuits for its low forward voltage drop.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Acts as a freewheeling diode in inductive load applications.
- **Reverse Battery Protection:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, making it suitable for modern electronic devices and automotive applications.
