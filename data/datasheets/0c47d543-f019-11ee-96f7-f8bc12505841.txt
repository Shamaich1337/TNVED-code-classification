#### Description:
The FH12-10S-0.5SH(55) is a 0.5mm pitch FPC/FFC (Flexible Printed Circuit/Flexible Flat Cable) connector designed for high-density applications. It features a one-touch rotating ZIF mechanism that allows for easy insertion and removal of the FPC/FFC without applying excessive force, which helps to prevent damage to the contacts. The connector is suitable for surface mounting and is designed to provide reliable electrical connections in compact spaces.
