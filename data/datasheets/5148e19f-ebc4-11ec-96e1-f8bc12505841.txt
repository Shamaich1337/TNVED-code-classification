### Component Summary: Xilinx Alveo U280 Data Center Accelerator Card (A-U280-P32G-PQ-G)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Not explicitly stated in the provided text.
- **Current Ratings**: Total electrical card load is 215W for the passive cooling version.
- **Power Consumption**: 215W (passive cooling version).
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Operating: 0°C to 45°C
  - Storage: -40°C to 75°C
- **Package Type**: Full height, ¾ length, dual width (passive cooling).
- **Special Features**:
  - Equipped with 8 GB of HBM2 memory and two 16 GB DDR4 RDIMMs.
  - Supports PCI Express Gen3 x16 or Gen4 x8.
  - Two QSFP28 Ethernet ports capable of 100 Gb/s each.
  - Designed for memory-bound, compute-intensive applications.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E)**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The Xilinx Alveo U280 is a Data Center accelerator card designed to enhance the performance of compute-intensive applications. It features a custom-built UltraScale+ FPGA (XCU280) that integrates high-bandwidth memory (HBM2) and DDR4 memory, making it suitable for tasks that require significant data processing capabilities. The card is available in both active and passive cooling configurations, with the passive version being lighter and designed for environments where airflow is managed externally.

#### Typical Applications:
The Alveo U280 accelerator card is primarily used in:
- **Database Analytics**: Accelerating data processing and query execution.
- **Machine Learning Inference**: Enhancing the performance of AI models during inference stages.
- **High-Performance Computing (HPC)**: Supporting complex computations in scientific research and simulations.
- **Data Center Operations**: Optimizing workloads in cloud environments and enterprise data centers.

This card is particularly beneficial in scenarios where high memory bandwidth and low latency are critical, making it a valuable asset for organizations looking to improve their computational efficiency and speed.
