### Component Summary: APG1005CGC-T

**Description:**
The APG1005CGC-T is a surface-mount device (SMD) light-emitting diode (LED) produced by Kingbright. It features a compact size of 1.0 mm x 0.5 mm and is designed to emit green light using AlGaInP material on a GaAs substrate. This LED is characterized by low power consumption and a wide viewing angle, making it suitable for various applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 20 mA: 2.0 to 2.4 V
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 25 mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 120 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 60 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40 to +85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 to +85 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 115 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMD Chip LED Lamp (0402 package size: 1.0 x 0.5 x 0.2 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low power consumption
  - Wide viewing angle (120°)
  - Compatible with automatic placement equipment
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant

**Typical Applications:**
The APG1005CGC-T is ideal for:
- Backlighting in displays
- Status indicators in various devices
- Home and smart appliances
- Wearable and portable devices
- Healthcare applications

**Additional Notes:**
- The LED is sensitive to electrostatic discharge (ESD), with a threshold of 3000 V (HBM). Proper handling precautions, such as using wrist bands or anti-static gloves, are recommended.
- The luminous intensity at 20 mA is specified as 12 mcd (minimum) and 35 mcd (typical), with a dominant wavelength of 571 nm.
- The device is designed for reflow soldering, and care should be taken to avoid stress during high-temperature exposure.

This concise summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the APG1005CGC-T LED, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and usage scenarios.
