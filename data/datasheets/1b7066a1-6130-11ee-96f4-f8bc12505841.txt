B0 \n           Subject to changes  \n \n3 General Product Description  \nThe SCC2230- E02 sensor consists of independent acceleration and angular rate sensing \nelements, and single Application- Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) used to sense and control those \nelements. Figure 19 contains an upper level block diagram of the component. The ASIC provides \none common SPI interface used to control and read the accelerometer and the gyroscope.  \n \n \nFigure 19. SCC2230- E02 component block diagram.  \n \nThe angular rate and acceleration sensing elements are manufactured using Murata proprietary High Aspect Ratio (HAR) 3D -MEMS process, which enables making robust, extremely stable and \nlow noise capacitive sensors.  \n The acceleration sensing element consists of four acceleration sensitive masses. Acceleration \ncauses capacitance change that is converted into a voltage change in the signal conditioning ASIC.  \n \nThe angular rate sensing element consists of  moving masses that are purposely exited to in- plane \ndrive motion. Rotation in sensitive direction causes in- plane movement that can be measured as \ncapacitance change with the signal conditioning ASIC.  \n \n \n \n \n \n \nMurata Electronics Oy   SCC2230- E02  21/38 \nwww.murata.com   Doc.Nr. 82 1888 00    Rev. B0 \n           Subject to changes  \n \n3.1 Factory Calibration  \nSCC2230- E02 sensors are factory calibrated. No separate calibration is required in the application. \nParameters that are trimmed during production include sensitivities, offsets and frequency \nresponses.
