### Component Summary: PX1120S by SkyTraq Technology

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Input Voltage: 3.3V DC ±10%
  - Backup Voltage (V_BCKP): 1.3V to 3.6V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Acquisition Current: 75mA @ 3.3V
  - Tracking Current: 65mA @ 3.3V
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Typical power consumption during acquisition and tracking is 75mA and 65mA respectively.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Surface Mount Device (SMD), dimensions: 16.0mm x 12.2mm x 2.9mm
- **Special Features**: 
  - Supports multiple GNSS constellations (GPS, Beidou, Galileo, GLONASS, QZSS)
  - High sensitivity with -148dBm cold start and -165dBm tracking sensitivity
  - Fast Time to First Fix (TTFF): 29 seconds cold start, 3.5 seconds with AGPS, 1 second hot start
  - Advanced multipath detection and suppression
  - Jamming detection and mitigation
  - Integrated components: LNA, SAW Filter, TCXO, RTC Xtal
  - Compatible with both active and passive antennas
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL4 (must be used for SMT reflow mounting within 72 hours after being taken out of the moisture barrier bag)

#### Description:
The **PX1120S** is a high-performance L1 quad-constellation GNSS receiver module designed for satellite navigation applications. It utilizes a single-chip Phoenix positioning engine, enabling it to autonomously search, acquire, and track satellite signals from multiple GNSS constellations. The module is compact and integrates essential components for signal processing, making it suitable for various applications requiring precise positioning and navigation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Navigation and Asset Tracking**: The PX1120S is ideal for use in devices that require accurate location tracking, such as personal navigation devices, vehicle tracking systems, and asset management solutions.
- **Timing Reference**: The module can also serve as a timing reference in applications where precise time synchronization is critical, such as in telecommunications and data logging systems.
- **Consumer Electronics**: It can be integrated into smartphones, tablets, and other consumer electronics that require location-based services.
- **Industrial Applications**: The robustness and sensitivity of the PX1120S make it suitable for industrial applications, including robotics and automation systems that rely on accurate positioning data.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the PX1120S GNSS receiver module, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and potential uses in various fields.
