### Component Summary: PMA2-123LN+

**Description:**
The PMA2-123LN+ is a low noise, wideband monolithic amplifier based on PHEMT (Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor) technology. It is designed to provide low noise figure and high linearity, making it suitable for sensitive, high-dynamic-range receiver applications. The amplifier operates on a single supply voltage of either 5V or 6V and is well-matched for 50Ω systems. Its compact 2 x 2 mm, 8-lead MCLP package allows for efficient use of space in dense circuit board layouts.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VDD): 5V to 6V
  - Maximum DC Voltage: 7V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (IDD): 51 mA (at 5V), 68 mA (at 6V)
  - Current Variation vs. Temperature: -11.7 to +9.3 µA/°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation: 0.7W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  - Junction Temperature: 160°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: MC1631-1 (Plastic package, exposed paddle)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low noise figure: 2.2 dB at 2 GHz, 2.6 dB at 8 GHz
  - High IP3: +28 dBm at 2 GHz, +27 dBm at 8 GHz
  - Wide bandwidth: 0.5 to 12 GHz with flat gain characteristics
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Typical Applications:**
The PMA2-123LN+ is ideal for a variety of applications, including:
- WiFi and WLAN systems
- UMTS and LTE communications
- WiMAX technology
- S-band radar systems
- C-band satellite communications

This amplifier is particularly useful in low noise receiver front-end (RFE) applications, where its combination of low noise and high linearity enhances overall system performance, allowing for improved sensitivity and dynamic range.
