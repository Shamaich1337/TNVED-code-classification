### Component Summary: ESD5V0D3

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** ESD Protection Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 5.0 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6.2 V (min), 7.3 V (max) at 1 mA
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 15 A (8/20 μs pulse)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1 μA at VRWM = 5.0 V
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation (PPK): 350 W (8/20 μs pulse)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction & Storage Temperature (TJ & TSTG): -45 to +125 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD323
- **Special Features:**
  - Uni-directional ESD protection for one line
  - Low leakage current
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The ESD5V0D3 is an ESD protection diode designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD). It provides a low-leakage, uni-directional protection mechanism, making it suitable for applications where ESD events can cause damage to sensitive circuitry. The diode is housed in a compact SOD323 package, which is ideal for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protecting sensitive components in smartphones, tablets, and laptops from ESD events.
- **Communication Equipment:** Safeguarding data lines and interfaces in networking devices.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Providing ESD protection for automotive control systems and infotainment units.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Protecting sensors and control circuits in industrial automation systems.

This component is particularly useful in environments where ESD can occur frequently, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices.
