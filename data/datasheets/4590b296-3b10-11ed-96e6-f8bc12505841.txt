TLV62568 isahigh-efficiency synchronous step-down converter. The device operates with anadaptive off-\ntime with peak current control scheme. The device operates attypically 1.5-MHz frequency pulse width\nmodulation (PWM) atmoderate toheavy load currents. Based ontheVIN/VOUTratio, asimple circuit sets the\nrequired offtime forthelow-side MOSFET. Itmakes theswitching frequency relatively constant regardless ofthe\nvariation ofinput voltage, output voltage, andload current.\n7.2 Functional Block Diagram\nFigure 4.TLV62568 Functional Block Diagram\n7.3 Feature Description\n7.3.1 Power Save Mode\nThe device automatically enters Power Save Mode toimprove efficiency atlight load when theinductor current\nbecomes discontinuous. InPower Save Mode, theconverter reduces switching frequency andminimizes current\nconsumption. InPower Save Mode, theoutput voltage rises slightly above thenominal output voltage. This effect\nisminimized byincreasing theoutput capacitor.\n7.3.2 100% Duty Cycle Low Dropout Operation\nThe device offers alowinput-to-output voltage differential byentering 100% duty cycle mode. Inthismode, the\nhigh-side MOSFET switch isconstantly turned onandthelow-side MOSFET isswitched off.The minimum input\nvoltage tomaintain output regulation, depending ontheload current andoutput voltage, iscalculated as:\nVIN(MIN) =VOUT+IOUTx(RDS(ON) +RL)\nwhere\n•RDS(ON) =High side FET on-resistance\n•RL=Inductor ohmic resistance (DCR) (1)\n7.3.3 Soft Startup\nAfter enabling thedevice, internal softstartup circuitry ramps uptheoutput voltage which reaches nominal output\nvoltage during astartup time. This avoids excessive inrush current and creates asmooth output voltage rise\nslope. Italso prevents excessive voltage drops ofprimary cells and rechargeable batteries with high internal\nimpedance.\n8TLV62568 ,TLV62568P\nSLVSD89B –NOVEMBER 2016 –REVISED NOVEMBER 2017 www.ti.com\nProduct Folder Links: TLV62568Submit Documentation Feedback Copyright ©2016 –2017, Texas Instruments IncorporatedFeature Description (continued)\nThe TLV62568 isable tostart into apre-biased output capacitor. The converter starts with theapplied bias\nvoltage andramps theoutput voltage toitsnominal value.\n7.3.4 Switch Current Limit\nThe switch current limit prevents thedevice from high inductor current and drawing excessive current from a\nbattery orinput voltage rail.Excessive current might occur with aheavy load orshorted output circuit condition.\nTheTLV62568 adopts thepeak current control bysensing thecurrent ofthehigh-side switch. Once thehigh-side\nswitch current limit isreached, thehigh-side switch isturned offandlow-side switch isturned ontoramp down\ntheinductor current with anadaptive off-time.\n7.3.5 Under Voltage Lockout\nToavoid mis-operation ofthedevice atlowinput voltages, under voltage lockout isimplemented thatshuts down\nthedevice atvoltages lower than VUVLO with VHYS_UVLO hysteresis.\n7.3.6 Thermal Shutdown\nThe device enters thermal shutdown once thejunction temperature exceeds thethermal shutdown rising\nthreshold, TJSD.Once thejunction temperature falls below thefalling threshold, thedevice returns tonormal\noperation automatically.\n7.4 Device Functional Modes\n7.4.1 Enabling/Disabling theDevice\nThedevice isenabled bysetting theENinput toalogic High. ### Component Summary: TLV62568DRLR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 2.5 V to 5.5 V
  - Adjustable Output Voltage: 0.6 V to VIN

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 1 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: 35 µA (not switching)
  - Shutdown Current: 0.1 to 2 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to 125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (5 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Up to 95% Efficiency
  - Low RDS(ON) Switches: 150 mΩ (high-side), 100 mΩ (low-side)
  - Power Save Mode for light load efficiency
  - 100% Duty Cycle for lowest dropout
  - Internal Soft Startup
  - Over Current Protection
  - Thermal Shutdown Protection
  - Power Good Output (available in TLV62568P variant)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TLV62568** is a high-efficiency synchronous step-down (buck) DC-DC converter designed for compact applications. It integrates internal switches capable of delivering up to 1 A of output current. The device operates in pulse width modulation (PWM) mode at a switching frequency of 1.5 MHz under medium to heavy load conditions, while it automatically transitions to Power Save Mode (PSM) during light load conditions to maintain high efficiency. The output voltage can be adjusted using an external resistor divider, and it features built-in protections such as overcurrent and thermal shutdown.

#### Typical Applications:
The TLV62568 is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- General Purpose Point of Load (POL) Supply
- Network Video Cameras
- Set-Top Boxes
- Wireless Routers

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management in compact designs, where space and thermal performance are critical. Its ability to operate efficiently across a range of loads makes it versatile for various electronic devices.
