#### Description:
The **JNR15S150L87YRW** is an NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) thermistor, specifically designed for inrush current limiting applications. NTC thermistors exhibit a decrease in resistance with an increase in temperature, making them suitable for applications where temperature compensation is required. They are commonly used to limit inrush current in electronic circuits that include capacitors, filament bulbs, fluorescent lamp inverters, and heaters.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Inrush Current Limiting**: Used in power supply circuits to prevent high inrush currents when devices are powered on.
- **Temperature Measurement**: Can be integrated into circuits for temperature sensing and control.
- **Home Appliances**: Commonly found in devices like refrigerators, air conditioners, and washing machines.
- **Office Automation**: Used in printers, copiers, and other office equipment.
