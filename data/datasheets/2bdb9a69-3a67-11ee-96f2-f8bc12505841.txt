**Manufacturer:** HONGFA  
**Type:** Miniature High Power Relay  
**Contact Arrangement:** 1 Form A (1H)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Max Switching Voltage: 440VAC / 300VDC
  - Coil Voltage Options: 5V, 6V, 12V, 24V, 48V, 60V DC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Max Switching Current: 20A
  - Contact Ratings: 
    - 18A at 250VAC (105°C)
    - 16A at 250VAC (125°C)
    - 12A at 400VAC (105°C)
    - 20A at 277VAC (resistive load)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Approx. 400mW (coil power)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - PCB mountable, with quick connect terminals (horizontal or vertical)
- **Special Features:**
  - 5kV dielectric strength (between coil and contacts)
  - Low height: 15.7mm
  - Flux-proofed construction
  - RoHS compliant
  - Insulation resistance: 1000MΩ (at 500VDC)
  - Mechanical endurance: 10 million operations
  - Electrical endurance: 100,000 operations (20A 277VAC, resistive load)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The HF115F-Q/012-1H is a miniature high power relay designed for switching applications. It features a compact design suitable for PCB mounting and is capable of handling high current loads, making it ideal for various power management tasks. The relay is equipped with a flux-proofed construction, ensuring reliability in environments with potential contaminants.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in circuits requiring the control of high voltage and current loads, such as in industrial automation and home appliances.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for applications where switching of signals is necessary, including telecommunications and data processing equipment.
- **Automotive Applications:** Can be utilized in automotive systems for controlling various electrical components.
- **HVAC Systems:** Employed in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for controlling motors and compressors.

This relay is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, and high reliability is required under varying environmental conditions.
