### Component Summary: PDS5100-13

**Description:**
The PDS5100 is a high voltage Schottky barrier rectifier designed for power management applications. It is capable of handling high surge currents and features low forward voltage drop, making it suitable for efficient power conversion in various electronic circuits.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 71 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 100 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 5 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 120 A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF): 
    - 0.74 V at 5 A, 25°C
    - 0.64 V at 5 A, 100°C
    - 0.60 V at 5 A, 125°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - POWERDI®5 (Molded Plastic, “Green” Molding Compound)

- **Special Features:**
  - Guard ring die construction for transient protection
  - High surge current capability
  - Low leakage current
  - Lead-free finish; RoHS compliant
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for high reliability

- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

### Typical Applications:
The PDS5100 is typically used in power management applications, including:
- Power supply circuits
- DC-DC converters
- Battery chargers
- Motor control circuits
- General rectification in power electronics

This component is particularly advantageous in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, such as automotive and industrial systems, due to its robust construction and compliance with stringent standards.
