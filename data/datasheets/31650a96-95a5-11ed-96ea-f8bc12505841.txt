- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Allowable Ripple Current (rms): 150 mA at +25°C
  - Maximum Allowable Ripple Current (rms): 100 mA at +85°C
  - Maximum Allowable Ripple Current (rms): 400 mA at +125°C
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - DC Leakage: 10.9 µA at +25°C
  - ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance): 150 mΩ at 100 kHz
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: D/7343-31 (Tantalum Surface Mount Capacitor)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: MSL 1 (unlimited floor life at ≤ 30°C/85% RH)
- **Special Features**: 
  - High surge current capability
  - High ripple current capability
  - 100% surge current test on specified sizes
  - RoHS compliant (6/6) and halogen-free

#### Description:
The T495D686K025ATE150 is a low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance), surge-robust tantalum surface mount capacitor designed for demanding applications that require high surge and ripple current capabilities. It is part of KEMET's T495 series, which is known for its excellent capacitance stability and improved resistance to high in-rush currents. The capacitor is constructed with a tantalum anode and a manganese dioxide cathode, encapsulated in a molded epoxy case.

#### Typical Applications:
This capacitor is typically used in:
- **Decoupling and Filtering**: It is ideal for applications requiring stable voltage and noise reduction.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Commonly found in power management circuits where efficient energy conversion is critical.
- **Portable Electronics**: Suitable for devices that demand compact size and high reliability.
- **Telecommunications**: Used in control units and communication devices that require robust performance under varying conditions.

The T495D686K025ATE150 is particularly well-suited for applications where space is limited, and high performance is essential, making it a reliable choice for modern electronic designs.
