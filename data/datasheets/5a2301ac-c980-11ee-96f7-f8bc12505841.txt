**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 9 - 18 VDC (12 VDC nominal)
  - Output Voltage: +15 VDC / -15 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 67 mA for both +15 VDC and -15 VDC outputs
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 2 Watts
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SIP-8 (Small Outline Integrated Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Remote On/Off control
  - High efficiency (typical efficiency of 84%)
  - Excellent load and line regulation
  - Continuous short-circuit protection
  - I/O isolation of 1600 VDC
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

**Description:**
The TMR 1223 is a 2 Watt isolated DC/DC converter module designed for efficient voltage conversion in compact applications. It features a wide input voltage range and provides dual output voltages of +15 VDC and -15 VDC. The converter is housed in a compact SIP-8 package, making it suitable for space-constrained environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The TMR 1223 is ideal for various applications, including:
- **Communication Equipment:** Used in devices requiring stable power supply for signal processing.
- **Instrumentation:** Provides reliable power for measurement and control systems.
- **Industrial Electronics:** Suitable for powering sensors and control circuits in industrial automation.
- **Embedded Systems:** Can be utilized in embedded applications where dual voltage rails are needed.

This converter's high efficiency, compact size, and robust protection features make it a versatile choice for designers looking to optimize power management in their electronic systems.
