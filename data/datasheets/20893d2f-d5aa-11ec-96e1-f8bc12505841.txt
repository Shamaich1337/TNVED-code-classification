### Component Summary: HPT603P

**Description:**
The HPT603P is a silicon phototransistor designed for high sensitivity and fast response times. It is primarily used as an infrared light detector in intelligent sensing terminals and optical switches. The component is characterized by its reliability and long operational life.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)CEO): 30 V
  - Emitter-Collector Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)ECO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Collector Current (ICM): 20 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PM): 100 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Ta): -25°C to +110°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +110°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Black epoxy package with a diameter of 3 mm.

- **Special Features:**
  - High photo sensitivity and fast response time.
  - RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.

**Typical Applications:**
The HPT603P is commonly used in:
- High-speed infrared light detection systems.
- Optical switches for various electronic applications.
- Intelligent sensing terminals, which may include remote controls, security systems, and automation devices.

This phototransistor is ideal for applications requiring quick response to infrared signals, making it suitable for both consumer electronics and industrial automation systems.
