### Component Summary: BAS521.115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): Up to 300 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 300 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 250 mA (T_sp ≤ 90°C)
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FRM): 1 A (tp = 1 ms)
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Current (I_FSM): 4.5 A (tp = 1 μs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 500 mW (T_sp ≤ 90°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD523 (SC-79) - Ultra small Surface-Mounted Device (SMD) plastic package

- **Special Features:**
  - High switching speed: t_rr ≤ 50 ns
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for SMD components.

#### Description:
The BAS521 is a single high-voltage switching diode designed for high-speed and high-voltage applications. It is fabricated using planar technology and is housed in an ultra-small SMD package (SOD523). The diode is characterized by its ability to handle high reverse voltages and fast switching times, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **High-Speed Switching:** The BAS521 is ideal for applications requiring rapid switching capabilities, such as in power management circuits.
- **High-Voltage Switching:** It is suitable for circuits that operate at high voltages, making it useful in automotive and industrial applications.
- **Signal Processing:** The diode can be used in signal processing applications where fast response times are critical.

This component is particularly beneficial in automotive electronics, industrial control systems, and consumer electronics where reliability and performance are paramount.
