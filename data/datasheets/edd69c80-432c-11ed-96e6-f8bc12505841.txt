### Component Summary: ADG709BRUZ

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single Supply: 1.8 V to 5.5 V
  - Dual Supply: ±2.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Continuous Current: 30 mA
  - Peak Current (Pulsed at 1 ms, 10% Duty Cycle): 100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low power consumption (<0.01 μW)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 16-lead Thin Shrink Small Outline Package (TSSOP)
- **Special Features:**
  - 3 Ω typical on-resistance
  - 0.75 Ω on-resistance flatness
  - 100 pA leakage currents
  - 14 ns switching times
  - Break-before-make switching action
  - TTL/CMOS-compatible inputs
  - Qualified for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The ADG709 is a low-voltage CMOS analog multiplexer that features four differential channels. It allows one of four differential inputs to be switched to a common differential output based on the binary address lines A0 and A1. The device includes an enable (EN) input to control the switching operation, ensuring that all channels are off when disabled. The ADG709 is designed for low power consumption and high-speed switching, making it suitable for battery-powered applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Used to route signals from multiple sensors to a single processing unit.
- **Communication Systems:** Facilitates the switching of audio and video signals in communication devices.
- **Relay Replacement:** Acts as a solid-state switch to replace mechanical relays, providing faster switching and longer life.
- **Audio and Video Switching:** Enables the selection of different audio/video sources in consumer electronics.
- **Battery-Powered Systems:** Ideal for portable devices due to its low power consumption and wide supply voltage range.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the ADG709BRUZ, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and applications in various electronic systems.
