- **Mode-field Diameter:**

  - 6.2 ± 0.3 μm @ 1060 nm

  - -53 ps/nm/km @ 980 nm
  - -38 ps/nm/km @ 1060 nm

- **Core Diameter:** 5.3 μm
- **Cladding Outside Diameter:** 125 ± 0.5 μm
- **Coating Outside Diameter:** 245 ± 10 μm
- **Core-to-Cladding Offset:** ≤ 0.3 μm
- **Standard Lengths Available:** 500 m, 1 km, 2 km, 5 km, 10 km (10 km lengths only available for HI 1060)
- **Proof Test:** 100 or 200 kpsi
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -60 to 85 °C

- High core index of refraction and efficient coupling capabilities.

- Not specified in the provided text.

### Description:
The HI 1060 is a high-index, bend-insensitive specialty optical fiber designed for use in photonic applications. Its primary function is to facilitate efficient light transmission in various optical systems, particularly in environments where space is limited and performance is critical.

### Typical Applications:
- **Photonic Products:** Used as component pigtails for efficient fiber coupling within photonic devices.
- **Fused Fiber Couplers:** Integral in the construction of couplers for optical signals.
- **EDFA (Erbium-Doped Fiber Amplifiers):** Serves as component fiber for EDFAs, enhancing signal strength in fiber optic communications.
- **DWDM Components:** Utilized in dense wavelength division multiplexing systems for high-capacity data transmission.
- **Laser Diode Pigtails:** Acts as pigtails for pump lasers, ensuring effective light delivery.
- **Gratings:** Employed in optical gratings for various signal processing applications.

This optical fiber is particularly noted for its high performance in miniature packaging, making it an industry standard for 980 nm pump pigtails in high-performance components and small footprint assemblies.
