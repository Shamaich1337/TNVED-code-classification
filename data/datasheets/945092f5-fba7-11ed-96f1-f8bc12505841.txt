### Component Summary: SMAZ5V6-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components (MCC)  
**Component Type:** Zener Diode  
**Primary Function:** Voltage regulation by providing a stable reference voltage.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Zener Voltage (VZ):** 5.6V (Nominal)
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Minimum Zener Voltage: 5.32V
  - Maximum Zener Voltage: 5.88V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Zener Current (IZM): 250 mA
  - Zener Test Current (IZT): 100 mA
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 1.0W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Junction Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Junction to Terminal: 30°C/W
  - Junction to Ambient: 100°C/W
- **Package Type:** SMA (DO-214AC)
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - High surge capability
  - Standard VZ tolerance of ±5%
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Suitable for automatic assembly
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating

#### Description:
The SMAZ5V6-TP is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It operates by allowing current to flow in the reverse direction when the voltage exceeds its Zener breakdown voltage, thus maintaining a stable output voltage. This characteristic makes it ideal for protecting sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and ensuring stable operation in various circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant voltage level.
- **Overvoltage Protection:** Protects sensitive components from voltage transients and spikes.
- **Signal Clamping:** Used in signal processing to limit voltage levels to safe values.
- **Reference Voltage Sources:** Provides a stable reference voltage for analog circuits.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial equipment where reliable voltage regulation is critical.
