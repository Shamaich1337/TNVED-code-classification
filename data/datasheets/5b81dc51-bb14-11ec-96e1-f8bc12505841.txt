### Component Summary: BC847B (Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 50 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 45 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 0.1 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Collector Power Dissipation (PC): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23

- **Special Features:**
  - Epoxy meets UL-94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Component Description:
The BC847B is an NPN transistor designed for general-purpose switching and amplification applications. It is part of a series that includes the BC846 and BC848, with the BC847B variant specifically rated for a maximum collector-emitter voltage of 45 V and a collector current of 100 mA. The transistor is housed in a compact SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The BC847B is commonly used in:
- **Signal Amplification:** It can amplify weak signals in audio and radio frequency applications.
- **Switching Applications:** It is suitable for low-power switching tasks in various electronic circuits.
- **General Purpose Electronics:** The transistor can be utilized in various consumer electronics, including amplifiers, oscillators, and signal processing circuits.

This component is ideal for applications where reliability and compact size are essential, such as in portable devices and consumer electronics.
