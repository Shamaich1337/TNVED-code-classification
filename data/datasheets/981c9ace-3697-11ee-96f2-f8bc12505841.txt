### Component Summary: IP4220CZ6,125

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Input Voltage (V_I): 0 to 5.5 V
  - ESD Protection Voltage (V_ESD): ±8 kV (contact discharge, IEC 61000-4-2, level 4)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Reverse Leakage Current (I_RM): Up to 100 nA at V_I = 3V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Not explicitly stated; primarily functions as a protection device.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to +125 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 6-lead SOT457 (SC-74) Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)

- **Special Features:**
  - Four pairs of ultra-low input capacitance (C_I = 1 pF) ESD rail-to-rail protection diodes.
  - Integrated Zener diode for low voltage clamping.
  - Pb-free, RoHS compliant, and free of halogen and antimony.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SMD packages are classified under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The **IP4220CZ6** is an integrated ESD protection device designed to safeguard sensitive I/O lines from electrostatic discharge (ESD) damage. It features four pairs of ultra-low capacitance rail-to-rail protection diodes and an integrated Zener diode, allowing it to provide robust protection for high-frequency analog signal ports and high-speed serial data transmission interfaces, such as USB 2.0 and DVI.

#### Typical Applications:
The IP4220CZ6 is commonly used in various electronic devices that require ESD protection, including:
- Cellular phones and Personal Communication System (PCS) mobile handsets
- PC/notebook USB 2.0 and IEEE1394 ports
- Digital Video Interface (DVI) connections
- Cordless telephones
- Wireless data systems (WAN/LAN)
- Mobile Internet Devices (MID)
- Portable Media Players (PMP)
- High-Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI)

This component is particularly suited for applications where high-speed data integrity is critical, and it helps prevent damage from ESD events, ensuring reliable operation of electronic devices.
