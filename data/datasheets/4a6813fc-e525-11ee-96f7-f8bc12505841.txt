### Component Summary: NST235-DSTR

**Manufacturer:** Novosense  
**Product Code:** NST235-DSTR  
**Type:** Analog Temperature Sensor

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage Range: 2.3V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Operating Current: 20μA
  - Maximum Output Current Drain: 500μA
  - Shutdown Current: 0.1μA (VDD ≤ 0.6V)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical: 20μA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SC70(5) and SOT-23(3)
- **Special Features:**
  - High accuracy: ±0.5°C (0°C to 70°C), ±2.5°C (-40°C to 150°C)
  - Output slope: 10.0 mV/°C
  - Output short protection
  - Predictable curvature error
  - Suitable for remote applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The NST235 is a high precision CMOS analog output temperature sensor designed for accurate temperature measurement. It provides a linear output voltage that corresponds to the temperature, eliminating the need for complex calculations or lookup tables. The device is characterized by its low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-operated applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Smartphones, notebook computers
- **Medical Devices:** Portable medical instruments
- **Industrial Applications:** Industrial Internet of Things (IoT), power supply modules, power-system monitors
- **Environmental Monitoring:** HVAC systems, thermal protection, and disk drives

The NST235 is ideal for applications requiring precise temperature measurements with minimal power usage, making it a versatile choice for both consumer and industrial markets.
