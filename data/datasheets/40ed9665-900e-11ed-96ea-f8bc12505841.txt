### Component Summary: SM05T1G

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 5.0 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 1 A
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 1.0 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation: 300 W (8/20 µs pulse)
  - Total Power Dissipation on FR-5 Board: 1.8 mW/°C (derating above 25°C)
  - Total Power Dissipation on Alumina Substrate: 2.4 mW/°C (derating above 25°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature: -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (TO-236)
- **Special Features:**
  - Dual common anode design allows protection of two separate lines in one package.
  - Low leakage current and flammability rating of UL 94 V-0.
  - Pb-Free device, suitable for automotive applications (AEC-Q101 qualified).
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The SM05T1G is a dual common anode ESD protection diode array designed for transient overvoltage protection. It is a monolithic silicon device that provides protection for voltage and ESD-sensitive equipment. The dual junction design allows for the protection of two separate lines while occupying minimal board space, making it ideal for compact electronic designs.

**Typical Applications:**
The SM05T1G is commonly used in:
- Computers and peripherals (e.g., printers, terminals)
- Communication systems
- Medical equipment
- Business machines
- Any application requiring robust ESD protection and transient voltage suppression.

This component is particularly beneficial in environments where space is limited and reliability against voltage spikes is critical.
