### Component Summary: STL100N10F7

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** STL100N10F7  
**Type:** N-channel Power MOSFET  
**Package Type:** PowerFLAT™ 5x6

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 100 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ± 20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current at TC = 25 °C: 80 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at TC = 100 °C: 70 A
  - Continuous Drain Current at Tpcb = 25 °C: 19 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current: 76 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Dissipation at TC = 25 °C: 100 W
  - Total Dissipation at Tpcb = 25 °C: 5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to 175 °C

- **On-Resistance:**
  - RDS(on) at VGS = 10 V, ID = 19 A: 0.0062 Ω (typical), 0.0073 Ω (max)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The STL100N10F7 is an N-channel Power MOSFET developed using the 7th generation of STripFET™ DeepGATE™ technology. This device features an ultra-low on-resistance, making it highly efficient for power management applications. The MOSFET is designed for high-performance switching applications, providing reliable operation with minimal energy loss.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Switching Applications:** The STL100N10F7 is primarily used in various switching applications, including power management circuits, motor drivers, and DC-DC converters.
- **Power Management:** Its low on-resistance and high current handling capabilities make it suitable for power supply circuits and energy-efficient designs.
- **Automotive and Industrial Applications:** While not specifically rated for automotive use, its robust specifications allow for potential applications in industrial environments where high efficiency and reliability are required.

This component is ideal for engineers looking for a high-performance MOSFET for their power management and switching needs, providing a combination of high voltage, low on-resistance, and substantial current capacity.
