### Component Summary: BF823

**General Description:**
The BF823 is a PNP high voltage transistor manufactured by Nexperia, designed in a small SOT23 surface-mounted device (SMD) plastic package. It is suitable for applications requiring low current and high voltage, making it ideal for telephony and professional communication equipment.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): -250 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): -250 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): -5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Collector Current (IC): -50 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): -100 mA
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): -50 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 250 mW (at T amb ≤ 25 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (3 terminals; dimensions: 2.9 mm x 1.3 mm x 1 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Low collector-base cut-off current (ICBO) of -10 nA at VCB = -200 V
  - DC current gain (hFE) of 50 at VCE = -20 V and IC = -25 mA
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT23 packages are classified under MSL 1 or 2.

**Typical Applications:**
The BF823 is primarily used in:
- Telephony systems
- Professional communication equipment
- Automotive electronics, due to its AEC-Q101 qualification

This transistor is particularly useful in circuits where high voltage handling is required while maintaining low current levels, making it suitable for signal amplification and switching applications in various electronic devices.
