### Component Summary: GS1M Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** GS1M  
**Type:** Surface Mount General Purpose Rectifier  
**Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1000 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 700 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 1000 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1.0 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 30 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave)
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 60 A (non-repetitive, 1ms square wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 1.0 A: 1.1 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Thermal Characteristics:**
  - Typical Thermal Resistance (RθJ-A): 70 °C/W
  - Typical Thermal Resistance (RθJ-L): 22 °C/W
  - Typical Thermal Resistance (RθJ-C): 20 °C/W

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** MSL Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The GS1M is a surface mount general-purpose rectifier designed for various electronic applications. It features a low-profile package that is ideal for automated placement, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing. The rectifier is constructed with a glass-passivated chip junction, which enhances its reliability and performance under high surge conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The GS1M rectifier is commonly used in:
- General-purpose rectification in power supplies
- Inverters and converters
- Freewheeling diodes in consumer electronics
- Telecommunication equipment

This component is particularly useful in applications where efficient power management and reliable rectification are required, making it a versatile choice for both consumer and industrial electronics.
