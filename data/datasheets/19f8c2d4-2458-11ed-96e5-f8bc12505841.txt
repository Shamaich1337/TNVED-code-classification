APPLICATIONS INFORMATION  \nOVERVIEW  \nThe π1xxxxx are 2PaiSemi digital isolators product family based on \n2PaiSEMI unique iDivider®  technology. Intelligent voltage Divider  \ntechnology ( iDivider®  technology) is a new generation digital isolator \ntechnology invented by 2PaiSEMI. It uses the principle of capacitor \nvoltage divider to transmit signal directly cross the isolator capacitor \nwithout signal modulation and demodulation. Compare to t he \ntraditional Opto -couple technology, icoupler technology, OOK \ntechnology,  iDivider®  is a more essential and concise isolation signal \ntransmit technology which leads to greatly simplification on circuit \ndesign and therefore significantly improves device p erformance, such \nas lower power consumption, faster speed, enhanced anti -\ninterference ability, lower noise.   \nBy using maturated standard semiconductor CMOS technology and \nthe innovative  iDivider®  design, these isolation components provide \noutstanding perfo rmance characteristics and reliability superior to \nalternatives such as optocoupler devices and other integrated \nisolators. The π1xxxxx isolator data channels are independent and are \navailable in a variety of configurations with a withstand voltage rating \nof 1.5 kV rms to 5.0 kV rms and the data rate from DC up to 600Mbps \n(see the Ordering Guide).  \nThe π14xExx are the outsta nding 200Mbps quad-channel digital \nisolators with  the enhanced E SD capability . the devices transmit data \nacross an isolation barrier by layers of silicon dioxi de isolation.  \nThe devices operate with the supply voltage on either side ranging \nfrom 3.0 V to 5.5 V, offering voltage translation of 3.3 V, and 5 V logic.  \nThe π14xExx have very low propagation delay and high speed. The \ninput/output design techniques allow logic and supply voltages over \na wide range from 3.0 V to 5.5 V, offering voltage translation of 3.3 V \nand 5 V logic. The architecture is designed for high common -mod e \ntransient immunity and high immunity to electrical noise and \nmagnetic interference . \nSee the Ordering Guide for the model numbers that have the fail -safe \noutput state of low or high.  \nPCB LAYOUT  \nThe low -ESR ceramic bypass capacitors must be connected bet ween \nVDD1 and GND 1 and between V DD2 and GND 2. The bypass capacitors are \nplaced on  the PCB as close to the isolator device as possible. The \nrecommended bypass capacitor value is between 0.1 μF and 1 0 μF. \nThe user may also include resistors (50 –300 Ω) in ser ies with the \ninputs and outputs if the system is excessively noisy, or in order to \nenhance the anti ESD ability of the system.  \n \nFigure 16.Recommended Printed Circuit Board Layout  \nAvoid reducing the isolation capability, Keep the space underneath \nthe isolator device free from metal such as planes, pads, traces and \nvias.  To minimize the impedance of the signal return loop, keep the solid \nground plane directly underneath the high -speed signal path, the \ncloser the better. The retu rn path will couple between the nearest \nground plane to the signal path. Keep suitable trace width for \ncontrolled impedance transmission lines interconnect.  \nTo reduce the rise time degradation, keep the length of input/output \nsignal traces as short as poss ible, and route low inductance loop for \nthe signal path and It’s return path .  \nJITTER MEASUREMENT  \nThe eye diagram shown in the  figure below provides the jitter \nmeasurement result for the π14xExx. ### Component Summary: П141E61 (2PAI Semiconductor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage: 3.0 V to 5.5 V
  - Isolation Voltage: 5.0 kVrms (for π141E61)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Average Output Current per Pin: ±10 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 
    - IDD1 (Q) at 5V: 0.48 mA (typical)
    - IDD2 (Q) at 5V: 1.20 mA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type**: WB SOIC -16
- **Special Features**: 
  - High data rate: 200 Mbps
  - High common-mode transient immunity: 120 kV/µs typical
  - Enhanced ESD protection: ±8 kV (HBM)
  - Fail-safe output state available
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: Level 2 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The П141E61 is a quad-channel digital isolator designed by 2PAI Semiconductor. It utilizes innovative iDivider® technology, which allows for the direct transmission of voltage signals across an isolation barrier without the need for signal modulation and demodulation. This results in lower power consumption, faster data rates, and improved noise immunity compared to traditional optocoupler devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General-Purpose Multichannel Isolation**: Used in various electronic systems requiring isolation between different voltage domains.
- **Industrial Field Bus Isolation**: Ensures reliable communication in industrial automation systems.
- **Isolation for Switch Mode Power Supplies**: Provides safety and performance in power management applications.
- **Isolated ADC/DAC**: Facilitates data conversion in mixed-signal applications.
- **Motor Control**: Enhances safety and performance in motor drive systems.

This component is particularly suited for applications where high-speed data transfer and robust isolation are critical, such as in industrial automation, medical devices, and communication systems.
