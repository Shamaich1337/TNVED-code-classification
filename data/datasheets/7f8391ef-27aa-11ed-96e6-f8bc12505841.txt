### Component Summary: PE42823A-X

**Description:**
The PE42823A-X is an UltraCMOS® SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) RF switch designed for high power and high performance applications in wireless infrastructure. It operates within a frequency range of 700 MHz to 6 GHz and is particularly suited for 4G/4.5G wireless infrastructures and pre-5G/5G massive MIMO systems. The switch utilizes Peregrine's HaRP™ technology, which enhances linearity and harmonics performance, making it a reliable choice for RF signal routing.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Power supply voltage: 2.3 V to 5.5 V (absolute maximum: 5.5 V)
  - Control voltage: -0.3 V to 3.6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power supply current: 120 µA to 200 µA
  - Control current: 10 µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is low, with a maximum of 200 µA at 5.5 V.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +105 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 16-lead 3 × 3 × 0.75 mm QFN (Quad Flat No-lead)

- **Special Features:**
  - High input power handling: 51 dBm peak power for LTE
  - Exceptional linearity performance: Input IP3 of 70 dBm and Input IP2 of 105 dBm
  - High ESD protection: 4.5 kV HBM on RF pins
  - No external blocking capacitors required if 0 VDC is present on RF ports
  - Immune to latch-up

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (suitable for standard handling)

**Typical Applications:**
The PE42823A-X is primarily used in:
- 4G/4.5G wireless infrastructure systems
- Pre-5G/5G massive MIMO systems
- TDD-based RF transceivers
- Any application requiring high-performance RF switching with minimal insertion loss and high isolation.

This component is ideal for applications that demand robust performance in high-frequency environments, making it a critical component in modern wireless communication systems.
