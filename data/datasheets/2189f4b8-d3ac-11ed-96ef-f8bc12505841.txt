Application information\nThe MMBZxAL series is designed for the protec tion of up to two unidirectional data or \nsignal lines from the damage caused by ESD and surge pulses. The devices may be used \non lines where the signal polarities are either positive or negative with respect to ground. \nThe MMBZ5V6AL, MMBZ6V2AL, MMBZ6V8AL, MMBZ9V1AL and MMBZ10VAL provide a surge capability of 24 W per line, th e MMBZ12VAL, MMBZ15 VAL, MMBZ18VAL, \nMMBZ20VAL, MMBZ27VAL and MMB Z33VAL provide a surge ca pability of 40 W per line, \nfor a 10/1000 μs waveform.\n \nCircuit board layout and pr otection device placement\nCircuit board layout is critic al for the suppression of ESD, Electrical Fast Transient (EFT) \nand surge transients. The following guidelines are recommended:\n1. Place the MMBZxAL series as close to the input terminal or connector as possible.\n2. The path length between the MMBZxAL series and the protected line should be \nminimized.\n3. Keep parallel signal paths to a minimum.4. Avoid running protected conductors in parallel with unprotected conductors.5. Minimize all PCB conductive loops including power and ground loops.6. Minimize the length of the transient return path to ground.7. Avoid using shared transient return paths to a common ground point.8. Ground planes should be used whenever possible. For multilayer PCBs, use ground \nvias.\n9. ### Component Summary: MMBZ12VAL.215

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 8.5 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 12 V (typical)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 2.35 A (10/1000 μs pulse)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM): 0.15 nA (at VRWM)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Power (PPPM): 40 W (10/1000 μs pulse)
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 265 mW (at 25°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB) - small surface-mounted device (SMD)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Unidirectional ESD protection for two lines
  - ESD protection up to 30 kV (contact discharge)
  - Low capacitance: Cd ≤ 140 pF (at 1 MHz)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Ultra-low leakage current: IRM = 5 nA

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The MMBZ12VAL is a unidirectional double ElectroStatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes and transients. It is encapsulated in a compact SOT23 package, making it suitable for surface-mount applications. The device features a common anode configuration, allowing it to protect two signal lines simultaneously.

#### Typical Applications:
The MMBZ12VAL is commonly used in:
- **Computers and Peripherals:** Protecting data lines from ESD damage.
- **Automotive Electronic Control Units:** Ensuring reliability in automotive applications where ESD events are prevalent.
- **Audio and Video Equipment:** Safeguarding sensitive signal paths in consumer electronics.
- **Portable Electronics:** Providing protection for mobile devices and accessories against transient voltage spikes.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust ESD protection while maintaining low capacitance to ensure signal integrity.
