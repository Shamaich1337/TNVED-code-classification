#### Description:
The ETC4-1-2TR is a 1:4 RF flux coupled step-up transformer designed for surface mount applications. It is particularly suited for high-volume cellular and wireless applications, facilitating single to balanced mode conversion and impedance matching. The transformer is packaged in tape and reel format, making it convenient for automated assembly processes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular Communication:** Used in RF circuits to match impedance and improve signal integrity.
- **Wireless Applications:** Enhances performance in various wireless communication systems.
- **Signal Processing:** Converts single-ended signals to balanced signals, which can reduce noise and improve performance in RF applications.

This transformer is ideal for engineers looking to optimize RF performance in compact designs, particularly in environments where space and efficiency are critical.
