### Component Summary: HFBR-2524Z

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): -0.5 to 7 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Collector Current (IOAV): 25 mA
  - High Level Supply Current (ICCH): 3.5 to 6.3 mA
  - Low Level Supply Current (ICCL): 6.2 to 10 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Collector Power Dissipation (POD): 40 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TS): -40 to +85 °C
  - Operating Temperature (TA): -40 to +85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-pin dual-in-line package (DIP)
- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS-compliant
  - High noise immunity
  - Integrated pull-up resistor (680 to 1700 Ω)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 4

#### Description:
The **HFBR-2524Z** is a fiber optic receiver designed for high-performance data communication applications. It is part of the HFBR-0500Z series, which includes a range of versatile fiber optic components. The HFBR-2524Z specifically operates at a data rate of 1 MBd and is optimized for use with plastic optical fibers. It features a monolithic, DC-coupled digital IC receiver with an open collector Schottky output transistor, providing high sensitivity and noise immunity.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Communications:** Ideal for local area networks (LANs) and other data transmission systems where high-speed communication is required.
- **Motor Controller Triggering:** Used in applications where reliable signaling is necessary to control motors.
- **Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC):** Suitable for systems requiring compliance with EMC regulations.
- **Isolation in Test and Measurement Instruments:** Provides electrical isolation in sensitive measurement equipment.
- **Automotive Communications:** Utilized in automotive control networks for reliable data transfer.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Ensures error-free signaling in manufacturing and industrial automation systems.

The HFBR-2524Z is particularly beneficial in environments where electromagnetic interference (EMI) is a concern, as it helps to reduce susceptibility to voltage transients and noise, ensuring stable and reliable communication.
