### Component Summary: DF35NA100

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** DF35NA100  
**Type:** Three Phase Bridge Rectifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1000 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO) at 60Hz sine wave with heatsink (Tc = 87°C): 35 A
  - Surge (Non-repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM) at 60Hz half-sine wave, 1 cycle, Tj = 25°C: 400 A
- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated; typically determined by the load and application.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:** TSB-5
- **Special Features:**
  - High surge current capability
  - UL recognition (file #E230084)
  - Solder dip 275 °C max. for 7 seconds, per JESD 22-B106
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The DF35NA100 is a three-phase bridge rectifier designed for converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). It is capable of handling high voltage and current levels, making it suitable for various power applications. The component is housed in a TSB-5 package, which is designed for efficient thermal management and ease of integration into electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Purpose Use:** The DF35NA100 is commonly used in AC/DC bridge full-wave rectification.
- **Switching Power Supplies:** It is ideal for applications requiring efficient power conversion.
- **Home Appliances:** Utilized in various household electronic devices.
- **Office Equipment:** Suitable for printers, computers, and other office machinery.
- **Industrial Automation:** Employed in control systems and machinery requiring reliable power rectification.

This component is particularly valuable in scenarios where high surge current capability is necessary, ensuring robust performance in demanding electrical environments.
