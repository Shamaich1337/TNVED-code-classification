The WSL36371L000FEA is a **Power Metal Strip® resistor** designed for low-value resistance applications, particularly in current sensing, voltage division, and pulse applications. - **Voltage Division**: Employed in circuits where voltage scaling is necessary.
- **Pulse Applications**: Suitable for applications that require handling of high-frequency signals.
- **Automotive Applications**: AEC-Q200 qualification indicates suitability for automotive environments, ensuring reliability under harsh conditions.
