### Component Summary: BSY9-1000IOV2M

**Description:**
The BSY9-1000IOV2M is a current sensor produced by BYD Microelectronics Co., Ltd. It utilizes the Hall effect measuring principle to provide accurate electronic measurement of currents, including DC, AC, pulsed, and mixed signals. The sensor features galvanic isolation between the primary and secondary circuits, making it suitable for various industrial applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VC): ±15 V (±5% tolerance)
  - R.M.S Voltage for AC Isolation Test (Vd): 5 kV @ 60Hz, 1 min
  - R.M.S Rated Voltage, Safe Separation (Vb): 500 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Primary Nominal Current (IPN): 1000 A
  - Primary Current Measuring Range (IP): ±3000 A
  - Overload Capacity (ICC): 30,000 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Current Consumption (IC): <±20 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Operating Temperature (TA): -40°C to +105°C
  - Ambient Storage Temperature (TS): -40°C to +105°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Insulated plastic case recognized according to UL 94-V0

- **Special Features:**
  - Isolation voltage of 5000 V
  - Extended measuring range (3 * IPN)
  - High immunity to external interference
  - Small size and space-saving design
  - Easy installation

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The BSY9-1000IOV2M current sensor is commonly used in various industrial applications, including:
- AC motor speed control
- Battery-supplied applications
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Power supplies for welding, cable TV, and telecommunication applications

This sensor is ideal for applications requiring precise current measurement with high isolation and reliability, making it suitable for both industrial and commercial use.
