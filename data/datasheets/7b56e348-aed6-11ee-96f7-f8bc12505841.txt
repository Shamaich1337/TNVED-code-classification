### Component Summary: MPXV5004DP

**Manufacturer:** NXP Semiconductors (formerly Freescale Semiconductor)  
**Product Code:** MPXV5004DP  
**Type:** Integrated Pressure Sensor (Piezoresistive Transducer)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VS): 4.75 V to 5.25 V DC (typical 5.0 V DC)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IS): Up to 10 mA DC
  
- **Pressure Range:**
  - Differential Pressure Range: 0 to 3.92 kPa (0 to 400 mm H2O)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption is minimal, with a maximum supply current of 10 mA.
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TA): 0 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -30 °C to 100 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in surface mount (SOP) and through-hole (DIP) configurations.
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Temperature compensated over the range of 10 °C to 60 °C.
  - Autozero feature for improved accuracy.
  - Maximum error specifications: 
    - 1.5% for 0 to 100 mm H2O (with autozero)
    - 2.5% for 100 to 400 mm H2O (with autozero)
    - 6.25% for 0 to 400 mm H2O (without autozero)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL is not explicitly stated in the provided text; typically, this information can be found in the detailed datasheet.

#### Description:
The MPXV5004DP is a state-of-the-art monolithic silicon pressure sensor designed for a wide range of applications, particularly those that involve microcontrollers or microprocessors with analog-to-digital (A/D) inputs. It utilizes a highly sensitive implanted strain gauge combined with advanced micromachining techniques to provide an accurate analog output signal that is proportional to the applied pressure.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Washing Machines:** Used for water level detection.
- **Appliance Liquid Level Measurement:** Ideal for measuring liquid levels in various household appliances.
- **Respiratory Equipment:** Can be utilized in medical devices for pressure measurement.
- **Microprocessor or Microcontroller-Based Systems:** Suitable for integration into systems requiring precise pressure measurements.

This sensor is particularly valued for its accuracy, temperature compensation, and ease of integration into electronic systems, making it a versatile choice for various industrial and consumer applications.
