### Component Summary: XNS40N180TR

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCE): 1800 V
  - Gate-Emitter Voltage (VGES): ±30 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC) at 25°C: 80 A
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC) at 100°C: 40 A
  - Pulsed Collector Current (ICRM): 120 A
  - Continuous Diode Forward Current (IF) at 25°C: 80 A
  - Continuous Diode Forward Current (IF) at 100°C: 40 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot) at 25°C: 535 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +175°C
  - Storage Temperature Range: -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247-3
- **Special Features:**
  - Advanced Trench + Field Stop IGBT technology
  - Low Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCEsat): 2.1 V at 25°C
  - Reverse-Conducting IGBT with body diode
  - Maximum junction temperature of 175°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The XNS40N180TR is a Reverse-Conducting Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) designed for high-voltage applications. It utilizes advanced trench gate technology combined with field stop technology to achieve low saturation voltage and high efficiency. The device is capable of handling high voltages up to 1800 V and continuous currents up to 80 A, making it suitable for demanding power applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Voltage Resonance Inverters:** Used in applications requiring efficient power conversion and control.
- **AC Switches:** Suitable for switching applications in AC circuits, providing reliable performance in various industrial and consumer electronics.
- **Power Management Systems:** Ideal for use in systems that require efficient control of power flow and conversion.

This component is particularly useful in applications where high efficiency and thermal performance are critical, such as renewable energy systems, motor drives, and industrial automation.
