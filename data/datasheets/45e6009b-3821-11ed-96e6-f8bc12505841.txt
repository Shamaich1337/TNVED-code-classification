### Component Summary: LFCN-113+

**Description:**
The LFCN-113+ is a ceramic low pass filter designed for microwave communication systems. Its primary function is to reject unwanted harmonic frequencies while allowing desired signals to pass through. This component is particularly useful in applications requiring high-frequency signal processing, such as VHF/UHF transmitters and receivers.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, it is designed for RF applications.
- **Current Ratings:** Maximum RF power input of 8W at 25°C (derated to 3W at 100°C).
- **Power Consumption:** Not applicable; operates based on RF power handling.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** FV1206-4 (small size: 0.12” x 0.06” or 3.20mm x 1.60mm).
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent power handling capability (up to 8W).
  - Temperature stable and hermetically sealed.
  - LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) construction for durability in harsh environments.
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 6,943,646.

**Electrical Specifications:**
- **Insertion Loss:** 
  - DC to 10,800 MHz: Max 2.5 dB
  - Cut-off Frequency (F2): 12,250 MHz: Max 3.0 dB
- **VSWR (Voltage Standing Wave Ratio):**
  - DC to 10,800 MHz: Max 1.6:1
- **Stop Band Rejection Loss:**
  - At 14,000 MHz: Min 20 dB
  - At 14,500-20,000 MHz: Min 40 dB

**Typical Applications:**
- **Harmonic Rejection:** Effective in filtering out unwanted harmonic frequencies in RF applications.
- **VHF/UHF Transmitters/Receivers:** Used in communication systems to ensure signal integrity.
- **Laboratory Use:** Suitable for experimental setups requiring precise frequency filtering.

This low pass filter is ideal for designers looking to enhance the performance of microwave communication systems by minimizing harmonic interference while maintaining a compact footprint.
