### Component Summary: CC0402JRNPO9BN101 (YAGEO)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 16 V to 50 V
- **Capacitance Range**: 0.22 pF to 100 nF
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: 0402 (1.0 mm x 0.5 mm)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features**:
  - NP0 (C0G) dielectric, which provides stable capacitance over temperature and voltage.
  - Lead-free terminations.
  - RoHS compliant and Halogen Free compliant.
  - Supplied in tape on reel for automated assembly.

#### Description:
The CC0402JRNPO9BN101 is a surface-mount ceramic multilayer capacitor (MLCC) designed for general-purpose applications. It utilizes NP0 (C0G) dielectric material, which is known for its excellent stability and low loss characteristics. This capacitor is suitable for applications requiring precise capacitance values and minimal drift over temperature and voltage variations.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in tuners, television receivers, and various types of cameras.
- **Telecommunications**: Employed in signal processing and filtering applications.
- **Data Processing**: Utilized in circuits requiring stable capacitance for timing and frequency control.
- **General Purpose**: Ideal for decoupling, bypassing, and filtering applications in various electronic devices.

This capacitor is particularly advantageous in high-frequency applications due to its low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and low dissipation factor, making it suitable for RF and high-speed digital circuits.
