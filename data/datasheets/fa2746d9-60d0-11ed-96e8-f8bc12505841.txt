### Component Summary: LEE-29+ (Mini-Circuits)

**Description:**
The LEE-29+ is a monolithic microwave integrated circuit (MMIC) amplifier designed for wideband applications. It operates over a frequency range from DC to 8 GHz and is optimized for high dynamic range and repeatable performance. The component is housed in a compact 3x3mm MCLP molded plastic package with an exposed metal bottom for effective heat dissipation. It is RoHS compliant and features a patented design.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.2 V to 4.0 V (typical 3.6 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Recommended Operating Current: 40 mA
  - Absolute Maximum Operating Current: 55 mA
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power consumption is primarily determined by the operating current and voltage, leading to a typical power consumption of approximately 144 mW (3.6 V * 40 mA).
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - 3x3mm MCLP (Molded Plastic) package with exposed paddle (Case Style: FG873)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Internally matched to 50 Ohms
  - Aqueous washable
  - Flat output power up to 10 GHz
  - Protected by US Patent 6,943,629
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020C

**Typical Applications:**
The LEE-29+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Cellular communication systems
- Personal Communication Services (PCS)
- Communication receivers and transmitters
- Satellite communication, including military applications

This amplifier is ideal for use in systems requiring high-frequency signal amplification with minimal distortion and high reliability, making it a versatile choice for modern communication technologies.
