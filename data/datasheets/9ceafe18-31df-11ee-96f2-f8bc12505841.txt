- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages including SOT-23 (DBV), SC70 (DCK), X2SON (DPW), and others.
- **Special Features:**
  - High output drive capability
  - Low static power dissipation
  - Ioff feature for partial power-down applications
  - ESD protection exceeding 2000 V (Human Body Model)
  - Supports live insertion and back drive protection
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **SN74LVC1G00** is a single 2-input positive-NAND gate designed for operation in a wide voltage range from 1.65 V to 5.5 V. It performs the Boolean function Y = A × B or Y = A + B in positive logic. This CMOS device is characterized by high output drive capabilities while maintaining low static power consumption, making it suitable for various applications.

- **Telecommunications:**
  - Power management in telecom and server applications.
- **Display Technology:**
  - LCD and digital TVs, including high-definition (HDTV) displays.
