### Component Summary: SN74LVC1G07DBVR

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.65 V to 5.5 V (operating), up to 6.5 V (absolute maximum)
  - Input Voltage: Up to 5.5 V
  - Output Voltage: Up to 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Drive Current: ±24 mA at 3.3 V
  - Maximum Sink Current: 32 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 10 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C (for most packages)
  - -40°C to 85°C (for DSBGA package)

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages including:
    - SOT-23 (DBV)
    - SC70 (DCK)
    - X2SON (DPW)
    - SON (DRY, DSF)
    - DSBGA (YZP, YZV)

- **Special Features:**
  - Open-drain output allows for wired-OR and wired-AND configurations.
  - I_off feature supports live insertion and partial-power-down mode.
  - Latch-up performance exceeds 100 mA per JESD 78, Class II.
  - ESD protection exceeds JESD 22 standards (2000 V HBM, 200 V MM, 1000 V CDM).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The SN74LVC1G07 is a single buffer/driver with an open-drain output designed for operation within a voltage range of 1.65 V to 5.5 V. It is capable of translating voltages both up and down, making it versatile for various applications. The open-drain output can be connected to other open-drain outputs to implement active-low wired-OR or active-high wired-AND functions. This device is particularly noted for its low power consumption and high drive capability.

#### Typical Applications:
The SN74LVC1G07 is suitable for a wide range of applications, including:
- **Consumer Electronics:** AV receivers, Blu-ray players, home theaters, digital video cameras, and portable media players.
- **Computing Devices:** Desktop and notebook PCs, solid-state drives (SSDs), and embedded PCs.
- **Communication Devices:** GPS personal navigation devices, digital radios, and network projectors.
- **Industrial Applications:** Smoke detectors and pro audio mixers.
- **High-Definition Displays:** Used in DLP front projection systems and digital picture frames.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high-speed data transmission, signal buffering, and interfacing between different voltage levels in digital circuits.
