### Component Summary: TL331MDBVTEP

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2V to 36V
  - Maximum Rated Supply Voltage: ±36V
  - Input Voltage Range: –0.3V to 36V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 0.4 mA (typical)
  - Output Current: 20 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Supply Current Drain: 0.4 mA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Military Grade: –55°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (DBV package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low Input Bias Current: 25 nA (typical)
  - Low Input Offset Voltage: 2 mV (typical)
  - Common-Mode Input Voltage Range Includes Ground
  - Output Compatible with TTL, MOS, and CMOS
  - Extended Product Lifecycle and Change Notification
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The TL331MDBVTEP is a single differential voltage comparator designed to operate from a single power supply over a wide range of voltages. It can also function with dual supplies, provided the difference between the two supplies is between 2V and 36V. The device is characterized by low power consumption and is suitable for applications requiring high reliability, such as defense, aerospace, and medical applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Signal Processing:** Used in applications where signal comparison is necessary, such as in analog-to-digital converters.
- **Power Management:** Can be utilized in battery management systems to monitor voltage levels.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Employed in systems that require precise voltage level detection.
- **Threshold Detection:** Useful in circuits that need to detect when a signal crosses a certain threshold.
- **Open-Collector Outputs:** Can be connected to other open-collector outputs to achieve wired-AND logic configurations.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where reliability and performance are critical, making it suitable for military and medical applications.
