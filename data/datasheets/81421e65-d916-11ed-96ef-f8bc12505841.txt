### Component Summary: HMC564LC4TR

**Description:**
The HMC564LC4TR is a high dynamic range Low Noise Amplifier (LNA) designed using GaAs pHEMT technology. It operates within a frequency range of 7 to 14 GHz and is housed in a compact, leadless RoHS compliant 4x4 mm surface mount package. This amplifier is particularly noted for its low noise figure and high gain, making it suitable for various microwave applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain Bias Voltage (Vdd1, Vdd2): +3.5 Vdc (maximum)
  - Single Supply Voltage: +3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd) at +3V: 51 mA (typical), 75 mA (maximum)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1.16 W (at T = 85 °C, derated)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 24-Terminal Ceramic Leadless Chip Carrier (LCC), dimensions 4x4 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Extremely flat small signal gain of 17 dB
  - Low noise figure of 1.8 dB
  - Output IP3 of +25 dBm
  - 50 Ohm matched input/output
  - Self-biased operation
  - DC blocked RF I/Os
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC564LC4 is ideal for use in:
- Point-to-Point Radios
- Point-to-Multi-Point Radios & VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal)
- Test Equipment and Sensors
- Military and Space applications

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where low noise amplification is critical, such as in communication systems and radar applications, where maintaining signal integrity is essential. Its compact size and high performance make it suitable for integration into various RF systems.
