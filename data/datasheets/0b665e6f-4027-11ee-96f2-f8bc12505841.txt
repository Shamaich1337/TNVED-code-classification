### Component Summary: PESD12VL2BT

**Description:**
The PESD12VL2BT is a low capacitance double bidirectional ElectroStatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode designed to safeguard two signal lines from ESD and other transient voltage spikes. It is housed in a compact SOT23 surface-mounted package, making it suitable for various electronic applications where space is a constraint.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 12 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 14.2 V (min) to 16.7 V (max) at 5 mA
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Current (IPPM): 5 A (8/20 µs pulse)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM): 1 nA (typical) at 12 V
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Pulse Power (PPPM): 200 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (3 terminals, dimensions: 2.9 mm x 1.3 mm x 1 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD protection up to 30 kV (IEC 61000-4-2, level 4)
  - Ultra-low leakage current (1 nA)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The PESD12VL2BT is commonly used in:
- Computers and peripherals
- Audio and video equipment
- Cellular handsets and accessories
- Communication systems
- Portable electronics
- SIM card protection

This component is particularly effective in environments where signal integrity is critical, and it provides robust protection against ESD events, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices.
