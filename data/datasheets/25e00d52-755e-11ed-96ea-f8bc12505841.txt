### Component Summary: SN74LVC2G125DCUR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.65V to 5.5V
  - Input Voltage: 0V to 5.5V
  - Maximum Input Voltage: 6.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Drive Current: ±24 mA at 3.3V
  - Continuous Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Continuous Current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 10 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - VSSOP (8 pins), dimensions: 2.30 mm x 2.00 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Protection: Exceeds 2000V (Human-Body Model), 1000V (Charged-Device Model)
  - Partial-Power-Down Mode with Ioff support
  - Over-voltage tolerant inputs (up to 5.5V)
  - High-speed operation with a maximum propagation delay (tpd) of 4.3 ns at 3.3V
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **SN74LVC2G125** is a dual bus buffer gate with 3-state outputs, designed for operation in the voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V. It features two independent buffers that can be enabled or disabled via output enable (OE) inputs. The device is particularly noted for its low power consumption and high output drive capability, making it suitable for high-speed applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Communication:** Used in cable modem termination systems and power line communication modems.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for high-speed data acquisition and generation, as well as military applications such as radars and sonars.
- **Motor Control:** Can be utilized in high-voltage motor control applications.
- **Video Broadcasting:** Suitable for video communications systems and scalable platforms for video broadcasting.
- **Storage Devices:** Applicable in solid-state drives (SSDs), both internal and external.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring reliable signal buffering and level translation, especially where input voltages may exceed the supply voltage. Its ability to handle high-speed signals and provide robust ESD protection makes it a versatile choice for various electronic designs.
