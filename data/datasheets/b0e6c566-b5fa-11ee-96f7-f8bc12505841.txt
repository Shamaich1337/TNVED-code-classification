### Component Summary: DDZ9693S-7

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** DDZ9693S-7  
**Document Number:** DS30409 Rev. 6 - 2  
**Date:** August 2009  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 7.5V (Nominal)
  - Minimum Zener Voltage: 7.13V
  - Maximum Zener Voltage: 7.88V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.1 μA at VR = 5.7V
  - Zener Test Current (IZT): 50 μA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-323 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Very sharp breakdown characteristics
  - Tight tolerance on VZ
  - Low leakage current
  - Lead-free and RoHS compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (per J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **DDZ9693S** is a precision Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It provides a stable reference voltage and is characterized by its sharp breakdown characteristics and low leakage current. The device is suitable for automated assembly processes due to its surface mount design.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power management circuits to maintain a constant voltage level.
- **Signal Clamping:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Reference Voltage Generation:** Provides a stable reference voltage for various electronic circuits.
- **Noise Filtering:** Helps in reducing noise in power supply circuits.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in applications where precise voltage regulation is critical, such as in power supplies, voltage reference circuits, and signal processing systems.
