### Component Summary: SV222M6R3G105ETRLZ (CapXon)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Capacitance**: 2200 µF
- **Voltage Rating**: 6.3 VDC
- **Surge Voltage**: 7 VDC
- **Capacitance Tolerance**: ±20% (@120Hz, +20°C)
- **Maximum Ripple Current**: 1190 mA (@100KHz, +105°C)
- **Equivalent Series Resistance (ESR)**: 0.09 Ω (@100KHz, +20°C)
- **Dissipation Factor (tan δ)**: 26% (@120Hz, +20°C)
- **Leakage Current**: 139 µA (max. @ +20°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +105°C
- **Endurance**: 3000 hours at 105°C
- **Package Type**: SMD (Surface Mount Device), dimensions 10mm x 10.5mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E guidelines.

#### Description:
The SV222M6R3G105ETRLZ is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's SV series. It is designed for surface mount applications and is characterized by its high capacitance value of 2200 µF and a voltage rating of 6.3 VDC. This capacitor is particularly suitable for applications requiring stable performance under varying temperature conditions, with an operating range from -55°C to +105°C.

#### Typical Applications:
This aluminum electrolytic capacitor is commonly used in:
- **Power Management**: Providing bulk capacitance for power supply circuits, smoothing out voltage fluctuations.
- **Signal Processing**: Filtering applications in audio and RF circuits.
- **Energy Storage**: In applications where quick bursts of energy are needed, such as in camera flashes or power backup systems.
- **Decoupling**: Reducing noise in power supply lines for sensitive electronic components.

The SV series capacitors are designed to meet rigorous performance standards, making them suitable for a wide range of electronic devices, including consumer electronics, automotive applications, and industrial equipment.
