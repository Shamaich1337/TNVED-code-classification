**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 18 - 36 VDC (Nominal: 24 VDC)
  - Output Voltage: 5 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 400 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output: 2 Watts
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +95°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SIP-8 (Single In-line Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - Fully regulated outputs
  - Continuous short circuit protection
  - Remote On/Off control
  - Designed to meet UL 62368-1
  - Isolation voltage: 1600 VDC
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The TEC 2-2411 is a 2 Watt DC/DC converter from the TEC 2 Series by Traco Power. It is designed to convert a wide range of input voltages (18 - 36 VDC) into a stable output voltage of 5 VDC, making it suitable for various applications requiring reliable power conversion. The converter features a compact SIP-8 package, which allows for easy integration into electronic circuits.

**Typical Applications:**
The TEC 2-2411 is typically used in applications such as:
- Power management systems
- Telecommunications equipment
- Industrial automation
- Embedded systems
- Medical devices
- Any application requiring a compact and efficient DC/DC power supply solution.

This converter is particularly advantageous in environments where space is limited and where high efficiency and reliability are critical. Its wide operating temperature range and robust design make it suitable for harsh conditions.
