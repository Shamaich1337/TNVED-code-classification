### Component Summary: LFCN-1800+

**Description:**
The LFCN-1800+ is a ceramic low-pass filter designed for RF applications. It operates within a frequency range of DC to 1800 MHz and is intended to attenuate high-frequency signals while allowing lower frequencies to pass through. This component is particularly useful in applications requiring harmonic rejection and signal integrity.

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, it is designed for RF applications with a maximum power input.
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified; power handling is indicated instead.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** FV1206 (small size).
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent power handling capability (10 W).
  - Temperature stable performance.
  - LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) construction.
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 6,943,646.
  - RoHS compliant.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Harmonic Rejection:** Used in systems where it is critical to suppress unwanted harmonics.
- **VHF/UHF Transmitters/Receivers:** Ideal for communication systems operating in the VHF and UHF bands.
- **Laboratory Use:** Suitable for experimental setups requiring precise filtering of RF signals.

This low-pass filter is designed to provide reliable performance in various RF applications, ensuring signal clarity and integrity by effectively blocking higher frequency noise.
