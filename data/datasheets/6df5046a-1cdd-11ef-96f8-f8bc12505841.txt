### Component Summary: APT1608SGC

**Description:**
The APT1608SGC is a Super Bright Green SMD (Surface Mount Device) LED (Light Emitting Diode) made with Gallium Phosphide (GaP). It is designed for low power consumption and features a compact size, making it suitable for various applications, including backlighting and status indicators.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at 20mA: 2.2V (typical) to 2.5V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 25mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 140mA
  - Reverse Current (IR) at 5V: 6µA (maximum)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 62.5 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +85°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 110°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Dimensions: 1.6 mm x 0.8 mm x 0.75 mm (SMD)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Wide viewing angle: 150°
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Ideal for backlight and indicator applications

**Typical Applications:**
The APT1608SGC is commonly used in:
- Backlighting for displays and panels
- Status indicators in home appliances and smart devices
- Wearable and portable electronic devices
- Healthcare applications, where visual indicators are essential

This LED is particularly valued for its compact size, low power consumption, and bright output, making it a versatile choice for various electronic applications.
