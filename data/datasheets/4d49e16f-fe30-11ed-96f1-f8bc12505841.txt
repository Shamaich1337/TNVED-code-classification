### Component Summary: ESD3V3D5

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** ESD Protection Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 3.3 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 5 V (at IT = 1 mA)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 16 A (non-repetitive, 8/20 μs waveform)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): 0.08 µA (at VRWM = 3.3 V)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Power Dissipation (PPK): 224 W (8/20 μs pulse)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction & Storage Temperature (TJ & TSTG): -45 to +125 °C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD523 (small outline diode package)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Uni-directional ESD protection for one line
  - Low leakage current
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The ESD3V3D5 is an ESD protection diode designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD). It provides low leakage current and is capable of handling high voltage spikes, making it suitable for protecting data lines and power lines in various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **ESD Protection:** Commonly used in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and computer peripherals to protect sensitive components from ESD events.
- **Signal Integrity:** Helps maintain signal integrity in high-speed data lines by clamping voltage spikes.
- **Power Management:** Can be used in power management circuits to protect against voltage transients.

This component is ideal for applications where ESD protection is critical, such as in mobile devices, laptops, and other portable electronics.
