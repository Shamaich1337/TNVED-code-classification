### KEMET T491X476K035AT - Technical Summary

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 35 VDC
- **Current Ratings**: Maximum allowable ripple current at 100 kHz is 16.5 mA at +125°C.
- **Power Consumption**: Maximum power dissipation is 165 mW at +25°C.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55°C to +125°C.
- **Package Type**: 7343-43 (EIA case size).
- **Special Features**:
  - 100% surge current tested.
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free terminations.
  - Halogen-free epoxy.
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 1 (unlimited floor life at ≤ 30°C/85% RH).

#### Description:
The KEMET T491X476K035AT is a solid tantalum surface mount capacitor designed for high-performance applications. It utilizes KEMET's proven solid tantalum technology, which is recognized for its reliability and efficiency in electronic circuits. This capacitor is particularly suitable for surface mount designs, making it ideal for automated assembly processes.

#### Typical Applications:
The T491 series capacitors are commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Decoupling and Filtering**: Providing stable voltage levels and reducing noise in power supply circuits.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Essential for energy conversion in portable electronics and telecommunications.
- **Control Units**: Used in automotive and industrial control systems for reliable performance under varying conditions.
- **Portable Electronics**: Ideal for smartphones, tablets, and other battery-operated devices where space and efficiency are critical.

This capacitor is designed to meet or exceed the requirements of EIA standard 535BAAC, ensuring high quality and performance in demanding applications.
