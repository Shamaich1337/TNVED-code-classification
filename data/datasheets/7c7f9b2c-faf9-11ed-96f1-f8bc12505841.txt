### Component Summary: USB260-M3/52T

**Manufacturer:** Vishay General Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** USB260-M3/52T  
**Component Type:** Ultrafast Plastic Rectifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 600 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 420 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 600 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current (IF(AV)): 2.0 A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 90 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF at IF): 1.0 V (at 2.0 A)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMB (DO-214AA)

- **Special Features:**
  - Glass passivated pellet chip junction
  - Ultrafast recovery times for high efficiency
  - Low forward voltage and low power losses
  - High forward surge capability
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (automotive applications)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The USB260-M3/52T is an ultrafast plastic rectifier designed for high-frequency rectification and freewheeling applications. It features a glass passivated junction that enhances reliability and performance in various electronic circuits. The component is suitable for automated placement, making it ideal for mass production environments.

#### Typical Applications:
- High-frequency rectification in power supplies
- Freewheeling applications in switching mode converters
- Inverters for consumer electronics, computers, and telecommunication devices
- Automotive applications (AEC-Q101 qualified versions)

This component is particularly useful in scenarios where efficiency and rapid switching are critical, such as in power management systems and signal processing circuits.
