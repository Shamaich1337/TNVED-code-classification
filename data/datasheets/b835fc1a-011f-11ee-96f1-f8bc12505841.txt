### Component Summary: UM3243EEUS

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): +3.0V to +5.5V
  - Input Voltage Range (RIN_): ±30V
  - V+ (Charge Pump Output): (VCC - 0.3V) to +7.5V
  - V- (Charge Pump Output): -7.5V to +0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Normal Operation): 0.6 mA (typical)
  - Supply Current (Auto Power-Off): 0.1 μA (typical)
  - Output Short-Circuit Current (VCC=3.3V): ±20 to ±40 mA
  - Output Short-Circuit Current (VCC=5.0V): ±30 to ±50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Power-Off Current: 0.1 μA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP28

- **Special Features:**
  - Auto Power-Off feature to save power when no valid RS-232 signal is detected.
  - Enhanced ESD protection: ±15kV (Human Body Model), ±8kV (IEC61000-4-2 Contact Discharge).
  - Always-active receiver for monitoring external devices in Power-Off mode.
  - Supports data rates up to 250 kbps.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The UM3243EEUS is a low supply current, 3-driver/5-receiver RS-232 transceiver designed for operation with supply voltages ranging from 3V to 5.5V. It features an Auto Power-Off mode that disables the charge pump and drivers when no valid RS-232 signal is detected, significantly reducing power consumption. The device is capable of transmitting data at rates up to 250 kbps and provides true RS-232 performance from a single supply voltage.

**Typical Applications:**
The UM3243EEUS is commonly used in various applications, including:
- Industrial controllers and instruments
- Notebooks, palmtop PCs, and laptops
- Networking routers and switches
- Peripheral devices
- Printers, PDAs, and point-of-sale (POS) systems

This component is ideal for systems requiring reliable RS-232 communication while maintaining low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-operated devices and energy-efficient designs.
