### Component Summary: LM336Z-5.0/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage: 5.0V (typical)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Reverse Current: 15 mA (maximum)
  - Forward Current: 10 mA (maximum)
  - Operating Current: 600 µA to 10 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Not explicitly stated, but operates as a shunt regulator with low dynamic impedance.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - 0°C to +70°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - TO-92
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low temperature coefficient
  - Easily trimmed for minimum temperature drift
  - 0.6Ω dynamic impedance
  - ±1% initial tolerance available
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The LM336Z-5.0/NOPB is a precision 5.0V shunt voltage reference diode. It operates as a low-temperature coefficient Zener diode with a dynamic impedance of 0.6Ω, making it suitable for applications requiring stable voltage references. The device features a third terminal that allows for easy trimming of the reference voltage and temperature coefficient, enhancing its usability in precision applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used as a voltage reference in power supplies to ensure stable output voltage.
- **Signal Processing**: Acts as a reference voltage for operational amplifiers and analog circuits.
- **Digital Voltmeters**: Provides a stable reference voltage for accurate measurements.
- **Calibration Equipment**: Utilized in devices that require precise voltage references for calibration purposes.
- **Adjustable Voltage References**: Can be configured to provide adjustable voltage outputs in various electronic circuits.

This component is particularly useful in applications where a stable and precise voltage reference is critical, such as in instrumentation and control systems.
