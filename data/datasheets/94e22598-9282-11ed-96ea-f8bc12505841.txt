### Component Summary: IRFS52N15DTRLP

**Description:**
The IRFS52N15DTRLP is a N-channel Power MOSFET designed for high-frequency applications. It is primarily used in power management systems, including DC-DC converters and plasma display panels. This component is characterized by low on-resistance and low gate-to-drain charge, which helps reduce switching losses, making it suitable for efficient power conversion.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): 150 V
  - Avalanche Voltage (VDS): 200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 51 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: 36 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 240 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 230 W (case)
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 3.8 W (ambient)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +175°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in D2Pak and TO-220AB packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Low gate-to-drain charge to minimize switching losses.
  - Fully characterized avalanche voltage and current.
