The component is characterized by its single-bead design and is available in a radial lead type configuration.

The B-01-RTS is commonly used in:
- **Audio-visual equipment**: To minimize noise and improve sound quality.
- **Office automation equipment**: Such as printers and copiers, where signal integrity is crucial.
- **Digital appliances**: Including computers and peripherals.
- **Home appliances**: To ensure reliable operation without interference.
- **Power supplies**: For filtering noise in DC signal lines.

This component is particularly useful in environments where signal integrity is paramount, and it helps to ensure compliance with electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) standards.
