#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 1200 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 1200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (I_C): 25 A (at 100°C)
  - Pulsed Collector Current (I_Cpuls): 100 A

  - Maximum Power Dissipation (P_tot): 326 W (at 25°C), 156 W (at 100°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_vj): -40°C to 175°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:** PG-TO247-3

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low V_CE,sat (Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage): 2.05 V (at 25°C, I_C = 25 A)
  - Low EMI (Electromagnetic Interference)
  - Soft, fast recovery antiparallel diode
  - Pb-free lead plating; RoHS compliant
  - Qualified according to JEDEC standards

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IKW25N120H3 is a high-speed Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) utilizing Trench and Fieldstop technology. It is designed for efficient switching applications, featuring a soft and fast recovery antiparallel diode that enhances its performance in high-frequency environments. The device is characterized by low saturation voltage and minimal electromagnetic interference, making it suitable for demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** The IGBT is used to manage power flow and ensure reliable operation during power outages.
- **Welding Converters:** It is employed in welding equipment for its ability to handle high currents and voltages.
- **Converters with High Switching Frequency:** The IKW25N120H3 is ideal for applications requiring rapid switching, such as in renewable energy systems and motor drives.

This component is particularly suited for applications where efficiency, reliability, and performance are critical, making it a valuable choice in power electronics.
