  - High-speed switching with low capacitances
  - High blocking voltage with low RDS(on) (16-22 mΩ)
  - Optimized package with separate driver source pin
  - Easy to parallel and simple to drive
  - ROHS compliant, halogen-free

  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The AMG100N1200MT4 is a 1200V N-Channel MOSFET utilizing Silicon Carbide (SiC) technology. This component is designed to provide superior switching performance and higher reliability compared to traditional silicon MOSFETs. Its low ON resistance and compact chip size contribute to low capacitance and gate charge, resulting in enhanced efficiency, faster operating frequencies, increased power density, reduced electromagnetic interference (EMI), and minimized system size.

#### Typical Applications:
The AMG100N1200MT4 is suitable for a variety of high-performance applications, including:
- Electric Vehicle (EV) motor drives
- High Voltage DC/DC converters
- Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)
- Solar inverters
- EV charging systems

This component is particularly advantageous in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability under demanding conditions, making it ideal for modern power management solutions.
