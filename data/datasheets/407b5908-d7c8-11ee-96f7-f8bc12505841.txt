### Component Summary: HMC509LP5E

**Description:**
The HMC509LP5E is a GaAs InGaP Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor (HBT) MMIC Voltage Controlled Oscillator (VCO) that operates with a half-frequency output. It is designed to provide low phase noise and high power output in a compact surface mount package. The device integrates resonators, negative resistance devices, and varactor diodes, eliminating the need for external matching components.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V (max +5.5V)
  - Tuning Voltage (Vtune): 0 to +15V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 200 mA (typ), 250 mA (max), 270 mA (at Vcc = +5.25V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Output (RFOUT): +13 dBm (typical)
  - Power Dissipation: 1.34 W (at T = 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 32 Lead QFN (5x5 mm) surface mount package

- **Special Features:**
  - Dual output: Fo = 7.8 - 8.8 GHz and Fo/2 = 3.9 - 4.4 GHz
  - Excellent phase noise performance: -115 dBc/Hz @ 100 kHz offset
  - No external resonator needed
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL3 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The HMC509LP5E is suitable for various applications, including:
- VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) Radio
- Point-to-Point and Multi-Point Radio Communications
- Test Equipment and Industrial Controls
- Military End-Use Applications

This component is ideal for systems requiring low noise and high-frequency oscillation, making it a valuable asset in RF communication and signal processing applications.
