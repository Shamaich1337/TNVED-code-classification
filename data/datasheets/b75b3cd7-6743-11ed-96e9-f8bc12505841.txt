### Component Summary: CPC1016NTR

**Manufacturer:** IXYS Integrated Circuits Division  
**Product Code:** CPC1016NTR  
**Type:** Solid State Relay (OptoMOS® Relay)  
**Configuration:** Single-Pole, Normally Open (1-Form-A)  
**Package Type:** 4-Lead SOP (Surface Mount)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Blocking Voltage:** 100 V (VP)
- **Load Current:**
  - Continuous: 100 mA (rms/mADC)
  - Peak (10ms): ±350 mA
- **On-Resistance (max):** 16 Ω at 100 mA
- **Input/Output Isolation Voltage:** 1500 Vrms
- **Input Control Current to Activate:** 0.5 to 2 mA
- **Input Control Current to Deactivate:** 0.3 to 0.4 mA
- **Input Voltage Drop:** 0.9 to 1.4 V at 5 mA
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 to +85 °C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40 to +125 °C
- **Total Power Dissipation:** 1400 mW
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** MSL 3 (according to IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The CPC1016N is a miniature, low-voltage, low on-resistance solid state relay designed for applications requiring reliable switching without the mechanical wear associated with traditional electromechanical relays. Utilizing optically coupled MOSFET technology, it provides high input/output isolation (1500Vrms) and is controlled by a highly efficient GaAIAs infrared LED. The device is packaged in a compact 4-lead SOP format, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Instrumentation:** Used in measurement devices for accurate readings.
- **Multiplexers:** Facilitates the switching of multiple signals.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Enables reliable data collection from various sensors.
- **Electronic Switching:** Acts as a switch in various electronic circuits.
- **I/O Subsystems:** Provides isolation and control in input/output systems.
- **Meters:** Suitable for watt-hour, water, and gas meters.
- **Medical Equipment:** Ensures patient/equipment isolation for safety.
- **Security Systems:** Used in alarm and monitoring systems.
- **Aerospace and Industrial Controls:** Provides reliable switching in harsh environments.
- **Reed Relay Replacement:** Offers a solid-state alternative to traditional reed relays.

### Special Features:
- **100% Solid State:** No moving parts, leading to higher reliability.
- **Low Drive Power Requirements:** Compatible with TTL/CMOS logic levels.
- **Arc-Free Operation:** Eliminates the need for snubbing circuits.
- **No EMI/RFI Generation:** Ensures minimal interference with other electronic devices.
- **Machine Insertable and Wave Solderable:** Facilitates automated assembly processes.
- **Tape & Reel Version Available:** CPC1016NTR for high-volume production.
