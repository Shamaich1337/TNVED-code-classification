### Component Summary: 1.5KE13A-E3/54 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 12.4 V to 13.7 V
  - Stand-off Voltage (VWM): 11.1 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 82.4 A (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Maximum Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 200 A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPPM): 1500 W
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 6.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +175 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: 1.5KE (molded epoxy body)
- **Special Features:**
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - Excellent clamping capability and very fast response time
  - AEC-Q101 qualified versions available
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Class 1A (for E3 suffix)

#### Description:
The **1.5KE13A-E3/54** is a **Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode** designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients. It is capable of clamping high voltage spikes, thereby preventing damage to downstream components. The device is available in both unidirectional and bidirectional configurations, making it versatile for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Electronics Protection:** Used in sensitive electronics to guard against voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes.
- **Automotive Applications:** Protects ICs, MOSFETs, and signal lines in automotive electronics.
- **Industrial and Telecommunications:** Provides protection for signal lines and components in industrial and telecommunication equipment.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Safeguards devices from voltage spikes that could lead to malfunction or damage.

This component is particularly useful in environments where voltage transients are common, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic systems.
