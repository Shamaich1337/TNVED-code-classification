### Component Summary: BC846,215 (NXP Semiconductors)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 65 V (max)
  - Collector-Base Voltage (V_CBO): 80 V (max)
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (V_EBO): 6 V (max)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (I_C): 100 mA (max)
  - Peak Collector Current (I_CM): 200 mA (single pulse, tp ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot):
    - SOT23: 250 mW
    - SOT323: 200 mW
    - SOT416: 150 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple SMD packages:
    - SOT23 (TO-236AB)
    - SOT323 (SC-70)
    - SOT416 (SC-75)

- **Special Features:**
  - General-purpose NPN transistor
  - Two different gain selections (h_FE groups A and B)
  - Suitable for surface mounting

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1 (no dry pack required)

#### Description:
The BC846 is a general-purpose NPN transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is available in surface-mounted device (SMD) plastic packages, making it suitable for compact electronic designs. The transistor features two different gain selections, allowing for flexibility in various circuit designs.

#### Typical Applications:
The BC846 is commonly used in:
- General-purpose switching applications, such as driving loads or controlling signals.
- Amplification circuits, where it can amplify weak signals in audio or radio frequency applications.
- Signal processing tasks in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.

This transistor is ideal for use in low to moderate power applications, where its compact size and versatility can be effectively utilized.
