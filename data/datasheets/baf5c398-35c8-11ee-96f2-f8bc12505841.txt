#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - High side floating absolute voltage (VB): -0.3V to 625V
  - High side floating supply offset voltage (VS): -25V to VB + 0.3V
  - Low side and logic fixed supply voltage (VCC): -0.3V to 25V
  - Low side output voltage (VLO): -0.3V to VCC + 0.3V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output source current capability: 1.4A
  - Output sink current capability: 1.8A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent supply current (IQCC): 0.4mA to 1.6mA (depending on logic state)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction temperature (TJ): -40°C to 150°C
  - Storage temperature (TS): -50°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Lead PDIP, 8-Lead SOIC, 14-Lead PDIP, and 14-Lead SOIC packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Floating channel designed for bootstrap operation.
  - Fully operational to +600V.
  - Undervoltage lockout for both channels.
  - 3.3V and 5V input logic compatible.
  - Matched propagation delay for both channels.
  - Lower di/dt gate driver for better noise immunity.
  - Lead-free (PbF) option available.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E is not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **IR2184** is a high voltage, high-speed power MOSFET and IGBT driver designed for half-bridge applications. It features dependent high and low-side referenced output channels, making it suitable for driving N-channel power MOSFETs or IGBTs in high-side configurations. The device utilizes proprietary HVIC and latch immune CMOS technologies, ensuring robust performance in demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The IR2184 is commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** Efficiently driving power MOSFETs and IGBTs in power conversion applications.
- **Motor Control:** Used in half-bridge configurations for driving motors in various industrial and consumer applications.
- **Switching Power Supplies:** Facilitating high-speed switching in power supply circuits.
- **Inverters:** Driving the switching elements in inverter circuits for renewable energy applications.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and reliability in high-voltage environments, making it a versatile choice for engineers in the power electronics field.
