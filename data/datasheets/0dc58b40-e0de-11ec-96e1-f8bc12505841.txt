### Component Summary: GVA-82+ Monolithic Amplifier

**Product Code:** GVA-82+  
**Manufacturer:** Mini-Circuits  
**Type:** Monolithic Amplifier  
**Package Type:** SOT-89 (DF782)  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 4.8V to 5.2V (typical 5V)
  - DC Voltage on Pin 3: 5.8V (maximum for operation)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 90 mA (typical), 106 mA (max), 120 mA (max)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.84 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Gain: 15 dB typical at 100 MHz
  - Output Power at 1 dB Compression (P1dB): 20 dBm typical
  - High IP3: 40 dBm typical at 100 MHz
  - Ruggedized design with excellent ESD protection
  - Transient protected (US patent 6,943,629)
  - Unconditionally stable

#### Description:
The GVA-82+ is a wideband monolithic amplifier designed for high dynamic range applications. It utilizes a patented Transient Protected Darlington configuration and is fabricated using InGaP HBT technology. The amplifier is RoHS compliant and features a robust design that ensures repeatable performance across different production lots.

#### Typical Applications:
The GVA-82+ is suitable for various applications, including:
- Base station infrastructure
- Portable wireless communication systems
- CATV (Cable Television) and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) and Wireless LAN
- LTE (Long-Term Evolution) networks

This amplifier is ideal for use in environments where high gain, low noise, and robust performance are critical, making it a versatile choice for modern communication systems.
