  - Output Voltage Range: -0.5 V to VCC + 0.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: ±8 mA at 1.8 V
  - Input Clamp Current: ±50 mA
  - Continuous Current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Power Consumption: 10 µA at 1.8 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to 85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (DBV)

- **Special Features:**
  - NanoFree™ package technology (using the die as the package)
  - Latch-Up Performance Exceeds 100 mA
  - ESD Protection: 2000-V Human-Body Model, 200-V Machine Model
  - Supports Partial-Power-Down Mode with I_off
  - Max propagation delay (t_pd): 1.6 ns at 1.8 V

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **SN74AUC2G34** is a dual buffer gate designed for low-voltage applications. It performs the Boolean function Y = A in positive logic. This component is optimized for operation at 1.8 V, making it suitable for modern low-power digital circuits. The NanoFree™ packaging allows for a compact design, which is beneficial in space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Signal Buffering:** Used to isolate and buffer signals in digital circuits.
- **Level Shifting:** Can be used in mixed-voltage systems to translate signals between different voltage levels.
- **Data Communication:** Ideal for applications requiring fast signal propagation with low power consumption.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other portable electronics where power efficiency is critical.

This component is particularly useful in applications where low power consumption and high-speed operation are essential, making it a versatile choice for engineers designing modern electronic systems.
