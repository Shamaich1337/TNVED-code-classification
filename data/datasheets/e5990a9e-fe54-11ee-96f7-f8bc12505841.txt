### Component Summary: SN74CB3Q3257RGYR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.3V to 3.6V
  - I/O Voltage: 0V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - ON-state switch current: ±64 mA
  - Continuous current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical ICC: 0.7 mA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (16 pins), dimensions: 4.00 mm x 3.50 mm
- **Special Features:**
  - High-bandwidth data path (up to 500 MHz)
  - 5V tolerant I/Os
  - Low ON-state resistance (ron = 4Ω typical)
  - Rail-to-rail switching capability
  - Bidirectional data flow with near-zero propagation delay
  - Low input/output capacitance (Cio(OFF) = 3.5 pF typical)
  - Partial-power-down mode operation (Ioff)
  - ESD protection: 2000V HBM, 1000V CDM
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **SN74CB3Q3257** is a high-bandwidth FET bus switch designed for low-voltage applications. It utilizes a charge pump to elevate the gate voltage of the pass transistor, resulting in low and flat ON-state resistance. This device is organized as two 1-of-4 multiplexers/demultiplexers, allowing for flexible data routing in digital circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Networking Equipment:** Ideal for optical modules and optical networking applications, such as video over fiber and EPON.
- **Telecommunications:** Used in IP phones (both wired and wireless) and private branch exchanges (PBX).
- **Wireless Infrastructure:** Suitable for WiMAX and other wireless infrastructure equipment.
- **Data Communication:** Effective for USB interfaces and differential signal interfaces, providing bus isolation and low-distortion signal gating.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring high-speed data transfer and minimal signal distortion, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
