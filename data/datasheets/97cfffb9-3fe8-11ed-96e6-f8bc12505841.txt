### Component Summary: ACPL-227-500E

**Manufacturer:** Broadcom Limited  
**Product Code:** ACPL-227-500E  
**Type:** Dual-channel half-pitch phototransistor optocoupler

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Isolation Voltage (VISO): 3750 Vrms (for 1 minute)
  - Collector-Emitter Breakdown Voltage (BVCEO): 80 V
  - Emitter-Collector Breakdown Voltage (BVECO): 7 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Current (IF(AVG)): 50 mA
  - Pulse Forward Current (IFSM): 1 A
  - Collector Current (IC): 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - LED Power Dissipation (1 channel): 65 mW
  - Collector Power Dissipation (1 channel): 150 mW
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (TS): -55 to 125 °C
  - Operating Temperature (TA): -55 to 110 °C

- **Package Type:** SO-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Current Transfer Ratio (CTR): 50% (min) at IF = 5 mA, VCE = 5V
  - Non-saturated response time (tr): 2 µs (typical)
  - Common Mode Rejection (CMR): 10 kV/µs (typical)
  - Safety and regulatory approvals: cUL, IEC/EN/DIN EN 60747-5-5
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The ACPL-227-500E is a dual-channel phototransistor optocoupler designed for DC input applications. It features two light-emitting diodes (LEDs) that are optically coupled to two separate phototransistors, allowing for electrical isolation between input and output circuits. This component is packaged in an 8-pin SO package, making it suitable for surface mount technology.

#### Typical Applications:
- **I/O Interface:** Used in programmable controllers and computers to facilitate communication between different circuit potentials.
- **Sequence Controllers:** Employed in automation systems for controlling sequences of operations.
- **System Appliances:** Utilized in measuring instruments and various system appliances for signal transmission.
- **Signal Transmission:** Ideal for applications requiring isolation between circuits of different potentials and impedances, ensuring safe and reliable operation.

This optocoupler is particularly useful in environments where electrical isolation is critical, such as in industrial automation and control systems.
