E | Page 11 of 14 THEORY OF OPERATION  \nThe ADR5040/ ADR5041/ ADR5043/ ADR5044 /ADR5045 use \nthe band gap concept to produce a stable, low temperature \ncoefficient voltage reference suitable for high accuracy data \nacquisition components and systems. The devices use the physical \nnature of a silicon transistor base -emitter voltage in the forward -\nbiased operating region. All such transistors have approximately  a \n−2 mV/°C temperature coefficient (TC), making them unsuitable \nfor direct use as a low temperature coefficient reference. \nExtrapolation of the temperature characteristic of any one of \nthese devices to absolute zero (with the collector current \npropor tiona l to the absolute temperature), however, reveals that \nits V BE approaches approximately th e silicon band gap voltage. \nTherefore , if a voltage develops with an opposing temperature \ncoefficient to sum the V BE, a zero temperature coefficient \nreference results.  \nAPPLICATIONS  INFORMATION  \nThe ADR5040/ ADR5041/ ADR5043/ ADR5044 /ADR5045 are  \na series of precision shunt voltage references. - **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but power consumption can be derived from the output voltage and current ratings.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +125°C

  - Low temperature coefficient: 75 ppm/°C (maximum)
  - No external capacitor required for stability
  - Qualified for automotive applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The ADR5044BRTZ-REEL7 is a precision micropower shunt mode voltage reference from Analog Devices. It is designed for applications requiring a stable reference voltage with high accuracy and low drift over temperature. The device operates without the need for an external capacitor, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. It is available in a compact SOT-23 package, which is ideal for portable and battery-powered applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Portable, Battery-Powered Equipment:** The low power consumption and compact size make it ideal for handheld devices.
- **Automotive Applications:** Qualified for automotive use, ensuring reliability in harsh environments.
- **Power Supplies:** Used as a reference voltage in power management circuits.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Provides stable reference voltages for accurate measurements.
- **Instrumentation and Process Control:** Ensures precision in various measurement and control systems.
- **Energy Management:** Utilized in systems that monitor and manage energy consumption.

This component is particularly valuable in applications where precision and reliability are critical, such as in automotive electronics and portable devices.
