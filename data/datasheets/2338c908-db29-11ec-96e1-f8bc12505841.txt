  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 175 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to 175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO247-4

- **Special Features:**
  - High blocking voltage with low on-resistance (80 mΩ)
  - High-speed switching with low capacitance
  - High operating junction temperature capability
  - Very fast and robust intrinsic body diode

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The IV1Q12080T4 is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFET designed for high-voltage applications. It features a low on-resistance of 80 mΩ, making it suitable for efficient power management. The device is capable of handling high voltages up to 1200 V and continuous currents of up to 42 A, which allows it to be used in demanding environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The IV1Q12080T4 is commonly used in:
- **Solar Inverters:** For converting DC from solar panels to AC for grid use.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** To provide backup power and protect against outages.
- **Motor Drivers:** For controlling electric motors in various applications.
- **High Voltage DC/DC Converters:** For efficient voltage conversion in power systems.
- **Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS):** For efficient power conversion in electronic devices.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability under high voltage and temperature conditions.
