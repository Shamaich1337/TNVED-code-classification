### Component Summary: ES2D Super Fast Recovery Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** ES2D

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 140 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 2.0 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 50 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave)
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A (non-repetitive, 1ms square wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 2.0 A: 1.85 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AA (SMB)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - High forward surge capability
  - Super fast reverse recovery time
  - Meets Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL) 1 per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The ES2D is a Super Fast Recovery Rectifier, which is a type of semiconductor device used to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). It is designed to handle high-frequency rectification applications, making it suitable for power supplies, inverters, and converters. The device features a low profile, making it ideal for automated assembly processes.

#### Typical Applications:
- High-frequency rectification in power supplies
- Inverters and converters for various electronic devices
- Freewheeling diodes in consumer electronics and telecommunication systems

This component is particularly useful in applications where fast switching and high efficiency are required, such as in modern power management systems and electronic circuits that demand rapid response times.
