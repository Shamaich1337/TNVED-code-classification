#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 2.7V to 3.6V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Power On Current: 50 mA (typical)
  - Operating Current (Sequential Read): 30 mA (typical)
  - Program Current: 30 mA (typical)
  - Erase Current: 30 mA (typical)
  - Standby Current: 1 µA (typical)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Active power consumption varies based on operation; standby current is minimal at 1 µA.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Industrial: -40°C to 85°C
  - Industrial Plus: -40°C to 105°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Available in 48-Pin TSOP (12 x 20 x 1.2 mm) and 63-Ball BGA (11 x 9 x 1 mm)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Supports Multiplane Program and Erase commands
  - One Time Programmable (OTP) area
  - 100,000 Program / Erase cycles (typical)
  - 10 Year Data retention (typical)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **S34ML08G2** is an 8 Gb (4 Gb x 2) SLC NAND Flash memory device designed for embedded applications. It features an 8-bit input/output bus width and is compliant with the Open NAND Flash Interface (ONFI) 1.0 standard. The device is structured as a dual-die stack, allowing for increased density and efficiency in data storage.

#### Typical Applications:
The S34ML08G2 NAND Flash memory is typically used in:
- **Embedded Systems**: Ideal for applications requiring reliable and high-density storage solutions.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and digital cameras for data storage.
- **Industrial Applications**: Suitable for environments that demand high reliability and extended temperature ranges, such as automotive and industrial control systems.
- **Data Storage Solutions**: Employed in SSDs and other storage devices where fast read/write speeds and durability are critical.

This component is particularly advantageous in scenarios where data integrity and longevity are paramount, making it a preferred choice for industrial and consumer applications alike.
