  - RoHS compliant (indicated by the + suffix)

#### Description:
The TC4-1T+ is a surface mount RF transformer designed for applications in the frequency range of 0.5 to 300 MHz. It is primarily used for impedance matching in RF circuits, ensuring efficient signal transfer and minimizing losses. The transformer features a compact design suitable for high-density PCB layouts.

#### Typical Applications:
- **CATV (Cable Television):** Used in signal distribution and amplification circuits.
- **RF Signal Processing:** Ideal for applications requiring impedance transformation and signal isolation.
- **Communication Systems:** Utilized in various RF communication devices for improved signal integrity.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices that require RF signal handling, such as televisions and radios.
