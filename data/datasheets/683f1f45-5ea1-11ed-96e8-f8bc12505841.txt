- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40 °C to +90 °C

- **Package Type:** 2.5 x 2.0 x 0.9 mm ceramic package
- **Special Features:** MultiVolt™ compatible with 1.8V, 2.5V, or 3.3V power supply; PbFree/RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The ECS-TXO-2520MV-500-AN-TR is a Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (TCXO) designed for high stability and low power consumption in portable and wireless applications. It features a compact 2.5 x 2.0 mm ceramic package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The device operates over a wide voltage range and offers excellent frequency stability across temperature variations.

#### Typical Applications:
This TCXO is ideal for applications requiring precise timing and frequency control, such as:
- **Wireless Communication:** Used in mobile devices, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi modules where stable frequency is critical.
- **GPS Systems:** Provides accurate timing for positioning systems.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Found in devices like smartwatches, fitness trackers, and other portable gadgets.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Utilized in various industrial applications where reliable timing is essential.

The ECS-TXO-2520MV-500-AN-TR is particularly beneficial in environments where temperature fluctuations can affect performance, ensuring consistent operation across a wide range of conditions.
