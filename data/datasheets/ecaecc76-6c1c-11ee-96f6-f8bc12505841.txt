### Component Summary: SBTC-2-10L+

**Description:**
The SBTC-2-10L+ is a surface mount power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications. It operates as a 2-way splitter, allowing for the division of input power into two output ports with minimal loss. This component is particularly suitable for use in UHF/VHF receivers and transmitters, as well as cellular communication systems.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; typically not a primary concern for RF splitters.
- **Current Ratings:** Power input (as a splitter) is 0.5W maximum.
- **Power Consumption:** Internal dissipation is 0.125W maximum.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** Surface mount (Case Style: AT1029).
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **Special Features:**
  - Low insertion loss (0.3 dB typical).
  - Excellent amplitude unbalance (0.1 dB typical).
  - Very good phase unbalance (1.0 degree typical).
  - Temperature stable LTCC base.
  - Aqueous washable.
  - Protected by US patent 6,963,255.
  - RoHS compliant.

**Typical Applications:**
- UHF/VHF receivers and transmitters.
- Cellular communication systems.
- Any RF applications requiring power splitting with minimal loss and high isolation.

This component is ideal for applications where space is limited and performance is critical, making it a valuable choice for modern communication devices.
