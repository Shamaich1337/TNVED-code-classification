  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The IRLMS2002TRPBF is an N-Channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for high-efficiency applications. The Micro6™ package allows for a compact footprint, which is ideal for space-constrained designs.

**Typical Applications:**

- Battery management systems
- Load management applications
- Power switching applications
- DC-DC converters
- Motor control circuits

The IRLMS2002TRPBF is particularly advantageous in applications where efficiency and thermal performance are critical, due to its low on-resistance and high current handling capabilities.
