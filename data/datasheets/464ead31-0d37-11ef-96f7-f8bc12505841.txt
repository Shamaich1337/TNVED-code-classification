#### Description:
The CC0603KRX7R9BB472 is a surface-mount ceramic multilayer capacitor (MLCC) from YAGEO's X7R series. It is designed for general-purpose applications and high capacitance needs. The X7R dielectric material provides stable capacitance over a wide temperature range, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in PCs, mobile phones, DVDs, and video cameras for decoupling and filtering applications.
- **Data Processing**: Employed in data storage devices and processing units to stabilize voltage and reduce noise.
- **Power Management**: Utilized in power supply circuits to smooth voltage fluctuations and improve overall circuit performance.
- **Signal Processing**: Acts as a coupling capacitor in audio and RF applications to block DC while allowing AC signals to pass.

This capacitor is ideal for applications requiring compact size and reliable performance under varying environmental conditions.
