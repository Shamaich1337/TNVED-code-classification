### Component Summary: RFF500F-AWW-DNG

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Power Voltage (VDD): 3.1V to 3.6V (typical 3.3V)
  - Backlight Voltage (VL): 16.8V to 20.4V (typical 19.2V)
  - Touch Panel Power Supply (VDDT): 3.1V to 3.5V (typical 3.3V)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Driver Current (Black): 65 mA to 97.5 mA (typical 3.3V)
  - Touch Panel Current (ICTP): 51 mA to 77 mA
  - Backlight Current (IL): 60 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total power consumption varies based on usage but includes driver and backlight consumption.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -30°C to +80°C
  - Storage Temperature: -30°C to +80°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 5.0-inch TFT LCD module

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Color active matrix TFT LCD with a resolution of 800x480 pixels.
  - Includes a capacitive touch panel (CTP) with I2C interface.
  - LED backlight with a lifespan of approximately 50,000 hours at typical operating conditions.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The RFF500F-AWW-DNG is a 5.0-inch color active matrix thin film transistor (TFT) liquid crystal display (LCD) module. It utilizes amorphous silicon TFT technology as a switching device, providing a resolution of 800x480 pixels. The display is designed for transmissive viewing and features a normally black LCD type, which enhances contrast and visibility.

**Typical Applications:**
This TFT LCD module is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
