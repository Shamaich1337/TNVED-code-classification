**Type:** High Pass Filter (Ceramic)

**Manufacturer:** Mini-Circuits

- **Voltage Ratings:** Not specified; however, it is noted that coupling capacitors are required if DC voltage is present at either input or output ports.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Package Type:** FV1206 (Hermetically sealed, LTCC construction)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent power handling capability (up to 7 W).
  - Temperature stable performance.
  - RoHS compliant.

**Description:**
The HFCN-1500+ is a high pass filter designed for RF applications, operating within a frequency range of 1600 to 5500 MHz. It is constructed using ceramic materials and features a compact design, making it suitable for various applications where space is a constraint. The filter is hermetically sealed, ensuring reliability and durability in various environmental conditions.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Sub-harmonic Rejection:** Used in systems where it is necessary to eliminate unwanted sub-harmonic signals.
- **Transmitters/Receivers:** Essential in communication systems to allow higher frequency signals to pass while blocking lower frequencies.
- **Laboratory Use:** Ideal for testing and measurement applications where precise frequency filtering is required.

This component is particularly useful in RF communication systems, ensuring that only the desired high-frequency signals are transmitted or received, thereby improving overall system performance.
