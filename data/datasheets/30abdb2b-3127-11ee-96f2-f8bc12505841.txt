### Component Summary: 1N5365B-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components Corp  
**Product Code:** 1N5365B-TP  
**Type:** Zener Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Zener Voltage (VZ):** 36V
- **Operating Junction Temperature Range:** -55°C to +200°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +200°C
- **Power Dissipation:** 5.0W
- **Power Derating:** 67mW/°C
- **Maximum Forward Voltage (VF):** 1.2V at IF = 1.0A, TL = 75°C
- **Maximum Thermal Resistance (Junction to Lead):** 15°C/W
- **Maximum Thermal Resistance (Junction to Ambient):** 83°C/W
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type:** DO-15
- **Special Features:** Halogen-free option available upon request (add suffix "-HF"), Lead-free finish/RoHS compliant.

#### Description:
The 1N5365B-TP is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It provides a stable reference voltage of 36V, making it suitable for various electronic circuits that require voltage clamping or regulation. The diode is capable of handling a maximum power dissipation of 5 watts, which allows it to operate effectively in high-power applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage despite variations in load current or input voltage.
- **Overvoltage Protection:** Protects sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Signal Conditioning:** Utilized in signal processing circuits to stabilize voltage levels.
- **Reference Voltage Source:** Acts as a reference in analog circuits for biasing and other applications.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in power management systems, automotive electronics, and consumer electronics where reliable voltage regulation is critical.
