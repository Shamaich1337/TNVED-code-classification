### Component Summary: ESDH5V0D5-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components (MCC)  
**Product Code:** ESDH5V0D5-TP  
**Type:** ESD Protection Device

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Working Voltage (VRWM): 5 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 22 A
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR) at VRWM: 0.5 μA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (PPP): 300 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -55°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-523
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low Clamping Voltage (VC): 8 V (at IPP = 22 A)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Halogen Free and RoHS Compliant
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 Flammability Rating

#### Description:
The ESDH5V0D5 is an ESD protection device designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) events. It features a low clamping voltage and is optimized for applications requiring robust protection against voltage spikes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protects sensitive components in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops from ESD damage.
- **Telecommunications:** Used in communication devices to ensure signal integrity and protect against transient voltage spikes.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Provides ESD protection in various industrial applications, ensuring reliability and longevity of electronic systems.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Safeguards automotive electronic systems from ESD, enhancing the durability of automotive components.

This component is particularly useful in environments where ESD events are common, ensuring that sensitive electronics remain operational and reliable.
