### Component Summary: SMAJ5949B-TP

**Description:**
The SMAJ5949B-TP is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is part of the SMAJ series, which includes various Zener diodes with reverse voltage ratings ranging from 3.3V to 200V. The primary function of this component is to maintain a stable output voltage despite variations in load current or input voltage, making it essential for protecting sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** 100V (Zener voltage, VZ)
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Maximum Zener Test Current (IZT): 250 mA
  - Maximum Reverse Current (IR): 1 µA at 100V
- **Power Consumption:** 1.5 Watts (DC Power Dissipation)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +175°C
- **Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low Zener Impedance
  - Low Regulation Factor
  - VZ Tolerance: ±10%
  - Lead-Free Finish / RoHS Compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The SMAJ5949B-TP is commonly used in:
- **Voltage Regulation:** To provide a stable reference voltage in power supply circuits.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protecting sensitive components from voltage spikes in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics.
- **Signal Clamping:** Ensuring signal integrity by clamping voltage levels in communication circuits.
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply designs to maintain voltage levels across varying load conditions.

This Zener diode is particularly suitable for surface mount applications, making it ideal for modern electronic designs where space is a constraint.
