### Component Summary: SN74CB3T3245PWR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.3V to 3.6V
  - Input/Output Voltage: 0V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - ON-state Switch Current: ±128 mA
  - Continuous Current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 40 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP (20 pins)
- **Special Features:**
  - 5V-tolerant I/Os
  - Low ON-state resistance (ron = 5Ω typical)
  - Supports mixed-mode signal operation
  - Bidirectional data flow with near-zero propagation delay
  - Partial-power-down mode operation (Ioff)
  - ESD protection: 2000V (HBM), 1000V (CDM)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (260°C, unlimited time)

#### Description:
The SN74CB3T3245 is an 8-bit FET bus switch designed for low-voltage applications, compatible with TTL and CMOS logic levels. It features low ON-state resistance, allowing for minimal propagation delay, and supports voltage translation that tracks the supply voltage (VCC). The device is capable of handling mixed-mode signal operations, making it suitable for interfacing between different voltage levels.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Level Translation:** Facilitates communication between devices operating at different voltage levels (e.g., 5V to 3.3V or 2.5V).
- **Bus Isolation:** Can isolate different parts of a circuit to prevent interference.
- **PCI and USB Interfaces:** Used in digital applications requiring reliable data transfer.
- **Memory Interleaving:** Helps in managing memory access in complex systems.
- **Low-Power Portable Equipment:** Ideal for battery-operated devices due to its low power consumption.

This component is particularly useful in applications where signal integrity and low power consumption are critical, such as in mobile devices, consumer electronics, and communication systems.
