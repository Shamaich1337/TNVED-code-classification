### Component Summary: PE42423B-Z

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 2.3V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 120 µA (typical), 200 µA (max)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power handling: 
    - 38.5 dBm @ 2.4 GHz
    - 37.0 dBm @ 6.0 GHz
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 16-lead 3x3 mm QFN
- **Special Features:**
  - Fast switching time of 500 ns
  - High linearity with IIP3 of 65 dBm and IIP2 of 120 dBm
  - Exceptional isolation: 47 dB @ 2.4 GHz, 43 dB @ 6.0 GHz
  - No external blocking capacitors required if 0V DC is present on RF ports
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The PE42423B-Z is an UltraCMOS® SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) RF switch designed for high-performance WLAN applications, specifically supporting 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac standards. It operates across a frequency range of 100 MHz to 6 GHz and is optimized for high power handling and exceptional isolation, making it suitable for various wireless communication applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Wireless Communication:** The PE42423 is primarily used in WLAN products, including carrier and enterprise Wi-Fi systems, where it facilitates the switching of RF signals between different paths.
- **High Power Applications:** Its high power handling capabilities make it ideal for applications that require robust performance under varying power conditions.
- **Signal Routing:** The switch can be utilized in systems that require efficient signal routing with minimal loss and high isolation, such as in RF front-end modules and other communication devices.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where space is limited, and performance is critical, combining the benefits of GaAs performance with the cost-effectiveness and integration capabilities of conventional CMOS technology.
