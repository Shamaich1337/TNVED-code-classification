### Component Summary: XAL1510-472MED (Coilcraft)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance**: 4.7 µH (±20%)
- **DC Resistance (DCR)**: 3.35 mΩ (typ), 3.80 mΩ (max)
- **Self-Resonant Frequency (SRF)**: 12.7 MHz (typ)
- **Saturation Current (Isat)**: 39.0 A (20°C rise), 21 A (40°C rise)
- **Rated Current (Irms)**: 29 A (40°C rise)
- **Operating Voltage**: 0 – 60 V
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to +125°C (with 40°C rise)
- **Maximum Part Temperature**: +165°C (ambient + temperature rise)
- **Storage Temperature**: -55°C to +165°C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL)**: 1 (unlimited floor life at <30°C / 85% relative humidity)
- **Package Type**: Shielded Power Inductor
- **Weight**: 12.4 – 15.0 g
- **Termination**: RoHS compliant tin-silver over copper (other options available)

#### Description:
The XAL1510-472MED is a shielded power inductor designed for high current applications. It features a low DC resistance and soft saturation characteristics, making it suitable for voltage regulator modules (VRMs) and voltage regulator down (VRD) applications. The inductor is constructed with a composite core material, which contributes to its efficiency and performance in power management circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in DC-DC converters and power supply circuits to smooth out voltage fluctuations and provide stable power.
- **Voltage Regulation**: Ideal for applications requiring precise voltage regulation, such as in computer power supplies and automotive electronics.
- **Signal Processing**: Can be utilized in filtering applications to reduce noise and improve signal integrity in various electronic devices.

This component is particularly well-suited for applications in automotive and industrial environments due to its wide operating temperature range and compliance with AEC-Q200 standards.
