### Component Summary: IHLP2020BZERR22M01 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Rated operating voltage across inductor = 50 V
- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Heat Rating Current (DC)**: Up to 17.0 A (for 0.10 μH variant)
  - **Saturation Current (DC)**: Up to 45.0 A (for 0.10 μH variant)
- **Power Consumption**: Not explicitly stated; however, the inductor is designed for high efficiency in energy storage applications.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: IHLP-2020BZ (dimensions: 5.49 mm x 5.18 mm x 2.0 mm)
- **Special Features**:
  - Shielded construction
  - Low DCR/μH in its package size
  - Handles high transient current spikes without saturation
  - Ultra-low buzz noise due to composite construction
  - Excellent for DC/DC energy storage up to 5 MHz
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The IHLP2020BZERR22M01 is a high-performance shielded inductor from Vishay's IHLP series, specifically designed for applications requiring high saturation current and low DC resistance. It is a surface-mount device (SMD) that provides efficient energy storage and filtering capabilities in power management circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Ideal for high current Point of Load (POL) converters, low profile, high current power supplies, and DC/DC converters in distributed power systems.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Suitable for use in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers.
- **Battery-Powered Devices**: Effective in applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, such as in battery-powered devices.
- **FPGA Power Supply**: Used in DC/DC converters specifically designed for Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs).

This inductor is particularly beneficial in applications where high efficiency and low noise are paramount, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
