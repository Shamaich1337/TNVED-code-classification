### Component Summary: SM6T36CA

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** SM6T36CA

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Stand-off Voltage (V_R): 36 V
  - Peak Pulse Voltage (V_PP): 30 kV (air discharge), 30 kV (contact discharge) as per IEC 61000-4-2
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Leakage Current (I_RM): 0.2 µA at 25 °C, 1 µA at 85 °C
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (P_PP): 600 W (10/1000 µs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - High power capability at T_j max: up to 515 W (10/1000 µs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SMB (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features:**
  - Unidirectional and bidirectional types available
  - Low leakage current
  - High power capability
  - Complies with various standards including IEC 61000-4-4 and MIL-STD-883
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 as per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The SM6T36CA is a Transil (transient voltage suppressor) device designed to protect sensitive electronic equipment from electrostatic discharges (ESD) and electrical overstress. It is capable of clamping high voltage transients and is suitable for applications requiring low leakage current and high reliability.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Surge Protection:** Used in power management systems to protect against voltage spikes.
- **Signal Line Protection:** Ideal for safeguarding data lines in communication equipment.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices that require protection from ESD and other transient events.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Utilized in machinery and control systems to ensure operational stability against electrical overstress.

This component is particularly valuable in high-end equipment and switch-mode power supplies (SMPS) where reliability and stability are critical.
