### Component Summary: 74AVC1T1004

**General Description:**
The 74AVC1T1004 is a translating 1-to-4 fan-out buffer designed for clock distribution applications. It features dual supply voltages (VCC(A) and VCC(B)) for voltage translation, allowing it to operate across a wide range of voltage levels. The device includes one data input (A), four data outputs (Yn), and an output enable input (OE). It is particularly useful for low voltage translation between various voltage levels, making it suitable for modern digital circuits.

### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCC(A): 0.8 V to 3.6 V
  - VCC(B): 0.8 V to 3.6 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output current (IO): ±50 mA
  - Supply current (ICC(A) or ICC(B)): 100 mA max

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total power dissipation: 250 mW (for both SOT552-1 and SOT1430-1 packages)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in two packages:
    - TSSOP10 (SOT552-1)
    - X2QFN10 (SOT1430-1)

- **Special Features:**
  - Schmitt trigger action at all inputs for tolerance to slower input rise and fall times.
  - I_OFF circuitry to prevent backflow current when powered down.
  - ESD protection: HBM ANSI/ESDA/JEDEC JS-001 Class 2 (exceeds 2 kV) and CDM JESD22-C101 (exceeds 1000 V).
  - Maximum data rates: Up to 380 Mbit/s depending on voltage levels.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Description:
The 74AVC1T1004 is a digital buffer that translates and distributes clock signals across multiple outputs. It is designed to handle different voltage levels, making it ideal for interfacing between components operating at different supply voltages. The device's ability to pull outputs low when enabled and its high-speed performance make it suitable for high-frequency applications.

### Typical Applications:
- **Clock Distribution:** Used in systems requiring the distribution of clock signals to multiple components.
- **Voltage Level Translation:** Facilitates communication between devices operating at different voltage levels, such as interfacing 3.3 V logic with 1.8 V or 2.5 V logic.
- **Signal Integrity Improvement:** Enhances signal integrity in high-speed digital circuits by buffering and driving signals to multiple loads.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices where low power consumption and high-speed operation are critical.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the 74AVC1T1004, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and applications in electronic systems.
