### Component Summary: TEN 60-2411WIR DC/DC Railway Converter

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 9–36 VDC (Nominal: 24 VDC)
  - Output Voltage: 5 VDC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 12,000 mA (12 A)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power: 60 Watts
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Compact 2” x 1” metal package (without heatsink)
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency up to 92%
  - I/O isolation voltage of 3,000 VDC
  - Under voltage lockout circuit
  - Adjustable output voltage
  - Remote On/Off functionality
  - Compliance with EN 50155 and EN 61373 for railway applications
  - Qualification for fire behavior according to EN 45545-2
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **TEN 60-2411WIR** is a ruggedized 60 Watt DC/DC converter specifically designed for railway applications. It features a wide input voltage range of 9 to 36 VDC and provides a stable output of 5 VDC at a maximum current of 12 A. The converter is built to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including electromagnetic interference, shock, vibration, and thermal shock, making it suitable for transportation systems.

#### Typical Applications:
The TEN 60-2411WIR is primarily used in:
- **Railway and Transportation Systems:** Due to its compliance with EN 50155 and EN 61373 standards, it is ideal for use in trains and other transport vehicles.
- **Power Management:** It can be utilized to convert and regulate power in various electronic systems within the railway infrastructure.
- **Industrial Applications:** Its rugged design allows it to be used in industrial settings where reliability and durability are critical.

This converter is particularly beneficial in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability under extreme conditions, ensuring stable operation in critical systems.
