#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Supply Voltage: 0.9V to 6.5V
  - Valid Reset for VDD: > 0.6V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 150 nA (typical)
  - Maximum Current on RESET pin: ±10 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply current into VDD pin: 150 nA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT-23 (DBZ) and X2SON (DQN) packages
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultralow power consumption
  - Push-pull RESET output
  - Factory-trimmed reset threshold voltages
  - Manual reset input (only in TPS3831 variant)
  - Hysteresis to ignore fast transients
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **TPS3839G33DBZR** is an ultralow power voltage supervisory circuit designed to monitor a single supply voltage. It asserts an active-low reset signal whenever the supply voltage (VDD) drops below a predetermined threshold. The reset output remains low for a specified duration after VDD rises above the threshold, ensuring that the system is stable before resuming normal operation. This device is particularly suited for battery-powered applications due to its low quiescent current.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable and Battery-Powered Equipment:** Ideal for devices that require minimal power consumption to extend battery life.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Used in systems that need reliable voltage monitoring to prevent malfunction during power fluctuations.
- **Cell Phones and Tablets:** Ensures that the device operates correctly under varying voltage conditions.
- **Wearable Devices:** Suitable for health monitoring devices that require low power consumption.
- **Glucose Monitors:** Provides reliable voltage supervision in medical devices.

The TPS3839G33DBZR is particularly useful in applications where power management is critical, ensuring that microcontrollers and other digital circuits are reset appropriately during power fluctuations.
