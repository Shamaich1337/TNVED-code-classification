### Component Summary: HPC817C-2

**Description:**
The HPC817C-2 is a photo-transistor output opto-coupler, which integrates an infrared LED chip and a photo-transistor chip in a single package. Its primary function is to provide electrical isolation between input and output while allowing for the transfer of signals through light.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Forward Voltage (VF): 1.2V to 1.4V (at IF = 10mA)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 6V
  - Collector-Emitter Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)CEO): 80V
  - Emitter-Collector Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)ECO): 6V
  - Isolation Voltage (VISO): ≥ 5000 Vrms

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Input Forward Current (IFM): 50 mA
  - Collector Current (ICM): 50 mA
  - Collector-Emitter Reverse Current (ICEO): 100 nA (at VCE = 20V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Input Power Dissipation (PM): 70 mW
  - Collector Power Dissipation (PC): 150 mW
  - Total Power Dissipation (PT): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOPR): -55°C to 110°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOP4L Plastic Package (4-lead)

- **Special Features:**
  - High isolation voltage between input and output (≥ 5000 Vrms)
  - UL and VDE safety certifications
  - Complies with RoHS and REACH regulations
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): MSL1
