### Component Summary: TVP5150AM1PBSR

**Manufacturer:** Texas Instruments  
**Product Code:** TVP5150AM1PBSR  
**Description:** The TVP5150AM1 is an ultralow-power NTSC/PAL/SECAM video decoder. It converts baseband analog video signals into digital YCbCr 4:2:2 component video, supporting various video formats and providing advanced features for video processing.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Digital I/O Supply Voltage (IO_DVDD): 3.0V to 3.6V
  - Digital Supply Voltage (DVDD): 1.65V to 1.95V
  - Analog PLL Supply Voltage (PLL_AVDD): 1.65V to 1.95V
  - Analog Core Supply Voltage (CH_AVDD): 1.65V to 1.95V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Typical Power Consumption: 115 mW (active mode), <1 mW (power-down mode)
  - Digital Supply Current (IDD(D)): 25.3 mA to 32.9 mA
  - Analog PLL Supply Current (IDD(PLL_A)): 5.4 mA to 7.1 mA
  - Analog Core Supply Current (IDD(CH_A)): 24.4 mA to 31.7 mA

- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Total Power Dissipation: 115 mW (normal mode), 1 mW (power-down mode)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to 70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:** 
  - Available in 32-terminal TQFP (PBS) and 48-terminal PBGA (ZQC) packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Supports multiple video formats (NTSC, PAL, SECAM).
  - Integrated 9-bit ADC with 2× oversampling.
  - Programmable video output formats (8-bit 4:2:2 or 8-bit ITU-R BT.656).
  - Macrovision copy protection detection.
  - I2C interface for configuration and control.
  - Advanced video processing features including adaptive comb filtering and automatic gain control.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** 
  - MSL Level 3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The TVP5150AM1 is designed for applications requiring video decoding from analog sources. It features a high-speed ADC and advanced processing capabilities to ensure high-quality video output. The device can handle various input formats and provides flexibility in output configurations, making it suitable for a wide range of consumer electronics, including digital televisions, PDAs, notebook PCs, and portable video projectors.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Digital Televisions:** For decoding broadcast signals.
- **Portable Devices:** Such as PDAs and handheld gaming consoles, where low power consumption is critical.
- **Video Recorders/Players:** For processing video signals from various sources.
- **Surveillance Systems:** To decode video feeds from cameras.
- **Automotive Applications:** As it is qualified for automotive use (AEC-Q100).

This component is ideal for designers looking to integrate video decoding capabilities into their products while maintaining low power consumption and high performance.
