### Component Summary: ADP-2-20+

**Description:**
The ADP-2-20+ is a 20 to 2000 MHz power splitter/combiner designed for surface mount applications. It is primarily used to split or combine RF signals with low insertion loss and excellent isolation characteristics. This component is suitable for various RF applications, including instrumentation, PCS/cellular communications, and GPS systems.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Frequency Range:** 20 MHz to 2000 MHz
- **Isolation:** 
  - Typ: 18 dB
  - Min: 15 dB
- **Insertion Loss:** 
  - Typ: 0.7 dB
  - Max: 1.0 dB
- **Phase Unbalance:** 
  - Typ: 0.5 degrees
  - Max: 0.8 degrees
- **Amplitude Unbalance:** 
  - Typ: 0.1 dB
  - Max: 0.3 dB
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Power Input (as a splitter):** 1W max
- **Internal Dissipation:** 0.125W max
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Case Style: CD542)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.

**Special Features:**
- Low insertion loss (typical 0.7 dB)
- Excellent insertion loss flatness (0.4 dB peak to peak)
- Aqueous washable
- Protected under U.S. Patent 6,133,525
- RoHS compliant

**Typical Applications:**
- **Instrumentation:** Used in measurement and testing equipment to ensure accurate signal processing.
- **PCS/Cellular Communications:** Facilitates signal distribution in mobile communication systems.
- **GPS:** Enhances signal integrity in GPS applications, ensuring reliable positioning data.

This component is ideal for engineers looking for a reliable and efficient solution for RF signal splitting and combining in various high-frequency applications.
