### Component Summary: NC7SZ00M5X

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 1.65V to 5.5V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5V to 6.0V
  - Input Voltage Tolerance: Up to 6V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - High Output Drive: ±24mA at 3V VCC
  - DC Output Current: ±50mA
  - Input Leakage Current: ±1µA (max at 5.5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 2µA (typical) at 1.65V to 5.5V
  - Power Dissipation: 
    - SOT-23: 200mW
    - SC70: 150mW
    - MicroPak™: 130mW
    - MicroPak2™: 120mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages: 
    - 5-Lead SOT23
    - 5-Lead SC70
    - 6-Lead MicroPak™
    - 6-Lead MicroPak2™

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Ultra-high speed with a typical propagation delay (t_PD) of 2.4ns into 50pF at 5V VCC.
  - Power down high-impedance inputs/outputs.
  - Over-voltage tolerance inputs for 5V to 3V translation.
  - Proprietary noise/EMI reduction circuitry.
  - High-impedance state when VCC is 0V.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The NC7SZ00M5X is a TinyLogic® Ultra-High Speed (UHS) two-input NAND gate manufactured by ON Semiconductor. It is designed using advanced CMOS technology, which allows it to achieve high-speed operation while maintaining low static power dissipation. The device is characterized by its ability to operate over a wide voltage range and its high output drive capability, making it suitable for various digital logic applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The NC7SZ00M5X is commonly used in:
- Digital logic circuits for implementing NAND functions.
- Signal processing applications where high-speed operation is critical.
- Voltage level translation between different logic levels (e.g., 5V to 3V).
- Power management systems where low power consumption is essential.
- General-purpose logic applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial devices. 

This component is ideal for applications requiring compact size and high performance, particularly in space-constrained environments.
