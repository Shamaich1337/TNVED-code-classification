### Component Summary: W3544A Penta Band Hybrid PCB Antenna

**Product Code:** W3544A  
**Manufacturer:** Pulse (Suzhou) Wireless Products Co., Ltd.  
**Datasheet Reference:** L143.A (02/13)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Frequency Bands:** 
  - 824-960 MHz
  - 1710-2170 MHz
- **Dimensions:** 7.65 x 26 x 3 mm (0.38 x 1.02 x 0.12 inches)
- **Weight:** 1.11 g (0.04 oz)
- **Mounting Type:** Surface Mount Device (SMD)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -45°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -45°C to +85°C
- **Nominal Impedance:** 50 Ω
- **Gain (dBi avg):**
  - 824-960 MHz: -1.9 (65% efficiency)
  - 1710-1880 MHz: -1.3 (74% efficiency)
  - 1850-1990 MHz: -1.3 (74% efficiency)
  - 1920-2170 MHz: -1.66 (68% efficiency)
- **Return Loss (dB):**
  - 824-960 MHz: -4.1 (W3544A), -6.5 (W3544B)
  - 1710-1880 MHz: -4.6 (W3544A), -5.7 (W3544B)
  - 1850-1990 MHz: -16.3 (W3544A), -18.5 (W3544B)
  - 1920-2170 MHz: -12.3 (W3544A), -13 (W3544B)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The W3544A is a compact hybrid PCB antenna designed for GSM and 3G applications. It features a small footprint suitable for surface mounting, making it ideal for integration into various electronic devices. The antenna is optimized for performance in both corner and edge placements on printed circuit boards (PCBs), with the W3544A variant specifically designed for vertical mounting at the board edge.

#### Typical Applications:
The W3544A antenna is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- GSM/3G routers
- Femto cell modems
- Industrial wireless machine-to-machine (M2M) communications
- Smart grid devices
- Mobile hotspots

This antenna provides a high-performance solution for wireless communication, ensuring efficient signal transmission across multiple frequency bands while maintaining a compact design. Its robust operating temperature range also makes it suitable for use in diverse environmental conditions.
