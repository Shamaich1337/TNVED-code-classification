### Component Summary: IR2011STRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies (formerly International Rectifier)  
**Product Code:** IR2011STRPBF  
**Type:** High and Low Side Driver

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Offset Voltage (V_OFFSET): 200V
  - High Side Floating Supply Voltage (VB): -0.3V to 225V
  - Low Side Fixed Supply Voltage (VCC): -0.3V to 25V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output High Short Circuit Pulsed Current (IO+): 1.0A
  - Output Low Short Circuit Pulsed Current (IO-): 1.0A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent VBS Supply Current (IQBS): 90 to 210 µA
  - Quiescent VCC Supply Current (IQCC): 140 to 230 µA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to 125°C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): Up to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Lead PDIP and 8-Lead SOIC packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Floating channel designed for bootstrap operation.
  - Fully operational up to 200V.
  - Tolerant to negative transient voltage and dV/dt immune.
  - Independent low and high side channels.
  - Undervoltage lockout for both channels.
  - Compatible with 3.3V and 5V logic.
  - CMOS Schmitt-triggered inputs with pull-down.
  - Matched propagation delay for both channels.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL2 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020.

#### Description:
The IR2011 is a high-speed power MOSFET driver designed for driving high and low side power MOSFETs in various applications. It features independent output channels for high and low side driving, making it suitable for half-bridge configurations. The device is compatible with standard CMOS and LSTTL logic levels, allowing for easy integration into digital control systems. Its robust design includes features such as undervoltage lockout and matched propagation delays, which enhance performance in high-frequency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Converters:** Used in DC-DC converters for efficient power management.
- **DC Motor Drives:** Ideal for driving DC motors in various applications, including robotics and industrial automation.
- **Power Management Systems:** Suitable for applications requiring precise control of power MOSFETs in power electronics.
