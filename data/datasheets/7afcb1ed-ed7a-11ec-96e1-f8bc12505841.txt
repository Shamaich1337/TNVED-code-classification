### Component Summary: AZ1117H-3.3TRE1

**Description:**
The AZ1117 is a low dropout linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with a maximum output current of 1A. It features a dropout voltage of 1.15V at 1A output current and includes built-in current limiting and thermal shutdown protection. The AZ1117 series is available in both fixed and adjustable output voltage configurations, with the AZ1117H-3.3TRE1 specifically providing a fixed output voltage of 3.3V.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): Up to 20V (recommended operating range: 15V max)
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): 3.3V (fixed)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (IOUT): 1A (maximum)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (IQ): 5-10 mA (typical)
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +125°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages including SOT223, SOT89, TO220-3, TO252-2, and TO263. The specific part AZ1117H-3.3TRE1 is in the SOT223 package.
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low dropout voltage of 1.15V at 1A output current.
  - On-chip thermal shutdown for protection against overheating.
  - Trimmed current limit for controlled short-circuit current.
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The AZ1117H-3.3TRE1 is commonly used in various electronic applications, including:
- Power management in PC motherboards and graphic cards.
- Voltage regulation for LCD monitors and DVD players.
- Telecom equipment and ADSL modems.
- Printers and other peripheral devices.

This component is ideal for applications requiring a stable voltage supply with low dropout characteristics, making it suitable for battery-powered devices and other low-voltage applications.
