### Component Summary: SMBJ48CA

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SMBJ48CA  
**Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)  
**Configuration:** Uni-directional

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 53.3V (min), 58.9V (max)
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RWM): 48V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Surge Current (I_PP): 1A
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): Refer to specific conditions in the datasheet
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Peak Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 600W (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Power Dissipation on Infinite Heat Sink (P_D): 5.0W at T_L = 75°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AA (SMB)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low-profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Excellent clamping capability with low incremental surge resistance
  - Fast response time
  - High temperature soldering guaranteed (260°C for 10 seconds at terminals)
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1 (meets J-STD-020E)
  - RoHS compliant

#### Description:
The SMBJ48CA is a surface mount transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It is uni-directional, meaning it is designed to clamp voltage spikes in one direction, making it suitable for various applications where overvoltage protection is critical.

#### Typical Applications:
- Protection of integrated circuits (ICs) and MOSFETs in consumer electronics
- Signal line protection in sensor units
- Applications in telecommunications and industrial equipment
- General electronic equipment requiring transient voltage suppression

This component is particularly useful in environments where electronic devices are exposed to potential voltage spikes, ensuring reliability and longevity of the devices it protects.
