### Component Summary: SPF5122Z

**Manufacturer:** Qorvo, Inc.  
**Type:** Low Noise Amplifier (LNA)  
**Technology:** GaAs pHEMT (Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor)  
**Product Code:** SPF5122Z

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VD): 5.00 V (typical), max 5.25 V
  - Absolute Maximum Device Voltage: 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (IDQ): 90 mA (typical), range 75 mA to 105 mA
  - Maximum Device Current (ID): 120 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Dissipated Power: 660 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Surface Mount Package (specific dimensions not provided in the summary)

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low noise figure: 0.60 dB at 900 MHz
  - High linearity: OIP3 = 40.5 dBm at 1900 MHz
  - Integrated matching networks for simplified implementation
  - Flexible biasing options (3-5V, adjustable current)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SPF5122Z is a high-performance low noise amplifier (LNA) designed for RF applications, operating over a wide frequency range from 50 MHz to 4000 MHz. It utilizes GaAs pHEMT technology, which provides excellent noise performance and linearity. The device features an on-chip active bias network that ensures stable operation across varying temperatures and process conditions. Its compact design and integrated matching networks facilitate easy integration into various RF systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Cellular and PCS Receivers:** Enhancing signal reception in mobile communication devices.
- **W-CDMA and ISM Band Applications:** Used in wireless communication systems for improved signal integrity.
- **WiMAX Receivers:** Supporting broadband wireless access technologies.
- **PA Driver Amplifier:** Serving as a driver stage for power amplifiers in RF transmission systems.
- **Low Noise, High Linearity Gain Block Applications:** Ideal for applications requiring minimal noise and high signal fidelity.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the SPF5122Z, making it suitable for engineers and designers looking to implement this component in their RF designs.
