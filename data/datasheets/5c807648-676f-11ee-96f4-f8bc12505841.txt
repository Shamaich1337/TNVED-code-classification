### Component Summary: BAT41ZFILM (STMicroelectronics)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 200 mA
  - Surge Non-Repetitive Forward Current (IFSM): 1 A (tp = 10 ms sinusoidal)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at 200 mA: 1.0 V (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Maximum Operating Junction Temperature (Tj): +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOD-523
- **Special Features:**
  - Low leakage current losses
  - Negligible switching losses
  - Extremely fast switching
  - Low capacitance diode (typical capacitance: 3 pF at 1 V, 1 MHz)
  - ECOPACK2 compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically SOD packages are MSL 1 or 2.

#### Description:
The BAT41ZFILM is a low-capacitance Schottky barrier diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It is packaged in a compact SOD-523 surface mount package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The diode is characterized by its low forward voltage drop and minimal reverse leakage current, which contribute to its efficiency in various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Rectification:** Used in power supply circuits to convert AC to DC.
- **Reverse Polarity Protection:** Protects sensitive components from damage due to incorrect power supply connections.
- **Voltage Clamping:** Limits voltage spikes in circuits to protect downstream components.
- **High-Speed Switching:** Ideal for applications requiring rapid switching, such as in RF applications and high-frequency circuits.

This component is particularly useful in power management systems, signal processing, and any application where fast switching and low power loss are critical.
