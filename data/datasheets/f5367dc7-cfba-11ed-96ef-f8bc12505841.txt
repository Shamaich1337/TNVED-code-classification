#### Description:
The **TE0703-06** is a carrier board designed by Trenz Electronic for 4x5 cm System on Modules (SoMs). It serves as a baseboard that exposes the MIO (Multi-I/O) and PS/PL (Processing System/Programmable Logic) pins of the SoM to accessible connectors. The board is equipped with various components that facilitate testing and evaluation of the SoMs, making it a versatile platform for development and prototyping.

#### Typical Applications:
The TE0703-06 is typically used in:
- **Embedded Systems Development:** It provides a platform for developers to test and prototype applications using various SoMs.
- **Industrial Automation:** The board's robust design and wide operating temperature range make it suitable for industrial applications.
- **Networking Solutions:** With integrated Ethernet and USB interfaces, it can be used in networking applications.
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** The Micro SD card socket allows for data storage and retrieval, making it suitable for data logging applications.
- **IoT Devices:** Its connectivity options and power management features make it ideal for Internet of Things (IoT) applications.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the TE0703-06 carrier board, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and intended use cases.
