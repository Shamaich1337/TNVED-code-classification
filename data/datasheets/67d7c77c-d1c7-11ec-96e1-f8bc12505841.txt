### Component Summary: C2M1000170D

**Description:**
The C2M1000170D is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Power MOSFET, specifically an N-Channel Enhancement Mode device. It is designed for high-speed switching applications, offering high blocking voltage and low on-resistance, making it suitable for efficient power management in various applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDSmax): 1700 V
  - Maximum Gate-Source Voltage (VGSmax): -10/+25 V
  - Recommended Operational Gate-Source Voltage (VGSop): -5/+20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID @ 25˚C): 5.0 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID(pulse)): 15 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 69 W (at TC=25˚C, TJ=150˚C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 to +150˚C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to +150˚C
  - Solder Temperature: 260˚C (for 10 seconds)

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247-3

- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed switching with low capacitances
  - Low on-resistance (RDS(on)): 1.0 Ω (typical)
  - Halogen-free and RoHS compliant
  - Easy to parallel and simple to drive
  - Ultra-low drain-gate capacitance

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
The C2M1000170D is commonly used in:
- Auxiliary Power Supplies
- Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS)
- High-voltage capacitive loads
- Applications requiring high efficiency and reliability in power management systems.

### Summary:
The C2M1000170D is a high-performance SiC MOSFET that excels in high-speed switching applications, providing significant advantages in efficiency and thermal management. Its robust voltage and current ratings make it ideal for demanding power supply applications, particularly in industrial and automotive sectors.
