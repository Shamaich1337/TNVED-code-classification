### Component Summary: GSOT36C-E3-08

**Manufacturer:** Vishay Semiconductors  
**Product Code:** GSOT36C-E3-08  
**Type:** Two-Line ESD Protection Device  
**Package Type:** SOT-23  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Working Voltage (V_RWM): 36 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (V_BR): 39.5 V to 47.7 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (I_PPM): 3.5 A
  - Reverse Current at V_R = 36.5 V (I_R): ≤ 1 µA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (P_PP): 60 W (at I_PP = 1 A)
  - Clamping Voltage (V_C) at I_PP = 3.5 A: 60 V
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Level 1 (according to J-STD-020)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD immunity according to IEC 61000-4-2: ±30 kV contact and air discharge
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Bidirectional and asymmetrical clamping behavior

#### Description:
The GSOT36C-E3-08 is a two-line ESD protection device designed to safeguard sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) events. It features a compact SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. The device operates by clamping transient voltages to protect downstream circuitry, effectively acting as a switch that opens under normal conditions and closes during transient events.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Protecting data lines in smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in automotive control systems to protect against ESD, complying with AEC-Q101 standards.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Safeguarding signal lines in industrial automation systems.
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting communication lines from voltage spikes and transients.

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust ESD protection while maintaining signal integrity, making it a critical component in modern electronic designs.
