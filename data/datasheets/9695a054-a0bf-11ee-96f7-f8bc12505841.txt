### Component Summary: YJD90G06A

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** N-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 60V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 90A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 100°C: 63A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 360A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 3.7W
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 100°C: 1.8W
- **On-Resistance:**
  - RDS(ON) at VGS=10V: <4.8mΩ
  - RDS(ON) at VGS=4.5V: <7.2mΩ
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +175°C
- **Package Type:** TO-252-B
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - 100% EAS Tested
  - 100% VDS Tested
  - Halogen Free
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 Flammability Rating

#### Description:
The YJD90G06A is an N-Channel Enhancement Mode MOSFET designed for high-efficiency power switching applications. Utilizing split gate trench technology, it offers low on-resistance and high current handling capabilities, making it suitable for demanding applications. The device is optimized for heat dissipation and features a high-density cell design.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Switching Applications:** Ideal for use in circuits requiring efficient switching of power.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** Provides reliable power management in backup systems.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Used in power conversion applications to regulate voltage levels efficiently.

This component is particularly well-suited for applications where high efficiency and low thermal resistance are critical, such as in power management systems, renewable energy systems, and electric vehicles.
