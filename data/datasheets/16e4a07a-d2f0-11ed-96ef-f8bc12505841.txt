### Component Summary: GVA-93+ Monolithic Amplifier

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Operating Voltage (Vcc): 4.8 V to 5.2 V (typical 5.0 V)
  - DC Voltage on Pin 3: 6 V (maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Device Operating Current: 52 mA (typical), 57 mA (maximum)
  - Operating Current at 5V: 100 mA (maximum)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 0.34 W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Case Style: DF782 (SOT-89)
  
- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Ultra-wideband performance (0.01 to 9 GHz)
  - Excellent gain flatness of ±0.7 dB from 0.05 to 8 GHz
  - No external matching components required
  - Excellent ESD protection (HBM: class 1C, MM: class M2)
  - Low additive phase noise (-169 dBc/Hz @ 10 KHz offset)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Description:**
The GVA-93+ is a monolithic amplifier designed for ultra-wideband applications, utilizing GaAs HBT technology. It provides a flat gain across a broad frequency range, making it suitable for various wireless communication applications. The amplifier is RoHS compliant and features good thermal performance due to its SOT-89 package design.

**Typical Applications:**
The GVA-93+ is commonly used in:
- Base station infrastructure
- Test instruments
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) and Wireless LAN
- LTE (Long-Term Evolution) systems
- Satellite communication
- Avionics

This amplifier is particularly advantageous in applications requiring consistent performance across a wide frequency range without the need for external matching components, making it ideal for modern communication systems.
