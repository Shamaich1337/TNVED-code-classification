- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Hold Current (Ihold)**: 0.12 Amps

- **Package Type**: 1206 Surface Mount

  - RoHS compliant
  - Halogen-free
  - AEC-Q200 compliant for automotive applications
  - Compact design for space-saving on PCBs
  - Fast response time to fault events

#### Description:
The **MF-NSMF012** is a **Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient (PPTC) resettable fuse** designed to protect electronic circuits from overcurrent conditions. When the current exceeds the hold current rating, the fuse will trip, increasing its resistance significantly and effectively interrupting the circuit. Once the fault condition is removed, the fuse resets itself automatically, allowing normal operation to resume without the need for replacement.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Port Protection**: Suitable for USB 2.0, 3.0, and OTG applications to prevent damage from overcurrent.
- **HDMI Source Protection**: Protects HDMI 1.4 sources from excessive current.
- **PC Motherboards**: Provides plug-and-play protection against overcurrent conditions.
- **Mobile Devices**: Used in mobile phones and PDAs for battery and port protection.
- **Game Consoles**: Protects ports from overcurrent, ensuring device longevity and reliability.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and consumer electronics applications where space is limited, and reliable overcurrent protection is essential.
