### Component Summary: CY2304NZZXI-1T

**Manufacturer:** Cypress Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** CY2304NZZXI-1T  
**Description:** Four Output PCI-X and General Purpose Buffer

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 3.0 V to 3.6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 25 mA (unloaded outputs at 66.66 MHz)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power-up time: 0.05 ms to 50 ms
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40 °C to 85 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-pin TSSOP (Thin-Shrink Small Outline Package)
- **Special Features:**
  - One input to four output buffer/driver
  - Buffers all frequencies from DC to 140 MHz
  - Output-to-output skew less than 100 ps (typical 60 ps)
  - Active HIGH output enable (OE)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Functional Description:
The CY2304NZ is a low-cost buffer designed to distribute high-speed clocks for PCI-X and other applications. It operates at a supply voltage of 3.3 V and can handle output frequencies up to 140 MHz. The device is particularly noted for its low output-to-output skew, making it suitable for applications requiring precise timing.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Clock Distribution:** Used in systems that require the distribution of a clock signal to multiple components, ensuring synchronized operation.
- **PCI-X Applications:** Specifically designed for PCI-X systems, which are used in high-performance computing environments.
- **General Purpose Buffering:** Can be utilized in various electronic circuits where signal buffering is necessary to maintain signal integrity over distances or through multiple stages.

This component is ideal for applications in telecommunications, data processing, and any system requiring reliable clock signal distribution.
