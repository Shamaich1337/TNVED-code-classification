### Component Summary: TPS3802K33DCKR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.6V to 6V
  - Threshold Voltage: 2.93V (typical)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current: 9 mA (typical)
  - Maximum Low-Output Current (IOL): 5 mA
  - Maximum High-Output Current (IOH): -5 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current: 9 mA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SC-70 (SOT-323), 5-Pin

- **Special Features:**
  - Power-On Reset Generator with Fixed Delay Time: 380 ms
  - Manual Reset Input (MR) available
  - Precision Supply Voltage Monitor with 2% accuracy
  - ESD protection built-in

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TPS3802K33DCKR is a voltage supervisor from Texas Instruments, designed to monitor supply voltages and provide circuit initialization and supervision. It asserts a push-pull RESET signal when the monitored voltage (VDD) drops below a preset threshold, ensuring that the system can reset and operate reliably. The device features a fixed delay time of 380 ms for the RESET signal, allowing for proper system stabilization after power-up.
