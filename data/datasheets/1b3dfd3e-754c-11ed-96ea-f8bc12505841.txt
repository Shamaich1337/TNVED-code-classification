### Component Summary: BAS52-02V H6327

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** BAS52-02V H6327  
**Component Type:** Silicon Schottky Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Voltage (VR): 45 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 750 mA
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IFAV): 500 mA (50/60Hz, sinusoidal)
  - Non-repetitive Peak Surge Forward Current (IFSM): 2000 mA (t = 100 µs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 500 mW (T_S ≤ 110°C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:** SC79 (single)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low forward voltage drop at 200 mA
  - High reverse voltage capability
  - Pb-free (RoHS compliant)
  - Qualified according to AEC Q101 (automotive grade)
  - ESD sensitive device; handling precautions required
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The BAS52-02V is a medium current rectifier Schottky diode designed for applications requiring low forward voltage drop and high reverse voltage capability. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching speeds and low forward voltage, making them ideal for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient rectification.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for RF applications due to low capacitance.
- **Voltage Clamping:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **DC-DC Converters:** Commonly used in switching power supplies and converters.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its AEC Q101 qualification, it is suitable for automotive electronics.

This diode is particularly useful in scenarios where efficiency and speed are critical, such as in power supply circuits and high-frequency applications.
