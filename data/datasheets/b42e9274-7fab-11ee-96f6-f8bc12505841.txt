### Component Summary: Nexperia BAS116H Low Leakage Switching Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Voltage (VR): 75 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 85 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 2 mA (typical), 5 mA (maximum)
  - Reverse Current (IR) at VR = 75 V: 0.003 nA (typical), 5.0 nA (maximum)
  - Non-repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFSM): 4 A (tp = 1 µs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 375 mW (at T amb ≤ 25°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65°C to +150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD123F (small and flat lead Surface-Mounted Device)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low leakage current
  - Excellent coplanarity and improved thermal behavior
  - AEC-Q101 qualified (suitable for automotive applications)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SMD packages are rated according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The BAS116H is a low leakage switching diode designed for general-purpose switching applications. It is encapsulated in a compact SOD123F package, making it suitable for surface mount technology (SMT) applications. The diode features low reverse leakage current, which is critical for applications requiring minimal power loss and high efficiency.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General-Purpose Switching:** The BAS116H is ideal for use in various electronic circuits where switching is required, such as in signal routing and protection circuits.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for automotive electronics, where reliability and performance under varying environmental conditions are essential.
- **Power Management:** The low leakage characteristics make it suitable for power management applications, helping to improve overall system efficiency.
