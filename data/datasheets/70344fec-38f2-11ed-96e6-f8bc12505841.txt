necessary toimplement anefficient high voltage\nbuck regulator using aminimum ofexternal components. This easy touseregulator integrates a75VN-Channel\nbuck switch with anoutput current capability of3Amps. ### Component Summary: LM5576MHX/NOPB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 6V to 75V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Adjustable down to 1.225V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: 3A
  - Cycle-by-Cycle Current Limit: 4.2A (Q1), 5.1A (Q0)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - VCC Regulator Output: 7V (typical)
  - Bias Current: 3.4 to 4.5 mA
  - Shutdown Current: 57 to 85 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Q1: -40°C to +125°C
  - Q0: -40°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - HTSSOP-20EP (Exposed Pad)
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated 75V, 170mΩ N-channel MOSFET
  - Adjustable operating frequency (50kHz to 500kHz)
  - Emulated current mode control
  - Built-in protection features (thermal shutdown, cycle-by-cycle current limit)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **LM5576** is a high-voltage step-down (buck) switching regulator designed for automotive and industrial applications. It integrates a 75V N-channel MOSFET and is capable of delivering up to 3A of continuous output current. The device employs an emulated current mode control architecture, which enhances line regulation and load transient response while simplifying loop compensation. The adjustable frequency allows for optimization of size and efficiency, making it suitable for a variety of power supply designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Power Management:** The LM5576 is ideal for automotive applications due to its wide input voltage range and high-temperature operation.
- **Industrial Power Supplies:** It can be used in industrial equipment where robust power supply solutions are required.
- **Telecommunications:** The device is suitable for telecom applications that require efficient voltage regulation from high input voltages.
- **Consumer Electronics:** It can also be utilized in consumer electronics that demand efficient power conversion.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the LM5576MHX/NOPB, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and use cases in electronic designs.
