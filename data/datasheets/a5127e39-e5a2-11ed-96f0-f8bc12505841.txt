### Component Summary: SC4PS-33+

**Description:**
The SC4PS-33+ is a 4-way power splitter/combiner designed for RF applications. It operates over a wide frequency range from 300 MHz to 3000 MHz, making it suitable for various communication systems, including CATV, cellular, GPS, and VHF/UHF receivers/transmitters. The device is surface mountable and is RoHS compliant.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; typically not a primary concern for RF splitters.
- **Current Ratings:** Power input as a splitter is 1W max.
- **Power Consumption:** Internal dissipation is 0.20W max.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - Operating: -40°C to 85°C
  - Storage: -55°C to 100°C
- **Package Type:** Surface Mount (Case Style: CK1704)
- **Special Features:**
  - Wideband operation (300 to 3000 MHz, usable from 100 to 3600 MHz)
  - Low insertion loss (typical 1.6 dB)
  - Good isolation (typical 17 dB)
  - Good amplitude unbalance (typical 0.4 dB)
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The SC4PS-33+ is commonly used in:
- Communication systems for distributing signals
- CATV (Cable Television) applications
- Cellular networks
- GPS systems
- VHF/UHF receivers and transmitters

This component is essential for applications requiring signal splitting or combining while maintaining signal integrity and minimizing loss. Its robust design and wide frequency range make it versatile for various RF applications.
