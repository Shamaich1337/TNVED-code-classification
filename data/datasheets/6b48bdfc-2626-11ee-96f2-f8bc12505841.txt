### Component Summary: ADG734BRUZ

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Single Supply: 1.8 V to 5.5 V
  - Dual Supply: ±2.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Current (S or D): 30 mA
  - Peak Current (S or D): 100 mA (pulsed at 1 ms, 10% Duty Cycle max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Power Consumption: <0.01 µW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Industrial: -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 20-Lead Thin Shrink Small Outline Package (TSSOP)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On Resistance: 2.5 Ω typical
  - Guaranteed Break-Before-Make Switching Action
  - TTL/CMOS Compatible Inputs
  - Low Leakage Currents: ±0.01 nA typical
  - Fast Switching Times: 19 ns typical

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The ADG734BRUZ is a low voltage, CMOS analog switch that comprises four independently selectable single pole, double throw (SPDT) switches. It is designed for applications requiring low power dissipation, high switching speed, and low on-resistance. The device operates within a wide supply voltage range, making it suitable for battery-powered and portable instruments.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Data Acquisition Systems:** Used for routing signals in measurement and data collection applications.
- **Communication Systems:** Ideal for switching signals in telecommunication devices.
- **Relay Replacement:** Can replace mechanical relays in various applications, providing faster switching and lower power consumption.
- **Audio and Video Switching:** Suitable for routing audio and video signals without significant distortion.
- **Battery-Powered Systems:** Its low power consumption makes it ideal for portable devices that rely on battery power.

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, such as in mobile devices, consumer electronics, and instrumentation.
