### Component Summary: DG35NA100

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** DG35NA100 (part of the DG35NA100 to DG35NA160 series)  
**Type:** Three Phase Bridge Rectifier

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1000 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 700 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 1000 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO):
    - With heatsink (Tc = 110°C): 35.0 A
    - Without heatsink (Ta = 25°C): 3.5 A
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM):
    - Non-repetitive @ 60Hz Half-sine wave: 400 A
    - Non-repetitive @ 1ms square wave: 800 A

- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but related to forward voltage drop and current ratings.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:** 3GBJ (Thin single in-line package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Glass passivated chip junction
  - High surge current capability
  - Solder dip 275°C max for 7 seconds, per JESD 22-B106
  - RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The DG35NA100 is a three-phase bridge rectifier designed for converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). It is particularly suited for applications requiring high voltage and current handling capabilities, making it ideal for power management in various electronic devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- General-purpose AC/DC bridge full-wave rectification
- Switching power supplies
- Home appliances
- Office equipment
- Industrial automation applications

This component is essential in circuits where efficient power conversion is necessary, providing reliable performance in demanding environments.
