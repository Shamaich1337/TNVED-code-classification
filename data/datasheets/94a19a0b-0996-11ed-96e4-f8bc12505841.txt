### Component Summary: TLP281GB

**Manufacturer:** Toshiba Electronic Devices & Storage Corporation  
**Product Code:** TLP281GB

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (V_CE): 80 V (min)
  - Emitter-Collector Voltage (V_EC): 7 V
  - Isolation Voltage: 2500 Vrms (min)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - LED Forward Current (I_F): 50 mA
  - Collector Current (I_C): 50 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Diode Power Dissipation (P_D): 100 mW
  - Collector Power Dissipation (P_C): 150 mW
  - Total Package Power Dissipation (P_T): 200 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (T_op): -55 to 100 °C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -55 to 125 °C

- **Package Type:** Surface mount package

- **Special Features:**
  - Current Transfer Ratio (CTR) for Rank GB: 100% (min)
  - UL-recognized (UL 1577, File No. E67349)
  - cUL-recognized (CSA Component Acceptance Service No. 5A, File No. E67349)
  - VDE-approved (EN 60747-5-5)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The TLP281GB is a phototransistor optocoupler that consists of an infrared emitting diode optically coupled to a phototransistor. It is designed for surface mount assembly, making it suitable for compact electronic applications. The device provides electrical isolation between its input and output, which is essential for protecting sensitive components from high voltages and noise.

#### Typical Applications:
The TLP281GB is commonly used in:
- Programmable controllers
- AC/DC input modules
- PCMCIA fax modems
- Signal isolation in various electronic circuits
- Data communication systems where electrical isolation is required

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and reliable isolation is necessary to ensure the integrity and safety of electronic systems.
