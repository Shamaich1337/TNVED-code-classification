### Component Summary: STLQ015M18R

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** STLQ015M18R  
**Package Type:** SOT23-5L

#### Key Specifications:
- **Input Voltage Range:** 1.5 V to 5.5 V
- **Output Voltage:** 1.8 V (specific to STLQ015M18R)
- **Output Current:** 150 mA (guaranteed)
- **Quiescent Current:**
  - 1.0 µA (typ.) at no load
  - 1.4 µA (typ.) at 150 mA load
  - 1 nA (typ.) in OFF mode
  - 200 nA max. in OFF mode at 125 °C
- **Dropout Voltage:** 112 mV at 150 mA
- **Output Voltage Tolerance:** ± 2% at 25 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 °C to 125 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.
- **Special Features:**
  - Logic-controlled electronic shutdown
  - Internal current and thermal limit
  - Compatible with ceramic capacitors (C_OUT = 1 µF)

#### Description:
The STLQ015M18R is an ultra-low quiescent current linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage of 1.8 V with a maximum output current of 150 mA. It operates within an input voltage range of 1.5 V to 5.5 V and features a very low quiescent current, making it ideal for battery-powered applications. The device includes a shutdown mode that reduces power consumption to as low as 1 nA, significantly extending battery life in portable devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mobile Phones:** To provide stable voltage to various components while minimizing power consumption.
- **Digital Still Cameras (DSC):** Ensures reliable power supply for image processing and storage.
- **Battery-Powered Equipment:** Ideal for devices requiring long standby times and low power draw.
- **Portable Media Players:** Maintains consistent voltage levels for audio and video processing while conserving battery life.

This component is particularly suited for applications where power efficiency is critical, such as in portable and battery-operated devices, electronic sensors, and microcontroller power supplies.
