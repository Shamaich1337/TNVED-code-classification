  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to 125°C
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 5-Pin SOT23 (DBV)

- **Special Features:**
  - High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 70 dB at 10 kHz
  - Ultralow Output Noise: 15 µVRMS
  - Fast Start-Up Time: 63 µs
  - Stable with any 1 µF ceramic capacitor
  - Very Low Dropout Voltage: 38 mV at full load (TPS79147)
  - Enable (EN) pin for shutdown mode

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS79101** is an ultralow noise, high PSRR, fast RF 100-mA low-dropout linear regulator (LDO) designed for applications requiring a stable and clean power supply. It features a low dropout voltage, making it suitable for battery-operated devices where efficiency is critical. The device is available in a small SOT23 package, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- Powering Voltage-Controlled Oscillators (VCOs) and Phase-Locked Loops (PLLs)
- Bluetooth and Wireless LAN devices
- Portable and battery-operated electronics
- Noise-sensitive analog applications, such as RF electronics

The TPS79101 is particularly beneficial in applications where low noise and high stability are paramount, making it an excellent choice for RF and signal chain applications. Its fast start-up time and low quiescent current also enhance its suitability for battery-powered devices, ensuring efficient operation and extended battery life.
