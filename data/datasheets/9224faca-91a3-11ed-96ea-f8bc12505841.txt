### Component Summary: HMC362S8GETR

**Description:**
The HMC362S8GETR is a low noise, divide-by-4 static divider utilizing InGaP GaAs HBT technology. It is designed for high-frequency applications, operating from DC to 12 GHz. The component is housed in an 8-lead surface mount plastic package, making it suitable for compact electronic designs.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V ± 0.25V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Icc): 68 mA (typical)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Output Power: -6 dBm (typical at 11 GHz)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-lead Standard Small Outline Package (SOIC_N_EP)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low SSB Phase Noise: -149 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz offset
  - Wide bandwidth operation from DC to 12 GHz
  - Reverse Leakage: 60 dB (both RF outputs terminated)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC362S8GETR is primarily used in:
- **Prescalers for DC to X Band PLL Applications**
- **Satellite Communication Systems**
- **Fiber Optic Communication**
- **Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multi-Point Radios**
- **VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) Systems**

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise frequency division and low noise performance, making it suitable for high-frequency communication systems and signal processing tasks.
