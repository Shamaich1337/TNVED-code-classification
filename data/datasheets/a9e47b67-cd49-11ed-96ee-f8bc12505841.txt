### Component Summary: IRLML6402TRPBF

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies (formerly International Rectifier)  
**Type:** P-Channel Power MOSFET  
**Package Type:** Micro3™ (SOT-23)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): -20 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±12 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: -3.7 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 70°C: -2.2 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): -22 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 1.3 W
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at 70°C: 0.8 W
  - Linear Derating Factor: 0.01 W/°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Maximum Junction-to-Ambient (RθJA): 75°C/W (typical), 100°C/W (max)
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra Low On-Resistance (RDS(on)): 0.065 Ω at VGS = -4.5 V, ID = -3.7 A
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Lead-Free, RoHS Compliant, Halogen-Free
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: MSL 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The IRLML6402TRPBF is a P-Channel Power MOSFET designed for efficient power management applications. It utilizes advanced processing techniques to achieve low on-resistance, which enhances its performance in various electronic circuits. The device is housed in a compact Micro3™ (SOT-23) package, making it suitable for applications where space is limited.

- **Battery Management:** Ideal for use in battery-powered devices due to its low on-resistance and efficient switching capabilities.
- **Load Management:** Commonly used in load switching applications where power efficiency is critical.
- **Portable Electronics:** Its low profile and small footprint make it suitable for portable devices such as smartphones, tablets, and PCMCIA cards.
- **Power Supply Circuits:** Can be utilized in power supply circuits for efficient voltage regulation and control.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, making it a preferred choice for designers in the electronics industry.
