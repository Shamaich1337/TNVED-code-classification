#### Description:
The DLW21SN900SQ2L is a common mode choke inductor designed for noise suppression in high-speed differential signal lines. It is particularly effective in applications where high-frequency signals are present, providing a high common mode impedance that helps to filter out unwanted noise while maintaining signal integrity.

- **Medical Equipment:** Suitable for non-implantable and non-surgical applications.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Applicable in various industrial settings, excluding transportation and energy equipment.
- **Display Technology:** Utilized in LVDS lines for notebooks and LCDs.
