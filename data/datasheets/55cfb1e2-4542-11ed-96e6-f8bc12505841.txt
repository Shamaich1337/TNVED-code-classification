- **Special Features:**
  - Endurance of 5,000 hours at 105°C

  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **KL100M400G200A** is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor designed for high voltage applications, particularly in automotive environments. It features a rated voltage of 400V DC and is capable of operating in temperatures ranging from -40°C to +105°C. This capacitor is characterized by its long endurance of 5,000 hours at high temperatures, making it suitable for demanding applications.

- **Power Management:** Filtering and smoothing in power supply circuits.
- **Automotive Electronics:** Used in various automotive applications due to its AEC-Q200 compliance, ensuring reliability in harsh conditions.
- **Signal Processing:** Coupling and decoupling applications in electronic circuits.
- **Energy Storage:** In applications requiring temporary energy storage and release.

This capacitor is ideal for use in environments where high reliability and performance under temperature stress are critical, such as in automotive electronics, industrial machinery, and power supply systems.
