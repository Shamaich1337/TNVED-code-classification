### Component Summary: SPU0410HR5H-PB

**Description:**
The SPU0410HR5H-PB is a miniature, high-performance, low-power silicon microphone utilizing Knowles’ SiSonic™ MEMS technology. It integrates an acoustic sensor, a low-noise input buffer, and an output amplifier. This microphone is designed for applications requiring excellent wideband audio performance and RF immunity.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.5 to 3.6 V
  - Absolute Maximum Rating: -0.5 to +5.0 V (VDD to Ground)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 120 µA (typical), 160 µA (maximum)
  - Input Current to Any Pin: ±5 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Operating Power: 120 µA (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 to +100 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Miniature surface mount device (SMD)

- **Special Features:**
  - Omnidirectional directivity
  - Low current consumption
  - MaxRF protection
  - Ultra-stable performance
  - Standard SMD reflow compatible

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Class 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
The SPU0410HR5H-PB is suitable for a variety of portable electronic devices, including:
- Cell phones and smartphones
- Laptop computers
- Digital still cameras
- Portable music recorders
- Other devices where high-quality audio capture is essential

This microphone is particularly useful in applications that require robust audio performance in environments with potential RF interference, making it ideal for consumer electronics and communication devices.
