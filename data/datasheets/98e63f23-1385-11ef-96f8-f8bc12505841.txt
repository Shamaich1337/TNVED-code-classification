### Component Summary: HMC952ALP5GETR

**Description:**
The HMC952ALP5GETR is a GaAs pHEMT (Pseudomorphic High Electron Mobility Transistor) MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) power amplifier designed for operation in the frequency range of 8 to 14 GHz. It features a built-in power detector and is capable of delivering up to 2 Watts of output power, making it suitable for various high-frequency applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +6V (max +6.5V)
  - Gate Bias Voltage (Vgg): -3V to 0V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 1400 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Supply Current: 1400 mA at +6V
  - Power Dissipation: 10 W (derated at 137 mW/°C above 85°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C
  - Operating Temperature: -55°C to +85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 24-lead Lead Frame Chip Scale Package (LFCSP), 5 mm x 5 mm body size

- **Special Features:**
  - High saturated output power: +34.5 dBm
  - High gain: 32 dB
  - High output IP3: +43 dBm
  - Integrated temperature-compensated power detector
  - 50-ohm matched input/output

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 3 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The HMC952ALP5GETR is ideal for:
- Point-to-point radios
- Point-to-multipoint radios
- Satellite communications (SATCOM)
- Other linear applications requiring high output power and efficiency in the specified frequency range.

### Conclusion:
The HMC952ALP5GETR is a robust power amplifier suitable for high-frequency applications, providing significant output power and gain while maintaining efficiency. Its integrated power detector and temperature compensation features enhance its usability in demanding communication systems.
