  - Remote On/Off control

  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The TDN 1-2411WI is a high-performance DC/DC converter from the TDN 1WI Series by TRACO POWER. It is designed to provide a compact and efficient solution for converting a wide range of input voltages into a stable output voltage of 5 VDC at a maximum current of 200 mA. The converter features full load operation up to 90°C, ensuring reliability in demanding environments. It includes essential protections such as short circuit protection and offers remote control capabilities.

- Power management in telecommunications equipment
- Industrial automation systems
- Embedded systems and microcontroller applications
- Medical devices
- Consumer electronics where compact power solutions are essential

This converter is particularly useful in scenarios where isolation and regulation are critical, making it ideal for sensitive electronic applications.
