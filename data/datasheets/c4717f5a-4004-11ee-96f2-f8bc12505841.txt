### Component Summary: SPW47N60C3

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Code:** SPW47N60C3  
**Type:** Power MOSFET (CoolMOS™ Power Transistor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 650 V
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): 600 V (typical), 700 V (at ID=20A)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 25 °C: 47 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 100 °C: 30 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID puls): 141 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot) at TC = 25 °C: 415 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - P-TO247

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low RDS(on): 0.07 Ω (at VGS = 10V, ID = 30A, Tj = 25 °C)
  - Low gate charge and capacitances
  - Periodic avalanche rated
  - Extreme dV/dt rated
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The SPW47N60C3 is a high-voltage power MOSFET designed for efficient power management applications. It features a revolutionary high-voltage technology that provides one of the best on-state resistances in its class, making it suitable for high-efficiency switching applications. The device is characterized by its low gate charge, which allows for faster switching speeds and reduced power losses.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Ideal for use in power supplies, converters, and inverters due to its high efficiency and low on-resistance.
- **Switching Applications:** Suitable for applications requiring fast switching, such as motor drives and lighting control.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Commonly used in industrial power systems and automation equipment.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Can be utilized in various consumer electronic devices that require efficient power conversion.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where thermal management and efficiency are critical, making it a preferred choice for engineers designing high-performance power circuits.
