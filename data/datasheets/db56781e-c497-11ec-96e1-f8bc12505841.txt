#### Description:
The **R413R34705000M** is a metallized polypropylene film capacitor designed for electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression. It is classified under the X1/Y2 safety categories, making it suitable for applications where failure could lead to electric shock hazards. The capacitor features self-healing properties, which enhance its reliability and longevity in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **EMI Suppression**: Used in "line-to-ground" and "across-the-line" applications to filter out unwanted electromagnetic interference.
- **Safety Applications**: Suitable for use in circuits where capacitor failure could pose a risk of electric shock.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Compliant with AEC-Q200 standards, making it suitable for automotive applications requiring high reliability.
- **General Power Supply Filtering**: Can be used in power supply circuits to improve performance and reduce noise.
