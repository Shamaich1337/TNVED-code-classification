### Component Summary: Axicom IM Relay (IM06GR)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Switching Voltage: 220 VDC / 250 VAC
  - Coil Voltage: 12 VDC
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Rated Current: 2 A
  - Maximum Switching Current: 2 A
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Coil Power: 140 mW (standard version)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SMT (Surface Mount Technology) with gull wing terminals
- **Special Features**: 
  - Slim line design (10x6mm), low profile (5.65mm)
  - High dielectric strength (up to 2500 Vrms)
  - High mechanical shock resistance (up to 50g functional)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL3 according to JEDEC J-STD-020F

#### Description:
The Axicom IM Relay (IM06GR) is a signal relay designed for various applications requiring reliable switching capabilities. It features a monostable configuration with two changeover contacts (2 form C) and is suitable for low power applications. The relay is compact, making it ideal for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunication**: Used in switching circuits for telecommunication equipment.
- **Access and Transmission Equipment**: Ideal for routers, switches, and other networking devices.
- **Optical Network Terminals**: Utilized in fiber optic communication systems.
- **Modems**: Employed in modems for signal routing.
- **Office and Business Equipment**: Suitable for printers, copiers, and other office machinery.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in various consumer devices requiring relay functionality.
- **Measurement and Test Equipment**: Applied in testing setups for signal routing.
- **Industrial Control**: Utilized in automation systems for controlling machinery.
- **Medical Equipment**: Suitable for medical devices requiring reliable switching.
- **HVAC Systems**: Used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for control purposes.

This relay is designed to meet the demands of modern electronic applications, providing a robust solution for signal switching in a variety of environments.
