#### Description:
The BZX84-B68 is a low-power voltage regulator diode designed for use in various electronic applications. It is part of the BZX84 series, which includes a range of diodes with different breakdown voltages. The primary function of this component is to provide stable voltage regulation, ensuring that the output voltage remains constant despite variations in load current or input voltage.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Regulation Functions**: The BZX84 series diodes are commonly used in power management circuits to maintain a steady voltage level.
- **Automotive Applications**: Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics, where reliability and performance are critical.
- **Consumer Electronics**: It can be utilized in various consumer electronic devices for voltage regulation.
- **Signal Processing**: The diodes can also be employed in signal processing applications where voltage stability is essential.
