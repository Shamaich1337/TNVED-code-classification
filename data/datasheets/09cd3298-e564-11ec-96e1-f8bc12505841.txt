  - Output Voltage: ±5 VDC

  - -40°C to +85°C

  - SIP (Single In-line Package)

  - High efficiency up to 81%
  - Reinforced insulation with isolation voltage of 4200 VAC or 6000 VDC
  - Patient leakage current: Max 2 µA
  - Compliance with EN60601-1 and IEC60950 standards
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Description:**
The H0505S-1WR2 is a DC/DC converter designed to provide isolated power conversion. It features a fixed input voltage of 5 VDC and outputs a dual ±5 VDC. This component is part of the G_S-1WR2 & H_S-1WR2 series from MORNSUN, which is known for its compact size, high isolation, and low leakage current characteristics. The converter is suitable for applications requiring reinforced insulation and is particularly designed for medical and industrial applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The H0505S-1WR2 is widely used in:
- Medical devices where isolation and safety are critical.
- IGBT driver circuits that require stable power supply.
- High voltage collection circuits where isolation between input and output is necessary.
- General power management applications where a compact and efficient power supply is needed.

This component is ideal for scenarios where the input voltage is stable (±10% variation) and where there are no stringent requirements for line regulation and output ripple/noise.
