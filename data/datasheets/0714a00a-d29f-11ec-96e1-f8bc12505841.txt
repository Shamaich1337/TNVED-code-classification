The SN74LVC1G32 is a single 2-input positive-OR gate designed for operation within a voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V. It performs the Boolean function Y = A + B, where Y is the output and A and B are the inputs. This CMOS device is characterized by high output drive capabilities while maintaining low static power dissipation across a broad operating voltage range. The device is particularly noted for its ultra-small package options, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.
