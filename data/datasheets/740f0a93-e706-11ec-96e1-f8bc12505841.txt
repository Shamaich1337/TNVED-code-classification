The MD60S16NM2 is a three-phase bridge rectifier module designed for high voltage and current applications. This module is specifically designed to handle significant electrical loads while maintaining thermal stability.

- **Power Supplies:** Used in three-phase rectifiers for converting AC to DC in power supply systems.
- **DC Motor Field Supplies:** Suitable for rectifying power for DC motors, ensuring efficient operation.
