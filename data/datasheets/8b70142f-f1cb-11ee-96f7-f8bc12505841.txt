D | 14 of 17PROXIMITY SENSORS\nProximity sensors can be capacitive or inductive and are used in\na variety of applications. One of the most common applications is\nliquid level sensing in tanks. This is particularly popular in pharma-\nceutical environments where a tank must know when to stop filling\nor mixing a given liquid. In aerospace applications, these sensors\ndetect the level of oxygen used to propel engines. Whether in a\ncombustible environment or not, capacitive sensors generally use\nlow voltage. The precision and low voltage of the AD8603/AD8607/\nAD8609  make the parts an excellent choice for such applications.\nCOMPOSITE AMPLIFIERS\nA composite amplifier can provide a very high gain in applications\nwhere high closed-loop dc gains are needed.
