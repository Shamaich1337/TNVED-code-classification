### Component Summary: HMC573LC3B

**Description:**
The HMC573LC3B is a GaAs MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) x2 active frequency multiplier designed for high-frequency applications. It operates within an input frequency range of 4 to 11 GHz and provides an output frequency range of 8 to 22 GHz. This component is particularly suitable for clock generation applications, point-to-point and VSAT radios, test instrumentation, and military and space applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vdd): +5V ± 0.5V
  - Maximum Supply Voltage: +6.0 Vdc

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (Idd): 92 mA (typical at Vdd = 5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 719 mW (at T = 85 °C, derating 8.0 mW/°C above 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Leadless RoHS compliant SMT package (3x3 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - High output power: +12 dBm (typical)
  - Low input power drive: 0 to +6 dBm
  - Fo Isolation: >20 dBc @ Fout = 16 GHz
  - 3Fo Isolation: >25 dBc
  - Low additive SSB Phase Noise: -134 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz offset
  - RoHS compliant, eliminating the need for wire bonding

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL3 (maximum peak reflow temperature of 260 °C)

### Typical Applications:
The HMC573LC3B is utilized in various high-frequency applications, including:
- **Clock Generation Applications:** Such as SONET OC-192 and SDH STM-64.
- **Point-to-Point and VSAT Radios:** Used in communication systems for signal transmission.
- **Test Instrumentation:** Employed in equipment for testing and measurement in RF applications.
- **Military and Space Applications:** Suitable for environments requiring robust performance under varying conditions.

This component is designed to provide efficient frequency multiplication with minimal noise, making it ideal for high-performance RF systems.
