### Component Summary: SM8A27HE3/2D

**Manufacturer:** Vishay General Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** SM8A27HE3/2D  
**Component Type:** Surface Mount Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 24V to 30V (at 10mA)
  - Maximum Working Stand-off Voltage (VWM): 22V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Non-repetitive Peak Reverse Surge Current (IRSM): 130A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 700A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (P_PPM): 6600W (10/1000 μs waveform)
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 8W (at T_C = 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DO-218AB

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Low leakage current and low forward voltage drop
  - High surge capability
  - Meets ISO7637-2 surge specification
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 245 °C

#### Description:
The SM8A27HE3/2D is a surface mount transient voltage suppressor designed to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage transients caused by inductive load switching and lightning strikes. It utilizes a passivated anisotropic rectifier technology that ensures high reliability and stability at elevated temperatures, making it suitable for automotive and high-reliability applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Electronics:** Protection against load dump and other voltage transients in automotive circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Safeguarding sensitive components in devices against voltage spikes.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Used in systems where inductive loads may cause voltage transients.
- **Telecommunications:** Protecting communication lines from voltage surges.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where high reliability and performance under extreme conditions are critical, such as in automotive and industrial applications.
