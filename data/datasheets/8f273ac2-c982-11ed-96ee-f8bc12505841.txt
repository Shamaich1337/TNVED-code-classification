### Component Summary: MBRS360T3G

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 60 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 60 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 3.0 A at T_L = 137 °C; 4.0 A at T_L = 127 °C
  - Nonrepetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 125 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF): 0.63 V at IF = 3.0 A, T_J = 25 °C
  - Maximum Instantaneous Reverse Current (iR): 0.03 mA at rated DC voltage, T_J = 25 °C; 3.0 mA at T_J = 100 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature Range (Tstg): -65 °C to +175 °C
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SMC (Case 403A) and SMB (Case 403AC) surface mount packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Small compact surface mountable package with J-bend leads.
  - Highly stable oxide passivated junction.
  - Excellent ability to withstand reverse avalanche energy transients.
  - Guard-ring for stress protection.
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 1 as per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Description:**
The MBRS360T3G is a Schottky power rectifier designed for low voltage, high frequency rectification applications. It utilizes the Schottky Barrier principle in a metal-to-silicon power diode configuration, featuring state-of-the-art epitaxial construction with oxide passivation. This component is particularly suited for applications where compact size and weight are critical.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power management systems, including power supply circuits.
- Signal processing applications where fast switching is required.
- Freewheeling diodes in inductive loads to prevent voltage spikes.
- Polarity protection circuits to safeguard sensitive components.
- Automotive applications, especially where AEC-Q101 compliance is necessary.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the MBRS360T3G, making it a versatile choice for various electronic designs requiring efficient rectification.
