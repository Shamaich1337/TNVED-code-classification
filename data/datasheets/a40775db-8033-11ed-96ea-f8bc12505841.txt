### Component Summary: SPF-5189Z

**Description:**
The SPF-5189Z is a high-performance Low-Noise Amplifier (LNA) utilizing GaAs pHEMT technology. It is designed for operation across a wide frequency range of 50 MHz to 4000 MHz. The amplifier features an on-chip active bias network that ensures stable current operation over varying temperatures and process conditions. It is optimized for low noise and high linearity, making it suitable for applications requiring high dynamic range.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (V_D): +3V to +5V (typical +5V)
  - Absolute Maximum Voltage: +5.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (I_D): 75 mA (typical 90 mA, maximum 105 mA)
  - Maximum Device Current: 120 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 660 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Lead Temperature (T_LEAD): -40°C to +85°C
  - Maximum Storage Temperature: -60°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low noise figure of 0.60 dB at 900 MHz
  - High linearity with Output IP3 of +39.5 dBm at 1960 MHz
  - Integrated broadband internal matching networks
  - Single DC supply operation

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 3 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020

**Typical Applications:**
The SPF-5189Z is widely used in various RF applications, including:
- Cellular and PCS receivers
- W-CDMA and ISM band applications
- WiMAX receivers
- PA driver amplifiers
- Low noise, high linearity gain block applications

This amplifier is particularly suited for high dynamic range receivers due to its high maximum input power specification and low noise figure, making it ideal for modern communication systems where signal integrity is critical.
