The IX3120GSTR is an optocoupler gate driver designed for driving IGBTs and MOSFETs in mid-power applications. It features an input infrared LED that is optically coupled to a power output stage capable of sourcing or sinking up to 2.5A of peak current. This component is particularly noted for its high noise immunity and low input LED current, making it suitable for applications requiring reliable operation in electrically noisy environments.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Isolated IGBT/MOSFET Gate Drive:** The IX3120GSTR is ideal for driving power transistors in various applications, ensuring safe and effective switching.
- **Switch Mode Power Supplies:** It can be used in power supply circuits to manage the gate drive of power switches.
- **Industrial Inverters:** The component is suitable for use in inverter circuits, providing isolation and control for power conversion.
- **Motor Drivers:** It is commonly employed in motor control applications, where precise gate driving is essential for performance.
