### Component Summary: IRF7469TRPBF

**Manufacturer:** International Rectifier  
**Product Code:** IRF7469TRPBF  
**Type:** Power MOSFET (HEXFET)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 40 V
  - Maximum Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 9.0 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 70°C: 7.3 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (IDM): 73 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at 25°C: 2.5 W
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) at 70°C: 1.6 W
  - Linear Derating Factor: 0.02 mW/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SO-8 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low gate impedance
  - Very low RDS(on) (17 mΩ at VGS = 10V, ID = 9.0A)
  - Fully characterized avalanche voltage and current
  - Lead-free construction

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The IRF7469TRPBF is a high-performance N-channel power MOSFET designed for use in various power management applications. It features low on-resistance and high-speed switching capabilities, making it suitable for high-frequency isolated DC-DC converters and synchronous rectification applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:**
  - High-frequency isolated DC-DC converters for telecom and industrial applications.
  - High-frequency buck converters for computer processor power management.
  
- **Signal Processing:**
  - Used in circuits requiring efficient switching and low power loss.

- **General Purpose Switching:**
  - Ideal for applications where low on-resistance and high current handling are critical.

This component is particularly beneficial in designs that require efficient power conversion and management, contributing to improved overall system performance and reliability.
