### Component Summary: PME271Y510MR30

**Manufacturer:** KEMET Electronics  
**Product Code:** PME271Y510MR30  
**Type:** Film Capacitor (Metallized Impregnated Paper, Class Y2)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Rating:** 250 VAC (50/60 Hz)
- **Capacitance:** 0.01 µF (10 nF)
- **Capacitance Tolerance:** ±20%
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +100°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text.
- **Package Type:** Through-hole (lead spacing options available: 10.2 mm, 15.2 mm, 20.3 mm, 25.4 mm)
- **Power Consumption:** Not applicable for capacitors.
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent self-healing properties for long life under overvoltage conditions.
  - Good resistance to ionization due to impregnated dielectric.
  - High dv/dt capability.
  - 100% screening factory test at 3,000 VDC.
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free terminations.
  - Approved by ENEC, UL, cUL, and CQC.

#### Description:
The PME271Y510MR30 is a metallized impregnated paper capacitor designed for AC line electromagnetic interference (EMI) suppression. It is classified as a Class Y2 capacitor, which means it is suitable for use in applications where it is connected between the line and earth. The capacitor is encapsulated in a self-extinguishing resin that meets UL 94 V-0 standards, ensuring high safety against flammability.

#### Typical Applications:
- **EMI Suppression:** Used in applications to reduce electromagnetic interference in AC lines.
- **RC Networks:** Commonly utilized in resistor-capacitor (RC) networks for filtering and timing applications.
- **Power Supply Circuits:** Helps in stabilizing voltage and reducing noise in power supply circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Employed in various consumer electronic devices to enhance performance and reliability.

This capacitor is particularly suited for applications requiring high reliability and stability, especially in environments with continuous operation and potential overvoltage conditions.
