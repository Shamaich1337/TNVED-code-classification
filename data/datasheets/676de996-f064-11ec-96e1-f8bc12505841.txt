### Component Summary: NB3N5573DTR2G

**Description:**
The NB3N5573 is a precision, low phase noise clock generator designed to meet PCI Express and Ethernet requirements. It utilizes a 25 MHz fundamental mode parallel resonant crystal to generate differential HCSL outputs at selectable frequencies of 25 MHz, 100 MHz, 125 MHz, or 200 MHz. The device is housed in a 16-pin TSSOP package and does not require an external loop filter.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.3 V ± 10%
  - Maximum Supply Voltage (VDD): 4.6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (IDD): 120 mA (typical), 135 mA (maximum)
  - Power Supply Current when Output Enable (OE) is Low: 65 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Supply Current: 135 mA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - TSSOP-16 (5.0 mm x 4.4 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - HCSL differential output or LVDS with proper termination
  - Four selectable multipliers for output frequency
  - Tri-state output enable
  - PCIe Gen1, Gen2, Gen3, Gen4, QPI, UPI jitter compliant
  - Phase noise performance at 100 MHz: 
    - -109.4 dBc at 100 Hz
    - -127.8 dBc at 1 kHz
    - -136.2 dBc at 10 kHz
    - -138.8 dBc at 100 kHz
    - -138.2 dBc at 1 MHz
    - -161.4 dBc at 10 MHz
    - -163.00 dBc at 20 MHz
  - Typical period jitter RMS: 1.5 ps
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (indefinite time out of dry pack)

**Typical Applications:**
The NB3N5573 is primarily used in applications requiring precise clock generation, such as:
- **Data Communication:** It is suitable for PCI Express and Ethernet interfaces, providing reliable clock signals for data transfer.
- **Networking Equipment:** Used in routers, switches, and other networking devices where accurate timing is critical.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Can be utilized in devices that require synchronization of digital signals.
- **Industrial Applications:** Suitable for various industrial control systems that depend on precise timing for operations.

This component is ideal for engineers looking for a reliable clock generator that meets stringent performance requirements in high-speed data applications.
