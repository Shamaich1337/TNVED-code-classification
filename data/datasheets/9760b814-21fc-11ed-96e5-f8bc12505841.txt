  - Quiescent Current: 37.7 mA (typical)
  - Output Current: 200 mA (maximum)

  - Power-Down Capability: 0.65 mA
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Quad Flat No-lead (QFN), 16 pins (RGT)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Fully-differential architecture
  - Low noise: 2.2 nV/√Hz
  - High slew rate: 5100 V/μs
  - Minimum gain of 0 dB
  - Output common-mode control
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **THS4513** is a high-performance, wideband, fully-differential operational amplifier designed for data acquisition systems operating at 3.3V to 5V. It features low noise and low distortion characteristics, making it suitable for applications requiring high fidelity signal amplification. The device is optimized for low power consumption while maintaining high performance, making it ideal for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Data Acquisition Systems**: The THS4513 is commonly used in high-speed data converters, such as ADCs, where it amplifies and converts single-ended signals to differential outputs.
- **Wireless Communication**: Its low noise and high linearity make it suitable for RF applications.
- **Medical Imaging**: The amplifier's precision and low distortion are beneficial in imaging systems.
- **Test and Measurement Equipment**: It is used in various measurement devices where accurate signal processing is critical.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the THS4513RGTT, providing a clear understanding of its capabilities and use cases in electronic designs.
