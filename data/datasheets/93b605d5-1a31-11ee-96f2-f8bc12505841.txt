### Component Summary: LP2985A-25DBVR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 2.5 V to 16 V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): Fixed at 2.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 150 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current (I_Q): 71 µA (at I_LOAD = 0 mA), 750 µA (at I_LOAD = 150 mA)
  - Shutdown Current: 1.12 µA (typical)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 5-pin SOT-23 (DBV)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low noise: 30 µV RMS with a 10-nF bypass capacitor
  - Output current limiting and thermal protection
  - Stable with 2.2-µF ceramic capacitors
  - High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 70 dB at 1 kHz, 40 dB at 1 MHz
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **LP2985A-25DBVR** is a low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage of 2.5 V with a maximum output current of 150 mA. It operates with a wide input voltage range from 2.5 V to 16 V, making it suitable for various applications. The device features low quiescent current, making it ideal for battery-powered devices. It also includes built-in protection features such as thermal shutdown and current limiting, ensuring reliable operation under varying load conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
The LP2985A-25DBVR is commonly used in:
- **Portable Electronics:** Such as mobile phones, tablets, and other battery-operated devices where low power consumption is critical.
- **Microcontroller Power Supply:** Providing stable voltage to low-voltage microcontrollers and processors.
- **Communication Equipment:** Used in land mobile radios and active antenna systems.
- **Consumer Appliances:** Such as washers and dryers, where reliable voltage regulation is necessary.
- **Motor Drives and Control Boards:** Ensuring stable operation of control circuits in motor applications.

This component is particularly valued for its low noise and high PSRR, which help in minimizing the impact of upstream switching regulators and improving overall system performance.
