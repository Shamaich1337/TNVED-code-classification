### Component Summary: PBSS5240T,215

**Manufacturer:** NXP Semiconductors  
**Product Type:** PNP Low V CEsat (BISS) Transistor  
**Package Type:** SOT23 (Plastic surface-mounted package; 3 leads)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): -40 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): -40 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): -5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (DC, IC): -2 A
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): -3 A
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): -300 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 
    - 300 mW (Tamb ≤ 25 °C, standard footprint)
    - 480 mW (Tamb ≤ 25 °C, larger mounting pad)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to +150 °C
  - Operating Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Special Features:**
  - Low collector-emitter saturation voltage
  - High current capability
  - Improved device reliability due to reduced heat generation
  - Replacement for SOT89/SOT223 standard packaged transistor

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The PBSS5240T is a PNP transistor designed for low voltage and high current applications. It features a low collector-emitter saturation voltage, which enhances efficiency in switching applications. The device is housed in a compact SOT23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Supply Line Switching Circuits:** Used to control power distribution in electronic devices.
- **Battery Management Applications:** Helps in managing battery charging and discharging processes.
- **DC/DC Converter Applications:** Utilized in power conversion circuits to regulate voltage levels.
- **Strobe Flash Units:** Employed in camera flash systems for quick discharge of energy.
- **Heavy Duty Battery Powered Equipment:** Suitable for driving motors and lamps in battery-operated devices.

This transistor is ideal for applications requiring efficient power management and reliable performance in a compact form factor.
