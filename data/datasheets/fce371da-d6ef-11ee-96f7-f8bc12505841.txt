### Component Summary: MGS12i GPS Splitter

**Manufacturer:** Shenzhen Gems Navigation Electronics Co., Ltd.

**Description:**
The MGS12i is a GPS splitter designed to divide the received signal from an active GPS antenna into two outputs for GPS receiving equipment. It features intelligent power selection, ensuring that the output port with the higher voltage supplies power to the active GPS antenna while simulating a load on the other port.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - DC Input Voltage: 4 to 12 VDC
  - Output Voltage: Typically around 5 VDC

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Device Current Consumption: 18 to 20 mA (excluding antenna current)
  - Maximum Source DC Current: 250 mA
  - Open Circuit Threshold: 15 to 75 mA
  - Short Circuit Threshold: 100 to 180 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Device Current: 18 to 20 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically housed in a compact enclosure suitable for electronic devices.

- **Special Features:**
  - Gain options: 0 dB, 21 dB, and a passive version available.
  - High isolation levels (> 28 dB).
  - Automatic voltage comparison for power selection.
  - Supports multiple GNSS signals (GPS, Glonass, Galileo, Beidou, IRNSS).

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The MGS12i is primarily used in wireless infrastructure applications, particularly in GPS signal distribution. It is suitable for:
- GPS signal splitting for multiple receivers.
- Applications requiring high isolation between outputs.
- Systems that need to manage power supply intelligently for active GPS antennas.
- Navigation systems that utilize various satellite systems (GPS, Glonass, Galileo, etc.).

This component is essential in scenarios where multiple GPS devices need to share a single antenna signal without interference, ensuring reliable performance in navigation and positioning applications.
