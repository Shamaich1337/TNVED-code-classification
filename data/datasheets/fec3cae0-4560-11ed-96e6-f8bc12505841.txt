### Component Summary: BGA824N6 E6327

**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Product Type:** Low Noise Amplifier (LNA)  
**Primary Function:** Amplification of signals for Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS) including GPS, GLONASS, Beidou, and Galileo.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 1.5 V to 3.6 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 3.8 mA (typical) in ON mode; 0.2 µA in OFF mode
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 60 mW (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Ambient Temperature (TA): -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - TSNP-6-2 and TSNP-6-10 (footprint: 0.7 x 1.1 mm²)
- **Special Features:**
  - Insertion Power Gain: 17.0 dB
  - Low Noise Figure: 0.55 dB
  - Digital on/off switch (1V logic level high)
  - RF output internally matched to 50 Ohm
  - 2kV HBM ESD protection
  - Pb-free (RoHS compliant)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BGA824N6 is a silicon germanium low noise amplifier designed specifically for GNSS applications. It operates within the frequency range of 1164 MHz to 1615 MHz, providing significant amplification while maintaining a low noise figure, which is critical for enhancing the quality of received signals in satellite navigation systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS):** Used in devices that require accurate positioning and timing information, such as GPS receivers, automotive navigation systems, and mobile devices.
- **Signal Processing:** Enhancing weak signals from satellites to improve the overall performance of GNSS receivers.
- **Telecommunications:** Can be utilized in RF front-end applications where low noise amplification is essential.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high sensitivity and low power consumption, making it suitable for portable and battery-operated devices.
