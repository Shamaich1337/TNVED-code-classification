**Description:**
The E2415S-2WR3 is a 2W isolated DC-DC converter from the MORNSUN E_S-2WR3 series. It is designed to convert a fixed input voltage of 24V into a dual output of ±15V. This component is characterized by its high efficiency, compact size, and robust electrical isolation, making it suitable for various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The E2415S-2WR3 is particularly suitable for use in distributed power supply systems. It is ideal for applications that require:
- Pure digital circuits
- Low-frequency analog circuits
- Relay-driven circuits
- Data switching circuits

This converter is especially useful in scenarios where the input voltage is relatively stable (with a variation of ±10% or less), and where high isolation voltage and moderate output regulation requirements are present. Its features make it a reliable choice for powering sensitive electronic devices in industrial and commercial environments.
