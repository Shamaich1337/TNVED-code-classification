**Manufacturer:** M/A-COM Technology Solutions Inc.  
**Product Type:** Surface Mount Low Barrier Schottky Diode  
**Series:** MA4E2054 Series  
**Package Type:** SOT-23 (287)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 3 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (V_B): 3.0 V (min) at I_R = 10 µA

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (I_F): 20 mA
  - Reverse Leakage Current (I_R): 
    - 100 nA max at 1 V
    - 500 nA max at 3 V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF Incident Power (CW): 75 mW (derate to 0 W at 125°C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -65°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +125°C

- **Special Features:**
  - Low reverse leakage current (<100 nA @ 1 V, <500 nA @ 3 V)
  - High detector sensitivity: -55 dBm TSS
  - Low capacitance: 0.30 pF
  - Low 1/F noise
  - Available in single, series pair, and unconnected pair configurations
  - Lead-free and RoHS compliant
  - Tape and reel packaging for high-volume applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The MA4E2054B1-287T is a low barrier N-type silicon Schottky diode designed for high-performance applications in RF signal processing. It is particularly suited for use as a mixer and detector diode, providing excellent sensitivity and low noise characteristics. The diode is available in a compact SOT-23 package, making it ideal for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Mixers and Detectors:** The MA4E2054B1 is optimized for use in balanced mixer and detector circuits, making it suitable for RF applications such as VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal) and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite) systems.
- **RF Tag Applications:** Its small size and low cost make it attractive for RF tag applications, including identification and toll collection systems.
- **Voltage Doublers:** The series pair configuration allows for use in voltage doubler circuits, enhancing its versatility in various electronic designs.

This diode is an excellent choice for engineers looking for reliable performance in RF applications, particularly where low noise and high sensitivity are critical.
