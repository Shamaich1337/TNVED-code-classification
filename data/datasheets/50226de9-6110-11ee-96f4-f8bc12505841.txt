### Component Summary: DEUTSCH DT06-2S

**Component Type:**  
Housing for Female Terminals

**Description:**  
The DEUTSCH DT06-2S is a wire-to-wire connector housing designed for female terminals. It features a 2-position configuration with a centerline (pitch) of 5.46 mm (0.215 in). The housing is sealable and is primarily used for power and signal applications in various industries, particularly automotive.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 250 VAC
  - Nominal Voltage Architecture: 12 V, 24 V, 48 V

- **Current Ratings:**

  Not specified in the provided data.

- **Primary Product Color:** Gray
