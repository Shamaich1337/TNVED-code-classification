### Component Summary: Mitutoyo M Plan APO 5X (Item Number: 378-802-6)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Model**: M Plan Apo 5X
- **Working Distance**: 34 mm (in air)
- **Corrected Wavelength**: 436-656 nm
- **Numerical Aperture (N.A.)**: 0.14
- **Focal Length (f)**: 40 mm
- **Parfocal Distance (P.D.)**: 95 mm
- **Resolving Power (R)**: 2 µm
- **Field of View (FOV)**:
  - FOV 1: Ø4.8 mm (when using Ø24 mm eyepiece)
  - FOV 2: 0.96 x 1.28 mm (when using a digital camera with 1/2" chip size)
- **Mass**: 230 g
- **Special Features**:
  - Long working distances
  - Infinity corrected optics
  - High-quality plan apochromat design
  - Flat image surface over the entire field of view
- **Compatibility**: Designed for use with microscope types VMU, FS-70, MF-U, and Hyper MF-U.
- **Notes**: To avoid optical flare when using coaxial illumination, it is recommended to use 1x objectives together with a polarization unit on the microscope and an attached 1/4 wavelength plate.

#### Description:
The Mitutoyo M Plan APO 5X is a high-quality optical microscope objective designed for bright field observation. It features a plan apochromat design, which ensures that images are flat and free from chromatic aberrations across the entire field of view. This objective is particularly suited for applications requiring high-resolution imaging and long working distances.

The M Plan APO 5X is commonly used in various microscopy applications, including:
- **Biological Research**: For observing biological specimens with clarity and detail.
- **Material Science**: To analyze materials and their properties at a microscopic level.
- **Quality Control**: In manufacturing processes where precise measurements and inspections are necessary.
- **Education**: In academic settings for teaching microscopy techniques and applications.
- **Industrial Inspection**: For examining components and assemblies in quality assurance processes.
