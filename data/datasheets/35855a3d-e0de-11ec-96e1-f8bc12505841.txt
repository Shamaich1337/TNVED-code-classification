### Component Summary: GVA-84+ (Mini-Circuits)

**Description:**
The GVA-84+ is a monolithic wideband amplifier designed for high dynamic range applications. It operates at a fixed voltage of 5V and is housed in a compact SOT-89 surface mount package. The amplifier utilizes InGaP HBT technology and features a patented Transient Protected Darlington configuration, ensuring robust performance and excellent ESD protection.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 4.8V to 5.2V (typical 5V)
  - DC Voltage on Pin 3: 5.8V (maximum)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current: 85 mA (typical), 160 mA (maximum)
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation: 1W
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature: -45°C to 85°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to 150°C
  
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89 (DF782)
  
- **Special Features:**
  - High Gain: 24 dB typical at 100 MHz
  - High Output Power (P1dB): 20.5 dBm typical at 100 MHz
  - High IP3: 37 dBm typical at 100 MHz
  - Unconditionally stable
  - Excellent ESD Protection (Class 1C, HBM)
  - Transient protected (US patent 6,943,629)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 in accordance with IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020D

**Typical Applications:**
The GVA-84+ is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Base station infrastructure
- Portable wireless communication systems
- CATV (Cable Television) and DBS (Direct Broadcast Satellite)
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) and Wireless LAN
- LTE (Long-Term Evolution) networks

This amplifier is ideal for use in environments where high gain and dynamic range are critical, making it a versatile choice for modern communication systems.
