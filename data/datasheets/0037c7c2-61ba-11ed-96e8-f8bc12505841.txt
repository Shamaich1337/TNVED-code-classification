### Component Summary for W3230 - Ceramic Patch Antenna

**Product Code:** W3230  
**Manufacturer:** Pulse (Suzhou) Wireless Products Co., Ltd.  
**Series:** Ceramic Patch Antenna  
**Issue:** 1838  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Frequency Range:** 2400 - 2500 MHz
- **Nominal Impedance:** 50 Ω
- **Return Loss:** -4 dB
- **Axial Ratio (Typical):** 3 dB
- **Peak Gain:** +6 dBic
- **Efficiency:** 60%
- **Polarization:** Right-Hand Circular Polarization (RHCP)
- **Power Withstanding:** 5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 to +105 °C
- **Package Type:** 18 x 18 x 4 mm
- **Weight:** 4.87 g
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not applicable for non-reflow part
- **RoHS Compliant:** Yes

#### Description:
The W3230 is a ceramic patch antenna designed for operation in the 2.4 to 2.5 GHz frequency range, commonly used in wireless communication applications. It features a compact size of 18 x 18 x 4 mm and is characterized by its right-hand circular polarization, making it suitable for various wireless protocols.

#### Typical Applications:
The W3230 antenna is ideal for applications that require high-gain directional antenna patterns, including:
- **WiFi Communication:** Enhancing signal strength and coverage in wireless networks.
- **Bluetooth Devices:** Improving connectivity and range for Bluetooth-enabled devices.
- **ISM Band Radios:** Utilized in industrial, scientific, and medical applications that operate within the ISM band.
- **Wireless Sensor Networks:** Providing reliable communication for sensor nodes in various environments.

This antenna is particularly beneficial in scenarios where space is limited, and high performance is required, such as in portable devices and embedded systems.
