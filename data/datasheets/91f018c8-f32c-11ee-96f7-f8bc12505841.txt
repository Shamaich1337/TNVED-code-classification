### KEMET Electronics T520A476M006ATE150 - Technical Summary

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Rated Voltage: 6.3 VDC
  - Surge Voltage: 8.2 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Allowable Ripple Current: 1,600 mA at +45°C
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - DC Leakage: 29.61 µA at 25°C
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55°C to 105°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Case Size: A/3216-18
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
  - No aging effects
  - High ripple current capability
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL3 per IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020

#### Description:
The KEMET Organic Capacitor (KO-CAP) T520A476M006ATE150 is a solid electrolytic capacitor featuring a conductive polymer cathode. This design allows it to deliver very low ESR and improved capacitance retention at high frequencies. The KO-CAP combines the advantages of multilayer ceramic capacitors, aluminum electrolytic capacitors, and tantalum capacitors into a single surface mount package. It is particularly noted for its long operational life and high ripple current capabilities, making it suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The T520A476M006ATE150 is commonly used in:
- **DC/DC Converters**: For efficient power management in various electronic devices.
- **Audio/Sound Circuits**: Such as in mobile phones, base stations, smartphones, and MP3 players.
- **Power Supply Inputs**: In portable electronics like notebook PCs, displays, SSDs, HDDs, and USBs.
- **Telecommunications**: Including high-speed servers and consumer electronics.
- **Microprocessor Decoupling**: To stabilize voltage and reduce noise in densely populated circuits.
- **High Voltage Applications**: Suitable for power input rails ranging from 12 V to 48 V.

This capacitor is ideal for applications requiring high reliability and performance in compact spaces, particularly where high ripple currents are present.
