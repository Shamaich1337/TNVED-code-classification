### Component Summary: MURS360T3G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 600 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 600 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage: 600 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 3.0 A at T_L = 130 °C
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 3.0 A, T_J = 25 °C: 0.875 V
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 3.0 A, T_J = 150 °C: 0.711 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +175 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (Surface Mount Component), 2-lead case 403AC

- **Special Features:**
  - High temperature glass passivated junction
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The MURS360T3G is an ultrafast power rectifier diode designed for high voltage and high frequency applications. It features a compact surface mount package that is ideal for automated handling and is suitable for applications where space and weight are critical. The diode is constructed with state-of-the-art epitaxial technology, providing excellent performance in rectification tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
The MURS360T3G is commonly used in:
- **Power Management:** As a rectifier in power supply circuits to convert AC to DC.
- **Signal Processing:** In high-frequency applications where fast switching is required.
- **Free Wheeling Diodes:** To protect circuits from voltage spikes in inductive loads.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, it is suitable for use in automotive electronics where reliability and performance are crucial.

This component is particularly valuable in designs that require efficient power conversion and protection in compact electronic systems.
