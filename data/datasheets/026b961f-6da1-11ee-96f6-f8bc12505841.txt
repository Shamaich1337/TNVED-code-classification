The first byte of communication used to wakeup the device will be lost/ignored.\nWhen in USB Communication mode, Sleep mode is\nactivated by a USB SUSPEND control transfer from the\nhost – the SleepDelay timer has no effect. Optionally, ifthe host is configured to allow the device to awaken the\nhost (USB REMOTE WAKE-UP), the host will preface\nthe USB SUSPEND with a control transfer to enableremote wake-up from the device. ### Component Summary: AR1100-I/SO

**Description:**
The AR1100 is a Resistive USB and RS-232 Touch Screen Controller produced by Microchip Technology. It is designed to provide a fully integrated solution for resistive touch screens, supporting various communication protocols (USB and RS-232) and touch sensor configurations (4-wire, 5-wire, and 8-wire). The controller is optimized for low power consumption and features built-in algorithms for drift compensation, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: 3.3V to 5.0V (±5%)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Standby Current: 
    - <10 µA (UART)
    - <325 µA (USB)
  - Operating Current: 
    - <17 mA (no touch)
    - <25 mA (touch)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power-saving Sleep mode available.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C (Industrial Temperature Range)

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 20-Lead SOIC, 20-Lead SSOP, and 20-Lead QFN (5 x 5 mm).

- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS Compliant
  - Built-in Drift Compensation Algorithm
  - 96 Bytes of User EEPROM
  - Automatic detection and selection of communication mode (USB or UART)
  - Supports multiple touch modes (Off, Stream, Down, Up)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

### Typical Applications:
The AR1100 is suitable for a variety of applications that require touch input, including:
- Mobile communication devices
- Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs)
- Global Positioning Systems (GPS)
- Touch Screen Monitors
- Kiosks
- Media Players
- Portable Instruments
- Point of Sale (POS) Terminals

### Conclusion:
The AR1100-I/SO is a versatile touch screen controller that combines ease of integration with robust performance features, making it an ideal choice for applications requiring reliable touch input across various environments. Its low power consumption and support for multiple communication protocols enhance its usability in both consumer and industrial applications.
