#### Key Specifications:
- **Core Material:** PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate)
- **Clad Material:** Fluorinated Polymer
- **Fiber Diameter:** 1000 ± 60 µm
- **Numerical Aperture (NA):** 0.5

- **Jacket Diameter:** 2200 ± 70 µm
- **Jacket Color:** Black

- **Elongation at Break Point:** ≥ 90%
- **Minimum Bending Radius:** 30 mm

- **RoHS Certification:** The product does not contain hazardous substances as per RoHS 2 regulations.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

### Description:
The TC-1000 (TC-0310N-E-01) is a **Plastic Optical Fiber** designed for light transmission applications. It features a core made of PMMA and a fluorinated polymer cladding, providing a flexible and durable medium for transmitting light signals. The fiber is characterized by its relatively large diameter, which allows for easier handling and installation.

### Typical Applications:
The TC-1000 is typically used in applications that require light transmission without the constraints of traditional glass fibers. Common applications include:
- **Data Communication:** Used in short-distance data transmission systems.
- **Lighting:** Suitable for decorative lighting and illumination systems.
- **Sensor Systems:** Employed in various sensor applications where light transmission is critical.
- **Industrial Applications:** Utilized in environments where flexibility and resistance to harsh conditions are necessary.

### Precautions:
The TC-1000 should not be used in applications that involve direct contact with the human body or food. It is also important to avoid exposure to heavy loads, chemicals, and extreme temperatures to maintain its optical properties and structural integrity. Proper installation and environmental controls are essential to ensure optimal performance and longevity of the fiber.
