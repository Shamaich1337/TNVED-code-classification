### Component Summary: BC856B.215 (Nexperia)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): -80 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): -65 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): -5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Collector Current (IC): 100 mA (DC)
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 200 mA
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 250 mW (at ambient temperature ≤ 25 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to +150 °C
  - Operating Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (plastic surface-mounted package; 3 leads)

- **Special Features:**
  - PNP general-purpose transistor suitable for low current and low voltage applications.
  - Complementary NPN transistors: BC846, BC847, and BC848.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SOT23 packages are classified under MSL 1 or 2.

#### Description:
The BC856B is a PNP general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is housed in a compact SOT23 package, making it suitable for surface-mount technology (SMT) applications. The transistor is characterized by its low current handling capability (up to 100 mA) and low voltage operation (up to 65 V), making it ideal for various electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Purpose Switching:** The BC856B can be used in various switching applications where low current and low voltage are required.
- **Amplification:** It is suitable for use in audio and signal amplification circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in consumer electronic devices for signal processing and control functions.
- **Automotive and Industrial Applications:** Due to its robust temperature range, it can be utilized in automotive and industrial environments.

This transistor is a versatile component that can be integrated into a wide range of electronic designs, providing reliable performance in both consumer and industrial applications.
