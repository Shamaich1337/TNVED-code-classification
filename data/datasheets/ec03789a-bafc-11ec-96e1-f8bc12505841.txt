### Component Summary: YJD112010DG1

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Type:** Silicon Carbide Schottky Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (Repetitive Peak): 1200 V
  - Reverse Voltage (Surge Peak): 1200 V
  - Reverse Voltage (DC): 1200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current at 25°C: 33 A
  - Continuous Forward Current at 135°C: 16 A
  - Continuous Forward Current at 157°C: 10 A
  - Non-repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (10 ms): 105 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation at 25°C: 211 W
  - Power Dissipation at 110°C: 91 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature: -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-252

- **Special Features:**
  - Positive temperature coefficient
  - Temperature-independent switching
  - Zero reverse recovery current
  - Essentially no switching losses
  - High-frequency operation
  - Reduction of EMI (Electromagnetic Interference)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Compliant with JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The YJD112010DG1 is a Silicon Carbide (SiC) Schottky diode designed for high-efficiency power applications. Schottky diodes are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed operation and minimal power loss.

#### Typical Applications:
The YJD112010DG1 is commonly used in:
- Power Factor Correction (PFC) circuits
- Solar inverters
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Motor drives
- Photovoltaic inverters
- Electric vehicles and chargers

This diode's high voltage and current ratings, along with its thermal stability, make it suitable for demanding applications in power electronics, where efficiency and reliability are critical.
