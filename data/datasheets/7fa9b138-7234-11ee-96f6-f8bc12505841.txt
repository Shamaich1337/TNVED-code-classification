### Component Summary: LS48-A3S-TI-N-B5

**Description:**
The LS48-A3S-TI-N-B5 is a single-mode bi-directional SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) transceiver designed for optical communication. It operates at wavelengths of 1550 nm for transmission and 1310 nm for reception, utilizing a simplex LC connector. This component is compliant with various standards including SONET/SDH, Fast Ethernet, and ITU-T G.985, making it suitable for high-speed data transmission applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): 3.1 V to 3.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 V to 4.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Operating Current (IOP): Up to 400 mA
  - Supply Current (ITX + IRX): Up to 200 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical power consumption is approximately 1.32 W (3.3 V at 400 mA).

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C (for LS48-A3S-TI-N-B5)
  - 0°C to 70°C (for LS48-A3S-TC-N-B5)

- **Package Type:**
  - Industry standard SFP package with a simplex LC connector.

- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant
  - Hot pluggable
  - Class 1 laser product compliant with EN 60825-1
  - TTL signal detect indicator
  - Differential LVPECL inputs and outputs

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SFPs are classified under MSL 1 or MSL 2.

**Typical Applications:**
The LS48-A3S-TI-N-B5 is primarily used in telecommunications and data communication systems. Typical applications include:
- ATM/SONET OC-3/SDH STM-1 networks
- Fast Ethernet (100Base-BX10-D)
- ITU-T G.985 compliant systems
- Long-distance data transmission over single-mode fiber, with a maximum distance of up to 20 km.

This transceiver is ideal for network equipment such as switches, routers, and other devices requiring high-speed data transfer over optical fibers. Its bi-directional capability allows for efficient use of a single fiber for both transmitting and receiving data, making it a cost-effective solution for modern communication networks.
