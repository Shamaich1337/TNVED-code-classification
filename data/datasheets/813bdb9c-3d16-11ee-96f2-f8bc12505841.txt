### Component Summary: PTH08080WAH (Texas Instruments)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 4.5 V to 18 V
  - Output Voltage Adjust Range: 0.9 V to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 2.25 A at 85°C
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Output Power: 10 W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Through-Hole Module (EUF)
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency (up to 93%)
  - On/Off Inhibit control
  - Undervoltage Lockout (UVLO)
  - Output Overcurrent Protection (Non-latching, Auto-Reset)
  - Overtemperature Protection
  - Safety Agency Approvals: UL/CUL 60950, EN60950
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (235°C for unlimited time)

#### Description:
The PTH08080W is a highly integrated, low-cost switching regulator module designed to deliver up to 2.25 A of output current. It operates efficiently across a wide input voltage range of 4.5 V to 18 V and can step down to output voltages as low as 0.9 V. The module's compact size (0.5 × 0.6 inches) and high efficiency eliminate the need for a heat sink, making it suitable for various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Telecommunications:** Used in devices requiring stable power supply under varying loads.
- **Instrumentation:** Ideal for test and measurement equipment that demands precise voltage regulation.
- **General-Purpose Circuits:** Suitable for consumer electronics and other applications where adjustable voltage is needed.
- **Power Management:** Effective in systems where efficient power conversion is critical, such as battery-operated devices.

This component is particularly valuable in applications where space is limited and efficiency is paramount, providing a reliable solution for power management in modern electronic systems.
