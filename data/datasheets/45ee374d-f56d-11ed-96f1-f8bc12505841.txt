### Component Summary: BAR90-02EL

**Product Code:** BAR90-02EL  
**Manufacturer:** Infineon Technologies  
**Type:** Single Silicon RF PIN Diode

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): Up to 80 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): Up to 100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 250 mW (at TS ≤ 133 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -55 °C to +125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - TSLP-2-19 (1 mm x 0.6 mm x 0.31 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - Very low capacitance (C = 0.23 pF at VR = 0 V, f = 1 GHz)
  - Low forward resistance (RF = 1.6 Ω at IF = 3 mA, f = 100 MHz)
  - Balanced ON/OFF harmonic distortion
  - Pb-free, RoHS compliant, and halogen-free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - Qualified for industrial applications according to JEDEC47/20/22

#### Description:
The BAR90-02EL is a single silicon RF PIN diode designed for high-frequency applications. It features high-voltage handling capabilities while maintaining low loss and distortion levels. The diode's low forward resistance, capacitance, and inductance make it suitable for compact designs, enabling the creation of smaller and lighter electronic devices.

#### Typical Applications:
The BAR90-02EL is optimized for low bias current RF and high-speed interface switching applications. Typical uses include:
- Set-top boxes
- Digital media players
- Laptops and desktop PCs

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring efficient signal processing and switching, making it ideal for modern communication and multimedia devices.
