### Component Summary: ESD5311X-2/TR

**Manufacturer:** Will Semiconductor Ltd.  
**Product Code:** ESD5311X-2/TR  
**Package Type:** WBFBP-02C-C

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Maximum Working Voltage (VRWM): 5.0 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 7.5 V (min) to 10.0 V (max) at 1 mA

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 4 A (8/20 μs)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): < 1 nA (typical at VRWM = 5V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (Ppk): 72 W (tp = 8/20 μs)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): 125 °C
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultra-low capacitance: CJ = 0.25 pF (typical)
  - Low clamping voltage: VCL = 22 V (typical at IPP = 16 A)
  - Solid-state silicon technology
  - ESD protection up to ±20 kV (contact discharge) according to IEC61000-4-2
  - Ultra-low leakage current: IR < 1 nA (typical)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The ESD5311X is a 1-line, bi-directional, ultra-low capacitance Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) designed to protect high-speed data interfaces from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and other transient voltage events. It features a combination of ultra-low capacitance steering diodes and a TVS diode, making it suitable for safeguarding sensitive electronic components connected to data and transmission lines.

#### Typical Applications:
The ESD5311X is commonly used in various high-speed data applications, including:
- USB 2.0 and USB 3.0 interfaces
- HDMI 1.3 and HDMI 1.4 connections
- SATA and eSATA interfaces
- DVI connections
- IEEE 1394 (FireWire) interfaces
- PCI Express slots
- Portable electronics and notebooks

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust ESD protection while maintaining signal integrity, making it essential in modern electronic devices that rely on high-speed data transmission.
