### Component Summary: TXB0104QRGYRQ1

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - VCCA (A-port supply voltage): 1.2V to 3.6V
  - VCCB (B-port supply voltage): 1.65V to 5.5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous output current: ±50 mA
  - Continuous current through VCCA, VCCB, or GND: ±100 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Ioff (when powered down): ±1 μA (max)
  - ICCA and ICCB (active current): 3.4 μA (typ) at VCCA = 1.2V and VCCB = 1.65V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - VQFN (14 pins), dimensions: 3.50 mm x 3.50 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Bi-directional voltage-level translation
  - Automatic direction sensing
  - ±15 kV ESD protection (Human Body Model)
  - OE (output enable) input for high-impedance state
  - Supports partial-power-down mode operation

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, 260°C, 1 year

#### Description:
The **TXB0104** is a 4-bit bi-directional voltage-level translator designed to facilitate communication between devices operating at different voltage levels. It features two separate power supply rails (VCCA and VCCB) that allow for seamless translation between low-voltage and higher-voltage logic levels. The device is particularly useful in applications where multiple voltage domains need to interact without damaging sensitive components.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Applications:** 
  - Used in automotive infotainment systems and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).
  
- **Telematics:** 
  - Facilitates communication between various electronic control units (ECUs) in vehicles.

- **General Level Translation:**
  - Ideal for interfacing devices with different logic levels, such as connecting 1.8V microcontrollers to 5V peripherals.

- **Signal Processing:**
  - Can be used in applications requiring signal integrity across different voltage levels.

The TXB0104 is particularly suited for applications that require robust ESD protection and reliable operation across a wide temperature range, making it a versatile choice for automotive and industrial designs.
