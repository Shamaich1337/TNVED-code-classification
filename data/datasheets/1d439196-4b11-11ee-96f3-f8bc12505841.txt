### Component Summary: FTR-LYCA024Y

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Coil Rated Voltage: 24 VDC
  - Max Switching Voltage: 250 VAC
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Contact Rating: 6 A (resistive)
  - Max Carrying Current: 6 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Rated Power: 170 to 217 mW
  - Operate Power: 74 to 95 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +85 °C (no frost)
- **Package Type:**
  - Slim type relay (15.0 mm x 5.0 mm x 28.0 mm for straight type)
- **Special Features:**
  - High insulation with an insulation distance of 8 mm (creepage/clearance)
  - Dielectric strength: 4,000 VAC (contacts to coil)
  - Surge strength: 6,000 V
  - Plastic sealed type (RTIII)
  - RoHS compliant
  - UL, CSA, VDE compliance
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not applicable to electromechanical relays.

#### Description:
The FTR-LYCA024Y is a power relay from Fujitsu's FTR-K1 series, designed for medium load control applications. It features a single pole double throw (1 form C) configuration, allowing it to switch between two circuits. The relay is compact, making it suitable for applications where space is limited. It is constructed with high insulation properties, ensuring reliable operation in various environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The FTR-LYCA024Y relay is typically used in:
- **Power Management:** Controlling power to devices in industrial and consumer applications.
- **Signal Processing:** Switching signals in communication devices.
- **Automation Systems:** Used in control panels and automation equipment for switching operations.
- **Home Appliances:** Implemented in devices requiring reliable switching capabilities, such as HVAC systems and lighting controls.

This relay is ideal for applications that require a robust and compact switching solution, ensuring high performance and reliability in various operational conditions.
