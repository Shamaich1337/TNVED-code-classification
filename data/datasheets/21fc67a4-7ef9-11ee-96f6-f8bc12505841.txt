### Component Summary: EGF1T-E3/67A (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 1300 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 910 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 1300 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Average Forward Rectified Current (IF(AV)): 1.0 A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 20 A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF at IF = 1.0 A): 3.0 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature Range (TSTG): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Package: GF1 (DO-214BA), molded plastic over glass body

- **Special Features:**
  - Ultrafast reverse recovery time (trr): 75 ns
  - Low switching losses and high efficiency
  - Avalanche surge energy capability (EAS): 15 mJ
  - AEC-Q101 qualified
  - Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL): Level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 250 °C

#### Description:
The EGF1T-E3/67A is a surface-mount glass passivated ultrafast rectifier diode. It is designed to provide high reliability and efficiency in rectification applications, particularly in high voltage environments. The diode features a superectifier structure that enhances its performance and reliability, making it suitable for automated placement in manufacturing processes.

#### Typical Applications:
The EGF1T is primarily used in high voltage rectification applications, such as:
- Photoflash circuits
- Power management systems
- General-purpose rectification in various electronic devices

This component is ideal for applications requiring fast switching and low power losses, contributing to overall system efficiency and reliability.
