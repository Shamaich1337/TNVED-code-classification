MBR shadowing provides a \nmechanism to boot into a secure pre -boot authentication environm ent to handle device unlocking.  \n⚫ Repurpose & End -of-Life: \nErasure of data within one or more.  \n⚫ Physical Presence SID (PSID):  \nPSID is defined by TCG OPAL as a 32 -character string and the purpose is to revert SSD back to its \nmanufacturing setting when the drive is still OPAL -activated.
