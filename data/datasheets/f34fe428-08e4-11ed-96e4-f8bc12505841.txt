### Component Summary: BAS321 General Purpose Diode

**Manufacturer:** Nexperia  
**Product Code:** BAS321  
**Type:** General Purpose Diode  
**Package Type:** SOD323 (SC-76) plastic surface-mounted package

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Continuous Reverse Voltage (VR): 200 V
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 250 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 250 mA
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 625 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFSM): 
    - 1.7 A (10 ms)
    - 9 A (1 µs)
    - 3 A (100 µs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 300 mW at 25 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C
- **Special Features:**
  - Fast switching speed: max. 50 ns
  - Small plastic SMD package
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The BAS321 is a general-purpose diode designed for various switching applications. It is fabricated using planar technology and is housed in a compact SOD323 package, making it suitable for surface-mounted circuit designs. The diode is characterized by its ability to handle significant reverse and forward voltages, making it versatile for different electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- General-purpose switching in surface-mounted circuits
- Power management systems
- Signal rectification in low-power applications
- Protection circuits in electronic devices

This diode is ideal for use in consumer electronics, automotive applications (non-automotive qualified), and other electronic devices where space-saving and efficient performance are critical.
