### Component Summary: TS3USB221DRCR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.3V to 3.6V
  - I/O Voltage: Up to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - ON-state switch current: ±120 mA
  - Continuous current through VCC or GND: ±100 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Power Mode: 1 μA (when OE is disabled)
  - Maximum Supply Current: 30 μA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - VSON (10 pins, 3.00 mm x 3.00 mm)
  - UQFN (10 pins, 1.50 mm x 2.00 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - High-speed USB 2.0 (480 Mbps) compatibility
  - Low ON-state resistance (rON = 6Ω max)
  - ESD protection >2000V (HBM)
  - High bandwidth (1.1 GHz typical)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The TS3USB221 is a high-speed USB 2.0 (480 Mbps) 1:2 multiplexer-demultiplexer switch designed for switching high-speed USB signals in portable and consumer applications, such as cell phones, digital cameras, and notebooks. It allows for the multiplexing of differential outputs from a USB host device to one of two corresponding outputs, ensuring minimal signal distortion and attenuation. The device is bidirectional and features low bit-to-bit skew and high channel-to-channel noise isolation.

#### Typical Applications:
- **USB Signal Routing:** The TS3USB221 is primarily used to route signals for USB 1.0, 1.1, and 2.0 applications, effectively expanding the limited USB I/O capabilities of hubs or controllers.
- **Mobile Industry Processor Interface (MIPI):** It can be utilized for MIPI signal routing in mobile devices.
- **MHL (Mobile High-Definition Link):** The switch can also be applied in MHL applications, facilitating high-definition video and audio transmission.

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal management in environments with limited space and power, making it suitable for modern portable electronics.
