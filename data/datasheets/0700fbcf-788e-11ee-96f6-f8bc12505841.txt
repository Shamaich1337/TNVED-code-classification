#### Description:
The **ER34615S** is a **Lithium Thionyl Chloride battery**, designed for high-temperature applications. It is a primary (non-rechargeable) battery known for its long shelf life and low self-discharge rate, making it ideal for devices that require reliable power over extended periods without frequent replacement.

#### Typical Applications:
The ER34615S battery is commonly used in:
- **Physical detection tools**: Such as sensors and monitoring devices.
- **Data acquisition instruments**: For collecting and storing data in various applications.
- **Electronic testing instruments**: Providing power for testing and measurement equipment.
- **Aerospace equipment**: Where reliability and performance in extreme conditions are critical.

This battery is particularly advantageous in applications where low current draw is prevalent, and long-term reliability is essential.
