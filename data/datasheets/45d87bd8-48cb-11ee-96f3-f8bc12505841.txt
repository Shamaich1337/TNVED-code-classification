Description\n8.1 Overview\nThe TL77xxA family ofintegrated-circuit supply-voltage supervisors isdesigned specifically foruse asreset\ncontrollers inmicrocomputer andmicroprocessor systems. The supply-voltage supervisor monitors thesupply for\nundervoltage conditions attheSENSE input. During power up,theRESET output becomes active (low) when\nVCCattains avalue approaching 3.6V.Atthis point (assuming that SENSE isabove VIT+),thedelay timer\nfunction activates atime delay, after which outputs RESET andRESET goinactive (high andlow, respectively).\nWhen anundervoltage condition occurs during normal operation, RESET andRESET goactive. Toensure thata\ncomplete reset occurs, thereset outputs remain active foratime delay after thevoltage attheSENSE input\nexceeds thepositive-going threshold value.\nDuring power down and when SENSE isbelow VIT−,theoutputs remain active until VCCfalls below 2V.After\nthis, theoutputs areundefined. Anexternal capacitor (typically 0.1μF)must beconnected toREF toreduce the\ninfluence offasttransients inthesupply voltage.\nFive versions ofthiscircuit areavailable:\n•TL7705A (Vt=4.55 V):Application inTTL-systems andmicrocomputer systems which require a5voltsupply\n(forexample, TMS7000)\n•TL7709A (Vt=7.6V):Application inmicrocomputer systems using theTMS1XXXNLL\n•TL7712A (Vt=10.8 V):Application inCMOS, microprocessor, andmemory circuits with a12voltsupply.\n•TL7715A (Vt=13.5 V):Application incircuits which operate with asupply voltage of15V,asisfound often in\nanalog circuits.\n•TL7702A (Vt=2.5V):Application insystems where other supply voltages areused. ### Component Summary: TL7702AID

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 3.5V to 18V (absolute maximum 20V)
  - Input Voltage (RESIN): -0.3V to 20V
  - Input Voltage (SENSE): TL7702A: -0.3V to 6V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - High-level output current (RESET): -16 mA
  - Low-level output current (RESET): 16 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current (ICC): 1.8 mA to 3 mA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Commercial: 0°C to 70°C
  - Industrial: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOIC (8 pins) and PDIP (8 pins)

- **Special Features:**
  - Power-On Reset Generator
  - Automatic Reset Generation After Voltage Drop
  - Precision Voltage Sensor
  - Temperature-Compensated Voltage Reference
  - Externally Adjustable Pulse Duration

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The TL7702A is an integrated circuit supply-voltage supervisor designed to function as a reset controller in microcomputer and microprocessor systems. It monitors the supply voltage for undervoltage conditions at the SENSE input. During power-up, the RESET output becomes active (low) when the supply voltage (VCC) reaches approximately 3.6V. The device includes a delay timer that ensures the reset outputs remain active for a specified duration after the voltage exceeds the threshold, ensuring proper initialization of the system.

**Typical Applications:**
The TL7702A is commonly used in various electronic devices, including:
- Computers
- Tablets
- Smartphones
- Servers
- Music players

It is particularly useful in applications requiring reliable power management and reset functionality, ensuring that microcontrollers and processors operate correctly during power fluctuations or startup sequences. The adjustable trigger level allows it to be tailored for different supply voltages, making it versatile for various designs.
