These are the so-called \n“valve”metals and include tita nium, zirconium, niobium, \ntantalum, hafnium, and aluminum. They are separated by an insulating material or dielectric. The dielectric used in all tantalum electrolytic capacitors is tantalum pentoxide.Tantalum pentoxide compound possesses high-dielectric strength and a high-dielectric constant. - **Special Features**: 
  - Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
  - 100% Surge Current Tested
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
  - Compatible with high-volume automatic pick and place equipment
  - Compliant with EIA-535-BAAC mechanical and performance requirements

#### Description:
The **293D476X9025D2TE3** is a solid tantalum surface mount chip capacitor from Vishay's TANTAMOUNT™ series. It is designed for applications requiring high reliability and stability, particularly in environments with varying temperatures. Tantalum capacitors are known for their high capacitance-to-volume ratio, making them suitable for compact electronic designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Industrial**: Used in power management circuits, filtering, and decoupling applications.
- **Telecom Infrastructure**: Employed in signal processing and power supply circuits.
- **General Purpose**: Suitable for consumer electronics, automotive applications, and military electronics where reliability is critical.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, and high capacitance is required without compromising performance. Its robust construction and compliance with industry standards make it a reliable choice for various electronic applications.
