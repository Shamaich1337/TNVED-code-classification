#### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance**: 3.3 µH (typical)
- **Rated Current (Irms)**: 3.4 A
- **Saturation Current (Isat)**: 3.6 A (inductance drops by 30% at this current)
- **DC Resistance (DCR)**: 0.034 Ω (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40 °C to +125 °C (temperature rise included)
- **Storage Temperature**: -10 °C to +40 °C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level**: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type**: Semi-shielded construction
- **Special Features**: RoHS compliant and halogen-free

#### Description:
The SRN6028-3R3M is a semi-shielded power inductor designed for use in various electronic applications. It is part of the SRN6028 series from Bourns, which features inductors with a range of inductance values from 0.90 µH to 100 µH. The primary function of this component is to store energy in a magnetic field when electrical current flows through it, which is essential for filtering and energy storage in power management applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **DC/DC Converters**: Used to convert direct current from one voltage level to another, essential in power supply circuits.
- **Notebook Computers**: Provides stable power supply and filtering for various components.
- **Digital Video Cameras**: Helps in managing power and reducing noise in video processing circuits.
- **Hard Disk Drives (HDDs)**: Used for power management and signal integrity.
- **Cellphones**: Essential for power regulation and noise filtering in compact designs.
- **Televisions and LCD Displays**: Used in power supply circuits to ensure stable operation and performance.

This inductor is suitable for applications requiring efficient energy storage and management, making it a versatile component in modern electronic devices.
