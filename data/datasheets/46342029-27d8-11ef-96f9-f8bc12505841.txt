  - Output Voltage: 2.5V (fixed)

  - Quiescent Current: 29 µA (typical)

  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C

  - SC70-5 (2.00 mm x 1.25 mm)

  - Low dropout voltage (250 mV at 150 mA for VOUT ≥ 2.5V)
  - Excellent line transient response: ±2 mV (typical)
  - Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): -60 dB at 1 kHz (typical)
  - Thermal overload and short-circuit protection
  - Fast turn-on time: 30 µs
  - Logic-controlled enable pin (active high)
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The **LP5951MG-2.5/NOPB** is a micropower, low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator designed for portable battery-powered applications. It provides a regulated output voltage of 2.5V with a maximum output current of 150 mA. The device features low quiescent current, making it suitable for applications where power efficiency is critical. The enable pin allows for easy control of the device, enabling it to enter a low-power shutdown mode.

#### Typical Applications:
The LP5951 is commonly used in:
- **Portable Battery-Powered Systems:** Ideal for devices requiring efficient power management.
- **Microcontrollers and Digital Signal Processors:** Provides stable voltage supply for sensitive digital circuits.
- **CMOS RAM Keep-Alive Applications:** Maintains power to memory devices during low-power states.
- **General Purpose Voltage Regulation:** Suitable for various electronic devices needing a stable voltage supply.

This component is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, as it is designed to work with small ceramic capacitors, enhancing its suitability for compact designs.
