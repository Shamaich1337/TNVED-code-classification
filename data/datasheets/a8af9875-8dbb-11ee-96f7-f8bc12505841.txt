  - SO14 (Plastic Small Outline Package), 14 leads, body width 3.9 mm

  - Schmitt-trigger inputs for noise immunity and transformation of slowly changing signals into sharply defined outputs.

  - ESD protection: HBM exceeds 2000 V, MM exceeds 200 V.
  - Complies with JEDEC standards JESD8C (2.7 V to 3.6 V) and JESD7A (2.0 V to 6.0 V).

  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description of the Component:
The **74HC132D** is a **quad 2-input NAND gate** featuring Schmitt-trigger inputs. This component is designed to provide high-speed logic operations while maintaining low power consumption. The Schmitt-trigger inputs allow for the handling of slow input signals, ensuring that the output remains stable and free from jitter, which is particularly useful in noisy environments.

#### Typical Applications:
The 74HC132D is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Wave and Pulse Shapers:** It can be utilized to create precise waveforms and pulse signals.
- **Astable Multivibrators:** The component can be configured to generate continuous square wave signals.
- **Monostable Multivibrators:** It can be used to create single pulse outputs in response to input triggers.
- **Signal Conditioning:** The Schmitt-trigger feature makes it suitable for cleaning up noisy signals in digital circuits.

This component is ideal for use in digital logic circuits where reliable signal processing is required, making it a versatile choice for engineers and designers in the electronics field.
