  - Output Voltage: 12 VDC

  - Operating Temperature: -40°C to +75°C

  - Storage Temperature: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Half brick, compact metal package
- **Special Features:**
  - High efficiency up to 91%
  - No minimum load requirement
  - Soft start feature
  - Adjustable output voltage (+10% / -20%)
  - Remote On/Off control
  - Under voltage lock-out circuit
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The TEP 160-4812WIR is a high-performance isolated DC/DC converter module designed for railway applications. It features an ultra-wide input voltage range and is housed in a rugged, sealed half-brick package. The converter is engineered for high efficiency, allowing it to operate at full power without the need for forced air cooling at 25°C, making it suitable for various demanding environments.

**Typical Applications:**
The TEP 160-4812WIR is primarily used in:
- **Telecom/Datacom Systems:** Providing reliable power distribution for communication equipment.
- **Industrial Control Systems:** Powering control devices and automation systems.
- **Railway Systems:** Specifically designed to meet EN 50155 standards for onboard power distribution in railway applications, ensuring robust performance in harsh conditions.

This converter is ideal for battery-operated systems due to its wide input voltage range and reverse input voltage protection, making it a versatile choice for various applications requiring reliable DC power conversion.
