### Component Summary: TRML-8100BG

**Manufacturer:** Optoway Technology Inc.  
**Product Code:** TRML-8100BG  
**Type:** Multi-Mode Transceiver

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage: +3.3 V
  - Absolute Maximum Power Supply Voltage: 0 to 4.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Power Supply Current (Icc): 180 to 240 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Typical Power Supply Voltage: 3.3 V
  - Power Supply Current: 240 mA (max)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -10 to 85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Small Form Factor (SFF), RJ-45 size, 2X5 pin package

- **Special Features:**
  - Bi-directional data link up to 4.25 Gb/s
  - Compliant with Fibre Channel FC-PH-2 and IEEE 802.3z 1000BASE-SX
  - Link distances of up to 150 m with 50/125 μm MMF cables at 4.25 Gb/s
  - Class 1 laser compliant (IEC-60825)
  - LVTTL Transmitter Disable Input and Signal Detect Output

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The TRML-8100BG is a high-performance, low-power multi-mode transceiver designed for bi-directional serial optical data communications. It operates at a nominal wavelength of 850 nm and is suitable for applications such as Fibre Channel and Gigabit Ethernet. The transmitter utilizes a vertical-cavity surface-emitting laser (VCSEL), while the receiver employs a GaAs detector preamplifier, ensuring efficient data transmission and reception.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Fibre Channel Communications:** Used in storage area networks (SANs) for high-speed data transfer.
- **Gigabit Ethernet:** Suitable for 1000BASE-SX applications, providing high-speed connectivity in local area networks (LANs).
- **Data Centers:** Ideal for interconnecting servers and storage devices due to its high data rate and distance capabilities.
- **Telecommunications:** Employed in various optical communication systems requiring reliable data transmission over multi-mode fiber.

This transceiver is particularly advantageous in environments where space is limited, and high data rates are essential, making it a versatile choice for modern networking solutions.
