### Component Summary: SESD0802Q4UG-0020-090

**Description:**
The SESD0802Q4UG-0020-090 is an ultra-low capacitance transient voltage suppression (TVS) diode array from Littelfuse's SESD series. It is designed to provide unidirectional electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection for high-speed serial interfaces while maintaining signal integrity. The component is particularly suitable for applications that require robust ESD protection in compact packages.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 9.00 V (typical at IT=1mA)
  - Reverse Working Voltage (VRWM): 7.0 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 2.0 A (for tP=8/20μs)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Clamping Voltage (VCL): 9.20 V at IPP=2.0A (tP=8/20μs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -55°C to 125°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTOR): -55°C to 150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 0802 DFN Array
- **Special Features:**
  - Low capacitance of 0.20 pF (typical)
  - ESD protection rating of ±20kV (contact and air, IEC 61000-4-2)
  - Low leakage current of 25-50 nA at VRWM=5.0V
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL): 1
  - RoHS compliant and lead-free

**Typical Applications:**
The SESD0802Q4UG-0020-090 is ideal for:
- High-speed data interfaces such as USB 3.1, 3.0, 2.0, HDMI 2.0, DisplayPort, and Thunderbolt.
- Consumer electronics, mobile devices, and portable electronics.
- Automotive applications requiring high ESD performance in compact designs.
- Tablet PCs and external storage devices with high-speed interfaces.

This component is particularly beneficial in environments where signal integrity is critical, and it helps protect sensitive electronic components from ESD events, ensuring reliable operation in various applications.
