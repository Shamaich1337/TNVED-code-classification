#### Description:
The AG303-86G is an InGaP HBT (Heterojunction Bipolar Transistor) gain block amplifier designed for high dynamic range applications. It operates over a frequency range from DC to 6000 MHz and is particularly optimized for wireless communication systems. The device features a Darlington-pair amplifier configuration, which enhances its performance characteristics, including gain and linearity.

#### Typical Applications:
The AG303-86G is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- **Mobile Infrastructure**: Enhancing signal strength and quality in cellular networks.
- **CATV / FTTX**: Used in cable television and fiber-to-the-x applications for signal amplification.
- **WLAN / ISM**: Applicable in wireless local area networks and industrial, scientific, and medical bands.
- **RFID**: Utilized in radio-frequency identification systems for improved read range and reliability.
- **WiMAX / WiBro**: Supports broadband wireless access technologies.

This component is ideal for designers looking for a reliable and efficient amplifier solution in modern wireless communication systems, ensuring robust performance across various frequency bands.
