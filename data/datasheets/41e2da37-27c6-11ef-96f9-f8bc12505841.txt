- **Power Consumption:**

  - Total Power Dissipation (PD) at 100°C: 0.71W
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-89
- **Special Features:**
  - Low RDS(ON):

  - Trench Power LV MOSFET technology
  - Excellent package for heat dissipation
  - Halogen Free
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 flammability rating
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

The YJH05N06A is an N-Channel Enhancement Mode MOSFET designed for efficient power switching applications. It features low on-resistance, which minimizes power loss during operation, making it suitable for high-efficiency designs. The device is built using advanced trench technology, which allows for a compact design with excellent thermal performance.

- **Power Switching Applications:** The YJH05N06A is ideal for use in power management circuits where efficient switching is critical.
- **Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS):** It can be utilized in UPS systems to manage power flow and ensure reliability during power outages.
- **DC-DC Converters:** The MOSFET is suitable for DC-DC conversion applications, where it helps in regulating voltage levels efficiently.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, such as consumer electronics, industrial power supplies, and renewable energy systems.
