  - Supply Voltage: 2.7V to 18V
- **Current Ratings:**

  - Operating Supply Voltage: 2.7V to 18V
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT-23 (5 pins)
- **Special Features:**
  - Wide common-mode voltage range
  - Low error: 3.0% maximum over temperature
  - Bandwidth up to 500 kHz
  - Three transfer functions available: 20V/V, 50V/V, and 100V/V
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The INA196AIDBVT is a current shunt monitor with voltage output designed to sense voltage drops across shunt resistors at common-mode voltages ranging from -16V to +80V. It operates from a single supply voltage of 2.7V to 18V and is capable of providing accurate current measurements with low error rates. The device features a bandwidth of up to 500 kHz, making it suitable for applications requiring fast response times.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Welding Equipment:** Monitoring current flow in welding applications.
- **Notebook Computers:** Power management and battery monitoring.
- **Cell Phones:** Current sensing for battery charging and discharging.
- **Telecom Equipment:** Monitoring power consumption and efficiency.
- **Automotive:** Current sensing in various automotive applications.
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to monitor current flow.
- **Battery Chargers:** Ensuring accurate charging by monitoring current levels.

The INA196AIDBVT is particularly useful in applications where precise current measurement is critical, and its wide common-mode voltage range allows it to be used in various environments, including automotive and industrial settings.
