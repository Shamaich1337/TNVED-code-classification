**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** 
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 4.5 V to 5.5 V (typical 5 V)
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Supply Current: 105 mA (typical), 122 mA (max)
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Power Dissipation: 0.53 W at 5 V
- **Operating Temperature Range:** 
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:** 
  - 3-Lead Small Outline Transistor Package (SOT-89)
- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Fixed gain of 20 dB
  - Integrated bias control circuit
  - Input and output internally matched to 50 Ω
  - ESD rating of ±2 kV (Class 2)
  - MSL -1 rated
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** 
  - MSL -1

**Description:**
The ADL5536 is a high-performance linear amplifier designed for intermediate frequency (IF) applications, operating within a frequency range of 20 MHz to 1.0 GHz. It features a fixed gain of 20 dB and is optimized for low noise and high linearity, making it suitable for various RF applications. The device is fabricated using a Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) HBT process, which contributes to its excellent performance characteristics.

**Typical Applications:**
The ADL5536 is commonly used in:
- Cellular communication systems
- CATV (Cable Television) applications
- Military communication equipment
- Instrumentation and measurement devices
- Any application requiring high dynamic range and low noise amplification in the RF frequency range.

This amplifier's design allows for easy integration into systems due to its internal matching to 50 Ω, requiring only minimal external components for operation. Its robust performance across a wide temperature range and low power consumption make it an ideal choice for modern RF applications.
