### Component Summary: BAV99 Dual Switching Diode

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** BAV99  
**Package Type:** SOT-23

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Reverse Voltage (V_RRM): 100 V
  - Reverse Voltage (V_R): 75 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (I_F): 200 mA (per leg)
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (I_FSM): 2.0 A (8.3 ms, half sine-wave, per leg)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D): 350 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_STG): -55°C to +150°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Maximum Thermal Resistance (R_thJA): 357 °C/W
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level:** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The BAV99 is a dual switching diode designed for high-speed switching applications. It features a compact SOT-23 surface mount package, making it suitable for automatic insertion in circuit boards. The diode is characterized by its fast switching speed and high conductance, which are essential for efficient signal processing in electronic circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
The BAV99 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Signal Switching:** Ideal for switching signals in communication devices.
- **Clamping and Protection Circuits:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Rectification:** Used in power supply circuits for converting AC to DC.
- **General Purpose Switching:** Suitable for a wide range of electronic applications where fast switching is required.

This diode is compliant with RoHS standards, ensuring it meets environmental regulations for hazardous substances.
