### Component Summary for PCIH47M400A1/AA

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Contacts 45, 46, and 47: 1,000 V r.m.s.
  - Contacts 1 through 20: 500 V r.m.s.
  - Contacts 21 through 44: 333 V r.m.s.
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Size 16 Power Contacts: 
    - Positions 45, 46, and 47: 40 A continuous
    - Positions 1 - 20: 28 A continuous
  - Size 22 Signal Contacts: 3 A nominal rating.
  
- **Power Consumption:** Not specified directly; power handling is determined by current ratings.

- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +125°C.

- **Package Type:** Connector (specifically designed for CompactPCI® applications).

- **Special Features:**
  - Fully compliant with PICMG® 2.11 Power Interface Specification.
  - Blind mating system for ease of connection.
  - Removable contacts with a "Closed Entry" design for reliability.
  - Available with compliant terminations for printed board mounting.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The **PCIH47M400A1/AA** is a high-performance power connector designed for use in CompactPCI® systems, particularly in modular power supply applications. It features a robust design that accommodates high current and voltage requirements, making it suitable for demanding environments. The connector includes size 16 power contacts and size 22 signal contacts, ensuring versatility in various applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Ideal for distributing power in CompactPCI® systems where high current capacity is required.
- **Modular Power Supplies:** Used in systems that require reliable and efficient power connections.
- **Telecommunications Equipment:** Suitable for applications in telecommunications where robust and reliable connections are critical.
- **Industrial Automation:** Can be utilized in industrial settings where high-performance connectors are necessary for power distribution.

This connector is particularly beneficial in environments where space is limited, and high reliability is essential, such as in telecommunications and industrial automation systems.
