#### Description:
The DBTC-13-4L+ is a surface mount directional coupler designed for RF applications. It provides a nominal coupling of 13 dB across a frequency range of 5 to 1000 MHz. The coupler is characterized by its low mainline loss and high directivity, making it suitable for various RF signal processing tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **VHF/UHF Receivers/Transmitters:** Used in communication systems to couple signals for monitoring or measurement.
- **Cellular Systems:** Employed in cellular base stations and mobile devices for signal routing and power management.
- **General RF Applications:** Suitable for any application requiring signal splitting or combining with minimal loss and high isolation.

This component is ideal for engineers looking for reliable performance in RF signal management, particularly in environments where temperature stability and compact size are critical.
