### Component Summary: MURS160-13-F

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** MURS160-13-F  
**Document Number:** DS30130 Rev. 11 - 2  
**Date:** October 2012  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 600 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 600 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 600 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 424 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO) at 135°C: 1.0 A
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 35 A (8.3 ms single half sine-wave)

- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated, but forward voltage drop at 1.0 A is 1.25 V at 25°C.

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55°C to +175°C

- **Package Type:** SMB (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Glass Passivated Die Construction
  - Super-Fast Recovery Time for High Efficiency
  - Surge Overload Rating to 35A Peak
  - Lead-Free Finish; RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free (Green Device)
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 per J-STD-020

#### Component Description:
The MURS160-13-F is a surface mount super-fast rectifier diode designed for high-efficiency applications. It features a glass passivated die construction that enhances reliability and performance. The diode is capable of handling high surge currents, making it suitable for demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The MURS160-13-F is typically used in:
- Power management circuits
- Switching power supplies
- DC-DC converters
- Rectification in various electronic devices
- Automated assembly processes due to its surface mount design

This component is ideal for applications requiring fast switching and high efficiency, particularly in environments where space is limited and reliability is critical.
