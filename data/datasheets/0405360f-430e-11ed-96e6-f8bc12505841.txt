### Component Summary: 2N7002WT1G

**Description:**
The 2N7002WT1G is a small signal N-channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) designed for low power applications. It features a compact SC-70 surface mount package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. This device is AEC-Q101 qualified, indicating its reliability for automotive applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (V_DSS): 60 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (V_GS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I_D):
    - 340 mA (at T_A = 25 °C)
    - 240 mA (at T_A = 85 °C)
  - Pulsed Drain Current (I_DM): 1.4 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (P_D):
    - 280 mW (steady state at T_A = 25 °C)
    - 330 mW (steady state at T_A = 85 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T_J, T_STG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SC-70 (SOT-323), Case 419

- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Protected
  - Low R_DS(on): 1.19 Ω (at V_GS = 10 V, I_D = 500 mA)
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant
  - 2V Prefix for automotive applications requiring unique site and control change requirements

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1

**Typical Applications:**
The 2N7002WT1G is commonly used in various applications, including:
- Low Side Load Switching
- Level Shift Circuits
- DC-DC Converters
- Portable Devices such as Digital Still Cameras (DSC), Personal Digital Assistants (PDA), and Cell Phones

This MOSFET is ideal for applications requiring efficient switching and low power consumption, making it a versatile choice for both consumer electronics and automotive systems.
