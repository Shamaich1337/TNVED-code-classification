#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Rated operating voltage across inductor = 50 V
- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Heat Rating Current (DC Typ.)**: 23.0 A
  - **Saturation Current (DC Typ.)**: 27.0 A
- **Power Consumption**: Not explicitly stated; however, the inductor is designed for high efficiency with low DCR (Direct Current Resistance).
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C (ambient + temperature rise should not exceed 125 °C)
- **Package Type**: IHLP-2020CZ (dimensions: 5.18 mm x 3.0 mm x 2.54 mm)
- **Special Features**:
  - Shielded construction
  - Frequency range up to 5.0 MHz
  - Low DCR/μH in this package size
  - Handles high transient current spikes without saturation
  - Ultra-low buzz noise due to composite construction
  - Excellent temperature stability for inductance and saturation
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IHLP2020CZER2R2M01 is a high-performance shielded inductor from Vishay's IHLP series, specifically designed for applications requiring high saturation current and low DC resistance. It is a surface-mount device (SMD) that provides excellent thermal stability and low noise operation, making it suitable for various power management applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Ideal for high current Point of Load (POL) converters and low-profile, high-current power supplies.
- **Battery-Powered Devices**: Suitable for use in portable electronics where space and efficiency are critical.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Commonly used in distributed power systems and for powering Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs).
- **Computing Devices**: Applicable in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers, where reliable power delivery is essential.

This inductor is particularly beneficial in applications that demand high efficiency and reliability under varying load conditions, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
