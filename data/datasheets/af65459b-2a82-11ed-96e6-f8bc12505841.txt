### Component Summary: MBRS330T3G

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 30 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 30 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage: 30 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 3.0 A at T_L = 110 °C
  - Nonrepetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 80 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF): 0.50 V at 3.0 A, T_J = 25 °C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (Surface Mount Component), 2-lead case 403AC

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low forward voltage drop
  - Excellent ability to withstand reverse avalanche energy transients
  - Guard-ring for stress protection
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Description:**
The MBRS330T3G is a Schottky power rectifier designed for low voltage, high frequency rectification. It utilizes the Schottky Barrier principle in a metal-to-silicon power diode configuration, featuring a compact surface mount package that is ideal for applications where space and weight are critical. The device is characterized by its low forward voltage drop and high surge current capability, making it suitable for various power management applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient rectification.
- **Signal Processing:** Acts as a freewheeling diode in switching applications.
- **Polarity Protection:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections.
- **Automotive Applications:** Suitable for automotive electronics due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, ensuring reliability under automotive conditions.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring compact size and high efficiency, such as in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.
