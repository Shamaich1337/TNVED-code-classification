### Component Summary: SRP1038A-220M (Bourns)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Inductance**: 22 µH (±20% tolerance)
- **DC Resistance (DCR)**: 
  - Typ: 8 mΩ
  - Max: 64 mΩ
- **Rated Current (Irms)**: 5 A (inductance drops 20% at Isat)
- **Saturation Current (Isat)**: 7 A
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +150 °C (temperature rise included)
- **Storage Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +150 °C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level**: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type**: Shielded construction with non-lead frame terminal
- **Special Features**: 
  - AEC-Q200 compliant (automotive grade)
  - RoHS compliant and halogen-free
  - High saturation current capability

#### Description:
The SRP1038A-220M is a shielded power inductor designed for use in various electronic applications. It features a carbonyl powder core, which provides high saturation current capabilities and low DC resistance, making it suitable for power management applications. The shielded construction minimizes electromagnetic interference, enhancing performance in sensitive circuits.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Used in DC-DC converters, voltage regulators, and power supply circuits to smooth out current and reduce ripple.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Due to its AEC-Q200 compliance, it is suitable for automotive applications, including power distribution and battery management systems.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in devices requiring efficient power conversion and energy storage, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
- **Industrial Equipment**: Utilized in various industrial applications where reliable power management is critical.

This inductor is ideal for applications that demand high efficiency and reliability, particularly in automotive and consumer electronics sectors.
