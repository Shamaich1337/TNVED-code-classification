### Component Summary: AD8672ARZ (Enhanced Product AD8672-EP)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Dual-supply operation: ±5 V to ±15 V
  - Input Voltage Range: ±12 V (for ±15 V supply)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current per Amplifier: 3 mA typical (up to 4.2 mA over temperature range)
  - Output Current: ±10 mA (for ±5 V supply), ±20 mA (for ±15 V supply)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Supply Current: 3 mA typical
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - −55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 8-Lead SOIC (Standard Small Outline Package)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Very low noise: Voltage noise density of 2.8 nV/√Hz
  - Low input bias current: 14 nA maximum
  - Low offset voltage: 75 μV maximum
  - High open-loop gain: 1000 V/mV (120 dB)
  - Unity-gain stable, no phase reversal
  - Enhanced product features for defense and aerospace applications
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The AD8672ARZ is a precision operational amplifier (op-amp) designed for applications requiring very low noise, low input bias current, and high precision. It features a gain bandwidth product of 10 MHz and is stable with capacitive loads exceeding 1000 pF. The amplifier is characterized by its low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
The AD8672ARZ is commonly used in:
- Phase-locked loop (PLL) filters
- Filters for GPS systems
- Medical and analytical instrumentation
- Precision power supply controls
- Automated test equipment (ATE)
- Data acquisition systems
- Professional quality audio applications

This op-amp is particularly well-suited for applications where high precision and low noise are critical, such as in instrumentation and audio processing. Its extended temperature range and enhanced product features make it ideal for use in harsh environments, including defense and aerospace applications.
