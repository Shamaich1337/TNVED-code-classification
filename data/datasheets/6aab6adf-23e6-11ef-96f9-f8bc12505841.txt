### Component Summary: FDMS86163P

**General Description:**
The FDMS86163P is a P-Channel MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) produced using ON Semiconductor's advanced POWERTRENCH technology. This technology is designed to minimize on-state resistance while maintaining superior switching performance, making it suitable for various applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-to-Source Voltage (VDS): -100 V
  - Gate-to-Source Voltage (VGS): ±25 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TC = 25 °C: -50 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at TA = 25 °C: -7.9 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current: -100 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at TC = 25 °C: 104 W
  - Power Dissipation (PD) at TA = 25 °C: 2.5 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - PQFN-8 (Power Quad Flat No-lead) with dimensions of 5x6 mm and a pitch of 1.27 mm.

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low RDS(on): 
    - 22 mΩ at VGS = -10 V, ID = -7.9 A
    - 30 mΩ at VGS = -6 V, ID = -5.9 A
  - Optimized for fast switching applications and load switch applications.
  - 100% UIL tested.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - JEDEC J-STD-020E: Level 1.

**Description:**
The FDMS86163P is a P-Channel MOSFET designed for high-efficiency switching applications. It features low on-resistance and is optimized for fast switching, making it ideal for power management tasks.

**Typical Applications:**
- Active clamp switches
- Load switches
- Power management circuits
- High-speed switching applications

This component is particularly useful in applications where efficient power control and minimal energy loss are critical, such as in power supplies, battery management systems, and various consumer electronics.
