  - Coil Voltage: 24 VDC

  - Rated Current: 2 A

  - -40°C to +85°C

- **Special Features:**
  - Monostable relay with 2 form C bifurcated contacts (2 changeover contacts)
  - High sensitivity for low power consumption
  - Immersion cleanable
  - Surge voltage resistance between contact and coil: 2.5 kV (2/10 μs)
  - Meets Telcordia Requirement GR-1089 and FCC Part 68
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - MSL3

#### Description:
The V23079-B1205-B301 is a signal relay from TE Connectivity's AXICOM series, specifically designed for telecom applications. It features a monostable design with two form C contacts, allowing it to switch between two states. The relay is characterized by its compact size (15 x 7.5 mm) and high sensitivity, making it suitable for low power consumption applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This relay is commonly used in various communication equipment, including:
- Line card applications for ringing and test access
- Private Automatic Branch Exchange (PABX) systems
- Voice over IP (VoIP) devices
- Office equipment
- Measurement and control systems
- Automotive applications (e.g., CAN bus, keyless entry)
- Medical equipment
- Consumer electronics (e.g., set-top boxes, HiFi systems)

The V23079-B1205-B301 is ideal for applications requiring reliable switching in environments where space is limited and power efficiency is critical.
