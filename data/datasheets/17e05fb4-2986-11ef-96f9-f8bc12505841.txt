### Component Summary: SMT-0826-S-R

#### Key Specifications:
- **Rated Voltage:** 3.6 V0-pk
- **Operating Voltage Range:** 2.5 – 4.5 V0-pk
- **Current Draw at Rated Voltage:** 90 mA (Max)
- **Coil Resistance:** 15.0 ± 3 Ohms
- **Minimum SPL @ 10cm:** 90.0 dBA
- **Resonant Frequency:** 2630 ± 500 Hz
- **Housing Material:** LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer)
- **Terminal Material:** Sn plated Phosphor Bronze
- **Weight:** 0.39 grams
- **Storage Temperature:** -40 to 85 °C
- **Operating Temperature:** -30 to 70 °C
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):** None
- **Environmental Compliance:** ROHS/REACH

#### Description:
The SMT-0826-S-R is a surface mount piezoelectric speaker produced by PUI Audio, Inc. It is designed to convert electrical signals into audible sound, making it suitable for various audio applications. The component operates efficiently within a specified voltage range and is characterized by its low power consumption and high sound pressure level (SPL).

#### Typical Applications:
The SMT-0826-S-R is commonly used in:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Such as alarms, notifications, and alerts in devices like smartphones, tablets, and home appliances.
- **Toys:** Providing sound effects and audio feedback.
- **Medical Devices:** For alerts and notifications in portable medical equipment.
- **Automotive Applications:** Used in warning signals and alerts within vehicles.
- **Industrial Equipment:** For signaling and alerting in machinery and control systems.

This component is particularly valued for its compact size, making it ideal for applications where space is limited, while still delivering a clear and loud audio output.
