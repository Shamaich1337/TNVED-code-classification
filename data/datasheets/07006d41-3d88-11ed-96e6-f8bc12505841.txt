### Component Summary: HMC558ALC3B

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; operates with LO drive levels as low as 9 dBm.
- **Current Ratings:** IF source/sink current is limited to 3 mA.
- **Power Consumption:** Continuous power dissipation (P_DISS) at 85°C is 495 mW (derating at 5.5 mW/°C above 85°C).
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +85°C.
- **Package Type:** 12-lead ceramic leadless chip carrier (LCC), E-12-4.
- **Special Features:**
  - Double-balanced mixer topology.
  - Requires no external components or matching circuitry.
  - RoHS compliant, eliminating the need for wire bonding.
  - Excellent LO to RF and LO to IF isolation due to optimized balun structures.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL3 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Description:**
The HMC558A is a general-purpose, double-balanced mixer designed for use as either an upconverter or downconverter within the frequency range of 5.5 GHz to 14 GHz. It is fabricated using a gallium arsenide (GaAs) MESFET process, which allows for high performance in microwave applications. The device is packaged in a leadless, surface-mount technology (SMT) format, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing.

**Typical Applications:**
The HMC558A is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Point-to-point microwave radios:** Facilitating high-frequency communication links.
- **Point-to-multipoint radios:** Enabling multiple connections from a single source.
- **Military end use:** Providing reliable communication in defense applications.
- **Instrumentation and automatic test equipment (ATE):** Used in testing and measurement devices.
- **Sensors:** Integrating into sensor systems for signal processing.

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient signal conversion and isolation in the microwave frequency range, making it a versatile choice for engineers in telecommunications and related fields.
