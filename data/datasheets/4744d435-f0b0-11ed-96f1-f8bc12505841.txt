### Component Summary: IHLP2020CZER3R3M11 (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: Rated operating voltage across inductor = 40 V
- **Current Ratings**:
  - **Heat Rating Current (DC)**: 26.3 A (for 0.1 μH variant)
  - **Saturation Current (DC)**: 20.5 A (for 0.1 μH variant)
- **Power Consumption**: Not explicitly stated; however, the low DCR indicates efficient power handling.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: IHLP-2020CZ (dimensions: 5.18 mm x 3.0 mm x 2.54 mm)
- **Special Features**:
  - Shielded construction
  - Lowest DCR/μH in its package size
  - Handles high transient current spikes without saturation
  - Ultra-low buzz noise due to composite construction
  - Excellent temperature stability for inductance and saturation
  - Suitable for DC/DC energy storage up to 1 MHz to 2 MHz
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The IHLP2020CZER3R3M11 is a low DCR shielded inductor from Vishay's IHLP series. It is designed for high current applications and features a compact size, making it suitable for various power management tasks. The inductor is characterized by its ability to handle high transient currents without saturating, which is crucial for maintaining performance in demanding applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management**: Ideal for high current point-of-load (POL) converters and low-profile power supplies.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers.
- **Battery-Powered Devices**: Suitable for applications requiring efficient energy storage and management.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Commonly found in distributed power systems and for field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs).

This inductor is particularly valuable in applications where space is limited, and efficiency is paramount, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
