### Component Summary: 1.5KE180CA

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** 1.5KE180CA

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 171V (min) to 189V (max)
  - Stand-off Voltage (VRM): 180V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 246A
  - Non-repetitive Surge Peak Forward Current (IFSM): 200A (10ms)

  - Power Dissipation on Infinite Heatsink: 5W at T_amb = 75°C
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation: 1500W (10/1000 µs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65°C to +175°C
  - Maximum Junction Temperature (T_j): 175°C
- **Package Type:** DO-201 (Plastic)
- **Special Features:**
  - Unidirectional and Bidirectional types available
  - Low clamping factor
  - Fast response time
  - UL recognized
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** JEDEC J-STD-020E Level 1

#### Description:
The **1.5KE180CA** is a **transil diode**, designed to provide high overvoltage protection through clamping action. It is particularly effective in protecting voltage-sensitive devices such as MOS technology and low-voltage supplied integrated circuits (ICs). The diode's instantaneous response to transient overvoltages makes it suitable for applications where rapid protection is essential.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Overvoltage Protection:** Used in circuits to protect sensitive components from voltage spikes.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Commonly employed in power management systems to safeguard against surges.
- **Signal Processing:** Protects signal lines in communication devices from overvoltage conditions.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Integrated into devices to enhance reliability and longevity by preventing damage from electrical transients.

This component is ideal for use in various electronic applications where transient voltage suppression is critical, ensuring the safety and functionality of sensitive electronic devices.
