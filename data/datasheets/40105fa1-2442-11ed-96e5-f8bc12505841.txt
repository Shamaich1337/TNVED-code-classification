### Component Summary: MMSZ4715T1G

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 34.20V (Nominal), with a range of 36V to 37.80V.
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Reverse Current (IZT): 50 µA at Zener voltage.
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Total Power Dissipation: 500 mW on FR-5 board at T_L = 75 °C, derated above 75 °C at 6.7 mW/°C.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Junction and Storage Temperature: -55°C to +150°C.
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOD-123 (Surface Mount).
- **Special Features**: 
  - ESD Rating of Class 3 (> 16 kV) per Human Body Model.
  - Pb-Free and RoHS Compliant.
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified for automotive applications.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The MMSZ4715T1G is a Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It is part of the MMSZ4xxxT1G series of low-power Zener voltage regulators, which are packaged in a compact SOD-123 surface mount configuration. The primary function of this component is to maintain a stable output voltage across varying load conditions, effectively protecting sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation**: Used in power management circuits to provide a stable reference voltage.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression**: Protects circuits from voltage spikes and transients.
- **Signal Conditioning**: Ensures signal integrity in various electronic applications.
- **Automotive Electronics**: Suitable for automotive applications due to its AEC-Q101 qualification, making it reliable for use in harsh environments.

This component is ideal for applications requiring compact size and high reliability, such as consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.
