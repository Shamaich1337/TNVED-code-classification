  - Bi-directional data link up to 4.25 Gb/s

  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The TRSL-8105AG is a high-performance, low-power single-mode transceiver designed for bi-directional serial optical data communications. It operates at a nominal wavelength of 1310 nm and is capable of supporting data rates of up to 4.25 Gb/s. The transmitter utilizes a multiple quantum well laser, while the receiver employs an integrated InGaAs detector preamplifier. This module is particularly suited for applications requiring high-speed data transmission over single-mode fiber.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Fibre Channel Communications:** The TRSL-8105AG is ideal for tri-rate Fibre Channel applications (1.063/2.125/4.25 Gb/s), making it suitable for storage area networks (SANs) and data center interconnects.
- **Gigabit Ethernet:** It is also used in 1000BASE-LX Gigabit Ethernet applications, providing reliable data transmission over long distances (up to 10 km at lower data rates).
- **Telecommunications:** The transceiver can be utilized in various telecommunications systems where high-speed data transfer is essential.

This component is designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern data communication systems, ensuring high reliability and performance in various networking environments.
