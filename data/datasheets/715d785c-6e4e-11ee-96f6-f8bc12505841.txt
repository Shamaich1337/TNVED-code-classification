### Component Summary: LFCN-2750+

**Description:**
The LFCN-2750+ is a ceramic low pass filter designed for RF applications. It operates within a frequency range of DC to 2750 MHz and is suitable for applications requiring harmonic rejection and signal integrity in VHF/UHF transmitters and receivers. The filter is constructed using LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) technology, which provides excellent temperature stability and power handling capabilities.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, it is designed for RF applications.
- **Current Ratings:** Not specified; power handling is indicated.
- **Power Consumption:** RF Power Input: 10 W max at 25°C; derates to 3.5 W at 100°C ambient.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C
- **Package Type:** FV1206 (small size)
- **Special Features:**
  - Excellent power handling (10 W)
  - 7-section design for improved performance
  - Temperature stable
  - RoHS compliant
  - Protected by U.S. Patent 6,943,646
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
- Harmonic rejection in RF circuits
- VHF/UHF transmitters and receivers
- Laboratory use for signal processing and filtering applications

This low pass filter is ideal for applications where it is crucial to eliminate high-frequency noise while allowing lower frequencies to pass through, ensuring clean signal transmission in various RF systems.
