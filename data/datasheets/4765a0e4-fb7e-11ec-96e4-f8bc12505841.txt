### Component Summary: 90WECAS - Endlaunch Waveguide to Coaxial Adapter

#### Key Specifications:
- **Frequency Range:** 8.2 - 12.4 GHz
- **VSWR (Voltage Standing Wave Ratio):** 1.25
- **Connector Type:** SMA-F (SubMiniature version A Female)
- **Waveguide Type:** WR90
- **Flange Type:** FBP100
- **Material:** Aluminum (Al)
- **Net Weight:** Approximately 0.06 kg

#### Description:
The 90WECAS is an endlaunch waveguide to coaxial adapter designed for high-frequency applications. It facilitates the transition between waveguide and coaxial cable, allowing for efficient signal transmission in the specified frequency range. The adapter is constructed from aluminum, ensuring durability and lightweight characteristics.

#### Typical Applications:
This component is typically used in microwave and RF applications, including:
- **Signal Processing:** Used in systems that require the conversion of waveguide signals to coaxial signals.
- **Telecommunications:** Suitable for high-frequency communication systems where signal integrity is crucial.
- **Antenna Systems:** Often employed in antenna feed systems to connect waveguide feeds to coaxial cables.
- **Test Equipment:** Utilized in laboratory setups for testing and measurement of microwave components.

The 90WECAS adapter is essential for applications that demand reliable performance in the 8.2 to 12.4 GHz frequency range, making it a valuable component in various RF and microwave systems.
