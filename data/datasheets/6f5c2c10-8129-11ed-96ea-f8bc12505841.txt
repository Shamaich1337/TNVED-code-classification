### Component Summary: BAV170Q-7-F

**Description:**
The BAV170Q-7-F is a dual surface mount low leakage diode manufactured by Diodes Incorporated. It is designed for applications requiring low leakage current and high reliability, making it suitable for automotive and other demanding environments. The device is housed in a SOT23 package, which is ideal for automated insertion processes.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 85 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 85 V
  - RMS Reverse Voltage (VR(RMS)): 60 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Continuous Current (IFM): 215 mA (single diode), 125 mA (double diode)
  - Repetitive Peak Forward Current (IFRM): 500 mA
  - Non-Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 4.0 A (1.0 µs), 1.0 A (1.0 ms), 0.5 A (1.0 s)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 250 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -65 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Very low leakage current
  - Fully RoHS compliant and lead-free
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for high reliability
  - Halogen and antimony-free, classified as a "Green" device
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 per J-STD-020

### Typical Applications:
The BAV170Q-7-F is typically used in applications such as:

- **Automotive Electronics:** Due to its high reliability and low leakage characteristics, it is suitable for automotive circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** It can be used in signal rectification and switching applications.
- **Power Management:** The diode can be utilized in power supply circuits to manage voltage levels and protect against reverse polarity.
- **General Purpose Switching:** It is applicable in various electronic circuits where low leakage and fast switching are required.
