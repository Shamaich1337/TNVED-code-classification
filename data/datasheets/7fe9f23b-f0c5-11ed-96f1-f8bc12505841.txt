- **Size:** 8 mm (D) x 8.7 mm (L)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to 105°C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The PV331M016F087PTR is a solid conductive polymer capacitor designed for surface mount technology (SMD). It features a low profile and high capacitance, making it suitable for applications where space is limited and high performance is required. The capacitor is characterized by its solid conductive polymer electrolyte, which provides improved performance over traditional electrolytic capacitors, including lower equivalent series resistance (ESR) and higher ripple current handling.

- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for smoothing and filtering applications.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for decoupling and bypassing in high-frequency circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as computers, smartphones, and other portable electronics where compact size and high capacitance are essential.
- **Automotive Applications:** Suitable for automotive electronics due to its wide operating temperature range and reliability.
