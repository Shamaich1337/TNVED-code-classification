### Component Summary: 3SMAJ5949B-TP

**Manufacturer:** Micro Commercial Components Corp.  
**Product Code:** 3SMAJ5949B-TP  
**Type:** Zener Diode  
**Package Type:** DO-214AC (SMA)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:** 100V (Zener Voltage)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum DC Zener Current (IZM): 3100 mA
  - Test Current (IZT): 250 mA
- **Power Consumption:** 3.0W (Steady State Power Dissipation)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **Thermal Resistance:**
  - Junction to Lead: 25°C/W
  - Junction to Ambient: 135°C/W
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Glass Passivated Junction
  - Surface Mount Application
  - Halogen Free, "Green" Device
  - Lead Free Finish/RoHS Compliant
  - Epoxy meets UL 94 V-0 Flammability Rating

#### Description:
The 3SMAJ5949B is a surface mount silicon Zener diode designed for voltage regulation and protection applications. It provides a stable reference voltage and is capable of clamping voltage spikes, making it essential in protecting sensitive electronic components from overvoltage conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Transient Voltage Suppression:** Protects circuits from voltage spikes and transients.
- **Signal Clamping:** Ensures that signal levels do not exceed specified limits, protecting downstream components.
- **Power Management:** Commonly used in power management systems to stabilize voltage levels.

This component is particularly suitable for applications in consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment where reliable voltage regulation and protection are critical.
