### Component Summary for FH471M016F115A (CapXon Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Capacitance**: 470 µF
- **Rated Voltage (VR)**: 16 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS)**: 18 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC)**: -20% to +20% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR)**: 
  - 533 mA at 120Hz, 105°C
  - 740 mA at 100kHz, 105°C
- **Dissipation Factor (tanδ)**: 16% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Leakage Current (ILEAK)**: 75 µA (2 min) at 20°C
- **Max. Impedance (Z)**: 0.1 Ω at 100kHz, 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to 105°C
- **Package Type**: Through-Hole (THT)
- **Size**: Diameter 8 mm x Length 11.5 mm
- **Pitch**: 3.5 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The FH471M016F115A is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's FH series, designed for high ripple current applications. It features low impedance characteristics, making it suitable for various electronic circuits where stable capacitance and reliability under high-frequency conditions are required.

#### Typical Applications:
This capacitor is typically used in:
- **Power Management**: Filtering and smoothing in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Processing**: Coupling and decoupling applications in audio and RF circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Used in devices that require stable voltage and current supply, such as televisions, computers, and audio equipment.
- **Industrial Equipment**: Applications that demand high reliability and performance under varying temperature conditions.

This component is particularly beneficial in environments where high ripple currents are present, and its wide operating temperature range allows for versatility in various applications.
