The G3S06520B is a 650V/20A Silicon Carbide (SiC) Power Schottky Barrier Diode. This diode is particularly beneficial in high-temperature and high-frequency environments.

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 650 V
  - Surge Peak Reverse Voltage (VRSM): 650 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 650 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF):
    - At 25°C: 40 A

  - Repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFRM): 50 A
  - Non-repetitive Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 120 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PTOT):

    - At 110°C: 71 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to 175°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to 175°C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-247-2L

- **Special Features:**
  - Zero reverse recovery current
  - Zero forward recovery voltage
  - Temperature-independent switching behavior
  - High-temperature operation
  - High-frequency operation

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

### Typical Applications:
The G3S06520B is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- Switched Mode Power Supplies (SMPS), particularly in Continuous Conduction Mode Power Factor Correction (CCM PFC)
- Motor drives
- Renewable energy systems such as solar applications and wind turbines
- Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
- Rail traction systems
- Electric Vehicles (EV) and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV)
