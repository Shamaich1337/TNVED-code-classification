#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Operating Voltage: 3.15V to 3.45V (typical 3.3V)
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Total Supply Current (Not Transmitting): 4 mA to 10 mA
  - Total Supply Current (Transmitting one bus at 50% duty cycle): 225 mA to 250 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power Dissipation (Not Transmitting): 0.06 W
  - Power Dissipation (Transmitting one bus at 100% duty cycle): 0.3 W to 0.5 W
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - Industrial: -40°C to +85°C
  - Extended: -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 44-pin Plastic Chip-Scale Package (QFN), 20-pin Plastic ESOIC, and 20-pin Ceramic DIP
- **Special Features**: 
  - Compliant with MIL-STD-1553A and B, ARINC 708A
  - Low power CMOS design
  - Integrated transmitter inhibit control signals
  - Smallest footprint available in 7 mm x 7 mm
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: 
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The HI-1573PSIF is a low-power CMOS dual transceiver designed for communication over the MIL-STD-1553 data bus. It features separate transmitter inhibit control signals for each transmitter and converts bi-phase differential data from the 1553 bus into complementary CMOS/TTL data suitable for input to a Manchester decoder. The device includes a transmitter section that converts CMOS/TTL Manchester II bi-phase data into differential voltages for driving the bus isolation transformer.

#### Typical Applications:
The HI-1573PSIF is primarily used in military and aerospace applications that require robust data communication. Typical applications include:
- **Data Bus Communication**: Facilitating communication in systems that adhere to the MIL-STD-1553 protocol.
- **Signal Processing**: Converting differential signals to CMOS/TTL levels for further processing.
- **Control Systems**: Used in avionics and military systems for reliable data transmission and control.
- **Isolation Interfaces**: Providing isolation between different parts of a system while maintaining signal integrity.

This component is ideal for applications requiring high reliability and compliance with military standards, making it suitable for use in harsh environments.
