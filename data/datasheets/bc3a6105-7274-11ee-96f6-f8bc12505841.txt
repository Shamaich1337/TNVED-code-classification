### Component Summary: TCM2-43X+

**Description:**
The TCM2-43X+ is a surface mount RF transformer manufactured by Mini-Circuits. It is designed for balanced transmission line applications and operates over a wide frequency range from 10 MHz to 4000 MHz. The transformer features a 2:1 impedance ratio and is suitable for various RF applications, including wideband push-pull amplifiers and cellular systems.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Not explicitly stated; however, the RF power rating is 0.4W.
- **Current Ratings:** DC current rating of 30 mA.
- **Power Consumption:** Maximum RF power of 0.4W.
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to 85°C.
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -55°C to 100°C.
- **Package Type:** Surface mount, Case Style DB1627.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.
- **Special Features:**
  - Wide bandwidth (10 to 4000 MHz).
  - Excellent return loss.
  - Aqueous washable.
  - RoHS compliant.

**Typical Applications:**
The TCM2-43X+ is commonly used in:
- **Wideband Push-Pull Amplifiers:** Enhancing signal integrity and performance in amplification circuits.
- **Cellular Systems:** Supporting RF signal processing in mobile communication devices.
- **PCS (Personal Communications Service):** Facilitating communication in personal mobile devices.

This transformer is ideal for applications requiring balanced transmission lines and is particularly useful in RF signal processing where low insertion loss and minimal amplitude and phase unbalance are critical.
