### Component Summary: BSS8402DW-7-F

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - N-Channel (Q1): Drain-Source Voltage (BVDSS) = 60V
  - P-Channel (Q2): Drain-Source Voltage (BVDSS) = -50V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - N-Channel (Q1): Continuous Drain Current (ID) = 115mA
  - P-Channel (Q2): Continuous Drain Current (ID) = -130mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Power Dissipation (PD) = 200mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG) = -55°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - SOT363
- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance (RDS(ON)): 13.5Ω (N-Channel) at VGS = 10V; 10Ω (P-Channel) at VGS = -5V
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Low Input Capacitance
  - Complementary Pair Configuration
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The BSS8402DW is a complementary pair enhancement mode MOSFET, consisting of an N-channel and a P-channel MOSFET. It is designed to minimize on-state resistance while maintaining superior switching performance, making it suitable for high-efficiency power management applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- General-purpose interfacing switches
- Power management functions
- Analog switches

This component is particularly useful in applications where low on-resistance and fast switching are critical, such as in power management circuits, signal routing, and automotive electronics. Its compliance with automotive standards and RoHS directives makes it a reliable choice for both consumer and automotive applications.
