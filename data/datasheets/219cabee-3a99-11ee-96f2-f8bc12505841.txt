### Component Summary: MA4SW210

**Manufacturer:** M/A-COM Technology Solutions Inc.  
**Product Code:** MA4SW210  
**Type:** HMIC™ SPDT Silicon PIN Diode Switch

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Reverse Voltage: 50 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Bias Current per Port: ±50 mA @ +25°C, ±20 mA @ +85°C
- **Power Consumption:**
  - RF CW Incident Power: 33 dBm @ +85°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating: -65°C to +125°C
  - Storage: -65°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature: +175°C
- **Package Type:** Gel Pack
- **Special Features:**
  - Broad bandwidth (50 MHz to 20 GHz, usable up to 26.5 GHz)
  - Low insertion loss and high isolation
  - Fully monolithic, glass-encapsulated chip
  - RoHS compliant
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The MA4SW210 is a series-shunt, broadband PIN diode switch designed using MACOM's proprietary HMICTM (Heterolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) technology. This technology allows for the integration of silicon components into a low-loss, low-dispersion glass substrate, resulting in a compact and efficient switch. The device is characterized by low insertion loss and high isolation, making it suitable for high-frequency applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The MA4SW210 is primarily used in:
- **Aerospace & Defense:** For applications requiring reliable and high-performance RF switching.
- **ISM (Industrial, Scientific, and Medical):** In systems that operate within the ISM bands, where efficient RF switching is critical.

This component is ideal for moderate power applications and offers superior performance compared to traditional discrete component designs, making it a preferred choice in advanced RF systems.
