### Component Summary: WP914CK/4GDT

**Description:**
The WP914CK/4GDT is a green light-emitting diode (LED) manufactured by Kingbright. It is designed with a quad-level configuration, making it suitable for applications where space-saving is essential. The LED emits light in the green spectrum and is constructed using Gallium Phosphide (GaP) material.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Forward Voltage (VF) at IF = 10mA: 2.0V (typical) to 2.4V (maximum)
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 5V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Forward Current (IF): 25mA
  - Peak Forward Current (IFM): 140mA
  - Reverse Current (IR) at VR = 5V: ≤ 10µA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 62.5 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Operating Temperature (Top): -40°C to +85°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40°C to +85°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): 110°C

- **Package Type:**
  - 2 x 3 mm Quad-Level Circuit Board Indicator

- **Special Features:**
  - Quad-level design for space efficiency
  - Black case for enhanced contrast ratio
  - RoHS compliant
  - Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) threshold: 8000V (HBM)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - According to JEDEC J-STD-020E, specific moisture sensitivity level is not explicitly mentioned in the provided text.

**Typical Applications:**
The WP914CK/4GDT is commonly used in various applications, including:
- Status indicators for devices
- Illuminators for displays and signage
- Decorative and entertainment lighting
- Commercial and residential architectural lighting

This LED is particularly suitable for applications where visual signaling is required, and its compact design allows for integration into space-constrained environments. The green light output makes it ideal for signaling and decorative purposes.
