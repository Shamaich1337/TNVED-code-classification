### Component Summary: BZT52C2V7

**Manufacturer:** Diotec Semiconductor  
**Product Code:** BZT52C2V7  
**Document Number:** DS18004 Rev. 38 - 2  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Zener Voltage (VZ): 2.7V (Nominal)
  - Minimum Zener Voltage: 2.5V
  - Maximum Zener Voltage: 2.9V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Test Current (IZT): 5 mA
  - Maximum Reverse Current (IR): 1.0 mA at VZ

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 500 mW at TL = +75°C; 370 mW at TA = +25°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65°C to +150°C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD123 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Planar Die Construction
  - General Purpose, Medium Current
  - Lead-Free and Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 Standards for High Reliability
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 1 per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The BZT52C2V7 is a surface mount Zener diode designed for voltage regulation applications. It provides a stable reference voltage of 2.7V, making it suitable for various electronic circuits that require voltage clamping or regulation. The device is constructed using planar die technology, ensuring reliability and performance in a compact form factor.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Voltage Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Signal Clamping:** Protects sensitive components from voltage spikes by clamping excess voltage.
- **Reference Voltage Generation:** Provides a stable reference voltage for analog circuits.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its qualification to AEC-Q101 standards, it is suitable for automotive electronics.

This Zener diode is particularly useful in applications where space is limited, and reliability is critical, such as in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.
