- **Power Consumption:**
  - Single power supply of 3.3V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Operating Temperature: -40°C to 85°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Small Form-factor Pluggable (SFP) with LC Simplex Connector

- **Special Features:**
  - RoHS compliant
  - SFF8472 diagnostic monitoring interface
  - Hot pluggable
  - Class 1 laser product compliant with EN 60825-1
  - TTL signal detect indicator

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The LS48-A3U-TI-N49-D5 is a single-mode bi-directional SFP transceiver designed for optical communication. It operates at wavelengths of 1490 nm for transmission and 1550 nm for reception, utilizing a single fiber for both sending and receiving data. The device supports data rates of up to 155 Mbps and is equipped with diagnostic monitoring capabilities, making it suitable for various networking applications.

#### Typical Applications:
This transceiver is typically used in:
- **Telecommunications:** For ATM/SONET OC-3/SDH STM-1 and Fast Ethernet applications.
- **Data Communication:** In environments requiring reliable and high-speed data transmission over long distances (up to 120 km).
- **Networking Equipment:** Such as switches and routers, where bi-directional communication is essential.
- **Diagnostic Monitoring:** Due to its built-in diagnostic features, it is useful in network management and monitoring systems.

This component is ideal for applications that require robust performance in harsh environments, given its wide operating temperature range and compliance with safety standards.
