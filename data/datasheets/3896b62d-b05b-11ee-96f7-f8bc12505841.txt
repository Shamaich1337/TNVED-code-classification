### Component Summary: AMG900G1200MED

**Description:**
The AMG900G1200MED is a 1200V, 900A Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) module designed for high-power applications. It utilizes MPT FS Technology (trench) to achieve low losses and features a short tail current and low reverse-recovery loss, making it suitable for efficient power management.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCES): 1200 V
  - Gate-Emitter Voltage (VGES): ±20 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 900 A (DC, at TC=100 °C, Tvj=175 °C)
  - Repetitive Peak Collector Current (ICRM): 1800 A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Collector-Emitter Saturation Voltage (VCE(sat)): 1.5 V @ 25 °C
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 to +125 °C
  - Junction Operating Temperature (TvJop): -40 to +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - Module with a weight of 345 g
- **Special Features:**
  - Low losses and short tail current
  - Low reverse-recovery loss
  - Isolation voltage: 4 kV (RMS, 50Hz, 1 min)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Automobile Motor Drives:** Used in electric and hybrid vehicles for efficient motor control.
- **Solar Applications:** Employed in inverters for solar power systems to convert DC to AC.
- **UPS Systems:** Utilized in uninterruptible power supplies for reliable power management.
- **Frequency Converters:** Applied in systems that require conversion between different frequency levels.

This IGBT module is ideal for high-efficiency applications where high voltage and current handling capabilities are essential, making it a critical component in modern power electronics.
