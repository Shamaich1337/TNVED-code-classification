### Component Summary: LD1117S33TR

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** LD1117S33TR  
**Description:** The LD1117 is a low-dropout (LDO) voltage regulator that provides a fixed output voltage of 3.3 V. It is capable of delivering up to 800 mA of output current and is available in various package types, including SOT-223, DPAK, SO-8, and TO-220.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (V_IN): Up to 18 V (when I_OUT is lower than 20 mA), typically 15 V.
  - Output Voltage (V_OUT): Fixed at 3.3 V.
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (I_OUT): Up to 800 mA.
  
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: Typically 5-10 mA depending on the input voltage.
  
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -40 to +150 °C.
  - Operating Junction Temperature: 0 to +125 °C (standard version), -40 to +125 °C (C version).
  
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT-223, DPAK, SO-8, and TO-220 packages.
  
- **Special Features:**
  - Low dropout voltage: 1 V (typical).
  - Supply voltage rejection: 75 dB (typical).
  - Internal current and thermal limit protection.
  - Tight output voltage tolerance: ±1% at 25 °C.
  - Requires only a 10 µF minimum capacitor for stability.
  
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E is not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The LD1117 is a low-dropout linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with minimal input-output voltage differential. It is particularly useful in applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical. The device features an internal NPN pass transistor, which allows for lower quiescent current and improved performance compared to PNP-based regulators.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to provide stable voltage levels for various electronic components.
- **Signal Processing:** Ideal for powering analog circuits that require a clean and stable voltage supply.
- **Data Storage:** Commonly used in devices that require regulated power for memory and storage components.
- **SCSI-2 Active Termination:** The 2.85 V performance is suitable for SCSI-2 active termination applications.
