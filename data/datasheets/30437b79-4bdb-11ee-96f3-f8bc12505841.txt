### Component Summary: CDRH2D18/HPNP-100NC

**Description:**
The CDRH2D18/HPNP-100NC is a surface-mount power inductor manufactured by Sumida. It features a ferrite drum core construction and is magnetically shielded, making it suitable for applications where space is limited and electromagnetic interference needs to be minimized. The inductor is designed for use in DC-DC converters, particularly in portable electronic devices.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Inductance:** 10.0 μH (±30%)
- **D.C. Resistance (DCR):** 245 mΩ (typical) at 20°C
- **Saturation Current:** 0.85 A (max), 0.65 A (typ) at 100°C
- **Temperature Rise Current:** 0.65 A (max), 0.90 A (typ) at 100°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +105°C (including coil’s self-temperature rise)
- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40°C to +105°C
- **Solder Reflow Temperature:** 260°C (peak)
- **Package Type:** SMD (Surface Mount Device)
- **Dimensions:** 3.2 mm x 3.2 mm x 2.0 mm (L x W x H)
- **Weight:** 65 mg (approx.)
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):** 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **RoHS Compliance:** Yes

**Typical Applications:**
The CDRH2D18/HPNP-100NC is ideally used in various portable electronic devices such as:
- Mobile phones
- Personal Digital Assistants (PDAs)
- MP3 players
- Digital Still Cameras (DSC)
- Digital Video Cameras (DVC)

It serves primarily as a DC-DC converter inductor, helping to regulate voltage and current in power management circuits, ensuring efficient energy conversion and stable operation of electronic devices.
