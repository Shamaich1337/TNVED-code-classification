**Type:** N-channel Depletion Mode SIPMOS® Small-Signal Transistor  
**Package Type:** PG-SOT223  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): 200 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): ±20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 25°C: 0.66 A
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at 70°C: 0.53 A
  - Pulsed Drain Current (ID,pulse): 2.6 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot) at 25°C: 1.8 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to 150°C

- **On-State Resistance:**
  - Maximum RDS(on): 3.5 Ω (at VGS=0 V, ID=70 mA)

  - MSL according to JEDEC J-STD-020E: Not specified in the provided text.

#### Special Features:

- Pb-free lead plating; RoHS compliant
- Qualified according to AEC Q101
- Halogen-free according to IEC61249-2-21
- Available with VGS(th) indicator on reel

### Description:
The BSP149 is an N-channel depletion mode SIPMOS® small-signal transistor designed for various electronic applications. It operates by allowing current to flow from the drain to the source when a negative gate-source voltage is applied. This transistor is particularly noted for its low on-state resistance and high voltage ratings, making it suitable for switching and amplification tasks in electronic circuits.

### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient switching and control.
- **Signal Processing:** Suitable for analog signal amplification and processing in various electronic devices.
- **Automotive Applications:** Qualified according to AEC Q101, making it suitable for automotive electronics.
- **General Switching Applications:** Can be used in various electronic circuits requiring reliable switching capabilities.
