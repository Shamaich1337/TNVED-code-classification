### Component Summary: ZTX658STZ

**Description:**
The ZTX658STZ is an NPN silicon planar medium power high voltage transistor manufactured by Diodes Incorporated. It is designed for applications requiring high voltage and moderate current handling capabilities. This transistor is particularly suitable for use in telephone dialer circuits and other similar applications.

**Key Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 400 V
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 400 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Collector Current (IC): 500 mA
  - Peak Pulse Current (ICM): 1 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (Ptot) at 25°C: 1 W
  - Derating above 25°C: 5.7 mW/°C

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 to +200 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to +200 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - TO-92 compatible package

- **Special Features:**
  - High voltage capability (400 V)
  - Low saturation voltage (VCE(sat) typically 0.25 V at 50 mA)
  - High transition frequency (fT = 50 MHz)

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically TO-92 packages are not moisture sensitive.

### Typical Applications:
The ZTX658STZ is commonly used in:
- Telephone dialer circuits
- Signal amplification in high voltage applications
- Switching applications where moderate current and high voltage are required

This transistor is ideal for applications that demand reliability and performance in high voltage environments, making it suitable for various consumer electronics and communication devices.
