### Component Summary: TPS3619-33DGK

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VDD): 1.65V to 5.5V
  - Backup Battery Voltage (VBAT): 1.5V to 5.5V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Supply Current (IDD): 40 µA (max)
  - Battery Supply Current (IBAT): 100 nA (max)
  - Continuous Output Current (IO): 300 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Supply Current: 40 µA (max)
  - Battery Supply Current: 100 nA (max)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - 8-Pin MSOP (DGK)
- **Special Features:**
  - Precision Supply Voltage Monitor for 3.3V and 5V
  - Backup-Battery Switchover for data retention
  - Power-On Reset Generator with a fixed 100 ms reset delay
  - Battery Freshness Seal to prevent battery discharge until needed
  - Pin-for-pin compatible with MAX819, MAX703, and MAX704
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TPS3619-33DGK is a supervisory circuit designed to monitor and control processor activity, particularly in applications requiring backup battery support for data retention. It provides a precision voltage monitoring function, ensuring that the system resets appropriately when the supply voltage drops below a specified threshold. The device features a backup-battery switchover mechanism that allows for seamless transition to battery power when the main supply fails, thus preserving the contents of RAM.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** The TPS3619-33 is commonly used in systems that require reliable power management, ensuring that critical data is retained during power interruptions.
- **Data Retention:** It is ideal for applications involving CMOS RAM where data integrity is crucial during power failures.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Typical applications include portable battery-powered devices, set-top boxes, advanced voice mail systems, automotive systems, and point-of-sale equipment.
- **Computer Equipment:** It is also used in various computer systems to ensure proper reset and power management during operation.

This component is particularly valuable in designs where low power consumption and reliable operation across a wide temperature range are essential.
