### Component Summary: HMC241LP3ETR (HMC241QS16 / HMC241QS16E)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Vdd (Supply Voltage): +5.0 Vdc ± 10%
  - Control Voltage (A & B): -0.5V to Vdd + 1 Vdc

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Idd (Typical): 4.0 mA
  - Idd (Maximum): 7.0 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Input Power: 
    - +20 dBm (0.05 - 0.5 GHz)
    - +27 dBm (0.5 - 3.5 GHz)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 to +150 °C
  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - 16 Lead QSOP (Low Stress Injection Molded Plastic)

- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated 2:4 TTL Decoder
  - Low Insertion Loss: 0.5 dB at 2 GHz
  - High Isolation: 40 dB at 1 GHz
  - RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (Max peak reflow temperature of 235 °C for HMC241QS16, 260 °C for HMC241QS16E)

#### Description:
The HMC241LP3ETR is a GaAs MMIC (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuit) SP4T (Single Pole, Four Throw) non-reflective switch designed for RF applications. It operates over a frequency range of DC to 3.5 GHz and is characterized by low insertion loss and high isolation, making it suitable for various communication systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Base Stations & Portable Wireless:** Used in mobile communication systems to switch RF signals efficiently.
- **CATV / DBS (Cable Television / Direct Broadcast Satellite):** Facilitates signal routing in cable and satellite systems.
- **Wireless Local Loop:** Provides connectivity in wireless telecommunication networks.
- **Test Equipment:** Employed in RF testing setups for signal routing and switching.

This component is ideal for applications requiring reliable signal switching with minimal loss and high performance across a wide frequency range.
