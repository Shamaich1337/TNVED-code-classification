### Component Summary: TQP7M9104

**Description:**
The TQP7M9104 is a 2W high-linearity amplifier designed for wireless infrastructure applications. It operates within a frequency range of 600 to 2700 MHz and is built using InGaP/GaAs HBT technology. This amplifier is particularly suited for use in transceiver line cards and high-power amplifiers in multi-carrier 3G/4G base stations.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): +5 V (max +5.25 V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Quiescent Collector Current (Icq): 435 mA
  - Reference Current (Iref): 19 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Consumption: 2.175 W (at 435 mA and +5 V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Case Temperature (Tcase): -40°C to +85°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to 170°C for >106 hours MTTF

- **Package Type:**
  - 24-pin QFN (Quad Flat No-lead) package, dimensions 4 mm x 4 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - High linearity with +49.5 dBm Output IP3
  - +32.8 dBm P1dB at 2.14 GHz
  - Internal RF overdrive protection
  - Internal DC overvoltage protection
  - Internal active bias
  - On-chip ESD protection
  - Shutdown capability
  - Capable of handling 10:1 VSWR at +5 VCC

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 (IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020)

**Typical Applications:**
The TQP7M9104 is primarily used in:
- Wireless infrastructure, including base transceiver stations (BTS)
- Repeaters
- High-power amplifiers for CDMA/WCDMA/LTE applications
- General-purpose wireless communication systems

This amplifier is designed to deliver high performance in terms of gain and linearity, making it ideal for applications that require robust signal amplification with minimal distortion.
