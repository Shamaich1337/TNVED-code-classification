### Component Summary: NC7SZ125M5X

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 V to 6.0 V
  - Input Voltage: -0.5 V to 6.0 V
  - Output Voltage: -0.5 V to 6.0 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Drive Current: ±24 mA at 3 V VCC
  - DC Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Input Leakage Current: ±1 µA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 2 µA (typical) at VCC = 5.5 V
  - Power Dissipation: 
    - SOT-23: 200 mW
    - SC70: 150 mW
    - MicroPak: 130 mW

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages: 
    - 5-Lead SOT23
    - 5-Lead SC70
    - 6-Lead MicroPak™
    - 6-Lead MicroPak2

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Ultra-high speed with a typical propagation delay (t_PD) of 2.6 ns into 50 pF at 5 V VCC.
  - High output drive capability with power-down high-impedance inputs/outputs.
  - Over-voltage tolerance inputs facilitate 5 V to 3 V translation.
  - Proprietary noise/EMI reduction circuitry.
  - Inputs and outputs are high impedance when VCC is 0 V.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Not specified in the provided text.

**Description:**
The NC7SZ125 is a single buffer with a three-state output from ON Semiconductor's TinyLogic® Ultra-High Speed (UHS) family. It is designed using advanced CMOS technology, allowing for ultra-high speed operation while maintaining low static power dissipation. The device is capable of functioning over a broad voltage range and features high impedance states when powered down.

**Typical Applications:**
The NC7SZ125 is commonly used in applications requiring:
- **Signal buffering**: To strengthen signals in digital circuits.
- **Data translation**: Facilitating communication between devices operating at different voltage levels (e.g., 5 V to 3 V).
- **High-speed data processing**: Ideal for applications in telecommunications, computing, and consumer electronics where fast signal processing is critical.
- **Power management**: Used in systems where power efficiency is essential, particularly in battery-operated devices.

This component is suitable for a variety of applications in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial controls, where high-speed and reliable signal integrity are paramount.
