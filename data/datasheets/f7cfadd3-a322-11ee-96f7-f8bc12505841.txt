regulation topology, thatcombines theadvantages of\nhysteretic, voltage mode andcurrent mode control including anACloop directly associated totheoutput voltage.\nThis control loop takes information about output voltage changes andfeeds itdirectly toafastcomparator stage.\nItsets theswitching frequency, which isconstant forsteady state operating conditions, andprovides immediate\nresponse todynamic load changes. ### Component Summary: TPS62160DSGR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: 3V to 17V
  - Adjustable Output Voltage Range: 0.9V to 6V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 1A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Current: Typically 17 µA (when not switching)
  - Shutdown Current: Less than 2 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature: -40°C to 125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in 8-Pin WSON (2.00 mm x 2.00 mm) and 8-Pin VSSOP (3.00 mm x 3.00 mm)
- **Special Features:**
  - DCS-Control™ topology for high efficiency
  - Seamless transition to power save mode
  - Short circuit protection and thermal shutdown
  - Power good output for voltage monitoring
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The **TPS62160** is a synchronous step-down DC/DC converter designed for high power density applications. It utilizes DCS-Control™ topology, which combines the benefits of voltage mode and current mode control, allowing for fast transient response and high output voltage accuracy. The device is optimized for applications requiring efficient power conversion from a wide input voltage range, making it suitable for battery-powered devices and systems with 12V power rails.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:**
  - Used in standard 12V rail supplies and point-of-load (POL) supplies from single or multiple Li-Ion batteries.
- **Embedded Systems:**
  - Ideal for powering embedded systems, digital cameras, mobile PCs, and tablet PCs.
- **LDO Replacement:**
  - Can replace linear regulators (LDOs) in applications where efficiency is critical.

The TPS62160 is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited and efficiency is paramount, making it a versatile choice for modern electronic designs.
