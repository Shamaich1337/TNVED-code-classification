### Component Summary: NSR01L30NXT5G

**Description:**
The NSR01L30NXT5G is a Schottky Barrier Diode designed for applications requiring low forward voltage drop and low leakage current. It is housed in a DSN2 (Dual Silicon No-lead) package, which allows for efficient use of board space and improved thermal performance. This diode is particularly suitable for high-speed switching applications.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 30 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Forward Current (IF): 100 mA
  - Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 4.0 A (60 Hz, 1 cycle)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Power Dissipation (PD): 312 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -40 to +125 °C
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): +150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DSN2 (0201) package
- **Special Features:**
  - Very low forward voltage drop: 400 mV @ 10 mA
  - Low reverse current: 0.2 µA @ 10 V
  - ESD Rating: Class 3B (Human Body Model), Class C (Machine Model)
  - Low capacitance: CT = 7 pF
  - Halide-free and Pb-free device
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

**Typical Applications:**
- LCD and keypad backlighting
- Camera photo flash
- Buck and boost DC-DC converters
- Reverse voltage and current protection
- Clamping and protection circuits

**Markets:**
- Mobile handsets
- MP3 players
- Digital cameras and camcorders
- Notebook PCs and PDAs
- GPS devices

This diode is optimized for applications where space is limited and efficiency is critical, making it ideal for modern electronic devices that require reliable performance in compact designs.
