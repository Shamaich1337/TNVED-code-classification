### Component Summary: LM139AWG/883

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage: 2 to 36 V DC or ±1 to ±18 V DC
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: 36 V DC
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current: 0.8 mA (independent of supply voltage)
  - Output Sink Current: 20 mA (approx.)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Power Dissipation: 
    - LCCC: 1250 mW
    - CDIP: 1200 mW
    - CLGA: 680 mW
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - Available in various packages including CDIP (14-pin), LCCC, and CLGA.
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low input offset voltage (±1 mV max)
  - Low input bias current (25 nA)
  - Radiation-hardened version available (Total Ionizing Dose: 100 krad (Si))
  - Compatible with TTL, DTL, ECL, and CMOS logic systems.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The LM139AWG/883 is a low-power, low-offset voltage quad comparator designed for precision voltage comparison applications. It consists of four independent comparators that can operate from a single power supply over a wide range of voltages. The device is particularly noted for its low power consumption and ability to interface directly with various logic families, making it suitable for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Limit Comparators**: Used to compare input voltages against predefined thresholds.
- **Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADC)**: Acts as a comparator stage in ADC circuits.
- **Pulse and Square Wave Generators**: Used in timing applications and signal processing.
- **VCOs (Voltage-Controlled Oscillators)**: Employed in frequency modulation and signal generation.
- **Timers and Multivibrators**: Utilized in timing circuits for generating precise time delays.
- **High Voltage Digital Logic Gates**: Can be used in high voltage applications due to its wide voltage range.

The LM139 series is particularly advantageous in applications requiring high precision and low power consumption, making it ideal for both consumer electronics and military applications where reliability and performance are critical.
