### Component Summary: BAT1503WE6327HTSA1 (BAT15-03W)

**Description:**
The BAT15-03W is a single silicon RF Schottky diode produced by Infineon Technologies. It is designed as a low barrier N-type device with an integrated guard ring for over-voltage protection. This diode is characterized by its low barrier height, low forward voltage, and low junction capacitance, making it suitable for high-frequency applications, particularly in mixer and detector functions up to 12 GHz.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): -4 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 4 V (at IR = 100 μA)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Forward Current (IF): 110 mA
  - Reverse Current (IR): 5 μA (at VR = 1 V)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PTOT): 100 mW (TS ≤ 78 °C)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOD323 (Dimensions: 2.5 mm x 1.25 mm x 0.9 mm)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low inductance (LS): 1.8 nH (typical)
  - Low capacitance (C): 0.28 pF (typical at 1 MHz)
  - Pb-free, RoHS compliant, and halogen-free
  - ESD sensitive device; handling precautions required

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - According to JEDEC J-STD-020E, the device is classified as moisture sensitive.

**Typical Applications:**
The BAT15-03W is primarily used in:
- Mixers and detectors in RF applications
- Sensor interfaces for security systems
- Telematic systems
- Compensators
- Radar systems for industrial use

This diode's characteristics make it ideal for applications requiring high-frequency signal processing and low power consumption, particularly in environments where space is limited due to its compact SOD323 package.
