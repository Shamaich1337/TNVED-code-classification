### Component Summary: TPS7A3001MDGNTEP

**Manufacturer:** Texas Instruments  
**Product Code:** TPS7A3001MDGNTEP  
**Description:** The TPS7A3001 is a negative, high-voltage, ultralow-noise linear regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with minimal noise and high power-supply rejection. It is capable of sourcing a maximum load of 200mA and is particularly suited for high-precision analog applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage Range: –3V to –36V
  - Adjustable Output Voltage Range: –1.18V to –35V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Output Current: 200mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Ground Current: 55μA (IOUT = 0mA), 950μA (IOUT = 100mA)
  - Shutdown Supply Current: 1.0 to 3.0μA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - –55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - MSOP-8 with PowerPAD
- **Special Features:**
  - Ultralow noise: 15.1μVRMS (10Hz to 100kHz)
  - High Power-Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR): 72dB (at 120Hz)
  - Built-in current limit and thermal shutdown protection
  - CMOS logic-level-compatible enable pin
  - Stable with ceramic capacitors ≥2.2μF
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Level 2, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The TPS7A3001 is a high-performance negative linear voltage regulator that utilizes bipolar technology to achieve low noise and high PSRR. It features an adjustable output voltage and is equipped with a soft-start function that can be programmed with an external capacitor. The device is designed to operate in demanding environments, making it suitable for military, aerospace, and medical applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **High-Precision Analog Circuitry:** Ideal for powering operational amplifiers, digital-to-analog converters (DACs), and analog-to-digital converters (ADCs) where clean voltage rails are critical.
- **Post DC/DC Converter Regulation:** Used to filter out output voltage ripple from DC/DC converters, enhancing performance in sensitive instrumentation and test equipment.
- **Audio Applications:** Provides low-noise power supplies for audio systems, ensuring minimal distortion and high fidelity.
- **Industrial Instrumentation:** Suitable for applications requiring stable and precise voltage regulation in harsh environments.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the TPS7A3001MDGNTEP, making it a valuable component for engineers and designers working on high-performance electronic systems.
