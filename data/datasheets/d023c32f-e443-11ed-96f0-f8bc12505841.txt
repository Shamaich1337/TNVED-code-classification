  - Continuous Output Current: ±50mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Supply Current (ICC): 10μA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to 125°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in multiple packages: SOT-23 (DBV), SC70 (DCK), SON (DRY, DSF), DSBGA (YZP)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Dual Open-Drain Buffer Configuration
  - Supports Voltage Translation (up and down)
  - Low Power Consumption
  - Ioff supports live insertion and partial-power-down mode
  - Latch-Up Performance Exceeds 100mA per JESD 78, Class II
  - ESD Protection: 2000V HBM, 200V MM, 1000V CDM

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level 1 per JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The SN74LVC2G07 is a dual buffer and driver with open-drain outputs designed for operation within a voltage range of 1.65V to 5.5V. It features a dual open-drain buffer configuration, allowing it to connect with other open-drain outputs to implement active-low wired-OR or active-high wired-AND functions. The device is capable of sinking up to 32mA of current, making it suitable for high-drive applications. The NanoFree™ packaging technology allows for a compact design, utilizing the die as the package.

#### Typical Applications:
The SN74LVC2G07 is commonly used in various applications, including:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Blu-ray players, DVD recorders, digital video cameras, and portable media players.
- **Computing Devices:** Desktops, notebook PCs, and embedded PCs.
- **Navigation Devices:** GPS and personal navigation devices.
- **Networking Equipment:** Network projectors and front-end devices.
- **High-Definition Displays:** Used in HDTVs and DLP front projection systems.
- **Storage Solutions:** Solid State Drives (SSDs) for enterprise applications.

This component is ideal for applications requiring signal buffering, voltage level translation, and interfacing with high-speed digital circuits while maintaining low power consumption.
