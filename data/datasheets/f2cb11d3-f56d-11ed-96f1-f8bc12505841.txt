### Component Summary: NC7SZ04P5X

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (V_CC): 1.65 V to 5.5 V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: -0.5 V to 6.5 V
  - Input/Output Voltage: -0.5 V to 6.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Drive Current: ±24 mA at 3 V V_CC
  - DC Output Current: ±50 mA
  - Input Leakage Current: ±1 µA (max)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Quiescent Supply Current: 2.0 µA (typical) at V_CC = 5.5 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature: -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Junction Temperature: -40 °C to +85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SC-88A (SOT-353)

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Ultra-high speed with a typical propagation delay (t_PD) of 2.4 ns into 50 pF at 5 V V_CC.
  - High output drive capability and power-down high-impedance inputs/outputs.
  - Over-voltage tolerant inputs for 5 V to 3 V translation.
  - Proprietary noise/EMI reduction circuitry.
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS compliant.

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (JEDEC J-STD-020E):**
  - Level 1 (not moisture sensitive).

**Description of the Component:**
The NC7SZ04 is a single inverter from onsemi’s Ultra-High Speed (UHS) series of TinyLogic devices. It is designed using advanced CMOS technology to achieve ultra-high speed performance while maintaining low static power dissipation across a wide operating voltage range. The device features high-impedance inputs and outputs when V_CC is at 0 V, making it versatile for various applications.

**Typical Applications:**
The NC7SZ04 is commonly used in:
- **Signal Processing:** As an inverter in digital circuits to manipulate signal levels.
- **Level Shifting:** Facilitating voltage translation between different logic levels (e.g., 5 V to 3 V).
- **Power Management:** In low-power applications where minimal static power consumption is critical.
- **Noise Reduction:** In circuits requiring EMI reduction due to its proprietary circuitry.

This component is ideal for applications in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and any digital logic design requiring high-speed operation and low power consumption.
