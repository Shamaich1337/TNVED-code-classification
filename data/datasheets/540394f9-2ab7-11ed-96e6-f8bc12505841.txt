### Component Summary: LDK320AM-R

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** LDK320AM-R  
**Type:** Low Dropout (LDO) Voltage Regulator

#### Key Specifications:
- **Input Voltage Range:** 2.5 V to 18 V
- **Output Current:** Up to 200 mA
- **Dropout Voltage:** 100 mV typical at 100 mA load
- **Quiescent Current:** 60 μA typical; 1 μA in off mode
- **Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR):** 88 dB at 120 Hz
- **Output Voltage Tolerance:** ±0.5% at 25 °C (fixed version)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 °C to 125 °C
- **Package Type:** Available in SOT23-5L and SOT-89
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.

#### Description:
The LDK320AM-R is a high-accuracy low-dropout voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage with minimal input-output voltage differential. It is capable of delivering up to 200 mA of output current while maintaining a low dropout voltage of 100 mV, making it suitable for battery-powered applications where efficiency is critical. The device features a logic-controlled enable pin for shutdown mode, which reduces power consumption to less than 1 μA.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Digital Still Cameras (DSC)**
- **Televisions (TV)**
- **Blu-ray and DVD players**
- **Personal Computers (PC)**
- **Industrial applications**

The LDK320AM-R is particularly well-suited for low-power applications due to its low quiescent current and ability to operate with ceramic capacitors, which are often used in compact designs. Its high PSRR and low noise characteristics make it ideal for sensitive electronic devices that require stable power supply without interference.
