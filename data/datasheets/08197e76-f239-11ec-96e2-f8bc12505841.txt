### Component Summary: SS56 Schottky Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SS56 (part of the SS52 to SS520 series)  
**Type:** Surface Mount Schottky Rectifier  
**Package Type:** DO-214AB (SMC)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM):** 60 V
- **Average Rectified Output Current (IO):** 5.0 A (at 60Hz sine wave, resistive load)
- **Forward Surge Current (IFSM):** 100 A (non-repetitive, 60Hz half-sine wave, 1 cycle)
- **Maximum Forward Voltage Drop (VF):** 0.70 V (at IFM = 5.0 A)
- **Maximum DC Reverse Current (IR):** 
  - 0.2 mA at Ta = 25°C
  - 20 mA at Ta = 100°C
- **Storage Temperature (Tstg):** -55°C to +150°C
- **Junction Temperature (Tj):** -55°C to +125°C
- **Thermal Resistance (RθJ-A):** 47°C/W (junction to ambient)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The SS56 is a Schottky rectifier designed for high-frequency applications. Schottky rectifiers are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them ideal for applications where efficiency is critical. The SS56 features a guard ring for overvoltage protection, ensuring reliability in various operating conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Low Voltage High Frequency Inverters:** Used in power conversion applications where efficiency and speed are essential.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Commonly employed in inductive load circuits to prevent voltage spikes.
- **DC/DC Converters:** Utilized in power management systems to convert DC voltage levels efficiently.
- **Polarity Protection:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections, ensuring device safety.

This component is suitable for a variety of electronic applications, particularly in power management and signal processing, where low power losses and high efficiency are required.
