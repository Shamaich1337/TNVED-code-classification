### Component Summary: 2N7002-7-F

**Manufacturer:** Diotec Semiconductor  
**Product Type:** N-Channel Enhancement Mode Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET)  
**Document Number:** DS11303 Rev. 38 - 2  
**Date:** December 2020  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (BVDSS): 60 V
  - Drain-Gate Voltage (VDGR): 60 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGSS): ±20 V (Continuous), ±40 V (Pulsed)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID) at VGS = 10V:
    - 210 mA (TA = +25°C)
    - 150 mA (TA = +85°C)
    - 135 mA (TA = +100°C)
  - Maximum Continuous Body Diode Forward Current (IS): 0.2 A (Continuous), 0.5 A (Pulsed)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (PD): 370 mW (standard), 540 mW (with specific PCB conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -55 to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (Surface Mount)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low On-Resistance (RDS(ON)): 5 Ω at VGS = 10V
  - Fast Switching Speed
  - Low Input Capacitance
  - Lead-Free and Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: Level 1 per J-STD-020

#### Description:
The **2N7002-7-F** is an N-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for high-efficiency power management applications. It features low on-state resistance and superior switching performance, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Motor Control:** Used in driving motors efficiently.
- **Power Management Functions:** Ideal for applications requiring efficient power regulation and management.
- **Signal Switching:** Can be utilized in signal processing circuits due to its fast switching capabilities.

This component is particularly beneficial in automotive applications, as it meets AEC-Q100/101/200 standards for high reliability. It is also suitable for general-purpose switching applications in consumer electronics and industrial equipment.
