#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 6.3 V to 2000 V
- **Capacitance Range**: 0.2 pF to 680 nF
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55 °C to +125 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: MSL 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Package Type**: 0603 (1.6 mm x 0.8 mm)
- **Special Features**:
  - Automotive grade
  - AEC-Q200 qualified
  - Lead-free terminations
  - High reliability with 100% automatic optical inspection prior to taping

#### Description:
The AC0603KRX7R9BB223 is a surface-mount multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) manufactured by YAGEO. It utilizes X7R dielectric material, which provides a stable capacitance over a wide temperature range and is suitable for various applications. The capacitor is designed for automotive applications, ensuring high reliability and performance under demanding conditions.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Electronics**: Used in power management systems, signal processing, and filtering applications within automotive circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Suitable for general-purpose applications in consumer devices where space-saving and reliability are critical.
- **Industrial Equipment**: Employed in various industrial applications requiring stable capacitance and high voltage ratings.
- **Telecommunications**: Utilized in communication devices for decoupling and filtering purposes.

This capacitor is ideal for applications that require compact size, high voltage tolerance, and robust performance, particularly in automotive and industrial environments.
