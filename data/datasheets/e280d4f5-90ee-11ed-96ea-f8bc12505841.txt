### Component Summary: SS110A Schottky Rectifier

**Manufacturer:** Yangzhou Yangjie Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.  
**Product Code:** SS110A (part of the SS12A to SS120A series)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 100V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Output Current (IO): 1.0A
  - Surge (Non-repetitive) Forward Current (IFSM): 30A
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage Drop (VF) at IFM=1.0A: 0.85V (typical for SS110A)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55°C to +150°C
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55°C to +125°C
- **Package Type:**
  - DO-214AC (SMA)
- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package ideal for automated placement
  - Guardring for overvoltage protection
  - Low power losses and high efficiency
  - High forward surge capability
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260°C
- **Flammability Rating:** Molding compound meets UL 94 V-0, RoHS-compliant, halogen-free

#### Description:
The SS110A is a surface mount Schottky rectifier designed for efficient rectification in low voltage, high-frequency applications. Schottky rectifiers are known for their low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities, making them suitable for various power management tasks.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits for efficient rectification.
- **DC/DC Converters:** Ideal for converting DC voltages with minimal losses.
- **Freewheeling Diodes:** Commonly used in inductive load applications to prevent voltage spikes.
- **Polarity Protection:** Protects circuits from reverse polarity connections.

This component is particularly useful in applications where efficiency and thermal performance are critical, such as in battery-powered devices, power adapters, and renewable energy systems.
