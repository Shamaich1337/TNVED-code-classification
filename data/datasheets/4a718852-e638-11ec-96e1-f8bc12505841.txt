- **Storage Temperature**: -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: Radial lead
- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL)**: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features**: RoHS compliant, suitable for high current circuits.

#### Description:
The RLB0914-102KL is a radial lead inductor manufactured by Bourns. It is designed to provide inductance in electronic circuits, specifically for filtering and energy storage applications. The inductance value for this part is 1000 µH with a tolerance of ±10%. Inductors like the RLB0914 series are essential components in power management systems, where they help smooth out voltage fluctuations and store energy in magnetic fields.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supplies**: Used in DC/DC converters and other power management applications to filter and stabilize voltage levels.
- **DC/DC Converters**: Essential for converting voltage levels efficiently while minimizing ripple and noise.
- **General Use**: Suitable for various electronic applications requiring inductance for signal processing or energy storage.

This component is particularly useful in applications where high current handling is necessary, and its robust temperature range makes it suitable for a variety of environments.
