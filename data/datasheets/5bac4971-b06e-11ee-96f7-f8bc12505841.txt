### Component Summary: BC817W,115 (Nexperia)

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Collector-Emitter Voltage (VCEO): 45 V
  - Collector-Base Voltage (VCBO): 50 V
  - Emitter-Base Voltage (VEBO): 5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Collector Current (IC): 500 mA
  - Peak Collector Current (ICM): 1 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)
  - Peak Base Current (IBM): 200 mA (single pulse, t_p ≤ 1 ms)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (Ptot): 200 mW (at T_amb ≤ 25 °C), 290 mW (with specific mounting conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -65 °C to 150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to 150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT323 (SC-70), a very small surface-mounted device (SMD) plastic package with 3 leads.

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - High current capability.
  - Available in three current gain selections (hFE).
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E).

#### Description:
The BC817W is an NPN general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is characterized by its high current handling capability and is packaged in a compact SOT323 (SC-70) format, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. The transistor features a wide range of current gain selections, allowing for flexibility in various circuit designs.
