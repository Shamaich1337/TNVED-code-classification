### Component Summary: PESD36VS1UL

**General Description:**
The PESD36VS1UL is a unidirectional ElectroStatic Discharge (ESD) protection diode designed to safeguard one signal line from ESD and other transient voltage spikes. It is housed in a leadless ultra-small SOD882 surface-mounted device (SMD) package.

---

### Key Specifications:

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Standoff Voltage (VRWM): 36 V
  - Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 39 V (typical at 2 mA)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Current (IPPM): 2.5 A (8/20 µs pulse)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IRM): < 1 nA at VRWM = 36 V

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Rated Peak Pulse Power (PPPM): 150 W

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Ambient Temperature (Tamb): -55 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - DFN1006-2 (SOD882), leadless ultra-small plastic package with 2 terminals.

- **Special Features:**
  - ESD protection up to 30 kV (IEC 61000-4-2)
  - AEC-Q101 qualified for automotive applications
  - Ultra-low leakage current
  - Suitable for unidirectional data line protection

- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically assessed under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

---

### Description and Function:
The PESD36VS1UL is primarily used as an ESD protection diode. Its main function is to protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge, which can lead to permanent damage. The diode clamps the voltage to a safe level, thereby preventing excess voltage from reaching the protected circuit.

---

### Typical Applications:
The PESD36VS1UL is suitable for a variety of applications, including:
- **Computers and Peripherals:** Protecting data lines and interfaces from ESD.
- **Audio and Video Equipment:** Safeguarding signal integrity in consumer electronics.
- **Cellular Handsets and Accessories:** Ensuring reliable operation in mobile devices.
- **Portable Electronics:** Protecting sensitive components in handheld devices.
- **Communication Systems:** Providing ESD protection in data transmission lines.

This component is particularly valuable in environments where devices are frequently connected and disconnected, increasing the risk of ESD events. Its compact size and high ESD protection capability make it ideal for modern electronic designs.
