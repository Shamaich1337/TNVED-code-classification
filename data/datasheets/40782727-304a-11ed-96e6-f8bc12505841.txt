  - Operating Temperature: -40 to +85 °C

  - High isolation: 50 dB at DC to 1 GHz

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

The HMC194AMS8ETR is ideal for various RF applications, including:
- Cellular and PCS base stations
- Portable wireless devices
- MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution Service) and Wireless LAN systems

The device's compatibility with CMOS and TTL logic families allows for easy integration into existing systems.
