  - Fully RoHS compliant
  - 3-year product warranty
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**

It features an ultra-wide input voltage range of 18 to 75 VDC and provides a tightly regulated output voltage of 5 VDC at a maximum current of 400 mA. This converter is designed for high efficiency and reliability, making it suitable for demanding applications.

- **Communication Equipment:** Used in devices requiring stable power supply for signal processing.
- **Instrumentation:** Provides reliable power for measurement and control systems.
- **Industrial Electronics:** Suitable for powering devices in harsh environments due to its wide operating temperature range and robust design.
