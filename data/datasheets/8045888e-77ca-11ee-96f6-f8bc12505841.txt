#### Description:
The HR10A-7R-6S(74) is a high-performance miniature circular connector designed for compact, high-density applications. It features a robust design that allows for reliable electrical connections in various environments. The connector utilizes a single-action push-pull locking mechanism, making it easy to connect and disconnect.

#### Typical Applications:
The HR10A series connectors are typically used in applications requiring reliable connections in limited space, such as:
- **Industrial Equipment**: For connecting sensors and actuators.
- **Telecommunications**: In devices where space and reliability are critical.
- **Medical Devices**: For secure connections in diagnostic and therapeutic equipment.
- **Automotive**: In applications where compact connectors are needed for various electronic systems.

This connector is particularly suitable for environments where vibration and movement may occur, due to its durable construction and locking mechanism.
