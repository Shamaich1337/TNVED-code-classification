  - Rated Voltage (VR): 33V DC
  - Surge Voltage (VS): 1.15 x VR (for VR ≤ 315V), 1.10 x VR (for VR > 315V)
  
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Maximum Ripple Current (IR) at +105°C: 2000 mA rms

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Not directly specified; however, leakage current and dissipation factor are relevant:
    - Leakage Current (ILEAK): ≤ 0.01 x CR x VR or 3µA after 2 minutes
    - Dissipation Factor (tanδ) at 20°C, 120Hz: 18% (for 33V)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +105°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Through-Hole Technology (THT) type

- **Special Features:**
  - Low impedance and high ripple current capability
  - High reliability with endurance ratings of 2000 to 5000 hours at 105°C depending on the diameter
  - Capacitance tolerance of ±20% at 120Hz and 20°C

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The KF330M400J200A is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's KF series, designed for low impedance applications. It is a polarized capacitor that operates effectively in high-temperature environments, making it suitable for various electronic circuits requiring stable capacitance and reliability.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Management:** Used in power supply circuits to smooth voltage fluctuations and provide stable power.
- **Signal Processing:** Employed in audio and RF circuits for filtering and coupling applications.
- **Energy Storage:** Acts as a reservoir for energy in applications like power amplifiers and motor drives.
- **Decoupling:** Helps in reducing noise in power supply lines, ensuring clean power delivery to sensitive components.

This capacitor is particularly suitable for applications in consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment where reliability and performance under high temperatures are critical.
