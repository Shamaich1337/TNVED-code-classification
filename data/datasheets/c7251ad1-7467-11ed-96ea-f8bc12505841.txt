### Component Summary for HY820M400Y350A00T (CapXon Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitor)

- **Rated Voltage (VR):** 400 V DC
- **Surge Voltage (VS):** 440 V DC
- **Capacitance Tolerance (ΔC):** -20% to +20% at 120Hz, 20°C
- **Ripple Current (IR):** 
  - 590 mA at 120Hz, 105°C
  - 885 mA at 100kHz, 105°C

- **Leakage Current (ILEAK):** 543 µA (5 min at 20°C)
- **Max. Impedance (Z):** Measured at 100kHz, 20°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +105°C
- **Package Type:** Through-Hole (THT), Slim design
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the datasheet
- **AEC-Q200 Qualified:** No

#### Description:
The **HY820M400Y350A00T** is an aluminum electrolytic capacitor from CapXon's HY series. It is designed for high reliability and long life, featuring a slim profile suitable for space-constrained applications. This capacitor is rated for a maximum voltage of 400 V DC and has a capacitance value of 82 µF, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
The HY series aluminum electrolytic capacitor is typically used in:
- **Power Management:** Filtering and smoothing in power supply circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Coupling and decoupling applications in audio and RF circuits.
- **Energy Storage:** Providing temporary energy storage in power circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices requiring compact and reliable capacitors for power supply stabilization.

This component is particularly useful in applications where space is limited, and high reliability is essential, such as in telecommunications, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment.
