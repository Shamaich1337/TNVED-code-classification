### Component Summary: HMC361S8GETR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (Vcc): +5V ± 0.25V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Supply Current (Icc): 83 mA (typical at Vcc = 5V)
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Maximum Power Dissipation: 0.79 W (at T = 85 °C)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40 °C to +85 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 8-lead Standard Small Outline Package (SOIC_N_EP) with exposed pad
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low additive SSB phase noise of -148 dBc/Hz at 100 kHz offset
  - Wide bandwidth operation from DC to 10 GHz
  - High reverse leakage isolation of 45 dB
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The HMC361S8GETR is a low noise, divide-by-2 static divider utilizing InGaP GaAs HBT technology. It is designed for high-frequency applications, capable of operating from DC up to 10 GHz with a single +5V DC supply. The device is packaged in an 8-lead surface mount plastic package, making it suitable for compact designs.

#### Typical Applications:
The HMC361S8GETR is primarily used in prescaler applications for phase-locked loops (PLLs) in various communication systems. Typical applications include:
- **Satellite Communication Systems**: Used for frequency division in satellite transceivers.
- **Fiber Optic Communication**: Employed in signal processing for fiber optic links.
- **Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multi-Point Radios**: Utilized in radio frequency systems for signal division.
- **VSAT (Very Small Aperture Terminal)**: Applied in satellite communication systems for efficient signal processing.

This component is ideal for applications requiring low noise and high-frequency performance, ensuring good system noise performance and reliability in communication systems.
