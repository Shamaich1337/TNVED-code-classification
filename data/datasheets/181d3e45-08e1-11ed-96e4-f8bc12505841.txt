### Component Summary: ATTINY24V-10SSU

**Manufacturer:** Microchip Technology (formerly Atmel)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Operating Voltage (VCC): 1.8V to 3.6V
  - Maximum Operating Voltage: 6.0V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - DC Current per I/O Pin: 30 mA
  - DC Current for VCC and GND Pins: 200 mA
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Active Mode (4MHz, VCC = 3V): 0.8 to 2.5 mA
  - Idle Mode (4MHz, VCC = 3V): 0.2 to 0.5 mA
  - Power-down Mode (WDT disabled, VCC = 3V): 0.2 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:**
  - Available in TU (14-lead SOIC) and PC (20-lead QFN) packages.
- **Special Features:**
  - 10-bit ADC with various characteristics including integral and differential non-linearity.
  - Internal RC oscillator with user calibration.
  - Brown-out detection (BOD) with configurable levels.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards for moisture sensitivity.

#### Description:
The **ATTINY24V-10SSU** is a low-power 8-bit microcontroller from the AVR family, designed for automotive applications. It features a 2KB Flash memory, 128 bytes of SRAM, and 64 bytes of EEPROM. The microcontroller operates at a voltage range of 1.8V to 3.6V, making it suitable for battery-operated devices. It includes several I/O pins, an analog comparator, and a 10-bit ADC, which allows for versatile interfacing with various sensors and actuators.

#### Typical Applications:
The ATTINY24V-10SSU is commonly used in:
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its automotive-grade specifications, it is suitable for use in vehicle control systems, sensor interfaces, and other automotive electronics.
- **Low-Power Embedded Systems:** Ideal for battery-powered devices where power efficiency is critical.
- **Sensor Data Acquisition:** The integrated ADC allows for direct interfacing with analog sensors, making it suitable for applications in environmental monitoring, automotive sensors, and more.
- **Control Systems:** Can be used in various control applications, including motor control, lighting control, and home automation systems.

This microcontroller is particularly valued for its low power consumption, wide operating temperature range, and robust performance in automotive environments.
