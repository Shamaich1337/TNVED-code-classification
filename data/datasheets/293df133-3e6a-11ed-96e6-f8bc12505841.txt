### Component Summary: REF3112AIDBZR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Output Voltage: 1.25 V
  - Input Voltage Range: VOUT + 0.05 V to 5.5 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Output Current: ±10 mA
  - Load Current: Up to 25 mA
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Quiescent Current: 100 µA (typical), 115 µA (maximum)
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40 °C to +125 °C
- **Package Type**: 
  - SOT-23 (3-pin)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low Dropout Voltage: 5 mV
  - High Accuracy: ±0.2%
  - Excellent Drift Performance: 15 ppm/°C (0°C to 70°C), 20 ppm/°C (-40°C to 125°C)
  - No load capacitor required; stable with any capacitive load
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - Level 1, according to JEDEC J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The REF3112AIDBZR is a precision, low-power, low-dropout voltage reference from Texas Instruments. It is designed to provide a stable reference voltage of 1.25 V with high accuracy and minimal drift over temperature. The device operates efficiently in a compact SOT-23 package, making it suitable for space-constrained applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Portable, Battery-Powered Equipment**: Ideal for devices requiring low power consumption and compact size.
- **Data Acquisition Systems**: Provides stable reference voltages necessary for accurate data conversion.
- **Medical Equipment**: Used in devices where precision and reliability are critical.
- **Hand-Held Test Equipment**: Ensures accurate measurements in portable testing devices.

The REF3112AIDBZR is particularly useful in applications where a precise voltage reference is needed, such as in analog-to-digital converters (ADCs) and operational amplifiers, ensuring that the performance of these components is optimized for accuracy and stability.
