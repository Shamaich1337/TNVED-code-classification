  - Shielded construction
  - Excellent DC/DC energy storage up to 1 MHz to 2 MHz

  - Ultra-low buzz noise due to composite construction

**Description:**
The IHLP3232DZER330M11 is a low DCR shielded inductor designed for high current applications. It is part of Vishay's IHLP series of commercial inductors, which are known for their compact size and high efficiency. The inductor has an inductance value of 33 μH with a tolerance of ±20%. It is optimized for use in DC/DC converters and other power management applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- Power management in PDAs, notebooks, desktops, and servers
- High current Point of Load (POL) converters
- Low profile, high current power supplies
- Battery-powered devices
- DC/DC converters in distributed power systems
- DC/DC converters for Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs)

This component is particularly suitable for applications requiring efficient energy storage and low noise operation, making it ideal for modern electronic devices that demand high performance in compact form factors.
