#### Description:
The JMV0805S260T221XXG is a multilayer chip varistor designed for surge protection and electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection in electronic circuits. Varistors are voltage-dependent resistors that exhibit a nonlinear resistance characteristic, allowing them to clamp voltage spikes and protect sensitive components from transient overvoltages.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Surge Protection:** Used in power supply circuits to protect against voltage spikes caused by lightning or switching events.
- **ESD Protection:** Commonly employed in data lines and sensitive electronic components to prevent damage from electrostatic discharge.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Integrated into devices such as smartphones, computers, and home appliances to enhance reliability and longevity.
- **Automotive Applications:** Suitable for automotive electronics due to AEC-Q200 compliance, ensuring performance under harsh conditions.

This component is essential for ensuring the reliability and safety of electronic devices by mitigating the risks associated with voltage transients and ESD events.
