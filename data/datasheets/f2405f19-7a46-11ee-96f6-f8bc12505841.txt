**Component Type:** Variable Capacitance Diode  
**Primary Function:** The 1SV280 is a silicon epitaxial planar type variable capacitance diode primarily used in voltage-controlled oscillators (VCOs) for UHF band radio applications.

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Voltage (VR): 15 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Reverse Current (IR): Up to 3 nA at VR = 15 V
- **Power Consumption:** Not explicitly stated; typically low for diodes.
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (Tj): Up to 125 °C
  - Storage Temperature (Tstg): -55 to 125 °C
- **Package Type:** Not specified in the provided text, but typically in a small surface mount or through-hole package.
- **Special Features:**
  - High capacitance ratio: C2V / C10V = 2.4 (typical)
  - Low series resistance: rs = 0.44 Ω (typical)
  - Useful for small size tuners.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** Not specified in the provided text.

#### Description:
The 1SV280 is a variable capacitance diode designed for applications requiring tunable capacitance, such as in radio frequency (RF) circuits. Its high capacitance ratio and low series resistance make it suitable for precise tuning in VCOs, which are essential components in communication systems.

#### Typical Applications:
- **VCOs for UHF Band Radio:** The diode is primarily used in voltage-controlled oscillators, which are critical for generating RF signals in communication devices.
- **Tuning Circuits:** Its variable capacitance allows for fine-tuning of frequency in small size tuners, making it ideal for compact radio receivers and transmitters.
- **Signal Processing:** Can be utilized in various RF signal processing applications where variable capacitance is needed.
