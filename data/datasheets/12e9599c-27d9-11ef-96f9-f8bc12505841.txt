### Component Summary: DESDALC5LP-7B

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 5.8V (min), 11V (typ), 17V (max)
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 9A (8/20µs)
  - Reverse Current (IR): 25nA (max at VR = 5V)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Package Power Dissipation (PD): 250mW
  - Peak Pulse Power Dissipation (PPP): 150W (8/20µs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (TJ, TSTG): -65°C to +150°C
- **Package Type:**
  - X1-DFN1006-2 (Low Profile, 0.53mm max height, 1.08mm x 0.68mm footprint)
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD Protection per IEC 61000-4-2 Standard: ±30kV (Air and Contact Discharge)
  - Low Channel Input Capacitance: 26-30pF
  - Totally Lead-Free & Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020E

**Description:**
The DESDALC5LP-7B is a single-channel bidirectional Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diode designed to protect sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge (ESD) damage. Its compact size and high surge capability make it particularly suitable for portable electronic devices.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Cellular Handsets:** Protects against ESD events that can damage internal circuits.
- **Portable Electronics:** Ideal for devices like digital cameras and MP3 players where space is limited.
- **Computers and Peripherals:** Provides ESD protection for USB ports and other interfaces.

This component is essential in applications where ESD protection is critical to ensure the reliability and longevity of electronic devices.
