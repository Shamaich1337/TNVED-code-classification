#### Description:
The STFT 12118 is a tubeless air-cooled condenser designed for efficient heat exchange in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. Its unique construction eliminates traditional copper tubes, instead utilizing steel fins with integrated collars that form the refrigerant pathways. This design maximizes thermal conductivity and minimizes the internal volume of the refrigerant circuit, leading to reduced refrigerant costs and improved performance.

#### Typical Applications:
The STFT 12118 is typically used in:
- **Refrigeration Systems**: As a condenser to reject heat from refrigerants, making it suitable for commercial and industrial refrigeration applications.
- **Air Conditioning Units**: It can be integrated into air conditioning systems to enhance cooling efficiency.
- **Marine Environments**: Its corrosion-resistant features make it ideal for use in coastal and marine applications where exposure to saltwater is a concern.
- **Compact Installations**: The reduced dimensions allow for installation in spaces where traditional condensers may not fit, making it versatile for various setups.

This component is particularly beneficial in applications requiring high efficiency and reliability, especially in challenging environmental conditions.
