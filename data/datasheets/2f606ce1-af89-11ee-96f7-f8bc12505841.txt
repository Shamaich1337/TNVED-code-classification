### Component Summary: DMN3018SFG

**Manufacturer:** Diodes Incorporated  
**Product Code:** DMN3018SFG  
**Document Number:** DS35638 Rev. 5 - 2  
**Date:** November 2016  

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Breakdown Voltage (V(BR)DSS): 30V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (V(GS)): ±25V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (I(D)) at V(GS) = 10V:
    - 30A at TC = +25°C
    - 25A at TC = +70°C
  - Continuous Drain Current (I(D)) at V(GS) = 4.5V:
    - 24A at TC = +25°C
    - 20A at TC = +70°C

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P(D)): 1.0W (steady state), 2.2W (with thermal bias)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction and Storage Temperature (T(J), T(STG)): -55°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - Power DI3333-8 (small form factor)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low R_DS(ON): 21mΩ at V(GS) = 10V, 35mΩ at V(GS) = 4.5V
  - ESD Protected Gate
  - Lead-Free and Fully RoHS Compliant
  - Halogen and Antimony Free
  - Qualified to AEC-Q101 standards for high reliability
  - Automotive-compliant variant available (DMN3018SFGQ)

- **Moisture Sensitivity Level (MSL):**
  - Level 1 per J-STD-020E

#### Description:
The DMN3018SFG is a 30V N-channel enhancement mode MOSFET designed for high efficiency in power management applications. It features low on-state resistance (R_DS(ON)), which minimizes power losses during operation, and superior switching performance, making it suitable for various electronic applications.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Backlighting:** Used in LED drivers for displays.
- **Power Management Functions:** Ideal for DC-DC converters and other power regulation circuits.
- **Automotive Applications:** Due to its high reliability and compliance with automotive standards, it can be used in various automotive electronic systems.

This MOSFET is particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, thanks to its compact package design, which allows for higher density in end products.
