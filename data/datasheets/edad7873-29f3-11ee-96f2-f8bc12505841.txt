### Component Summary: AP7383-33W5-7

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (VIN): 3.5V to 30V
  - Output Voltage (VOUT): 3.3V (fixed version)

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Output Current (IOUT): Up to 200mA (maximum)
  - Current Limit: 150mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Low Ground Current: 1.8µA (typical at IOUT = 0A)
  - Standby Current: 0.01µA (when EN is OFF)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -40°C to +125°C
  - Storage Temperature: -65°C to +150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT25 (W5 package)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low Dropout Voltage: 500mV at IOUT = 50mA
  - Thermal Shutdown Function
  - High Output Voltage Accuracy
  - Compatible with Low ESR Ceramic Capacitors
  - Excellent Line/Load Regulation
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 
    - SOT25: Level 1 per J-STD-020

**Description:**
The AP7383-33W5-7 is a positive voltage regulator integrated circuit (IC) designed to provide a stable output voltage of 3.3V. It features a wide input voltage range of up to 30V, making it suitable for various applications. The device includes essential components such as a voltage reference, error amplifier, and current limit circuit, ensuring high accuracy and low dropout performance.

**Typical Applications:**
The AP7383 series is ideal for use in:
- Battery-powered equipment
- Laptop, palmtop, and notebook computers
- Portable information appliances
- USB devices and other portable electronics

This voltage regulator is particularly beneficial in applications where low power consumption and high efficiency are critical, such as in battery-operated devices. Its compact size and low dropout voltage make it suitable for space-constrained designs.
