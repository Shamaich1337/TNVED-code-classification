#### Description:
The CL31A106KAHNNNE is a multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC) manufactured by Samsung. MLCCs are widely used electronic components that temporarily store electrical charge. They are characterized by their small size, high capacitance values, and low ESR, making them suitable for various applications in modern electronic devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **General Electronic Circuits**: Used in filtering, decoupling, and bypassing applications.
- **Power Management**: Essential in power supply circuits to stabilize voltage and reduce noise.
- **Signal Processing**: Employed in RF circuits and audio applications to filter signals.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Commonly found in smartphones, computers, and other compact electronic devices due to their miniature size and efficiency.

This capacitor is particularly suitable for applications where space is limited and high reliability is required, such as in mobile devices and high-frequency circuits.
