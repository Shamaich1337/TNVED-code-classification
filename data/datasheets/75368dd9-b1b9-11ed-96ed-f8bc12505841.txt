  - Not specified in the provided data.

#### Description:
The BAS516 is a small-signal fast switching diode designed for applications requiring rapid switching capabilities. It is characterized by its ability to handle moderate current levels while providing a high reverse voltage rating, making it suitable for various electronic circuits.

- **Extreme Fast Switching:** The BAS516 is ideal for applications that require quick response times, such as in signal processing and switching circuits.
- **Rectification:** It can be used in rectifier circuits where fast recovery times are essential.
- **General Purpose Diode Applications:** Suitable for use in low-power electronic devices, including power management systems and signal modulation circuits.
