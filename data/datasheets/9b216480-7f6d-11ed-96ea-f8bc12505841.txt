### Component Summary: HMC686LP4ETR

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:** Vcc1, Vcc2, Vcc3 = +5V (absolute maximum +5.5V)
- **Current Ratings:** 
  - Typical supply current (Icc) = 105 mA
  - Maximum supply current = 140 mA
- **Power Consumption:** 
  - Total supply current = 105 mA (typical) at Vcc = 5V
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40°C to +85°C
- **Package Type:** 24-lead 4x4 mm QFN (Quad Flat No-lead)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** MSL1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)
- **Special Features:**
  - Integrated LO amplifier
  - High dynamic range passive MMIC mixer
  - Optimized for both high-side and low-side LO frequency plans

**Description:**
The HMC686LP4ETR is a BiCMOS mixer with an integrated local oscillator (LO) amplifier, designed for RF applications in the frequency range of 700 MHz to 1500 MHz. It features excellent input third-order intercept point (IP3) performance of +34 dBm, making it suitable for high-performance communication systems. The device is packaged in a compact 4x4 mm QFN format, which is ideal for space-constrained applications.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Cellular Communication:** Suitable for 3G and LTE applications, including base stations and repeaters.
- **Wireless Communication:** Used in GSM, CDMA, and OFDM systems.
- **Transmitters and Receivers:** Ideal for RF signal processing in various communication devices.

The HMC686LP4ETR is particularly well-suited for applications requiring high linearity and low conversion loss, making it a preferred choice for modern wireless communication systems.
