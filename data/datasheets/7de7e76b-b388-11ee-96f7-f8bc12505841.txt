### Component Summary: ESD5344D-10/TR

**Manufacturer:** Will Semiconductor Ltd.

**Description:**
The ESD5344D is an ultra-low capacitance Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) array designed to protect high-speed data interfaces from electrostatic discharge (ESD) and other transient voltage events. It features four pairs of ultra-low capacitance steering diodes and a TVS diode, making it suitable for safeguarding sensitive electronic components connected to data and transmission lines.

**Key Specifications:**
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Reverse Maximum Working Voltage (VRWM): 5.0 V
  - Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VBR): 7.0 - 9.0 V
- **Current Ratings:**
  - Peak Pulse Current (IPP): 4 A (8/20 μs)
  - Reverse Leakage Current (IR): <1 nA (at VRWM = 5V)
- **Power Consumption:**
  - Peak Pulse Power (Ppk): 50 W (tp = 8/20 μs)
- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): 125 °C
  - Operating Temperature (TOP): -40 °C to 85 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -55 °C to 150 °C
- **Package Type:**
  - DFN2510-10L
- **Special Features:**
  - ESD protection up to ±20 kV (contact discharge) according to IEC61000-4-2
  - Low clamping voltage (VCL): 14 V typ. @ IPP = 16 A (TLP)
  - Ultra-low capacitance: CJ = 0.4 pF typ.
  - Solid-state silicon technology
- **Moisture Sensitive Level:** 
  - Not specified in the provided text, but typically assessed according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

**Typical Applications:**
The ESD5344D is commonly used in various high-speed data interface applications, including:
- USB 2.0 and USB 3.0
- HDMI (versions 1.3, 1.4, and 2.0)
- SATA and eSATA
- DVI
- IEEE 1394
- PCI Express
- Portable electronics and notebooks

This component is ideal for applications requiring robust ESD protection while maintaining signal integrity, making it suitable for consumer electronics, data communication devices, and other sensitive electronic systems.
