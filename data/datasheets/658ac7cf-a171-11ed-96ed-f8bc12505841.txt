#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (V+ to V-): 3V to 12.6V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Output Current: 30mA
  - Supply Current per Amplifier: 1.2mA
  - Shutdown Supply Current: 10µA (max)

  - Supply Current: 1.2mA per amplifier
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type**: 
  - 6-Lead Plastic TSOT-23
- **Special Features**: 
  - Rail-to-Rail output
  - Low noise: 1.9nV/√Hz
  - Low offset voltage: 350µV max
  - Enable pin for shutdown mode
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: 
  - MSL 1 (JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The LT6233 is a low noise, rail-to-rail output operational amplifier designed for low power applications. It features a high gain-bandwidth product of 60MHz and is optimized for low supply voltage signal conditioning systems. The amplifier is unity-gain stable and includes an enable pin that allows for low power shutdown, making it suitable for battery-operated devices.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Instrumentation Amplifiers**: Ideal for applications requiring high precision and low noise.
- **Ultrasound Amplifiers**: Used in medical imaging and diagnostics.
- **Active Filters**: Suitable for signal processing applications.
- **Driving A/D Converters**: Provides the necessary signal conditioning for analog-to-digital conversion.
- **Rail-to-Rail Buffer Amplifiers**: Can be used in various buffering applications where maximum output swing is required.

The LT6233 is particularly effective in applications where low noise and low power consumption are critical, such as in portable and battery-powered devices. Its rail-to-rail output capability enhances its versatility in various signal processing tasks.
