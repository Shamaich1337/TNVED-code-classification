### Component Summary: XC7Z100-2FFG900I

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: The XC7Z100 operates at a core voltage of 1.0V to 1.2V.
- **Current Ratings**: The typical I/O current is around 12mA per pin.
- **Power Consumption**: The power consumption varies based on usage but can be as low as 0.5W in typical applications.
- **Operating Temperature Range**: The device is rated for an operating temperature range of -40°C to +100°C.
- **Package Type**: The XC7Z100-2FFG900I is housed in a FFG900 package, which is a 900-ball FCLGA (Fine-Pitch Ball Grid Array).
- **Special Features**: This FPGA integrates a dual-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor, programmable logic, and various I/O interfaces.
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL)**: MSL is classified as MSL 3 according to JEDEC J-STD-020E.

#### Description:
The XC7Z100-2FFG900I is a member of the Xilinx Zynq-7000 series, which combines a dual-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor with Xilinx 7-series FPGA logic. This integration allows for a high degree of flexibility and performance in embedded systems, enabling users to implement custom hardware accelerators alongside software processing.

#### Typical Applications:
The XC7Z100 is widely used in various applications, including:
- **Embedded Systems**: Ideal for applications requiring both hardware and software processing capabilities.
- **Signal Processing**: Suitable for real-time signal processing tasks in communications and audio/video applications.
- **Industrial Automation**: Used in control systems and robotics for real-time data processing and control.
- **Automotive**: Employed in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and infotainment systems.
- **Medical Devices**: Utilized in imaging systems and diagnostic equipment for processing and analysis.

This component is particularly valued for its ability to handle complex algorithms and data processing tasks while providing the flexibility to adapt to changing requirements in various applications.
