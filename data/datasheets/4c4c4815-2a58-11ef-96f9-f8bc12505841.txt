### Component Summary: STMPS2171STR

**Key Characteristics and Specifications:**

- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Input Voltage (V_IN): 2.7 V to 5.5 V
  - Output Voltage (V_OUT): 0 V to 5.5 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Output Current: 1000 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Standby Supply Current: 12 µA maximum
  - Power Dissipation: 90 mW (typical)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_J): -40 °C to 125 °C
  - Ambient Temperature: -40 °C to 85 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - Available in SOT23-5L, SO-8, and MSOP8 packages.

- **Special Features:**
  - Thermal and short-circuit protection with overcurrent logic output.
