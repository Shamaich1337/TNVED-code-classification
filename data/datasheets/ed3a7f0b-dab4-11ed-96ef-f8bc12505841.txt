### Component Summary: SS24-E3/52T (Vishay)

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Maximum Repetitive Peak Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 40 V
  - Maximum RMS Voltage (VRMS): 28 V
  - Maximum DC Blocking Voltage (VDC): 40 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Forward Rectified Current (IF(AV)): 2.0 A
  - Peak Forward Surge Current (IFSM): 75 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF) at 2.0 A: 0.70 V

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -65 °C to +150 °C
  - Storage Temperature (TSTG): -65 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMB (DO-214AA)

- **Special Features:**
  - Low profile package suitable for automated placement
  - Guardring for overvoltage protection
  - Low power losses and high efficiency
  - Low forward voltage drop
  - High surge capability
  - AEC-Q101 qualified version available for automotive applications
  - Moisture Sensitive Level: MSL level 1, per J-STD-020, LF maximum peak of 260 °C

#### Description:
The SS24-E3/52T is a surface-mount Schottky barrier rectifier designed for low voltage, high-frequency applications. It features a low forward voltage drop and high surge capability, making it suitable for various power management tasks. The component is housed in a compact SMB package, which facilitates automated assembly processes.

#### Typical Applications:
The SS24-E3/52T is commonly used in:
- Low voltage high-frequency inverters
- Freewheeling applications
- DC/DC converters
- Polarity protection circuits

This component is ideal for applications requiring efficient rectification and minimal power loss, particularly in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial power supplies.
