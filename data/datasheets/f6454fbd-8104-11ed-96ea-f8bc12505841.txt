#### Description:
The ACPL-K74T is a dual-channel high-speed digital CMOS optocoupler designed for applications requiring reliable signal isolation. It features integrated photodiodes, high-speed trans-impedance amplifiers, and voltage comparators with output drivers, making it suitable for high-speed digital communication.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Automotive Applications:** Ideal for signal isolation in automotive systems, including CAN Bus and SPI communications interfaces.
- **Power Management:** Used in automotive IPM drivers for DC-DC converters and motor inverters.
- **Signal Isolation:** Provides high-temperature digital/analog signal isolation, ensuring safe operation in harsh environments.

This component is particularly valuable in automotive and industrial applications where high reliability and fast switching speeds are critical.
