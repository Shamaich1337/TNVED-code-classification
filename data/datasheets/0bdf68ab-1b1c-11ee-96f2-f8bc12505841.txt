#### Description:
The SN74ABT244ADWR is an octal buffer and line driver designed for high-speed applications. It features two 4-bit buffers/line drivers with separate output enable (OE) inputs. When the OE input is low, the device passes non-inverted data from the A inputs to the Y outputs. When OE is high, the outputs enter a high-impedance state, allowing for bus-oriented applications.
