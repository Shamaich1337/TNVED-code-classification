### Component Summary: TL431AIL3T

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** TL431AIL3T  
**Type:** Adjustable Shunt Voltage Reference

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Adjustable output voltage: 2.5 V to 36 V
  - Cathode to anode voltage (VKA): Up to 37 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Sink current capability: 1 mA to 100 mA
  - Reference input current range: -0.05 mA to 10 mA

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Off-state cathode current: Up to 1000 nA

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Automotive version: -40 °C to +125 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23-3 (L3T)

- **Special Features:**
  - AEC-Q100 qualified (automotive grade)
  - 0.5% voltage precision (TL431B version)
  - 1% and 2% voltage precision for other versions
  - Typical output impedance: 0.22 Ω

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: 1 (per JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TL431 is an adjustable shunt voltage reference designed to provide a stable reference voltage across a wide range of operating conditions. It is particularly suited for automotive applications due to its extended temperature range and AEC-Q100 qualification. The output voltage can be set using two external resistors, allowing for flexibility in various circuit designs.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Power Supply Regulation:** Used in power supply circuits to maintain a constant output voltage.
- **Industrial Equipment:** Employed in industrial control systems for voltage reference.
- **Automotive Systems:** Utilized in automotive electronics for voltage regulation and reference in various applications, including battery management systems and sensor circuits.
- **Signal Processing:** Acts as a reference voltage in signal conditioning circuits.

This component is ideal for applications requiring precise voltage regulation and stability over a wide temperature range, making it a versatile choice for both automotive and industrial environments.
