### Component Summary for Littelfuse 0437003.WR

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 32V AC / 35V DC
- **Current Ratings**: 7.0A
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Nominal Voltage Drop at Rated Current: 0.13V
  - Nominal Power Dissipation at Rated Current: 0.91W
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -55ºC to +150ºC
- **Package Type**: Surface Mount (1206)
- **Special Features**:
  - 100% Lead-free, Halogen-free, and RoHS compliant
  - High I²t values for inrush current withstand capability
  - Suitable for both leaded and lead-free reflow/wave soldering
  - UL Recognized to UL/CSA/NMX 248-1 and UL/CSA/NMX 248-14
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: IPC/JEDEC J-STD-020, Level 1

#### Description:
The Littelfuse 0437003.WR is a fast-acting ceramic fuse designed for surface mount applications. It provides overcurrent protection for circuits that may experience high ambient temperatures, with a maximum operating temperature of 150ºC. The fuse is constructed from advanced ceramic materials, ensuring excellent temperature stability and reliability in performance.

#### Typical Applications:
This fuse is commonly used in various electronic devices and systems, including:
- **LCD Displays**
- **Servers**
- **Printers**
- **Scanners**
- **Data Modems**

These applications benefit from the fuse's ability to handle high inrush currents and its robust construction, making it suitable for environments where reliability and thermal stability are critical.
