The RS-Helios-1615 is a 3D LiDAR sensor produced by RoboSense, designed for high-precision distance measurement and environmental mapping. It features 32 laser channels with a vertical field of view ranging from -16¬∞ to +15¬∞, making it suitable for various applications in autonomous vehicles, robotics, and mapping.

- **Voltage Ratings:** 9V - 32V
- **Current Ratings:** 1.2A (typical)
- **Power Consumption:** 12W (typical), 20W (peak)

- **Storage Temperature Range:** -40¬∞C to +85¬∞C
- **Package Type:** œÜ97.5 mm x H100 mm

- **Special Features:**
  - Class 1 laser safety (eye-safe)
  - Supports multiple laser return modes (Strongest, Last, First, Dual)
  - Time synchronization via GPS and PTP
  - IP67 rated for dust and water resistance

**Description:**
The RS-Helios-1615 is a high-performance LiDAR sensor that utilizes laser technology to capture 3D spatial data. It emits laser pulses and measures the time it takes for the light to return after reflecting off objects, allowing it to create detailed point clouds of the environment. The sensor is equipped with advanced features such as phase locking and multiple return modes, enhancing its capability to detect and analyze complex environments.

**Typical Applications:**
- **Autonomous Vehicles:** Used for navigation and obstacle detection.
- **Robotics:** Enables robots to perceive their surroundings for tasks such as mapping and navigation.
- **Mapping and Surveying:** Provides accurate 3D models of terrain and structures.
- **Industrial Automation:** Assists in monitoring and managing automated processes in manufacturing environments.

This summary encapsulates the essential characteristics and applications of the RS-Helios-1615, highlighting its role as a versatile tool in modern sensing and automation technologies.
