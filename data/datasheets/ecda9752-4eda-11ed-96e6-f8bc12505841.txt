### Component Summary: LDL1117S50R

**Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics  
**Product Code:** LDL1117S50R  
**Package Type:** SOT223

#### Key Specifications:
- **Input Voltage Range:** 2.5 V to 18 V
- **Absolute Maximum Input Voltage (AMR):** 20 V
- **Output Voltage:** Fixed at 5.0 V
- **Guaranteed Output Current:** 1.2 A
- **Dropout Voltage:** 350 mV @ 1.2 A
- **Power Dissipation:** Internally limited
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -40 °C to 125 °C
- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):** Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.
- **High Power Supply Rejection Ratio (PSRR):** 87 dB @ 120 Hz

#### Description:
The LDL1117 is a low-dropout (LDO) linear voltage regulator designed to provide a stable output voltage of 5.0 V with a maximum output current of 1.2 A. It operates efficiently with an input voltage range from 2.5 V to 18 V, making it suitable for various applications where a regulated voltage is required. The device features a typical dropout voltage of 350 mV at full load, which allows it to maintain regulation even when the input voltage is close to the output voltage.

#### Typical Applications:
- **Consumer Electronics:** Used in devices requiring stable voltage supplies.
- **Industrial Applications:** Provides regulated power for industrial control systems.
- **Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS):** Acts as a post-regulator to ensure output stability.
- **Motherboard Point of Load (P.O.L.):** Supplies power to critical components on motherboards.
- **DC-DC Post-Regulation:** Used in conjunction with DC-DC converters to refine output voltage.

#### Special Features:
- **Thermal Protection:** The LDL1117 includes internal thermal shutdown and current limiting features to protect against overheating and overcurrent conditions.
- **High PSRR:** The high power supply rejection ratio makes it suitable for applications where noise reduction is critical.
- **Capacitor Selection:** Requires external capacitors for stability, with a minimum input capacitor of 1 μF and an output capacitor of 4.7 μF recommended.

This summary provides a comprehensive overview of the LDL1117S50R voltage regulator, highlighting its key specifications, functionality, and typical use cases in electronic applications.
