### Component Summary: BQ78350DBTR-R1

#### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings**: 
  - Supply Voltage (VCC): 2.4V to 2.6V
  - Absolute Maximum Supply Voltage: 2.75V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Operating Mode Current: 650 µA
  - Sleep Mode Current: 300 µA
  - Shutdown Mode Current: 0.1 to 1 µA
  - Charge/Discharge Current Reporting: Up to 320 A
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Operating Mode: 650 µA
  - Sleep Mode: 300 µA
  - Shutdown Mode: 0.1 to 1 µA
- **Operating Temperature Range**: -40°C to 85°C
- **Package Type**: TSSOP (30 pins)
- **Moisture Sensitive Level**: MSL Level 2 (260°C, 1 year)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Compensated End-of-Discharge Voltage (CEDV) gauging algorithm
  - Supports SMBus host communication
  - Flexible configuration for 3 to 15 series Li-Ion and LiFePO4 batteries
  - On-chip temperature sensor option
  - Lifetime data logging
  - Programmable protection features (voltage, current, temperature)
  - Supports CC-CV charging

#### Description:
The **BQ78350DBTR-R1** is a Battery Management Controller designed for Li-Ion and LiFePO4 battery systems. It serves as a companion to the bq769x0 family of Analog Front End (AFE) devices, providing comprehensive battery management functionalities. The device features an accurate fuel gauge, state-of-health (SoH) monitoring, cell balancing, and a full range of protection features based on voltage, current, and temperature.

#### Typical Applications:
The BQ78350DBTR-R1 is suitable for various applications, including:
- **Light Electric Vehicles (LEVs)**: eBikes, eScooters, and pedal-assist bicycles.
- **Power Tools**: Gardening tools and other battery-operated devices.
- **Battery Backup Systems**: Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) and telecom power systems.
- **Consumer Electronics**: Handheld vacuum cleaners and robot vacuums.

This component is particularly useful in applications requiring precise battery monitoring and management, ensuring safety and efficiency in battery usage.
