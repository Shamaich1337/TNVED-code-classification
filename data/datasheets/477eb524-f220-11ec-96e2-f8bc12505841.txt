### Component Summary: BSS84AK,215 (Nexperia)

#### Key Characteristics and Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Drain-Source Voltage (VDS): 50 V
  - Gate-Source Voltage (VGS): -20 V to 20 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Continuous Drain Current (ID): 180 mA at VGS = -10 V and T_amb = 25°C
  - Peak Drain Current (IDM): 0.7 A (single pulse, t_p ≤ 10 µs)

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Total Power Dissipation (P_tot): 350 mW at T_amb = 25°C (can be increased to 420 mW under specific conditions)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (T_j): -55°C to 150°C
  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -55°C to 150°C
  - Storage Temperature (T_stg): -65°C to 150°C

- **Package Type:**
  - SOT23 (TO-236AB), a small surface-mounted device (SMD) package with 3 leads.

- **Special Features or Notes:**
  - Logic-level compatible
  - Very fast switching capabilities
