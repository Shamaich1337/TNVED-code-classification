  - Surge Voltage: 8 V
- **Current Ratings**: 
  - Maximum Ripple Current (IRMS): 1.5 A
- **Power Consumption**: 
  - Maximum ESR at 100 kHz: 0.24 Ω
- **Operating Temperature Range**: 
  - -55 °C to +125 °C (with voltage derating above 85 °C)
- **Package Type**: 
  - Molded Chip Capacitor (Surface Mount)
- **Special Features**: 
  - Low ESR (Equivalent Series Resistance)
  - Surge current tested
  - Qualified to EIA-717
  - Compatible with high-volume automatic pick and place equipment
  - Moisture Sensitivity Level: 1 (according to JEDEC J-STD-020E)

#### Description:
The TR3B106K025C0450 is a solid tantalum surface mount chip capacitor from Vishay's TANTAMOUNT™ series. It is designed for applications requiring high reliability and low equivalent series resistance (ESR). The capacitor features a molded case, which provides enhanced durability and stability in various electronic applications.
