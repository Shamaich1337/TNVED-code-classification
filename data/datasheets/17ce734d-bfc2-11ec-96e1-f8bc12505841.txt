### Component Summary: MBRS4201T3G

**Manufacturer:** ON Semiconductor (now onsemi)

**Component Type:** Schottky Power Rectifier

**Description:**
The MBRS4201T3G is a Schottky power rectifier designed for high-efficiency applications. It features a fast soft-recovery characteristic, making it suitable for various power management tasks. The device is housed in a compact SMC package, which facilitates automated handling and is ideal for surface mount technology.

### Key Specifications:
- **Voltage Ratings:**
  - Peak Repetitive Reverse Voltage (VRRM): 200 V
  - Working Peak Reverse Voltage (VRWM): 200 V
  - DC Blocking Voltage (VR): 200 V

- **Current Ratings:**
  - Average Rectified Forward Current (IF(AV)): 4 A
  - Nonrepetitive Peak Surge Current (IFSM): 100 A

- **Power Consumption:**
  - Maximum Instantaneous Forward Voltage (VF):
    - At 25 °C: 0.86 V (IF = 4 A)
    - At 150 °C: 0.61 V (IF = 4 A)

- **Operating Temperature Range:**
  - Junction Temperature (TJ): -55 °C to +150 °C

- **Package Type:**
  - SMC (Surface Mount, 2-lead, Case 403AC)

- **Special Features:**
  - Fast Switching Speed: Reverse Recovery Time (trr) < 35 ns
  - Soft Recovery Characteristics: Softness Factor (tb/ta) ≥ 1
  - AEC-Q101 Qualified for automotive applications
  - Pb-Free, Halogen Free/BFR Free, and RoHS Compliant

- **Moisture Sensitive Level (MSL):**
  - MSL Level: Not specified in the provided text, but typically follows JEDEC J-STD-020E standards.

### Typical Applications:
The MBRS4201T3G is commonly used in:
- Engine and convenience control systems
- Motor controls
- Battery chargers
- Switching power supplies

### Benefits:
- Significantly reduced electromagnetic interference (EMI)
- Eliminates the need for snubber circuits
- Low switching and heat losses
- Improved thermal management

This component is particularly advantageous in applications requiring efficient power conversion and management, making it suitable for both consumer electronics and automotive systems.
