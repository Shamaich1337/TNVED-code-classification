  - Ambient Temperature (T_amb): -65 °C to +150 °C

  - SOT323 (SC-70), Surface-Mounted Device (SMD)
- **Special Features:**
  - General-purpose PNP transistor suitable for low voltage and low current applications.

  - Not specified in the provided data, but typically SMD components are classified under JEDEC J-STD-020E.

The BC857W is a PNP general-purpose transistor designed for switching and amplification applications. It is housed in a compact SOT323 (SC-70) package, making it suitable for space-constrained designs. The transistor is characterized by its low voltage and current ratings, making it ideal for low-power applications.

- **General-Purpose Switching:** The BC857W can be used in various switching applications where low power is required.
- **Amplification:** It is suitable for signal amplification in audio and other electronic circuits.
- **Consumer Electronics:** Commonly found in devices such as audio equipment, sensors, and small signal applications.
- **Signal Processing:** Used in circuits that require signal modulation or processing.

This transistor is a versatile component that can be utilized in a wide range of electronic applications, particularly where compact size and low power consumption are critical.
